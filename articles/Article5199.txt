 Dollar Firms on Possible G-7 Meeting, Signs German Rates Won't Rise Instantly   By Choi, Audrey, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1990  The dollar firmed on Dec 11, 1990 on indications that the Group of Seven nations may meet in Jan 1991 to discuss the dollar's frailty and on signs that German interest rates aren't immediately rising. Late in New York, the dollar was quoted at 1.4792 marks, up from 1.4725 marks late the previous day in New York.
