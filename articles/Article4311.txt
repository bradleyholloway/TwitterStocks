 Netflix Digs Deep for 'Gossip Girl'   By Schuker, Lauren A E; Woo, Stu, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 2011  Fresh from its disastrous attempt to split its DVD and video-streaming services, Netflix Inc. agreed to pay CBS Corp. and Time Warner Inc. about $1 billion for the rights to stream CW series such as "Gossip Girl" and "The Vampire Diaries," people familiar with the deal said.
