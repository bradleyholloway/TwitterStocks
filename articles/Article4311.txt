 Dome Petroleum Rejects Bid to Buy Dome Mines Stake   By By Gary Lamphier, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1987  CALGARY, Alberta -- Dome Petroleum Ltd. rejected an offer by Giant Yellowknife Mines Ltd. to purchase its 21.5% stake in Dome Mines Ltd. for 448.6 million Canadian dollars ($339 million).  Dome Petroleum declined to comment on its reasons for rejecting the offer, but said it would vote its shares in favor of a previously reported plan to merge Dome Mines with two other gold mining concerns. Giant Yellowknife had planned to vote against the merger if it acquired the shares.
