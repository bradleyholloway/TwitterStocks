GRACE AGREED to combine its National Medical business with a unit of Germany's Fresenius, creating a dialysis giant. Grace will receive $2.3 billion in the pact, which is structured to leave the new company with a liability equal to that amount. Grace's holders will end up with 44.8% of the firm. Despite the pact, Grace effectively invited a higher offer from Baxter, which recently bid $3.8 billion for the Grace unit and was turned away.
