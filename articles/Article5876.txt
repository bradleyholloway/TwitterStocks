 UAL launches fare cuts for the fall, and some other big airlines follow   By Carey, Susan, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1996  Some big airlines, attempting to pump up fall-travel bookings ahead of the reinstatement of a 10% federal ticket tax, announced aggressive fare cuts on Aug 9, 1996. UAL Corp's United Airlines launched the promotion, offering reductions of as much as 35% on 21-day advance-purchase tickets for travel in the US.
