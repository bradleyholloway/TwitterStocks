 Volatility of Merck, Caterpillar Increase Before Profit Reports   By Schreiber, Cynthia, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 2005  Five companies whose stocks are Dow industrial components -- Caterpillar, Coca-Cola Co., McDonald's Corp., Merck and Microsoft Corp. -- plan to post results today, one of the busiest days of the season for Dow reporters. But options on Caterpillar and Merck showed the most notable gains in expected stock volatility.
