 GM's Mexican houses on shaky ground   By Blumenstein, Rebecca; Solis, Dianne, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 20, 1997  General Motors Corp is helping thousands of its workers in Ciudad Juarez Mexico buy their first homes, complete with indoor plumbing and electricity. But what began as an effort at good corporate citizenship seems to have drawn controversy. Critics charge that GM should be raising wages for its Mexican workers.
