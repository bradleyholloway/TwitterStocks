 Virgin's Air Plan Draws More Fire   By Carrns, Ann, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 2005  Continental Airlines asked the DOT last week to require more information from Virgin America about its ownership. Continental's filing said that U.S. air carriers must be under "actual control" of U.S. citizens, but that Virgin America "has not submitted adequate information to determine whether this requirement is met."
