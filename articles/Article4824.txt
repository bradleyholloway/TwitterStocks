 Labor Letter: No So Generous   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1991  According to the Bureau of National Affairs, because Christmas and New Year's are on Wednesdays in 1991, only 58% of 545 employers polled will give three or more paid holidays, down from 70% a year ago. Christmas and New Year's eves will be days off for only 47% and 22%, respectively.
