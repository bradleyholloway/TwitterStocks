 Help-Wanted Advertising Fell Sharply in December   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1988  Help-wanted advertising fell sharply in December, further confirming indications that economic growth has begun to slow.  The Conference Board said its help-wanted advertising index dropped to 155 in December, seven points below the revised November level of 162. The December reading was 17 points above the December 1986 mark of 138.
