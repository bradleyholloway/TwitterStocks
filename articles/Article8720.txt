 Congoleum Corp.: Weakness in market causes loss for the first quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 1996  Congoleum Corp reported a 1996 first-quarter loss of $1 million, citing a weak market for its vinyl floor products. The loss compared with year-earlier earnings of $2.5 million. First-quarter sales were $54.1 million, down 14% from $63.2 million in 1995.
