 U.S. Clears Phillips Petroleum of Trying to Mislead Inquiry into Fatal Explosion   By Sullivan, Allanna; Solomon, Caleb, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 20, 1992  A six-month inquiry by the Labor Department's Office of Inspector General cleared Phillips Petroleum Co of allegations that it urged its employees to withhold information from federal investigators about causes of a 1989 chemical plant explosion that killed 23 people.
