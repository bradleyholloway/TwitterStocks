 This Government Mandate Has Support of Truckers   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 2006  "Diesel Blues" for the entire trucking industry go beyond whether an adequate supply of diesel fuel will be available in all parts of the country. In addition to the potential supply disruptions during the transition, ULSD ultimately will cost more to refine and distribute than today's diesel fuel. It also will have a slightly negative impact on fuel economy as a result of lower energy content. It is true that the transition to ULSD will be expensive; however, clean air is not free.
