 Winter Olympics 1992: Scalps in the Alps, Izdag's Zigzags and other Tales of the 5-Ring Circus   By Thurow, Roger; Forman, Craig, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 1992  With the 1992 Winter Olympics ending in Albertville France on Feb 23, 1992, several unconventional ways of judging Olympic performance are discussed. One way is to count the number of athletes that fell during competition and another is to check the increased prices in the surrounding towns.
