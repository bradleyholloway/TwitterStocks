 Stocks End Mixed in Tokyo, Advance in London and Rise to a New High for Year in Frankfurt   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 1991  Tokyo stocks ended mixed on Jun 11, 1991, supported by bargain-hunting from investment trust funds, as the Nikkei index of 225 issues rose 64.21 to close at 24662.59. In London, prices advanced to finish just below the day's best levels and the Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-share index gained 30.7 points to 2542.6. In Frankfurt, the DAX index increased 10.88 points, or 0.6%, to 1715.80, up 25.6% from the start of 1991 and down just 4.7% from a year earlier.
