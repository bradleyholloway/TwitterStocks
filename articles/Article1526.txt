 Travel (A Special Report): U.S. --- Trip Planner: Cusco   By Raub, Kevin, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 2010  Perched at nearly 11,000 feet in Peru's Andes, Cusco is the former capital of the lost Incan empire, and today's starting point for treks to the legendary citadel Machu Picchu. Backpackers and tour groups from around the world pass through Cusco's steep thoroughfares lined with massive Incan walls.
