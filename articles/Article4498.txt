 Air One Gives Option For Up to 90% Stake To Obtain Financing   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1985  ST. LOUIS -- Air One said it agreed to help Zenith International Airlines begin service and gave Zenith the option to buy as much as 90% of Air One common stock in exchange for much-needed financing.  In exchange, Air One will get as much as $1 million. Air One said following approval of its reorganization plan, Zenith could convert the payments made to Air One into Air One stock. Air One said Zenith can buy the 90% stake on or before Feb. 22. The company said further details of its reorganization plans will be presented to the court soon.
