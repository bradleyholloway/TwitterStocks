 Universal Music to Test Lifting Online-Sales Copy Protection   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 2007  The tracks will be available for purchase on the recording artists' Web sites and through several established online music retailers. They will be playable on devices that are compatible with the MP3 format, including Apple Inc.'s iPods.  The test by Vivendi SA's Universal Music Group, the world's largest recording company, suggests the rest of the major recording companies may follow.  Among the online retailers that will be selling the tracks are Amazon.com Inc., Google Inc., Wal-Mart Stores Inc., Best Buy Co., RealNetworks Inc.'s Rhapsody, Trans World Entertainment Corp., PassAlong Networks and Puretracks Inc., Universal said.
