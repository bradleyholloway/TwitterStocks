 Countrywide Financial Corp.: Net Income Declines by 47% As Mortgage Refinancings Fall   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 21, 2004  Residential mortgage lender Countrywide Financial Corp.'s third- quarter profit dropped 47% after a slowdown in refinancings. Countrywide reported net income of $582.2 million, or 94 cents a share, compared with $1.1 billion, or $1.93 a share, in the year- earlier period.
