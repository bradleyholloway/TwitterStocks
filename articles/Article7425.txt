 Hudson Financial Seeks to Raise Stake In Hubco Inc. to 28%   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 1985  Hubco is the Union City, N.J.-based bank holding company for Hudson United Bank. Hudson Financial is a limited partnership that currently owns about 11.2% of Hubco, or 194,248 shares. Hudson Financial's partner is Sheldon S. Goldstein, a New York businessman. Mr. Goldstein doesn't own any Hubco shares currently but has agreed to acquire half of Hudson Financial's current stake, Hudson Financial said.
