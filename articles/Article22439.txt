 OrthoLogic Agrees To Pay U.S. $1 Million In Lawsuit Settlement   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 1998  OrthoLogic, which denies any wrongdoing or liability alleged in the complaint, said it settled to avoid the expense, distraction and "uncertainty of litigation" in the case. In return, OrthoLogic said, the Justice Department released company employees, directors and officers from any civil damages or penalties relating to allegations in the lawsuit.
