 Warner-Lambert Joins Glaxo in Pact to Sell Nonprescription Drugs   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 1993  MORRIS PLAINS, N.J. -- Warner-Lambert Co. entered a joint venture with Glaxo Holdings PLC to develop and sell nonprescription versions of Glaxo prescription drugs, including the Zantac ulcer treatment.
