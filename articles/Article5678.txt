 When in Rome, please be polite: Keep your phones off the table   By Racanelli, Vito J, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1994  Cellular phones are getting out of hand socially in Italy as the popular portable phones become prevalent in elevator rides, Sunday walks in the park, luncheon dates and bus trips. Telecom Italia has published a Courtesy Guide for cellular phone users to help prevent resentment from others.
