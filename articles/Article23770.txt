 Golf Journal: A Course Grows in the Bronx --- The Nicklaus-Designed Ferry Point at Last Nears Reality; Majors? 'Absolutely,' Says Jack   By Newport, John Paul, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 2009  In the late 1960s, Ferry Point was briefly considered as the site for a new Yankee Stadium, but essentially it sat dormant until 1998, when the Giuliani administration, with the long-standing support of some in the local community, signed a 35-year lease with a private group called Ferry Point Partners to supervise capping the landfill in accordance with environmental regulations and to develop and run a golf course.
