 TeleQuest's Chairman, Marcheschi, Is Named To 2 Additional Posts   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1987  TeleQuest Inc. said Henry Marcheschi, chairman, will assume the additional posts of president and chief executive officer.  Mr. Marcheschi, 56 years old, succeeds Robert E. Lee, who becomes executive vice president, engineering and manufacturing. Mr. Lee, 46, held that same position prior to being named president and chief executive in 1985.
