 Inventories Rose 0.2% in November, U.S. Data Show   By Harper, Lucinda, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1992  The Commerce Department reported that business inventories climbed 0.2% in Nov 1991, while sales edged up 0.1% after a 0.4% gain in October. The ratio of inventories to sales remained at 1.50 in November.
