 FRIDAY JOURNAL --- Television -- Sightings: In the Footsteps of Alan Lomax: The Artists Behind the Music   By Teachout, Terry, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 2012  "Hoedown" is a high-stepping orchestral fantasy based on "Bonaparte's Retreat," a 19th-century fiddle tune that Mr. Copland ran across in "Our Singing Country," a 1941 book co-edited by Alan Lomax, the celebrated folk-song collector.
