 Health Brief -- Wyeth: Unit Is Fined $54.1 Million In Patent-Infringement Suit   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 2002  A federal judge in Denver issued a $54.1 million judgment, plus interest, against Wyeth's American Cyanamid Co. unit for fraud, unjust enrichment and exemplary damages in a patent-infringement lawsuit brought by the University of Colorado Foundation Inc. and others.
