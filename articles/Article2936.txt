The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2001  BRIEFS: The average cost to produce a national TV ad in 2000 dropped 3% to $332,000, according to the American Association of Advertising Agencies' annual production survey. Lower production-company costs and lower director fees fueled the drop. It is only the third decrease in the survey's 14-year history. . . . Dockers Europe, a unit of Levi Strauss, has appointed Amsterdam-based marketing agency BlueberryFrog to create a pan-European campaign.
