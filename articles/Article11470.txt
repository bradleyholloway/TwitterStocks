 In Wake of Gulf War, New Military Fight Looms over Use of Reservists and Women in Combat   By Pasztor, Andy, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 1991  A major fight has begun brewing over the use of reservists and women soldiers in future combat situations since the Persian Gulf War. The prospect of continued military spending cuts has sharpened the debate, since a leaner staff means that women and reservists could be called on more often.
