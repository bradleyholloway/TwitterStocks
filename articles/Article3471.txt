 Business Brief -- Medtronic Inc.: Net for Fiscal First Quarter Swelled 6% to $301.5 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 2001  Medtronic Inc. said fiscal first-quarter net income rose to $301.5 million, or 25 cents a diluted share, up 6% from $284.1 million, or 23 cents a share, the prior year.
