 Business Brief -- Racal Electronics PLC: Unit Agrees to Buy Division Of Thorn EMI's Subsidiary   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 1995  Racal Electronics PLC's unit Racal Defence Radar Ltd. said in London that it agreed to buy the Sensors division of Thorn EMI PLC's Thorn EMI Electronics Ltd. unit for #17.5 million ($28.1 million).
