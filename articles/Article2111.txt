 Ames, hit by slow sales over holiday, to close 17 stores, lay off over 1,000   By Johannes, Laura, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1996  Ames Department Stores Inc said it would close 17 of its 307 stores and lay off more than 1,000 workers. The announcement comes after slower-than-expected holiday sales and amid stiff competition.
