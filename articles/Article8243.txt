 Journal Reporters Are Given Awards in Major Contests   The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 1992  Wall Street Journal reporter Alan Murray received a 1992 Gerald Loeb Award for Distinguished Business & Financial Journalism. Peter Gumbel, the Journal's Paris bureau chief, received the Hal Boyd Award from the Overseas Press Club of America, which also gave citations to Journal reporters Geraldine Brooks and Tony Horwitz.
