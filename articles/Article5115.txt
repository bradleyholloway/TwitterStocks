 FPL Group to slash dividend by 32%, plans repurchase to boost share price   By de Lisser, Eleena, The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 1994  In a highly unusual move for a healthy company, FPL Group Inc said it will cut its quarterly dividend 32% to $.42 a share from $.62 a share, payable June 15, 1994 to holders of record May 27. In addition, FPL said it would buy back 10 million common shares outstanding over the next three years.
