 Corrections & amplifications - (01)   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 1997  Presstek Inc said its chairman, Robert Howard, and president, Robert Verrando, reached "agreements in principle" with the staff of the SEC in connection with an SEC investigation of Presstek stock activity. An Aug 20, 1997 Wall Street Journal article inaccurately said Presstek disclosed that the executives would sign "cease and desist" orders agreeing to abide by securities laws in the future.
