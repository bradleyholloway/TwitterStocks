 Newell Sees Earnings Below Expectations For Fourth Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 11, 1998  The consumer-products concern said earnings would be between 56 cents and 61 cents a share, compared with analysts' consensus estimate of 67 cents. In the fourth quarter of 1997, Newell had earnings of $90.6 million, or 56 cents a share, on sales of $914.1 million.
