 Municipal-bond industry faces dual challenges: Falling debt issuance and rising SEC scrutiny   By Smith, Randall; Connor, John, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1995  The municipal-bond business is facing the double whammy of a sharp drop-off in new-issue volume and increased scrutiny by the SEC. On Feb 3, 1995, unemployment news spurred a rally in the benchmark 30-year Treasury bond, which climbed 1 1/4 points to 98 13/32, dropping the yield to nearly 7.62% from nearly 7.74% the previous day.
