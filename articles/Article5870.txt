 Business Brief -- EGAIN COMMUNICATIONS CORP.: Investors Bring $88.5 Million Through Private Placement   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 2000  EGain Communications Corp. said it raised $88.5 million through a private placement from investors that include its president Gunjan Sinha and Oak Hill Capital Partners LP to help it reach profitability.The Sunnyvale, Calif., provider of Internet customer-service software said it expects to become profitable in the second half of 2002.
