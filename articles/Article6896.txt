 Office Depot Inc.: Robust North American Sales Help Chalk Up 15% Net Rise   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 11, 2005  Office Depot Inc.'s fourth-quarter net income rose 15% amid healthy sales gains at its North American businesses. The Delray Beach, Fla., office-supplies retailer reported net income of $52.8 million, or 17 cents a share, up from $45.8 million, or 15 cents a share, a year earlier.
