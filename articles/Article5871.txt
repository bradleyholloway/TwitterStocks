 Horizon-CMS says no charges result from probe of unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1996  Horizon/CMS Healthcare Corp said federal prosecutors notified the company that they are taking no action, following a nearly two-year probe of a unit the company acquired in 1995. The nursing-home chain said that US attorneys' offices in Harrisburg PA and Sacramento have closed their investigation of the unit, formerly known as Continental Medical Systems Inc.
