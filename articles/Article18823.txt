 Water   By By Michael Totty, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 1996  The 1996 drought, which has hammered farmers and ranchers in much of the state, is also beginning to drain the water supplies for many Texas cities. More than 200 public water systems have been placed on the Texas Natural Resource Conservation Commission's watch list of systems where shortages are possible, and a handful of smaller communities face imminent shortages.
