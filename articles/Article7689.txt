 Ceradyne Announces Its President, Carberry, 'Has Been Separated'   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1987  COSTA MESA, Calif. -- Ceradyne Inc. said John Carberry, president and chief operating officer, "has been separated" from the company, and was succeeded on an interim basis by the company's chairman and chief executive officer, Joel Moskowitz.
