 Business Brief: Cleveland-Cliffs Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 1988  The Wisconsin Electric Power Co. unit of Wisconsin Energy Corp., Milwaukee, said it completed the purchase of a coal-fired power plant in Marquette, Mich., from Cleveland-Cliffs Inc. and Upper Peninsula Power Co., Houghton, Mich., for about $283.4 million.
