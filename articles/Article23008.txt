 Small Talk: Inventories and Outlays   By By Richard Breeden, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 2003  The net percentages -- companies planning increases minus those planning decreases -- over the past 17 months suggest that an inventory buildup should get under way as the economy strengthens, according to William Dunkelberg, chief economist of the NFIB. During April, plans for a buildup weakened with construction companies and retail firms, but were stronger in manufacturing, transportation and agriculture.
