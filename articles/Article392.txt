Mr. and Mrs. Paul B. McKeever of New Canaan, Conn., have announced the engagement of their daughter, Jane A. McKeever, to Kenneth Drury Toumey, son of Mr. and Mrs. Hugh J. Toumey, also of New Canaan. An August wedding is planned.  Miss McKeever, who is with the law firm of Badger, Fisher, Cohen & Barnett in Stamford, Conn., was graduated from Marymount College of Virginia. Her father is the manager of the corporate accounting systems group of the Union Carbide Corporation in Danbury, Conn.  Mr. Toumey, a research associate in the Stamford office of Yankolovich, Skelly & White, a marketing and social researc h firm, was graduated from the Fairfield Preparatory School and Wesleyan University.  Mr, Tourmey's father recently retired as chairman and chief executive officer of Cannon Mills Inc. He is a director of the Cannon Mills Company and chairman of the board of trustees of the College of Mount St. Vincent in Riverdale, the Bronx.
