 SEC Affirms Sanctions Against 5 Ex-Officials Of Blinder Robinson   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 13, 1992  The NASD, the self-regulatory body of the over-the-counter securities market, said the officials are Meyer Blinder, who was president; John J. Cox, vice president, compliance; Steven B. Theys, executive vice president; Harold W. Gorden, vice president, trading; and Anthony J. Beshara, chief trader. Blinder Robinson was a former nationwide broker-dealer with its main office in Englewood, Colo.
