 ITT may sell most of its hotels in fighting Hilton takeover bid   By Binkley, Christina, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 1997  ITT Corp's most effective strategy to fend off Hilton Hotels Corp's takeover bid may be to turn itself into primarily a hotel-management company. By considering the sale of its most prized hotels, and by taking over the managment of many new hotels it doesn't own, New York-based ITT could end up a shadow of its former self.
