Dr. Dong Sun Chu, a specialist in rehabilitation medicine for 23 years at the New York University Medical Center, died Tuesday at White Plains Hospital after suffering a cerebral hemmorhage on Nov. 9 at his home in Scarsdale, N.Y. He was 63 years old.  Dr. Chu was born in South Korea and received two medical degrees from Seoul National University. After serving as chief resident in neurosurgery at University Hospital in Seoul, he came to the United States in 1965 and joined the staff at the Howard A.
