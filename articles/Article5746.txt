 Wheat prices soar on reduced crop expectations   By McGee, Suzanne, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 1995  Wheat prices soared on the CBOT on Jul 11, 1995 on the expectation the government will sharply cut the size of its projections for the 1995 crop. December wheat surged $.095 to $4.360 a bushel. In other commodity markets, cotton prices declined and copper prices surged.
