Two people were killed and a third person was critically injured in the crash of a small plane in fog, rain and high winds near Westchester County Airport late Wednesday, officials said. The survivor was found in the wreckage seven hours after the crash.  The plane, a single-engine Piper Cherokee 6, went down at 11:35 P.M. on Great Island, a heavily wooded area about one mile north of the airport, said Leut.
