 Small stock focus: Stocks tick up, helpd by talk about mergers   By O Brien, Robert, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 1995  Small stocks ticked up slightly in selective buying on Jan 9, 1995, with merger talk in bank and computer issues helping to forge the modest advance. The Russell 2000 Index edged up 0.29 to 248.37. The Nasdaq Composite Index advanced 2.40 to 752.09.
