 Lloyd's of London Predicts Rate Rise in Event of War   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 11, 1991  Lloyd's of London predicted sharp increases in shipping insurance rates if war is started in the Mideast.  Stephen Merrett, chairman of the big insurance market's underwriters' association, declined to predict levels insurance rates might reach. But he said, "If there's substantial damage for shipping, rates would rise substantially."
