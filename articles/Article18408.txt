 Head protection rules for autos revised by U.S.   By Ingersoll, Bruce; Lavin, Douglas, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1993  The NHTSA proposed a complex package of rules to better protect drivers and passengers in all cars, light trucks, vans and other passenger vehicles from head injuries. The proposal, which could require a costly redesign of vehicles, could save an estimated 1,400 lives a year.
