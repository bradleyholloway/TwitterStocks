 Eurodollar Bonds Post Modest Increase During Quiet Session --- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 2, 1985  Eurodollar bond prices ended about 1/4 point to 3/8 point higher in a quiet market, after being marked up as much as one point yesterday morning in the wake of Wednesday's U.S. Treasury rally.  Among new issues, Comcast Corp., a U.S. operator of cable television systems, launched $50 million of convertible Eurobonds that found favor with investors.
