 Apple to report strong earnings for its 4th period   By Clark, Don, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1994  PC maker Apple Computer said it expects to report earnings slightly greater that $.90 a share for the fiscal fourth quarter ended Sep 30, 1994, compared with analysts' estimates ranging from $.65 to $.70. Revenue was about $2.5 billion, more than 5% higher than expected.
