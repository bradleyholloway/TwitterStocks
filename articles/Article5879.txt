 World wire: Brazil prioritizes public works   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1996  Brazil's government gave priority status to 42 of 1,500 recently announced projects to improve highways, ports, waterways, electricity and natural-gas distribution, and other public works. Under a four-year plan, spending on the projects in the next two years will total 54.35 billion reals ($53.26 billion).
