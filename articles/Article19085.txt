 Business Brief -- SNAP-ON INC.: Restructuring Begun to Close More Than 25% of Facilities   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 1998  Snap-On Inc, Kenosha WI, warning that third-quarter net income will be lower than a year earlier's, said it began implementing a restructuring announced in Jun 1998 to boost sluggish easrnings. The tool manufacturer said it is closing more than a quarter of its world-wide facilities and has increased its number of planned layoffs, among other things.
