 Laptop Outlets in Coach   By Flandez, Raymund, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 2004  Check your flight itinerary online to find out what plane you'll be flying. Then go to SeatGuru.com, which shows the location of the power ports for about 175 aircraft and more than 20 airlines. A black dot on the seating map indicates a power port.
