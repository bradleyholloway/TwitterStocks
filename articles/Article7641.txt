 Canadian Pacific Posts First-Quarter Profit After Year-Ago Loss   The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 1987  In a major turnaround, Canadian Pacific Ltd. reported first-quarter profit of $318.7 million (Canadian), or $1.06 a share, including $193.3 million in special gains.  Without the gains, operating profit in the quarter totaled $125.4 million, or 42 cents a share. The special gains included $172.5 million from the previously reported sale of Canadian Pacific Air Lines Ltd. and $20.8 million from the sale of a building in London by its CP Ships unit.
