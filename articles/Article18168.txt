 CrossLand Had Wider Deficit in 4th Quarter   By Castro, Laura L, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1991  CrossLand Savings FSB reported much wider fourth-quarter and full-year losses after making additions to its loan-loss reserves and increasing real estate write-downs. The company had a fourth-quarter loss of $169.3 million compared with a year-earlier loss of 31.7 million.
