 Business Brief -- MNC Financial Inc.: GE Capital to Purchase Equipment Leasing Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 1990  MNC Financial Inc. said it reached a definitive agreement to sell the equipment leasing division of MNC Credit Corp. to GE Capital Corp. before the end of the year.  MNC officials of the Baltimore regional banking concern declined to indicate the price. A GE Capital spokeswoman placed the value of the assets at $350 million. GE Capital is unit of General Electric Co., Stamford, Conn.
