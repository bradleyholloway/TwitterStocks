 Legal beat: Distribution of Texaco payments to blacks is delayed by tax issue   By Sullivan, Allanna, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 11, 1997  Texaco Inc's black employees have yet to receive $115 in lump-sum payments promised because of confusion over how to tax the distributions. Texaco agreed to the payments in Nov 1996 as part of a record $176 million settlement of a race-discrimination lawsuit brought by the black employees.
