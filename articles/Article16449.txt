 Industrial Brief -- Honeywell International Inc.: Almost $2.2 Million to Be Paid To Settle Discrimination Suit   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 2004  Honeywell International Inc. agreed to pay close to $2.2 million to more than two-dozen employees to settle allegations of age discrimination. Six of the workers who claimed they were fired or demoted because of their age will receive awards ranging from $275,000 to $475,000.
