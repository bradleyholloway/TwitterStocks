 Program Trading   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 2000  Program trading in the week ended Jan. 7 accounted for 19.1%, or an average 204.2 million daily shares, of New York Stock Exchange volume.  Brokerage firms executed an additional 78.1 million daily shares of program trading away from the NYSE, mostly on foreign markets. Program trading is the simultaneous purchase or sale of at least 15 different stocks with a total value of $1 million or more.
