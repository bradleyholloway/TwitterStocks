 Sina.com Ouster Puts Structure Under Spotlight   By By Leslie Chang, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 2001  Sina announced late Sunday from its Sunnyvale, Calif., headquarters that Wang Zhidong, its CEO and president, is leaving the company "to pursue other interests," although he would continue to hold the nonemployee post of chief adviser. Taking Mr. Wang's CEO spot and his seat on the company's board will be Daniel Mao, currently Sina's chief operating officer. The company's new president will be Wang Yan, general manager of China operations. (Mr. Wang is unrelated to the founder.)
