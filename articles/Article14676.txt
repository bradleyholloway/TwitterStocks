 Business Brief -- Research Inc.: Computer Firm to Post Net For Fiscal Second Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 1990  AST Research Inc., Irvine, Calif., said it expects to report profit of more than $7 million for its second quarter, compared with a year-earlier $8.9 million net loss, marking what it called a "signficant breakthrough" in earnings and customer acceptance.
