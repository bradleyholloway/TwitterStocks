 Business Brief -- LIBERTY FINANCIAL COS.: Accord Is Reached to Buy Crabbe Huson Group Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 1998  Liberty Financial Cos, Boston, said it agreed to acquire Crabbe Huson Group Inc, Portland OR, for as much as $147.5 million. Liberty said Crabbe Huson, an investment firm, has about $5.3 billion in assets under management.
