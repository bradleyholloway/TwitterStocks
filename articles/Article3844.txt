 Bausch & Lomb Inc.: Third-Period Net Leapt 34% Amid a Solid Increase in Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 21, 2004  Bausch & Lomb Inc., propelled by a sharp rise in sales of contact lenses, eye-care drugs and vision-correction surgical products, posted a 34% jump in third-quarter profit. The Rochester, N.Y., eye-care- products maker reported net income of $43.3 million, or 79 cents a share, up from $32.3 million, or 60 cents a share, a year earlier.
