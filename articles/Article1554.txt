 Calpers Set to Bankroll Developers   By White, James A, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1992  The California Public Employees Retirement System, known as Calpers, with holdings of $67 billion in state pension funds, announced plans to invest $225 million in an attempt to invigorate California's stalled home-building business. Calpers will provide construction loans to California home builders and the organization expects to profit by charging market interest rates on the loans. Calpers will share in any profits when the finished properties are sold.
