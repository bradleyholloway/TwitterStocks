 Enel SpA: Russian Stake Acquired In $1.52 Billion Deal   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 7, 2007  Enel SpA, which is bidding to take over Spanish utility Endesa SA, beat out three rivals to buy a 25.03% stake in Russian electricity generator OAO OGK-5 for 39.2 billion rubles ($1.52 billion).
