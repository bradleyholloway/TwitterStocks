 Taft Broadcasting Co. Sells a Theme Park For About $65 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 1986  The park was bought by a newly formed Canadian company, Wonderland Ltd., which is 80%-owned by Kings Entertainment Co. and 20% by Taft.
