 Moderate price gains in the bond market push 30-year's yield to lowest point since early '94   By Creswell, Julie; Gutscher, Cecile, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 13, 1995  Buoyed by good news on the inflation front, bonds posted moderate price gains on Oct 12, 1995, driving the 30-year bond yield down to its lowest point since Feb 1994. The benchmark 30-year Treasury bond was up more than 1/2 point at 106 9/32. Its yield fell to 6.39% from 6.43% on Oct 11.
