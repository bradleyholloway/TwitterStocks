 Global view: Europe struggles with the burdens of old age   By Melloan, George, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1994  George Melloan discusses the economic situations in Belgium and Italy in an article on the logistics of privatizing pensions. The point is made that however distasteful discussions of this sort may be, the politics of pensions will be overridden by economics in the not too distant future and governments would be wise to heed the warning.
