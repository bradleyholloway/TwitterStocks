 Law: In a Bid to Trim Costs, Many Companies Are Forcing Law Firms to Reduce Fees   By Pollock, Ellen Joan, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1991  General Motors Corp, Irvine Co and Motorola Inc are leading a movement to force law firms to reduce their prices, sometimes by as much as 20%. The effort represents a revolutionary shift in the balance of power between big-time legal firms and their clients and is the result both of increased competition in the legal industry and of cost-cutting efforts by clients.
