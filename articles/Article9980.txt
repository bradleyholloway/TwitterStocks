 Nasdaq and Jasdaq Look at Closer Ties   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 2006  The discussions between the Jasdaq, one of Japan's three markets focusing on start-up companies, and New York-based Nasdaq have been taking place amid dramatic consolidation in the world's exchanges. Last month, Chicago Mercantile Exchange Holdings Inc. and Chicago Board of Trade parent CBOT Holdings Inc. announced an $8 billion merger, creating a commodities titan and the world's biggest financial market.
