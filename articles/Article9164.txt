 Marketing & Media Brief -- LISTEN.COM INC.: Five Big Record Concerns Have Invested in Company   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 2000  Listen.com Inc, which provides a free online directory and reviews of MP3 and other digital-audio files, said it received investments from the five big record companies, which include the BMG Entertainment unit of Germany's Bertelsmann AG, EMI Group PLC, of the UK, the Universal Music Group of Seagram Co of Montreal, and the Warner Music Group unit of Time Warner Inc of New York.
