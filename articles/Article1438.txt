 U.S. News: Coast Guard Exercise Stirs Alarm in Capital   By Simpson, Cam, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 2009  A routine U.S. Coast Guard training mission along a stretch of the Potomac River near the Pentagon on the anniversary of the Sept. 11 terrorist attacks sparked a security scare Friday after cable TV news mistakenly reported gunshots from one of the speedboats in the exercise.
