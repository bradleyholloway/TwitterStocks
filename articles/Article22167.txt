 Truckers' boycott at Los Angeles port loses steam despite rally at city hall   By Rose, Frederick, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1996  A month-old boycott by truckers serving Los Angeles harbor--a strike that has had little effect recently on operations at the nation's largest port--sprang to public attention again with a raucous rally at Los Angeles City Hall on May 31, 1996, but it is likely to have provided only moral support.
