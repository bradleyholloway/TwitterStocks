 Dollar Rises on Treasury's Comments, Speculation on Lower Japanese Rates   By Choi, Audrey, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1991  The dollar strengthened on Feb 13, 1990 as the US Treasury's comments defending it encouraged traders. In New York, the dollar was quoted at 1.4634 marks, up from 1.4545 the day before. The dollar was changing hands at 129.60 yen, up from 128.72 yen the previous day.
