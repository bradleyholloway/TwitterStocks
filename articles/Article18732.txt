 Phar-Mor Inc. reaches a settlement of claim against Coopers & Lybrand   By Murray, Matt, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1995  Phar-Mor Inc settled its claim against its former accountants, Coopers & Lybrand LLP, ending its role in a lawsuit in federal court that accuses Coopers of negligence in the 1992 Phar-Mor fraud and embezzlement scandal. Terms of the settlement were not disclosed.
