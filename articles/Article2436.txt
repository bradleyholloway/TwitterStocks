 MagneTek Inc.: Loss of $40.5 million posted for firm's fiscal 2nd period   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 21, 1993  Electrical equipment maker MagneTek Inc reported a second-quarter net loss of $40.5 million, compared to net income of $3.8 million in the year-earlier fiscal quarter ended Dec 31. The loss reflected charges related to accounting changes for retiree medical benefits and income taxes.
