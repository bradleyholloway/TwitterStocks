 Business Brief -- Canfor Corp.: Company Agrees to Acquire Balfour Forest Products Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 1989  Canfor Corp. said it agreed to purchase all the shares outstanding of closely held Balfour Forest Products Inc. for 93.8 million Canadian dollars (US$78.8 million), part of which may be paid by issuing as many as one million Canfor common shares at Balfour's option.
