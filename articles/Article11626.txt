 OTC Focus: Decline in Tech Stocks, Gloomy Data on Economy Pull Index Down 0.77%   By Elliott, Christopher, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 6, 1992  On Aug 5, 1992 OTC stocks declined, with the Nasdaq Composite Index falling 4.45 points, or 0.77%, to 576.87. Following an analyst's downgrade of IBM, OTC markets reacted by witnessing a sell-off in a number of technology issues.
