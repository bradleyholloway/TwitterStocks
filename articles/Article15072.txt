 Linotype-Hell AG: Company to resume paying shareholder dividend for '92   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 1993  After omitting a dividend in 1991, Linotype-Hell AG, a German printing machinery manufacturer, said it will resume paying a shareholder dividend of 8 marks ($5.03) a share for 1992. The company also said its first-quarter orders had fallen "below expectations."
