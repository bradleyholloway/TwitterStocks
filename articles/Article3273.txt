 Automotive Brief -- Nissan Motor Co.: Accounting Change at Venture In China to Increase Earnings   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 2004  Nissan Motor Co. will fully include half of the earnings of a 50-50 Chinese car joint venture in its consolidated results for the latest fiscal year. The Japanese auto maker expects the move to boost its earnings, as it expects rapid growth in the operations of the venture, Dongfeng Motor Co.
