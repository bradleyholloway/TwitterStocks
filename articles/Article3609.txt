 Investors Added $19.4 Billion To Money Funds in Past Week   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 2006  The average seven-day simple yield for tax-free money-market funds fell to 2.82% from 2.93%, and the seven-day compound yield fell to 2.86% from 2.97%. The 30-day simple yield fell to 2.93% from 2.94%, and the 30-day compound yield slumped to 2.98% from 2.99%.  The highest seven-day compound yield among retail tax-free money- market funds was Alpine Municipal MMF/Investor at 3.37%, followed by Vanguard Tax-Exempt MMF at 3.34% and by Vanguard PA Tax-Exempt MMF at 3.32%.
