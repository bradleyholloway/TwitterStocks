 World News: China Responds to Fresh Protests in Urumqi --- Beijing Tightens Security After Han-Uighur Tension Flares Again Following Riots in July That Killed Nearly 200   By Fairclough, Gordon, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 2009  SHANGHAI - Authorities stepped up already heavy security in Urumqi -- the northwestern Chinese city torn by deadly ethnic riots in July -- after thousands of protesters took to the streets Thursday criticizing the government for failing to protect people after a recent spate of attacks by assailants wielding syringes.
