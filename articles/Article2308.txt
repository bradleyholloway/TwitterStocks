 Fleet, acquisition-hungry again, may change its diet   By Hirsch, James S, The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 1997  Although Fleet Financial Group has focused on operating problems for the past two years, it is turning its eye toward acquisitions again in 1997. Unlike the acquisitions of banks that transformed it into a New England powerhouse, Fleet is now deeply interested in nonbank businesses, such as brokerage firms, money-management concerns and insurance agencies.
