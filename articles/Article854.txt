 Shamrock Holdings Says It May Make Bid For Holly Sugar Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1987  Shamrock Holdings of California Inc. said a Holly Sugar Corp. management group's proposal to take the sugar producer private "appears inadequate" and that Shamrock might make its own proposal to acquire the company.  In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, Shamrock, a Burbank, Calif.-based investment firm, said it sent a letter to Holly Sugar asking for access to information it believes is necessary to determine whether it should make a competing proposal.
