 Inman may have problems resembling Zoe Baird's   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 1993  The White House disclosed that retired Admiral Bobby Inman, President Clinton's choice for defense secretary, may have a "Zoe Baird" problem. However, Inman sent a $6,000 check to the IRS for taxes owed on work performed by a part-time housekeeper, and the issue isn't expected to block his nomination.
