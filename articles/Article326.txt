The dollar ended mostly lower yesterday but steadied against the yen for the first time since its frenetic slide began more than a week ago, helped by rumors of central bank sales of the Japanese currency.  Although the dollar still finished with a slight loss against the yen for the day, traders were buying dollars as the week drew to a close, a sign that some believe the dollar may be stabilizing.
