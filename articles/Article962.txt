 Boeing Unit Finds Buyers for 4 Jets, Has Orders for 3 New Ones   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 13, 1987  The airliner unit of the aerospace concern said the West German carrier, Germania, ordered three 737-300s that were among four to have been delivered to AirCal Inc., which was acquired earlier this year by the American unit of Dallas-based AMR Corp. Germania operates three 737-300s, and will take delivery of the new planes next November and December, the Boeing unit said.
