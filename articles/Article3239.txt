 UPS Says It Plans Stock-Market Debut In Early November   By By Douglas A. Blackmon, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1999  United Parcel Service of America Inc. hopes to launch its $3 billion initial public offering in early November, according to the delivery concern's filings with the Securities and Exchange Commission.  The Atlanta company filed a tentative proxy and revised registration statement with the SEC Friday, outlining a possible schedule for the IPO. If approved, UPS will hold a special shareholder meeting in October to approve steps necessary for the public offering and then begin a two-week roadshow of meetings with large institutional investors.
