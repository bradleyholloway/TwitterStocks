 RTZ Corp.: U.K. Mining Firm's Profit Increased in First Half of '92   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 11, 1992  The UK's RTZ Corp reported after-tax profit in the first half of 1992 rose 2.6% to 158 million British pounds ($311.7 million) from the previous year. The mining company attributed the gain to tighter cost controls and new projects offsetting lower metal prices.
