 Democrat Leaders Say Trade Bill To Pass This Year   By By Monica Langley, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 1987  But many other lawmakers acknowledge that time is running out for Congress to complete work on a major trade bill this year. Sen. Bob Packwood of Oregon, the ranking Republican on the Senate Finance Committee, predicted earlier this week that, "There won't be a major trade bill passed this year." And Rep. Sam Gibbons (D., Fla.), chairman of the House Ways and Means trade subcommittee, yesterday said, "There's a good chance we'll never get agreement on this monster."
