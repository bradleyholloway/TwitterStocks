 Home & Family -- Cheapskate: Where Is the Love? A Valentine's Lesson   By Templin, Neal, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 2009  The average American husband plans to spend $118.30 on a Valentine's Day gift this year, according to a Discover Card survey. The most popular gift from husbands: a special dinner, followed by flowers, candy, a night on the town, and clothing or lingerie.
