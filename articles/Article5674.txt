 Venezuela concern is No. 2 in ranking of top oil firms   By Tanner, James, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1994  State-owned Petroleos de Venezuela SA has overtaken Royal Dutch-Shell Group in the latest ranking of the top 50 oil and gas companies. The company is now the world's second-largest oil company, Petroleum Intelligence Weekly is to report in the Dec 12, 1994 edition. Saudi Arabian Oil Co remains entrenched as No 1.
