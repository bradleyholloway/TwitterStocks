 FBI Issues Alert for Possible Terror Attack, Warns of Yemeni Suspect and Associates   By By Gary Fields, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 2002  Acting on information gained from detainees being held in Cuba and Afghanistan, the Federal Bureau of Investigation issued an unusually specific alert to U.S. law-enforcement agencies and the public regarding a Yemeni man and several associates it believes could be planning terrorist attacks against the U.S. as soon as today.
