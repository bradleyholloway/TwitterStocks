 Jim Crow was a pervasive tyranny   By Eisenstadt, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1994  Peter Eisenstadt, Managing Editor, Encyclopedia of African-American Culture and History, responds to the Sep 19, 1994 Leisure and Arts television review. Eisenstadt says one suspects that the writer is unacquainted with the extent of the formal and informal discrimination against African-Americans denoted by the term "Jim Crow."
