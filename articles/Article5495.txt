 Paris Bourse Clears $180-a-Share Offer By Gehe AG for OCP ---- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 11, 1993  The Societe des Bourses Francaises said that the German pharmaceutical distributor and generic drug maker could proceed with its 985 franc ($180) a share offer for OCP. That competes with an existing offer by Cooperation Pharmaceutique Francaise SA and Bergen Brunswig Corp. at 865 francs a share.
