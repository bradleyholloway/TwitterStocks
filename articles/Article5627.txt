 Kidder trader in bond options is dismissed   By Siconolfi, Michael, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 1994  Kidder, Peabody Group Inc, already reeling from a government-bond trading scandal, quietly fired Peter N.J. Bryant, an options trader in London, who the firm alleges hid losses totaling $6 million on roughly a dozen options trades on French and Spanish government treasury bonds.
