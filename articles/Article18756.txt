 GM is to unveil plans for selling electric car, truck   By Blumenstein, Rebecca; Stern, Gabriella, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1996  According to people close to the situation, General Motors Corp Chairman John F. Smith Jr will announce on Jan 4, 1996 at the Los Angeles Auto Show that GM will begin selling electric passenger cars in Arizona and California--and electric pickup trucks nationally in 1997.
