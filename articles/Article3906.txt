 Corporate News: BT Group to Cut 10,000 Jobs   By Herrero-Martinez, Erica, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 2008  BT Group PLC said it would cut 10,000 jobs, or 6.3% of its global work force, by March in an effort to rein in costs. The U.K. telecommunications company, meanwhile, posted an 18% rise in its fiscal second-quarter profit.
