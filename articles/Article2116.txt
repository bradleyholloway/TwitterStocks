 Stelco Forms Venture With Mitsubishi Unit For Steel Coating Plant   The Wall Street Journal,  May 21, 1990  HAMILTON, Ontario -- Stelco Inc. said it formed a joint venture with Mitsubishi Corp.'s Canadian unit to build a 200 million Canadian dollar (US$170.2 million) steel coating plant.  Robert Milbourne, vice president of manufacturing at Stelco's steel division said Mitsubishi would hold a 40% equity interest in the operation, known as the Z-Line. Stelco will hold the remaining 60% equity stake.
