 Skip the sequins: Now auto-show models talk torque   By Blumenstein, Rebecca, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1996  Many professional models who travel the nationwide auto-show circuit are now known as "narrators" and "product specialists." They wear prim tailored suits, and instead of flashing come-hither glances, ply customers with talk of torque and transmissions--in Spanish, if need be. Nowadays, most consumers want detailed information instead of vacuous smiles, and models who demonstrate "product knowledge" and "people skills" are hot commodities.
