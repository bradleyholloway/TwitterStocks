 Business Brief -- DECORA INDUSTRIES INC.: Warrants Are Bought Back From a Textron Inc. Trust   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1999  Decora Industries Inc said it bought back warrants held by a Textron Inc trust for stock and cash valued at about $3 million. Under the transaction, Decora said it acquired and canceled the warrants in exchange for 400,000 Decora shares and $750,000 cash.
