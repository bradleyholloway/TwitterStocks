 GOP Aims To Tame Benefits Programs --- Boehner Promises Entitlement Review   By Bendavid, Naftali; Hook, Janet, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 2011  In an interview with The Wall Street Journal, Mr. Boehner said House Republicans would offer a budget for the next fiscal year that sets goals for bringing the programs' costs under control. A Wall Street Journal/NBC News poll this week showed less than a quarter of Americans support making significant cuts to Social Security or Medicare to tackle the government's financial woes.
