 Business Brief -- Newell Rubbermaid Inc.: Second-Quarter Net Rose 23% As Sales Increased by 10%   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 2, 2002  Newell Rubbermaid Inc.'s second-quarter net income climbed 23% on strong growth across all its businesses. The Freeport, Ill., consumer-products company reported net of $88.6 million, or 33 cents a share, up from $72 million, or 27 cents a share, a year earlier.
