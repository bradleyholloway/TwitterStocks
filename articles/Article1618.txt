 Cross Country: Jerry Brown's Good Deed Gets Punished   By Greenhut, Steven, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 2011  According to the state controller, redevelopment agencies consume about 12% of all state-wide property taxes -- money that would otherwise go to critical public services. Because city governments have become so dependent on these agencies diverting revenue to them from the state, the pushback against the governor's plan has been powerful.
