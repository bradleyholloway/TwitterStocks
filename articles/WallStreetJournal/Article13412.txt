 Earnings Digest -- Food: Brown-Forman Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 2007  Brown-Forman Corp. reported a 15% drop in fiscal fourth-quarter profit but strong growth in its Jack Daniel's and Finlandia brands and a rosy outlook lifted its shares $2.58, or 3.8%, to $70.56 in 4 p.m. New York Stock Exchange trading.
