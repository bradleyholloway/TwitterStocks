 AT&T Stalls Launch Of U-Verse Service   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 2007  Also to prepare for the start-up in the Southeastern region, AT&T raised its estimate for capital spending on U-verse by $500 million for 2007 and 2008, bringing it to between $4.5 billion and $5 billion. Since the additional spending is for preparatory work, it won't result in U-verse being available to additional homes by 2008.
