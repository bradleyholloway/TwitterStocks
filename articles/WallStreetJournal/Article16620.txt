 Barron's Insight: Garmin Looks Good to Go   By Palmer, Jay, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 2006  Garmin's stock isn't cheap. It trades at about 23 times analysts' earnings estimate for 2006. But the company's earnings growth could go a long way in justifying that premium.  Garmin is probably the best bet for U.S. investors. The company commands more than half of the American market, which is growing faster than Europe's. Last month, the U.S. company reported an 82% jump in fourth-quarter profit, to $87.1 million -- far ahead of analysts' expectations.
