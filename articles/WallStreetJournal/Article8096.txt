 Bush to Push Mideast Peace Plan Requiring Israel to Give Up Land   By Seib, Gerald F; Birnbaum, Jeffrey H, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1991  President Bush opened a new Middle East peace drive by suggesting Israel can best ensure its security by trading occupied land for peace. In a nationally televised speech to Congress, Bush also urged the passing of new transportation and crime bills.
