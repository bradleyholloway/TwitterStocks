 Yoder & Sons: The "Trip": So Far, So Good for Both of Us   By Stephen Kreider Yoder; Yoder, Isaac S; Yoder, Levi, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 2011  (That's not to say, of course, that Levi has lost his teenage impertinence.) We've trundled through East Africa on buses, trucks, ferries and trains since we landed in Uganda with our backpacks. Almost every time we got lost in Stone Town we found the same man selling mangos, purposefully unripe for their tartness, cut up into little french-fry-size slices with a hot pepper powder/salt concoction sprinkled on top.
