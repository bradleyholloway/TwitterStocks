The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 1995  An editorial discusses the mysterious telephone number that surfaced during the Senate Whitewater Committee's investigation of the aftermath of the suicide of Vincent Foster, noting that while Bell Atlantic says the number hasn't been in service since 1978, phone records show that First Lady Hillary Clinton placed a ten-minute call to the number following Foster's death.
