 FDA clears Chem Trak cholesterol kit, the first test to be used in the home   By Gutfeld, Rose, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1993  The FDA approved Chem Trak Inc's home-test kit for cholesterol on Mar 2, 1993, the first such home-test kit to be approved by the agency. The kit, which is designed to produce results in 15 minutes, will be marketed under the name of CholesTrac by American Home Products Corp's Whitehall Laboratories.
