 Nordstrom Aim: Midwest Success Amid Recession   By Schwadel, Francine, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1991  The ability of Nordstrom Inc to successfully open and maintain a full-priced retail store in the Midwest during a recession is assessed.
