 Fuji to cite data showing availability of Kodak film   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 1995  Fuji Photo Film Co on Dec 21, 1995 will release new statistics to the US government showing that film products made by rival Eastman Kodak Co are widely available throughout Japan, and that consumers don't buy Kodak primarily because they "prefer other brands." The data mark Fuji's latest volley in a seven-month trade dispute in which Kodak has accused Fuji and the Japanese government of restricting access to Japan's photo market.
