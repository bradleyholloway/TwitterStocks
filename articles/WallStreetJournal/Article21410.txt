 DEA Agents in Peru Face Obstacles in Struggle to Stanch Flow of Cocaine   By Barrett, Paul M, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 2, 1990  US DEA agents in Peru have been hindered in their efforts by clashes between Peruvian forces and turf battles between US agencies. Sources report that the US military has not done an adequate job of transporting supplies to agents in the field, but some say that the problems are being worked out.
