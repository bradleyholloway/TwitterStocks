 Qingling Motors Co.: Net Profit in 2004 Fell 30% For Chinese Maker of Trucks   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 2005  China's Qingling Motors Co. said its 2004 net profit fell 30% to 109.9 million yuan ($13.3 million) from 157.6 million yuan in 2003. Revenue shrank to 2.95 billion yuan from 3.57 billion yuan.
