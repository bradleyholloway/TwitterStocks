 World wire: EU's unleaded gas use rises   The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 1994  The EU reports motorists in its 12 member countries are increasingly using unleaded gasoline, which contributes less to air pollution. Leaded gas deliveries in 1993 fell below 50% of the fuel total in the trade bloc for the first time, while the unleaded share rose to 53.3% from 47.1%.
