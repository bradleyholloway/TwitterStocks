 Investor's Calendar   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 2003  Mutual-Fund Fallout: State and federal securities regulators filed civil fraud charges against Invesco Funds Group and Chief Executive Ray Cunningham, alleging they allowed market-timing activities. Amvescap, Invesco's British parent, said neither the firm nor Mr. Cunningham engaged in wrongful conduct and the charges would be fought. Separately, Richard Strong quit as head of Strong Financial over questions about fund trading.  China Visits: Chinese Premier Wen Jiabao arrives in the U.S. today for a four-day visit. Trade issues will be high on the agenda after the U.S. recently put quotas on some Chinese textiles. Some U.S. producers say a weak Chinese currency gives China an unfair edge.
