 Online Sites Keep Hotel Rates Low, Bear Stearns Says   By By Motoko Rich, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 9, 2002  Currently, the top five online sites where customers book hotel rooms areExpedia.com, Orbitz.com and Hotels.com. While the lodging industry remains sluggish, these online travel sites are posting record revenue. In July, Hotels.com, which is majority owned by USA Interactive, reported a 66% increase in second-quarter revenue to $229.7 million. Expedia Inc., which also is majority owned by USA, reported an 85% increase in revenue to $145 million in the second quarter.
