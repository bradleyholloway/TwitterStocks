 Yields on CDs Slid In the Latest Week   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1998  The average yield on six-month, "jumbo" CDs, which typically require deposits of $95,000 or more, slid to 4.72% from 4.81%, according to a weekly index prepared by BanxQuote Inc. On five-year jumbos, the average yield dropped to 4.95% from 5.09%, the information service said.
