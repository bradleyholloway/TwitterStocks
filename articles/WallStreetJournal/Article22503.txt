 IMF Criticizes Indonesia for Delay on Banking Reform   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1999  The Indonesian statistics bureau said imports rose to $2.49 billion in December from $2.29 billion in November, but were down from $3.16 billion a year ago. Exports were even on the month at $3.9 billion, but down from $4.7 billion a year earlier. Merrill Lynch & Co. economist Min Lan Tan in Singapore expects the trade surplus to narrow this year.
