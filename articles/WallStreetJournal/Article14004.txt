 States Are Shaking Up Their 529-Plan Lineups   By Anonymous, The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 2012  Last month, Nevada's Upromise 529 plan replaced its investment manager, Vanguard Group, with State Street Global Advisors, and moved from offering mutual funds and other investments to exchange-traded funds. By July, Nebraska will shake up the investment lineup in three of its 529 plans by adding global bond funds, dividend-focused stock funds, ETFs and investments with emerging-markets exposure.
