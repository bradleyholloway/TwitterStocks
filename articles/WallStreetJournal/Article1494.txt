 Farallon Regroups After Knockdown   By Strasburg, Jenny; Cimilluca, Dana, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 2010  The more-open approach marks an evolution for Farallon, long resistant to disclosing details about its holdings, and shows how even well-established hedge funds are bowing to clients who want more information about where their money is invested. Prominent fund managers like Citadel Investment Group and Glenview Capital Management also were among those to lose billions during the financial crisis as the value of their holdings fell and clients lined up to pull money.
