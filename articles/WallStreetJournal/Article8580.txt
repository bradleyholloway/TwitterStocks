 Schneider weighs merger with unit, Spie Batignolles   By Kamm, Thomas, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1995  Schneider SA is in advance discussions to merge with its ailing construction unit Spie Batignolles SA, according to people close to the negotiations. Following the merger, these people say Schneider will likely seek to spin off Spie's contruction and real estate activities and retain only the electrical installation operation that's complementary with Schneider's business.
