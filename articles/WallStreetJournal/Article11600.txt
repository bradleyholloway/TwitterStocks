 Christie's Opens Spring Art Sale, Finds Demand for Works with Lower Prices   By Peers, Alexandra, The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1992  Christie's International PLC sold 51 works of contemporary art, or 73%, of the 70 pieces it placed on the block for the first auction of the spring 1992 season. Sales totaled $11.3 million, lower than the $12.6 million to $17.2 million expected by Christie's.
