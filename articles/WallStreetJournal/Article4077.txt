 Massey Reports Loss Totaling $5.9 Million For Fiscal 2nd Period   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1985  Massey-Ferguson Ltd. reported a net loss of $5.9 million for the fiscal second quarter ended July 31, in contrast to net income of $7.4 million, or three cents a share, a year earlier.  The loss included a $7.6 million charge related to the closure of unprofitable tractor plants in France and Italy. Massey makes farm equipment.  Sales in the quarter fell 8.5% to $372.5 million from the year-earlier $406.9 million, reflecting a continued severe slump in North American demand for combine harvesters, Massey said.
