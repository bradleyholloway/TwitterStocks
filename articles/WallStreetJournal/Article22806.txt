 Aftermath: Securing Transport: Priceline.com Reports Demand Is Increasing Faster Than Expected   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 2001  Priceline unveiled plans to buy a majority equity stake in priceline.com Europe Ltd., which holds the rights to the priceline business in Europe and sells travel products in the United Kingdom.
