 International Finance: Dueling Plans on Regulation in Germany   By Smith, Geoffrey T, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 2009  Two ministries within the German government are coming up with competing proposals to expand financial regulator BaFin's powers, as party rivalry tints the government's attempts to avoid a repeat of the near-collapse of Hypo Real Estate Holding AG. With September's general election closing in, representatives from banks are cautioning that politicians shouldn't let a desire for publicity rush reforms as to when the government can seize a bank at risk of insolvency.
