 Business Brief -- Metro AG: Germany's Biggest Retailer Posts 14% Gain in Net Profit   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 2003  Metro AG, Germany's largest retailer, reported a 14% increase in third-quarter net income, despite an unusually warm summer in Europe that hurt sales of autumn clothing at department stores. Net profit was 59.3 million euros ($68.6 million), compared with 51.9 million euros in the year-earlier period.
