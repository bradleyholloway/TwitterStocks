 Yahoo! Departures Continue With Exit Of Canada Executive   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 2, 2001  His exit marks the fourth departure among Yahoo's international managers in the past month. Fabiola Arredondo, the head of the company's European operations, and Savio Chow, head of its Asian properties, quit in mid-February. Ms. Arredondo left to pursue private business interests, and Mr. Chow left to spend more time with his family. Jin Youm, chief executive of Yahoo! Korea, left the company due to a family illness.
