 Money-Fund Assets Fell in Week   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 2004  Assets in taxable money funds decreased by $17.076 billion to $1.638 trillion. Institutional investors redeemed $10.133 billion, and retail investors drew on $6.94 billion. The average seven-day simple yield for taxable funds rose to 0.53% from 0.52%, while average seven-day compound yields, which assume reinvestment of dividends, did the same. Thirty-day simple and compound yields were steady at 0.52%.
