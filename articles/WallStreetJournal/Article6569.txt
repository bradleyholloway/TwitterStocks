 Deposition Notes Freddie Mac Ploy To Thwart Critics   By Paletta, Damian, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 2006  The numerous consultants were part of a long-rumored strategy that Freddie Mac used to fend off legislation aimed at creating stricter oversight of it and rival Fannie Mae. The admission came in the 277- page transcript of the Federal Election Commission's interview last year with R. Mitchell Delk, who led Freddie Mac's lobbying shop until he resigned in March 2004. He didn't specify which year Freddie Mac spent $10 million on outside consultants.
