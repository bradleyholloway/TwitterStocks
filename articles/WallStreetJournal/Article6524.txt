 NTT DoCoMo Inc.: Television Is Seen as Vehicle To Increase Cellphone Business   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 2006  As cellular carriers face shrinking revenue from voice and data traffic, NTT DoCoMo Inc., Japan's top provider, is targeting television advertisements as a source of growth. DoCoMo announced a tie-up with broadcaster Nippon Television Network Corp. to create TV programs for its cellphones and use these to sell ads.
