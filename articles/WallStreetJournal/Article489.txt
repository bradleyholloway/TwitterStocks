 MarketWatch: Go Camping to Cut Vacation Costs   By Hoak, Amy, The Wall Street Journal,  May 24, 2009  For those testing the waters, it's also possible to rent equipment -- from tents and sleeping bags to Global Positioning System equipment and RVs. While features at individual locations will vary, it's not hard to find a campsite that has a place for kids to swim or do crafts, or for the whole family to watch a movie under the stars -- for less than the cost of a hotel stay.
