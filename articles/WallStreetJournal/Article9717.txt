 Automotive Brief -- General Motors Corp.: Auto Maker to Consolidate Some Saab, Opel Production   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 7, 2004  General Motors Corp. said it plans to consolidate production of its Saab and Opel midclass cars in one plant in a continuing effort to restructure its unprofitable European operations. The auto maker played down speculation that the move means it will close one of its two flagship factories.
