 Yen Continues Gain Against the Dollar; Gold Declines $6.40 --- A Wall Street Journal News Roundup   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 1986  The U.S. dollar gained against major European currencies Friday but continued to slip against the Japanese yen.  Many foreign-exchange dealers say sheer momentum will probably push Japan's currency still higher against the dollar this week. "Japan may have a runaway currency on its hands," said Garrett R. Glass, vice president of foreign exchange for First National Bank of Chicago.
