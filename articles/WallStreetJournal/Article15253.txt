 Dollar loses all of its Monday gains against mark, is flat in relation to yen   By Luxemburg, Carolyn J, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 1994  The dollar retreated against the German mark Oct 4, 1994, but remained flat against Japan's yen. In late New York trading, the US currency stood at 1.5470 marks and 99.66 yen, compared to DM1.5543 and Y99.53.
