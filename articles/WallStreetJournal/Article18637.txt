 Marketing: A trusting Oracle to enter market via Internet   By Rigdon, Joan E, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1995  Oracle Corp, the king of database software for entire companies, will try to break onto workers' desktops with a new product called Personal Oracle 7. To even its odds against the mighty distribution networks of Microsoft Corp, Borland International Inc and Apple Computer Inc's Claris unit, Oracle will bypass retail vendors and distribute its actual product over the Internet.
