 'Infomercial' Makers Try to Clean Up Act   By Lipman, Joanne, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1991  Infomercials, program-length commercials that masquerade as entertainment or news shows and are often aired on late-night TV, have been investigated several times by the FTC. Industry leaders have gotten together in an attempt to establish guidelines for legitimate conduct.
