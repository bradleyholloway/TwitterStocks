 Advertising: Arbitron Pulls the Plug on ScanAmerica   By Lipman, Joanne, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 1992  Citing a lack of support among advertisers, ad agencies and TV networks, Arbitron pulled the plug on its TV ratings service, ScanAmerica. Arbitron's move marks the end of what many ad executives called the industry's future.
