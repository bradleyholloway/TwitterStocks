 Business Brief -- CML Group Inc.: Irwin Jacobs Sells His Stake Of 14.6%, or 930,000 Shares   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 1990  Minneapolis investor Irwin Jacobs has sold his entire position, approximately 930,000 shares or 14.6% of the common stock, in CML Group Inc., a direct marketer of consumer products.
