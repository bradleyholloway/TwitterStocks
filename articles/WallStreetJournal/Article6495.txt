 Mossberg's Mailbox   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 2005  Apple itself sells such a program, QuickTime Pro, which is the paid version of its free QuickTime multimedia player for both Windows and Mac computers. The software, which costs $30, can import many types of video clips and convert them to a format that's optimized for the iPod. There's a special option under the program's "Export" menu called "Movie to iPod." In addition, other individuals and companies have issued new conversion programs since the new iPod was announced, all aimed at transforming existing video clips into iPod-friendly files.
