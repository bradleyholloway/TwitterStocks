 Mutual funds: Quarterly review: Tales from the comeback trail: The outlook for three rebounds   By O Connell, Vanessa, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 1995  Three mutual fund managers who are candidates for the Comeback of the Year Award are profiled, and their odds of staying in the upper ranks for the long term are analyzed. The three managers are Shelby Davis of Davis New York Venture Fund, John Horseman of GAM International Fund and David Alger of Alger Small Capitalization Fund. Davis's odds are rated "excellent," Horseman's "good" and Alger's "uncertain."
