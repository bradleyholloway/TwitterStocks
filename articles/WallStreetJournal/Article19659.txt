 SAS to Install Web Access for Longer Flights   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 2003  The first SAS flights to be equipped with the technology will be trans-Atlantic, and routes to and from Asia, said Jens Willumsen, senior vice president for market and product management at Scandinavian Airlines.
