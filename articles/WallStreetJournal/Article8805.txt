 Washington wire: Two contenders   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 1997  Two contenders for financial posts in a fight to replace the NRA leadership the weekend of Feb 7, 1997 were officials of a group that was fined $808,000 in 1995 for violating California's political-finance reporting laws. Richard Carone was a founder of Californians Against Corruption and C. Russell Howard was treasurer.
