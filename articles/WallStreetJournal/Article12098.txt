 Purchases of high-yielding currencies from Europe lift dollar against mark   By Patterson, Rebecca, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 1996  Movements in European currencies dominated foreign-exchange activity on Jul 4, 1996, with dollar dealings muted by Independence Day. Late in London, the dollar was at DM1.5245, up from DM1.5205 on Jul 3 in New York, and at Y110.41, up from Y110.35.
