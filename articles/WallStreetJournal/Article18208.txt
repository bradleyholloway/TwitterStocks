 Duty Free Approves 2-for-1 Split; Reports Record Sales, Earnings   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 1991  Duty Free International Inc's board approved a 2-for-1 stock split and the company reported record first-quarter sales and earnings. The stock split will be distributed Jul 5, 1991 to holders of record Jun 14. Earnings for the fiscal quarter ended Apr 30, 1991 rose 46% to $2.9 million from $2 million a year earlier.
