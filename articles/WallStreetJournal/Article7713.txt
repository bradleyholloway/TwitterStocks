 Business Brief: Duro-Test Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 1987  Duro-Test Corp., North Bergen, N.J., said it agreed to provide certain confidential information to TCA-IV L.P., which has made an unsolicited $14-a-share, or $66 million, tender offer for the Duro-Test shares it doesn't already own.
