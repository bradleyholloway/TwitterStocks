 Investor's Calendar   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 2006  -- SEC Under Fire: The Securities and Exchange Commission is expected to face criticism in a Senate Judiciary Committee hearing Tuesday. Possible issues include the SEC's relationship with the Justice Department and its controls on hedge funds. The SEC has been on the defensive since senators claimed it was weak on insider trading.  -- Billionaire Unloads GM: Investor Kirk Kerkorian sold his entire stake in General Motors, once 9.9%, after a 20-month effort to reshape the auto maker. Mr. Kerkorian had pushed for an alliance with Nissan Motor and Renault that GM rebuffed.
