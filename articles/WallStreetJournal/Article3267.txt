 VeriSign to Pay $1.4 Billion To Add to E-Commerce Services   By By David P. Hamilton, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 1999  VeriSign, of Mountain View, Calif., said it will acquire closely held Thawte Consulting, of Cape Town, South Africa, in a stock deal valued at $575 million. VeriSign will issue an as-yet-undetermined number of shares in exchange for Thawte.  The e-commerce service provider said it also will purchase closely held Signio Inc., in a stock transaction. Signio, of Redwood Shores, Calif., makes software that manages payments and financial authorizations for businesses over the Internet. Under the terms of that deal, VeriSign will issue approximately 5.6 million shares of its common stock for Signio.
