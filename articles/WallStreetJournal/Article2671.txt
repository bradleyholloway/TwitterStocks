 Legal beat: Dow Chemical sheds new light on its role in implants   By Burton, Thomas M, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 1994  In Nov 1994, Dow Chemical Co's former general counsel, Wayne M. Hancock, acknowledged in sworn testimony that a 1992 affidavit saying that the company had not sold breast implants was mistaken and that a former Italian unit of Dow had, in fact, sold the devices overseas.
