 Letters to the Editor: Stunning Solution To Fits of Air Rage   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 2000  In the Marketplace article "Passenger's Death Prompts Calls for Improved `Air Rage' Procedures" (Sept. 26) we learn that, after multiple serious episodes involving passengers who threaten the safety of the entire aircraft, the airlines still have no procedures for dealing with such incidents. The pilots/attendants unions have advocated "conflict-resolution training" for their members. Several airlines "equip their planes with wrist restraints."
