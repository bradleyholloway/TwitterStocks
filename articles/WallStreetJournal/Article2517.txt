 Advertising: Coors tries PC ads to key up viewers   By Goldman, Kevin, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1993  In pushing its new Coors Light brand beer, Adolph Coors Co became one of a handful of companies that are advertising on PC on-line services such as Prodigy. The new attention to the so-called New Media--advertising that involves interactivity, infomercials and media such as computers and CD-ROMs--is on the rise as increased zapping and channel surfing make it harder for advertisers to grab consumers' attention.
