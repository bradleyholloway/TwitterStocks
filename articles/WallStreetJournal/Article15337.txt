 Redemption Notices   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 1995  COUNTY OF SENECA, OHIO will redeem, on July 31, 1995, the following of its industrial development first mortgage revenue bonds: coupon bonds and registered bonds, due June 1, 1999. NationalCity Bank, Cleveland, is trustee.  FREEPORT, ILL. will redeem, on Aug. 1, 1995, $385,000 principal amount of its single family mortgage revenue bonds, 1979 Series A. Bank of America Illinois, Chicago, is paying agent.  ILLINOIS ENVIRONMENTAL FACILITIES FINANCING AUTHORITY will redeem, on Aug. 1, 1995, its $35,000 principal amount of its small business pollution control revenue bonds, Series 1981 A2, due Aug. 1, 2001. LaSalle National Trust is trustee.
