 Coach Leatherwear Sues Three Concerns over Handbag Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1990  In a lawsuit filed on May 21 in the Southern District of New York, Coach charged that the defendants' sale of allegedly imitation Coach bags constituted "false designations of origin and false and misleading descriptions" with the intention of "creating confusion and deception in the marketplace . . . and for the purpose of implying an association between these products sold by Ann Taylor and Coach."
