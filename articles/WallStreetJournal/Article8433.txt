 Trust sells Seagram shares   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 1993  A family trust controlled by Charles R. Bronfman, co-chairman of Seagram Co, sold 3.7 million shares of Seagram stock, reducing the family stake in the spirits maker by 2.15% to 36.4%. At Seagram's closing price of $26.75 on Dec 6, 1993, the sale of shares generated about $99 million.
