 U.S. News: Harrisburg Bankruptcy Filing Voided   By Stech, Katy; Nolan, Kelly, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 25, 2011  Judge Mary France of U.S. Bankruptcy Court in Harrisburg ruled Wednesday that a group of city councilors failed to follow the state's procedures for distressed cities when they sought last month to protect the city of roughly 49,000 residents from creditors through Chapter 9 of U.S. bankruptcy law.
