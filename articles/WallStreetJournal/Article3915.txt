 Metro AG: Stake Is Lifted in China Venture To Speed Expansion in Nation   The Wall Street Journal,  May 20, 2005  German retail and wholesale giant Metro AG raised its stake in Metro Jinjiang Cash & Carry Co., a joint venture with China's Jinjiang International Group, to 90% from 60% to accelerate its expansion in China, Metro Jinjiang Cash & Carry said.
