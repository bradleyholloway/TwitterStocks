 Business Brief -- PIA MERCHANDISING SERVICES INC.: Management Jobs to Be Cut In a Move to Reduce Costs   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1997  PIA Merchandising Services Inc said it has decided to reduce its management ranks by 77 people, or 27%, and take a $6 million restructuring charge in the third quarter, as part of a previously announced plan to cut costs.
