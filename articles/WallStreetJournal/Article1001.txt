 Ad Notes...   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 2006  Anheuser-Busch's Budweiser is teaming with Time Warner's Sports Illustrated to promote the magazine's widely anticipated annual swimsuit issue. In addition to buying pages in the edition, due to publish Valentine's Day, Anheuser will also advertise on SI.com, the magazine's Web site, and engage in retail and event marketing and other promotions.
