 Business Brief -- SportsLine.com Inc.: Agreement Is Set to Acquire Sandbox.com for Cash, Stock   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 2001  SportsLine.com Inc., publisher of Internet sports service CBS SportsLine.com, signed a definitive agreement to buy online fantasy-sports producer Sandbox.com Inc. for $1 million and 1.6 million SportsLine shares. Shares of SportsLine rose four cents to 95 cents in 4 p.m. Nasdaq Stock Market trading.
