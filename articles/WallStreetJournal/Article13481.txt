 Mutual Funds Quarterly Special Report -- Encore: New Funds Pitch Steady Income to Retirees   By Lauricella, Tom, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2008  So-called income distribution funds attempt to combine the ease of traditional mutual funds (which make deposits and withdrawals relatively simple) with the predictable payouts associated with a pension or an insurance-based annuity. Money managers will be choosing underlying investments with an eye toward ensuring the target income can be delivered -- no easy task given the ups and downs of the stock and bond markets.
