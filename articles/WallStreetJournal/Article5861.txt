 Economists' Salaries Rise6.25%, Beating Inflation   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 2000  At securities and investment firms, economists earned a median base salary of $107,500 and additional income of $49,996. Manufacturing-company analysts earned base salaries of $103,000 and additional income of $33,570.  The smallest firms in the survey, those with one to five employees and those with six to 49 employees, paid a total of $114,000 and $115,000, respectively, in median salaries and additional compensation.
