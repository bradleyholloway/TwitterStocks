 Guilty Plea in Abu Ghraib Case   By Cooper, Christopher, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 21, 2004  Staff Sgt. Ivan "Chip" Frederick, a Virginia reservist and civilian prison guard, pleaded guilty to five crimes related to the abuse as his court martial was scheduled to begin in Baghdad. Sgt. Frederick, who supervised the night shift at Iraq's Abu Ghraib prison a year ago, is the highest-ranking soldier charged in the abuse case. He could be sentenced as early as today.
