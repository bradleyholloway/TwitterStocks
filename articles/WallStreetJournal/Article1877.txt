 International Corporate Report: Two Big Real-Estate Firms Post Profit Gains in Japan   The Wall Street Journal,  May 20, 1988  Mitsui Real Estate's unconsolidated net rose 22% in the fiscal year, to 18.66 billion yen ($149.5 million) from 15.26 billion yen a year earlier, as sales grew 23%, to 394.87 billion yen ($3.16 billion) from 320.71 billion yen. In the current fiscal year, Mitsui Real Estate expects net to rise to 21 billion yen and sales to 460 billion yen.
