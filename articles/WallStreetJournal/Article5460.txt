 Accor SA: Holding in Compass PLC is cut to 10% from 21.5%   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 1997  French hotel group Accor SA said on Mar 7, 1997 it had successfully sold 36.5 million shares it owned of Compass PLC, a British institutional catering concern. The sale raised 2.35 billion francs, or $406.1 million.
