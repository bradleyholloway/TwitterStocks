 Tyco International Plans To Meet With Analysts To Target Recent Rumors   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 21, 2002  Tyco's stock has been buffeted by a spate of rumors and weighed down by a gloomy earnings forecast for the current fiscal second quarter. Market rumors swirling last week included speculation that the company's chief financial officer, Mark Swartz, was leaving Tyco and that the company was poised to make a big convertible-debt offering. Tyco officials have called all of the rumors untrue.
