 House of the Week: Equestrian Estate   By By Felicia Paik, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1999  Where: Rancho Santa Fe, Calif., 4 miles from Del Mar Horse Park; 25 miles from downtown San Diego  Amenities: The house has a solarium, a library and game and potting rooms. The property has 57 horse stalls, a riding ring, five pastures and a two-acre lake.
