 Columbia Gas Profit From Ongoing Lines Fell 54% in 4th Period   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1986  WILMINGTON, Del. -- Columbia Gas System Inc. reported that fourth-quarter earnings from continuing operations declined 54% to $24.8 million, or 61 cents a share, from $53.8 million, or $1.36 a share, a year earlier.  A year earlier, a $25.9 million loss from discontinued operations made net income $27.9 million, or 71 cents a share. Revenue rose 1.9% to $1.23 billion from $1.21 billion.
