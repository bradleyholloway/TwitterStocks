 Business Brief -- TOLL BROTHERS INC.: Luxury-Home Builder Had 13% Increase in Net Income   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1998  Toll Brothers Inc said fiscal fourth-quarter net income for the period ended Oct 31, 1998 rose 13% to $27.9 million, or 73 cents a diluted share, from $24.6 million, or 66 cents, a year earlier.
