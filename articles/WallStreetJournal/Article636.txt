 BankAmerica Corp. Rating Reduced by Keefe Bruyette   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 1985  Keefe, Bruyette & Woods Inc. reduced its credit rating for BankAmerica Corp., but the move is expected to have little if any effect on the big bank holding company or its units.  Keefe Bruyette, a New York-based bank securities firm, cut its rating on BankAmerica to C/D from C. Many money market funds and some major corporations avoid buying deposits from units of bank holding companies rated less than C by Keefe Bruyette.
