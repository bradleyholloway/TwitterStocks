 Telesat Canada Manages To Repair Faulty Satellite   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 1991  Telesat, which operates Canada's domestic satellite communications system, launched the 300 million Canadian dollar (US$262.6 million) satellite in April, but was unable to deploy one of the satellite's two antennae. Without both antennae, the new satellite would have been useless.
