 Ryka Inc. asks L.A. Gear for loan, new takeover pact   By Pereira, Joseph, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1995  Ryka Inc, a cash-strapped athletic-footwear maker that is fighting to remain in business, has asked L.A. Gear Inc, its suitor, for a short-term loan and is seeking to renegotiate the terms of the companies' acquisition agreement. Ryka said failure to reach a revised agreement will result in the termination of the merger agreement.
