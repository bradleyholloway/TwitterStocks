 Matra, British Aerospace plan missile venture   By du Bois, Martin, The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 1993  Matra-Hachette SA and British Aerospace PLC, in a major step toward greater consolidation of Europe's troubled defense industry, are close to merging their missile businesses. The combined operation would have annual sales of about 10 billion French francs ($1.9 billion).
