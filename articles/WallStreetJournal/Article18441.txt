 FAG Kugelfischer KGaA: Ball-bearing firm may sell or shut half of its plants   The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 1993  FAG Kugelfischer KGaA, a manufacturer of ball-bearings, announced that it may sell or shut half of its factories by the end of 1994. The company plans to sell operations with 800 million marks ($504.5 million) in sales and 7,550 workers by that time, although the streamlining may be accomplished in 1993.
