 Business Brief -- EVERGREEN RESOURCES INC.: Energy Concern Buys Tract From Amoco, Ending Suit   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 1998  Evergreen Resources Inc said it acquired a 100% working interest in 27,590 acres in the Raton Basin of Colorado from Amoco Production Co, resolving litigation between the two companies. Terms of the purchase weren't disclosed.
