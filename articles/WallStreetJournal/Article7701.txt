 Eurodollar Bonds Rise Moderately; Austria Offers Straight Issue --- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 1987  Seasoned Eurodollar bond prices rose moderately yesterday as Austria successfully launched the first major Eurodollar straight debt issue in the aftermath of the equities crash.  Austria's $250 million of three-year, 8 1/2% bonds traded strongly and were quoted at less 1/2, less 3/8. But that doesn't mean the dollar sector is back to full health, some underwriters said, because investors remain selective to large issues by the most credit-worthy borrowers and to issues paying a good premium over outstanding issues. That could leave many would-be borrowers on the sidelines.
