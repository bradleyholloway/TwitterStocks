 Ford's China Venture Reports Leap in Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2007  In December, Changan Ford Mazda's Ford-branded sales to consumers totaled 19,401 units, a monthly record, the venture said. Changan Ford Mazda is a joint venture with Ford's Japanese affiliate Mazda Motor Corp. and state-owned Changan Automobile Group.
