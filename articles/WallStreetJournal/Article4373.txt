 Net Deposit Inflow At S&Ls Continued During December   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 1988  The net inflow of deposits to thrift institutions that began during October's stock market drop continued through the year's end, the Federal Home Loan Bank Board reported.  Deposits in savings institutions insured by the Bank Board's Federal Savings and Loan Insurance Corp. exceeded withdrawals by $2.4 billion in December, the board said. This compared with November's net deposit inflow of $3.2 billion and the $1 billion net deposit outflow in December 1986.
