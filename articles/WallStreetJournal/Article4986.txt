 Poland announces first privatization of a key state bank   By Bray, Nicholas, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 9, 1993  Poland announced the privatization in early Apr 1993 of a major state-owned bank, Wielkopolski Bank Kreditowy, in a $28 million capital-raising program backed by the EBRD. WBK is Poland's third-largest bank in terms of capital.
