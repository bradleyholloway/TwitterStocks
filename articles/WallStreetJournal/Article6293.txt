 Cable Channel Yields to LBJ Aides   By Flint, Joe, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 2004  The documentary, titled "The Guilty Men," alleges that while serving as vice president, Johnson was involved in the assassination of President Kennedy. It was part of a series of documentaries titled "The Men Who Killed Kennedy" that examined numerous conspiracy theories that the History Channel carried in November in connection with the 40th anniversary of the assassination. "The Guilty Men" drew an audience of 2.2 million when it ran on Nov. 17; it also ran several more times.
