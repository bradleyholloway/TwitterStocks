 Arts & Entertainment: The Show Always Goes On at the Tank   By Catton, Pia, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 2010  Visitors on a given night could expect anything from a mid-March "St. Purim's Day" celebration, which creatively combined the Jewish holiday of Purim with the Irish observance of St. Patrick's Day, to election-season events like "Sleeping With the Enemy," a bi-partisan party co-hosted by the group Drinking Liberally and the New York Young Republican Club.
