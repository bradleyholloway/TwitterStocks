 `Titanic' Chair Stirs Furor Over Authenticity at eBay   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 2000  In recent months, eBay has been plagued by problematic sales of high-price goods online. In May, the sale of an abstract painting for $135,805 was later voided following suspect bidding and rumors that it was a fake. Unlike the painting, this chair was guaranteed by auctioneer Butterfields, an eBay subsidiary.
