 Business Brief -- Scott's Hospitality Inc.: Special Dividend Is Slated Following Hotel-Chain Sale   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1989  Scott's Hospitality Inc. said it will pay a special dividend of three Canadian dollars (US$2.54) a share, or a total of about C$180 million, following a "one-time windfall" gain from the recent sale of a hotel chain.
