 Abreast of the market: Blue chips fall, as Philip Morris leads declines in tobacco issues   By O Brien, Robert, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 1997  Investors treated blue-chip stocks to nearly the same sort of selling that had plagued technology stocks during the week of Mar 17, 1997, but that group, surprisingly, bounced back in a mixed session of stock trading. The Dow Jones Industrial Average fell 57.40 to 6820.28, finishing near its lows in a session of surprising volatility even for what has been a surprisingly volatile market.
