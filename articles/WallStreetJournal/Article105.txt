 New Securities Issues [2]  The Wall Street Journal,  May 25, 1990  National Rural Utilities Co-op -- $150 million of 9 1/2% collateralized trust bonds, due May 15, 1997, priced at 99.931 to yield 9.512%. The noncallable bonds were priced at a spread of 85 basis points above the Treasury's seven-year note. Rated double-A-3 by Moody's Investors Service Inc. and single-A-plus by Standard & Poor's Corp., the issue will be sold through underwriters led by Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc.  Arizona Public Service Co. -- $125 million of 10 1/4% first mortgage bonds, due May 15, 2020, priced at 99 to yield 10.358%. The bonds were priced at a spread of 180 basis points above the Treasury's 30-year bond. Rated Baa-3 by Moody's and triple-B-minus by S&P, the issue will be sold through underwriters led by Salomon Brothers Inc. Arizona Public Service is a unit of Pinnacle West Capital Corp.
