 Biotechnology: Gene Scientist to Leave NIH, Form Institute   By Bishop, Jerry E; Stout, Hilary, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 1992  Craig Venter, the federal scientist whose research has triggered a battle over the patenting of genes, is leaving the NIH to set up his own private, nonprofit research institute with funds from a venture capital firm. The NIH plans to continue the gene sequencing operation Venter began.
