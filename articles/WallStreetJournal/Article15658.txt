 CPSC Seeks Recall Of Sprinkler Heads From Central Sprinkler   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1998  The federal agency said Omega heads built by Central Sprinkler Co., Lansdale, Pa., "are defective and are likely to fail in a fire." The heads have failed in at least six blazes in the past three years, including one at a veterans hospital, the agency said in asking an administrative judge in Washington to order a recall. No injuries occurred in any of the fires.
