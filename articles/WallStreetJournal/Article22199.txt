 CBS may air episode of Letterman's show without commercials   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 1996  CBS's "Late Show with David Letterman" will try to draw attention to itself, and its advertisers, with a one-time commercial-free program expected to air later in Sep 1996. The network is soliciting four sponsors for the show. They would get verbal and possibly written billing at the beginning of the show and during the program.
