 Leona Helmsley Sells Remainder of a Stake In New York Building   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 3, 1998  Howard Rubenstein, a spokesman for Mrs. Helmsley, confirmed the deal but declined to reveal the sale price. The New York Daily News reported that the 18% stake fetched $6.3 million. Mrs. Helmsley already sold off her controlling interest in the building last year.
