 Marketscan: Sales of private-label goods keep rising   By Deveny, Kathleen, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 1993  Supermarket sales of private-label products continued to rise in the second quarter from a year earlier. Sales grew 2.6% to $7.42 billion during the 13 weeks ends Jul 4, 1993, while unit volume increased 1.4%. Total supermarket sales rose only 0.1%, and unit volume declined slightly. Private-label accounted for 14.8% of total dollar sales, and 19.8% of all items sold in grocery stores.
