 Tough Business: A Car Salesman Finds It's Also Hard for Him to Get a Good Deal   By Patterson, Gregory A, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 1992  Car salesman Robert Williams is profiled as an example of how tough it is to make a living in that career. Williams and many other car sellers are the odd men out these days in the tough match between price-wise buyers and profit-pinched dealers. There are about 144,000 car salesmen in the US.
