 Business Brief: Mitsui & Co.:   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 2003  Mitsui & Co. reported a 4.2% gain in its first-half earnings, helped by strength in its chemical-products and machinery operations. The Japanese trading house posted a group net profit of 25.82 billion yen ($236.2 million) for the six months ended Sept. 30, up from its year- earlier profit of 24.79 billion yen.
