 World News: U.K.'s Treasury Chief to Challenge Opposition on Timid Budget Plans   By Norman, Laurence, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 2010  U.K. Treasury Chief George Osborne is expected to launch an attack Monday on the new leader of the opposition Labour Party, Ed Miliband, accusing him of protecting "vested interests" in the unions by refusing to address the budget deficit. Mr. Osborne will say the Conservative argument of tackling the deficit now has backing from the International Monetary Fund, Bank of England Gov. Mervyn King, credit-rating agencies and the bond markets.
