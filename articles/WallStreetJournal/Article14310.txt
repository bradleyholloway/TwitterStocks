 Money Fund Assets Fell $1.03 Billion In the Latest Week [1]  The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 1986  Assets of the nation's 375 money market funds fell $1.03 billion to $233.90 billion in the week ended Wednesday, the Investment Company Institute, a Washington-based trade group, said.  "Improvement in stock prices prompted some investors in general purpose and broker-dealer funds to move dollars from money funds to equity products," said Alfred Johnson, vice president and chief economist at the institute.
