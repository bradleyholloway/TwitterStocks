 Commercial Property Market's Troubles May Deepen   By Barsky, Neil, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 1992  The fate of the building at 55 Water St in Manhattan NY is an example of "rollover risk," the virus that industry experts say will eventually creep into virtually every commercial office building, no matter how well-managed or situated it may be. The building is owned by Olympia & York Developments.
