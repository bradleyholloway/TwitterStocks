 USDA Cuts Corn Forecast --- July's Heat Wave Prompts 4% Lowering of Harvest Outlook   By Kilman, Scott; Polansek, Tom, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 2011  A July heat wave over the Midwest forced the U.S. Agriculture Department to slice its one-month-old forecast of the U.S. corn harvest by a deeper-than-expected 4%, signaling that rising retail food prices could continue to sting consumers even as the direction of the sluggish U.S. economy grows more uncertain.
