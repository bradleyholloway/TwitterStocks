The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 2007  HEINEKEN INTERNATIONAL NV (Amsterdam) -- Kheri Holland Tillman was appointed vice president for the Amstel Light and Dos Equis brands at Heineken USA Inc., the importer subsidiary of this brewer, effective immediately. Ms. Tillman, 37, succeeds Willem van der Hoeven, who will manage operations for Heineken South Africa. Ms. Tillman was a brand director for new beverage products at Kraft Foods Inc.
