 Waiting On Return of 401(k) Match   By Laise, Eleanor; Greene, Kelly, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 3, 2010  Rival United Parcel Service Inc. raised its dividend more than 4% earlier this year but still hasn't restored the 401(k) match it suspended in early 2009. Since some workers also own UPS shares, the dividend boost "is certainly helping employees as well as our outside shareowners," said Norman Black, a UPS spokesman.
