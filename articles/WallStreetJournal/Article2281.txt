 Moody's to Review Big Four Japan Firms' $2.1 Billion in Debt   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 20, 1991  Moody's Investors Service Inc, citing a sluggish Japanese stock market and the corruption scandal in Japan's securities market, has placed the $2.1 billion debt of Nomura Securities Co, Nikko Securities Co, Daiwa Securities Co and Yamaichi Securities Co under review.
