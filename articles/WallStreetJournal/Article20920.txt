 Argo Gets Extension On Meeting Obligations Of Credit Agreement   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 1986  SANTA MONICA, Calif. -- Argo Petroleum Corp. said Continental Illinois National Bank & Trust Co. extended through Jan. 31 waivers of certain requirements for Argo's revolving credit agreement. Earlier this week, Argo said it couldn't pay about $2 million in interest due Wednesday on some debentures.
