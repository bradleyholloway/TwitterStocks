 Lowe's Cos. to Take $71.3 Million Charge in Fiscal 4th Period   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 1992  Lowe's Cos plans to take a $71.3 million pretax restructuring charge in its fiscal fourth quarter ended Jan 31, 1992. Separately, Moody's Investors Service Inc placed the debt ratings of Lowe's under review for possible downgrade.
