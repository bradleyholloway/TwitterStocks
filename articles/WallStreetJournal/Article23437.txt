 More Changes In the Journal's Market Tables   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 2007  Today we've also added a feature on world stock markets. Because bond markets were open, today readers will see a new daily bond-data section (comparing U.S. Treasurys with international debt; daily performance of investment-grade and high-yield, or junk, debt; and Tracking Bond Benchmarks), and some futures listings.
