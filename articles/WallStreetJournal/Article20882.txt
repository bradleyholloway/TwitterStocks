 Campeau Plans Offer to Buy Its Warrants at $7.50 Each   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 1985  Campeau Corp. said it plans to make an offer Sept. 18 on the Toronto and Montreal stock exchanges to purchase all of its 1,440,000 warrants at $7.50 (Canadian) each, or a total of $10.8 million.
