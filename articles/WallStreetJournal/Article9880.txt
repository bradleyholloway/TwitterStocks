 Forget the Wall -- Reform Immigration Laws   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 2006  Victor Davis Hanson whips up righteous indignation regarding illegal immigration ("Mi Casa es Su Casa," editorial page, Dec. 28), but his aim is misguided. Yes, Mexico needs to take care of its own citizens, and yes, some employers are taking advantage of the situation. But no one "supports" illegal immigration -- unfortunately, there is no legal alternative.
