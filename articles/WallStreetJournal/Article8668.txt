 Immigration reform   By Smith, Lamar, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 1995  Rep Lamar Smith (R-TX) responds to the Nov 27, 1995 article "Immigration: Where We Go From Here." Smith says the article mischaracterizes his National Interest Act, which will permit higher legal immigration levels than during 65 of the past 70 years, or more than 700,000 legal immigrants annually.
