 Family Misfortunes: A Father's Ambitions for His Son's Success Cripple Them Both   By Lublin, Joann S, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 20, 1991  The experiences of Joseph Brooks, the owner of AnnTaylor Stores Corp, in trying to force the success of his son Thomas Brooks, are described. The elder Brooks tried to force his son's promotion to the president of AnnTaylor Stores Corp, only to meet with a series of embarrassing incidents. Now, Thomas is out of a job and Joseph will soon be. Joseph's rise to the top and his fall are examined.
