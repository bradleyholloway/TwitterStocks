 Grocery Price Wars Squeeze Marketers   By Stern, Gabriella, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1991  Price wars among US supermarkets are welcome news to consumers, but are inflicting harm on the makers of household products. Procter & Gamble Co and Kimberly-Clark Corp are two industry giants that are feeling the squeeze from price wars, as well as from cheaper store brands and from stores like Phar-Mor Inc and F&M Distributors Inc that sell premium-priced products at a discount.
