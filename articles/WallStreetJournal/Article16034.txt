 Health Brief -- Visx Inc.: Net to Exceed Projections; Stock Buyback Is Slated   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 2001  Visx Inc. said its first-quarter earnings will exceed Wall Street's expectations, due in part to a 23% increase in licensing revenue from the fourth quarter. The Santa Clara, Calif., maker of equipment for laser eye surgery said it expects net income of 20 cents to 21 cents a diluted share for the first quarter.
