 Former Boesky Employee Is Said to Get Immunity   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 1987  David Jachimczyk, a former stock trader for Mr. Boesky, is the latest potential witness about the activities of Michael R. Milken, head of Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc.'s junk bond operations. The government is investigating a $5.3 million payment from Mr. Boesky to Drexel in March 1986. People familiar with the investigation have alleged that the payment may have been part of an illegal stock trading scheme between Mr. Boesky and Mr. Milken.
