 Viacom Results Hurt by Severance Charge, Film Unit   By Karnitschnig, Matthew, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 2006  The results come after a quarter that saw the ouster of Mr. [Tom Freston], who ran MTV Networks for many years but served only eight months as chief executive. Sumner Redstone, Viacom's controlling shareholder and executive chairman, fired Mr. Freston in early September amid disappointment with Viacom's share price and Internet strategy. Viacom booked $62 million in severance-related charges in the third quarter related to Mr. Freston's departure.
