 Work week: Daily grind   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1997  Staci Dickson, an eight-year veteran firefighter with the US Forest Service, is used to sliding 200 ft down a rope from a helicopter into a forest fire. Firefighters are paid $11 an hour, and time and a half for overtime. She clocked nearly 1,500 hours of overtime in summer 1996.
