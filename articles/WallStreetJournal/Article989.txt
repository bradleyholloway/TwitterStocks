 Federated's Questrom To Succeed Stone As a Vice Chairman   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 1988  The appointment of Mr. Questrom, chairman and chief executive officer of Federated's Los Angeles-based Bullock's/Bullocks Wilshire division and an executive vice president of the parent, makes him one of the company's top officers. Federated has three vice chairmen, a president, and a chairman and chief executive.
