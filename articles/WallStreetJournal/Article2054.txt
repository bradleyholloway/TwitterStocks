 Former Stockbroker Enters Plea of Guilty To 13 Fraud Charges   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1989  Walter F. Curran, a former Dean Witter Reynolds Inc. stockbroker, pleaded guilty in U.S. District Court here to a total of 13 counts of mail fraud and bank fraud, plus one count issued in New York for currency reporting violations.
