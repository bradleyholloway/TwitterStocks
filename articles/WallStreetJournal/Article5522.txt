 Columbia/HCA warns of profit decline   By Lagnado, Lucette, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1997  Citing the financial toll from a huge federal Medicare-fraud probe, Columbia/HCA Healthcare Co warned on Sep 9, 1997 that third-quarter profit will fall well short of year-earlier results. Columbia's shares fell 9.4%, or $3.0625, to $29.625 on the news.
