 Western Preferred To Sell Two Insurers And Stake in a Third   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1985  FORT WORTH, Texas -- Western Preferred Corp. said it agreed in principle to sell two more insurance companies and its stake in a third as part of its effort to cure a default on $82.5 million of bank debt.  The insurance holding company declined to disclose terms of any of the proposed transactions. Earlier last week, it said it agreed to sell its United Fidelity Life Insurance Co. unit to a unit of Great Western Financial Corp.
