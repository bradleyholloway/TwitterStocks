 Bond prices fall as speculation of rate increase by Fed sends a shudder through major markets   By Vogel, Thomas T, Jr; Schultz, Abby, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1994  Speculation that the FRB may be near to tightening monetary policy sent the bond markets downward Feb 3, 1994. The US 30-year bond fell over 3/8 point in price to 99 4/32, boosting the yield to 6.31% from 6.28%.
