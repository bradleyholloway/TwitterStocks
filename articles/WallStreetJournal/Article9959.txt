 LVMH Reports Profit Rose 46% During First Half   By Simon Clow and Kenneth Maxwell, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 7, 2006  with a stable including Moet & Chandon and Krug champagnes, the Kenzo and Givenchy fashion brands, Christian Dior perfumes, TAG Heuer watches and diamond retailer De Beers -- posted net profit of 817 million euros ($1.05 billion), up from 559 million euros in the year-earlier period, when profits were hit by currency losses and the cost of refurbishing the group's flagship La Samaritaine department store in Paris.
