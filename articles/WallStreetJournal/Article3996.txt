 Stock Prices Climb In London and Tokyo; Indexes Set Records --- A Wall Street Journal News Roundup   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 1985  On the London Stock Exchange, the Financial Times industrial share index rose 11.9 points, to a record 983.1. Traders said electronics and electrical issues paced the rally, which began earlier in the week after news of an unexpected fall in Britain's money supply in the latest month eased trader concern about higher interest rates.
