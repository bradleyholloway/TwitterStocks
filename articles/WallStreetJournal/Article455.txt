 Tip of the Week: Book Early And Save   By Badal, Jaclyne, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 25, 2007  Mr. [Brandon Hart] and two friends booked round-trip tickets to Newark, N.J., so they could spend Thanksgiving in New York. The first person booked on Monday, Oct. 29, and paid $460.60. The second person booked that Friday and paid $540.60. By the time Mr. Hart booked, on Nov. 9, the total cost had jumped to $625.60.
