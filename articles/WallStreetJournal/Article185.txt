 Wyman-Gordon Co.: Shareholders vote to acquire Cooper Industries Inc.'s unit   The Wall Street Journal,  May 25, 1994  Wyman-Gordon Co said its shareholders voted to approve its acquisition of Cooper Industries Inc's Cameron Forged Products Co unit for 16.5 million new Wyman-Gordon common shares and $5 million in cash, in a transaction valued at about $107 million.
