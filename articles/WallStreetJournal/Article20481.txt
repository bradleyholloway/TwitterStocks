 A Tale of Three Cities   By Carney, Brian M, The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 2010  There is something in the national character of these three countries to be found in their responses to the threat of national insolvency -- even if the threat is further off in Britain and the U.S. The American response -- to double down on government's obligations -- reflects the national sense that the future's possibilities are still limitless, and that the U.S. can grow its way out of this problem as it's risen above others in the past.
