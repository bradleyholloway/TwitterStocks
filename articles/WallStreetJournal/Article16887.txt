 RBS Is Dodging a Bullet; As the Stock Rises, A 'Rights Offering' Might Do Well   By Cimilluca, Dana; Shah, Neil, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 2008  RBS stock, which had been slumping since the bank announced in April that it would sell shares at a discount to existing holders in the so- called rights issue, dropped as much as 3.8% Monday following news that U.K. mortgage lender Bradford & Bingley PLC took the unusual step of slashing the price of its own planned rights issue to appease nervous underwriters.
