 Food Brief: Tyson Foods Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 2003  Tyson Foods Inc., Springdale, Ark., slashed its fiscal second-quarter and fiscal-year earnings forecast, citing "more difficult operating conditions" across its entire business. The food company said it now expects to break even from ongoing operations in the second quarter ending March 29, down from its previously forecast earnings of 10 cents to 14 cents a share.
