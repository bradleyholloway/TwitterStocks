 Morgenstern on Film -- Review: 'Julie & Julia' Is Two Half Loaves --- Streep masters the art of being chef Child; a richly comic Giamatti can't warm 'Cold Souls'   By Morgenstern, Joe, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 2009  Before and after everything else, Nora Ephron's "Julie & Julia" gives us Meryl Streep in a grand comic performance -- a fearless actress playing the fearless Julia Child in post-World War II Paris, where she's in the process of transforming herself from an embassy wife into a world-famous apostle of French cuisine.
