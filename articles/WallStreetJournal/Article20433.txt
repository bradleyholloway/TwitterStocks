 Corporate News: Holidays Start Slowly at Retailers; Deeper Price Cuts Loom   By Zimmerman, Ann, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 2009  Shoppers stayed home for most of November, before venturing out during the Thanksgiving weekend to buy sharply discounted electronics and apparel, executives and analysts said. Since discounts appear to be luring shoppers, retailers are expected to continue and broaden them -- though no one expects a return to the extremes of last year's inventory markdowns.
