 Purolator Posts Profit On Continuing Lines For the Fourth Period   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1985  BASKING RIDGE, N.Y. -- Purolator Courier Corp. posted fourth-quarter earnings from continuing operations compared with a loss in the year-earlier period.  Fourth-quarter earnings from continuing operations were $1.4 million, or 18 cents a share, compared with a loss of $966,000. In the year-earlier quarter, profit of $734,000 from discontinued operations and a gain of $6.9 million from sale of discontinued operations made net $6.6 million, or 88 cents a share. The gain on sale of discontinued operations was related to its armored car operation and a small filter business.
