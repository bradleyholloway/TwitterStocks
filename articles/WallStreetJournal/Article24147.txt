 World News: Israel Freezes Fund Transfer to Palestinians --- Tax Revenue Is Withheld, in Country's Latest Sanction Against Abbas's Government Following U.N. Vote on Statehood   By Mitnick, Joshua, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 2012  Under Israel's economic accord with the Palestinians, Israeli tax authorities collect customs duties on goods entering the Palestinian territories via Israeli-controlled ports and land borders, as well as value-added taxes on goods and services sold in Israel and intended for consumption in the occupied territories.
