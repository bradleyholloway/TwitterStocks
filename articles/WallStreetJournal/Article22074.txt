 Physicians Health Services Inc.: Higher Enrollments lift company earnings by 20%   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 3, 1995  Physicians Health Services Inc of Trumbull CT said its earnings for the second quarter of 1995 gained 20%, to $3.7 million from $3 million in the year-before period. Higher enrollment in the company's medical plans was cited for the rise.
