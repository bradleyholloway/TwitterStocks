 Business Brief -- MCDONALD & CO. INVESTMENTS INC.: Profit More Than Doubles On 56% Jump in Revenue   The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 1998  McDonald & Co Investment Inc's fiscal fourth-quarter earnings for the period ended Mar 27, 1998 more than doubled to $16.1 million, or 87 cents a diluted share, from $7.6 million, or 42 cents a diluted share, a year earlier.
