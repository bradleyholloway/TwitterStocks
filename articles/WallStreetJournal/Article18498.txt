 Abreast of the market: Industrials tumble 35.77 as a rise in interest rates worries investors   By Pettit, Dave, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1993  Stock prices tumbled Nov 3, 1993 as increases in market interest rates spooked investors and caused a broad-based sell-off. The Dow Jones Industrial Average ended at 3661.87, down 35.77.
