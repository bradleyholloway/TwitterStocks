 Corning will take pretax charge of $62 million for third quarter   By Bounds, Wendy, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 1995  Corning Inc, which said profit comparisons for the company as a whole "will remain difficult" over the next few quarters, said it will take a third-quarter pretax charge of $62 million to offset the effect of billing problems in its clinical labs unit. Corning said the charge would push per-share net income for the quarter 30% below the $76.9 million reported in the same quarter a year earlier.
