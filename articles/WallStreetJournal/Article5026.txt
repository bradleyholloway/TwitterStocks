 Entertainment: Bertelsmann goes shopping for film studio   By Rohwedder, Cacilie; Choi, Audrey, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1993  Germany's Bertelsmann AG, the world's second-largest media company, has expressed an interest in investing in a Hollywood production company, though which company has been targeted is uncertain. An executive at Castle Rock Entertainment said Bertelsmann has been in competition with Turner Broadcasting Inc for a large stake in Castle Rock, but Turner appears to be the front-runner in that race. The history of Bertelsmann's investment strategy is examined.
