 Nearly 40% of Advest Brokers Leave After Merrill Acquisition   By Juan, Evelyn, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 2005  Advest, which Merrill bought from AXA SA's AXA Financial Inc. in a deal that closed Friday, has so far lost 200 of its 515 financial advisers, with most of the departures seen as the acquisition date neared, estimated Danny Sarch, president of executive-recruiting firm Leitner Sarch Consultants.
