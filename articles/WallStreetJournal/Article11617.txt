 Azerbaijan Joins Oman, Kazakhstan in Oil Consortium   By Tanner, James, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 1992  The former Soviet republic of Azerbaijan has agreed to become the third founding partner in a consortium, along with Kazakhstan and Oman, that is planning a huge pipeline system to transport oil from the Caspian Sea area. The group will operate as a limited liability company named the Caspian Pipeline Consortium Ltd.
