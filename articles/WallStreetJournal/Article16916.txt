 WEEKEND JOURNAL; The Home Front: Meet the Nouveaux Neighbors; Canadians Now Biggest Foreign Buyers of U.S. Homes; Baguettes, Pate at Parties   By Fletcher, June, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 2008  The largest proportion of foreign buyers of U.S. homes from May 2007 to May 2008 -- 24% -- were Canadian, double the percentage a year earlier, according to a recent report by the National Association of Realtors. In the four years since they bought a two-bedroom condo in Hawaiian Gardens in Lauderdale Lakes, Fla., Rachel and Pierre Valois say the number of fellow French Canadians in their six-building section of the community has grown from a handful to the point where they now exceed the Americans.
