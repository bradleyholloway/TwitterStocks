 Macmillan Buys Stake Of 5.28% in Zondervan   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 13, 1985  Macmillan Inc. said it bought a 5.28% stake in Zondervan Corp., an unprofitable publisher of Bibles and evangelical Christian materials that recently ran into trouble with the Securities and Exchange Commission.  The stake was disclosed in an SEC filing. Macmillan, a publishing, printing and educational services concern, said it holds 213,000 Zondervan common shares, including 23,000 shares purchased Aug. 29 at $12.34 apiece.
