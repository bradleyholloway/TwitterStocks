 Retail Chains' February Sales Top Estimates   By Agins, Teri, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 1992  Big retail chains reported better-than-expected sales gains for the second month in a row, with Feb 1992 sales rising on the average of 7.5%, according to Alex. Brown's index of 16 retailers. PaineWebber's composite of 21 retailers put the month's gains at 9.7% from a year earlier.
