 Investing in Funds: A Quarterly Analysis --- Last Year's Losers --- Alternative-energy funds were among the laggards of 2010   By Arends, Brett, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 2011  Overall, it was a good year for mutual-fund investors, with most stock and bond funds ending in the black. The iShares S&P Global Clean Energy Index exchange-traded fund slumped 27%; Market Vectors Global Alternative Energy, 19%; and PowerShares Global Clean Energy Portfolio, 15%, according to fund tracker Lipper.
