 City News: Man Is Shot to Death After Dropping Off His Mother at Home   By El-Ghobashy, Tamer, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 7, 2010  A 27-year-old Long Island man, who had just dropped off his mother at her home, was shot and killed while driving away from the Suffolk County neighborhood late Sunday, police said.  Charles Wilson was hit by a single bullet on the left side of his body at about 9:40 p.m. while driving on West Lane in Gordon Heights, police said. He tried to drive back to his mother's home on the same block, but crashed into a pick-up truck that was parked in the driveway of another house.
