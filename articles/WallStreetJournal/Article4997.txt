 Apple Computer Takes Initial Step For Debt Offering   By By Thomas Weber, The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 1993  Apple Computer Inc. is taking the first step toward its initial offering in the bond market.  Apple filed for a $500 million shelf offering covering not only debt but common stock and warrants. Despite the breadth of the filing, though, Apple doesn't expect to sell any new stock.  "At this point, we have no plans to issue any new equity," said Bill Slakey, investor relations spokesman for Apple.
