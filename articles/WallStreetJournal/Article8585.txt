 Airline group sets sights on Europe's telephone market   By Pope, Kyle, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1995  After years of focusing on the obscure data-communications business, Paris-based Societe Internationale de Telecommunications Aeronautiques--or SITA Group, as it is better known--is moving to become a formidable force in Europe's corporate phone business. SITA's move will be aided by its data-communications network, already the largest in the world.
