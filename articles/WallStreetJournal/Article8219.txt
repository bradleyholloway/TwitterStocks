 Dublin Is Seeking Piece of the Market for EC-Wide Funds   By Fleming, Charles, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 1992  Dublin Ireland has set aside an area of derelict docklands near the city center for its International Financial Services Center. The city is attracting foreign investment to the site by offering tax incentives and by the enticement of Ireland being a full member of the EC. American International Group, IBM Corp and Morgan Grenfell are three of the firms that accepted the incentives.
