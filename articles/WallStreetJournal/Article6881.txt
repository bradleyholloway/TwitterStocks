 Entertainment & Culture -- Books & Authors: A Writer Ponders His 'Reader' --- Schlink on the war-torn past and the movie version of his bestseller   By Trachtenberg, Jeffrey A, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 2009  Bernhard Schlink, 64 years old, has waited more than a decade for his story about love and betrayal, "The Reader," to be transformed into a film -- rights were originally acquired by Harvey Weinstein and Miramax Films in 1996. A very good friend of mine, the novelist Joyce Hackett, told me that after all the years of moral ambiguity associated with the Bush administration there is heightened interest in moral problems where there is a clear answer.
