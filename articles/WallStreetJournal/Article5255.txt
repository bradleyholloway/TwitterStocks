 Jobless rate dropped to 5.6% during June   By Georges, Christopher, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1995  The Labor Department said the unemployment rate dropped 0.1% in Jun 1995 to 5.6% of the work force, the second consecutive monthly decline since the rate hit 5.8% in April. However, 40,000 jobs were lost in the manufacturing sector.
