 Millions of Playpens Are Being Recalled For Posing Hazard   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 25, 1998  WASHINGTON (AP) -- Millions of playpens are being recalled because they pose a hazard of strangulation for children wearing loose clothing or pacifier strings.  Eight children have died since 1982 when strings around their necks or their clothing got caught on metal rivets sticking out of the playpens, the Consumer Product Safety Commission said yesterday.
