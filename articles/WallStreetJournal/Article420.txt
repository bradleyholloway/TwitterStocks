 MarketWatch Weekend Investor / Machinery Stocks   By Cassidy, Padraic, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 25, 2006  Analysts' views are mixed on the sector overall and Caterpillar in particular. Citigroup rates Caterpillar a "buy," seeing demand for the company's core vehicles, which could keep earnings moving higher for several quarters. But Lehman Brothers has cut its rating on Caterpillar to "equal-weight" from "neutral weight," believing that Caterpillar's earnings cycle has played out.
