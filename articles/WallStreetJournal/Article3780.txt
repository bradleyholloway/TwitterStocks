 Richmond Radiator Co. Plans to Merge Into Rheem Manufacturing   By By a WALL STREET JOURNAL Staff Reporter, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 20, 1955  NEW YORK--A proposed merger of Richmond Radiator Co., Metuchen, N.J., manufacturer of plumbing fixtures, into Rheem Manufacturing Co., big producer of water heat  ers and other home appliances, has been approved by directors of both companies. Stock holders will meet early next year to vote on the plan.
