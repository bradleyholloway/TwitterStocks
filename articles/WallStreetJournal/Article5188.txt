 Domestic auto firms' weekly output plans are about even to '93   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 9, 1994  The 10 US auto makers said they planned to build 249,411 cars and light trucks in the week ending Dec 9, 1994, about 5% lower than the 262,593 made the week before but about even with the 249,230 made in the same period of 1993.
