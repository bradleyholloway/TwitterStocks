 Watch Out For Job Scams   By Glazer, Emily, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 2010  "Folks looking for jobs, maybe even desperate at the time, are more apt to consider these opportunities that might not be legitimate," says Felicia Thompson, a vice president of communications for the Better Business Bureau.The biggest red flag is a request for your sensitive personal information, such as a bank account or Social Security number.
