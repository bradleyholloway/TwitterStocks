 Hoechst's new chief is building fire in giant's belly   By Moore, Stephen D, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 1995  Juergen Dormann, the new CEO of Hoechst AG, is trying to change the image of the pharmaceuticals-and-chemicals company as a plodding giant. People familiar with Hoechst say it is in talks to at least buy a controlling stake in Marion Merrell Dow Inc, which is 71% owned by Dow Chemical Co.
