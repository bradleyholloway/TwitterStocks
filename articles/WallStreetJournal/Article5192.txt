 GOP softens plan to grant line-item veto   By Kuntz, Phil, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 9, 1994  Incoming House Speaker Newt Gingrich (R-GA) indicated Nov 8, 1994 that Republicans now favor giving the president line-item veto authority only temporarily. The "Contract With America" on which Republicans ran called for giving the president permanent authority to veto specific spending items and targeted tax benefits in legislation without having to veto the entire bill.
