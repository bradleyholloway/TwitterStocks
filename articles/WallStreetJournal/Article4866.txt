 Dean Witter Pays Investors Who Had Junk-Fund Losses   By Siconolfi, Michael, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 1992  Dean Witter Reynolds Inc paid a total of $100,000 to several small investors who accused the brokerage firm of misleading them into buying two big junk-bond mutual funds, the Dean Witter High Yield Securities Inc fund and the High Income Advantage Trust II fund.
