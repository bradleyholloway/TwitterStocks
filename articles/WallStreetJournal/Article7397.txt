 Genex Corp. Seeks An Infusion of Capital To Market Products   The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 1985  ROCKVILLE, Md. -- After boosting its manufacturing capacity, Genex Corp. is looking for capital to bring more biotechnology products to market, said J. Leslie Glick, chairman and chief executive officer.  Mr. Glick said the company was talking with a number of prospects, whom he identified only as "major corporations." Meantime, Genex has obtained a $3 million line of credit and expects proceeds of $2 million to $2.5 million from a land sale.
