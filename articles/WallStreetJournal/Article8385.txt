 British new-car sales climbed 7% in July from a year earlier   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 6, 1993  Britain's Society of Motor Manufacturers and Traders announced sales of passenger vehicles, as measured by new-car registrations, rose 7% in Jul 1993 to 35,376, compared with 33,051 a year earlier. The latest result put the total for the first seven months of the year at 874,411, up 9.1% from 801,796 a year earlier. Imports were 55% of the July market, up from 52% a year earlier.
