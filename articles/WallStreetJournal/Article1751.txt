 Software Glitch Mars Nokia's U.S. Re-Entry   By Bensinger, Greg, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 2012  Nokia Corp.'s Lumia 900 smartphone was supposed to help usher in a new era for the once-dominant cellphone giant, but a software glitch could derail the company's splashy re-entry into the U.S. Nokia said a small percentage of the new Lumia smartphones could at times lose their high-speed data connection and that the company would effectively give away the device for a short period.
