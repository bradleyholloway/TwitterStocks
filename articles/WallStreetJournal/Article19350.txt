 Business Brief -- Conectiv Inc.: Telecommunications Unit Plans to Trim 20% of Staff   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 2000  Conectiv Communications, the telecommunications unit of Conectiv Inc., Wilmington, Del., said it plans to eliminate 80 positions, or 20%, of its 400-person work force in an effort to cut costs and make the company more attractive to a potential partner.
