 Phillips-Van Heusen Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1992  Apparel concern Phillips-Van Heusen Corp's fiscal first-quarter net loss narrowed to $123,000 from $880,000 a year earlier. The latest quarter included an accounting charge of $1.9 million compared with a charge of $800,000 in the 1991 quarter.
