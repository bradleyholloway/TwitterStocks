 Quarterly Mutual Funds (A Special Report): Target-Date Funds Regain Some Bragging Rights   By Maxey, Daisy, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 2009  For the Wells Fargo Advantage DJ Target 2010 fund (STNRX), Jim Lauder, lead portfolio manager of the Wells Fargo Advantage DJ Target-Date funds, says he started out with a glide path more conservative than on many other target-date funds, knowing that "someone who is two, three or four years from retirement cannot afford a loss of 20% or 30% right before they hit retirement" and start taking distributions.
