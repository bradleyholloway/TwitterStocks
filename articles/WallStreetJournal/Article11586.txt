 U.S. Car Sales Edged Higher in Late March   By Stertz, Bradley A, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 1992  The Commerce Department reported that North American-built cars sold at a seasonally adjusted annual rate of 6.2 million vehicles in the Mar 21-31, 1992 period, compared with a 6.3 million-car pace a year earlier. The sales pace for all of Mar 1992 was 6 million vehicles.
