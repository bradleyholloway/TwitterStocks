 Bentsen orders probe into Treasury's role in Texaco Haiti case   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1994  Treasury Secretary Lloyd Bentsen has ordered an investigation into how the Office of Foreign Assets Control handled an embargo-violations case against Texaco Inc. The agency has been accused in press reports of dragging its feet following up on a case involving Texaco's shipment of oil to Haiti between 1991 and 1993.
