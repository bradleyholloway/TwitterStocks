 Marketing: M&M/Mars Sweetens Prices with Refund   By Hwang, Suein L, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1992  M&M/Mars Inc announced an aggressive marketing ploy in which consumers receive a $.05 refund for every single and king-size candy wrapper turned in. There is no limit on the promotion, which runs until Oct 15, 1992. Candy analysts say the move is an attempt to gain ground on competitor Hershey Foods Corp.
