 Newhall Says Profit and Revenue Rose in Fourth quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 1992  Newhall Land & Farming Co expects to record that fourth-quarter net income grew to about $16 million to $17 million on revenue of $62 million to $64 million. The results would compare with net income of $10.2 million in the year-earlier fourth quarter.
