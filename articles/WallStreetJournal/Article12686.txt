 Albertson's Net Rises By 3.9%, Exceeding Estimates by a Penny   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 2001  Albertson's, which operates more than 2,500 retail stores in 36 states, said total sales in the quarter increased to $9.3 billion from $9 billion, a year earlier. Sales at stores open at least a year rose 1.4%, the company said. "Our cost structure is still far too high," Larry Johnston, Albertson's recently appointed chairman and chief executive officer, said, adding that the chain hopes for a reduction of $250 million in distribution and other expenses.
