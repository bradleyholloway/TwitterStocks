 Letters to the Editor: The Oil Is There; Let's Get It   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 2000  As George Melloan notes in his Sept. 26 Global View, Al Gore's "dipping into the strategic reserve will not solve the U.S. oil supply problem," especially when it has been the Clinton-Gore administration that has contributed to the supply problem by restricting domestic exploration, particularly in the Arctic National Wildlife Refuge (ANWR) of Alaska. The U.S. Geological Survey says the ANWR contains as many as 16 billion barrels of oil, a sum that could replace all the oil from Saudi Arabia for the next 30 years.
