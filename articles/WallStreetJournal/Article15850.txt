 AlliedSignal, Honeywell to Sell Avionics Assets   By By Karen Jacobs, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 1999  The agreement in principle with the U.S. Department of Justice is expected to be finalized over the next couple of weeks as the parties work out the timing of the sales, said Tom Crane, an AlliedSignal spokesman. The companies said the divestitures represent only about 1% of the merged company's overall revenue, or about $250 million.
