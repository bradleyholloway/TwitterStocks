 Globalink Inc.: Loss expected for quarter; Shares set 52-week low   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 1995  Globalink Inc said it estimates a 1995 third-quarter loss of about $1.3 million, due in part to sales below expectations. The supplier of language-translation equipment announced its estimates after the markets closed, but even before the news, shares of Globalink fell $1.25, or 13%, to $8.25 a share in AMEX trading on Oct 4, a new 52-week low.
