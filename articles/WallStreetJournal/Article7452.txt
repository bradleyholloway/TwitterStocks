 Chrysler, Mitsubishi Agree On Details of Joint Venture   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1985  Chrysler Corp. and Mitsubishi Motors Corp. of Japan have agreed on the details of a joint venture to build small cars in the U.S.  Lee Iacocca, chairman of Chrysler, and Toyoo Tate, president of Mitsubishi, were expected to jointly announce the project this morning at Chrysler headquarters in Highland Park, Mich.
