 Big Companies Go to Washington to Fight Regulations on Fancy Derivatives   By Scannell, Kara, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 2009  Companies from Caterpillar Inc. and Boeing Co. to 3M Co. are pushing back on proposals to regulate the over-the-counter derivatives market, where companies can make private deals to hedge against sudden moves in commodity prices or interest rates.
