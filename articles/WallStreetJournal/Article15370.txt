 Dollar eases amid some profit-taking, clings to above its key support levels   By Brinsley, John H, Jr, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 1995  The dollar finished lower in quiet but choppy trading on Oct 4, 1995. In late afternoon New York trading, the dollar was quoted at 1.4347 marks and 101.03 yen, down from DM1.4375 and Y101.38.
