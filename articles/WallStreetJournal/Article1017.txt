 Tip of the Week: Save Money At Tax Time   By Ransom, Diana, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 2006  Getting a handle on your deductible spending is worthwhile even if your financial situation isn't terribly complex. The more disorganized you are, the more likely you are to miss a deduction or misplace a receipt that you may still need if the Internal Revenue Service ever comes knocking. For a list of key information and documents you shouldn't forget to bring to your tax preparer, find the "Tax Prep Checklist" at H&R Block's site. Go to hrblock.com and scroll halfway down the page, on the right side below "Use our handy tools. . ."
