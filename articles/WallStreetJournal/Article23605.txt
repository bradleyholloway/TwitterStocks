 U.K. Voters Deal a Blow To Labour, PM Brown   By MacDonald, Alistair; Douglas, Jason, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 2008  Labour won 24% of votes cast Thursday in elections for city, town and village councils in parts of England and Wales, putting it in third place behind the Conservatives with 44% and the Liberal Democrats, a party with little power in a country dominated by the two major parties, with 25%, according to polling firm Ipsos MORI.
