 Finanical Services Brief -- Ping An Bank Ltd.: New Headquarters in Shanghai Marks Formal Relaunch   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 2005  Ping An Bank Ltd., a joint venture between a Chinese insurer and London-based HSBC Holdings PLC, said it opened a new headquarters in Shanghai. The move marks the formal launch of the revamped financial institution, which is 73% owned by Ping An Insurance (Group) Co. of China Ltd.
