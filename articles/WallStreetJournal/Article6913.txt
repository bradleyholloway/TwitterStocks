 Short Selling: NYSE Bearish Bets Rose in Late March   By Martinez, Alejandro J, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 2009  Last September, when the SEC imposed a ban on short selling on some financial companies, it also added disclosure rules that required short sellers to confidentially provide a weekly list of short positions.Marketwide, the short ratio, or the number of days' average volume represented by outstanding short positions, fell to 3.1 days from 3.2 days at the Nasdaq in mid March.
