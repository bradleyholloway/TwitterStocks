 Avant, I-Many, SeeBeyond Gain In Tech-Stock Surge on Russell   By By Larry Bauman, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 2001  The overall Nasdaq market also continued to rally, with the Nasdaq Composite Index posting sharper gains than the [Russell] 2000.
