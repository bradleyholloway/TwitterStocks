 Charitable Contributions In U.S. Rose 10.4% in '89   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 1990  Contributions by individuals rose 11.7% to more than $96.4 billion. Corporate contributions rose 4.2% to $5 billion. Contributions by foundations rose 8.9% to $6.7 billion while bequests of estates were flat at $6.57 billion.
