 Texscan Corp. Is Sued For Alleged Statements About Fiscal '84 Profit   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1985  PHOENIX, Ariz. -- Texscan Corp. said a lawsuit alleging that it issued false and misleading public statements about its net income for the first three quarters of its fiscal year ended April 30, 1984, was filed in federal district court in New York.
