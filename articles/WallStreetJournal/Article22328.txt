 American Electric, DuPont Unit Plan Joint Electricity Ventures   By By Allanna Sullivan, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 1997  Taking advantage of opportunities being opened by electricity deregulation, DuPont Co.'s Conoco oil unit and utility American Electric Power Co. agreed to set up two joint ventures aimed at meeting power needs of heavy industrial users.  AEP Conoco Energy Capital, the first joint-venture company, will buy the power plants of various manufacturing companies, including steel, refined-oil, petrochemical and paper producers. After upgrades and other refinements made by the joint venture, the power plants will be leased back to the companies as part of a 17-year contract.
