 Business Brief -- Capital Environmental Resource: Florida Operations to Expand Through $149 Million Purchase   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 25, 2003  Capital Environmental Resource Inc. agreed to buy Florida Recycling Services Inc. in a cash-and-stock transaction valued at about $149 million. The acquisition, which is expected to close by Jan. 31, is the Canadian solid-waste-services company's second in Florida this month.
