 Heard on The Links   By Anonymous, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 2012  If golf had been part of the London Olympics, as it will be at the 2016 Games in Rio de Janeiro, only two countries would have sent more than two male players: the U.S. and the U.K. Under the scheme most likely to be in effect for Rio, all players in the top 15 of the world rankings are automatically eligible, with a limit of four top-15 players from any one country.
