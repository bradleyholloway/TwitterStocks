 Calgene Picks Director Stacey as President, Chief Operating Officer   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1992  Roderick N. Stacey, one of Calgene Inc's directors, was appointed to the posts of president and chief operating officer. Stacey fills the positions that were vacated by Zachary Wochok, who left the agricultural biotechnology concern to pursue other interests.
