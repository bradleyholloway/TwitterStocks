 Tobacco industry is sued by unions for health outlays   By Geyelin, Milo, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 1997  A growing number of health-and-benefit funds for construction trade unions are suing the tobacco industry to recover their health-care outlays for treating sick smokers. The latest lawsuit was filed Jul 1, 1997 in federal court in Hartford CT by a fund for the Connecticut Pipe Trades and the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers, Local 19.
