 World News: Oil Supplies Will Tighten And Prices Jump, IEA Warns   By Neil King Jr. and Spencer Swartz, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 2008  The world faces mounting uncertainty and escalating costs on the energy front in the years ahead, as companies scramble to find new pockets of oil and squeeze more production from aging fields, the International Energy Agency says in a largely gloomy annual report.
