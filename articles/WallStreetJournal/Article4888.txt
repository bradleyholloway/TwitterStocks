 Business Brief -- Noxso Corp.: W.R. Grace Increases Stake In the Company to 17.9%   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1992  Noxso said Grace purchased 151,386 newly issued shares for $170,839 under an option agreement that will be in effect through 1993. According to the company, Grace currently holds 1,286,164 Noxso common shares.
