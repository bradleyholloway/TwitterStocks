 Tokyo stocks firm on late buying spree; Prices in London end little changed ahead of holiday   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 9, 1993  Tokyo's Nikkei 225-stock index gained 138.04 to 19967.27, spurred by a late buying spree. London's Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-issue index slipped 0.3 to 2821.8, while Frankfurt's DAX 30-share index gained 5.42 to 1655.73.
