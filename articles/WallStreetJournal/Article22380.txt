 Owens-Illinois Inc. Considers Debt Issue To Aid Acquisition   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1998  TOLEDO, Ohio -- Owens-Illinois Inc. is considering issuing around $1.5 billion in publicly traded junk-rated debt to finance its planned $3.6 billion acquisition of the world-wide glass and plastic-packaging business of Britain's BTR PLC.  In other market action, Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp. analyst Skip Carpenter said he raised his investment rating of Owens-Illinois to buy from market perform based on the company's acquisition plan. Merrill Lynch also upgraded Owens-Illinois to near-term buy from near-term accumulate.
