 Business Brief -- Mobley Environmental Services Inc.: Company Reports a Net Loss Of $884,000 for 1st Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 1993  Mobley Environmental Services Inc. reported a first-quarter net loss of $884,000, or 11 cents a share, but said, "We are now moving toward a break-even situation in the second quarter and further gains in each succeeding quarter."
