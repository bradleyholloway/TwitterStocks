 Postal Service, DHL Expand an Alliance For Delivery Services   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 2000  The expanded service includes guaranteed two-business-day delivery between about 20,000 retail postal outlets, or almost 60% of the 34,000 U.S. postal locations, and almost any address in more than 200 foreign countries and territories. The alliance began early last year in 11 major U.S. cities and 18 foreign countries, and had grown to reach about 10,000 U.S. post offices as of June.
