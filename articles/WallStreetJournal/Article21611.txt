 Firms' Optimism for Sales, Profits in 4th Period Slips   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 1991  A Dun & Bradstreet Corp survey of national business expectations for the fourth quarter led to a drop in the sales optimism index to 46, down from 48 in the third quarter. Profit expectations for the fourth period fell to 40 from 47.
