 The Cain Bonfire   By Anonymous, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 2011  The last few days witnessed the spectacle of Mr. Cain's campaign first blaming the liberal media for reporting years-old claims of sexual harassment, then denying the claims as "baseless," then acknowledging that he might have known something about the settlements reached by the National Restaurant Association where he was in charge. Mr. Cain's remarkable success so far, riding to the top of the GOP preference polls, is a testament to his skills at communicating and his willingness to offer bold policy proposals.
