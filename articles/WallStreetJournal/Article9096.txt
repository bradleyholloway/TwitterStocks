 New Policy to Require Marketers to Disclose Private-Data Sharing   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 1999  NEW YORK (AP) -- The Direct Marketing Association will require all of its members doing business in the U.S. to disclose to consumers when they are sharing their private information with other marketers.  The new policy, which was expected to be announced today, is intended to prevent unwanted solicitations from reaching consumers and to protect consumers' privacy.
