 Grand Wilshire's Li Gets Prison Sentence Totaling 5 1/2 Years   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 1991  A federal judge here sentenced Bruce Li, former executive vice president and chief financial officer of Grand Wilshire Group, to 5 1/2 years in prison and a $150,000 fine for his role in defrauding numerous lenders of more than $50 million.
