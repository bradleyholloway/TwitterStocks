 World wire: Japanese executives glum   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1994  A survey of 100 Japanese companies' top officials by the Kyodo News Service found more than 80% of the executives expect economic growth of less than 1% in calendar 1994. A recovery was predicted by 61% of the respondents for the second half and by 22% for the 1995 first half.
