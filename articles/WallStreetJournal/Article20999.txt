 C.H. Butcher Jr. Indicted For Tax-Fraud Conspiracy   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 1986  The indictment came three days after Messrs. Butcher and Steiner were acquitted on 25 fraud charges connected to the collapse of Southern Industrial Banking Corp. in 1983. Mr. Butcher was chairman and chief executive officer of the uninsured thrift; Mr. Steiner was its president.
