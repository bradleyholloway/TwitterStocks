 SEC moves back schedule for new Nasdaq rules   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1997  The SEC slowed down the phase-in schedule for its new order-handling rules for the Nasdaq Stock Market. The SEC said it eliminated the Feb 7, 1997 deadline for the next 100 stocks scheduled to come under the new rules. Instead, 50 of those stocks will be phased in on Feb 10 and the other 50, on Feb 24.
