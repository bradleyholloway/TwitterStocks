 Microsoft Faces Suit Alleging Violations Of West Virginia Laws   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 2001  Separately, in the European Union's antitrust case against Microsoft, competitors will have until Jan. 7 to respond to Microsoft's decision to withdraw from planned hearings in the case. In a response to European antitrust charges last month, Microsoft argued that the company's proposed settlement with the Justice Department should be accepted in Europe as well. In the proposed settlement, Microsoft agreed to disclose some details of how its many software products interact with the monopoly Windows operating system.
