 Fourth Financial Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1992  Fourth Financial Corp said it expects to report second-quarter net income exceeded analysts' estimates of $.41 to $.46 a share by "10% or a little better." In the 1991 second quarter, Fourth Financial had net of $6 million, or $.32 a share. Chairman Darrell Knudson said he expects the company to exceed analysts' projections of net of $2 a share for the year. The company had 1991 net of $23.1 million.
