 International Brief -- Ing. C. Olivetti & Co.: Italy Allows 2,800 Layoffs, Rejects a Retirement Plan   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 1990  Italy decided to allow Ing. C. Olivetti & Co. to lay off 2,800 employees starting Jan. 1, but rejected a request by the financially troubled office automation-equipment maker for a special early retirement program for 5,000 workers.
