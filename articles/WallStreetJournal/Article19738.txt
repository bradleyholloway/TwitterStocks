 Ligand Pharmaceuticals, Nabi Climb   By Schreiber, Cynthia, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 2004  "Biotech and drug stocks have been on a tear," said Matt Ruane, director of U.S. equities and sales trading at Credit Lyonnais Securities. But anticipation of tomorrow's payrolls report held stocks in check overall, Mr. Ruane said.  Large-cap Toys "R" Us led gainers in the specialty retailing group after announcing the sale of some stores. Among small issues, Hollywood Entertainment jumped 47 cents, or 4.2%, to 11.73, Pep Boys- Manny Moe & Jack (NYSE) gained 45 cents, or 1.9%, to 23.98, and Cost Plus rose 50 cents, or 1.3%, to 39.43.  Giant Industries (NYSE) tumbled 4.04, or 17%, to 19.60, after Friedman Billings Ramsey downgraded the stock to "underperform" from "outperform," citing the stock's surge this year.
