 U.S. delegations send mixed signals to China over renewal of trade status   By Chen, Kathy, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1994  The split in Washington over renewal of Most Favored Nation trading status for China was underscored by the simultaneous visits to Beijing of US Commerce Undersecretary Jeffrey Garten and Undersecretary of State John Shattuck. Garten promoted US exports while Shattuck demanded that China meet certain human rights conditions to retain its MFN status.
