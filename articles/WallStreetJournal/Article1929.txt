 Dow Jones President Ray Shaw, 54, Will Take Early Retirement March 31   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 21, 1988  Dow Jones & Co., publisher of The Wall Street Journal, announced that Ray Shaw, president and chief operating officer, has decided to take early retirement next March 31.  Warren H. Phillips, 62, chairman and chief executive, will assume Mr. Shaw's duties and serve as chairman and president after Mr. Shaw's retirement, the company said. Mr. Phillips had held both titles prior to Mr. Shaw's appointment as president in 1979.
