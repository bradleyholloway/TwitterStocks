 International Corporate Report: Montedison Bid for Bi-Invest Approved by Both Concerns   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 1985  In a share-and-cash transaction valued at more than $134 million, Montedison, a chemical concern, agreed to increase its stake in Bi-Invest, a financial holding company, to at least 66.8% from 36.8% currently.
