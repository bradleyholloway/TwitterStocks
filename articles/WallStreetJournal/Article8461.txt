 Tourism in Florida takes a hard jolt in wake of assaults   By Thomas, Emory Jr, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1994  The number of tourists to Florida fell 7.6% in Dec 1993 from a year earlier, according to figures released by the state Department of Commerce. However, the state's $32 billion travel industry still showed a gain of 1.2% for all of 1993. The string of highly publicized attacks on foreign visitors and surging competition hit the travel industry harder than expected.
