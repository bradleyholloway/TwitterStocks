 Doubts persist on Mexico's rescue plan   By Carroll, Paul P; Torres, Craig, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 1995  Mexican Finance Minister Guillermo Ortiz Martinez faces the urgent task of persuading mutual funds and other investors to put aside their anger about Mexico's mishandling of the peso's devaluation and to roll over billions of dollars of government debt that will mature soon. Investors' persistent doubts about the country's rescue plan are discussed.
