 REVIEW --- Report On the Recent Piggybanking Crisis   By Hague, Eric, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 2011  The following report, prepared by a bipartisan committee comprising both Mommy and Daddy, lays out a timeline of the principal events of last week's Piggybanking Crisis and its aftermath. 4:15 p.m., last Friday Daughter Georgia becomes aware that Addison Murphy from next door is placing a recently refurbished dollhouse on the market at an asking price of $30. Because Georgia barely earns enough from her weekly chores to eke out the occasional candy purchase, she has never really considered herself a candidate for doll-homeownership.
