 Prudential Securities' reserves for suits over partnerships near $500 million   By Siconolfi, Michael; Steinmetz, Greg, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1993  The Prudential Insurance Co of America's Prudential Securities Inc unit set aside $125 million in 1992 for legal costs for lawsuits over its soured limited-partnership business, bringing the total reserves so far to about $500 million. Another $80 million to $100 million is likely to be set aside in 1993.
