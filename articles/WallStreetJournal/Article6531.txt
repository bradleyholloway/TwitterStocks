 Korean Tobacco Company Rejects Proposals by Icahn-Led Group   By Laura Santini and Lina Yoon, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 2006  KT&G was thrust into the spotlight Feb. 3, when a group of funds led by Mr. [Carl Icahn] disclosed they had accumulated a 6.6% stake in KT&G and intended to push for changes at the company. Mr. Icahn and his partner, U.S. hedge fund Steel Partners LP, want KT&G to dispose of its ginseng business, which generates a small portion of its overall revenue. The group also is pressing KT&G to divest itself of laggard property assets.
