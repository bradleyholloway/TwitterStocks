 Sunrise Technologies Expects Another Loss On Distributor's Woes   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 1992  Sunrise said the distributor, American Dental Laser Inc., has placed no orders for Sunrise laser products so far this year and is in violation of several distribution agreements. As a result, Sunrise will post a second-quarter loss in addition to a previously announced loss for the first quarter, Sunrise Chairman Arthur Vassiliadis said. He declined to specify the size of either loss.
