 Corporate News: Brooks Details Cameron Ties   By Sonne, Paul; Bryan-Low, Cassell, The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 2012  The former chief executive of News Corp.'s U.K. newspaper unit described a close relationship with British Prime Minister David Cameron that included expressions of support when she was under fire, frequent text messages and, at times, discussions of issues regarded as sensitive for both the company and the government.
