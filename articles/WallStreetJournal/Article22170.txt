 World-wide: Serbia's president   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1996  Serbian President Slobodan Milosevic declined to guarantee the arrests of two leading war-crimes suspects, US Secretary of State Warren Christopher said after holding talks with him in Geneva. Christopher announced that NATO peacekeepers will expand their efforts to arrest Radovan Karadzic and Ratko Mladic, both Bosnian Serbs.
