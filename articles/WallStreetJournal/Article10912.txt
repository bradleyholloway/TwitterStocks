 Pan Am Corp. Posts Loss of $152.4 Million For Second Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 6, 1986  Pan Am Corp., parent of Pan American World Airways, posted a second-quarter net loss of $152.4 million, more than double its $72 million loss for the year-earlier quarter.  Pan Am said it was hit hard by a drop in transatlantic travel because of concerns over terrorism and the Chernobyl nuclear accident.
