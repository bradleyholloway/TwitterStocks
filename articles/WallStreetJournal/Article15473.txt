 Fakes and flakes: Investigators follow a trail of shampoo to some dry ends   By Narisetti, Raju, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 1996  Counterfeiting costs US companies nearly $200 billion in annual sales of genuine brands. Procter & Gamble Co has tracked fake Head & Shoulders dandruff shampoo across two countries and is still stumped over the whereabouts of the mastermind of the shampoo caper.
