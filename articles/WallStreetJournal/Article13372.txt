 Dementia Tied To Loneliness In the Elderly   By Jennifer Corbett Dooren, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 2007  Overall, those who ranked the highest on a loneliness scale were twice as likely to develop the type of dementia associated with Alzheimer's disease. Dementia refers to people who have lost memory, have trouble thinking and caring for themselves. Alzheimer's disease is the most common cause of dementia.
