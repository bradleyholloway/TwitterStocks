 Dole Food Expects 1992 Profit to Slip on Charges and Weak Banana Prices   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1992  Dole Food Co expects net income for 1992 to decline to $15 million, compared with $133.7 million a year earlier. The decline reflects a $50 million charge for accounting changes, a $27 million charge for a sweeping cost-cutting program and weak banana prices.
