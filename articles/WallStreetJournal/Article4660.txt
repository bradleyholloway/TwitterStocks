 ConAgra Says U.S. Asked for More Data on Beatrice Purchase   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1990  OMAHA, Neb. -- ConAgra Inc. said federal antitrust regulators requested additional information about the company's planned $1.34 billion cash and stock purchase of Beatrice Co.  ConAgra said the request, under Hart-Scott-Rodino regulations, extends the waiting period for completion of the transaction until 20 days after the Federal Trade Commission's request has been fully met.
