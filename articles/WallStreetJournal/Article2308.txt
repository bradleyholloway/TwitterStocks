 Bread Prices Rising as U.S. Ships Wheat to Ex-Soviet States   By Gibson, Richard; Kilman, Scott, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 20, 1991  Continental Baking Co, the largest US bakery and a unit of Ralston Purina Co, has increased wholesale prices, a move that is expected to be followed by competitors. The higher prices come as exporters rush to ship millions of bushels of grain to Russia and other former Soviet republics.
