 Productivity Made Recovery in 2nd Quarter   By Ferguson, Rodney, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 1990  Paced by an increase in the manufacturing sector, the productivity of US businesses rebounded in the second quarter from a disappointing first quarter, according to the Labor Department. Productivity increased at an annual rate of 1.9%, after falling at a 1.5% rate in the first quarter.
