 Mobil Unit to Buy a Big Development Northeast of Dallas   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1991  A unit of Mobil Corp, the Mobil Land Development Corp, has agreed to buy Stonebridge Ranch, one of the largest properties in the government's portfolio of real estate inherited from defunct thrifts. The ranch is a residential development located northeast of Dallas. A previously announced sale of Stonebridge to a Japanese investor fell through.
