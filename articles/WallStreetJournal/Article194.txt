 Tokyo stocks follow the dollar higher; Prices spurt up in London, rise broadly in Frankfurt   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 25, 1994  A rising dollar helped Japanese stocks Aug 24, 1994 and the Nikkei 225-stock index advanced 130.82 points to 20511.60. London's Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-share index rose 30.1 to 3205.2, while Frankfurt's DAX 30-issue average advanced 18.50 to 2126.37.
