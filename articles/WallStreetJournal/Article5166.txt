 Investors react coolly to Conseco's plan to fund acquisition   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 1994  Although analysts were upbeat, investors reacted warily to Conseco Inc's latest plan to finance its $2.68 billion acquisition of Kemper Corp with an offering for an estimated $325 million of convertible debt. Kemper's stock dropped $1.375 to $58.125 in NYSE trading, reflecting continued concern about Conseco's ability to complete the $67-a-share acquisition.
