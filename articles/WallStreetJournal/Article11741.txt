 Marketing: 'See the movie! Read these other books.'   By Cox, Meg, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 1993  The two authors hogging the paperback bestseller list in the summer of 1993, John Grisham and Michael Crichton, wrote titles adapted for current movies. Crichton's novel "Jurassic Park" inspired the summer's megahit film, while Grisham's "The Firm" is being published in paperback with the movie poster on the cover.
