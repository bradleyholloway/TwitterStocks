 Hiram Walker Posts Fiscal 3rd-Period Loss, Cites Takeover Fight   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 1986  Hiram Walker Resources Ltd. reported a loss of $4 million (Canadian) for the third quarter ended June 30, in part reflecting costs of $35 million incurred fighting a takeover bid from Olympia & York Developments Ltd.'s Gulf Canada Corp. unit.  Calgary-based Gulf Canada in April acquired 69% of Hiram, a distilled spirits, oil and gas, and natural gas distribution company, for $3 billion. Gulf Canada plans to merge with Hiram Walker later this year. Olympia & York is a real estate developer owned by the Reichmann family of Toronto.
