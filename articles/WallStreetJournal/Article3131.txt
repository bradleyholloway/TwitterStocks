 Business Brief -- RAYTHEON CO.: Net Income Plummets 95% With a 29% Revenue Gain   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 21, 1998  Raytheon Co's fiscal third-quarter profit fell 95% amid weakened demand overseas for its engineering services. The company posted net income of $11 million, or 3 cents a diluted share, for the quarter ended Sep 27, 1998, compared with year-earlier net income of $211 million, or 88 cents a diluted share.
