 Larrabee Is Convicted Of Securities Fraud In Bank of Boston Case   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 24, 1999  A former employee of a law firm that represented Bank of Boston Corp. was convicted of nine counts of securities fraud for tipping a stockbroker in 1995 that Bank of Boston was about to acquire BayBanks Inc.  Following a six-day trial, John C. Larrabee, who was director of the fiduciary services department of the Boston law firm of Bingham Dana LLP, was found guilty by a federal jury of insider trading.
