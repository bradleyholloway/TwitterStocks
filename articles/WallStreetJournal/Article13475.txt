 Eyes on the Road: Auto Makers Wrestle With Fantasy and Reality Ahead of Detroit Show Online edition  By White, Joseph B, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2008  Detroit newspapers and some automotive enthusiast magazines have cleared space for detailed exploded view renderings of the car's hand- built, supercharged engine, accompanied by awestruck commentary about the beauty of its parts. Reality is $100-a- barrel oil, the new energy bill's demand that car makers boost to 35 miles per gallon the average fuel efficiency of their fleets and an increasingly clear shift in consumer buying patterns toward smaller vehicles.
