 Saab-Scania AB   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 1993  Saab-Scania AB's Saab Cars USA Inc unveiled a Gold Visa credit card that it will offer jointly with MBNA American Bank of Newark DE. Saab's Visa, unlike other credit cards issued by auto makers, won't offer a rebate on the purchase or lease of a Saab vehicle.
