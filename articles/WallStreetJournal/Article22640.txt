 For Home Sales, A Seller's Market   By By Hilary Sargent, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 2000  Massachusetts posted the biggest fourth-quarter gain, 9.4%. In Connecticut, home prices rose at a rate of 6.6%, more than three times faster than the rate the previous year, when the median price rose only 1.9%. New Hampshire home prices jumped 3% in the quarter. They were up 4.2% in Vermont, 3.7% in Rhode Island and 4.3% in Maine.
