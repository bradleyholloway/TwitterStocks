 MCI WorldCom Says CompuServe to Assist New Internet Service   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1999  MCI Communications was forced to sell its Internet assets in 1998 to Cable & Wireless PLC to receive regulatory approval for its WorldCom merger. MCI WorldCom offers Internet access to its midsize-to-large business customers.  MCI WorldCom, as expected, will use the Internet backbone run by its UUNet Technologies unit to provide access to the Internet. The Jackson, Miss., company said CompuServe will provide the content for its main home page. The Web site will include customized features for news, weather and frequent-flier plans.
