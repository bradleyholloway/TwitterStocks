 Dollar Expected to Drop in Near Term on U.S. Economy, Germany's High Rates   By Cumberbatch, Candace, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 1992  Predictions are that the dollar's fortunes will soon deteriorate, as a result of mixed economic signals in the US and high interest rates in Germany. On Apr 3, 1992 the dollar was trading at 1.6265 marks, down from the previous day's DM1.6440, and at 133.40 yen, also a decline from Y133.73.
