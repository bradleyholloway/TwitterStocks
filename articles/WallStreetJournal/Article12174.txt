 Risk-management services beefed up by Ernst & Young   By McGee, Suzanne; MacDonald, Elizabeth, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1997  As demand for professional risk-management services escalate, Ernst & Young LLP has taken a step toward beefing up its own menu by hiring all the prinicipals of a well-known boutique operation. Robert Baldoni and the five other principals of Emcor Financial Risk Management Consulting left Emcor Eurocurrency Management Corp the week of Feb 3, 1997 to join the Big Six accounting firm.
