 Plessey to Join Consortium Led By U.S. Firms   By By Richard L. Hudson, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 1986  Plessey Co., a British electronics company, has agreed to join the group led by Boeing Co. and Westinghouse Electric Corp. that is bidding to supply early-warning aircraft to the British government, a Plessey source said.  The agreement, to be formally announced by Plessey and Westinghouse tomorrow at a London news conference, also calls for Plessey to collaborate with Westinghouse on future development of airborne radar systems of the type used in early-warning aircraft. In Pittsburgh, Westinghouse confirmed that Plessey has agreed to join the group.
