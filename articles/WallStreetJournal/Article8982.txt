 Options on Internet Companies Receive Boost From Speculation About Ties to Media Firms   By By Gaston F. Ceron, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 1998  A number of Internet stocks traded sharply higher yesterday, taking bullish call options along for the ride. Short squeezes and speculation about combinations between Web-navigation and media companies were mentioned as likely culprits for the recent rise in share prices of the Internet companies, certainly not a new phenomenom in this market. Positive news from the sector -- such as a stock split announced by Lycos Inc. and a sharp sequential increase in Egghead.com Inc.'s Internet auction revenue -- probably helped as well.
