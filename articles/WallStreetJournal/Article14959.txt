 Pinault S.A.: French Concern's '91 Profit Dropped 52% to $90.9 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 1992  Pinault SA, a big French distributor, said that consolidated net earnings after payments to minority interests fell to 503 million francs ($90.9 million), a 52% drop from the year before's FFr1.05 billion figure.
