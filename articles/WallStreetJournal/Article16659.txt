 Politics & Economics: Strong Growth Rate Expected in Germany   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 2006  DIW, a Berlin think tank, forecast real gross domestic product growth of 1.8% for this year and 1.4% for 2007. The forecast for this year is in line with the prediction in April made by Germany's top six economic research institutes, while DIW's forecast for 2007 is higher than the institutes' joint forecast of 1.2%.
