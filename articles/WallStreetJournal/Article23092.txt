 Letters to the Editor: Our Immigration Laws Have Not Been Enforced   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 2004  The answer is: The federal government has not been enforcing our immigration laws. So it is no wonder that illegal immigration has increased. Illegal aliens know that once they get past the border, most are unlikely ever to be deported. The loud message broadcast to the world is: Get in illegally and we'll make it easy for you to stay.
