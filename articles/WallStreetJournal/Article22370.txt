 Genesco Pulls Out Of Western Boots; Shares Advance 9.3%   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 1998  NASHVILLE, Tenn. -- Genesco Inc. said it would pull out of the Western-boot business and take an $18 million one-time charge. The market for Western boots has been weak since the early 1990s. Genesco had a loss of about $4 million in its boot business last year, analysts said.
