 Business Brief -- E.On AG: German Utility Concern Sells Stake in Union Fenosa of Spain; Dow Jones Newswires   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 21, 2004  E.On AG said it sold its 4.99% stake in Spanish utility Union Fenosa SA for 217 million euros ($268 million), ending months of speculation about E.On's plans for the stake.
