 ACE Doesn't Plan to Increase Its Bid For Tempest, Despite IPC's Higher Offer   By By Leslie Scism, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 1996  ACE Ltd., a Bermuda-based business insurer, said it doesn't plan to sweeten its $810.5 million proposal to acquire Tempest Reinsurance Co., despite a rival $943 million offer from another Bermuda insurer, IPC Holdings Ltd.  ACE's chairman, Brian Duperreault said he is "convinced that the ACE/Tempest combination is superior for Tempest shareholders," despite its lower face value, because of "a number of contingencies and uncertainties" surrounding IPC's unsolicited proposal, which was announced Tuesday.
