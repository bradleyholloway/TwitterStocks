 $20 Billion Fight Over Auction Notes   By Rappaport, Liz, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 2, 2010  Abercrombie & Fitch has $230 million, or 33% of its cash on hand, tied up in the long-term securities, bought from several banks, including UBS AG and Bank of America Corp. "If we had more cash, we'd be running different [business] models, with more stores and more inventory," said Abercrombie & Fitch treasurer Everett Gallagher.
