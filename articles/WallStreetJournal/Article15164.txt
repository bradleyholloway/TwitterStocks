 Advertising: Quaker State taps Chiat   By Cooper, Helene, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 1994  Quaker State Corp fired Grey Advertising from its $15 million to $20 million motor-oil account and consolidated the bulk of its advertising with Chiat/Day of Venice CA, which has created ads for the company's MinitLube chain since 1989.
