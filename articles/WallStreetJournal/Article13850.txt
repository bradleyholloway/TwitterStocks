 Nintendo Ready to Take Off With New 3-D Game Player   By Ward, Sandra, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 2011  Some bullish observers see its stock and American depositary receipts rising about 25% from recent levels of 23,000 yen and about $35 a share for the ADRs as a result of strong demand for the portable console, which will be priced at about $250 in the U.S. Nintendo forecasts four million unit sales in the roughly one-month period from the launch date to the March 31 end of its fiscal year.
