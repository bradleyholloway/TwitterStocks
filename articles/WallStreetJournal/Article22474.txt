 Letters to the Editor: Teachers Aren't Doing Their Job   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1998  The Outlook column "Who Will Teach Johnny to Read?" Nov. 9 does not do justice to a fundamental problem in American education. Simply, grade and high schools must teach the "Three Rs" of reading, writing and arithmetic. No discussion. They obviously do not. Therefore, a discussion of remedial teaching after high school preposterously absolves teachers who do not do their job.
