 Alltel Urges RIM to Settle BlackBerry Patent Dispute   By Sharma, Amol, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 2006  Several other large U.S. operators have made contact with RIM over the matter, people familiar with the situation say. U.S. operators jointly serve nearly four million BlackBerry users, making the U.S. RIM's largest single market, analysts say.  For months, U.S. wireless-service providers that market and sell BlackBerry devices to their subscribers have been reluctant to comment on RIM's legal woes, not wanting to interfere in the business affairs of a key partner and influence the outcome of RIM's legal fight.
