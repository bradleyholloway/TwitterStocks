 TMX in Talks to Buy Direct Edge --- Move Into U.S. Underscores Canadian Bourse's Global Push   By Dummett, Ben; Bunge, Jacob; Patterson, Scott, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 2012  Direct Edge was launched in 2005 by Knight Capital Group Inc. as an electronic-trading system designed to provide a platform for a fast-expanding breed of computer-driven traders and to compete with exchange operators such as the Nasdaq OMX Group Inc. The trading network was spun off in 2007, garnering investors such as Citadel LLC, a Chicago hedge fund, and Goldman Sachs Group Inc. The firm in January confirmed it was exploring "capital market alternatives," which William O'Brien, its chief executive, said could include an initial public offering.
