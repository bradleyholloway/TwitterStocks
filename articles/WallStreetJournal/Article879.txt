 Martin Marietta Units Win Defense Work For $225.8 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 13, 1987  Units of Martin Marietta Corp. won contracts totaling $225.8 million for Titan IV space-launch vehicles for the Air Force and munitions for the Army.  Avondale Industries Inc. was awarded a $100.6 million Navy contract for a fleet-replenishment oiler.  Pennsylvania Shipbuilding Co. also won a $95 million Navy contract for a fleet-replenishment oiler.
