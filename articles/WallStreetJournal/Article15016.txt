 Prudential Unit Gets Lower Finance Rating; Hurricane Losses Cited   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 1992  A. M. Best Co lowered the financial rating of Prudential Property & Casualty Group, a unit of Prudential Insurance Co of America, citing the magnitude of losses from Hurricane Andrew and exposure to further losses in Florida. Best also confirmed the A-plus rating of ITT Hartford Insurance Group, the insurance unit of ITT Corp.
