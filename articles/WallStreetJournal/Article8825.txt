 Doubletree Corp.: Earnings in fourth quarter rose sharply to $5.5 million   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1997  Hotel franchise Doubletree Corp said its fourth-quarter net income nearly doubled to $5.5 million, or 16 cents a share, from $2.8 million, or 12 cents a share, a year earlier. The result was below a consensus estimate of five analysts surveyed by First Call Corp of 21 cents a share.
