 Advertising: Dr Pepper wraps ads in stars and stripes   By Goldman, Kevin, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1993  In a new TV advertising campaign scheduled to begin on Oct 5, 1993, Dr Pepper invokes heart-tugging images of small-town America and even Old Glory itself to nudge its image closer to the American mainstream. Young & Rubicam produced the commercials.
