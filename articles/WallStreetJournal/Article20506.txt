 Ranking States By Obesity Rates   By Maya Jackson Randall, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 2010  (The National Institutes of Health has an online BMI calculator at nhlbisupport.com/bmi.) The U.S. Surgeon General in 2001 called for public-health action to help fight obesity and a federal initiative set a goal of reducing obesity rates to 15% of the adult population, a target that hasn't been met in any state.
