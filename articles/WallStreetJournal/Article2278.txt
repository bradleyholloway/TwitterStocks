 Investing in Indonesia   By Borsuk, Richard, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 20, 1991  The risks and rewards of investing in Indonesia are discussed. Investors must be patient and prepare themselves for risks. The complexities of doing business in Indonesia are illustrated by the experiences of Royal Dutch/Shell, Scott Paper Co, Freeport-McMoRan Copper & Gold Co and Teijin Ltd.
