 Program Trading   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 1998  The first two periods occurred Monday when the industrial average moved more than 50 points. The following firms reported making program trades between 11 a.m.-12:30 p.m.: BNP Securities, Credit Suisse First Boston, Salomon Smith Barney (mostly selling), Deutsche Bank Securities and Lehman Brothers. The following firms reported making program trades between 2:15-3:45 p.m.: BNP Securities, Salomon Smith Barney (mostly selling), Deutsche Bank Securities (mostly selling), Credit Suisse First Boston and Lehman Brothers.  Three periods occurred Tuesday when the industrial average moved more than 50 points. The following firms reported making program trades between 9:30-10:30 a.m.: BNP Securities, Donaldson Lufkin & Jenrette, Interactive Brokers (selling), Credit Suisse First Boston and Thomas Williams. The following firms reported making program trades between 12:30-2 p.m.: BNP Securities, Morgan Stanley Dean Witter, Credit Suisse First Boston, W&D Securities and Deutsche Bank Securities. The following firms reported making program trades between 2:45-3:45 p.m.: BNP Securities, Credit Suisse First Boston, Salomon Smith Barney, Deutsche Bank Securities and Goldman Sachs (mostly buying).
