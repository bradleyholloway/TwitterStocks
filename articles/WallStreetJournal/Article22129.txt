 Motorola pager sales show strong growth in new India market   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 2, 1996  Motorola Inc's fledgling paging business in India is showing strong initial success, according to a company official. He said sales during the first nine months of marketing in India are better than the first year in China. Motorola's pagers make up some 80% of the 24 million pagers used in China.
