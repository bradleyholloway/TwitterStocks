 Media Brief -- Lagardere Groupe: EU Opens Four-Month Probe Of Vivendi Unit Takeover   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 2003  The European Union Commission intensified its inquiry into Lagardere Groupe's 1.25 billion euro ($1.46 billion) takeover of Vivendi Universal's publishing business, opening a full-scale four-month investigation. The commission delayed a decision about whether France could vet the deal itself.
