 Two Ex-Executives Of Enron Charged In a Fraud Scheme   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 1989  Two former executives of Enron Corp.'s oil-trading subsidiary, Enron Oil Corp., were charged with defrauding the parent through a series of scam oil-trading contracts.  In U.S. District Court in White Plains, N.Y., Mr. Borget, 51 years old, was charged with one count of conspiring to defraud Enron, based in Houston, and the Internal Revenue Service; one count of aiding in the filing of a false corporate tax return; and one count of filing a false personal tax return. Mr. Mastroeni, 45, was charged with one count of conspiracy to defraud and one count of filing a false personal tax return.
