 In rural India, video vans sell toothpaste and shampoo   By Jordan, Miriam, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 1996  Four years of free-market reforms and strong agricultural output are cracking open the mammoth market in India for everything from toiletries to TV sets. Consumer giant Colgate-Palmolive Co has deployed vans to the rural areas that provide infomercials, which advertise Colgate Palmolive products.
