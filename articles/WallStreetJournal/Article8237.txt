 Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co.: Tire Production at a Plant Will Be Increased by 10%   The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 1992  In an expansion that will cost $8 million, Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co will increase tire production at its Lawton OK plant by 10% over the next year, with most of the growth focused on its Eagle high-performance passenger tires.
