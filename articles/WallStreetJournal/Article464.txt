 Drugstore.com: Charge of $2.5 Million Coming for Fiscal 2007   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 25, 2008  Drugstore.com Inc. will record a pretax charge of $2.5 million, or three cents a share, for the fiscal year ended Dec. 30, as a result of a recent tax-court decision in New Jersey, the company said in a Securities and Exchange Commission filing.
