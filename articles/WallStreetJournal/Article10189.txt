 Family-Style Fantasy Sports Father-son pairs are discovering that fantasy sports can forge powerful bonds and teach children important lessons about business. Online edition  By Difino, Nando, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 2008  Best of all, despite his father's dire warnings ("You're throwing your money away!"), the youngster outwitted the grown-ups and rode New England's record-setting offense to the top spot, finishing the season as the league champion. FamilyFantasySports.com also puts some juice into the action -- the owner of the fantasy team that scores the most points in their league's championship football game this season will win a $25,000 deposit in a 529 account for their child's college education.
