 France's AGF says profit increased 49% to $140.6 million   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 20, 1996  French insurer Assurances Generales de France, privatized earlier in 1996, said its first half profit rose 49% to FFr723 million ($140.6 million) due to improved non-life insurance activities and a rise in operations outside of France.
