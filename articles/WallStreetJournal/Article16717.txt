 WEEKEND JOURNAL; The Home Front: House of the Week / Lakefront House, Landscaping Included   By Casselman, Ben, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 2007  Due Diligence: To build this lakefront home as close to the water as possible, Anthony Musca, who runs a commercial-real-estate firm, and his wife, Molly, spent a year tearing out a 60-foot cliff and hauling away items that had been discarded on the lot. (After paying $525,000 for it, they spent twice that much preparing the lot for construction.) The house, completed in 1999, maximizes lake views with floor-to-ceiling windows and big patios, one with a barbecue area with running water....
