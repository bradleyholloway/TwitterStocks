 Business Brief -- Cobalt Group Inc.: Purchase by Warburg Pincus Will Take Company Private   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 2001  Cobalt Group Inc. said it will be acquired by Warburg Pincus Equity Partners LP, a closely held New York equity investment firm and its largest shareholder, for $35 million to become a private company. Cobalt shares surged 73%, or $1.43, to $3.38 at 4 p.m. on the Nasdaq Stock Market.
