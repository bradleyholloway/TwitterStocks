 Well, It Depends What You Mean By `Article'...   By By Sara Kehaulani Goo, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1999  So what is Mr. [Francis X.] Joyce accused of doing? According to a letter Board Chairwoman Gloria C. Larson wrote to Mr. Joyce and quoted at a board meeting last week, Mr. Joyce will face charges at a closed-door meeting tomorrow that he "exceeded his authority as an employee of the Authority" by publishing an opinion article in the Boston Herald without consent from the board. In the article, Mr. Joyce suggested the board consider investing in a hotel that was part of a convention-center project.
