 Card Issuers Aren't Dealing Out Low Rates   By Asinof, Lynn, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1991  Despite all the talk of a credit crunch, most credit-card issuers are still aggressively seeking to get their cards into the hands of more and more people. But the card issuers' battle for market share doesn't mean credit-card customers are getting a better deal.
