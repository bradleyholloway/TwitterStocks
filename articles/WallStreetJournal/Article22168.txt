 Japan appears to offer the U.S. concession on chips   By Hamilton, David P, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1996  In negotiations with the US over the renewal of a 1991 semiconductor trade agreement, Japan offered an apparent compromise. Electronic Industry Association Chairman Norio Ohga, who is chairman of Sony Corp, told reporters the association would be willing to accept an interim agreement extending to 1999.
