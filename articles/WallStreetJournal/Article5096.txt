 Nixon upsets Yeltsin by meeting first with two of Russian president's foes   By Rosett, Claudia, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 1994  Russian President Boris Yeltsin canceled his plans to meet with former US President Richard Nixon, citing Nixon's meetings on Mar 7 and 8, 1994 with former Russian Vice President Alexander Rutskoi and Communist Party chief Gennady Zyuganov, two foes of Yeltsin.
