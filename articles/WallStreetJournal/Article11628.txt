 Officers of General Development Corp. Convicted of Fraud   By Pearl, Daniel, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 6, 1992  A Miami federal jury convicted four former officers of General Development Corp on fraud-related charges for the company's sale of 12,000 Florida homes at allegedly inflated prices. The four ex-officers are Robert Ehrling, Tore DeBella, David Brown and Richard Reizen.
