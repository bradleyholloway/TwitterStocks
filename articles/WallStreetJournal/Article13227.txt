 Westin Hotels to Ban Smoking, Charge Violators   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 2005  Eight Westin hotels already are smoke-free, and at least 5% of the rooms at the others had been set aside for nonsmokers, Ms. [Sue Brush] said. But market research found that 92% of Westin's guests were requesting nonsmoking rooms, and some of those who couldn't get them were "quite upset," she said.
