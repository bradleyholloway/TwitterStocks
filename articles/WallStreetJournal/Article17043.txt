 World News: BP Lobbied U.K. on Benefits of Libya Prisoner-Transfer Deal   By Swartz, Spencer; MacDonald, Alistair, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 2009  Mark Allen, a special adviser to BP who played a prominent role as a chief U.K. negotiator in talks that led to Libya's renunciation of its nuclear weapons program in 2003, called U.K. Justice Secretary Jack Straw in October 2007 to discuss the slow progress on a transfer deal, a BP spokeswoman said Friday.
