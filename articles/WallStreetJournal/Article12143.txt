 Business & race: Hispanic TV viewers pick popular shows   By Wynter, Leon E, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 1996  Hispanics shared 10 of their top 20 TV shows with the overall US market in the 1995-96 season, compared with four shows in the previous season. The prime reason for the change: the feverish marketing of NBC's top-rated Thursday night lineup.
