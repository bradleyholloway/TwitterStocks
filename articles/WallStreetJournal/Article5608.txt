 Polaroid's Earnings For Period and Year To Trail Projections   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1998  CAMBRIDGE, Mass. -- Polaroid Corp. said second-quarter and full-year earnings will fall below analysts' expectations because of steps it is taking to reduce inventories in retail and wholesale channels.  The instant-camera maker said it expects second-quarter net income to be between 55 cents and 60 cents a share, down from year-ago earnings of $34.5 million, or 76 cents a share, on sales of $564.9 million. Analysts surveyed by First Call had forecast net income this quarter would be flat with last year.
