 Manager's journal: The power of negative thinking   By Feinberg, Mortimer, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1994  Mortimer Feinberg offers advice to companies seeking to prevent themselves from getting too comfortable. He says steps that senior management can take to foster a fire in the belly include banishing titles, practicing pessimism, getting back to basics, providing punishments as well as perks and instituting reapplications.
