 Corporate News: Verdict Likely Soon In Verizon-Cox Case   By Sharma, Amol, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 2008  A jury verdict is expected as early as Monday in Verizon Communications Inc.'s patent infringement case against cable provider Cox Communications Inc., people familiar with the situation say, the latest in a series of moves the telecom giant has made to assert its intellectual property in Internet telephony.
