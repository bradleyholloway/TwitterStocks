 Two Concerns to Change Names   The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 1998  Among recent stock-listing changes, Chicago Miniature Lamp Inc, traded on the Nasdaq Stock Market, is changing its name to SLI Inc and is now trading on the NYSE with new symbol SLI. Pittston Burlington Group, trading on the NYSE, is changing its name to Pittston BAX Group, using the same symbol PZX.
