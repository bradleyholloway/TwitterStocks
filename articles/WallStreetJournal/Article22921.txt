 American Electric Says SEC Is Seeking Data on Wash Trades   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 2002  AEP has reviewed its trading activities from Jan. 1, 1999, through March 31 of this year, a period that included more than 1.2 million trades. The company found domestic transactions involving sequential trades with the same terms and counterparties amounted to about 0.25% of trading transactions. The contribution of those transactions to revenue isn't material, the company has said.
