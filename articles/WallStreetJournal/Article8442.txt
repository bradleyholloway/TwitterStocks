 Delta Air lines: Petition is filed with DOT on London route decision   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 1994  Delta Air Lines has filed a petition with the federal appeals court in Washington, challenging a decision by the DOT to give AMR Corp's American Airlines two new routes to London. The petition followed a DOT move in Nov 1993 to select Nashville TN and Raleigh-Durham NC over Salt Lake City, where Delta had proposed establishing one of the routes at its westernmost hub.
