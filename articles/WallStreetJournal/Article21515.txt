 Bush Move to Boost Firepower in Gulf May Push Yearly Cost to Over $25 Billion   By Pasztor, Andy, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1990  President Bush's decision in Nov 1990 boosting US firepower in the Persian Gulf is likely to raise the annual cost of Operation Desert Shield to more than $25 billion from $15 billion, according to Pentagon officials and congressional aides.
