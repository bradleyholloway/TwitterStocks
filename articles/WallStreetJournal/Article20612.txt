 Residents of Old Mining Town Want Gold to Stay in Them Thar Hills --- In a Nevada Historic District, Tourism Prospectors Don't Dig Plans for a Real Open Pit   By Carlton, Jim, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 2011  Mr. Toll says he and others don't want an open-pit mine in the Virginia City Historic District -- a designated national landmark that also includes nearby towns such as Silver City -- because it would desecrate a historic mining area. The outskirts of Virginia City are filled with remnants of bottles and other artifacts used by the old miners -- objects that open-pit mining could potentially wipe out, they claim.
