 Pillsbury's Doughboy Aims Punch at Sunshine Biscuits   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 1991  Pillsbury Co, a unit of Grand Metropolitan PLC, filed suit against Sunshine Biscuits Inc, the GF Industries Inc unit, claiming infringement of the firm's Pillsbury Doughboy trademark and citing similarity with Sunshine Biscuits' "Drox" character.
