 Personal Technology: New Spreadsheets Are Turning a Page toward Easier Use   By Mossberg, Walter S, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1992  Walter S. Mossberg reviews Borland International's Quattro Pro spreadsheet for Windows. Quattro Pro possesses most of the user-friendly features of Microsoft's Excel 4.0 spreadsheet, however, it adds some unique features that make the software even easier to use.
