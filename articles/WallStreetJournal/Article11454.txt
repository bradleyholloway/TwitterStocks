 New Securities Issues [1]  The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1991  Municipal Assistance Corp. (New York) -- $138.7 million of Series A bonds, due 1992-2003 and 2008, priced by a Goldman Sachs & Co. group to yield from 5.30% in 1992 to 6.90% in 2008. The bonds are rated single-A-1 by Moody's and single-A by Standard & Poor's Corp.  Health and Educational Facilities Authority (Wisconsin) -- $65 million revenue refunding bonds, Series 1991 A, due Aug 15, 1991-2003, and 2009, and revenue bonds, Series 1991 B, due Aug. 15, 1993-2003, 2011 and 2020, tentatively priced by a Goldman Sachs group to yield from 5.15% in 1991 to 6.94% in 2020. Series A consists of $21.465 million bonds. Series B consists of $43.54 million bonds. The bonds are AMBAC-insured and rated triple-A-1 by Moody's and triple-A by S&P.
