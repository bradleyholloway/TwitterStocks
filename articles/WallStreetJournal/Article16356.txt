 Industrial Brief -- U.S. Steel Corp.: Pension-Fund Contribution For 2003 Totaled $75 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 2004  U.S. Steel Corp. expects to report a fourth-quarter pretax gain of $55 million, which is intended to represent the excess of the fair value of timber-cutting rights that the company contributed to its pension fund. In late December, U.S. Steel voluntarily contributed $59 million in timber-cutting rights to its defined pension plan.
