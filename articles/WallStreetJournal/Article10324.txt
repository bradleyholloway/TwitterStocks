 Once-Robust Polygon Is Forced to Adjust   By Bryan-Low, Cassell, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 2009  Hedge-fund head Reade Griffith became one of the highest-profile managers here by picking battles with corporate boards of companies from a nuclear power generator to a telecom firm. Though investors are returning to hedge funds, the industry continued to see more cash exit than arrive for the first half of this year, according to Hedge Fund Research Inc. Managers like Mr. Griffith who imposed lengthy bans on withdrawals could face a particularly tough time attracting money.
