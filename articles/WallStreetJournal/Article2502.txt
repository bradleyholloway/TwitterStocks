 Ginsburg vows cautious interpretation of constitutional rights if confirmed   By Barrett, Paul M, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1993  On the first day of the Senate Judiciary Committee's confirmation hearings on Ruth Bader Ginsburg, Judge Ginsburg vowed that, if confirmed as a Supreme Court Judge, she would interpret constitutional rights cautiously, despite urging from liberal Democrats to be expansive in her interpretation.
