 Advertising: Another AT&T review   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 25, 1994  AT&T Co, three months after reshuffling about $200 million in TV and print advertising, is conducting a review for direct marketing of AT&T consumer services. Contenders include Saatchi & Saatchi Direct, Lowe Direct, FCB Direct and Ogilvy & Mather Direct.
