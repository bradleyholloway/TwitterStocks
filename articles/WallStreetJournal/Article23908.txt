 Pepsi Juices Up in Russia --- Beverage Giant Leapfrogs Rivals With Deal to Buy Drinks Maker for $5.4 Billion   By Chazan, Guy; Cimilluca, Dana; McKay, Betsy, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 2010  PepsiCo Inc. is buying Russian dairy products and fruit-juice maker OAO Wimm-Bill-Dann in a deal valuing the company at $5.4 billion, marking one of the biggest foreign investments in Russia outside the energy sector.
