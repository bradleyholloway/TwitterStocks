 Heard on the street: Small investors angered by growth of after-hours profits reports   By Browning, E S, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 21, 1997  IBM Corp has joined a growing list of companies that report earnings after-hours. But many small investors are angered by after-hours reports, saying the purported efforts to level the playing field actually give professionals even more time to trade ahead of them.
