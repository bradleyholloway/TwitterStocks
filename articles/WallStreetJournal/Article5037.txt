 That little bundle of joy will cost you a bundle   By Asinof, Lynn, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1993  The cost of children to their parents during their first 18 years of life is discussed. For a family making at least $52,000 a year, the USDA estimates that a child born in 1992 will cost its parents $180,690 by age 18.
