 German Parliament Moves Closer to Approving Maastricht Treaty   By Gumbel, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 9, 1992  The German Parliament, apparently undeterred by strong doubts among citizens and unmoved by controversy elsewhere in Europe, is pushing ahead with ratification of the EC's Maastricht treaty. The only dissent in the Bundestag came from a handful of former East German communists.
