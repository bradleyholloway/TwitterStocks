 Deutsche Post, Exel Are In Talks on Deal Valued at $6.4 Billion   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 2, 2005  Exel shares rose 17% to GBP 11.79 in a strong U.K. market. Shares of Deutsche Post, the German postal carrier and logistics giant, fell 3.9% to 19.65 euros in Frankfurt on concerns it might overpay.  Merrill Lynch, together with UBS AG, is now advising Exel in its talks with Deutsche Post. Morgan Stanley is advising Deutsche Post.
