 Corporate News: Philip Morris Pushes Smokeless --- Firm Wants FDA to Position Tobacco Product as Safer Alternative to Cigarettes   By Kesmodel, David, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2010  Philip Morris and U.S. Smokeless Tobacco Co., both units of Altria Group Inc., said in a letter to the FDA late last month that a regulatory framework that takes into account the "continuum of risk" of different tobacco products could "have a significant public health benefit."
