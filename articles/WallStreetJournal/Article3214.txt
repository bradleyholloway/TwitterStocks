 Environmentalists Plan Suit Against Timber Panels   By By Janet I. Tu, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1999  Seattle's Earthjustice Legal Defense Fund -- on behalf of the Sierra Club, the Bellingham-based Northwest Ecosystem Alliance and four other organizations -- have submitted intent-to-sue notices. The federal lawsuit they say they plan to file in Seattle will argue that the makeup of two panels that counsel the administration on forest-products trade violates the Federal Advisory Committee Act.
