 Africans for Drug Patents   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 2003  What's more, President [Yoweri Museveni] argued that future drug innovation depends on the incentive effects that patents provide. He told us of a tribal proverb: "You can't be so hungry as to eat the seeds." He said that any rules on patent exemptions should be limited to cases of real health emergency, and not enable people to reap commercial benefits from other people's "pioneering" work.
