 Letters to the Editor: Fed Rate Nitwittery   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 2002  Give me a break. Reducing the Fed rate as Mr. [Angell] proposes may or may not increase pricing power for big business, but it would certainly reduce to practically zero the already unconscionably low interest rates paid on saving accounts and certificates of deposit needed by many senior citizens to augment Social Security.
