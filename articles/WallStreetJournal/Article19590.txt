 Business Brief -- Boston Properties Inc.: Global Holdings to Buy Building In New York for $370.1 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 2002  Boston Properties Inc. said it has signed a contract to sell 875 Third Ave. in New York for $370.1 million to Global Holdings Inc. Situated in Manhattan's Midtown, the building totals about 719,000 square feet.
