 Chris-Craft Decides Warner Must Spin Off Stake in BHC Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 1989  The disposition of Warner's shares in BHC, Chris-Craft's principal broadcasting subsidiary, was one of the principal issues in a lawsuit Chris-Craft filed against Warner in Delaware chancery court. Chris-Craft, which owns 57.5% of BHC, had several other options to choose from, including buying the stake back from Warner or having Warner sell the shares in a public offering. Chris-Craft is also Warner's biggest shareholder.
