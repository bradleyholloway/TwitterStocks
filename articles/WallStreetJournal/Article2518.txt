 NBD Bancorp's Fisher to quit 3 top posts; Istock, Jeffs named   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1993  NBD Bancorp President, Chairman and CEO Charles T. Fisher III said he will retire Dec 31, 1993. Fisher will be succeeded by Verne G. Istock as chairman and CEO, and Thomas H. Jeffs, a vice chairman, will assume the duties of president and COO.
