 Technology Brief -- Redback Networks Inc.: Chapter 11 Filing Is Made With Plan to Eliminate Debt   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 2003  Redback Networks Inc. filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy-court protection with a prepackaged reorganization plan that calls for the elimination of substantially all of its debt. The telecommunications- equipment maker said the move is the concluding step in a financial restructuring first announced in July.
