 Times Mirror tries to seal Netscape gain in 'Peps' sale   By Rose, Frederick, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1996  Times Mirror Co, seeking to lock in a gain from an early investment in highflying Netscape Communications Corp, said it will sell innovative new securities tied to Netscape stock. Times Mirror will offer as many as 1.5 million premium equity participating securities, or Peps, in a public sale.
