 SmartMoney: Is Your Home in a Bubble?   By Nancy Nall Derringer, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 2004  To sort through those local economies, SmartMoney's editors turned to a unique index, the Local Market Monitor, which looks at housing costs market by market. Publisher Ingo Winzer creates the index by compiling statistics on salaries, home prices, population trends and new-home building. Most important, he compares local incomes with house prices. When salaries and prices stay roughly in line, he calls a market fairly valued.
