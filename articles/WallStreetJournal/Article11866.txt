 Pier 1 Imports July sales rose   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 1994  Pier 1 Imports Inc said sales totaled $58.5 million in the four weeks ended Jul 30, 1994, up 2.9% from $56.9 million a year earlier. Sales at stores open at least a year increased 2.6%. The figures did not include sales for approximately 50 stores scheduled for closing this year.
