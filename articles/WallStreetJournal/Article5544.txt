 FCC Urged to Lower Local Carrier Charges For Long Distance   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1997  "If the FCC will follow the wisdom of this filing . . . consumers and business users will save $8 billion to $10 billion in long-distance costs, and local rates will be driven down to cost," said Gene Kimmelman, co-director of Consumers Union. "We will finally start seeing the fruits of competition." The petition was filed by the Consumer Federation of America, the International Communications Association and the National Retail Federation. Consumers Union said it supports the filing.
