 Bond prices end a bit weaker as market digests economic reports, Greenspan's confirmation   By Scheer, Steven; Zunitch, Victoria M, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 1996  Bond prices closed marginally weaker on Jun 20, 1996, responding mainly to additional indications of fairly strong economic activity, but taking them in stride. In late trading, the price of the 30-year Treasury bond was down 1/32, to 86 9/32, to yield 7.10%, unchanged from Jun 19.
