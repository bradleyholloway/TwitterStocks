 Ashton-Tate Posts A $5.6 Million Loss For Fourth Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 1991  TORRANCE, Calif. -- Ashton-Tate Corp. said it had an operating profit for the fourth quarter compared with a third-quarter operating loss, but that a charge from shareholder litigation resulted in a $5.6 million net loss for the fourth period.  The software company cited a 12% increase from the third quarter in sales of its dBASE IV database program for personal computers. As reported, Ashton-Tate began shipping the new version of its flagship dBASE line last July 31.
