 Options Report: Blue Chips Intel, Oracle and AOL Are Active As Traders Seek Ways to Profit in Nasdaq Rise   By By Steven M. Sears, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1999  The mood in the rest of the market was noncommittal, an attitude justified late in the day as the Dow Jones Industrial Average and Standard & Poor's 500-stock index relinquished earlier gains and Nasdaq fell from its highs though it closed in positive territory for the session. The option market's fear gauge, the Chicago Board Options Exchange Market Volatility Index, or VIX, gained 1.71 to 23.94.
