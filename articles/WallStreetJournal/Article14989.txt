 Collor Scrambles for His Career, Causing Split in Brazil's Cabinet   By Kamm, Thomas, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 1992  Brazilian President Fernando Collor de Mello is trying to avert near-certain impeachment by pressuring his government to fund programs dear to those allies whose votes in the country's Congress can stop the proceedings. Collor's efforts have resulted in a crisis within his cabinet and fears for the economy.
