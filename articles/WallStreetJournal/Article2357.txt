 Advertising: Study Shows Clients Jump Ship Quickly   By Lipman, Joanne, The Wall Street Journal,  May 21, 1992  A study by Sanders Consulting Group of Richmond VA found that, in the past five years at the 50 largest advertising agencies, an amazing 67% of clients have parted ways. However, a number of ad executives who responded to the statistics claimed the findings were simplistic. The increasing lack of loyalty in the advertising industry is discussed.
