 Recount Tilts Outcome in Mexico   By David Luhnow and Jose de Cordoba, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 2006  Mexico on Jul 5, 2006 undertook a tense official recount of its contested presidential election that with 91% of the vote counted showed a different outcome than the Jul 2 results, with populist Andres Manuel Lopez Obrador leading conservative candidate Felipe Calderon by a 36.2% margin.
