 Letters to the Editor: Sex Education, With Caution   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 2, 1999  Ms. (Wendy) Shalit says girls don't get advice from YM, but I wonder how much of any one issue she read before she came to her convenient conclusion. She makes a glib reference to one bullet point in our April issue, "get cyber sexy," but doesn't tell readers that this was a tip that encouraged girls to spend more time talking to boys via e-mail about a variety of topics, in the event that boys, in person, make them too nervous.
