 Why Dave Is Now a Goldbug   By Kansas, Dave, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 2010  The scrum surrounding the yellow metal ranges from those who deride it as an archaic investment ("a barbarous relic!") to those who believe it is the essential store of value through the ages (goldbugs). Commodities provide two basic things over time: diversification away from stocks and a hedge against inflation.
