 Hinderliter Industries Urges Vortec Board To Oust Top Officer   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 25, 1988  Vortec Corp.'s board was urged to remove Leslie R. Inglis as chairman, chief executive officer and president by Hinderliter Industries Inc.  Hinderliter, Tulsa, Okla., which holds a 19.5% stake in the compressed-air-products maker, cited Vortec's recently announced $7.7 million loss for 1987 on revenue of $4.6 million. For 1986, Vortec had a loss of $125,635 on revenue of $4.1 million.
