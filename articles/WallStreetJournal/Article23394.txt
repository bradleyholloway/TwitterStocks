 Mining Brief -- Companhia Vale do Rio Doce: Net Rises 12% on Higher Prices For Iron Ore, Output Gains   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 3, 2006  Brazilian mining titan Companhia Vale do Rio Doce, or CVRD, reported a second-quarter net profit of 3.91 billion Brazilian reals ($1.79 billion), up 12% from 3.48 billion reals in the same quarter a year ago on higher iron-ore prices and sales volumes.
