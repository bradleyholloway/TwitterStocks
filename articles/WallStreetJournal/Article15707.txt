 American Industrial Sells Stake of 17% To Cleveland REIT   By By Robert Tomsho, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 1998  American Industrial said Developers, Cleveland, acquired 949,147 newly issued common shares for $14.7 million, or $15.50 a share. Under a separate agreement, Developers swapped five light-industrial properties in Ohio for an additional 1.3 million American Industrial shares. After both transactions, Developers holds 2.25 million of American Industrial's 13.3 common shares outstanding.
