 Northrop's bid is said to lead Grumman list   By Harris, Roy J, Jr, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1994  People close to the matter say Northrop Corp submitted the highest bid for Grumman Corp, making it likely that the Bethpage NY defense contractor will announce an acquisition agreement early Apr 4, 1994. The price was said to be close to the $62 a share that Northrop had conditionally offered.
