 Massey Will Propose Refinancing of Debt To Its Holders in '86   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 1985  The troubled maker of farm equipment made the announcement after its stock was temporarily halted yesterday on the Toronto Stock Exchange because of unusually heavy trading of its Class A and Class B preferred shares. A spokesman for Massey said the refinancing would involve both common and preferred holders, and that investors "jumped the gun" yesterday because "there are important parts that are yet to be negotiated."
