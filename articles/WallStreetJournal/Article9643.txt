 Advert Mailbox   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 2004  A: You have identified one of the greatest marketing battles in modern-day advertising. The fight to lure consumers to choose one Internet intermediary over another is truly fierce, and Time Warner's AOL and Microsoft's MSN have been steady combatants. Some branding consultants suggest AOL's "Running Man" is a hip, cool icon that connects with young males. The critics of Microsoft's "Butterfly" think it is too soothing, and not the type of image associated with a technology powerhouse.
