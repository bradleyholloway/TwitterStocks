 Business Brief -- ICN Pharmaceuticals Inc.: Annual Meeting Is Delayed; Dissident Effort Is Backdrop   The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 1993  ICN Pharmaceuticals Inc. said it is delaying its annual shareholder meeting and hasn't set a new date. The Costa Mesa, Calif., drug company gave no official reason for the postponement of the meeting, which had been set for May 12.
