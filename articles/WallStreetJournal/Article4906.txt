 Equitable, Unable to Secure Credit Line of $600 Million, Cuts the Amount Sought   By Lipin, Steven, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1992  According to bankers familiar with the transaction, concerns about the real estate portfolio of Equitable Life Assurance Society of the US have prevented the nation's fifth-largest life insurer from getting a $600 million credit line. Equitable has cut the amount it is seeking to $400 million.
