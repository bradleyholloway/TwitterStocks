 British banks setting aside up to 40% of outstanding loans to Eurotunnel   By Bray, Nicholas, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 1996  British banks are setting aside reserves to cover as much as about 40% of their outstanding loans to Eurotunnel SA, the Franco-British operator of the channel tunnel, as they prepare for negotiations on the concern's 8 billion pound ($12.4 billion) debt. UK banks are believed to have a combined BP800 million in outstanding Eurotunnel debt.
