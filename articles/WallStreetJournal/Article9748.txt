 U.S. Acquires More Flu Vaccine   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 2004  The government is purchasing as many as five million additional doses of flu vaccine from plants in Canada and Germany to help relieve the U.S. shortage.  Canadian flu shot maker ID Biomedical Corp. has just over one million doses to sell to the U.S., while British drug manufacturer GlaxoSmithKline PLC can provide four million doses from a German plant, HHS Secretary Tommy Thompson said in October.
