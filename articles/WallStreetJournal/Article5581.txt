 J.P. Morgan Cut CEO's Compensation By 6% Last Year to About $3 Million   By By Stephen E. Frank, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 1998  Mr. Warner's salary increased to $691,667 from $600,000, while his cash bonus fell to $2,350,000 from $2,630,000. He also was awarded $2,350,000 in restricted stock which vests in five years. In 1996, Mr. Warner received $2,630,000 in restricted stock that vests after five years, as well as a one-time special restricted stock award of $2,940,000 that vests after 10 years. In 1997, Mr. Warner exercised options on 51,568 shares of stock, realizing a value of $3.6 million.
