 Business Brief -- W.R. Grace & Co.: Bid for Grace Energy Stock To Be Lifted to $19 a Share   The Wall Street Journal,  May 21, 1992  W.R. Grace & Co. said it agreed in principle to sweeten its bid for the 17% of Grace Energy Corp. it doesn't already own, to $19 a share from the $16.50 it initially proposed.
