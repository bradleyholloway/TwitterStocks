 Legal Beat: Negligence Suits Pose a Threat to Firefighters   By Hayes, Arthur S, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 1992  The implications of a recent decision by Massachusetts' highest court, which upheld a jury verdict stating that a fire department was negligent in putting out a fire that destroyed several warehouses in Lowell MA, are discussed. Many fear the decision opens a door for lawsuits against firefighters.
