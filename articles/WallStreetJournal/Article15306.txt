 World-wide: The CIA's ties have been terminated   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 1995  A White House aide said the CIA's ties have been terminated with a Guatemalan army intelligence unit, but the US agency retains other links with Guatemala on matters such as drug control. President Clinton took the step amid a controversy over charges that Guatemalan officers on the CIA's payroll killed US and Guatemalan citizens.
