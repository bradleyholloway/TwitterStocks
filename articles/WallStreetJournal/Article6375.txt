 Marks & Spencer Group PLC: First-Half Profit Drops 40%; Management Shuffle Is Unveiled   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 2004  Marks & Spencer Group PLC posted a 40% drop in fiscal first-half net profit, warned of deteriorating business conditions and unveiled a management shuffle. The retailer said profit slid to GBP 140.1 million ($259.8 million) in the six months ended Oct. 2 from GBP 234.4 million a year earlier.
