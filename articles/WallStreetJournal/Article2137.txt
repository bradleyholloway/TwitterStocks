 International Brief -- Ferranti International PLC: Fiscal 1990 Pretax Loss Of $293.6 Million Posted   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 20, 1990  Ferranti International PLC, a British electronics and engineering concern, posted a pretax loss for the year ended March 31 of #161.7 million ($293.6 million), compared with pretax profit of #13.1 million a year earlier, citing its "most difficult year" in the wake of fraud discovered in September 1989.
