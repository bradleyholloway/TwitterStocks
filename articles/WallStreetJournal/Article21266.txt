 Marketing Brief -- Selkirk Communications Ltd.: Chief Quits in Wake of Bid From Maclean Hunter Ltd.   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 2, 1988  Mr. Meadows, 50 years old, took a temporary leave of absence from Selkirk last month, shortly after Maclean Hunter Ltd. announced an offer to acquire the broadcasting and cable television concern. Donna Soble Kaufman, the chairman of Selkirk, assumed Mr. Meadows's duties, and will succeed him as chief executive, the company said.
