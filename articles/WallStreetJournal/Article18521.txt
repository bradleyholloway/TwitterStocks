 French banks cut base-lending rates to 7.95% in move to spur recovery   By Goldstein, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1994  French commercial banks cut base-lending rates to 7.95% from 8.15% on Jan 3, 1994, partly in response to complaints from top French officials that the benefits of lower interest rates from the central bank haven't been passed on to borrowers.
