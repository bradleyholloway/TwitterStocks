 Mary Kay's Holders Clear Buyout of Firm By Its Management   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1985  Mary Kay Cosmetics Inc. shareholders approved a management buyout led by the company's chairman and founder, Mary Kay Ash.  Analysts valued the buyout at $14.50 to $15 a share, or $304 million to $315 million. The buyout is subject to the management group arranging financing, but the company said the final financing papers were expected to be signed this morning in New York.  Mary Kay officials said Mary Kay Ash and her son, Richard R. Rogers, president, have wanted to take the company private for some time. They have indicated that they don't like the pressure to maintain quarterly profits that goes along with being a publicly traded company.
