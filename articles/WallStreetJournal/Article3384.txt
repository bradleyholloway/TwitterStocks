 Salon Web Site Is Cutting Staff; Official Resigns   By By Sharon Cleary WSJ.com, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 2000  "We're hoping we'll be profitable in the first two quarters," said Michael O'Donnell, Salon.com's chief executive officer and president. Regarding the overall state of online advertising, Mr. O'Donnell said, "Too many sites and too many companies are competing for ad dollars and driving prices down to next to nothing." He added that Salon may consider a subscription model "as the Internet develops."
