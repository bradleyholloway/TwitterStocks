 Business Brief -- AMERICAN GREETINGS CORP.: Wider Loss Is Reported For Fiscal Second Period   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 2000  American Greetings Corp., Cleveland, reported a wider loss for the fiscal second quarter, citing an accounting change that will shift revenue to a later quarter. For the quarter ended Aug. 31, the greeting-card company reported a loss of $35.5 million, or 55 cents a diluted share, compared with a loss of $26.3 million, or 39 cents a diluted share, a year ago.
