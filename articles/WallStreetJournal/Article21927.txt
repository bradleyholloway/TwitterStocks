 Apollo advisors seeks $1 billion of new money   By Jereski, Laura; Pacelle, Mitchell, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1994  Apollo Advisors, the investment firm run by Leon Black, is seeking $1 billion in new money from institutional investors. Apollo's eagerness to raise such a large amount of new cash cannot help but raise questions about its relationship with its longstanding principal source of funds, Credit Lyonnais. Speculation within the industry regarding what the Apollo move may signify is provided.
