 Antitrust Verdict Is Reinstated against Florida Hospital   By Marcus, Amy Dockser; Hagedorn, Ann, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1991  A federal appeals court reinstated a $2.3 million verdict against Venice Hospital, a nonprofit hospital in Venice FL, for violating antitrust laws by encouraging patients and home health care providers to buy from its medical equipment supply business.
