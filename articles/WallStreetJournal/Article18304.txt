 BellSouth Unit and Swatch to Introduce Wristwatch Pager, Joint Marketing Plan   By Carnevale, Mary Lu, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1992  BellSouth Corp's paging unit and Swatch plan to introduce a wristwatch pager and a joint marketing arrangement that could expand the personal pager market. Bell South's MobilComm unit announced the deal with Swiss Corp for Microelectronics & Watchmaking Industries, of which Swatch is a part.
