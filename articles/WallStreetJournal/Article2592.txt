 AMR loss narrowed in first quarter; USAir deficit widened substantially   By Needham, Marjorie; McCarthy, Michael J, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 1994  AMR Corp, parent of American Airlines, and USAir Group Inc both posted first quarter losses. AMR's deficit narrowed to $7 million; USAir's widened to $196.7 million for the period.
