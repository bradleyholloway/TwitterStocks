 Letters to the Editor: A Shocking Result   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1999  In the Dec. 9 article "Groups Push to License Electricians," the Journal reported a proposal from the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers to require that electricians in California be licensed by the state. I'm sure that the intention of this group is a simple restriction in the supply of electricians to raise the price of their services.
