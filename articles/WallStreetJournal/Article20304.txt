 Off the Beaten Track / Kalamazoo, Michigan   By Rundle, Rhonda L, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 2008  Walk over to the library to pick up a free brochure and map of Kalamazoo's Historic Homes, then stroll down tree-lined South Street, past Bronson Park, to view the old mansions along a two-block stretch considered part of the city's architectural heritage.
