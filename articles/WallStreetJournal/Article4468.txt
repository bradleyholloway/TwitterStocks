 Offer of Hong Kong Telecom Shares To Be Widened ---- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 9, 1988  Shares of Hong Kong Telecommunciations Ltd., the colony's telephone monopoly, which are to be sold to the public as part of an offer to local investors, will be increased to 163 million from 130 million, the three underwriters of the Hong Kong offer said.  Cable & Wireless PLC of Britain and the Hong Kong government announced the sale Dec. 1 of a combined maximum of 923 million shares, or about 8% of the total issued share capital, of Hong Kong Telecommunciations to investors in Hong Kong and overseas for 4.55 Hong Kong dollars (58 U.S. cents) a share.
