 U.S., partners agree to invite Russia to join larger space-station project   By Rosewicz, Barbara, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 1993  The US, Europe, Canada and Japan agreed to ask Russia to join them in building an international space station that would merge Russia's future Mir 2 space station with a still-unbuilt orbiting laboratory under design for the past 10 years.
