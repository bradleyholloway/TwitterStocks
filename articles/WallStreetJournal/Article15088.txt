 Artisoft ousts Schoof from top two posts, names Keiper chief   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 1993  After a special meeting of its board, Artisoft Inc said it fired its chairman and CEO, C. John Schoof II, and replaced him with William C. Keiper. Schoof, who founded the company in 1982, was said to be "shocked and surprised" by the action.
