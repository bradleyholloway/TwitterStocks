 Michaels Stores' Sales Rise   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1992  Jan 1992 sales at Michaels Stores Inc were $30.6 million, up 9.3% over the year-ago period. Sales at stores open more than a year rose 7.2% for the month. For the fourth quarter ended Feb 2, sales rose 15% to $144.2 million from $125.5 million a year earlier.
