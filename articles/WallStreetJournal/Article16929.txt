 Indexing & ETFs: Stock Falloff Uncovers ETF Flaws   By Laise, Eleanor, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 2008  The Securities and Exchange Commission's temporary ban on short sales of financial stocks caused troubles for the Short Financials ProShares (SEF), UltraShort Financials ProShares (SKF) and Rydex Inverse 2x S&P Select Sector Financial (RFN) ETFs, three funds designed to benefit when financial shares fall.
