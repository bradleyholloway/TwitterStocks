 Economy shows additional signs of a slowdown   By Georges, Christopher, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1995  Fresh evidence of an economic slowdown surfaced on Apr 3, 1995. The National Association of Purchasing Management said its index fell to 51.4% in Mar 1995 from 54.5% in February. The Commerce Department said consumer spending increased a mere 0.1% in February, the smallest rise in nearly a year.
