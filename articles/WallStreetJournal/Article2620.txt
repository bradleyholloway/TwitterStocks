 Sweden sells Pharmacia shares   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1994  Sweden's Ministry of Industry and Commerce said that it has sold 7.5 million shares of pharmaceutical group Pharmacia AB in addition to the 72 million shares sold in Jun 1994. Of the total 79.5 million Pharmacia shares the state has sold, 47.5 million were acquired by private investors.
