 U.S. Steel, Revising Policies Again, Sets Some Price Boosts   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1985  U.S. Steel Corp., again revising its pricing policies, said it will raise prices $40 a ton for hot-rolled bar and semifinished steel products, effective on Jan. 1.  Many customers doubt, however, that U.S. Steel and other producers will fully receive the price rises recently announced for these and other products.
