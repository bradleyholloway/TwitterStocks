 Pepsi-Cola Pureto Rico posts loss of $45.9 million for its 3rd quarter   By Deogun, Nikhil, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 1996  Pepsi-Cola Puerto Rico Bottling Co reported a net loss of $45.9 million for its fiscal third quarter ended Jun 30, 1996. The company said it does not expect it was profitable in its fiscal fourth quarter ended Sep 30.
