 Public Pension Funds Profit From Wall Street's Job Losses   By Karmin, Craig, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 2009  Public pension funds, many of which continue to hire to fill current positions or in some cases new ones, according to recruiters, fund executives and Web-site job listings. Ash Williams, executive director of the Florida organization that manages state pension funds, worked more than a decade in New York including at the hedge fund Fir Tree Partners; he returned to his job as head of the pension last year.
