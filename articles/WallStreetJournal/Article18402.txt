 Heard on the street: Xilinx's supporters say its stock looks cheap despite recent surge, but short position is big   By Torres, Craig, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1993  Since semiconductor shares were one of the hottest stock sectors in 1992, investors are looking for cheap chip stocks. Many investors are pouring money into Xilinx, hoping to obtain a towering return in 1993. The company's stock, which is currently trading at 25 1/4, has increased 64% since its low for 1992 of 15 3/8 in October.
