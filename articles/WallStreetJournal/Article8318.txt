 Keating convicted on criminal charges stemming from Lincoln S&L scandal   By Stevens, Amy, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 1993  A federal court jury convicted Charles H. Keating Jr on all 73 counts of various racketeering and fraud charges resulting from his association with the failed Lincoln S&L. Keating was previously convicted on state securities-fraud charges and is serving a 10-year sentence. Keating's son, Charles Keating, was also found guilty of all 64 counts applied to him.
