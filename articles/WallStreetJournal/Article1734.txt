 Facebook's Hughes Buys New Republic   By Adams, Russell, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 2012  The group acquired it from Canadian media company CanWest Global Communications Corp. Mr. Hughes, who co-founded Facebook in 2004 with Harvard roommates Mark Zuckerberg and Dustin Moskovitz, said in a statement that he will focus on adapting the magazine "to the newest information technologies while investing in the serious journalism that has made the New Republic what it is today."
