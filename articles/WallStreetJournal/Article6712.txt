 Diversification at Macquarie   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 2007  Macquarie's risk-management processes, as they relate to Macquarie's Fortress products, ensured the offering documents for the Fortress investments fully disclosed the potential risks and rewards associated with leveraging the investments. Additionally, the decision the investment manager recently took to sell loans and reduce leverage -- undertaken to position the portfolio to be able to withstand further price declines should they arise -- was part of the risk-management response to the current volatility in global financial markets.
