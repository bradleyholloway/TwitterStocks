 Barron's Insight: Pogo Might Shed Image As Hard-Luck Energy Stock   By Ablan, Jennifer, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 2005  The company is receiving $820 million for its Thai assets and Duane Grubert, managing director of oil and gas analysis at Fulcrum Global Partners, expects Pogo to use the money to buy domestic energy properties. But the real kicker for Pogo would be a takeover, which Mr. Grubert thinks could be done at $65 a share, at least. Pogo declined to comment. He rattles off a list of possible suitors including Apache (APA), Forest Oil (FST), Houston Exploration (THX), Noble Energy (NBL) and Pioneer Natural Resources (PXD).
