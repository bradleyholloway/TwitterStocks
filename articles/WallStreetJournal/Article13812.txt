 Shell-Shocked Dog of War Finds a Home With the Family of a Fallen Hero --- Jason's Death in Iraq Left Room for a Marine at the Dunhams' House; Gunner Fit the Bill   By Phillips, Michael M, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 2010  SCIO, N.Y. -- Gunner, a bomb-sniffing dog mustered out of the Marines for canine post-traumatic stress disorder, has found a new home with Deb and Dan Dunham, whose Marine son died in Iraq protecting the men beside him. Told to hunt for explosives during trial runs, he'd make a token effort then circle back to his trainer's side, hoping to play fetch.
