 Small stock focus: Nasdaq still trying to find cause of glitch   By Getler, Warren, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1994  Nasdaq officials are breathing easier now that its vast computer-based trading system, which was beset with problems that caused intermittent trading delays the week of Jul 11, 1994, is running smoothly. However, officials do not know what caused the malfunction. In Jul 20, 1994 trading, the Russell 2000 Index slipped 1.61 points to 243.65.
