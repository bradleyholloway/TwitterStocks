 CNN Pays $20 Million For Stake in Service Booking Trips Online   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 1999  CNN News Group, a unit of New York's Time Warner Inc., is taking a $20 million stake in Leisureplanet.com, a Web-based travel-booking concern.  As part of the agreement, Leisureplanet, a subsidiary of Leisureplanet Holdings Ltd., Miami, will provide travel services through most of CNN's Web sites.
