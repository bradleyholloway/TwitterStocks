 Nextel Communications Inc.: Firm closes on acquisition of stake in Mexican concern   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1995  Nextel Communications Inc said it closed a previously announced deal to acquire a stake in a Mexican dispatch operator, under revised terms to reflect "Mexico's current economic uncertainties." Nextel had initially agreed to acquire a 22% stake in Mobilcom SA for $107.5 million. Under the new terms, the cash commitment will decline to $15 million from $107.5 million, and Nextel will convert a $42.5 million loan to Mobilcom into equity.
