 Petro-Canada Offer To Public Is Indicated By Ottawa Official --- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1987  EDMONTON, Alberta -- Canadian energy minister Marcel Masse indicated that the Canadian government has decided to offer shares of Petro-Canada to the public.  "We accept in principle that Petro-Canada should be privatized," Mr. Masse told reporters at an informal news conference.
