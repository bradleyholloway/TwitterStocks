 Remarks on Sale Of France Telecom Provoke Strikers   By Lavin, Douglas, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1995  "It's almost as though the government wanted to help us rally workers to strike," one France Telecom union organizer said. Indeed, the unions are using Mr. [Francois Fillon]'s latest remarks to help call for a France Telecom protest today.  Franck Borotra, minister for industry and telecommunications and Mr. Fillon's superior in the French government, said on French radio that France's tradition of "public service" companies will be maintained and seemed to back away from Mr. Fillon's statements.
