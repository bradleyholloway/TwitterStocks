 National Westminster Names Bernard Horn To Head a Division ---- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 1991  Mr. Horn, 44 years old, succeeds John Tugwell, a NatWest director, who was assigned to the chairman and chief executive post of NatWest Bancorp, the banking group's U.S. arm.  Mr. Tugwell, who had been a director of NatWest Bancorp prior to his latest assignment, continues to report to NatWest Group Chief Executive Tom Frost under the current management arrangement, and remains a director of the parent bank.
