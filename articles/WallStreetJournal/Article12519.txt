 Delphi Automotive To Put $575 Million Into Pension Fund   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2000  Delphi Automotive Systems Corp. said it would contribute $575 million to its U.S. hourly pension fund to reduce future obligations and to drive growth.  The payment will bring the total amount of pension contributions to $1.8 billion since Delphi's separation from General Motors Corp. in May. Delphi said the big upfront payment will lower pension costs by 10% this year, adding $57.5 million to earnings.
