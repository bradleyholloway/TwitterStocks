 McDonald's to Sell Boston Market Chain   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 2007  [McDonald]'s acquired Boston Market in 2000 after the chain was undone by growing too fast. McDonald's agreed to pay $173.5 million, which included debt assumption, for most of Boston Market's assets, including 751 restaurants and franchise rights for another 108. Boston Market currently has 630 locations in 28 states. In addition to its restaurants, Boston Market, Golden, Colo., has a line of foods sold in supermarkets.
