 WEEKEND JOURNAL; Style -- She Shops, He Shops: Lean and Green for Father's Day; Two columnists assess the gamut of thrifty, eco-friendly gifts   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 7, 2008  Carbon Credits: I'm not sure I buy this premise and I don't know what my Dad would make of it, but in the interest of giving him the opportunity to do his part to save the planet, Carbonfund.org's gift cards come with either two tons or 10 tons of carbon offset, an environmental photo on the front, climate-savings text on the back and room for a personal message. Apparel is always a good gift for Dad, but when I tried to buy mine a Ralph Lauren polo shirt for his birthday recently, he took one look at the price tag and tried to leave the store.
