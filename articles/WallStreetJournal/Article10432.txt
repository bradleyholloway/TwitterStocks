 For AIG, a Loss, More 'Progress'   By Ng, Serena, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 2010  AIG said it recently began discussions with its various overseers on a strategy to repay the Federal Reserve Bank of New York and allow the government to exit its nearly 80% ownership stake in AIG. Since its bailout in 2008, AIG has received more than $130 billion in U.S. taxpayer assistance from the New York Fed and Treasury Department and is expected to repay about $100 billion of it.
