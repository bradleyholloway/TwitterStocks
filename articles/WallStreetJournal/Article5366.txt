 Abu Dhabi court upholds sentences of 8 BCCI officials   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1996  An appeals court in the United Arab Emirates on Jun 9, 1996 upheld sentences against eight former Bank of Credit & Commerce International officials, but acquitted two others. The eight former executives must pay $8.3 million in addition to the original $9 billion in civil damages.
