 QVC Network's attempt to gain ground in Paramount bidding raises questions   By Smith, Randall; Roberts, Johnnie L, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 1994  QVC Network Inc, behind in the bidding for Paramount Communications Inc, is navigating a legal and regulatory landmine as it attempts to regain momentum in the takeover contest with Viacom Inc. QVC's Chairman Barry Diller, barred from changing QVC's Feb 1, 1994 best bid, is hinting during meetings with analysts and traders of possible future efforts to shore up his bid, which consists of both cash and QVC stock.
