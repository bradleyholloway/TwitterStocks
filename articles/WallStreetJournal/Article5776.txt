 Saks Inc. Appoints Christina Johnson CEO and President   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 1999  BIRMINGHAM, Ala. -- Saks Inc. said Christina Johnson will succeed Philip B. Miller as chief executive of Saks Fifth Avenue and add the role of president of the luxury-retail chain, effective Feb. 1.  Ms. Johnson, 48 years old, has been vice chairman of the chain since last year and was named chief operating officer in May. She will continue as vice chairman, and the operator position will be phased out. The president post has been vacant for three years.
