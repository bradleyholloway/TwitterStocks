 Stronger Dollar Expected to Boost Eurobond Demand --- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1986  The stronger dollar "will turn people's psychology around," says Peter Breese, syndication manager at Union Bank of Switzerland (Securities) Ltd. "A dollar which is no longer declining but which is stabilizing and going up is bound to make securities denominated in that currency more attractive."
