 Job search by Perella heats up   By Smith, Randall, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1993  Joseph Perella, who resigned as chairman of Wasserstein Perella & Co in Jun 1993, is currently pondering possible positions at Merrill Lynch & Co, Morgan Stanley Group Inc and Bear Stearns Cos. A decision by Perella, who commands intense interest in a hot market for top merger advisers, could come in a week or so.
