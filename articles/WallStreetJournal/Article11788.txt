 AMA, in policy reversal, may oppose making employers pay for health care   By Anders, George, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 1993  Doctors from nearly a dozen states have introduced resolutions at the AMA's convention in New Orleans urging that individuals, rather than their employers, be ultimately responsible for buying health insurance. The proposals part company with the White House on how to pay for health-care reform.
