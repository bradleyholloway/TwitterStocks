 Barneys, rebutting rumors of woes, says net doubled in a five-month period   By Reilly, Patrick M, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 25, 1994  Trend-setting retailer Barneys New York, rebutting industry speculation that it is in financial straits after a period of nationwide expansion, took the unusual step of releasing earnings and an internal sales forecast. Barneys said earnings for the five months ended Jan 2, 1994 more than doubled to $5.9 million from $2.8 million a year earlier.
