 Airborne Receives Bid of $29 a Share, Or $172 Million   By By Mike Tharp, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1986  TNT Ltd., an Australian freight-transporation concern, proposed to offer $29 a share, or $172 million, for Airborne Freight Corp. and indicated it might raise the price.  TNT proposed to merge its U.S. unit, TNT Transport Group Inc., and Airborne. "Our goal is to accomplish a friendly acquisition of Airborne," said Sir Peter Abeles, managing director and chief executive of TNT. "I hope that Airborne's board of directors would be receptive to our proposal." Airborne has 5.9 million shares.
