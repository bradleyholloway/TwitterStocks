 Marriott International Inc.: Net Income Fell in Quarter On Weak Synthetic-Fuels Arm   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 2006  Marriott International Inc.'s fiscal third-quarter earnings slipped 5.4% as revenue stagnated and the company's synthetic-fuels business posted a loss. But revenue per available room -- a key industry measure -- rose 9.4%.
