 Business Brief -- Yahoo Inc.: Front Page Is Being Redesigned In Effort to Lure Advertisers   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 2002  Yahoo Inc. is redesigning its front Web page, along with shopping and e-mail features, as the Internet company tries to woo more advertisers and to drive users to make transactions and adopt premium services. The makeovers, which will be rolled out today, combine new technology with better integration of Yahoo's network of Web sites.
