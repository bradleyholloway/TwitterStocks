 Alexander & Baldwin Posts 59% Drop in Net For Scond Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1989  Alexander & Baldwin Inc. reported that net income in its second quarter fell 59%, against a year-ago quarter that included large one-time gains.  The company posted net of $21.5 million, or 50 cents a share, down from $52.2 million, or $1.04 a share, the previous year. Revenue for quarter dropped 27% to $152.4 million from $208.5 million.
