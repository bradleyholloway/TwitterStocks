 British economy mends, slowly   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 1993  New data on the UK's consumer credit and money supply provided more evidence of a slow recovery from recession. Net lending dropped to a seasonally adjusted 49 million British pounds ($74.4 million) in Mar 1993 from BP151 million in February. Cash in circulation climbed 1.1% in March and 4.9% from a year earlier.
