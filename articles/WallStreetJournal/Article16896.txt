 WEEKEND JOURNAL; Books: The Glossies   By Johnson, Ian, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 2008  Portrait of a Country Edited by Liu Heung Shing Taschen, 423 pages, $59.99 In putting together this collection of work by Chinese photographers, editor Liu Heung Shing -- himself a Pulitzer Prize- winning photographer with Time and the Associated Press -- realized that a full portrait of China was impossible unless the book included work from recent decades that had been never been published for fear of government retribution.
