 Grace's stock soars on chance of Hercules bid   By Fritsch, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 1996  W. R. Grace & Co's shares soared 14% on speculation that Hercules Inc would pursue a bid for the rival chemical concern--a deal that analysts say could carry a price tag of at least $5 billion. In NYSE composite trading on Mar 5, 1996, Grace stock jumped $9 to close at $77.625.
