 SmartMoney.com: Save on College Texts   By Grant, Kelli B, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 2008  [...] a bill just passed by Congress and expected to be signed by President George W. Bush requires publishers to share pricing information with professors and forces them to unbundle packages of textbooks and supplementary materials so students can buy only items they need.
