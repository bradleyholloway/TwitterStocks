 Style -- In My (Shoe) Closet: The Foot Behind Jimmy Choo --- Founder Tamara Mellon knocked down a wall to make room for her large accessories collection   By Dodes, Rachel, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 2010  Ms. Mellon, founder of the Jimmy Choo label, known for its towering stilettos worn by the characters on "Sex in the City," hired a contractor to knock down a wall and expand her closet by six feet to help accommodate her collection. With a $250,000 investment from her late father, the entrepreneur behind the Vidal Sassoon hair-care empire, she created a luxury brand from scratch, partnering with Mr. Choo, a custom-footwear maker for elite clients like Princess Diana.
