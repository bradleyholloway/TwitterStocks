 Walt Disney Co. Sues Blockbuster For $120 Million   By By John Lippman and Martin Peers, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2003  Disney alleges that Blockbuster improperly deducted promotional costs, which resulted in a smaller share of video revenue to Disney and failed to account for "hundreds of thousands of missing videocassettes" valued at more than $26 million. Disney also claims that Blockbuster prematurely sold videos before it was supposed to and sold other videos it was supposed to keep on shelves for rental.
