 Marketscan: Michelina's frozen entrees trip up giants   By Gibson, Richard, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1993  The impressive marketing success Jeno Paulucci has had with his inexpensive Michelina line of frozen pasta dishes is examined as Paulucci prepares to rock the frozen food market once more with the introduction of a line of frozen Chinese and Mexican foods. The success of Paulucci's company, Luigino's Inc, has forced big players like Conagra, Nestle SA's Stouffer unit and Philip Morris Co's Kraft unit to offer their own similar lines of frozen meals.
