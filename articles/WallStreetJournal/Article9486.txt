 Letters to the Editor: Real Asbestos Victims Losers at Tort Trough   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 2002  The intimation that asbestos attorney Fred Baron has made regarding the plaintiff's bar should concern Americans ("The Baron and His Fiefdom," Review & Outlook, Sept. 25). As a former three-term senator, I am troubled that the perception exists among trial lawyers like Mr. Baron that needed legal reform may never take place because they (trial attorneys) control the Senate.
