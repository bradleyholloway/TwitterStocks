 Birtcher Medical Systems Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 20, 1992  Birtcher Medical Systems Inc, a maker and marketer of advanced surgical products, will report lower-than-expected sales of $13 million to $13.5 million and earnings of $.08 to $.10 a share for its third quarter ending Mar 31, 1992. The company had sales of $7.8 million and earnings of $.07 a share a year earlier.
