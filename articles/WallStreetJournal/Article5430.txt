 VW 'deeply regrets' feud with GM   By Mitchener, Brandon; Blumenstein, Rebecca, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1996  Volkswagen AG Chairman Ferdinand Piech said that he "deeply regrets" VW's long-running feud with the General Motors Corp over the alleged theft of trade secrets by former VW executive Jose Ignacio Lopez de Arriortua. Piech said the companies were inching closer to an out-of-court settlement.
