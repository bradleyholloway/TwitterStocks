 Business Brief: Mazda Motor Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 1988  Members of United Auto Workers union local 3000 this week ratified by a 90% margin their initial contract with Mazda Motor Corp.  The plant's 2,000 workers will be paid wages at 85% of Big Three levels in the first year of the contract, and will reach parity by the time the contract expires on March 3, 1991.
