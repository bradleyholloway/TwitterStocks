 Potomac watch: He's baaack. How Clinton was Darmanized   By Gigot, Paul A, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1994  Paul A. Gigot comments on Bob Woodward's new book "The Agenda: Inside the Clinton White House," which reveals that while candidate Clinton promised to be a Democrat who would revive the Jack Kennedy magic, in reality, by filling his administration with people who loved Richard Darman's 1990 deal, he has become "Darmanized."
