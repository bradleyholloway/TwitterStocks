 Will 2013 Be the Time For Home Buyers to Jump? Home prices are recovering, yet still low enough to lure buyers   By Ensign, Rachel Louise, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 25, 2012  The median price of an existing single-family home was $178,700 in October, up 11% from a year earlier, according to the National Association of Realtors. Tougher Standards While home prices are trying to inch back to pre-crash levels, lenders are not returning to the lax lending standards of the boom market, when loans with no down payments were not rare, says Mike Fratantoni, vice president of research at the Mortgage Bankers Association.
