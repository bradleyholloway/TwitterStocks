 Letters to the Editor: Israeli Group Glorifies Adolescent Rebellion   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 1991  Adolescent rebellion is not restricted to Orthodox Jewish families. It would be fair to say that every culture, ethnic and religious group is susceptible to this form of family conflict. Having practiced, taught and researched family therapy with Orthodox Jewish families for more than 20 years, I know that the anti-social behavior of some Orthodox Jewish adolescents and the violent overreactions of their families are more indicative of deep-rooted, complex communication problems than ideological disputes.
