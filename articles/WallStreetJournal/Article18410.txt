 Imperiled promises: Risk to retirees rises as firms fail to fund pensions they offer   By Karr, Albert R, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1993  Although Pan American World Airways assured workers that their pensions were protected under federal law, should worst come to worst, workers got a jarring surprise after Pan Am entered Chapter 11 bankruptcy proceedings. As more giant companies fall on hard times, retirees may face a wave of broken promises because the Pension Benefit Guarantee Corp's system falls far short of what many pensioners are led to believe is a universal government protection.
