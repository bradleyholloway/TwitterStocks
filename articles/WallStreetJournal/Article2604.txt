 Letters to the Editor: Military Operations Hurt Drug Smugglers   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 1994  Your May 9 article "A Lucky Bust Gives Drug Agents a Look at Smugglers' Ploys" discusses the case of Jimmie Ellard and the smuggling operation he ran during the 1980s, implying that the case demonstrates that U.S. spending on interdiction is a waste of money. But the U.S. did not increase funding for interdiction until the end of the period during which Ellard's ring was operating.
