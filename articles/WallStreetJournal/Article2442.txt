 AMR posts loss of $200 million for 4th quarter   By O Brian, Bridget, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 21, 1993  AMR Corp, the parent of American Airlines, posted a fourth-quarter loss of $200 million to cap off a loss-ridden 1992. The company said it remains pessimistic about 1993. The loss compares to a year-earlier loss of $124.9 million.
