 Figure In Galleon Case Steps Down From Job   By Clark, Don; Coker, Margaret, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 2009  Hector Ruiz, the highest-ranking executive linked to the Galleon Group insider-trading case, is stepping down early as chairman of a chip-manufacturing venture backed by Abu Dhabi and Advanced Micro Devices Inc. Globalfoundries Inc. on Monday said Mr. Ruiz, AMD's former chief executive and chairman, is taking a voluntary leave of absence, effective immediately, and will leave the company permanently in January.
