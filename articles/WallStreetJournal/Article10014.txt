 Merrill Joins List of Big Investors In Growing Trading System BATS   By Ceron, Gaston F, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 2007  The Kansas City, Mo., company said Merrill, the New York securities firm, has made a minority investment in BATS. Financial terms weren't disclosed. Merrill's investment comes after BATS had previously raised about $40 million from a roster of other investors, including Morgan Stanley and Lehman Brothers Holdings Inc.
