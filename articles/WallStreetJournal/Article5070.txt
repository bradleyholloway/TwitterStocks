 China: Mutual interest   By Steiner, Robert; McGough, Robert, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1993  Stout-of-heart small investors have several ways short of a joint venture to invest in China: mutual and closed-end funds that own shares of companies with factories or other interests in China; "red chips;" and Class B shares. Advice on investing in such instruments is offered.
