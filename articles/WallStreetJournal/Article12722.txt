 Zahn to Join CNN as Anchor, Is Fired by Fox   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 2001  NEW YORK (AP) -- Paula Zahn is headed to CNN, where she is expected to anchor a new morning broadcast. When news of the move reached Fox News Channel, it fired Ms. Zahn, alleging breach of contract.  "I'm looking forward to signing with CNN," the 45-year-old Ms. Zahn said. "I'm thrilled." CNN is a unit of AOL Time Warner Inc.
