 Buffett's Dollar Bet   The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 2005  It's good to be a billionaire, not least when you're betting against the national currency. Warren Buffett disclosed on the weekend that he's continuing to make a -- gulp -- $21 billion dollar bet that the U.S. dollar will decline in value, despite having lost $310 million on that gamble in the first quarter of this year as the dollar rose.
