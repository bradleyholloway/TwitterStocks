How to extinguish 'Guiding Light'   By Chozick, Amy, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 2009  Actors are sending out their resumes and bracing for unemployment, fans have formed support groups and staged "Save the Light" protests, and they're all wondering how things will end for Reva, Josh and the other inhabitants of the fictional town of Springfield. "Guiding Light" experienced a ratings boom in 1983 with the arrival in Springfield of Reva Shayne, a daughter of the Lewis family's housekeeper and the long-time love of Joshua Lewis, the black sheep son of billionaire oilman H.B. Lewis.\n
