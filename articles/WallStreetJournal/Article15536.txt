 World-wide: A large-scale breast cancer study   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1997  A large-scale breast cancer study found that self-examination failed to reduce the death rate over five years among 133,000 Chinese women who were intensively trained in the technique. The research, conducted by scientists in Seattle, suggested public-health programs should emphasize the use of mammograms to detect tumors.
