 KaiserTech Considers Selling the Company To Benefit Holders   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 1987  OAKLAND, Calif. -- KaiserTech Ltd. said it is considering the sale of the company and other unspecified measures to "maximize value to the company's shareholders."  The holding company for Kaiser Aluminum & Chemical Corp. didn't elaborate. The company had hinted in October that it might be looking for a buyer when it appointed Salomon Brothers Inc. and Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc. as its investment bankers. The move followed the disclosure of stock market losses incurred by Alan Clore, KaiserTech's chairman and largest shareholder.
