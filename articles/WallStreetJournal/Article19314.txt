 Business Brief -- AURORA FOODS INC.: Wider Second-Quarter Loss Is Reported on Probe Costs   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 2000  Aurora Foods Inc. reported a wider second-quarter loss, hurt by expenses from an accounting probe. Aurora, St. Louis, said its net loss was $18.9 million, or 28 cents a share, compared with a restated loss of $3.7 million, or six cents a share, in the year-earlier period.
