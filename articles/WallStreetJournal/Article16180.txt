 France's New Premier Sounds Conciliatory Tone   By By Charles Fleming, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 2002  One of France's feistiest union leaders welcomed the tone of Mr. [Jean-Pierre Raffarin]'s speech. Marc Blondel, secretary general of Force Ouvriere, said it suggested that fears of labor strife this year are unfounded. "It was a wise and realistic speech," said Mr. Blondel, who has met with Mr. Raffarin twice in the past month. "It's too early to say whether he will act on what we say, but at least he listens, and that's a change."
