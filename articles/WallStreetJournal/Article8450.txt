 AnnTaylor Stores Corp.: Sales for owned stores rose 9.3% for four-week period   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 1994  AnnTaylor Stores Corp said sales for owned stores rose 9.3% in the four weeks ended Jan 29, 1994, to $30.5 million from $27.9 million, while owned same-store sales fell 1.4%.
