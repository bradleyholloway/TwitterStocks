 Administration plans bill by February to boost telephone, cable competition   By Pearl, Daniel, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 1993  Although no specific endorsements are expected, the Clinton administration, struggling to turn its "information superhighway" goals into specific policies, is expected to give general praise on Dec 21, 1993 to three bills whose aim is to increase telephone and cable competition. Administration officials say they expect to file their own legislation by Feb 1994 to break down barriers to competition in telecommunications.
