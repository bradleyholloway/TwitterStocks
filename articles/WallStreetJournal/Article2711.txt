 American Express profit was flat in first quarter   By Knecht, G Bruce, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 1995  American Express Co reported $353 million in first-quarter 1995 income, basically flat with the same period a year earlier. Travel Related Services, the company's flagship charge-card and travel unit, reported $263 million in profit during the quarter, a 13% increase from same period a year earlier.
