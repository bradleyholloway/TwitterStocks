 Health & Technology: Tellabs Warns Of Drop in Sales, Plans to Cut Jobs   By By Dennis K. Berman, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 2002  Tellabs has one of the better balance sheets, with nearly $1 billion in cash and virtually no debt. Nonetheless, the sales shortfalls have pummeled shares. Thursday's news sent Tellabs stock down 9% to close at $5.24 in 4 p.m. composite trading on the Nasdaq Stock Exchange. Nearly all of its peers were also brought lower by the news.
