 Investors Sue J.P. Morgan --- Potential Class-Action Suits Allege Foreclosure Fraud; Perfect Trading Quarter   By Benoit, David, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 2010  J.P. Morgan said it was facing suits from nine Federal Home Loan Banks, Cambridge Place Investment Management Inc. and Charles Schwab Corp. Those suits, in state and federal courts across the country, make largely the same allegations: that banks didn't present the material facts about the underlying mortgages that made up the mortgage-backed securities the plaintiffs invested in.
