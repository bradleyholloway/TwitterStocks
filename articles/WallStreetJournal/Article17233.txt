 Corporate News: Solvay to Buy Rhodia In Chemicals Merger   By Berton, Elena; Torello, Alessandro, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 2011  Belgian chemical and plastics company Solvay SA said Monday that it agreed to buy Rhodia SA in a friendly deal that values the French specialty-chemical maker at 3.4 billion euros ($4.84 billion) and will expand Solvay's footprint in fast-growing emerging markets.
