 SABMiller PLC: Harbin Brewery Shuns Offer, Paving Way for Anheuser-Bush   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 2004  Less than a day after SABMiller PLC made a hostile $553 million takeover bid for Chinese brewer Harbin Brewery Group Ltd., Harbin urged its shareholders to shun the offer, clearing the way for a counterbid by rival brewer Anheuser-Busch Cos. of the U.S.
