 World News: Weak China Data Show Why Beijing Acted Fast   By Andrew Batson in Beijing and Norihiko Shirouzu in Guangzhou, China, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 2008  While China's large trade surpluses were once seen as the result of a mighty export engine, recent increases came as export growth has slowed, and reflect more the rapid deceleration in imports -- a sign of domestic economic weakness, not strength.
