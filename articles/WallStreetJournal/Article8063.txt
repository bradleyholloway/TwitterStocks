 'Dart' Stock Pickers again Beat the Pros   By Dorfman, John R, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1990  For the second time in a row, stocks chosen by a toss of the darts bested a quartet of stocks selected by experienced investment professionals in this column's stock-picking contest. The dart stocks lost 16.3% of their value from May 1, 1990 to Oct 31, while the pros' picks lost 37.8%.
