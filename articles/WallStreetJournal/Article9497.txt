 FTC Chief Pledges Antitrust Vigilance In Health Sector   By By Bernard Wysocki Jr., The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 2002  Tenet Healthcare Corp., Santa Barbara, Calif., said Tuesday the commission had subpoenaed documents concerning the combination of two hospitals in Missouri, both now owned by Tenet. The FTC had tried to stop that merger, but ultimately lost its case on appeal.  The FTC won't confirm or deny any specific targets of its investigations. However, Evanston and several health plans doing business with Evanston have confirmed the FTC is looking into that union.
