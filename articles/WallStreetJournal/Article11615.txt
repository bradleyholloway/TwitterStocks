 Super Rite Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 1992  Grocery wholesaler and retailer Super Rite Corp's net loss for its fiscal first quarter ended May 30, 1992 was $1.7 million, compared with net income of $1.3 million in the year-earlier period. The latest quarter loss was due to one-time charges related to debt and preferred stock redemptions.
