 Double Play: Sports Arenas Add Upscale Nightclubs   By Campo-Flores, Arian, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 2012  In 2010, Sun Life Stadium, where the Miami Dolphins football team plays, launched LIV, an offshoot of one of the hottest nightclubs in Miami Beach, offering cabanas and bottle service. At the Clevelander, a spinoff of a popular bar in South Beach, "we were looking for a fan not necessarily interested in sitting in a seat for nine innings," says Mike Palma, executive vice president of hospitality at Brio Investment Group, which owns the Clevelander.
