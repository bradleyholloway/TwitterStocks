 Family feud: Ouster of father puts emir in place to lead change in Persian Gulf   By Pearl, Daniel, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 1996  Sheik Hamad Bin Khalifa Al-Thani of Qater is profiled. Since ousting his father, Sheik Khalifa, in Jun 1995, Hamad has been making his own rules and raising eyebrows across the Persian Gulf by flirting with democracy and snubbing the Saudis. For the US, Qatar presents a dilemma. The US wants to encourage the kind of reforms Qatar is pursuing, but it doesen't want to destabilize Saudi Arabia, a stanch ally.
