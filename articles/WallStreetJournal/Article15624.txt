 Business Brief -- LA-Z-BOY INC.: Net Rose 10% in 2nd Period On Demand for Products   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 1997  La-Z-Boy Inc said fiscal second-quarter net income rose 10% to $16.8 million, or 94 cents a share, for the period ended Oct 25, 1997 from $15.3 million, or 84 cents a share, a year earlier.
