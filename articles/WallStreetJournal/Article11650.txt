 Business Brief -- Burlington Northern Inc.: Fleet for Transporting Grain Is Increased by 1,400 Cars   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 1992  Amid a surge in orders from shippers for grain cars, Burlington Northern Railroad, a unit of Burlington Northern Inc., said it boosted the size of its covered hopper fleet for transporting grain by 1,400 cars to a total of 25,000 cars.
