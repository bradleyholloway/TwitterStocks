The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 2007  HOT TUNA (INTERNATIONAL) PLC (London) -- Ranjit Murugason is stepping down as executive chairman and reverting to a non-executive role with this apparel company. Mr. Murugason cited his increasing time commitment to another business venture. Elle Macpherson, 44, resigned as executive director and a board member. Hot Tuna said it is in discussions with Ms. Macpherson about a consulting role.
