 International Brief -- Cie. Financiere de Paribas: Net Rose an Estimated 58% To $238 Million Total in '93   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1994  Cie. Financiere de Paribas, Paris, estimated yesterday that its 1993 group net income rose 58% to 1.4 billion francs ($238 million) from 886 million francs in 1992.
