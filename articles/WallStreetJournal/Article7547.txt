 Ex-Ernst & Whinney Partner Is Censured By SEC Over Audits   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 1986  The Securities and Exchange Commission censured Michael Hope, a former partner with the accounting firm Ernst & Whinney.  The agency censured Mr. Hope for "improper professional conduct" in connection with the firm's audits of U.S. Surgical Corp. in 1980 and 1981.
