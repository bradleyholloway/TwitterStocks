 The Buzz: Citi's Bid to Buy Nikko May Get Big Credit Line   By Hiroyuki Kachi and Jamie Miyazaki, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 2007  In what would amount to Japan's biggest syndicated loan ever, Mizuho, Bank of Tokyo-Mitsubishi UFJ, Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corp. and Citigroup's Citibank NA "are preparing to provide" a 1.4 trillion yen line of credit, with an option for an additional 300 billion yen, a person close to the matter said Friday.
