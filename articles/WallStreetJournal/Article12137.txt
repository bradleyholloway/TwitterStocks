 Norfolk Southern ends talks with CSX, reaffirms its plans to bid for Conrail   By Lipin, Steven; Machalaba, Daniel, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 1996  Norfolk Southern Corp said it ended talks with CSX Corp about splitting up Conrail Inc and reaffirmed plans to proceed with its unsolicited bid to buy all of Conrail for $9.1 billion, or $100 a share.
