 Business Brief: Vortec Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 9, 1987  Hinderliter, a Tulsa, Okla.-based manufacturing and heat-treating concern, said in a Securities and Exchange Commission filing it may try to acquire control of Vortec, a Cincinnati-based supplier of home health-care equipment, by buying more Vortec shares if Vortec's directors aren't interested in a merger.
