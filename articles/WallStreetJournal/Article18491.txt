 Salinas unveils program to cut Mexico's taxes   By Solis, Dianne, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1993  President Carlos Salinas announced his new tax-cutting program in an effort to stimulate the economy and improve real wages for the lowest-paid Mexican workers. Under the program, which takes effect on Jan 1, 1994, the number of workers exempted from personal income taxes will double to 2.6 million.
