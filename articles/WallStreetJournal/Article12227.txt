 Business Brief -- PENNSYLVANIA ENTERPRISES INC.: Net Income Is Reported After Year-Earlier Loss   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 6, 1997  PENNSYLVANIA ENTERPRISES Inc. reported second-quarter net income of $151,000, compared with a loss of $476,000 a year earlier. An article yesterday incorrectly said net income was $151 million, compared with a loss of $476 million. (WSJ Aug. 7, 1997)
