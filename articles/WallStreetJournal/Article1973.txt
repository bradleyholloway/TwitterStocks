 General Development Rejects Buy-Out Bid By AmRuss Partners   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 1989  General Development Corp. said its board rejected AmRuss Partners' recent buy-out offer valued at $193.2 million, or $23 a share, in cash and debentures.  Earlier this month, AmRuss Partners, a Colorado general partnership, made an unsolicited offer to acquire all the shares outstanding of General Development, a real estate developer, through a tender offer of $18 a share in cash and $5 a share in debentures.
