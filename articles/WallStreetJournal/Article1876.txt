 Bank of New York Offering Securities As Required by Fed   The Wall Street Journal,  May 20, 1988  Both issues were priced at stated value with an initial dividend of 8.875% on the adjustable-rate stock and 9.25% on the fixed/adjustable. Dividends on the ARPS aren't cumulative and are payable quarterly in each year beginning Sept. 1, 1988. The dividend rate for each quarter ending before June 1, 1993, will be 20 basis points over the highest of the 90-day U.S. Treasury bill rate, the three-year Constant Maturity U.S. Treasury rate and the five-year Constant Maturity U.S. Treasury rate and will be subject to a minimum rate of 6.5% and a maximum of 12.5%.
