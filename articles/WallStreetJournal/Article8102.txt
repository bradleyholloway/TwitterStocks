 Amgen Wins Biotech Drug Patent Battle   By Rundle, Rhonda L; Stipp, David, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1991  A Washington DC appeals court's decision to block Genetics Institute Co from selling its version of an anti-anemia drug in the US was a major victory in Amgen Inc's long-running battle with Genetics Institute Co over rights to a new biotechnology drug.
