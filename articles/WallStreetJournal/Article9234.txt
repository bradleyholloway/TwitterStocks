 Wal-Mart Appoints Canadian Unit Chief As Its European CEO   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 7, 2000  Wal-Mart, the world's largest retailer, said its current European head, Allan Leighton, is leaving the company for personal reasons. Mr. Leighton, 47 years old, was CEO of Asda PLC, the British supermarket chain acquired by Wal-Mart last year. He later was appointed head of Wal-Mart's European operations, which include 95 stores in Germany, 240 Asda stores and a recently opened Wal-Mart-branded superstore in Bristol, England.
