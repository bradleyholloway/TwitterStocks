 Retailers report uneven sales for last month   By Bird, Laura, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 1995  The nation's largest retailers reported uneven sales in Jul 1995 as shoppers snapped up summer apparel bargains but remained tightfisted when it came to spending for anything else. Sales at stores open at least a year climbed 3.6% in July, not quite matching the 4.4% growth measured in Jul 1994.
