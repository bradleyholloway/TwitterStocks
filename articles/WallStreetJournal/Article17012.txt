 Jet Sensors Are Probed In Brazilian Air Crash   By Pasztor, Andy; Michaels, Daniel, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 2009  According to people familiar with the details, an international team of crash investigators as well as safety experts at Airbus are focused on a theory that malfunctioning airspeed indicators touched off a series of events that apparently made some flight controls, onboard computers and electrical systems go haywire.
