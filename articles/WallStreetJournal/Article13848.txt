 For Investors, China's Boom Is a Rough Ride   By Zuckerman, Gregory, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 2011  China Mobile, for example, is the world's largest mobile-phone company by subscribers; its stock trades as American depositary shares on U.S. markets under the symbol CHL.
