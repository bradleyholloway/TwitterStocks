 Expand Your Investment Frontiers   By Kansas, Dave, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 2010  According to Bank of America Merrill Lynch, $100 invested in the Standard & Poor's 500-stock index on Dec. 31, 1999, would have been worth $91 a decade later.
