 Unit of Bayer AG Is Cited by Canada in Antitrust Case   By Tamburri, Rosanna, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1992  The antitrust division of the Canadian government accused Chemagro Ltd, a unit of Bayer AG, of bid-rigging and conspiracy in connection with insecticide sales to provincial government forestry departments. The government decided against prosecuting another company, Abbott Laboratories Ltd, because Abbott took the initiative in disclosing the alleged offenses.
