 Global Finance: CFTC Said to Be Probing Natural-Gas Trades   By Lynch, Sarah N; Baskin, Brian, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 2010  Natural-gas futures are traded on the New York Mercantile Exchange, owned by CME Group Inc. Over-the-counter natural-gas contracts also are listed by the Atlanta-based IntercontinentalExchange Inc. The Henry Hub contract, ICE's largest natural-gas product, is cash-settled based on the price of the Nymex's Henry Hub physically delivered futures contract.
