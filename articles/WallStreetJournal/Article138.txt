 Air Wis Services Says American Air Fails to Make New Offer   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 25, 1991  Air Wis Services Inc said it hasn't received a new proposal from American Airlines for the acquisition of certain assets. The deadline for a new offer expired Nov 21, 1991. American previously offered $150 million for the lease rights on a portion of Air Wis's landing rights at O'Hare International Airport in Chicago.
