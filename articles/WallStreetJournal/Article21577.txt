 Tokyo Prosecutors Arrest 4 in Swindle of Steel Concerns   By Kanabayashi, Masayoshi, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 1991  Tokyo prosecutors arrested a former Marubeni Corp official and three directors of Kyowa Co, a bankrupt steel-processing company, on suspicion that they swindled 3.65 billion yen ($26.4 million) out of Iida Sangyo KK and Okafuji Shoji KK, two medium-sized, steel-trading companies.
