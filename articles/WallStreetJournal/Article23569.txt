 Love & Money: There's No Place Like Home, Including Home   By Opdyke, Jeff D, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 2008  For as long as I've known Amy, both Thanksgiving and Christmas were huge family soirees at her grandfather's house, replete with parents, grandparents, nephews, nieces, grandchildren and great-grandchildren. Perhaps most unsettling to Amy is that she envisioned our kids would know the childhood she and I had growing up in Baton Rouge, when we could ride our bikes all over the city or hang out in the street after dark playing with friends.
