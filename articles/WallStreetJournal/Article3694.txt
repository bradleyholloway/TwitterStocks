 Ernst Legal Case Blasted by SEC As Fiction Work   By Bryan-Low, Cassell, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 2003  In its 100-page brief, the SEC attacked earlier arguments made by Ernst, calling them "a red herring" in one instance. In a filing last month, Ernst argued that a group established with the agreement of the SEC examined independence policies last year at all large firms and found Ernst's policies were effectively designed and implemented. The SEC said Friday the agency "hasn't verified or accepted these conclusions."
