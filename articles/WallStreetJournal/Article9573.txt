 Health Care Brief -- Guidant Corp.: Panel's Preliminary Finding Is Stent Infringed Cordis Patent   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 2003  An arbitration panel made a preliminary ruling that one of Guidant Corp.'s coronary-stent systems infringes the patent of a stent made by Cordis Corp., a unit of Johnson & Johnson, New Brunswick, N.J.
