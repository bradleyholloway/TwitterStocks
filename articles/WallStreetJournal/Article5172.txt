 Iwerks, which has posted losses, says 3 directors, including Ueberroth, quit   By King, Thomas R, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 1994  Iwerks Entertainment Inc said three directors, Peter Ueberroth, Douglas W. Broyles and Mark Siegel, have resigned. Iwerks has been posting losses for the past few quarters and industry executives speculate that turmoil at the company is continuing.
