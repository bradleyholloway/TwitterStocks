 Stahl Takes Control of Apple Bancorp; Ex-Chief Sues Firm   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 1990  Stanley Stahl has won his takeover battle for Apple Bancorp Inc, by getting 97% of the shares from shareholders. Also, Apple and its former chairman and CEO, Jerome R. McDougal Jr, sued each other over what McDougal claims is at least $8.5 million due to him under a severence agreement.
