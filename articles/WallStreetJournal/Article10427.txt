 The DL Bug Bites Phillies, Mets   By Sielski, Mike, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 2010  Centerfielder Carlos Beltran, once the Mets' most complete player, was waiting there, convalescing from off-season surgery to his right knee, preparing to help the team make a playoff push. Since the All-Star break, though, the Mets had gone 6-14 entering Friday despite Mr. Beltran's presence, second baseman Luis Castillo's return from foot problems and a recovery by shortstop Jose Reyes from an oblique strain (though he was not DL'd for it).
