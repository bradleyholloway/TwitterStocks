 Chicago Merc to Trade Options on Swiss Franc, British Pound Futures   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 25, 1985  Each Swiss franc options contract will cover one futures contract controlling 125,000 francs, or about $44,300 at current exchange rates. Each British pound options contract will cover one futures contract controlling 25,000 pounds, or about $27,000 at current exchange rates. Trading months will be March, June, September and December, the exchange said.
