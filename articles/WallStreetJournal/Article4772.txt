 Bridgeport's Bankruptcy Petition Offers Reminder of the Plight of Cities, States   By Herman, Tom, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1991  The bankruptcy petition of Bridgeport CT provides a grim reminder of the severe financial strains afflicting many cities and states, particularly in the Northeast. The city's action, which the state has vowed to challenge in court, is an attempt to gain's the court's help in rewriting its labor contracts, rather than raising taxes and slashing public services to balance its budget.
