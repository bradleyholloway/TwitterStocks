 Collins & Aikman: Sale of a fabrics division is put under consideration   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 1996  Collins & Aikman Corp, moving to shed units unrelated to its growing automotive business, said it is considering a sale of its decorative-fabrics division. The division, Mastercraft Group, is a leading manufacturer of flat-woven upholstery fabrics used in residential and office furniture.
