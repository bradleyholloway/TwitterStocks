 Medicine & health: What might be   By Bishop, Jerrey E, The Wall Street Journal,  May 20, 1994  For all the speedy advances made in the biotechnology industry since the 1970s, many goals of researchers remain unrealized. A few such elusive feats that researchers hope to one day reach, including self-fertilizing plants, the development of protein pills and the slowdown of the aging process, are examined.
