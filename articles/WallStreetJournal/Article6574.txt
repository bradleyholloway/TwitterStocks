 MDU Resources Group Inc: Seattle's Cascade Natural Gas To Be Bought for $26.50 a Share   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 2006  MDU Resources Group Inc. said it agreed to acquire Cascade Natural Gas Corp. for $26.50 a share. MDU, of Bismarck, N.D., said Cascade's customer base in Washington and Oregon will roughly double its natural-gas utility customers.
