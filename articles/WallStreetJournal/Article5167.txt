 Clearly Canadian Beverage Corp.: Company to take a charge for the fourth quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 1994  Clearly Canadian Beverage Corp said it will take a one-time charge in the fourth quarter due to staff cuts and writedowns, with fourth-quarter results below analysts' forecasts. The company reported sales in the fourth quarter ended Dec 31, 1993 of $9 million.
