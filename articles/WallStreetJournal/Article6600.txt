 Investor's Calendar   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 2006  -- BP Back on the Hill: Robert A. Malone, chief executive of BP's American operations, is expected to testify before the Senate Energy and Natural Resources Committee. At House hearings last week, Mr. Malone and other BP executives pledged to fix operational problems in Alaska, site of a big oil spill in March and a partial oil-pipeline shutdown by BP last month.  -- Pretexting Probe at H-P: Hewlett-Packard's handling of a probe into boardroom leaks surrounding the ouster of former CEO Carly Fiorina is being investigated by the Securities and Exchange Commission and California's attorney general. The firm acknowledged using "pretexting," or posing as a person in order to obtain information about him or her, in the course of its inquiry.
