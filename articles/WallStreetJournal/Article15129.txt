 Form plus function: Sewage plants start cleaning up profiles   By Pierson, John, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 1993  A group of planners and engineers is taking garbage and sewage public. Phoenix AZ is about to open a new solid waste management plant that also will be a school and a social center, and which will have classrooms, guided tours and big windows for viewing the recycling. Also, Scottsdale AZ plans to turn its sewage treatment plants into an educational "campus" with tours.
