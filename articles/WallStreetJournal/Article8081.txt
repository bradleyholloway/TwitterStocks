 European Peatniks Are Trying to Save Historic Irish Bogs   By Mapes, Glynn, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 1991  Scientists and conservationists, intent on saving the peat bogs in Ireland and the UK, have called for a boycott of horticultural peat by claiming that if the exploitation isn't slowed sharply, Irish and British peatlands will be largely destroyed in another 10 to 20 years.
