 There's No Joy in Mudville, After All   By Dugan, Ianthe Jeanne, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 2010  For decades, cities have pumped billions of dollars into baseball parks, playing on the lore of the game and promises of economic benefits and prestige. The group, called Citizens for Responsible Government, had sued the city and Miami-Dade County in December 2009 in Sarasota Circuit Court attempting to stop the government from floating bonds to fund a $31 million renovation of the Orioles spring-training camp, known as Ed Smith.
