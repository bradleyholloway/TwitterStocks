 Primark Says Suitors Have Shown Interest In Possible Buyout   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1997  WALTHAM, Mass. -- Primark Corp., having just agreed to sell a large information-technology business, said it has received inquires about possible buyouts that would value the company at between $1.2 billion and $1.5 billion, well above its current market valuation of $1.04 billion.
