 Tuesday's Markets: Industrials Climb 14.98 to a Record   By Sease, Douglas R; Sesit, Michael R, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1992  On Mar 3, 1992, encouraging economic data sent the Dow Jones Industrial Average up 14.98 points to a record 3290.25 in heavy trading. The dollar also soared but bonds were battered.
