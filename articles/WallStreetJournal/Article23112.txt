 Level 3 Communications Inc.: Wholesale Dial Access Business Acquired from Colorado Firm   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 2, 2004  Level 3 Communications Inc. acquired the wholesale dial access business of ICG Communications Inc., Englewood, Colo., for about $35 million. ICG's wholesale dial access business provides dial-up Internet access to America Online, EarthLink, MSN and other Internet service providers.
