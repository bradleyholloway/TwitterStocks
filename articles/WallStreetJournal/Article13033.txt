 New Stock Listings   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 2004  The Big Board began trading the IPOs of iShares NYSE 100 Index Fund, a San Francisco-based exchange-traded fund, under the symbol NY; and iShares NYSE Composite Index Fund, also a San Francisco-based exchange-traded fund, NYC. The exchange also began trading shares of Perini Corp., a Framingham, Mass., provider of general contracting, construction management and design-build services, under the symbol PCR.
