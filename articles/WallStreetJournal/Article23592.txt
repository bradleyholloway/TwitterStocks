 Wonder Land: Hearts and Minds, Again   By Henninger, Daniel, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 2008  Most of the time, the national Democratic party is at pains to avoid the label "San Francisco Democrats" that was coined by Jeane Kirkpatrick in her devastating "Blame America, First" speech to the 1984 GOP convention. "Hearts & Minds" makes associations between the war and a gung-ho high-school football team in Niles, Ohio; uniformed marching bands; young boys (Scouts) in formation in parades, and displays of tiny flag-waving.
