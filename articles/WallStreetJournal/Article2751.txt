 Legal beat: More plead guilty in AT&T-insider case   By McMorris, Frances A, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 1995  Joseph Cusimano and William Mylett, two more members of an insider-trading ring accused of making $2.6 million in profits on tips about AT&T Corp's takeover plans, pleaded guilty on Aug 18, 1995. In addition, federal prosecutors say they have expanded their probe to half a dozen additional suspects.
