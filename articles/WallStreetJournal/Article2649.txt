 U.S. companies see greater investment in emerging markets, especially in Asia   By Carrington, Tim, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 21, 1994  US multinationals may be poised to close the gap between themselves and their European and Japanese counterparts in pushing into emerging markets. An Ernst & Young International Ltd survey indicates a higher portion of American multinationals are expecting to increase their investments in such markets, especially in Asia and Latin America.
