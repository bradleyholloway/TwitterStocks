 Health & Wellness: Apps to Help Track Seniors' Medical Needs   By Martin, Anya, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 2011  Taking care of an aging or ailing relative with health-care issues can be stressful and confusing, but a growing number of free or cheap smartphone apps aim to help caregivers keep track of medication dosages, nutritional requirements and other daily health-care needs.
