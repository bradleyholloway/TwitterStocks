 Air France Weighs A Thai Air Stake In Delta Alliance   By By Zach Coleman, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1999  Mr. (Pierre-Henri) Gourgeon also confirmed reports that Air France and Delta are talking with Korean Airlines about joining their group.
