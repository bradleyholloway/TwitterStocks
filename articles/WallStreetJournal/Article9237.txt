 Ad Notes ....   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 7, 2000  The brands are split between True North's FCB Worldwide and Omnicom Group Inc.'s BBDO Worldwide. FCB Worldwide, which has about $900 million in DaimlerChrysler billings, handles the Chrysler and Jeep brands, as well as spot media buying for Chrysler's dealers in the U.S. FCB recently was awarded most of the car company's media buying and planning duties overseas. Omnicom's BBDO Worldwide handles the Dodge brand, with billings estimated at $500 million. BBDO also handles Chrysler's entire U.S. media-buying account through its Pentacom shop, which was established in 1993.
