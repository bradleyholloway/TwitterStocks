 Focus on franchising: Ruling chills franchisers' use of evictions as leverage   By Tannenbaum, Jeffrey A, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 1993  Because they hold the leases of their franchisees, many franchisers use evictions, or eviction threats, to gain leverage in disputes. However, an Illinois state court upheld an unusual award of $1 million in a case involving eviction efforts against Doctor's Associates Inc, a franchiser of the huge Subway Sandwiches & Salads chain.
