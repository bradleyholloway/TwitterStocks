 Microsoft creates new executive group, realigns duties of two star managers   By Clark, Don, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1996  Microsoft Corp established a nine-member executive committee, headed by Chairman and CEO Bill Gates, that replaces a group called the office of the president. The software giant also shifted the responsibilities of two rising management stars who are leading the company's most important product-development projects--Jim Allchin and Brad Silverberg.
