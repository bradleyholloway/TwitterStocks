 Cloned Livestock Poised to Receive FDA Clearance   By Jane Zhang in Washington; Lauren Etter in Chicago; John W. Miller in Brussels, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 2008  While many consumer groups still oppose it, the FDA declaration that cloned animal products are safe would be a milestone for a small cadre of biotech companies that want to make a business out of producing copies of prize dairy cows and other farm animals -- effectively taking the selective breeding practiced on farms for centuries to the cutting edge.
