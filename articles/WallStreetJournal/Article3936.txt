 Americans Still Want To Ride Those Rails   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 2005  To argue that $15 billion spent on Amtrak over the past 20-plus years "have gone up in smoke" ("The Little Engine That Shouldn't," Review & Outlook, July 7) is to ignore the tangible benefits that money produced. This includes electrification all the way to Boston, and elimination of the long waits to change locomotives in New Haven.
