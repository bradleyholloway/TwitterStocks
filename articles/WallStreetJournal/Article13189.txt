 Merck Opens Defense in Vioxx Case   By Ilan Brat and Heather Won Tesoriero, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 2005  Testifying for Merck was Alan Nies, a Merck senior vice president of clinical sciences who ran the Vioxx project development team but who now is retired. He said Merck acted responsibly in its development of Vioxx, and the Food and Drug Administration had thought Merck's clinical testing of Vioxx was adequate. His testimony is set to continue today.
