 U.S. Filter Lifts Bid To Acquire Memtec To About $380 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1997  PALM DESERT, Calif. -- U.S. Filter Corp. said it increased its offer to acquire Memtec Ltd. to $36 a share, or about $380 million, and the Australian water-filter company's board agreed to recommend it to shareholders.  "U.S. Filter is a good strategic fit with Memtec and we see opportunity to expose our excellent filtration and other products to a much broader, global customer base," Denis Hanley, Memtec's chairman and chief executive officer said in a joint statement with U.S. Filter. Memtec is based in Sydney.
