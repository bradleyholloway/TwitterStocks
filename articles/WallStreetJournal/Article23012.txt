 Food Brief -- Altadis SA: Franco-Spanish Company Buys 80% of Morocco Tobacco Firm   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 2003  Franco-Spanish tobacco concern Altadis SA bought an 80% stake in Morocco's state-run tobacco monopoly, Regie des Tabacs Marocains, for 1.29 billion euros ($1.52 billion). Altadis said its success in the privatization contest gave it a major platform for growth in Morocco and in North Africa.
