 Tate & Lyle PLC   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 2005  Tate & Lyle PLC, hit by a charge related to a lawsuit, said its fiscal full-year net profit fell 9%. The producer of sugar, cereal sweeteners and starches, as well as Splenda sucralose, said net profit in the year ended March 31 fell to GBP 140 million ($254 million) from GBP 154 million a year earlier.
