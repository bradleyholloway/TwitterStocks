 Wired Ventures Fires 20% of Its Work Force To End Restructuring   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1997  "This is the essential completion of a restructuring that began in 1996," said Jeff Simon, Wired's chief financial officer. Including the latest cuts, Wired said it has reduced its staff by more than 40% this year and now has about 250 employees. "We have been focusing the business around the lines that are our strength," Mr. Simon said.
