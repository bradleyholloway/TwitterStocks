 Business Brief -- Helen of Troy Corp.: Beauty Biz Assets Are Sold To Alberto-Culver Subsidiary   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1993  Hurt by slow retail sales, Helen of Troy Corp., El Paso, Texas, said it sold the assets of its Beauty Biz Inc. retail beauty supply unit to Sally Beauty Co., Denton, Texas, a unit of Alberto-Culver Co., a Melrose Park, Ill.-based maker of personal-care products.
