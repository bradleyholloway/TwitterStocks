 Du Pont Plans Facility to Make CFC Substitute   By Miller, James P, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 1991  Du Pont Co disclosed plans to build a $100 million chlorofluorocarbon-substitutes manufacturing facility in Texas. The new products have the trade name of Suva and one-fiftieth or less of the ozone-depleting potential of Freon, the coolant based on CFCs.
