 Tent Evacuees To Be Moved At Racetrack   By Weaver, Christopher; Maloney, Jennifer, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 2012  Hundreds of New Jersey evacuees living in a tent city at the Monmouth Park Racetrack were told they would be moved Saturday into the track's four-story grandstand building after complaints arose that the camp designed for out-of-state utility workers wasn't appropriate for storm victims.
