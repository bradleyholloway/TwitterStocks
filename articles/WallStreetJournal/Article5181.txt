 Revlon Inc.: Cosmetics company wins trademark fight with P&G   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 1994  A US district court has denied a request by Procter & Gamble Co's Giorgio Beverly Hills Inc unit for a preliminary injunction restraining the sale of Charlie Red, a new fragrance from Revlon Inc. P&G claimed that the Revlon product infringed on P&G's Giorgio Red and Red for Men trademarks.
