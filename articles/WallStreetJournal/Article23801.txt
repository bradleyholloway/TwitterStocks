 MarketWatch: Relax, You Can Beat That Stress   By Gerencher, Kristen, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 2010  Many companies offer an employee-assistance program (EAP), wellness program or a health plan that offers resources for combating stress. Use of an EAP is confidential and can help you personalize a plan to overcome stress, says Kathleen Jahnke, a licensed mental-health counselor and vice president of counseling services for ESI Employee Assistance Group, a Wellsville, N.Y., employee-assistance company.
