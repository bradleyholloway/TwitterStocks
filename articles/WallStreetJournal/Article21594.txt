 Bond Slump Pulls Down Stock Prices   By Sease, Douglas R, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 2, 1991  A slumping bond market pulled stock prices lower on Aug 1, 1991, despite evidence the economic recovery is continuing, and the dollar gained against most major currencies. The Dow Jones Industrial Average lost 7.15 to 3017.67 in active trading. A report from the nation's purchasing managers showed a modest upswing in the manufacturing side of the economy and weekly jobless claims fell 21,000 in the week ended Jul 20, 1991.
