 NTT DoCoMo Sets Price On $8.21 Billion Offering   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 2001  NTT DoCoMo Inc. priced its offering of 950 billion yen ($8.21 billion) in new shares at 2.066 million yen a share, laying the ground for one of Japan's biggest share sales, bankers involved in the sale said.
