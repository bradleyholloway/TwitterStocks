 Marketing: Ads Draw the Curious to Toll-Free Lines   By Gibson, Richard, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 9, 1992  Companies such as Johnston & Murphy Shoe and Red Lion Hotels & Inns use toll-free telephone numbers to promote their services. Johnston & Murphy plays humorous commentaries on the presidential campaign filled with footwear puns and Red Lion uses ad teasers to entice people to call its 800 number.
