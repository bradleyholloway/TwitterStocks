 Key Tronic Says Talks On Merger Are Ended   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 1986  SPOKANE, Wash. -- Key Tronic Corp. said negotiations for its acquisition by Minebea Co. of Tokyo have been ended.  Key Tronic and Minebea in late June jointly terminated an agreement for the acquisition of 30% of Key Tronic by Minebea, citing the complexities of partial ownership.
