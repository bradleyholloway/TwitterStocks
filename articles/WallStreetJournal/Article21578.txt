 Postal Service Says It Needs Higher Rates   By Karr, Albert R, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 1991  The Postal Service asked the independent Postal Rate Commission to reconsider a rate ruling it issued six months ago. That decision authorized various rate adjustments, including an increase in first-class service to 29 cents for the first ounce, instead of the 30-cent rate the service had sought.
