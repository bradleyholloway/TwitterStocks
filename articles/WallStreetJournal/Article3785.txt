 RadioShack Corp.: First-Quarter Profit Rose 21% On Strength in Wireless Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 2004  RadioShack Corp.'s first-quarter profit jumped 21%, thanks largely to brisk sales of wireless products and services. The consumer- electronics retailer posted net income of $68.3 million, or 41 cents a share, up from $56.6 million, or 33 cents a share, a year earlier. Sales rose 2.1% to $1.09 billion.
