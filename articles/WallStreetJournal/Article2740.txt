 Prices end mixed in Tokyo and slip in London as global bourses keep wary eye on Wall Street   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1995  Tokyo stocks ended narrowly mixed on Jul 20, 1995 as the main indexes rose but the broader market fell. Tokyo's Nikkei 225-stock index rose 30.98 to 16453.42, while London's Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-share index slipped 4.9 to 3400.4 and Frankfurt's DAX 30-stock index rose 1.45 to 2184.33.
