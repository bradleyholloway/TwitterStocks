 Group Led by Gore Buys Cable Network   The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 2004  Terms of the deal, which had been in the works for a year, weren't disclosed. People familiar with the situation said Mr. [Al Gore]'s group, INdTV Holdings LLC, paid $70 million for the network. Mr. Gore will serve as chairman of INdTV and Joel Hyatt, founder of Hyatt Legal Services, will be chief executive.
