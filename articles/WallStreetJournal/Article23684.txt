 Blame Television for the Bubble   By Sollisch, Jim, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 2009  "The guy can barely string together two sentences!" And yet on episode after episode for this entire irrational decade, HGTV pumped up the housing bubble by parading the most mediocre, unworthy-looking homeowners into our living rooms to watch while they put their tacky, run-of-the-mill tract homes on the market for twice what they paid and then went out and bought houses with price tags too obscene to repeat.
