 Monday's markets: Markets react little to Russia   By Levingston, Steven E; Sesit, Michael R, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 1993  Stock and bond prices were mixed Oct 4, 1993, despite violence in Russia. Overseas stock prices were more muted than expected, while the Dow Jones Industrial Average slipped 3.35 to 3577.76. Bond prices were mixed and the dollar fell.
