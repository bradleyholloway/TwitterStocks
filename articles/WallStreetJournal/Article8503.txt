 Labatt to buy 22% of Femsa unit in bid to boost presence in Mexico   By Greenberg, Larry M, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 1994  Toronto-based brewer John Labatt Ltd agreed to pay $510 million for a 22% stake in the brewing division of Mexican beverage maker Fomento Economico Mexicano SA, or Femsa, in a bid to get into Mexico's fast-growing beer market. The deal is a blow for Philip Morris Cos's Miller Brewing Co unit, which already controls a 7.9% stake in Femsa and wants a stronger presence in Mexico.
