 Business Brief -- HANDSPRING INC.: Fourth-Quarter Net Loss Is Narrower Than Expected   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 2000  Handspring Inc., the Mountain View, Calif., handheld-device company, posted a narrower-than-expected quarterly loss in its first filing since its initial public offering in June. For the fiscal fourth quarter ended July 1, Handspring reported a net loss of $5.9 million, or 13 cents a diluted share.
