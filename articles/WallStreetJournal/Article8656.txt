 Aetna hits snag in talks to sell assets to Travelers   By Scism, Leslie, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1995  Aenta Life & Casualty Co has suspended talks to sell its huge property-casualty operations to longtime rival Travelers Group, though talks are likely to resume within weeks. The talks reportedly snagged over price-related issues. Aetna continues to talk to CNA Financial Corp and possibly others.
