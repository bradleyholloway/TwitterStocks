 Corporate News: Starbucks Workers Plan Chile Strike   By Jargon, Julie, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 2011  In April, the union presented Starbucks with a proposal seeking a monthly lunch bonus of $100 and listing about 20 other requests, such as having the company assume the full cost of employee health insurance. Because the demands haven't been met, the union voted on June 25 to strike.
