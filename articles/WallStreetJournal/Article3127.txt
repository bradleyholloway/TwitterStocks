 Chase Earnings Decreased 15% In Third Quarter   By By Allanna Sullivan, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 21, 1998  Chase Manhattan Corp., buffeted along with other major banks by global-market unrest and eroding economies in Asia and Russia, said third-quarter earnings slid 15%.  The nation's third-largest commercial bank reported earnings of $837 million, or 94 cents a diluted share, compared with $982 million, or $1.08 a diluted share, during the year-earlier quarter.  Chase reported operating income of 82 cents a diluted share, beating Wall Street estimates. Analysts had expected earnings of 77 cents a diluted share, according to a First Call Corp. consensus.
