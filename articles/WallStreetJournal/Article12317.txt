 Business Brief -- SIMON DEBARTOLO GROUP INC.: Increase of 28% Is Posted In Funds From Operations   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1998  Simon DeBartolo Group Inc, the biggest retail REIT, reported a 28% increase in its first-quarter funds from operations to $69 million from $54 million the previous year, citing the acquisition of new shopping malls and upgrades of existing properties.
