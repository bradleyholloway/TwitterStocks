 Hong Kong Deal Collapses as China's Checks Bounce   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1992  World Trade Center Group Ltd's sale of its primary asset--Hong Kong's 44-story World Trade Center, was canceled after the would-be buyer, Resourceful River Ltd, bounced three down-payment checks in less than a month. Resourceful River is backed by a Chinese government concern.
