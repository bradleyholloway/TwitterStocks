 Nasdaq Composite Inches Up 0.67%; Technology, Health Care Issues Rally   By Pettit, Dave, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 1991  Buyers crept back into some of the OTC stock market's most battered issues on May 2, 1991, lifting the Nasdaq Composite Index 3.28 points to 491.13. Apple Computer and Amgen had gains on the OTC market and Homedco Group had an initial public offering, closing up at 17 1/2 from the offering price of 15.
