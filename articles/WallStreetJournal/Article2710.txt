 Apple's fiscal 2nd-quarter net income rises sharply but is below expectations   By Zachary, G Pascal, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 1995  Buoyed by strong demand for its products, Apple Computer Inc posted sharply higher net income of $73 million for its second fiscal quarter ended Mar 31, 1995. Still, Apple's $.59 a share net fell well below analysts' forecasts of about $.93 a share.
