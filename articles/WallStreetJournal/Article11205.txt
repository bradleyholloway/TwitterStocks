 New Securities Issues [2]  The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1989  Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp. -- $350 million of Remic mortgage securities being offered in six classes by First Boston Corp. The offering, Series 35, brings Freddie Mac's Remic issuance this year to $3.35 billion and its total Remic volume to $17.85 billion. The offering is backed by Freddie Mac 10% securities. According to available details, yields range from 9.96%, a spread of 90 basis points over seven-year Treasury securities, to 10.35%, a spread of 135 basis points over 10-year Treasurys.
