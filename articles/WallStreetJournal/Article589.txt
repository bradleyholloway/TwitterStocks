 Whirlpool Seeks New Business Lines Beyond Appliances   By By Marj Charlier, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1985  The Benton Harbor, Mich., company said it increased to slightly more than 50% from 43% its stake in Inglis Ltd., a Canadian household appliance maker. And as previously reported, Whirlpool last week agreed to buy the KitchenAid appliance business from Dart & Kraft Inc.'s Hobart Corp. That acquisition is contingent on U.S. Justice Department clearance because of antitrust considerations.
