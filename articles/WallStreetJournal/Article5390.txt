 Chemed renews its offer for rest of Roto-Rooter   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 9, 1996  Chemed Corp, following an unsuccessful bid to buy the remaining 42% of Roto-Rooter Inc it does not own, said it will try again with a tender offer for any and all of the 2.2 million shares of Roto-Rooter not held by Chemed.
