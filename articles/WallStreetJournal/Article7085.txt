 MarketWatch: Watch Out for ATM Skimming   By Waters, Jennifer, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 2010  Sophisticated skimming devices placed right over a card-reader slot allow scammers to capture the information embedded on the magnetic strip of a debit or credit card. While a traditional bank heist will net the thief an average of $5,000, ATM pinching yields an average of $50,000, according to Doug Johnson, vice president of risk-management policy for the American Bankers Association.
