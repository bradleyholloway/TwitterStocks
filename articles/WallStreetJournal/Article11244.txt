 Niagara Exchange Says Holder Expresses Interest in Purchase   The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 1989  BUFFALO, N.Y. -- Niagara Exchange Corp.'s largest shareholder, disappointed by the insurer's stock performance, said it might be willing to purchase Niagara "on a friendly basis."  John R. Riedman, president of Riedman Corp., said in an April 24 letter to Niagara Chairman Charles Clauss that "a friendly sale would be in the best interest of both Niagara's shareholders and employees." Reidman, based in Rochester, N.Y., holds an 11.6% stake in Niagara.
