 Well, That Looked Familiar --- Just Like in Last Year's Finale, the Yankees Can't Hang on Against the Rays   By Barbarisi, Daniel, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 2012  The longtime Ray, exiled to the Cubs last season, drove in the winning run with a deep, bases loaded fly ball against Mariano Rivera as his Tampa teammates stormed the field. The dramatic ending capped a wild day full of questionable managerial decisions, where Joe Girardi walked a middle infielder to pitch to a 40-homer slugger, Rays manager Joe Maddon tried to have his catcher squeeze-bunt with two strikes, and where Girardi walked the bases loaded in the final inning and had his team play with five infielders.
