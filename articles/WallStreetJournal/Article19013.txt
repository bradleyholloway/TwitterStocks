 Exar, Benchmarq Microelectronics End Higher As Technology Shares Help Lift Small Stocks   By By Larry Bauman, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1998  Bolstered by the continued strength of the technology sector, small-capitalization and Nasdaq stocks pushed solidly higher, moderately outperforming the broader market.  The Russell 2000 index of small-capitalization stocks rose 3.38, or 0.78%, to 437.80, and the Nasdaq Composite Index gained 13.50, or 0.81%, to 1666.34, its highest close since Oct. 22.  "The larger-cap technology names have led the Nasdaq higher," said Peter Coolidge, managing director of equity trading at Brean Murray & Co.
