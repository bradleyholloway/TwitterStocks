 Metro AG: Preliminary Data Show Sales Increased by 4.1% Last Year   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 2006  German retailer Metro AG said the company's full-year 2005 preliminary sales were up 4.1%, reflecting strong performance internationally, although its sales in Germany slipped. Metro said 2005 revenue should total 55.7 billion euros ($67.2 billion) up from 53.5 billion euros in 2004.
