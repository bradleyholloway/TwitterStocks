 Business Brief -- BayBanks Inc.: New England Bank to Open 40 Branches in Stop & Shops   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1995  BayBanks Inc., Boston, said it has formed a partnership with the New England grocery chain Stop & Shop Cos. to open as many as 40 new bank branches in Stop & Shop Superstores during the next two years.
