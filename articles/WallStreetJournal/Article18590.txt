 Merger stars of the '80s are hot again   By Smith, Randall; Steinmetz, Greg, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 1994  With takeovers resurgent and hostile deals once more in vogue, merger advisers such as Robert Greenhill, Joseph Perella and Eric Gleacher--veterans of the biggest battles of the 1980s--are in demand again. Gleacher is advising American Home Products Corp on its $8.5 billion hostile takeover bid for American Cyanamid Co.
