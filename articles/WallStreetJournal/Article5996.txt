 Letters to the Editor: Breed Your Own Flies At Home! Here's How!   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 2001  Mr. Varadarajan's columns are a delight to read and a welcome diversion from the weightier topics on your editorial page. Mr. V's (excuse the familiarity) column on fruit flies doing a kamikaze dive into his red wine glass was especially welcome because he provided me with a use for the AOL disks that appear everywhere -- from my mail box to my cereal box. Mr. V tops his wine glass with CDs to prevent the wine from being invaded by fruit flies. I will use the CDs to prevent wasps, another plague that accompanies hot weather, from flying into my pop cans while outdoors.
