 Humana Inc.: Profit Rises 37% Amid Growth In Members for Medicare Plans   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 2006  Humana Inc.'s fourth-quarter profit jumped 37%, helped by increased membership in its Medicare plans. The Louisville, Ky., managed-care company posted net income of $64.6 million, or 39 cents a share, compared with $47.1 million, or 29 cents a share, a year earlier.
