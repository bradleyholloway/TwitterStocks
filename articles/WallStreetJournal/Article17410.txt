 Arts & Entertainment -- the Jazz Scene: Cabaret Illusions   By Friedwald, Will, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 2012  Politics aside, this is a truly stellar lineup, heavy on star saxophonists (Joe Lovano, Kenny Garrett, Jimmy Heath, Ravi Coltrane), but also pianists (Kenny Barron, Arturo O'Farrill, Geri Allen), bassists (Ron Carter, Christian McBride), drummers (Roy Haynes, Jeff "Tain" Watts), guitarist Jim Hall and superlative vocalist Dee Dee Bridgewater.
