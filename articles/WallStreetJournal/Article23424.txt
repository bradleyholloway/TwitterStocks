 UST's Kessler to Take CEO Post In January, Succeeding Gierer   By Vanessa O'Connell and Christina Cheddar Berk, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 2006  The promotion of Mr. [Murray S. Kessler], 47, comes at a time when makers of chewing tobacco and other noncigarette forms of tobacco face new competition from the two major cigarette giants, Altria Group Inc.'s Philip Morris USA and Reynolds American Inc.
