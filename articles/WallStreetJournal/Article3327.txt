 CMGI Invests $50 Million to Back Cash-Strapped Engage Inc. Unit   By By Joseph Pereira, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 2000  The investment moves by CMGI come as numerous Internet concerns have postponed or canceled public offerings. With funding from the public markets drying up in recent months, some investors have become wary of Internet incubators like CMGI, which partly rely on their ability to take public firms in which they have investments. CMGI holds minority and majority stakes in approximately 75 Internet companies. Mr. [David S. Wetherell] sought to calm worries about his company, assuring analysts that CMGI has $517 million in cash along with $1.6 billion worth of marketable of securities, giving the company sufficient means to fund all of its operations for 31 months.
