 EC Ministers Agree To Compromise On Mixed Credits --- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 1987  BRUSSELS, Belgium -- Common Market finance ministers agreed to a compromise proposal on rules to govern the use of mixed export credits.  European Community Commission negotiators discussed the proposal with other members of the Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development in Paris last month. But the EC needed formal ministerial approval before holding further talks within the OECD.
