 Investing in Funds: A Quarterly Analysis --- Will Stock-Picking Managers Be the Next Big Thing for ETFs?   By Light, Larry, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2010  "A whole slew of them will be coming to market," says Bryan Place, head of Place Financial Advisors in Manlius, N.Y.
