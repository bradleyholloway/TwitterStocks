 Legal beat: Payments to ex-partners   By Sangchompuphen, Tommy, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1995  The New York Court of Appeals ruled that a law firm can cut payments to departing partners based on their earnings in their new jobs without improperly barring competition. The case was brought against Milbank, Tweed, Hadley & McCloy by Kevin Hackett, a lawyer who left the firm in 1991 to join Fried, Frank, Harris, Shriver & Jacobson.
