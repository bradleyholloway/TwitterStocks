 Lockheed production of C-130s to increase on foreign demand   By Cole, Jeff, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 1996  Lockheed Martin Corp expects a 25% jump in production of its upgraded C-130 military transport planes by 1998, largely because of expected international sales and a new electronic-warfare version of the aircraft, a senior company executive said. Lockheed's entry into the growing global market for sophisticated battle-management planes is a challenge to rivals Boeing Co and Northrop Grumman Corp.
