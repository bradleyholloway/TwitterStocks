 World-wide: An Air Force captain   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 1995  An Air Force captain was cleared in 1994's "friendly fire" downing of two US helicopters over Iraq, closing the case with no one held criminally responsible for the 26 deaths. The verdict came in the court-martial of Jim Wang, a radar plane officer in charge of monitoring the no-fly zone when two F-15 fighters downed the helicopters.
