 The Americas: Don't Lock the Caribbean Basin Out of Free-Trade Zone   By Rodriguez, Miguel Angel, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 1992  Miguel Angel Rodriquez says that while negotiators in Mexico and the US are cheering the coming signing of the North American Free Trade Agreement, the countries of Central America and the Caribbean fear the agreement may exclude them from favorable trade arrangements. He says the region is not asking for direct aid, only better trade opportunities and investment alternatives.
