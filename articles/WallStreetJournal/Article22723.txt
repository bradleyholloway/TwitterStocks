 Web Upstart MuniAuction Wins Bond-Trade Patent   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 2001  MuniAuction conducted the first auction of a new bond issue over the Internet for the city of Pittsburgh in November 1997. Since then, the firm says it has conducted more than 1,100 auctions exceeding $1.1 trillion in volume. MuniAuction's patent was granted on Dec. 12.
