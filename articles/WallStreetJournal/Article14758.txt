 Nonfinancial Firms' Productivity Rises at 1.7% Annual Rate   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 1990  The productivity of nonfinancial corporations rose in the second quarter at a 1.7% annual rate after falling at a 2.2% rate in the first period, the Labor Department reported.  The increase in productivity -- output per hour of work -- stemmed from a rise in production at a 1.6% annual rate while working hours declined at a 0.1% pace.
