 Litton Wins Verdict Against Honeywell, Its Second in 2Years   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1998  WOODLAND HILLS, Calif. -- Litton Industries Inc. won a $250 million jury verdict in U.S. District Court in Los Angeles in its long-running antitrust case against Honeywell Inc.  The damage verdict is the second time in two years that Litton has won a trial on its allegations that Honeywell had attempted to monopolize the markets for commercial aircraft-guidance systems.
