 Staking out a clean corner on Broadway   By Wilson, Edwin, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1994  Dodger Productions, led by Michael David, its guiding spirit and resident guru, is profiled. Dodger Productions is now the most active new producing entity on Broadway. The Dodgers are represented at the moment on Broadway by "Guys & Dolls" and "Tommy" and are general managers of Disney's "Beauty and the Beast."
