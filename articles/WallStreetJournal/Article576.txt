 United Industrial Says It Will Sell Affiliated To British Company   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1985  United Industrial Corp. said it agreed to sell its 73%-owned Affiliated Hospital Products Inc. unit to Smith & Nephew Associated Cos. PLC for $57.9 million in cash and notes.  Smith & Nephew, a London-based maker of bandages, surgical dressings and pharmaceutical and paper products, agreed to pay $42.7 million, or $38 a share, in a 10-year note to United Industrial for its 73% stake in Affiliated.  For the remaining 27% of Affilated stock not owned by United Industrial, Smith & Nephew agreed to pay $15.2 million, or $36 a share, in cash. Affiliated has about 1,550,000 shares outstanding.
