 Corporate News: Alcoa Curbs Alumina Production   By Day, Matt; Stynes, Tess, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 2012  Alcoa Inc. said Thursday it plans to cut alumina production by about 2% this year, in an effort to match output with its plans to reduce smelter capacity amid global overcapacity and slumping prices for the lightweight metal.
