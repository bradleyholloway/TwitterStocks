 Retail Brief -- Kingfisher PLC: Retailer's Same-Store Sales Rose 4.1% for First Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 2003  Pan-European retailer Kingfisher PLC said fiscal first-quarter same-store sales, or sales at stores open at least a year, rose 4.1%, lifted by the British trend for making home improvements and the weaker pound.
