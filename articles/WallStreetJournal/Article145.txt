 Phillips Petroleum, Sumitomo Chemical Form Joint Venture   The Wall Street Journal,  May 22, 1992  BARTLESVILLE, Okla. -- Phillips Petroleum Co. and a Sumitomo Chemical Co. unit formed a partnership to expand the production of polypropylene in the U.S.  The partnership, Phillips Sumika Polypropylene Co., will be based in Houston with ownership split equally. But in 1993, Phillips said ownership will be based on capital contributions.
