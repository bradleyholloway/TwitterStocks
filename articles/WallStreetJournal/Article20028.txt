 Allianz AG: German Insurer's Net Profit Rises 64% to $2.91 Billion   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 2006  German insurer Allianz AG reported a 64% rise in second-quarter net profit and raised its full-year profit outlook, helped by operating improvements in all segments. Europe's largest insurer by gross premiums posted net profit of 2.28 billion euros ($2.91 billion), compared with 1.39 billion euros in the year-ago quarter.
