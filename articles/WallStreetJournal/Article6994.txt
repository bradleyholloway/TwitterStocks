 MarketWatch: It's Time to Cash In Old U.S. Bonds   By Coombes, Andrea, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 2010  (The good news is that, unlike money under your mattress, there are ways, through the U.S. Treasury, to recover savings bonds destroyed in a fire or other disaster.) Yes, interest rates on savings accounts are abysmally low now, but earning 1% or so is better than nothing at all, right? The U.S. Treasury has a team of workers who attempt to contact people with unredeemed bonds, and they encounter a variety of responses, says Joyce Harris, a spokeswoman with the U.S. Treasury Department's Bureau of the Public Debt.
