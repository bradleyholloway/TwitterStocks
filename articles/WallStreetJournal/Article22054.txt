 Growth is slowing in half of the states   By Bleakley, Fred R, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 2, 1995  According to indexes compiled by Wefa Group, nearly half the states are showing signs of slowing growth, with California, Iowa and Vermont exhibiting the weakest trends. It is a departure from the previous economic slowdown in 1990-1991, which affected mainly the Northeast and the West Coast.
