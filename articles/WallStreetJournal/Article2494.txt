 Tax report: Alimony alibis   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1993  The state of California hopes to recover $10 million by uncovering excessive alimony deductions by payers and unreported income by recipients. In 1993, the state sent 70,000 questionnaires to people who claimed $900 million in alimony deductions on 1990 state returns.
