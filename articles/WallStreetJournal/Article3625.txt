 details: E-Wear Hits Malls, Wired for Fashion   By By Mei Fong, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 20, 2002  FOR YEARS, Dale Coffing resigned himself to wearing bulky fisherman vests and capacious, unflattering pants. It wasn't his weight -- it was his gadgets. But now, all his gear -- cellphone, MP3 player, digital camera and pocket PC -- is stowed in a slim-fitting blue jacket. His $140 Scott "e-vest," from Technology Enabled Clothing, has hidden chambers for each gadget and secret channels through the lining for all the wires. "I feel like a secret agent," he says.
