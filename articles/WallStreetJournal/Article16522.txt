 Money-Market Fund Assets Fall   The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 2005  The highest seven-day compound yield among retail taxable general- purpose money funds belonged to PayPal Money Market Fund at 2.96%, followed by Scudder MM Series Premium/AARP K at 2.83%, followed by Scudder MM Series Prime Reserve/AARP at 2.79%.  The average seven-day simple yield for tax-free money-market funds rose to 2.19% from 2.06%, while the seven-day compound yield climbed to 2.22% from 2.08%. The 30-day simple yield rose to 1.86% from 1.71% and the 30-day compound yield rose to 1.87% from 1.72%.
