 Kimberly-Clark Sets Employee Agreement At Alabama Factory   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 1988  The maker of health care, personal care and paper products said the agreement with the United Association of Journeymen and Apprentices of the Plumbing and Pipefitting Industry; United Papermakers International Union; International Association of Machinists and Aerospace Workers, and International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers also extends their contracts with the company to September 1992. The contracts were to expire Sept. 15, 1989.
