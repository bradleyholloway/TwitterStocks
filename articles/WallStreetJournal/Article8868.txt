 Platinum and palladium futures prices plunge   By Podolsky, Paul, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 1997  Platinum and palladium futures took a plunge on the New York Mercantile Exchange on Aug 6, 1997, with the October platinum contract closing down $18.60 at $431, and September palladium closing down $12.00 at $215.60 an ounce.
