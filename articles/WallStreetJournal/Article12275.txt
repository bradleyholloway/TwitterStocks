 Business Brief -- FREEPORT-MCMORAN SULPHUR INC.: Production Lowered by 8% Because of U.S. Oversupply   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1998  Freeport-McMoRan Sulphur Inc, New Orleans, said it will reduce its production of sulphur in 1998 by about 8% as a result of an oversupply in the US of the material used in phosphate fertilizers. Total production is projected at between 2.3 million and 2.5 million tons.
