 AFG Industries Inc. Holders Sue to Block Offer by Chairman   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 1988  Two AFG Industries Inc. shareholders filed class-action lawsuits in Delaware Chancery Court, seeking to block a $33-a-share tender offer for AFG by a unit of Clarity Holdings Corp., which was recently formed by Randall D. Hubbard, AFG's chairman.  The complaints, brought by Gary Goldberg and United Operating Co., also name Clarity Holdings, Mr. Hubbard and other AFG officers as defendants. The suits were attached to a Securities and Exchange Commission filing.
