 European stocks tumble again in reaction to lingering jitters of Wall Street investors   By Calian, Sara, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 1997  European stocks tumbled for a second day in a row on Apr 2, 1997, still reacting to Wall Street's two-day plunge on Mar 27 and Mar 31 and the continued jitters since then. Frankfurt's 30-share IBIS DAX stock index dropped 70.52 points to close at 3210.94, while London's Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-stock index fell 11.5 points to 4236.6. In Tokyo, the Nikkei 225-stock index gained 167.71 points to close at 18037.30.
