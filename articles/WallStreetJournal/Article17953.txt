 Pickens Isn't on Payroll At Mesa, but Fees Pay Well   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1989  Mr. Pickens doesn't receive a salary from Mesa, a large independent natural gas producer. But the partnership paid him $1.8 million in general partner fees last year. And he received $21.1 million in acquisition fees, including a cut related to Mesa's $715 milion purchase of Tenneco Inc. properties last year and a fee for the sale of some assets.
