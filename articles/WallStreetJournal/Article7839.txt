 Take your pick of population scenarios   By Anonymous, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 1989  The Census Bureau has come up with 30 distinct US population growth possibilities for the next 90 years. The variables include immigration, births, and deaths. According to how these variables play out, the population could: 1) increase to 320 million over the next 25 years and then continue to grow; 2) grow to 265 million by 2015 and then fall; and 3) grow to 302 million by 2038 and then fall.
