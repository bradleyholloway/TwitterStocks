 America West Airlines: Debt and Class B warrants are headed for repurchase   The Wall Street Journal,  May 21, 1996  America West Airlines said it had undertaken debt and equity purchases totaling more than $50 million. The transactions include a $25 million principal prepayment on its 10 3/4% senior unsecured notes, due 2005, and the purchase of about 2.2 million warrants to purchase its Class B shares.
