 Koscot Interplanetary Creditor Panel Delayed First Such Committee Is Accused Of Being Closely Tied to Firm Which Is Under Chapter 11  The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 9, 1973  ORLANDO, Fla. An attempt to form a creditors committee in a Koscot Interplanetary Inc. Chapter 11 bankruptcy proceeding had to be postponed, following charges that an unofficial committee was closely linked to the company and had tried to mislead creditors.
