 Business Brief -- RAO Norilsk Nickel: Conversion to ADRs Planned For up to 50 Million Shares   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 2001  Russian metals concern RAO Norilsk Nickel will convert as many as 50 million shares, equal to about 20% of its equity outstanding, into American depositary receipts, General Director Alexei Zhdanov said.
