 On Investing: For Munis, Don't Sweat Insurer Woes   By Pollock, Michael A, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 2008  Concerns stem from moves in recent years by Ambac Financial Group, MBIA and other, smaller insurers to expand into riskier parts of the bond market, such as subprime mortgage-related issues. Ratings firms have threatened to cut ratings of the insurers because of concerns about the financial impact of having to compensate bondholders for missed interest payments, or ultimately, for some borrowers' failure to return investors' money.
