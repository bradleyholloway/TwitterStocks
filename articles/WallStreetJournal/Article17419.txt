 Heard & Scene: Swapping Storm Stories At Weekend Openings   By Vilensky, Mike; Brillantes, Mariam, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 2012  A few blocks east, "SEAL Team Six," a TV movie about the military raid that killed Osama bin Laden last year, was screened at the Core Club by the Peggy Siegal Co. A somewhat subdued crowd there swapped storm stories in the lobby of the club before the film began.
