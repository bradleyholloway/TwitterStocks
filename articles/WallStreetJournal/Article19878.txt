 Automotive Brief -- Magna International Inc.: Production Cuts by Customers Lead to 4% Decline in Earnings   The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 2005  Magna International Inc. reported a 4% decline in first-quarter earnings, as the automobile-parts manufacturer struggled to cope with the effects of production cuts at its biggest customers. The Aurora, Ontario, concern said net income fell to $172 million, or $1.68 a share, from $179 million, or $1.84 a share, a year earlier.
