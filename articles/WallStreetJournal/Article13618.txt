 Commercial Property Doing Relatively Well   By Anonymous, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 2009  Once construction loans are pulled out, commercial/multi-family mortgages have the lowest charge-off rates of any category of bank-held loans, are second lowest (to commercial and industrial loans) in terms of 30-plus day delinquency rates, and are second lowest (to non-credit card loans to individuals) in the deterioration in 30-plus day delinquency rates from the third quarter to the fourth.
