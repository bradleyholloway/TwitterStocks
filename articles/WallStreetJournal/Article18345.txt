 Playboy, Hefner to Sell Class B Stock to Public   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 1992  Playboy Enterprises Inc. said founder Hugh Hefner plans to sell two million of his non-voting Class B common shares in a public offering.  A trust Mr. Hefner controls owns 9.9 million, or 72%, of the 13.8 million non-voting Class B shares currently outstanding.  Mr. Hefner's voting control of Playboy won't be diminished by the planned sale, however: His trust also owns 71% of the about 4.7 million Class A Playboy shares, which are the only shares with voting power.
