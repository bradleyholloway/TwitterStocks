 Campaign '08: McCain, Obama Canvass Campuses to Win Over Young Voters   By Amy Chozick in Abington, Pa., and Stephanie Simon in Pueblo, Colo., The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 2008  Presidential nominees Barack Obama and John McCain held events on college campuses this week, with the former hoping a strong student turnout can help offset potential losses among the white working class, and the latter looking belatedly to claim his share of a growing part of the electorate.
