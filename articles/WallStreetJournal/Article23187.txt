 Walt Disney Co.: Three Executives Are Awarded More Than $10 Million in Stock   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 2004  Walt Disney Co. this week awarded stock valued at more than $10 million total to three executives, including President and Chief Operating Officer Robert Iger, according to regulatory filings. The stock represents payouts of stock units Disney granted to each of the officers in 2002, according to the filings.
