 Deal Journal / Breaking Insight From WSJ.com   By Moore, Heidi N, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 2008  Taking Aim At a Tax Break --- Measure Would End A Ruling That Aided PNC, Wells Fargo Buys Wells Fargo's $15.1 billion acquisition of Wachovia and PNC Financial Services Group's $5.52 billion acquisition of National City were the first of their kind, deals in which the acquiring banks were encouraged to buy troubled rivals in part by tax breaks promised by the Internal Revenue Service.
