 Honorable disobedience   By Jaspert, W P, The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 1995  W. P. Jaspert responds to Lawrence P. Rockwood's Apr 4, 1995 commentary "Court Martial--A Soldier's Story" by saying that his family always stressed that they had been proud Germans until the Nazis arrived and killed his father and brother for their opposition to the Nazis. Jaspert says his family learned not to obey immoral orders.
