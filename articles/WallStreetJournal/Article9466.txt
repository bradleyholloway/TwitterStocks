 Letters to the Editor: `Reproductive Rights Are Human Rights'   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 2002  The Bush administration has consistently denied women access to life-saving reproductive health services, like birth control, checkups and HIV education and prevention. By contrast, the United Nations Population Fund, which the president de-funded, provides a voluntary alternative to China's coercive and demeaning program. President Bush's own investigative team found no connection between UNFPA programs and forced abortion.
