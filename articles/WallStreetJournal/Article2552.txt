 Freeport-McMoRan Copper & Gold   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 1993  Freeport-McMoRan Copper & Gold Inc said its Indonesian operating unit, P.T. Freeport Indonesia Co, sold some nonmining assets, mostly Indonesian multifamily residential properties, for $90 million to a joint venture that is two-thirds owned and operated by Alatief Nusakarya Corp.
