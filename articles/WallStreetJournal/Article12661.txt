 Letters to the Editor: Oil-Use Predictions Wrong Since 1974   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 2001  Many such predictions have been heard since 1974, when the Persian Gulf nations produced 21.6 million barrels daily (mbd). Last year, they produced 19.9 mbd, and even this was the highest in several years. Moreover, in 1973 less than a million bd were consumed at home; today, more than 3 mbd.
