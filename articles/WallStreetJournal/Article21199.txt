 W.R. Grace Sheds Fertilizer Facilities, Buys Chemical Firm   The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 1988  W.R. Grace & Co. said it sold part of its agricultural chemical business for $55 million and bought a specialty chemical concern for $20 million.  Grace, a chemical, energy and manufacturing concern, said it sold fertilizer facilities in Memphis, Tenn., and Wilmington, N.C., to Western Branch Holding Co. of Chesapeake, Va.
