 Business Brief -- Adelphia Communications Corp.: Co-Borrowings Are Calculated At Lower Total Than Expected   The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 2002  Adelphia Communications Corp., seeking to soothe investor concerns about off-balance sheet loans, announced that its co-borrowing arrangements with members of the company's controlling family totaled $1.6 billion last year, rather than the $2 billion or more expected by analysts.
