 Sale of Stake in American Express Unit Set   By Pae, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1992  The American Express Company is expected to make a public offering of shares of its Information Services Corporation subsidiary. Information Services is the parent of First Data Resources, a third party processor. American Express wants to sell only 35% to 45% of its interest, in an effort to improve its capital base. Moody's Investors Service recently lowered its rating of American Express' debt from double A-2 to A-1.
