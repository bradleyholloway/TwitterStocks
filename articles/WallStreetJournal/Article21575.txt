 Japanese Investor Nangaku Discusses Dunes Hotel Sale With at Least 2 Parties   By By Pauline Yoshihashi, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1991  LAS VEGAS, Nev. -- Japanese investor Masao Nangaku is talking to Hilton Hotels Corp. and at least one unidentified private party regarding a sale or other arrangement for his Dunes Hotel & Country Club, persons familiar with the situation said.  The talks come just weeks after the 72-year-old businessman won a lengthy battle for a permanent casino license from the Nevada Gaming Commission. The regulators' investigation aired concerns about the financial stability of Mr. Nangaku's world-wide investments and his ability to keep the Dunes afloat.
