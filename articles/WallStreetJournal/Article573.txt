 Multifoods Forms 2 Operating Groups And a New Top Office   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1985  International Multifoods Corp. created an office of the chief executive and two new operating groups, to strengthen management and improve the company's efficiency.  The new office will consist of Andre Gillet, 58-year-old president and chief executive officer, and Robert M. Howard, who will remain executive vice president.  The two new operating groups, agri-industrial and non-U.S. operations, are a "logical and efficient combination of related operations and support the strategic direction we have set for the company," Mr. Gillet said. The two groups will report to the office of the chief executive.
