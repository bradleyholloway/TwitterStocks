 Fina Near Accord to Give Saudi Group Stake in Refining, Marketing Operations   By Solomon, Caleb; Sullivan, Allanna, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 1992  Fina Inc, the Dallas-based affiliate of Petrofina SA of Belgium, has reached an agreement with Saudi businessmen on the major outline of an oil refining joint venture. Dec 31, 1992 has been set as the deadline for completing the deal.
