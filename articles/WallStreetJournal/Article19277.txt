 Money-Market Funds See Net $21.56 Billion Taken Out by Investors   The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 2000  Investors pulled a net $21.56 billion out of money-market funds in the week ended Tuesday, according to Money Fund Report, a newsletter.  Seven-day simple yields on taxable money funds stayed steady at 5.45%, as did seven-day compound yields at 5.60%. Thirty-day simple yields also stayed steady at 5.46%, as did 30-day compound yields, at 5.61%, Money Fund Report said. Compound yields assume reinvestment of dividends.
