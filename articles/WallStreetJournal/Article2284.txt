 Procter & Gamble's Joint Ventures to Get Clearance From EC ---- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 21, 1991  The European Community Commission said it would clear a series of sanitary-product joint ventures between Procter & Gamble Co. of the U.S. and Italy's Finaf S.p.A. following agreement by the companies to make changes.
