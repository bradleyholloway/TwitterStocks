 Metall Mining to Sell Some Teck Shares In a Public Offering   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 1992  The Class B shares of Teck Corp. dropped sharply yesterday in heavy trading, after a major shareholder said it plans a public offering of five million of the shares at 18.75 Canadian dollars (US $15.04) each.  Metall Mining Corp. of Toronto, the holder, said it plans to use gross proceeds of C$93.8 million to help finance its recent C$136.6 million acquisition of a 50.4% stake in Toronto mining concern Minnova Inc. Metall is due to pay the final C$100 million installment for the Minnova purchase on Dec. 31.
