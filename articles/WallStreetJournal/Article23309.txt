 Picks -- Radio: Holiday Listening; More stations switch to all-Christmas music for the holidays -- and race to do it first   By Jurgensen, John, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 2005  CHRISTMAS HAS COME early for many radio stations this year. In recent years, some stations have started playing holiday music around the clock in the weeks before Christmas, a strategy that has boosted ratings. This year, the competition is getting tougher. About 300 stations nationwide are making the holiday switch this year, up from 272 last year, according to Scott Fybush, who tracks radio trends on his industry Web site, 100000watts.com. And they're starting earlier: WTTH-FM in Atlantic City, N.J., flipped on Oct. 17, three weeks earlier than anyone had the year before.
