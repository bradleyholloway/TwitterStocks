 Meredith to Record Gain From Sale of TV Station   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1986  DES MOINES, Iowa -- Meredith Corp. said it expects to record a gain equal to $1.25 to $1.35 a share during the fiscal year ending June 30, 1987, from the proposed sale of WPGH-TV, Pittsburgh, to Lorimar-Telepictures Corp.
