 International Brief -- Petrofina SA: Belgian Oil Group's Profit Dropped 8.1% in First Half   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1993  Belgian oil group Petrofina SA said first-half net income fell 8.1% to 3.8 billion Belgian francs ($105.7 million), despite a capital gain of 2.5 billion francs from the sale of Belgian real estate and U.S. gas properties.
