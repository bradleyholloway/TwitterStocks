 ConocoPhillips: Liquefied-Natural-Gas Project Is Planned in Gulf of Mexico   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 21, 2005  ConocoPhillips, Houston, plans to develop a liquefied-natural-gas terminal in the Gulf of Mexico, 50 miles south of Louisiana. The project is the latest in a series of offshore ports proposed in the gulf to receive LNG shipments from overseas.
