 Storms Whip the Market...a Hurricane, Too   By Zuckerman, Gregory, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 2012  The market's post-Sandy performance revealed a world of worries besetting Wall Street: disappointing third-quarter results, this week's presidential election, the looming "fiscal cliff" and the possible departure of Ben Bernanke at the helm of the Federal Reserve. "Sales misses have been widespread across all sectors," says Savita Subramanian, head of U.S. equity and quantitative strategy at Bank of AmericaMerrill Lynch.
