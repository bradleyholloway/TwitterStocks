 Conair Corp. Expects Financing of Buyout By June 20 Meeting   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1985  EDISON, N.J. -- Conair Corp.'s chairman and president hasn't gotten the funds to take the company private in a leveraged buyout, but company officials say they expect financing to be completed by the annual meeting June 20.  As previously reported, Leandro P. Rizzuto, who owns 40% of the company's 9.6 million shares outstanding, has offered $24.70 a share, or $168.9 million, for the remaining shares.
