 EDS Net Surges, Aided by Business With Governments   By Richmond, Riva, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 2006  EDS said revenue from U.S. government customers jumped 28% during the quarter, thanks to improvements to and the extension of its giant contract with the Navy. During the quarter, EDS won a three-year extension to a contract to build and run a communications network for the U.S. Navy and Marine Corps.
