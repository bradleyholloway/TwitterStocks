 Martin Revson settles SEC claims of insider trading   By Woo, Junda, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1993  Martin E. Revson, a former chairman of Del Laboratories Inc, agreed to pay almost $1.6 million to settle a civil suit by the SEC. Revson was accusing of taking part in a high-society insider-trading ring that allegedly profited from trading Kidde Inc securities in 1987.
