 U.S. Treasury's Yield On 52-Week Bill Sale Decreases to 5.68%   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 13, 1987  The Treasury sold $9.516 billion of 52-week bills at an average annual yield of 5.68%.  The yield was down from the 5.74% average annual yield at the previous auction Feb. 12 and was the lowest since the auction held Jan. 15 yielded 5.44%.
