 Al Qaeda Could Seek New Base In Asia, U.S. Commander Warns   By By Barry Wain, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 2001  Investigators may soon confirm links between al Qaeda and the Abu Sayyaf organization that operates in the southern Philippines, Adm. [Dennis Blair] said. While some members of Abu Sayyaf are known to have trained in Afghanistan, and Philippine intelligence officials said Mr. bin Laden has funded the group, those ties were thought to have ended about five years ago. But as Philippine armed forces bear down on Abu Sayyaf, Adm. Blair said, "we're seeing increasing evidence that there are potential current links" to al Qaeda. He declined to elaborate.
