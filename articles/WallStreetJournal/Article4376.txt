 Rabbi, 2 Family Members Sentenced for Tax Scheme   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 1988  The eldest of the three, Schnejer Zalman Gurary, was sentenced to three years in prison and fined $2 million. His son-in-law, Nochum Sternberg, received 18 months, and his daughter, Esther Sternberg, was sentenced to 120 days. The Sternbergs also were fined a total of $1 million. All three also were given five years of probation.
