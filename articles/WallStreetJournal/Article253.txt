 Wausau Paper to acquire Mosinee for stock valued at $442 million   By Miller, James P, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 25, 1997  Wausau Paper Mills Co has agreed to acquire Mosinee Paper Co for stock currently valued at about $442 million, according to people familiar with the situation. Directors of the two Wisconsin paper concerns are said to have approved the combination over the weekend of Aug 23, 1997.
