 More Self-Destructive Protectionism from Detroit   By Ingrassia, Paul, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 7, 1991  Paul Ingrassia says that the Big Three's filing of a petition charging Japanese auto makers with dumping minivans in the US is clearly self-destructive. Ingrassia argues that Japanese competition is rejuvenating the US auto industry in ways not even Big Three executives expected a few years back.
