 Boston Globe Went Really Deep To Score With Patriots Fans   By Nelson, Emily, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 2004  The Globe sold the paper for 25 cents in Texas and a few big "sponsor" advertisers helped defray the costs. Dunkin' Donuts purchased an ad wrapping around the Globe on Sunday and Monday and local Houston sports bar Tavern on Gray had a wrap ad on Friday. Tavern on Gray sold about $80,000 in beer, or about 17,000 beers, during the Super Bowl and surrounding days, said owner Charlie Watkins, many to New England fans. "We were crazy-packed," he said.
