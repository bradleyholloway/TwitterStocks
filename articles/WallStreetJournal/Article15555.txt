 PC's Big Three to join digital-TV fight   By Bank, David; Takahashi, Dean, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1997  The Big Three of the personal-computer industry will seek to persuade TV broadcasters to adopt their technical standards for digital TV, an uphill battle to ensure PCs play a major role in delivering the next generation of home entertainment. Microsoft, Intel Corp and Compaq Computer Corp on Apr 7, 1997 will propose a digital-TV strategy that emphasizes Internet-based information services and interactivity, as well as high-definition picture quality.
