 CNBC will air a show owned, vetted by IBM   By Beatty, Sally Goll, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 1996  NBC on Jun 3, 1996 revealed plans for "Scan," a new program on its CNBC cable channel that the network hailed as a first of its kind: a series about technology's impact on people's lives that will be aired in markets around the world. IBM Corp, one of the world's biggest-spending advertisers, will be the series' exclusive worldwide sponsor and will have final say over its content.
