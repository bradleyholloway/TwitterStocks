 Letters to the Editor: St. Petersburg Da, Leningrad Nyet   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 1985  David Satter's editorial-page article of Oct. 22 ("Moscow Feeds a Lap-Dog Foreign Press") charging the Western press with gross failure of initiative helps to answer a question that has bothered me since visiting Leningrad last year. My expectations were largely colored by experiences in Copenhagen, Stockholm, Oslo and Helsinki. Leningrad would be yet another Northern European city -- under a different political system, of course, but shaped over centuries by the same shared forces of geography, culture, climate that give those other graceful cities at least a superficial similarity.
