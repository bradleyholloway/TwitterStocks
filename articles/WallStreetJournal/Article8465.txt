 Politics & people: In praise of presidential prerogatives   By Hunt, Albert R, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1994  Albert R. Hunt comments on the notion following the frenzy of the Whitewater scandal that the president is not supposed to set the policy of his own administration. Hunt says it is absurd to reach such a conclusion, yet it has accelerated in the early weeks following Whitewater.
