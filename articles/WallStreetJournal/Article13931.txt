 Pressure Mounts in Forex Probe Of BNY   By Carrick Mollenkamp, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 2011  Amid a weak economy, the banking industry has lobbied for less-stringent oversight to allow financial institutions to recover. Besides New York, state prosecutors in Virginia and Florida have sued BNY Mellon, and California has sued rival State Street Corp., accusing them of improperly pricing currency trades.
