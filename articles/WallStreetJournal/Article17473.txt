 Emergency Aid For Israel Clears House Committee   By By Robert S. Greenberger, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1985  The House Foreign Affairs Committee, ignoring a Reagan administration request, approved $1.5 billion in emergency economic aid for Israel.  The administration has been withholding the aid to prod Israel into taking additional austerity measures to correct its severe economic problems. Earlier yesterday, Secretary of State George Shultz told a Senate appropriations subcommittee that "Israel's economy needs a lot of straightening around." He added that "in my judgment they aren't there yet and we should wait."
