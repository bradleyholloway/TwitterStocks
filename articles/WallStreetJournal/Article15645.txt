 Artra Group Receives $11 Million to Settle Suit Against Salomon   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 1998  NORTHFIELD, Ill. -- Artra Group Inc. said it received $11 million to settle two lawsuits it filed against Salomon Brothers Inc., D.P. Kelly & Associates L.P. and others in connection with the 1989 leveraged buyout of Envirodyne Industries Inc.
