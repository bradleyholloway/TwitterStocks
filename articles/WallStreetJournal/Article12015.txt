 World-wide: Georgian leader Shevardnadze   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 1995  Georgian leader Eduard Shevardnadze voiced confidence as the country's voters cast ballots in presidential and parliamentary elections. Shevardnadze faced four challengers in his re-election bid, but the only serious rival was a Soviet-era Communist Party boss, Dzhumber Patiashvili.
