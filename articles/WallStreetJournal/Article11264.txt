 Legal People   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 1989  FORMER TIME INC. COUNSEL JOINS FIRM: William M. Guttman, formerly vice president, general counsel and secretary of Time Inc., joined the New York law firm of Baer Marks & Upham yesterday. Mr. Guttman, 58 years old, is a partner in the corporate department of the 95-lawyer firm. He came to Time as an associate general counsel in 1978 and became the company's general counsel in 1984.
