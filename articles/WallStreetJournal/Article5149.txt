 Barclays earnings more than tripled for the first half   By Bray, Nicholas, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1994  Barclays PLC's pretax earnings for the first half rose to 1.04 billion British pounds ($1.60 billion) from BP335 million a year earlier, significantly exceeding analysts' expectations. The rise in earnings was due to a 64% plunge in provisions for losses on bad and doubtful loans.
