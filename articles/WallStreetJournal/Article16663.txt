 TPI Polene PCL: Thai Cement Maker Aims To Triple Capital, Trim Debt   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 2006  TPI Polene PCL plans to more than triple its registered capital and use the proceeds to reduce debt. The Thai cement producer seeks to raise 16.78 billion baht ($440.8 million) via a rights issue and a private share placement in August, the company's board said in a statement to the Stock Exchange of Thailand.
