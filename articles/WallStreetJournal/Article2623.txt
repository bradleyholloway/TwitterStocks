 Vitamins C, E and Beta Carotene fail to cut risk of new colon polyps in test   By Bishop, Jerry E, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1994  A four-year experiment involving people who had developed one benign colon polyp found that taking vitamin supplements with C, E or beta carotene did not prevent additional polyps, believed to be the precursors to malignant tumors, from developing.
