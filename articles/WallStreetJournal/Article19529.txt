 Career Journal -- Help Wanted: Foundation Fishes For Operating Chief   By By Kris Maher, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 2002  Since its creation in 1984, the federally chartered nonprofit has handed out more than 5,000 grants to help conserve native fish, plants, wildlife and habitats. Projects range from helping ranchers develop better grazing practices to restoring fish populations in rivers. The organization has a staff of about 72 in 10 offices. It supports about 2,000 continuing projects and disburses between $30 million and $50 million annually.
