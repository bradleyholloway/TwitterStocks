 Valeo SA: International sales push up first-quarter revenue 11%   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1997  French car-parts maker Valeo SA said first-quarter revenue rose 11% from a year ago to 8.24 billion French francs ($1.47 billion). The company had reported first-quarter 1996 revenue of 7.4 billion francs, but its structure has been altered since then.
