 Skinner Accused of Taking Golf Trips at Taxpayer Cost   The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 1992  The CBS News program "60 Minutes" reported that White House Chief of Staff Samuel Skinner, while serving as secretary of the DOT, took government planes on 10 trips that included golf outings and upgraded his pilot's license. The total expense to taxpayers was about $1 million.
