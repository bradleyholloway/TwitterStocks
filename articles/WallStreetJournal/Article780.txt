 Share Prices Surge On Tokyo Exchange, But Fall in London --- A Wall Street Journal News Roundup   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 1986  Share prices surged in Tokyo Friday in reaction to the U.S.'s cut in its discount rate, but stocks fell in London.  On the Tokyo Stock Exchange, the Nikkei stock average soared 200.95 points, to 17670.77, in heavy trading. The exchange's index climbed 16.30 points, to 1375.15.
