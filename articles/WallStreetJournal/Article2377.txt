 CalFed Loss Widens in Quarter as Problem of Bad Loans Persists   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1992  Trying to avoid a government takeover, CalFed Inc posted a wider second-quareter loss of $23.2 million compared to the year-earlier loss of $18.6 million. The poor second quarter reflected continuing high levels of bad loans and other troubled assets, mostly related to real estate.
