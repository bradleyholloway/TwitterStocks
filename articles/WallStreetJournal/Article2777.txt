 SoftKey International plans software alliance   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1995  SoftKey International Inc is teaming up with Minnesota Educational Computer Corp to create a new line of low-cost educational software for home use. SoftKey will license teaching programs produced by Minnestoa Educational and produce scaled-down versions selling for $12.95, a significant discount over current prices of about $40-$50.
