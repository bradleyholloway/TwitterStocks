 Levi Strauss Associates Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 1992  Levi Strauss Associates Inc said fourth-quarter profit rose 40%, backed by the popularity of its jeans and Dockers label clothing. The company posted profit of $105.5 million on a 17% jump in revenue to $1.36 billion, compared with net income of $75.3 million on revenue of $1.16 billion a year earlier. It was the company's fifth consecutive year of record sales.
