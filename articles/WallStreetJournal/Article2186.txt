 New York Area Lost 100,000 Jobs in 1990, Data Say   By Graven, Kathryn, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 1990  The New York-northeastern New Jersey area lost some 100,000 private sector jobs in 1990, and the employment outlook is expected to remain bleak in 1991, the US Department of Labor's Middle Atlantic Regional office said.
