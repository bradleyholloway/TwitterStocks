 USAir Group Inc.: Some fares are reduced as much as 40% for fall   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 1995  USAir Group Inc said that it cut fares on some transcontinental routes by as much as 40% for the fall and winter seasons. The discounts are good for travel before Mar 7, 1996, and tickets must be bought at least 14 days in advance.
