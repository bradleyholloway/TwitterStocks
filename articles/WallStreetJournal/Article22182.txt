 An Ambush Ad? They're Shocked -- Just Shocked!   By By Eleena de Lisser, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 1996  Anheuser-Busch Cos. is the official beer sponsor of the Summer Olympics. But that's not stopping Heineken, one of its competitors, from catching a piece of the action.  Heineken, the Dutch brewer, is running a series of billboards in Atlanta that feature snippets of conversation overheard in metro Atlanta bars. But the pieces of conversation offered up on the billboards appear to have a connection to the Olympics.
