 The Perils of Treating Siblings Differently   By Opdyke, Jeff D, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 2010  Through the years, our son has always made a big deal of the games he plays -- showing Amy and me what he's doing, inviting his friends to spend the night so they all can play and generally expressing great enthusiasm about the games. While the obsessive game playing has had its pluses -- our son already knows he wants to pursue a college degree in computer graphics, for instance -- the main impact has been negative.
