 Moving the Market: Prosecutors in KPMG Shelter Case Show No Signs of Backing Down   By Davies, Paul, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 2006  In a 173-page brief filed in federal court in Manhattan late Friday, attorneys for the government argued that motions by the defense to have the case thrown out should be denied. Last month, attorneys representing the 19 defendants -- 17 of whom are former KPMG tax professionals -- filed 28 briefs totaling 950 pages that accused prosecutors of "distorting" facts and called for the dismissal.
