 Dollar Is Lifted by Purchases to Cover Oversold Positions in Lackluster Market   By Lee, Charlene, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1992  The dollar rose in a late bout of short-covering on Jun 9, 1992, but the market is still uncertain of the currency's near-term direction. In late New York trading, the dollar was quoted at 1.5914 marks and 127.44 yen, up from DM1.5873 and Y127.28 the day before. Sterling was unchanged at $1.8345.
