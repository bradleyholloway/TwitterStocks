 Corporate News: Amazon Adds Epix Content --- Multiyear Licensing Deal With Cable Channel Raises Competition With Netflix   By Sherr, Ian; Ben Fox Rubin, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 2012  Amazon.com Inc. upped its competition against Netflix Inc., signing a multiyear licensing deal with cable channel Epix, bringing a raft of popular films such as Paramount Pictures' "Iron Man 2" and "The Hunger Games" from Lions Gate Entertainment Corp. to Amazon's fledgling movie streaming service.
