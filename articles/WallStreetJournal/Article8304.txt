 Computers: 'Virtual Reality' Patents Gained by French Firm   By Zachary, G Pascal, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 1992  France's Thomson CSF SA obtained the basic American virtual reality patents of VPL Research Inc, a closely held concern in which Thomson holds a minority stake, after VPL failed to repay a series of loans made by Thomson's vanture-capital arm. Technical guru Jaron Lanier leads VPL's top-flight team of unconventional engineers who have made the company a US leader in virtual reality.
