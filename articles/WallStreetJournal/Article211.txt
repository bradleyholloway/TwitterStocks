 Germany lowers interest rates   By Roth, Terrence; Sesit, Michael R, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 25, 1995  Germany's central bank lowered two key interest rates, amid signs of a slowing economic recovery and complaints from German companies that the nation's strong currency is hurting business. The interest-rate cuts sent the value of the US dollar on a wild roller-coaster ride.
