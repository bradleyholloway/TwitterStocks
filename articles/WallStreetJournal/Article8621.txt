 Mutual funds quarterly review: A Latin fund manager's bumpy ride reflects the sector's zigzag fortunes   By Calian, Sara, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 1995  Huge swings in performance can make Latin American mutual-fund managers such as William "Ted" Truscott of Scudder Latin America Fund into emotional wrecks. Truscott's fund was up 13.31% for the three months ended Jun 29, 1995, a big reverse from a drop of 23.2% in the first quarter.
