 Applied Digital to Sell ID Chip That Goes Under the Skin   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 2002  VeriChip emits a radio signal and has been derided by some for its "Big Brother" implications. Applied Digital has said it could prove invaluable in emergency situations when someone is either unconscious or can't otherwise give information. The ID code could be cross-referenced with a database to detail any kind of information.
