 Fund track: Insurers see mixed blessing on popular variable annuities   By O Brian, Bridget, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1997  The boom in variable annuities, which offer mutual funds wrapped in a tax-deferred insurance package, threatens to become a mixed blessing for insurance companies. On the one hand, variable annuities are the hottest product line for many companies, showing double-digit growth. However, insurance companies are competing with banks, brokers and financial planners for market share.
