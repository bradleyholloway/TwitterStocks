 Single-Family Home Sales Declined 1.5% in October   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1987  Sales of new single-family houses in October fell to a seasonally adjusted annual rate of 657,000 from 667,000 in September, the Commerce Department estimated.  Brian Field, a department spokesman, said the estimate is based on such a small sample that the 1.5% decrease is statistically insignificant.
