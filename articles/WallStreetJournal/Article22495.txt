 Mentor Posts a Loss; Charges for Purchases, Divestiture Are Cited   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 1999  Mentor blamed the loss on charges resulting from relocation of its European distribution center, several purchases and a divestiture. Excluding the charges, which were $19.1 million, or 23 cents a share, Mentor's earnings were 21 cents a share, compared with analysts' consensus of 18 cents a share, according to First Call Corp.
