 Tracking the Real Estate Market   By By Jim Carlton, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 2, 1992  Sales of new, single-family homes fell 6.1% in August to a seasonally adjusted annual rate of 570,000, the Commerce Department reported this week. The decline was the largest drop since sales fell 11.5% in March. Last week, the National Association of Realtors reported that sales of existing, single-family homes fell 3.2% during the month.
