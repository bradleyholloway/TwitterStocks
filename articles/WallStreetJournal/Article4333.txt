 Grolier Plans to Buy Publishing Firm, Units   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 9, 1987  DANBURY, Conn. -- Grolier Inc. said it plans to acquire closely held Regensteiner Publishing Enterprises Inc. and its subsidiaries. Terms weren't disclosed.  The subsidiaries are Childrens Press and Regensteiner Printing Co. The transaction will be completed before year's end, according to the company.
