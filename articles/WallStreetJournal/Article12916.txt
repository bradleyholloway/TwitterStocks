 Health Care Brief -- Oxford Health Plans Inc.: Net Rises 2.1% Despite Charge Related to Settlement of Suit   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 2003  Oxford Health Plans Inc. said first-quarter profit rose 2.1%, despite a charge related to settlement of a 1997 class-action lawsuit. Revenue grew 15% to $1.35 billion from $1.17 billion. Net income rose to $72.9 million, or 86 cents a share, from $71.4 million, or 78 cents a share, a year earlier.
