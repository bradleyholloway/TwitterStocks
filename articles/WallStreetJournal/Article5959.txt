 Probe of Computer Associates Focuses On Trading Moves   By By Mark Maremont, The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 2001  The investigation was active as recently as last month, when the SEC asked CA for more information about the employees, Mr. [Robert Gordon] said. To protect confidentiality of the employees' personnel records, he said, CA requested and received a subpoena from the SEC before turning over the information. At least some of the employees involved had previously received subpoenas from the SEC compelling them to turn over records of their trading in CA shares, Mr. Gordon added.
