 Lockheed Investment In Astrolink Poses Threat of Big Charge   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 2001  As of Sept. 30, Lockheed had invested $400 million for a 31% stake in Astrolink, which planned to build a constellation of satellites that would deliver high-speed Internet services to corporate customers. Lockheed also is under contract to build four satellites, the rockets that would launch them and other system-related services. Lockheed is in the process of terminating these contracts.
