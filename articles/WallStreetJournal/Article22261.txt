 Korean bank loans in default at year end totaled $2.82 billion   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1997  Loans in default at 25 commercial banks in South Korea totaled 2.44 trillion won ($2.82 billion) at the end of 1996, up 6.5% from the end of 1995, the Office of Bank Supervision reported. Loans in default totaled 2.11 trillion won for 15 Seoul banks and 338.3 billion won for ten provincial banks, according to the Bank of Korea's regulatory body.
