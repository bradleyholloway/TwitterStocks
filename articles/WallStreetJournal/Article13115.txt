 Coffee Contracts Fall on Selling By Speculators   By Sykora, Allen, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2005  "We saw some fund and small-spec selling," said Boyd Cruel, soft- commodities analyst for Alaron Trading. "They are holding a large net long position and are lightening up at this time."  CRUDE OIL: Futures on the New York Mercantile Exchange fell, dragged down by a selloff in heating-oil futures that followed reports of a larger-than-expected build in U.S. commercial distillate inventories, which include heating oil and diesel fuel. Benchmark February light, sweet crude-oil futures fell 52 cents to $43.39 a barrel.
