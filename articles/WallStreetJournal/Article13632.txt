 Corporate News: Argentina Antitrust Agency Delivers Blow to Telecom Italia   By Casey, Michael, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 2009  Under the shareholding structure, Telecom Italia holds a controlling stake in Telecom Argentina via its majority position in the operating company's local holding company, Sofora Telecomunicaciones SA. On Jan. 9, the commission had announced a formal investigation into charges that the 1.8% stake in the Argentine company that can be indirectly traced to the Spanish company via a complex hierarchy of shareholdings had caused conflicts of interest with Telefonica de Argentina SA. Telefonica's Argentine unit is Telecom Argentina's primary competitor in the local market.
