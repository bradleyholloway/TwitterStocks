 Iasis Healthcare Corp.: Group's Purchase of Company To Improve Access to Capital   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 2004  Iasis Healthcare Corp. agreed to be acquired by an investor group led by Texas Pacific Group for an estimated $750 million and $650 million in debt. The company disclosed only the total value of the deal at $1.4 billion, but recent public filings indicate Iasis has about $650 million in debt.
