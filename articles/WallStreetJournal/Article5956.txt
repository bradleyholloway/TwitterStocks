 Exodus to Cut Staff by 15% and Take a Charge   By By Mylene Mangalindan, The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 2001  Ellen Hancock, Exodus chairman and chief executive officer, said she doesn't anticipate any other layoffs unless the economy changes dramatically. The company, which acquired rival Web-hosting company GlobalCenter Inc., is trying to eliminate redundant positions from the acquisition as well as deal with the slowing economic conditions. Some jobs will be eliminated through attrition.
