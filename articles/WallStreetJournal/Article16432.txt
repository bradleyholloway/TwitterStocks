 Crude-Oil Futures Fall as OPEC, Yukos Ease Supply Worries   By Leah McGrath Goodman, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 2004  Reassuring remarks from the Organization of Petroleum Exporting Countries also helped end crude's streak of record-breaking rises. Also helping were easing fears regarding OAO Yukos after media reports that Russian court officials would allow the oil giant to use its capital to pay for crude production and transport.
