 Ask Encore / Focus on Retirement   By Greene, Kelly, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 2006  Unfortunately, the impact that a 529 plan would have on your grandchildren's financial-aid eligibility is harder to determine. Your grandchildren wouldn't have to report 529 savings on the standardized federal financial-aid form, says Joe Hurley, founder of www.savingforcollege.com, an independent Web site that provides information about 529 plans. "However, when the 529 funds are actually used to pay for the student's expenses, they may or may not be considered in determining eligibility based on the policies of the [individual] college."
