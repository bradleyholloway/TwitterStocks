 Transportation Brief -- Air New Zealand: Boeing Picked to Supply Jets In $849.2 Million Agreement   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 2004  Air New Zealand chose Boeing Co. to supply it with 10 long-haul aircraft -- including two of Boeing's new 7E7 Dreamliner jets -- in a deal valued at 1.35 billion New Zealand dollars (US$849.2 million) that the airline said represented a good price because of the slump in world air travel.
