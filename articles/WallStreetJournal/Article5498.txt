 News Corp. has sold its 40% stake in PMP to Australian broker   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1997  Rupert Murdoch's News Corp has sold its 40% stake in Australian magazine publisher PMP Communications Ltd to broker J. B. Were for about A$320 million. A News Corp spokesman said that the stake was sold to a broker because of the difficulty in finding a single buyer for the block.
