 Tokyo Stocks Advance on Index-Linked Buying; Shares Ease at London but Climb at Frankfurt   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 1990  Tokyo stock prices finished higher in light trading helped by index-linked buying by investment trust funds in the afternoon session.  At Tokyo, the Nikkei index of 225 selected issues was up 219.99 points to close at 32160.23. The index lost 165.95 Friday.  The Second Section index, which gained 22.19 points Friday, was down 10.91 points, or 0.26%, to close at 4189.90. Volume in the Second Section was estimated at 18.4 million shares, down from 28 million Friday.
