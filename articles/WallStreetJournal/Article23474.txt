 Pfizer Scores Patent Victory Over Lipitor   By Loftus, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 2007  The Supreme Court declined to intervene in Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.'s appeal of a lower-court ruling upholding a Pfizer patent for Lipitor. Ranbaxy, an Indian generic-drug maker, wanted to sell a generic version of Lipitor.
