 Business Brief -- Amazon.com Inc.: Lawsuit Says Expedia Owes $3.7 Million Under Agreement   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 2002  Amazon.com Inc. has filed a lawsuit in Seattle against Expedia Inc., saying the Internet travel company hasn't made payments under an agreement to promote its services on Amazon's site. Under the agreement, Expedia, Bellevue, Wash., said it would pay Amazon at least $16 million in 12 fixed installments, the complaint said.
