 Letters to the Editor: Designing Quality Products   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1988  The designer's expertise not only involves concerns about appearance but also an important understanding of marketplace needs and an application of ergonomics to serve the customer effectively. In addition, many design groups within corporations in the U.S. and abroad are generating new product concepts. The original product concepts for the Sony Walkman, Watchman and the "My First Sony" group of products all came from Sony's in-house design groups.
