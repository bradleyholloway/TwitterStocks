 Magna International Inc.: Concern posts 10% increase for fiscal third-quarter net   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 1995  Magna International Inc said profit for its fiscal third quarter ended Apr 30, 1995 rose 10% to 79.8 million Canadian dollars ($57.9 million) from C$72.4 million a year earlier. Magna predicted continued strength in the North American automotive market for the next few years.
