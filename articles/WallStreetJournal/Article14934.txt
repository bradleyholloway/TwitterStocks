 Nestle Gets Boost as French Agency Criticizes Perrier   By Browning, E S, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1992  Nestle SA's hostile bid attempt for Source Perrier SA got a boost when the French government's securities industry watchdog, the Commission des Operations de Bourse, critized Perrier's management. Perrier said the report actually helps it against Nestle's takeover.
