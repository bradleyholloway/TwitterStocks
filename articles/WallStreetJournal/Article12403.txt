 Modem Pioneer Hayes Ends Operations, Fires Staff After Long Slide   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1999  Virtually all of its 250 employees were laid off Monday, and the company will sell its assets, said Charles Campbell, an Atlanta attorney representing unsecured creditors of Hayes.  Hayes owes about $42 million to creditors, Mr. Campbell said. Its primary lender, NationsCredit Commercial Corp., a unit of BankAmerica Corp., Charlotte, N.C., declined to advance funds to allow the company to continue operations while it sought a refinancing or sale of the company, Hayes said in a news release.
