 Intuit Inc.: Software firm posts loss of $53.6 million for period   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 2, 1994  Intuit Inc reported a fiscal first-quarter loss for the period ended Oct 31, 1994 of $53.6 million, compared with net income of $7.3 million a year earlier. The net results reflected the company's purchase of Parsons Technology Inc.
