 Cullinet to Introduce Software for Use With Digital's VAX   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1986  Cullinet Software Inc., struggling to regain lost momentum, said it will seek to hitch itself to Digital Equipment Corp.'s rising star by selling software for Digital's popular VAX minicomputer.  Cullinet mostly sells data-base management software for International Business Machines Corp. mainframe computers. Cullinet's growth came to an abrupt halt last year with the industrywide slump and stepped-up competition from IBM.
