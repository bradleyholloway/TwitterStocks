 U.S. Business Failures Decreased by 6.6%; New York Posts Rise   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 24, 1989  On a regional basis, failures were down in six of the nation's nine census regions. Only the Middle Atlantic states -- which include New York and New Jersey -- showed a sharp rise in business failures. In New York, bankruptcies rose 38.1% to 2,360 last year while in New Jersey the failures rose 15.1% to 860. In neighboring Pennsylvania, failures declined 8.5% to 1,399 last year.
