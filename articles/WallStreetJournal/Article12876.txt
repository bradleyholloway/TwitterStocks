 Business Brief -- RadioShack Corp.: Consumer-Electronics Retailer Lowers 4th-Quarter Estimates   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2003  RadioShack Corp. said its fourth-quarter earnings will fall short of its previous estimates because of slower-than-expected sales. The Fort Worth, Texas, consumer-electronics retailer expects earnings per share of 58 cents to 60 cents, down from its previous forecast of 63 cents to 68 cents.
