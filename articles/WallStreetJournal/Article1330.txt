 SmartMoney.com: Oil Is a Bright Spot Amid the Turmoil   By Grant, Kelli B, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 2008  Volatility in the financial markets has pushed oil down as world- wide demand lessens and investors abandon futures contracts for safer investments, says Richard Ebeling, a senior research fellow with think tank American Institute of Economic Research. Higher oil prices were such a drain on airlines that despite the recent drop, industry losses could still tally $5.2 billion this year, according to the International Air Transport Association.
