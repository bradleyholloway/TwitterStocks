 Castle & Cooke Is Sued Over Its '84 Purchase Of Hurwitz's Shares   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1985  The suit also claims that the so-called greenmail payment to Houston financier Charles Hurwitz forced the food-products and real estate company into its planned merger with Flexi-Van Corp. The plaintiffs, Harry and Belle Weisman of Pikesville, Md., seek $300 million in damages and an injunction forcing Castle & Cooke to sue its directors and insurers. The Weismans said they own 1,440 Castle & Cooke shares.
