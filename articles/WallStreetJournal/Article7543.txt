 Leutwiler Denies Resigning As Pretoria Debt Mediator   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 1986  The denial came after his spokesman in Zurich, Switzerland, Erich Heini, said Mr. Leutwiler had resigned because of lack of political progress in South Africa. Mr. Leutwiler's representative in London, Claude Hankes-Drielsma, said Mr. Heini's statement was based on a misunderstanding.
