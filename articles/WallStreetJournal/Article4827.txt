 Knight-Ridder Unit in Philadelphia Plans to Cut Staff by 160   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1991  Philadelphia Newspapers Inc, the owner of the Philadelphia Inquirer and Philadelphia Daily News, said it plans to cut 160 jobs through voluntary reductions, along with other cost-control measures. The company is a unit of Knight-Ridder Inc. The company said it would delay the opening of a printing plant until Jul 1992.
