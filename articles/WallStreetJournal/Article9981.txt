 Swift Transportation Gets $2.2 Billion Bid From Its Ex-CEO   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 2006  Swift shares rose $5.79, or 24%, to $29.84 in 4 p.m. Nasdaq Stock Market composite trading, an indication that some investors anticipate a higher offer.
