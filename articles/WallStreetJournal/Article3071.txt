 Business Brief -- OCCIDENTAL PETROLEUM CORP.: First-Quarter Net Slipped On Lower Prices for Oil   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 1998  Occidental Petroleum Corp reported first-quarter net income of $177 million, down slightly from $179 million in the year-earlier quarter. The energy and chemicals company said diluted per-share earnings were 49 cents, up from 46 cents.
