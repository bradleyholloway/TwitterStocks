 Noted...   The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 2007  NUTRACEA Inc. (Phoenix) -- Wesley K. Clark was named to the board of this biotechnology company, effective June 1. Gen. Clark, 62, will succeed Elliot Drell, 52. Gen. Clark is a retired four-star U.S. Army general and former NATO supreme allied commander. The board will remain at eight directors.  DEBT RESOLVE Inc. (White Plains, N.Y.) -- David M. Rainey was named chief financial officer and treasurer of this debt-resolution company. Mr. Rainey, 47, succeeds Katherine A. Dering. Ms. Dering, 59, remains as senior vice president-finance in a previously planned transition. Mr. Rainey was finance chief and treasurer of Hudson Scenic Studio Inc.
