 AmerisourceBergen Corp.: Trent Drugs Ltd. of Canada Is Acquired for $40 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 2005  AmerisourceBergen Corp. acquired Trent Drugs Ltd., one of the largest national pharmaceutical distributors in Canada, for about $40 million and will also assume Trent's debt of about $43 million.
