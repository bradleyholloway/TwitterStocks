 Heard on the Street: If Bush Campaign Is in Trouble, Could Stocks Be Far Behind?   By Dorfman, John R, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 1991  The effects on the stock market if it looks like President Bush is in danger of losing the 1992 presidential election is examined and the stock market's reaction to Democratic presidential victories is discussed. If Bush is in danger of losing, stocks will probably take the news hard. However, even though popular Wall Street wisdom says that the market votes Republican and market history tends to support that view, stocks have actually done slightly better during Democratic administrations.
