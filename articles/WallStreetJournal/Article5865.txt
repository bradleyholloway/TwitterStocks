 Deutsche Telekom To Digest Deal In U.S. Before Listing Mobile Unit   By By William Boston, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 2000  Pricing the offer would have been difficult in the face of investor concerns about the VoiceStream deal. While VoiceStream shares appear to be tracking those of Deutsche Telekom, they are trading at a significant discount relative to Deutsche's offer, reflecting investor concern about how long it could take for the deal to be completed. Based on midday New York Stock Exchange trading yesterday, Telekom's offer valued VoiceStream at $164 a share. But the U.S. company's shares were trading on the Nasdaq Stock Market at just over $125.
