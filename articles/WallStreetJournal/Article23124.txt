 Investors Await Factory Data, Fed Meeting and Jobs Report   By Tan, Kopin, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 2004  Optionetics analyst Frederic Ruffy was among those who think the market could become more volatile. "One of the stabilizing factors through April was actually earnings," he said. Although the uncertainty about corporate profits can sometimes stir the markets, the strong first-quarter profits many companies reported actually had helped counter the jitters over rising interest rates and geopolitical tension. "But earnings is almost over now," he said.
