 Kaufman & Broad Inc. And Units Buy Stake Of 7.7% in Cyclops   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1985  Kaufman & Broad Inc. said it and two subsidiaries acquired 7.7% of Cyclops Corp., a Pittsburgh-based maker of steel and metal products.  Separately, Kaufman & Broad completed the previously announced purchase of $575 million of annuity reserves from closely held Capitol Life Assurance Co. of Denver for about $30 million. Capitol is an underwriter of single-premium annuities. As part of the transaction, Kaufman has acquired 50% of Clico Holding Inc., Capitol's parent.
