 Wednesday's Markets: Blue Chips Gain a Bit; Dollar Rises   By Levingston, Steven E, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1992  Blue chip stocks gained slightly on Dec 9, 1992, as smaller issues declined. Bond prices were unchanged and the dollar rose. The Dow Jones Industrial Average increased 1.63 points to 3323.81 in heavy trading.
