 El Paso Corp.: Net Profit More Than Triples Amid Energy-Hedging Gains   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 2006  El Paso Corp.'s first-quarter net profit more than tripled, helped by gains from oil and gas hedging. The Houston natural-gas producer and pipeline company posted net income of $356 million, or 49 cents a share, compared with $106 million, or 17 cents a share, a year earlier.
