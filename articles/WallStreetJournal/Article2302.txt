 Milken Files Counterclaims, Saying Drexel Owes Him Hundreds of Millions   By Lambert, Wade, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1991  Former junk-bond king Michael Milken, who has been accused by Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc of improperly arranging for himself and others to profit from employee-partnership deals, filed counterclaims against his ex-employer seeking hundreds of millions of dollars in compensation and benefits.
