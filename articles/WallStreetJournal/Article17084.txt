 So Who's the NFL's Worst Team? --- In Our Inaugural Misfit Bowl, Losers Advance and Winners Go Home; Our Simulator Tells All   By Gay, Jason, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 2010  The team that loses moves on to the next round -- and three losses crowns a Misfit Bowl champ. Because we want to crunch this out scientifically, we've turned to our friends at AccuScore, the sports-forecasting service, which took into account each club's strengths (ha) and weaknesses (gulp) and current injuries (like Detroit's done-for-the-year QB Matthew Stafford) and simulated each game 10,000 times before issuing a well-considered final score.
