 NYSE Will Delay Circuit-Breaker Test   By Peterson, Kristina; Donna Kardos Yesalavich, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 2010  After the May 6 market plunge, the stock exchanges agreed with regulators to start a six-month pilot program to pause trading in any stock in the Standard & Poor's 500-stock index if the stock falls more than 10% within a five-minute period.
