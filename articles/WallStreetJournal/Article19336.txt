 MBIA Net Rises 2.6% On Slight Increase In Insurance Business   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 2000  Though the company met analysts estimates, in 4 p.m. composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange MBIA's stock fell $2.75 to $67.88 due in part to concerns that the insurer might face increased competition and pricing pressures. MBIA said it believes there are still opportunities in the global market and expects year-end figures to meet the 10% to 13% earnings growth that it predicted at the beginning of the year.
