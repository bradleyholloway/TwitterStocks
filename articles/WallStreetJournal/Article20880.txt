 Buttes Gas & Oil Says Banks Declared Firm In Default on Debt   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 1985  OAKLAND, Calif. -- Buttes Gas & Oil Co. said its banks declared the company in default on payments due on $150.6 million in secured debt.  The troubled oil and gas producer said it is seeking to restructure its debt but repeated its earlier warning that "avoidance of the bankruptcy courts is not assured." As of June 30, Buttes had $285 million in long-term debt.
