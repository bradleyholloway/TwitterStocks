 Japanese Were Net Buyers Of Foreign Bonds in Month --- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1986  Japanese investors were net purchasers of foreign bonds by $10.4 billion in September, the Finance Ministry reported. The bulk of the purchases continued to be in U.S. Treasury securities, ministry officials said.  Japanese investors bought $123.79 billion in foreign bonds and sold $113.39 billion. The resulting $10.4 billion net purchase was up from $7.56 billion in August.
