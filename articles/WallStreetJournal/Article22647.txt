 Program Trading   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 2, 2000  It occurred Monday when the DJIA fell more than 200 points. The following firms reported making the most program trades between 9:30 a.m. to 10:45 a.m. EDT: Bear Stearns, Interactive Brokers, BNP Securities, TLW Securities LLC and RBC Dominion.  Of the five most-active firms overall for the week, Deutsche Bank Securities, Bear Stearns, BNP Securities and RBC Dominion executed all or most of their program trading activity for customers as agents. Morgan Stanley split its program activity between its own accounts and those of its customers.
