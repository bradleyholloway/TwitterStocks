 Hutton Group President May Also Head Its Brokerage Unit Soon, Sources Say   By By Scott McMurray, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 1986  E.F. Hutton Group Inc.'s president of six months, Robert Rittereiser, is expected to extend his control over the securities company in a management shake-up soon, sources said.  Officials inside and outside of Hutton said that plans for all the management changes haven't been completed. But they speculated that Mr. Rittereiser, within the next several weeks, is likely to receive the additional duties and title of president of E.F. Hutton & Co., the brokerage firm that is the company's main subsidiary.
