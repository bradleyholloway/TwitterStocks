 Rohr is said to agree to plead guilty in $7 million settlement of U.S. charges   By Pasztor, Andy, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 1994  Rohr Inc agreed to plead guilty to criminal charges of fraud and falsifying tests of military aircraft equipment as part of an anticipated $7 million settlement with the US. The settlement will also require the firm to provide extensive warranties for the suspect engine parts.
