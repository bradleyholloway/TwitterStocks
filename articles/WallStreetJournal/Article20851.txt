 Astrotech's Auditor Resigns, Is Succeeded By Coopers & Lybrand   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1985  Charles A. Schliebs, vice president and general counsel of Astrotech, said Ernst & Whinney gave no reason for its resignation as the company's independent auditor. "The resignation was given to use in writing and did not enumerate any reasons for the resignation," he said. "We queried them about whether there were any disagreements and they said there were none."
