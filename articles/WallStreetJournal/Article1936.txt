 Buy-Out of Durakon Seems to Be Imperiled By Weakening Profit   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1988  Masco Industries Inc., which is assisting a Durakon management group led by Durakon Chairman Michael Wayne in the buy-out, said Friday, "at this time, based on the material shortfall in Durakon's earnings, it doesn't appear the merger will go forward." Masco, a diversified manufacturer based in Taylor, Mich., issued the statement on behalf of Durakon Holdings Inc., a company it formed with Mr. Wayne's group for the transaction.
