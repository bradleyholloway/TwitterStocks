 Leagal Beat: Toyota   By Green, Wayne E; Miller, Krystal; Hayes, Arthur S; Nomani, Asra Q, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1990  Toyota Motor Corp has been ordered to pay damages to a Bloomfield Hills MI couple for defective seat belts in their 4runner truck.
