 Universal Music Group: Def Jam President Steps Down In Latest Management Change   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 9, 2004  Kevin Liles, president of Universal Music Group's Def Jam Recordings label, abruptly left the company yesterday, the company and a spokesman for Mr. Liles said. Mr. Liles's departure is the latest in a series of changes in upper management at Universal's Island Def Jam Music Group.
