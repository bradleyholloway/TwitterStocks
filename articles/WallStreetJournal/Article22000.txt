 Economy is expected to slow to half its current rate of growth by the second half of 1995, survey reports   By Bleakley, Fred R, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 1995  Economists participating in The Wall Street Journal's semiannual survey predicted that the GDP will grow at an annual rate of 2.9% in the first half of 1995 and 2.2% in the second half. A sampling of interest-rate, economic and currency forecasts for Jun 1994 and new forecasts for 1995 is provided.
