 How to Be a Name Dropper In the Age of the Internet   By By Shirley Leung, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 1999  Last month when "Sunset Beach" soap star Hank Cheyne gave an art showing at the ABC Entertainment Center, Levine Communications Office Inc. zapped a mass e-mail to some 250 clients, customers and friends.  Along with the usual media contacts, there were some star-studded addresses: alanis@rockweb.com (Alanis Morrisette); g_clooney@nbc.er.com (George Clooney), and mel_gibson@hotmail.com (Mel Gibson).
