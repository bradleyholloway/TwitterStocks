 International Brief -- Banca Nazionale del Lavoro: Italian Official Backs Merger With Domestic Bank Firm   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 1989  Italian Treasury Undersecretary Maurizio Sacconi sees Banca Nazionale del Lavoro merging with an internationally savvy domestic bank to create a financial powerhouse that would rank among the top 10 banks in Europe.
