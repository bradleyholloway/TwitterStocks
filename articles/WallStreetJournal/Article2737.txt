 Sony forms movie venture with Henson Productions   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1995  Sony Pictures Entertainment Inc has formed a joint movie venture with Jim Henson Productions Inc in a move to expand into the family-entertainment market. The deal calls for Henson to supply Sony Pictures with 10 to 15 movies over the next three to five years, in which Sony will invest up to $75 million.
