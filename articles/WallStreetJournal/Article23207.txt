 Duke Energy Corp.: Higher Prices for Commodities, Electricity Sales Lead to Profit   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 2005  Duke Energy Corp. returned to profitability in the fourth quarter and the full year, helped by the absence of plant-impairment charges in North America, better industrial sales at its electric utility and the benefit of higher oil and natural-gas prices at its natural-gas liquids business.
