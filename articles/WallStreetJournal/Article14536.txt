 Corporate Report: KLM Royal Dutch Airlines   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1988  KLM Royal Dutch Airlines said net income rose 26% in the quarter ended Sept. 30, to 180.3 million guilders ($90.3 million) from 143 million guilders a year earlier.  The airline, 39.4%-owned by the Dutch government, said revenue in the fiscal second quarter increased 11%, to 1.66 billion guilders ($831.5 million) from 1.49 billion guilders.
