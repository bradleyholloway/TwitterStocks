 Melville Corp. Names Stanley P. Goldstein To Post of President   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 1985  HARRISON, N.Y. -- Melville Corp. said it named Stanley P. Goldstein president, a move that appears to put the 51-year-old executive in line for the chief executive officer's post.  Mr. Goldstein was one of three executive vice presidents reporting to Melville's chairman and chief executive, Francis C. Rooney Jr., 64. The two remaining executive vice presidents, Robert Kuhn and Richard Anderson, will report to Mr. Goldstein.
