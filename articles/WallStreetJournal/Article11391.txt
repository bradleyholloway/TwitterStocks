 Board of Allegheny International Acts to Surrender Firm   By Ansberry, Clare, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 6, 1990  Directors at Allegheny International Inc agreed to a plan that will turn the firm over to archrival Japonica Partners. The directors also rewarded themselves with a half-year's severance pay. The bankruptcy plan is a result of pressure from banks, creditors and shareholders.
