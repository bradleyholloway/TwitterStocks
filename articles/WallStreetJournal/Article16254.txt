 Technology Brief -- Hitachi Ltd.: Unexpected Profit Won't Alter Outlook for Full Fiscal Year   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 2003  Hitachi Ltd. posted an unexpected profit, but the results weren't good enough to cause the Japanese electronics concern to upgrade its financial forecasts for the year. The Tokyo company reported net income of 1.33 billion yen ($11.1 million) for the fiscal third quarter ended Dec. 31, compared with a year-earlier loss of 115.82 billion yen.
