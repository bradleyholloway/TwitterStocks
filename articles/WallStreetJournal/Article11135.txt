 West German F-4 Jet Crashes, Lifting Toll Of Recent Mishaps   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 1988  STADE, West Germany (AP) -- A West German Air Force F-4 Phantom fighter jet crashed into a field and exploded yesterday, seconds after its two pilots ejected to safety, police said.  The crash is the latest in a series of military flight accidents in West Germany since March.  Police said the jet went down 12 miles south of Stade, about 25 miles west of the northern port city of Hamburg.
