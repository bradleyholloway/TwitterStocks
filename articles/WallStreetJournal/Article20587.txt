 World News: Japan's Efforts to Plug Leak Fall Short --- Ocean Radiation Level Sparks Fear of Wider Contamination   By Sekiguchi, Toko; Mochizuki, Takashi, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 2011  Japanese officials concluded over the weekend that radioactive water was leaking directly from the crippled Fukushima Daiichi nuclear plant into the nearby ocean, but failed on Sunday in repeated attempts to seal a crack they had identified as one source of the pollution. Separately, the plant operator, Tokyo Electric Power Co., said Sunday it had discovered the bodies of two workers who had been missing since the March 11 earthquake and tsunami hit Fukushima Daiichi, crippling its backup systems.
