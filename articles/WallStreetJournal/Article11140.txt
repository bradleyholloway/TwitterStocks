 Aid for Rover Group To Be Decided by End Of July, Officials Hope ---- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 1988  The United Kingdom and the European Community Commission hope that a decision will be made by the commission on proposed British government aid to Rover Group PLC by the end of July, according to British and commission spokesmen.  The spokesmen were commenting on a three-hour meeting yesterday between British Trade and Industry Secretary Lord Young and the commission's Competition Commissioner Peter Sutherland on the Rover case.
