 Year-End Review of Markets & Finance 2005; New Listings Rise on Nasdaq, NYSE   By Ceron, Gaston F, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 2006  The Big Board signed up 192 new listings, up from 165 in 2004. Preliminary numbers from Nasdaq showed 269 new listings there, nine more than in 2004. Nasdaq's 2005 tally included 124 initial public offerings of stock, down from 151 IPOs in 2004. The NYSE had 63 domestic IPOs last year, down from 69 such IPOs in 2004; excluding transfers, the Big Board had 15 international new listings last year, down from 17 in 2004.
