 Science and Tech Funds Set Torrid Pace   By By Richard C. ten Wolde, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1999  Science and technology stock funds shot past all other U.S. mutual-fund sectors in 1998's fourth quarter, and money managers say the sector should prove strong through this year's first half.  The funds were propelled in part by fervor for youthful Internet-related concerns, including online auctioneers eBay Inc. and uBid Inc. and Internet darling Amazon.com Inc., a big online seller of books, music and a growing number of other items. Funds also got a boost from more established Internet names, such as America Online Inc., managers said.
