 Money-Fund Assets Fell for Week   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 2002  Average seven-day simple yields for tax-free money-market funds rose to 0.88% from 0.83%, while seven-day compound yields moved up to 0.88% from 0.84%. Thirty-day simple yields fell to 0.84% from 0.85%, as 30-day compound yields stayed at 0.85%. The average maturity of tax-free funds rose to 47 days from 45 days.
