 Clinton set to cut China textile quotas, lift ban on sale of satellites to Beijing   By Davis, Bob; Greenberger, Robert S, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1994  The Clinton administration plans to cut China's textile quotas by as much as one-third while lifting a ban on the sale of two satellites built by Martin Marietta Corp in exchange for Beijing's agreement to begin regular discussions with the US on weapons-proliferation issues.
