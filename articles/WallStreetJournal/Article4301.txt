 Letters to the Editor: Health-Care Taxes: Bad Medicine   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1987  Why not look to tax credits for businesses and individuals who devote their resources to supporting and financing reforms in health care, social service, job training, etc.? More rigorous enforcement of current tax laws tempered by credits for direct investment in social services would avoid cumbersome new legislation, encourage more activism in business and cut out the middleman -- government -- which must take its share of the pie.
