 Italy's Woes Weigh on Europe --- Prime Minister Resists Calls for New Measures, Anxiety Spreads Across Continent   By Meichtry, Stacy; Forelle, Charles; Enrich, David, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 2011  Jose Manuel Barroso, the president of the European Commission, the European Union's executive arm, released a blistering statement calling the weakening bond prices of Spain and Italy "a cause of deep concern" and urging governments to signal "resolve to address the sovereign debt crisis with the means commensurate with the gravity of the situation."
