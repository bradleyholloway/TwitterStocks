 Pathe's ex-chief, two others settle fraud complaint   By Rundle, Rhonda L, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1996  Former CEO Giancarlo Parretti, former chairman Florio Fiorini and former CFO Fernando Cappuccio of Pathe Communications Corp have settled security fraud charges with the SEC related to Pathe's $1.3 billion purchase of MGM/UA Communications in 1990.
