 U.S. clears transfer of La Guardia slots from TWA to Delta   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 1995  The DOT approved the transfer of 10 slots at New York's La Guardia Airport from Trans World Airlines to Delta Air Lines. The agency warned that both it and the Justice Department are continuing to investigate allegations of antitrust violations and unfair competition lodged against the two carriers by ValuJet Airlines.
