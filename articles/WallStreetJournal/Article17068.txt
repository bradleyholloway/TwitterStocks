 Japan Halts Landmark Postal Sale   By Tudor, Alison, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 2009  Japan's new government pushed through parliament a law freezing next year's planned sale of shares in Japan Post Holdings Co., which combines mail delivery with nationwide banking and insurance services. For nearly two decades, Japan has battled over which was the best cure for its economic decline, which began with the bursting of an asset bubble in the early 1990s and evolved into a "lost decade" of banking crisis, deflation and political drift.
