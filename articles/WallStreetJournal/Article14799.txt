 Jurors Should Reject Laws They Don't Like, Activist Group Argues   By Adler, Stephen J, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1991  The Fully Informed Jury Association, which wants to require that juries be told the truth about how much power they have, is profiled. The movement springs from the fact that juries in criminal cases possess an absolute but unadvertised power to ignore the dictates of the law.
