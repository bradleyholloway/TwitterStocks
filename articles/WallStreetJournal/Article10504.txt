 The $2 Million Patients --- New York Faces Review for Medicaid Rates Charged in Care of Mentally Disabled   By Gershman, Jacob, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 2011  Over the course of a year, Medicaid spends $1.9 million for every resident, or $2.5 billion in total -- with half coming from the federal government. Since the spending is split between the state and the federal government, the institutions generate a lucrative subsidy for the state and the agency that oversees Medicaid and other programs for developmentally disabled people.
