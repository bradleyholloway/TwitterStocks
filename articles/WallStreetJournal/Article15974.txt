 Business Brief -- PITNEY BOWES INC.: Firm Lowers Estimates For Its Full-Year Earnings   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 2000  Pitney Bowes Inc., Stamford, Conn., lowered its earnings estimate for the full year to $2.44 to $2.48 a share, before one-time items, below the $2.58 a share of analysts' consensus according to First Call.
