 Fieldcrest Picks Horn To Become President   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 1987  Mr. Horn, 48 years old and previously executive vice president of this textile maker he joined in 1963, succeeds Francis X. Larkin. Mr. Larkin, 60, remains vice chairman and will devote his time to special assignments, such as integrating new plants or acquired operations. Both men will continue as part of a six-member office of the chief executive.
