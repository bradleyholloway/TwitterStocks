 Japan Air's Pilots Walk Out to Protest Hiring of Foreigners   By Brauchli, Marcus W, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1991  Pilots at Japan Air Lines went on strike for a day, asserting that the company's plan to hire foreign pilots, mainly Americans, would imperil safety. The partial strike forced the airlines to cancel 23 flights on Jul 9, 1991.
