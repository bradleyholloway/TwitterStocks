 Style -- Tricks of the Trade: A Cosmetics Exec Gets Bronzed   By Cheryl Lu-Lien Tan, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 2007  To get a natural look, Ms. [Laura Mercier] sometimes combines all three -- starting with a light layer of tinted moisturizer. Then, she rubs a dime-sized dollop of bronzing gel into her cheeks and finishes by taking a very large brush and applying a very light dusting of bronzing powder on her face and neck so her face doesn't look unnaturally darker than the rest of her body.
