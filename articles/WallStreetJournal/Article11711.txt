 Indonesian TV no longer just a Suharto family affair   By Cohen, Margot, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 1993  The Jan 1, 1993 debut of ANTEVE, a TV station based in Lampung Indonesia, is discussed. The entrance of the new TV station signals sweeping change to the Indonesian broadcasting industry, where a group of conglomerates are poised to break up the Suharto family's monopoly.
