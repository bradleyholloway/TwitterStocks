 U.S. Plans to Appeal Wireless-Spectrum Case to High Court   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 2001  At stake are licenses the FCC had reclaimed from NextWave after the company was unable to pay the $4.7 billion it bid for the spectrum in a 1996 auction. The FCC resold them this January in an auction that raised more than $16 billion from wireless companies linked to giant concerns like Verizon Communications Inc.'s Verizon Wireless joint venture and AT&T Wireless Services Inc.
