 AT&T to Offer BlackBerry, Treo In Shades of Red   By Vascellaro, Jessica E, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 2007  The ruby Pearl will be available nationwide starting today, and is the first wireless handset to be branded for the new AT&T. (Cingular has been rebranded as AT&T, after AT&T gained 100% ownership of Cingular through the acquisition of BellSouth Corp.)
