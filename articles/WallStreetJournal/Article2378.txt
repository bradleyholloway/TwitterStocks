 Letters to the Editor: World War I Victors Drew a Tragic Map   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1992  Reviewing current events in what used to be Yugoslavia ("Cross-Purposes: How Europe's Leaders Let Yugoslav Crisis Get Out of Control," page one, July 1) and the breakup of Czechoslovakia make one wonder why nobody brings up the original, misguided efforts of the World War I victorious Western powers in creating these countries.
