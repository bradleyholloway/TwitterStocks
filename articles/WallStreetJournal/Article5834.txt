 Business Brief -- TODAY'S MAN INC.: Closing of Three Stores Is Planned by Company   The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 2000  Today's Man Inc plans to close three stores, located in Norwalk CT; Manhasset NY; and Philadelphia, by Sep 9, 2000. The company said the three locations represent a disproportionate amount of its losses and are no longer consistent with long-term plans for the Today's Man brand.
