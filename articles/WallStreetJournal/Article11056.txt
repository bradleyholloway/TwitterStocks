 Currency Trader for VW Is Arrested in California --- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 1987  FRANKFURT, West Germany -- Joachim Schmidt, a Frankfurt currency broker who is suspected of playing a key role in the currency fraud that cost Volkswagen AG 473 million marks ($283.8 million), was arrested in Hollywood, Calif., the West German police said.
