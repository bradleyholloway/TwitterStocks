 Texas bank fights a war of independence: First Republic shuns outside financial help   By Apcar, Leonard M, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1987  Through the selling of assets, better management of its loan portfolio, and the extraction of more cost savings from its takeover of Dallas' InterFirst Corp., First RepublicBank Corp. plans to avoid the fate of other Texas banks, many of which have either been taken over or merged with other banks. Many bank analysts believe this is impossible, citing First Republic Bank's $350 million fourth quarter 1987 loss and expected $100 million 1988 loss as well as other factors.
