 Republic Industries to buy National Car just two months after acquiring Alamo   By Miller, Lisa; Brannigan, Martha, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1997  Less than two months after acquiring Alamo Rent-A-Car, Republic Industries Inc signed a definitive agreement with National Car Rental System Inc. The deal, which the companies said would be announced Jan 6, 1997, involves a $600 million stock swap and the assumption of $1.7 billion of vehicle debt. Republic Chairman H. Wayne Huizenga's aggressive strategy for his burgeoning car-rental empire, which focuses as much on selling cars as renting them, is discussed.
