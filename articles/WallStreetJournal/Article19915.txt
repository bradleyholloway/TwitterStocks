 Hutchison Telecom Swings to a Loss Amid Charges   By Buckman, Rebecca, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 2005  Subscriber growth in India is slowing, and company officials say there is no guarantee its plans to push into new rural regions of India will succeed. Hutchison Telecom said yesterday its Indian unit is in advanced talks to acquire one Indian company, Essar Spacetel Ltd., and is "exploring" how to buy the operating subsidiaries of another, BPL Communications Ltd., which last month agreed to sell a controlling stake to Hutchison's Indian business partner.
