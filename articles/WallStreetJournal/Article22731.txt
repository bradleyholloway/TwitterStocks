 Canada Says Suspicious Envelope Sent to Ottawa Had No Anthrax   By By Wall Street Journal staff reporters Joel Baglole in Ottawa and Ann Zimmerman in Dallas, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 2, 2001  Separately, a Wal-Mart department store in Victoria, British Columbia, received a letter Monday postmarked from Florida that claimed to be contaminated with anthrax, according to Wal-Mart Stores Inc. spokesman Andrew Pelletier. Wal-Mart said preliminary testing found no bacteria consistent with anthrax, but that the letter will be sent for further tests. "Local health and police authorities believe it is likely a hoax, " Mr. Pelletier said.
