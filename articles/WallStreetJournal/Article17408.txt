 MANSION --- The Inner Rooms of Maya Angelou --- The 84-year-old poet splits her time among three art-filled houses; But when she writes, she checks into a hotel   By Tanaka, Sanette, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 2012  After years of traveling the world -- first as a dancer and singer, then a journalist and later a civil-rights activist -- she gained widespread recognition with the 1969 publication of "I Know Why the Caged Bird Sings," the first installment in a six-part autobiography. In 1981, after she and her husband divorced, Ms. Angelou accepted a lifetime professorship of American studies at North Carolina's Wake Forest University.
