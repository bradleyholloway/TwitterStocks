 Adidas and the Yankees play hardball with MLB over huge sponsorship pact   By Fatsis, Stefan, The Wall Street Journal,  May 9, 1997  Footwear and apparel maker Adidas America Inc said it will move forward with a blockbuster sponsorship agreement with the New York Yankees despite an escalating conflict with Major League Baseball. The deal between the two is worth $93 million over 10 years.
