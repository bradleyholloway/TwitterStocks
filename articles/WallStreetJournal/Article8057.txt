 American Economy Now Has Less Impact Abroad, Foreigners Say   By Revzin, Philip; Ono, Yumiko; Darlin, Damon; Kamm, Thomas, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1990  In the past when the US economy faltered or slipped into a recession, other countries braced for a slump of their own. Now, the US economy is viewed as just another powerhouse, not the key to global economic development.
