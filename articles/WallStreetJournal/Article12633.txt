 Business Brief -- R.H. Donnelley Corp.: Exploration of Sale Is Ended On Debt-Financing Market   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 2000  R.H. Donnelley Corp., a marketer of yellow-pages advertising, said it won't pursue the sale of the company at this time, and it will resume a $100 million buyback that was discontinued in August when it began exploring options with its advisers.
