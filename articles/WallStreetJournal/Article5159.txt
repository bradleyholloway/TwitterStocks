 DuPont unit to join Australian company's methane gas project   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 9, 1994  The Conoco Inc unit of DuPont Co has agreed to join the coal-bed methane gas project in Queensland Australia of MIM Holdings Ltd. Conoco will spend 22 million Australian dollars ($16.35 million) on exploration, research and trial production under the joint venture deal.
