 Wisconsin Central Transportation   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1993  A consortium led by Wisconsin Central Transportation Corp will pay $220 million to acquire New Zealand Rail, a state-owned railroad system being privatized by the New Zealand government. Wisconsin Central and Berkshire Partners will each hold a 27% stake of NZ Rail; New Zealand investors led by Fay, Richwhite & Co will hold the remaining 46% stake.
