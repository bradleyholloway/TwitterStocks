 International Finance: More Quit Nomura as Bonuses Lapse   By Or, Amy, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 2010  Nomura, which bought Lehman's Asian and European operations in late 2008 and guaranteed bonuses to key staff, lost its vice chairman for its China investment-banking business, Jane Wang, and the Asian-Pacific head of equity capital markets, Geoffrey Feldkamp, to competitors earlier this week, a person familiar with the situation said Thursday.
