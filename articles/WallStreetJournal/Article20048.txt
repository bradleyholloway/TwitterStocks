 Politics & Economics: Railroads Weigh New Basis for Fees Amid U.S. Review   By Machalaba, Daniel, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 2006  The possible changes follow heightened interest in fuel surcharges by the federal Surface Transportation Board, which has been urged by auto makers, utilities and other major rail customers to overhaul how railroads calculate and apply the fees. In August the agency proposed forcing railroads to tie surcharges more closely to actual changes in fuel costs and abandon the practice of linking surcharges to the base shipping rate.
