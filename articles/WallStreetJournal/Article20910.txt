 Fannie Mae Planning Securities Offerings Totaling $1.65 Billion   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1985  The government-sponsored, privately owned corporation, known as Fannie Mae, said it will offer $200 million of mortgage-backed securities tomorrow and $1.45 billion in new debentures Thursday.  The $200 million of securities will be backed by long-term, fixed-rate mortgages held by Fannie Mae. The sale represents part of Fannie Mae's efforts to lengthen the maturity of its debt and shorten the maturity of its assets.
