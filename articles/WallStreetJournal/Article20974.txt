 Stake in Damon Corp. Is Increased to 7.74% By Investment Group   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1986  An investment group led by Larry L. Pfirman, a Pennsylvania businessman, disclosed that it holds a 7.74% stake in Damon Corp. and that it may seek board representation.  In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the group said it owns 502,300 Damon common shares, including 45,200 purchased between April 14 and May 23 at $17.75 to $23 a share.
