 Union Pacific Corp. Drops Plans to Buy Katy Industries Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 1986  Union Pacific Corp. said it dropped plans to acquire a railroad unit of Katy Industries Inc. but would continue to explore other acquisition alternatives.  Acquisition of the 3,100-mile railroad, which carries freight mainly in Missouri, Kansas and Texas, would have helped make rail service in that area more efficient, Union Pacific had said.
