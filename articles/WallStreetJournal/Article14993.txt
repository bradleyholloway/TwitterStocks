 A War Breaks Out among Followers of a Peacemaker   By Darlin, Damon, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 1992  Despite Buddhism's prohibition against intentionally killing or injuring any living thing, monks of the Chogye order in South Korea are battling it out over issues such as money, power and sex. Dissidents accuse Master Monk Suh Eui Hyun of sexual misconduct, among other things.
