 Law Brief: Noted... [1]  The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1989  MERGER: Wyatt, Tarrant & Combs, Kentucky's largest law firm, merged today with Gilbert & Milom, a 15-lawyer firm based in Nashville, Tenn. Gilbert & Milom represents entertainment industry clients such as the country music stars Loretta Lynn and Ricky Skaggs. The 140-lawyer Wyatt Tarrant, based in Louisville, specializes in media, banking and securities law. The new firm retains the Wyatt Tarrant name in Kentucky; in Tennessee, it is called Wyatt, Tarrant, Combs, Gilbert & Milom.
