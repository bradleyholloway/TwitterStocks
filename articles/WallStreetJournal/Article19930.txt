 Construction Brief -- Cie. de Saint-Gobain SA: Acquisition Offer Is Extended For Plasterboard Producer BPB   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 2005  French building-materials maker Cie. de Saint-Gobain SA said it will extend its GBP 3.7 billion ($6.5 billion) offer for BPB PLC after receiving acceptances from less than 1% of the British plasterboard maker's shareholders.
