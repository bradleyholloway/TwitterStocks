 U.K.'s Kingfisher says it is holding talks with Darty of France   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1993  UK retailer Kingfisher PLC announced that it was in discussions with Darty SA, a French electrical appliance maker, following a newspaper report that Kingfisher planned to buy Darty and the suspension of trading in Darty at the Paris Bourse.
