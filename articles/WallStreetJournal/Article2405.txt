 HSBC Holdings PLC: Marine Midland Posts Profit of $29.9 Million for Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 21, 1992  Aided by a gain from the sale of mortgage-backed securities, the Marine Midland Banks Inc unit of HSBC Holdings PLC posted a $29.9 million profit for the third quarter, compared to a $56.9 million loss the year before.
