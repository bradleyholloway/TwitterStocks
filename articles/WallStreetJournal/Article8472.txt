 Mutual funds quarterly review: Foreign funds go small, but in diverse ways   By Clements, Jonathan, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1994  The foreign stock funds that proliferated in recent years use a wide variety of stock-picking methods, and it has become important not just to pick the right market, but also to have the right fund. While some favor stocks in developing markets, others favor small stocks in developed countries.
