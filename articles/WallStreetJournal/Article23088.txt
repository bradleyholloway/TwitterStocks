 Business Brief -- Renal Care Group Inc.: Company Agrees to Acquire Dialysis-Services Providers   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 2004  Renal Care Group Inc. agreed to acquire dialysis-services provider National Nephrology Associates Inc. for $167 million. Besides the cash payment, Renal Care will assume NNA's outstanding debt, including its $160 million of 9% senior subordinated notes due 2011 and capital leases.
