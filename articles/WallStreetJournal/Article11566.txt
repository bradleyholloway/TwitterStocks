 Berlitz International Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1992  Berlitz International Inc adopted an anti-takeover plan to protect the company while it settles issues of share ownership. About 55.3% of Berlitz is still controlled by Macmillan Publishing Co, a unit of Maxwell Communication Corp. Maxwell announced in Nov 1991 that the 55.3% stake had been sold to Fukutake Publishing Co of Japan, but several creditors objected.
