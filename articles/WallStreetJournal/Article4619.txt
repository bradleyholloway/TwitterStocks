 Business Brief -- Nippondenso Co.: Pretax Profit, Net, Sales For 1989 All Mark Records   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 9, 1990  Nippondenso Co. of Japan, a producer of electronic and electrical parts for autos, said its consolidated pretax profit for 1989 rose 19% to a record 113.4 billion yen ($723.5 million) from 95.6 billion yen in the previous year.
