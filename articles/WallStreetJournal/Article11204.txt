 Argentina Workers Given 2 Days Off Due to Energy Crisis ---- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1989  Argentina's government declared a holiday for the government workers today and Monday because of a nationwide energy crisis.  Daniel Larriqueta, chief adviser to the Interior Ministry and a member of the emergency energy committee, said in a news conference that closing public offices was an energy-saving measure.  Government banks, ministries, customs in the port of Buenos Aires, and municipal offices in Buenos Aires will be closed, among others.
