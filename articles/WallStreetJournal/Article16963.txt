 Toyota Isn't Immune From the Recession   By Ingrassia, Paul, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 2009  The No. 1 car company in Japan -- and the world -- recently announced it would suffer an operating loss for the first time in 70 years, which is how long it has been in the car business. The proximate cause of Toyota's troubles is the global economic slowdown that is hitting every car company -- and indeed virtually every industry (bankruptcy lawyers excepted) -- around the world.
