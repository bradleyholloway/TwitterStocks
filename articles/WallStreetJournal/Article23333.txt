 CVS Corp. Profit Surges by 59% on Sales Of Generic Prescription Drugs   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 2006  CVS Corp.'s fourth-quarter profit surged 59%, fueled by brisk sales of high-margin generic prescription drugs. The Woonsocket, R.I., drugstore chain reported net income of $406.4 million, or 48 cents a share, up from $255.1 million, or 30 cents a share, a year earlier.
