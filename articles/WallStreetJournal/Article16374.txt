 Energy Brief -- El Paso Corp.: Valero Energy Set to Acquire Aruba Refinery and Affiliates   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 2004  El Paso Corp. is selling its Aruba refinery and related affiliates to Valero Energy Corp. for $465 million in cash and $175 million of working capital. The working capital consists primarily of crude-oil inventory, and the affiliates are related to marine, bunkering and marketing operations, said a spokesman for El Paso.
