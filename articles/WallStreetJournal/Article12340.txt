 Business Brief -- HIGHWOODS PROPERTIES INC.: Purchase of J.C. Nichols For $300 Million Approved   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 1998  Highwoods Properties Inc, a large REIT, said shareholders of J. C. Nichols Co voted to approve Highwoods' acquisition of Nichols for $300 million in cash and stock and the assumption of $229 million in debt.
