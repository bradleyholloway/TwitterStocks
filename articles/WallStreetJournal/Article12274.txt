 Business Brief: Best Buy Expects Quarterly Profit To Be Far Better Than Forecasts   By By Evan Ramstad, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 1997  Best Buy Co. forecast a fiscal third-quarter profit far exceeding Wall Street's expectations, and the electronics retailer's shares jumped 12% to a 52-week high.  Meanwhile, Circuit City Stores Inc. said it expected flat profit growth for its electronics store chain and a loss for its automotive superstore operation for the quarter.  Both companies reported slightly lower comparable-store sales for November in their electronics outlets. Meanwhile, a third major electronics retailer, Tandy Corp., reported a 2% gain for sales at stores open at least a year during the period.
