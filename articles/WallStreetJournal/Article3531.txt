 Questioning the Books: GE Agrees to Buy Part of Enron Unit For About $250 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 2002  The deal doesn't affect wind farms that are owned or operated by Enron, but GE said it would provide operational support for the farms. Financial terms weren't disclosed, but people familiar with the deal said GE is paying about $250 million in cash and an additional $150 million in assumed debt and other considerations. Credit Suisse First Boston and Blackstone Group advised Enron Wind and Enron Corp. on the deal.
