 TEXAS JOURNAL --- Around the State, Makers Of Tofu See an Opening   By By Tara Parker-Pope, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 1993  Huntington's restaurant, in Dallas's Westin Galleria, has a thriving Totally Texas menu. In fact, the state fare accounts for three-quarters of its business. There's just one little detail: The most popular Totally Texas item, panseared and served with sun-dried blueberry vinaigrette, is ostrich.
