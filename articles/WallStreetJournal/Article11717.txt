 Marketing Brief: Noted.... [2]  The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1993  DISNEY ON STAGE: Walt Disney Co. said a live "Broadway musical version" of its animated movie "Beauty & the Beast" will play for four weeks at the Music Hall in Houston beginning Dec. 2. Further engagements haven't been set, but Disney insiders say the show could open on Broadway as early as spring 1994. The musical will be mounted by Theatre Under the Stars, a nonprofit civic light opera company in Houston.
