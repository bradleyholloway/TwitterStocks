 MCI posts 21% earnings rise but sets layoffs and charge for third quarter   By Keller, John J, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 3, 1995  MCI Communications Corp reported a rise of 21% in its earnings for the second quarter of 1995, but it will lay off as many as 3,000 workers and take a restructuring pretax charge of as much as $800 million, triggering a major loss in the third quarter. Second-quarter net income was $260 million, up from $215 million in the same period a year earlier.
