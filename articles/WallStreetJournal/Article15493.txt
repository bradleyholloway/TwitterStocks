 Parker Hannifin Corp.: Profit estimate lowered on disappointing orders   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1996  Parker Hannifin Corp said it expects to report that earnings for the fiscal first quarter ended Sep 30, 1996 were 10% to 15% lower than expectations and year-earlier results of $57.4 million. Earnings were hurt by disappointing industrial-order rates that resulted in lower shipments in September. The Cleveland company makes motion-control components for fluid systems in industrial and automotive markets.
