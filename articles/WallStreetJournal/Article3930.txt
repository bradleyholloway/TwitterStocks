 Court Won't Clarify Sentencing   By Bravin, Jess, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 2005  Yesterday, the high court declined to hear the case of Vladimir Rodriguez, who was convicted of a federal drug dealing offense in Florida, within the jurisdiction of the 11th U.S. Circuit Court of Appeals in Atlanta, which applies the harsher resentencing standard. After that court upheld his 109-month sentence, he appealed to the Supreme Court. The Justice Department supported the 11th Circuit's opinion, but asked the Supreme Court to hear the case to resolve the "deep and real" split among the appellate courts.
