 In Brief   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 2007  Ford Motor Co. said its joint venture in China, Changan Ford Mazda Automobile Co., sold 38,908 cars in the first quarter, up 41% from the year-earlier period. The Dearborn, Mich., auto maker said Chinese sales of vehicles that carry brands wholly owned by Ford -- such as Ford, Lincoln, Volvo, Jaguar and Land Rover -- reached 42,746 units, up 16%.
