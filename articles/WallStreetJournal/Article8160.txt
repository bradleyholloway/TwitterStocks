 Would-Be Immigrants Scramble for Position in U.S. Visa Lottery   By Valeriano, Lourdes Lee, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 1991  The State Department will preside over a lottery in Oct 1991, with 40,000 immigrant visas as the prize and the US Postal Service drawing the numbers. All entrants are required to do is mail an application in during a specific time period. There is no filing fee and no limit on the number of entries an applicant can submit.
