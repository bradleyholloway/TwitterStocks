 Business Brief -- ASPEN TECHNOLOGY INC.: Acquisition of Setpoint Inc. Completed; Dispute Settled   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 1996  Aspen Technology Inc., Cambridge, Mass., said it completed its acquisition of Setpoint Inc., a closely held Houston controls software company, for $26.3 million. Setpoint, which is profitable, had revenue of about $45 million in 1995, Aspen said.
