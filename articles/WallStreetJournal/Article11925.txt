 Business Brief -- Chipcom Corp.: Concern to Post 70% Growth In Fourth-Quarter Earnings   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1995  Robert Held, chief executive, said Chipcom had strong gains in all sales channels including International Business Machines Corp. More than a third of Chipcom's sales are from IBM, which sells the Southborough, Mass., concern's networking devices. Chipcom is expected to report earnings tomorrow.
