 Eaton Financial Stake Of 6.4% Is Purchased By Group in Toronto   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 1988  FRAMINGHAM, Mass. -- A group of entities controlled by John Clark of Toronto said it holds a 6.4% stake in Eaton Financial Corp.'s common shares outstanding.  In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the group said it holds 237,000 Eaton shares that were purchased for $1,582,925.
