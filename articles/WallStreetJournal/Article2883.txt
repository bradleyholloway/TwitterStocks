 Bed Bath & Beyond Inc.: Fiscal 2nd-period net rose 38% on 36% sales increase   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 20, 1996  Bed Bath & Beyond Inc said net income rose 38% to $15.1 million for its fiscal second quarter ended Aug 25, 1996. The home-furnishings retail chain said sales increased 36% to $203.5 million.
