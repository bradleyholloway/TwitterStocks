 New Securities Issues [1]  The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1989  Salomon Brothers Inc. -- $250 million of CMO Trust 61 Remic mortgage securities, offered in five classes. The offering, by a trust established by Salomon Brothers Inc. and backed by Ginnie Mae 10% securities, was priced, where details are available, at market pricing with a spread ranging from 90 basis over the two-year Treasury note to 150 basis points over the 10-year Treasury bond.
