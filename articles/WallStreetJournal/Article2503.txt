 Greenspan says risk of inflation isn't over yet   By Wessel, David, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1993  On Jul 20, 1993, FRB Chairman Alan Greenspan warned the House Banking Committee about the risks of inflation and the imperative of squashing even unjustified expectations that it may accelerate. Greenspan added that short term interest rates controlled by the FRB were close to zero, saying that they could not remain that low indefinitely.
