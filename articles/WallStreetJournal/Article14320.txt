 Zico Investment Extends Hostile Offer For Control of Bancroft   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 1987  Zico, a British Virgin Islands company controlled by investor Michael B. Javett, said its latest offer to buy 965,000, or 51%, of Bancroft's shares will expire at midnight Jan. 15. Its original offer expired last Wednesday. Zico is offering $31 a share less declared dividends, or $29 a share.
