 National Power joins U.K. utilities fray as U.S. firms, others search for deals   By Parker-Pope, Tara, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 1995  Continuing the utility takeover binge in the UK, National Power PLC as expected announced on Oct 2, 1995 the 2.8 billion-pound agreed buyout of Southern Electric PLC, one of the 12 regional companies privatized in 1990. The Southern Electric takeover is one of six agreed or pending takeovers, and analysts speculated that the merger frenzy will continue among the remaining six independent utilities, with big US utilities figuring heavily in their speculation.
