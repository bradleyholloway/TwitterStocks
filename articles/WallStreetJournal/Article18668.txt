 Health: Kobe women tend to mental aftershocks   By Reitman, Valerie, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1995  A small band of volunteers, mostly housewives with scant counseling experience, have stepped in where Japan's limited system for mental-health care tails off and are visiting shelters to help survivers of the Kobe earthquake deal with their anxiety and depression.
