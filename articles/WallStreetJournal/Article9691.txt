 Citigroup Inc.: Insurance Venture Approved As Foreign Presence Expands   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 7, 2004  China's insurance-industry regulator approved a life-insurance joint venture between Citigroup Inc.'s Travelers Insurance Co. unit and Shanghai Alliance Investment Ltd. The equally owned venture will be based in Shanghai. The planned launch date is early 2005, Citigroup said.
