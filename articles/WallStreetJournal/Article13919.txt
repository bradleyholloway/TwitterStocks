 The Blues: Hugh Laurie Is Happy Performing the Blues   By Fusilli, Jim, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 2011  When we met in his trailer on the Fox back lot, an electric piano at hand in the cramped space, Mr. Laurie said he was both eager and hesitant to do an album that took on the New Orleans canon. Despite his delight with the record, Mr. Laurie doesn't know how the marketplace will react, especially in the U.S. where he's known as a TV character who occasionally plays the piano, organ or guitar on his popular series.
