 Cap Cities/ABC plans to launch all-news service   By Jensen, Elizabeth, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 1995  Capital Cities/ABC Inc is expected to announce on Dec 5, 1995 that it plans to launch a 24-hour news network that could be delivered to viewers by a mix of cable, telephone or new technologies sometime in 1997, according to people familiar with the situation. The network sees the news channel as a way to take advantage of ABC News's strong repuation and brand name.
