 Scandal Spills Over (Check the Gift Shop) At Brooklyn Museum   By By Ken Bensinger, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1999  The gift shop at the Brooklyn Museum of Art's now-infamous "Sensation" exhibit of contemporary British art seeks to be as cutting-edge as the show. While most exhibitions hawk sun-dappled posters and postcards, at "Sensation" you can buy a plastic oozing head.
