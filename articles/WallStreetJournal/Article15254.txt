 Honda to switch production site   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 1994  Honda Motor Co said it will start manufacturing a large motorcycle model with a 650-cubic-centimeter engine at its Italian plant, shifting the production of the popular model in Europe from its Japanese operations. Production of the NX 650 Dominator model will start before the end of 1994 at Honda Italia Industriale-ApA's Atessa plant in Italy.
