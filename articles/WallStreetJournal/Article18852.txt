 Chateau Properties Inc.: Manufactured Home's bid to discuss deal rebuffed   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1996  Chateau Properties Inc, Clinton Township MI, rebuffed an attempt by Manufactured Home Communities Inc to open talks about a merger, reaffirming its intention to merge with ROC Communities Inc, another REIT. Chicago-based Manufactured Home recently extended its $26-a-share hostile tender offer for Chateau.
