 Business Brief -- Broderbund Software Inc.: Fiscal First-Quarter Profit Put at High End of Forecast   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1993  Broderbund Software Inc. said it expects to report fiscal first-quarter profit in the range of 60 cents to 63 cents a share -- at the high end of Wall Street forecasts, which ranged from 55 cents to 63 cents.
