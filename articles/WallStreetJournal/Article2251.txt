 Kidder Chooses Theodore Johnson as Research Chief   By Power, William, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 1991  Kidder, Peabody & Co named as research director Theodore J. Johnson, after unsuccessfully trying to lure the research chief of Prudential Securities. Johnson is a 15-year veteran of Kidder, and his appointment has been only one of many among Wall Street research directors in recent months.
