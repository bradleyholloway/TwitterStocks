 Mutual funds quarterly review: Munis and mortgage bonds draw praise   By Lauricella, Thomas D, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1995  Bond fund managers believe that, by the middle of 1995, the economy should start slowing, relieving inflationary pressures and leading to a good year for bonds. Many advisers recommend funds holding Treasuries as well as funds holding municipal bonds.
