 Assisted-Suicide Pioneer Stirs a Legal Backlash   By Ball, Deborah; Mengewein, Julia, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 2010  Corrections & Amplifications Assisted suicide is legal in Washington, while in Montana, the state Supreme Court ruled on Dec. 31 that nothing in state law prevents physician-assisted suicide. A Feb. 6 front-page article about the organization Dignitas incorrectly said that Oregon is the only state where doctors are allowed to help people die and incorrectly stated that right-to-die organizations began assisting the terminally ill die in the 1960s.
