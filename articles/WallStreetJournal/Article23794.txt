 World News: Thai Court Keeps Projects Frozen on Environmental Concerns   By Oranan Paweewun; Murray, Leigh, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 2009  A Thai court ruled that dozens of industrial projects in the country's east must remain suspended because of environmental concerns, a decision that disappointed markets and may weigh on economic growth and the earnings of two of Thailand's largest conglomerates next year.
