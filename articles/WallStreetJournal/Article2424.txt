 Business Brief -- Investcorp: Bahrain Firm Acquires Half Of German Clothes Concern   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 1992  Investcorp, a Bahrain-based company that specializes in luxury-retailing investments, said it acquired a 50% interest in the German clothes designer Mondi Group from its owner, Herwig Zahm, who will retain the remaining 50% stake.
