 Is BP Stock a 'Buy'? --- Investors Review the Scenarios, and See Some Hope   By Denning, Liam, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 2010  The biggest risk to the company is a government-driven collapse, but experts doubt the U.S. government can carry out its harshest threats, such as forcing BP to pay the salaries of workers laid off because of the federal moratorium on deepwater drilling. BP's stock is trading at just 5.5 times analysts' consensus estimate of the company's 2010 earnings per share, compared with a multiple of 9 times for shares of Royal Dutch Shell, BP's closest competitor.
