 Bush to Bolster Alliance in Fight against Iraqis   By Seib, Gerald F; Greenberger, Robert S, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 1990  President Bush is planning an intensive campaign to shore up support for the international campaign against Iraq's invasion of Kuwait. Steps include forgiving much of Egypt's nearly $7 billion in military debts to the US and a summit meeting in Helsinki Finland with Soviet leader Mikhail Gorbachev.
