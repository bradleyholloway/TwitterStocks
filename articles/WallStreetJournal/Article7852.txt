 Rise in Burlington Resources Is Tied To Pennzoil Stake   By By Dianna Solis and Caleb Solomon, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 1989  Pennzoil Co.'s 8% stake in Burlington Resources Inc. has put the Seattle company in play -- and has possibly eliminated a number of other potential takeover targets from Pennzoil's aim.  Burlington Resources common stock surged $9.875 a share to $49.125 on extremely heavy volume of 4.3 million shares in New York Stock Exchange composite trading, following Pennzoil's previously reported disclosure that it had accumulated the stake. In Big Board trading, Pennzoil closed at $83.25 a share, up $1.50.
