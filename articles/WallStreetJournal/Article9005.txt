 Russian Premier Promises Back Pay On Eve of Protests   By By Mark Whitehouse, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1998  Russian Prime Minister Yevgeny Primakov issued a last-minute call for calm on the eve of nationwide strikes and protests scheduled for today, pledging to pay back wages and pensions.  But he offered no details of where the money would come from and said the government, after more than three weeks in office, was still working on an economic program. Mr. Primakov said that an emergency budget for the fourth quarter of this year could be ready within a week.
