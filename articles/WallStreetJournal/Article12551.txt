 Aetna Investors Plan Meeting to Pressure Firm to Boost Value   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 2000  Aetna, Hartford, Conn., decided last month to separate its health-insurance and finacial-services lines into separate companies rather than pursue a potential $70-a-share takeover proposal from ING America Insurance Holdings Inc. and WellPoint Health Networks Inc. William H. Donaldson, Aetna's chairman, described the offer as "totally inadequate."
