 East European roadblocks don't deter Daewoo, GM's Opel from racing in   By Mitchener, Brandon, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1996  Many auto makers without full-scale plants in Central and Eastern Europe are now reluctant to put them there, but General Motor Corp's Adam Opel AG unit and Daewoo Motor Corp are about to do just that. Both Opel and Daewoo are planning substantial investments, primarily in Poland. The risks of investing in the East European auto industry are analyzed.
