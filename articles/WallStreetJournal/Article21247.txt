 McDonnell Douglas Gets $208.2 Million In Defense Contracts   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 1988  McDonnell Douglas Corp. received $208.2 million in contracts for aircraft instruments for the Navy, space-defense systems for the Army and aircraft parts for the Air Force.  Sanders Associates was awarded $75.3 million in contracts for Navy electronics equipment and Air Force communications gear.  Lockheed Corp. received $50.4 million in contracts for Air Force computer services and weapons support.
