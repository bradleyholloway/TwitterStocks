 Recall the old chestnut? Public and private interest may conflict   By Stepankowsky, Paula L, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 1994  Ferdinand Moreno and Jo Hale Lyndon, two residents of Portland OR, say their spreading chestnut trees are so prolific that Moreno must do yard work in a hard hat and Lyndon worries she'll have to give up her house one day in self-defense. The city won't allow them to cut the trees down.
