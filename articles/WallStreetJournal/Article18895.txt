 Campaign fund limits unwise and perverse   By Gora, Joel M, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1997  Joel M. Gora responds to the Jan 17, 1997 editorial on campaign finance reform, "Daring Doolittle." Gora agrees that limits on political giving and spending are unconstitutional and unwise and suggest positive measures rather than "false reforms" that limit spending.
