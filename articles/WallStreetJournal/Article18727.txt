 Credit Ratings   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1995  HOLLYWOOD CASINO Corp. (Dallas) -- Moody's Investors Service assigned a B2 rating to this casino company's proposed senior unsecured notes due 2003. Moody's said the rating reflects the company's high debt load, limited financial flexibility, and exposure to newly established gambling jurisdictions. Moody's also said the rating reflects the company's "satisfactory" current operating results. Moody's said the proceeds will be used to fund the $25 million construction of a new hotel.
