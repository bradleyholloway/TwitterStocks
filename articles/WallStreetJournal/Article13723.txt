 A Triumph Of Faith Over Experience   By Denning, Liam, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2010  When considering their faith in central bankers and government officials to defy larger trends indefinitely, investors might do well to consider this admission in 2007 by someone who epitomized the notion of the all-powerful mandarin, former Federal Reserve boss Alan Greenspan: "Global forces, combined with lower international trade barriers, have diminished the scope of national governments to affect the paths of their economies."
