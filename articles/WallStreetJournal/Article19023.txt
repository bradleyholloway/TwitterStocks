 Technology Brief -- SYSTEMSOFT CORP.: Holders File Suits Alleging Misleading Financial Data   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1998  Several law firms representing SystemSoft Corp shareholders announced the filing of lawsuits against the Natick MA software company, contending that during 1996 SystemSoft made false or misleading financial statements about prospects for its SystemWizard software. The suits also contend that officers and directors sold more than 1 million shares for as high as $36.50 each.
