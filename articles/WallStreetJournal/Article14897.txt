 Legal Beat: Race Bias Suit   By Harlan, Christi; Hwang, Suein L, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 1991  Tempel Steel Co agreed to make a $4 million payment to settle an EEOC lawsuit alleging that the firm's recruiting and hiring practices are racially discriminatory. The money is to go to blacks who applied, or can show they would have applied, for factory jobs at the firm from Nov 1981 through the end of 1988.
