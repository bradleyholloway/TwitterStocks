 Prohibition: A Cautionary Tale   By Fleming, Thomas, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 2010  [...] the vocal minority who made Prohibition law believed they were marching in the footsteps of the abolitionists who sponsored a civil war to end another moral evil -- slavery.
