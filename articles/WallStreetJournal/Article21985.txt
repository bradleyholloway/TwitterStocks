 A derivative tied to Snapple shares takes sharp drop   By Steinmetz, Greg, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1994  After agreeing to an acquisition by Quaker Oats Co for $14 a share, the price for Snapple Beverage Co stock fell an additional $.50 on Nov 2, 1994, to $13.75, while a derivative tied to Snapple fell an additional $2.50, or 14%, to $15.875.
