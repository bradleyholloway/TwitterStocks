 Investing in Funds: A Monthly Analysis --- Interview: Is Your Manager Skillful...or Just Lucky? --- There's no way to be sure, but Michael Mauboussin offers pointers on how to sort it out   By Damato, Karen, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 2012  Investors can track, to a hundredth of a percentage point, how individual mutual funds are performing relative to their market benchmarks. To explore that, we spoke with Michael Mauboussin, the chief investment strategist at Legg Mason Inc.'s Legg Mason Capital Management unit and the author of a new book, "The Success Equation: Untangling Skill and Luck in Business, Sports, and Investing."
