 Pennzoil Will Restate Earnings From 1999 Through Most of 2001   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 2002  Pennzoil said the restatement is a result of an error in the financial statements of Excel Paralubes, in which it holds an equal partnership with Conoco Inc. Pennzoil said Excel Paralubes recently determined that the allocation of certain products meant to be distributed between Excel's facility and an adjacent Conoco refinery in Louisiana had been measured incorrectly.
