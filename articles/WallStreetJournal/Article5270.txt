 Host Marriott Corp. plans a spinoff of concessions business from lodging   By King, Thomas R, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1995  Host Marriott Corp plans to spin off its relatively mature airport and toll-road concessions business from its more promising lodging real-estate business. The lodging real-estate business will retain the Host Marriott Corp name, while the new company will be called Host Marriott Services Corp.
