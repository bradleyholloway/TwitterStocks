 World-Wide, Bad News You Can Take to Bank   By Sidel, Robin; Fitzpatrick, Dan; Kapner, Suzanne, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 2011  A federal regulator filed lawsuits Friday over failed mortgage investments, seeking compensation from 17 financial institutions that could run into the tens of billions of dollars. In meetings with bank executives around the country, analysts from Keefe Bruyette & Woods recently noted a "more conservative sentiment from bank managements as to lack of loan growth, lack of consumer demand, intense competition pressuring [profit] margins and a lack of imminent" mergers and acquisition activity in the banking industry.
