 De Lorean Plant Is Sold To U.K. Furniture Maker --- A Wall Street Journal News Roundup   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 1987  A British furniture maker has purchased the former De Lorean Motor Co. assembly plant in Northern Ireland. Terms weren't disclosed. The 70-acre complex has remained nearly vacant since the early 1980s, when the Industrial Development Board for Northern Ireland began seeking a buyer.
