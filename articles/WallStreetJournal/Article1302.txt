 World News: Political Shift in India May Spur Asset Sales   By Gangopadhyay, Abhrajit, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 2008  The government, led by Prime Minister Manmohan Singh's Congress Party, said Friday it will hold a parliamentary confidence vote this month to determine its fate and the future of a civilian nuclear deal with the U.S. The proposed nuclear deal had prompted leftist groups to withdraw support from the coalition, though Mr. Singh secured alternative backing from a pro-business party.
