 Lobbyists Show No Mercy On Nurses Association   By By Mitchel Benson, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1997  The union has been booted from a powerful lobbying coalition of health-care providers and insurers for supporting efforts to loosen the lid on medical-malpractice awards.  The 25,000-member nurses group was one of the founders of Californians Allied for Patient Protection. The alliance's sole purpose is to preserve a 1975 law that restricts lawyers' fees and awards for pain and suffering.
