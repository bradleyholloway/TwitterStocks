 The American Who Manages The Decline of a Japanese Hamlet --- Jeffrey Irish Leads Elderly Folk Who Expect Their Town to Pass Away With Them   By Wakabayashi, Daisuke, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 2009  Having decided that he wanted to live in a farming community, he hopped on a scooter and rode around the prefecture until, in 1998, he came across a hilltop cabin with breathtaking views in Tsuchikure. Since the prefecture owned the property, he negotiated with government officials a yearly rent of 2,500 yen, or $28, and didn't bother to ask permission from the village.
