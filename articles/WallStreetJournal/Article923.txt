 Ralston Purina to Sell Drake Bakeries Unit In Leveraged Buyout   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 1987  ST. LOUIS -- Ralston Purina Co. said it signed a definitive agreement to sell its Drake Bakeries Inc. unit in a leveraged buyout led by Drake management.  The food and animal feed concern didn't disclose terms of the pact. But it said the sale will generate a pre-tax gain in the fourth quarter of more than $115 million. Ralston paid about $115 million to acquire the unit from Borden Inc. in July 1986. The company said it expects to complete the sale within 45 days.
