 Letters to the Editor: Nixon's Real Crime Was the Coverup   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1994  Sam Dash ("Whitewater, Watergate," editorial page, March 11) is as foggy on Whitewater as he was on Watergate 20 years ago. He distinguishes them this way: Richard Nixon committed "serious crimes in office solely to advance his political ambitions." Any transgressions by Bill and Hillary Clinton, on the other hand, involved only "commercial transactions" that occurred "years before he was elected president."
