 Some Publishers Wary Of Selling on iTunes   By Ovide, Shira; Adams, Russell, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 2010  Geoff Reiss, general manager of Newsweek's digital unit, said it isn't worth sacrificing the seamless iTunes payment system to gather data about a relatively small number of early iPad magazine buyers. Next Issue Media and other initiatives by News Corp., Hearst-owned Skiff LLC and the Associated Press's Gateway unit, are continuing to talk to potential partners about creating a digital storefront, packages of subscriptions and standard content templates for newspapers and magazines to be read on the iPad and other digital devices.
