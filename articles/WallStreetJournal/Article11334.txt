 New Securities Issues [1]  The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1990  McDonald's Corp. -- 100 million New Zealand dollars of 12 1/2% notes due Feb. 21, 1993, priced at par. Rated double-A-2 by Moody's Investors Service Inc. and double-A by Standard & Poor's Corp., the non-callable note issue was comanaged by Merrill Lynch Capital Markets, Kidder Peabody & Co. and Prudential-Bache Securities Inc.  Grant County Public Utility District No. 2, Wash. -- $51.5 million of Wanapum Hydroelectric Development and Priest Rapids Hydroelectric revenue bonds, Series 1990 (AMT), due Jan. 1, 1993-2005, 2009 and 2018, tentatively priced by a Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co. group to yield from 6.40% in 1993 to 7.70% in 2018. Current interest serial bonds are priced at par to yield from 6.40% in 1993 to 7.50% in 2005. The Wanapum series term bonds consist of $3.1 million of 7.60% bonds priced at par due in 2009 and $11.2 million of 7.70% term bonds priced at par. The Priest Rapids series term bonds consist of $4.8 million of 7.60% bonds priced at par and $17.8 million of 7.70% term bonds priced at par. The bonds are rated single-A-1/single-A-plus. They are rated double-A-minus by Fitch Investors Service Inc. Interest on the bonds will be treated as a preference item in calculating the federal alternative minimum tax that may be imposed on certain investors.
