 Venture Capital Saw Fund-Raising Drop In the Third Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 2003  VCs are finding it more difficult to persuade limited partners to increase or continue their commitments to venture investing, said Jesse Reyes, director of Thomson Venture Economics.  Most of these -- 73% -- were follow-on funds for existing partnerships. The largest funds included Lehman Brothers Venture Partners 2003, ComVentures VI and Crosslink Crossover Fund IV, according to the survey.
