 Corporate News: NRG Energy Rejects Exelon's $6.2 Billion Offer; Power Generator Says Bid 'Manifestly Undervalues' Firm, Expresses Doubt About Suitor's Ability to Finance Deal   By Smith, Rebecca, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 2008  In an unsolicited bid last month, Exelon offered to pay a fixed exchange ratio of 0.485 Exelon share for each share of NRG, which is based in Princeton, N.J. As recently as April, NRG shares were selling for about $45, but they fell to about $15 prior to the offer, battered by the credit- market crisis and pessimism about power sales in a recession by unregulated generators like NRG.
