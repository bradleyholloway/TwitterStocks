 For Experts, a Delicate Dance --- When Investors Come Calling, Doctors-as-Consultants Can Say This, Not That   By Eder, Steve, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 2010  The government's broad insider-trading investigation is focusing in part on how hedge funds may have traded on information obtained from consultants such as doctors who were hired to discuss their work with investors. Investigations are leading to scrutiny of the line between providing basic industry analysis and divulging nonpublic information, and they are underscoring how common consultations with experts are.
