 The carnage that led to the bomb   By Butler, Fred G, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1994  Fred G. Butler responds to the Sep 13, 1994 letter "We Didn't Have to Drop the Bomb," saying that his experience as a naval officer during WWII leads him to the belief that it was not necessary to drop atomic bombs upon urban populations in order to bring the Japanese to their knees.
