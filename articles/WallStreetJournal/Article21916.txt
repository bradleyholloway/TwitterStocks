 Advertising: Cereal account review   The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 1994  Ralcorp Holdings, which was formerly part of Ralston Purina Co, has put its big cereal advertising business into review. Contenders include Avrett, Cree & Ginsberg, Tatham RSCG, Backer Spielvogel Bates and D'Arcy Masius Benton & Bowles.
