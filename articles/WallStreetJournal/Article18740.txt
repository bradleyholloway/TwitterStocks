 Disney, Capital Cities shareholders to vote on acquisition Jan 4   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1995  Walt Disney Co and Capital Cities/ABC Inc set Jan 4, 1996 as the date for the two companies' shareholders to separately vote on Disney's proposed $19 billion acquisition of Capital Cities, The entertainment concerns said they hope to begin mailing a joint proxy statement to shareholders about Nov 15, 1995, subject to SEC approval of the document.
