 Technology (A Special Report) --- Still on Top? Microsoft's Steve Ballmer and Ray Ozzie on where the company is leading -- and where it isn't   By Mossberg, Walter S, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 7, 2010  Steve Ballmer, chief executive of Microsoft Corp., has the unenviable task of keeping the world's leading software maker for personal and business computing in first place. To help him in this job, he has Ray Ozzie, the industry pioneer who created Lotus Notes and who in 2006 took over the role of chief software architect from Microsoft co-founder Bill Gates.
