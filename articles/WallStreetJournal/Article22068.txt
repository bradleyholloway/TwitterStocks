 Skepticism surrounds surge in the dollar   By Sesit, Michael R, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 3, 1995  A package of Japanese regulatory changes, plus heavy dollar buying by the US and Japan, sent the dollar soaring 3% to a four-month high against the yen on Aug 2, 1995. In late afternoon New York trading, the dollar rose to 90.97 yen and 1.3980 marks, from the previous day's Y88.05 and DM1.3760.
