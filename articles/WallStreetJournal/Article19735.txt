 Retail Brief -- Federated Department Stores Inc.: Stronger-Than-Expected Sales Bring Rise in Earnings Outlook   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 2004  Federated Department Stores Inc. raised its earnings forecast for the fiscal fourth quarter and full year after posting better-than- expected sales. The Cincinnati company said January same-store sales, or sales at stores open at least a year, rose 5.5%. The forecast for sales had ranged from a 1% fall to a 1% increase.
