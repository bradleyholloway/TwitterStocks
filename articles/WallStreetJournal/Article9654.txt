 Tip of the Week: Check Math On Credit   By Herman, Tom, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 2004  Some taxpayers are making other errors with the credit. If you've already filed and realize you goofed, don't fret. "You don't have to do anything until you hear from the IRS," Internal Revenue Service spokesman Don Roberts says. If you filed for a refund, the IRS typically reduces the size of your refund. If your mistake means you owe more taxes, the IRS will tell you how much to send in, he says.
