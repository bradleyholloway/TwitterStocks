 U.S. News: Study Seeks More Checks of Kids' Cholesterol   By Winslow, Ron, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 2010  National recommendations now say doctors should check cholesterol in children and adolescents when parents or grandparents have been diagnosed with heart disease prior to age 55, when such family history isn't known, or when a parent has total cholesterol over 240.
