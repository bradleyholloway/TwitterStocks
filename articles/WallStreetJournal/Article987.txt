 Days Inn Approves Sweetened Proposal To Take Firm Private   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 1988  Days Inns Corp. said its board approved a sweetened proposal by Reliance Capital Group L.P. to take the lodging concern private in a transaction valued at $161.5 million.  Under the proposal, Reliance will pay $7.75 in cash, plus preferred stock it values at $2.25, for each of the approximately 16.1 million Days Inns shares it doesn't already own.
