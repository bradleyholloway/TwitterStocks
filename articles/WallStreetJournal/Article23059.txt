 Energy Brief: Anadarko Petroleum Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 2003  Anadarko Petroleum Corp. said third-quarter profit jumped 45% on strong oil and natural-gas prices. The Woodlands, Texas, oil and natural-gas producer reported net income of $276 million, or $1.09 a share, compared with $190 million, or 74 cents a share, a year earlier.
