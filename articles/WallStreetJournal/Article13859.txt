 Fore! Watch Out for Those Golf Expenses!   By Opdyke, Jeff D, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 2011  Golf clearly demands an outlay of cash thatfar exceeds what my wife, Amy, and I used to spend when soccer was my son's sport of choice. When you structure your budget with one activity in mind, as I've done with my son's soccer expenses, a child's switch to a far-pricier endeavor necessarily imposes a financial impact that you have to account for -- and that can have effects elsewhere in the family.
