 Money's Worth / Travel   By O'Connor, Brian J, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 2003  What You Get: Deluxe resort with 7 eateries to choose from, and a 1- acre "aquatic playground." Unlimited access to Disney theme parks, with free shuttle to Disney attractions.  What You Get: Kids' alcove features bunk beds, child-size table and a night light. Try the resort's five restaurants or dine at Disney World. The shuttle there is $14 a head.  Notable: A "pool concierge" hands out water toys, floats, and CD players and CDs for the hotel's three pools. Or just stay in the room and surf the Web via your TV.
