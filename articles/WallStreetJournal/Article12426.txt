 LNR Property Is Set To Invest in Venture In Mortgage Securities   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 1999  In a sign of continued recovery in the market for commercial mortgage-backed securities, LNR Property Corp. said it committed $100 million to a venture that plans to acquire $2.2 billion of the real-estate backed assets.  Like many Wall Street firms, LNR, a real-estate investment, finance and management company, retreated from the market. But in recent months, returns on commercial mortgage-backed securities have improved, analysts say, and LNR and others have gradually moved back in.
