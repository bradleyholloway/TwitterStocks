 Tip of the Week: Look to CDs To Grab 5%   By Ransom, Diana, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 2006  As of Friday, there were five-year CDs yielding as much as 5.72%, with minimum deposits ranging from $500 to $10,000, according to Bankrate.com's list of the highest-yielding nationally available CDs. Among the highest: Intervest National Bank of New York at 5.72% and First Internet Bank of Indiana, in Indianapolis, at 5.64%.
