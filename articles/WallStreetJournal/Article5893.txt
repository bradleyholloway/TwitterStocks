 NAACP Grades HotelsOn Their Diversity Efforts   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 2000  Marriott International Inc. earned a B from the NAACP; Cendant Corp. hotels, B-minus; Hilton Hotels Corp., C-plus; and Hyatt Hotels Corp, C-plus. The NAACP issued grades below C to four hotel chains: Starwood Hotels & Resorts Worldwide Inc., C-minus; Carlson Companies Inc.'s Radisson Hospitality Worldwide, D-plus; Omni Hotels, D-plus; and Wyndham International, D.
