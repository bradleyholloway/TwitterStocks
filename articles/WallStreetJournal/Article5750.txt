 GM to Consolidate Internet Ventures Under One Group   By By Fara Warner, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1999  The expected move signals GM's resolve to take its Internet initiatives out of the experimental stage and make them a core part of its business strategy. As a signal of the importance GM executives attach to this, GM President and Chief Operating Officer G. Richard Wagoner and Ron Zarrella, executive vice president and president of GM North America, are scheduled to unveil the new strategy at a press conference this morning.  GM is expected to consolidate its various Internet programs, including its divisional Web sites such as Chevrolet.com and online car-shopping site GMBuyPower, under a global Internet group, said people familiar with GM's plans. GM is expected to name an executive to run the new organization today.
