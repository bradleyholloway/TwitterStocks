 Borden stock dives on plan to restructure   By Hwang, Suein L, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1994  The stock of Borden Inc took a dive Jan 5, 1994 as investors expressed disappointment in a new restructuring plan that includes the sale of the company's North American snacks business, the sale of two small seafood and jams and jellies businesess and the halving of its annual dividend to $.30.
