 Ford to offer dressed-down Taurus model   By Suris, Oscar, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1996  Ford Motor Co said it will offer a dressed-down version of its top-selling Taurus line of midsize cars, knocking about $600 off the cost of the base model that has been criticized as being priced too high. The new G version will start at $18,545, including shipping, compared with $19,150 for the previous base model, the GL version. Ford posted the sharpest Jan 1996 sales decline of the Big Three, off 3.4% from a year earlier.
