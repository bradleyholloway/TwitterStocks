 DaimlerChrysler AG: Mercedes Car Group Sales Increased by 2.2% Last Month   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 2005  The Mercedes Car Group division of DaimlerChrysler AG said it sold 100,200 vehicles world-wide during July, up 2.2% from a year earlier. The unit, based in Stuttgart, Germany, cited increased demand in the U.S., where sales reached a record 20,800 for the month due to demand for the new M-Class and E-Class sedans.
