 Slovenia Awakens to Price of Its Dream   By Thurow, Roger, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1991  The people of Slovenia are realizing the economic price that may be paid for their announced declaration of independence from Yugoslavia. Their considerable trade with Yugoslavia is vanishing and as of yet, they are unrecognized by the nations of the world. The country is eager to find new markets in the West for its exports.
