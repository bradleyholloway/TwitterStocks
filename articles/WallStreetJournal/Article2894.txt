 ServiceMaster L.P.: Furniture Medic to expand into Britain and Canada   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1996  ServiceMaster LP, which has been rapidly expanding its businesses in recent months, plans to extend its Furniture Medic business into Britain and Canada by 1997. ServiceMaster will likely enter a licensing agreement to allow companies in Britain and Canada to operate under the Furniture Medic name.
