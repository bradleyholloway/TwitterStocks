 The perils of increased immigration   By Matloff, Norman, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1997  John Tanton responds to a Jan 24, 1997 Potomac Watch article titled "Getting the GOP Back to Reagan on Immigration" by Paul Gigot.
