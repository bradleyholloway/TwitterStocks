 Business Brief -- GOODYEAR TIRE & RUBBER CO.: Remaining Contred Stake Is Bought for $59 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1998  Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co announced that it has bought the remaining 40% stake in Contred, a South African tire maker, from Anglovall Industries Ltd, for about $59 million. Separately, Goodyear, Akron OH, said it has completed the sale of its latex-manufacturing facility in Calhoun GA to GenCorp for an undisclosed amount.
