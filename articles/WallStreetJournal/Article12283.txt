 Business Brief -- LAIDLAW ENVIRONMENTAL SERVICES INC.: Cash Part of Safety-Kleen Offer Could Be Adjusted   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1998  Laidlaw Environmental Services Inc, Columbia SC, said it may adjust the cash terms of its $1.76 billion cash-and-stock offer for Safety-Kleen Corp, depending on the outcome of a lawsuit filed by Laidlaw. Safety-Kleen has accepted a $1.58 billion bid from Philip Services Corp and would have to pay Philip a $75 million break-up fee if Laidlaw loses its suit.
