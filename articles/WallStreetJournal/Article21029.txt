 International Corporate Report: GEC Earnings Rise 1.7%; Firm Mulls Takeover Bids   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1986  GENERAL ELECTRIC CO. of Britain said earnings in its first half ended Sept. 30 rose 1.7%, to the equivalent of $258 million from $254.1 million the year before. Sales gained 2%, to $3.6 billion from $3.53 billion.
