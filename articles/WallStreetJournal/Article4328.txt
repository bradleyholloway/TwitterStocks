 Harcourt Finds Potential Buyers For Unit, 2 Lines   By By Michael Allen, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 9, 1987  ORLANDO, Fla. -- Harcourt Brace Jovanovich Inc. said it found potential buyers for its magazine unit and two other businesses that the company is trying to sell.  Harcourt didn't disclose the names of the possible buyers but said it doesn't dispute news reports that the entire package may fetch $350 million to $400 million. The company is trying to sell the unit and businesses to raise cash to pay off debt.
