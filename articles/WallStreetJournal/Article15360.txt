 Legal beat: Jury awards rise for improper care of the elderly   By Felsenthal, Edward, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 1995  With new federal regulations regarding Medicare and Medicaid funding, along with patients-rights laws in more than a dozen states, the number of jury awards in nursing-home negligence cases has increased significantly, with the mean award rising from $238,285 in 1987 to $525,853 in 1994.
