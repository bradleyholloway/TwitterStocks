 Fiat to Offer 31% Of New Holland To Raise $1 Billion   By By Brandon Mitchener, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 1996  Fiat SpA plans to float 31% of its New Holland NV agricultural equipment unit in an issue that would raise about $1 billion toward Fiat's expansion in developing countries.  About 46.5 million common shares, with an overallotment option for an additional 6.975 million shares, would be listed on the New York Stock Exchange later this month, Fiat said yesterday in its filing with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission.
