 Financial Services Brief -- Sumitomo Mitsui Financial Group: Write-Offs of Bad Loans To Result in Loss for Year   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 2003  Japan's Sumitomo Mitsui Financial Group Inc. said it expects a group net loss of 470 billion yen ($3.92 billion) for the latest fiscal year ended March 31, a reversal from a 30 billion yen profit projected earlier, due to accelerated bad-loan write-offs and losses from stockholdings....
