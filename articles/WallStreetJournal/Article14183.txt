 Resorts Is Said To Discuss Casino's Sale to U.S. Capital   By By Steve Swartz, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 1985  ATLANTIC CITY, N.J. -- Resorts International Inc. has begun negotiations for the possible sale of its casino hotel here to U.S. Capital Corp. of Columbia, S.C., according to sources close to the negotiations.  Resorts, which also operates a casino hotel in the Bahamas, is building a second casino hotel here about a block away from its existing facility. The new Resorts property, which isn't expected to be operating fully until 1988, is planned to be twice the size of Resorts existing casino hotel and to cost an estimated $400 million.
