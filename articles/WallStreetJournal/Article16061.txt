 Business Brief -- Sports Club Co.: Gym Chain Finds Financing For a Total of $15 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 2001  Sports Club Co., Los Angeles, renewed its $15 million bank-credit facility three months after it lost previous financing. The health-club operator said the new credit facility matures on May 31, 2002, and bears interest at the London Interbank Offered Rate, plus 2.25% -- the bank's prime rate.
