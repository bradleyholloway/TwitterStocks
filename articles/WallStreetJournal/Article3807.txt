 Siemens AG: Prices on Some Mobile Phones Will Be Cut to Counter Nokia   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 2004  Siemens AG will reduce prices on some handsets to counter a move by market leader Nokia Corp. but won't be drawn into a price war, the head of the company's mobile-phone division said. Nokia started reducing prices earlier this year after reporting steeper-than- expected declines in market share.
