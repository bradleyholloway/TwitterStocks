 Backers of anti-affirmative-action plan in California claim to be out of money   By Zachary, G Pascal, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1995  Backers of California's proposed anti-affirmative-action measure say they are out of money and have stopped gathering signatures to place their measure on the Nov 1996 ballot. Without an infusion of cash, the effort will shut down.
