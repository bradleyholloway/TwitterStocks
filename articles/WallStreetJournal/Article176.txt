 Brown-Forman Corp.: Second-period results trail expectations of analysts   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 24, 1993  Brown-Forman Corp, citing sluggish beverage sales, posted less-than-expected second-quarter net income of $62.5 million compared with net of $51.2 million a year earlier. The company said it's "no longer certain" that fiscal 1994 earnings will improve from the year before.
