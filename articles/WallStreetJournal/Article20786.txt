 Corporate News: TNK-BP Shareholders To Drop Suit Against BP   By Marson, James, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 2012  Minority shareholders in the traded unit of Russian oil company TNK-BP Ltd. have filed to withdraw a $3 billion suit against BP PLC, as the British energy giant and its partners close in on deals to sell Russia's No. 3 oil producer.
