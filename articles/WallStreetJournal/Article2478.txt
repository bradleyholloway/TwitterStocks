 Retailing: Wal-Mart bows to pricing reality by changing 4 letters   By Ortega, Bob, The Wall Street Journal,  May 21, 1993  Bowing to the industry reality that nobody always has the lowest price, Wal-Mart Stores Inc will begin phasing out its slogan "Always the low price. Always," replacing it with a new line, "Always low prices. Always." The change, though small, reflects the changes the discount retailer has had to make now that it must compete in prices with other big retailers such as Kmart Corp, Target and Venture Stores Inc.
