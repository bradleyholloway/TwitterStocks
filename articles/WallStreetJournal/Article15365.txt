 Advertising: McDonald's joins America Online to send information to PC users   By Goldman, Kevin, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 1995  McDonald's Corp is hoping PC users who subscribe to America Online will tap into the fast-food chain's latest advertising vehicle: a regular on-line site. Capitalizing on the company's family-oriented image, McDonald's has created a site called McFamily that features information about parenting and family life.
