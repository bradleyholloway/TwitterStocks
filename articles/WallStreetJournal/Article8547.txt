 Advertising: Ally & Gargano's 'retail mentality' is a marked change for the agency   By Goldman, Kevin, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 1994  Bill Luceno, chairman and CEO of Ally & Gargano, makes no apology for the agency's serviceable, retail-oriented advertising for such clients as Dunkin' Donuts, Bank of New York and the New York Daily News. The agency had been known for its cutting-edge creativity until recently.
