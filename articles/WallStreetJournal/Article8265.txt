 Family Dollar Inc.: Three Retail Chains Report Mixed Sales for Last Month   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 1992  Family Dollar Inc said sales rose 25% in Jul 1992 to $102.6 million from $82.1 million a year earlier. Claire's Stores Inc's July sales fell 3% to $17.2 million from $17.8 million a year earlier, while Catherines Stores Corp's July sales rose 20% to $9.7 million.
