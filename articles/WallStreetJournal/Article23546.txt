 Nasdaq Opens Beijing Office to Woo Listings   By Leow, Jason, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 2007  Nasdaq has had indirect access to China for more than a decade working out of New York and London, where it has offices. From 1998 to 2004, the exchange operator had a "preparatory office" in Shanghai, but its function was tightly restricted. "By requirement, we couldn't even put up the [Nasdaq] sign," Mr. [Guang Xun Xu] said.
