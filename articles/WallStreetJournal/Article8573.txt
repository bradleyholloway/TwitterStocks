 Fraenkel unexpectedly resigns as head of global equity research at Lehman   By Raghavan, Anita; Dorfman, John R, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 1995  Fred Fraenkel, head of global equity research at Lehman Brothers Inc, resigned unexpectedly on Feb 6, 1995. He said that he was tired of a research director's duties and wanted to take time to evaluate his job options.
