 Marketing Brief -- Vestron Inc.: Company Seeks Investment To Expand Movie Business   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 1989  Vestron said the company hired Merrill Lynch Capital Markets Group to assist it in raising $150 million or more via such means as a private placement. Vestron's founder and chairman, Austin Furst Jr., who controls 85% of the company, said in a statement that Vestron wants to find minority investors "with interests and long-term goals compatible with ours."
