 WEEKEND JOURNAL; Adviser: Datebook / Oct. 3-9   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 2008  Longtime star William Petersen will exit as a regular on the show in January, and in the ninth episode (scheduled for Dec. 11), actor Laurence Fishburne (co-star of "What's Love Got to Do With It?" and "The Matrix") joins the cast as a college lecturer who becomes a member of the Las Vegas Crime Lab.
