 Tax report: Pick a number   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1993  William J. McCollough of Bridgeport IL, who used false numbers for bank accounts and failed to file tax returns for 1980-84, pleaded guilty of willful failure to file returns for 1980-82. After McCollough and his wife learned that they were being audited, they put the title of their house in their minor children's names and refused to cooperate with the IRS.
