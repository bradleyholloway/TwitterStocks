 GCA Misses Payment Of $1.7 Million Owed On $30 Million Notes   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1985  BEDFORD, Mass. -- GCA Corp., hit hard by the semiconductor slump, said it didn't pay $1.7 million in interest due on $30 million in subordinated convertible notes held by Teachers Insurance & Annuity Association of America.
