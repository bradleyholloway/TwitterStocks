 Automotive Brief: PSA Peugeot-Citroen   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 2005  French car maker PSA Peugeot-Citroen said it would cut 850 jobs at an English production plant this year. Peugeot said the jobs will cut at the Ryton plant, near Coventry, northwest of London -- which produces the Peugeot 206 -- with the elimination of a weekend production shift.
