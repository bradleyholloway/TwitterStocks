 Business Brief -- LTV Corp.: Steel Bar Line to Be Sold; Special Charge Is Expected   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 1988  The Dallas steel, energy and aerospace concern said it hired Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc. to help it sell the division, which employs 5,400 and makes about 1.2 million tons of steel a year. The sale will result in a "substantial special charge" for the third quarter, LTV said, but it wouldn't elaborate.
