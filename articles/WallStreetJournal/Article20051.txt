 Kodak's Incoming Finance Chief Sees a Successful Turnaround   By Bulkeley, William M, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 2006  Mr. [Frank Sklarsky], currently the chief financial officer at ConAgra Foods Inc. and a veteran of the financial staffs at DaimlerChrysler AG and Dell Inc., said after investigating Kodak's situation, he concluded, "it was a scenario that was at least as attractive as situations I've been in before." Mr. Sklarsky was at Chrysler during the early 1980's when the auto maker's survival was in question. "My assessment is there's a good chance of success," he said.
