 Trilon to Buy Stakes In Insurance Brokers During Next 2 Years   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 1986  Trilon is 46%-owned by Brascan Ltd., a holding company indirectly controlled by Toronto financiers Edward and Peter Bronfman.
