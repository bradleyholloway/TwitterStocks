 Geon-Synergistics Purchase   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1997  Geon Co said holders of all the common shares and more than 95% of the outstanding Class A shares of Synergistics Industries Ltd, a Canadian plastics maker, have tendered their share under Geon's offer to acquire all of the shares for $83.5 million, or about $15.75 a share.
