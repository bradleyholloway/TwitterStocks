 The Battle at Big Sky --- Yellowstone Club Dispute Roils Enclave for the Ultrawealthy   By Frank, Robert, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 2009  On Tuesday, a group of founding members who hold equity in the club filed a lawsuit against the Blixseths in U.S. Bankruptcy Court in Montana, accusing them of breaching their fiduciary duty in how they used the loan proceeds. In a separate suit filed this week, a group of unsecured creditors that includes construction firms, utilities and other companies that did work for Yellowstone alleged that Credit Suisse aided the Blixseths in their fiduciary breach.
