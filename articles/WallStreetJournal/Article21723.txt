 Business Brief -- Trefoil Capital Investors L.P.: Company Exercises Right To Appoint More Directors   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 1992  Trefoil, an investment fund headed by Roy E. Disney, elected Clifford A. Miller, 64 years old, and V.A. Ravindran, 44, expanding the company's board to 11 members. Trefoil said it reserves the right to name two additional members, which would give Trefoil majority control.
