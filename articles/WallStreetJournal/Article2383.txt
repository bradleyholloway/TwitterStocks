 Business Brief -- Goody Products Inc.: Firm Posts 2nd-Period Loss Of $16.7 Million on Charges   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1992  Goody Products Inc., Kearny, N.J., posted a second-quarter net loss due to $28.1 million it incurred in special charges for restructuring.  The hair products manufacturer reported a loss of $16.7 million, or $2.55 a share, compared with net income of $2.1 million, or 32 cents a share, a year earlier. Sales fell 8% to $60.5 million.
