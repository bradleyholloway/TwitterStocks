 Channukah, Passover celebrate freedom   By Showley, Devon, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1994  Devon Showley responds to the Dec 23, 1993 editorial "In Hoc Anno Domini," saying the editorial's thrust would have been appropriate not only to Christmas but also to Channukah and/or Passover, which he says are nothing if not a celebration of political liberties and religious freedom.
