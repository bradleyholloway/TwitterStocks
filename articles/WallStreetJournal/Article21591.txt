 Midwest Communications Corp.: Firm Will Cease Operations, Liquidate Its Core Business   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 2, 1991  Midwest Communications Corp, a supplier of video and audio equipment to the TV industry, filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy-law protection after giving up its effort to raise cash through asset sales. The firm said it would take substantial liquidation charges for the year ended Jun 30, 1991 and for fiscal 1992.
