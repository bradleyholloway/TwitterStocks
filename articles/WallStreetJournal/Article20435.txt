 U.S. News --- CAPITAL JOURNAL: Timetable Reflects Isolationist Surge   By Seib, Gerald F, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 2009  The silver lining for Mr. Obama as he undertakes his new Afghan policy is that foreign-policy elites -- that is, Council of Foreign Relations members, who were surveyed separately -- are considerably more supportive of the Afghan surge and far less inclined toward pulling in America's horns. The broader reality, though, is that the president is asking the country to support an expanded military effort abroad, along with allied nations, at a time when the country is less inclined to expand efforts abroad, and less inclined to do so in partnership with other countries.
