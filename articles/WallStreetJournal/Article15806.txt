 Business Brief -- CONSECO INC.: Deals Are Set to Acquire Three Insurance Marketers   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 1999  Carmel IN-based Conseco Inc said it has acquired two health-insurance marketing firms and has agreed to acquire a third. Terms of the transactions weren't disclosed. All three firms are closely held. They are Consolidated Marketing Group, Inter-State Service Inc, and TLC National Marketing Co.
