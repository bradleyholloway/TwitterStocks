 Economy still is expanding at solid pace   By Chang, Yi-Hsin, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 1994  In its periodic regional economic report, the Federal Reserve said the economy is "continuing to expand at a solid pace," although there are signs of weakness in the South and the Midwest. A detailed summary of economic developments in the 12 Federal Reserve districts in late June and Jul 1994 is presented.
