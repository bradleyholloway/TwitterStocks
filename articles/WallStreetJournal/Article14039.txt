 Apple Chip Suppliers in Focus   By Tibken, Shara, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 2012  In the chip sector, some of Apple's past suppliers include audio chip maker Cirrus Logic and camera sensor maker OmniVision Technologies Inc. Wireless chip giant Qualcomm is expected to supply a 4G Long Term Evolution processor for the phone, while Broadcom Corp. is the likely provider of Wi-Fi and other connectivity.
