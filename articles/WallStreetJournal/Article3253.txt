 Lockheed Martin Seems Close to Deal To Sell $8 Billion of F-16s to Emirates   By By Anne Marie Squeo, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 21, 1999  Lockheed Martin Corp., after years of haggling and missed deadlines, finally appears close to inking an $8 billion contract to sell 80 of its F-16 fighter jets to the United Arab Emirates.  U.S. Defense Secretary William Cohen, on a nine-nation tour of the Middle East, said that U.S. officials have resolved the technical issues that have so far blocked the deal, and that he hoped a deal might be announced before the annual Dubai Air Show in mid-November, the Associated Press reported.
