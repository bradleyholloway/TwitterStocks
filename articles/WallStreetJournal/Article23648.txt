 Not Much Fun Now With the Fund of Funds   By Bryan-Low, Cassell, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 2008  Swiss private bank Julius Baer Holding AG's GAM asset-management unit, which includes a fund-of-funds business, has seen its assets shrink by 18 billion Swiss francs ($16 billion) in the first six months of the year to 68 billion francs, on a combination of withdrawals and negative performance, as well as currency impact.
