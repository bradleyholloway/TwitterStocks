 New Securities Issues [4]  The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 1992  Arkla Inc. -- $225 million of notes, due April 15, 1997, priced as 9 7/8 % at 99.896 to yield 9.9%. The non-callable notes were priced at a spread of three points above the Treasury's five-year note. Rated Ba-1 by Moody's Investors Service Inc. and double-B-plus by Standard & Poor's Corp., the issue will be sold through underwriters led by First Boston Corp.
