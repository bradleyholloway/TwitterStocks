 Cannavino joins the exodus of old IBMers   By Hays, Laurie, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1995  James Cannavino, a blunt-spoken maverick and one of the last members of the old guard at IBM Corp, is leaving the company just 15 months after being named its top strategist. Cannavino said on Mar 2, 1995 that he's retiring, and added that the decision was his own. However, it has been clear in recent months that the fiery 32-year veteran of IBM seemed to be on the outside looking in as Chairman Louis V. Gerstner Jr formed his inner circle of top lieutenants.
