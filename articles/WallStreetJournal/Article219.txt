 Small stock focus: Nasdaq Composite Index drops 0.37%, dragged down by technology issues   By Baker, Molly, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 24, 1995  Small-capitalization stocks and the Nasdaq Stock Market continued to lag behind their larger brethren and the Dow Jones Industrial Average on Nov 22, 1995. The Russell 2000 Index gained 0.12 at 301.24, but the Nasdaq Composite Index lost 3.75 at 1021.24.
