 Business Brief -- OLSTEN CORP.: Restated Results Reflect Shift of Periods for Charges   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1999  Olsten Corp restated results for its fiscal year and first quarter. The action was taken to shift the periods in which the company accounted for a $56 million charge related to the settlement of two federal health-care investigations and $2 million of other charges.
