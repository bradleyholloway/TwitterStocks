 Romania's privatization, slow to start, whets foreign investors' appetite now   By King, Neil Jr, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 9, 1996  Bucking the skeptics, Romania has turned 1996 into a banner year for mass privatization and now shows every indication of wanting to push further still. Western analysts are growing increasingly excited by prospects that shares in some of the country's best smaller companies will soon be available to foreign buyers.
