 Two Years and 3 Fingertips Later, A New Way to Erase Is Approved   By By Cynthia Crossen, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1985  There's no such thing as a typical inventor, so don't picture Stephanie A. Vlahos, inventor, as a mad scientist surrounded by steaming, bubbling beakers.  In fact Ms. Vlahos, 30 years old, is a former airline reservations agent with no degrees in anything. But in the first week of October, Ms. Vlahos, of San Mateo, Calif., was granted Patent No. 4,543,680 for a one-piece molded plastic handle that clamps onto a chalkboard eraser. A blackboard-eraser handle.
