 Dollar Gets Boost From U.S. Jobs Data   By Alves, Fabio, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 2010  The dollar's rally picked up steam, erasing overnight losses against the euro, after the release of a better-than-expected U.S. private-sector job report in January as well as a survey showing an improvement in service-sector activity in the world's biggest economy.
