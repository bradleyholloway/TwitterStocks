 Telecommunications Brief -- Vonage Holdings Corp.: Bankers Give IPO Investors More Time to Pay for Shares   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 2006  Vonage Holdings Corp.'s underwriters are giving investors more time to pay for the initial-public-offering shares they ordered. Underwriters Smith Barney, a unit of Citigroup Inc., Deutsche Bank AG and UBS AG will give investors until June 13 to pay for the stock they ordered, according to Karina Byrne, a spokeswoman for UBS.
