 Dow Jones Buys Back Four Million Shares Of Common Stock   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 1998  Dow Jones said the initial purchase price was $192.1 million, or $48.25 a share. However the price could change at a later date depending on the future price of the company's shares. The buyback was announced after the stock market closed. Dow Jones shares finished the day at $48, up 31.25 cents on the New York Stock Exchange.
