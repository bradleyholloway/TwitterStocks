 Property -- Open House: Carroll Gardens Keeps Its Appeal in Bloom   By De Avila, Joseph, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 2011  While the influx of restaurants and bars have brought added cachet to Carroll Gardens, the area is still characterized by its brownstones and brick townhomes along the quite, residential side streets. Private schools in the area include the International School of Brooklyn, running from preschool to elementary school and the Hannah Senesh Community Day School, a primary and middle school.
