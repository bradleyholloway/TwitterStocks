 Cattle Futures Rally as Storms Hit Some Areas   By Cote, Jim, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 2005  COPPER: Futures on the Comex division of the Nymex underwent a massive meltdown on speculative liquidation fueled by recent gains in the U.S. dollar. The active March high-grade copper contract fell 13.05 cents to $1.3430 a pound while front-month January futures fell 12.85 cents to $1.395.  Authorities in the U.S. and Canada have said the second mad-cow finding wouldn't change U.S. plans to lift the 19-month ban on live Canadian cattle. But the new mad-cow case has given fresh ammunition to opponents of reopening the border.
