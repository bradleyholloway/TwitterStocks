 Takeoffs & Landings   By By Mei Fong, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 20, 2002  A bad in-flight movie can make a long flight seem even longer. InMotion Pictures, which rents DVD movies and players at 18 airports, says orders are filling up fast for the holiday season. Last Christmas season, the company ran out of players, says co-founder Barney Freedman. This year, there's an online-reservation system that may help bring a happier ending.
