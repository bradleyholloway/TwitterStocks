 The Economy: Williams Is to Sell Refinery Stake   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 2002  Struggling with petroleum-supply disruptions, Williams worked for more than a year to bring in Yukos as an equity partner and long-term petroleum supplier for the refinery. A deal was finally signed in June, shaving Williams's stake to its current 26.85%. Yukos owns a 26.85% stake and the Lithuanian government holds a 40.66% interest.
