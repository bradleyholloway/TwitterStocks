 Evergreen Media Corp.: Quarterly loss attributed to acquisition expenses   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 9, 1996  Irving TX-based Evergreen Media Corp, a radio-broadcasting concern, reported a second-quarter loss of $2.2 million, or 18 cents a share, compared with net income of $448,000, or a loss of seven cents a share after the payment of preferred dividends, a year earlier.
