 Zale Corp.: Quarterly earnings leap; Sale of unit is planned   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 1997  Zale Corp said its fiscal fourth-quarter net income rose sharply to $1.6 million, or four cents a share, from $12,000, or zero cents a share, in the year-earlier period. Separately, Zale expects to announce that it plans to sell its Diamond Park Fine Jewelers unit to Finlay Enterprises Inc.
