 In the Boroughs: In Death, They've Become Larger Than Life   By Reddy, Sumathi, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 2011  On Sunday, family and strangers alike came to grieve as a community, to leave flowers before a rock where the names of all 343 city firefighters who died that day are etched. For in death these victims have became larger than life, visible from posters and memorials, annual surfing and golf tournaments held in their names, and the streets signs that forever memorialize them.
