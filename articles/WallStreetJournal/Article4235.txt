 Tseng Labs to Report Earnings for '86 Rose   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 9, 1987  NEWTOWN, Pa. -- Tseng Laboratories Inc., benefiting from a fourth-quarter surge in sales, expects to report 1986 net income of more than $258,000, or about two cents a share, compared with 1985's $165,000, or less than one cent a share, Jack Tseng, president, said in an interview.
