 Rockwell International Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1991  Rockwell International Corp posted a 14% jump in fiscal fourth-quarter net income for the period ended Sep 30, 1991 to $152.4 million, compared with $133.9 million a year ago. The company also authorized a $500 million extension of its share-purchase program and announced a 7% common dividend increase.
