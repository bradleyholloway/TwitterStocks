 FASB modifies derivatives approach in a move to reduce profit fluctuations   By Berton, Lee, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 1996  The FASB modified a controversial approach to using derivatives as hedges that would have caused more volatility in profits. But the FASB, the chief rule-making body for accountants, still set up some roadblocks to completely discarding this approach.
