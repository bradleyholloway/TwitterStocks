 Details: A Car That's Truly Convertible -- Into a Sofa   By By Rebecca Lowell, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1999  Mr. (Noel) Grace decided to turn his pastime into a business called Authentic Designs a year ago after his designs began to be snapped up by customers at auto shows. "It seems like every time we took one, somebody would buy it on the spot," he says. Mr. Grace has competition: At least two other companies make furniture from cars: Desert Automotive, of Yuma, Ariz., and Car's the Star in Kansas City, Mo.
