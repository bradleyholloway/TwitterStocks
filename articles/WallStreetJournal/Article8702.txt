 GOP field thins as Alexander, Lugar drop out and endorse resurgent Dole   By Harwood, John, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1996  Former Gov Lamar Alexander of Tennessee and Sen Richard Lugar of Indiana on Mar 6, 1996 abandoned the GOP presidential race as a result of their own lagging showings and the overwhelming strength of front-runner Robert Dole. Both men endorsed Dole, who was attempting to rally party leaders around him.
