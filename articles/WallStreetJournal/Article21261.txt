 Corporate Report: Kawasaki Heavy Industries Ltd.   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1988  Kawasaki Heavy Industries Ltd. posted unconsolidated, or parent-company, pretax profit of 3.72 billion yen ($29.8 million) in the six months ended Sept. 30, compared with a pretax loss of 13.45 billion yen a year earlier.
