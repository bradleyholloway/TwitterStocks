Howard Is a Laker   By Herring, Chris; Cacciola, Scott, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 11, 2012  A few hours before Team USA and Argentina took the court at North Greenwich Arena for their semifinal Friday, the NBA approved a four-team blockbuster trade that sent Dwight Howard of the Orlando Magic, a six-time All-Star and the game's best big man, to a Los Angeles Lakers team that's already stocked with the league's most decorated, if relatively aged, backcourt.
