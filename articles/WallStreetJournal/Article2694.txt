 Americans grow ugly in Mexicans' eyes   By Solis, Dianne, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 1995  Although the US helped save Mexico from a financial crash by organizing a $52 billion bailout package, many Mexicans view the rescue program as a lead parachute that could plunge Mexico into a serious recession. Most viscerally, they stew about provisions that make exports by the state oil monopoly, Petroleos Mexicanos, collateral for the rescue package.
