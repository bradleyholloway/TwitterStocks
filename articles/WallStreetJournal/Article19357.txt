 Unitrin Has Concerns Regarding Northrop's Plan to Acquire Litton   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 2001  Unitrin officials declined to comment further, but people familiar with the matter said the company is concerned it could have to face a large tax bill because of Northrop's cash offer. On Dec. 22, Northrop agreed to pay $80 a share for Litton common stock outstanding, or about $3.8 billion, and assume $1.3 billion in debt.
