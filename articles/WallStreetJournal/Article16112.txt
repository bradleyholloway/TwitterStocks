 As Recession Sets In and Stocks Struggle, Brokers' Interest in Options Trading Picks Up   By By Kopin Tan, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 2001  This year, as the recession sets in and stocks struggled, the OIC responded to demand and began running seminars targeted at brokers as well. "We figured if you reach and educate a broker, we're reaching at least dozens of investors and accounts," said OIC president Paul Stevens. "And a good broker is always looking for ideas."
