 Automobile production   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 25, 1994  The ten US auto makers expect to build 267,350 cars and trucks during the week ended Feb 26, 1994, an increase of 19.5% from the 223,713 vehicles built in the year-earlier week. For the year to date, 1,917,802 vehicles were produced, versus 1,433,302 a year earlier.
