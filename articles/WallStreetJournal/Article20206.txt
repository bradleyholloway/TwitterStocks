 Fortunoff to File for Chapter 11 Before Sale   By O'Connell, Vanessa, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 2008  The company is a joint venture between real-estate developer William Mack and Lee Neibart, who are principals at private-equity concern Apollo Real Estate Advisors, and father and son Robert C.
