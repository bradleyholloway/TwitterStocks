 Corporate Report: KLM Royal Dutch Airlines   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 1989  Buoyed by higher revenue and lower fuel costs at its airline operations, KLM Royal Dutch Airlines said net income in the fiscal third quarter ended Dec. 31 rose 5.2% to 60.8 million guilders ($28.2 million) from 57.8 million guilders in the year-earlier quarter.
