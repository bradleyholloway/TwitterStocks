 Child care is worse than believed, with safety jeopardized, study suggests   By Shellenbarger, Sue, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1995  A major study involving 400 randomly chosen child-care centers in California, Colorado, Connecticut and North Carolina indicated the quality of care provided by the nation's child-care centers is even lower than previously believed, with 40% of infants and toddlers in conditions that threaten their health and safety and discourage learning.
