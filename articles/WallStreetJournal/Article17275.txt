 Corporate News: MGA Wins $309 Million In Bratz Spat With Mattel   By Zimmerman, Ann, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 2011  A U.S. district judge in California rejected Mattel Inc.'s request to set aside a jury verdict in a dispute over the Bratz line of dolls and entered a final judgment that awarded MGA Entertainment Inc. $309 million in total damages, lawyers fees and other costs.
