 Real-Estate Finance: U.K. Landlords Face New Hit; Tax Shift on Empty Property Stokes Debate on Rent Effect; Industrial Holders Suffer Most   By Carney, Beth, The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 2008  According to the King Sturge Global and Industrial Office Rents Survey of about 90 global cities, London was the most expensive city in the world in which to occupy office space at the end of 2007, with average rent in the West End costing GBP 112.50, or about $222, per square foot per year.
