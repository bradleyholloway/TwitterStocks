 Reports of Growing Crude Oil Stocks in U.S., Bearish Sentiment Push Futures to 8-Month Low   By Sullivan, Allanna, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1992  The American Petroleum Institute's report that US crude oil inventories are growing and the bearish sentiment after OPEC's latest meeting sent crude oil futures contracts tumbling on Dec 3, 1992. Crude oil for Jan 1993 delivery fell $.37 to $19.08 a barrel. Elsewhere, wheat, coffee and cotton futures rallied.
