 Corporate News: Mongolia Signs Copper Deal   By Behrmann, Elisabeth; Sparshott, Jeffrey, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 2009  Mongolia's government Tuesday signed a long-awaited investment agreement for the $4 billion Oyu Tolgoi copper-gold mine, marking a significant step forward for mining investment in the country, which has some of the world's largest untapped reserves of coal, copper and other commodities.
