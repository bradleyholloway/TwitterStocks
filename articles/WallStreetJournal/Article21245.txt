 Business Brief: Noted...   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 2, 1988  PRESS: Media General Inc., Richmond, Va., said Garden State Newspapers Inc., a newspaper group in which Media General holds a 40% interest, has acquired the York Sunday News, a family-owned Sunday newspaper in York, Pa., with a circulation of about 43,000. Terms weren't disclosed. Garden State Newspapers already owns the York Dispatch, a newspaper published Monday through Saturday with a circulation of 49,000.
