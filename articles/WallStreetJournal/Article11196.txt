 Intermetrics Net Rose 19% to $716,000 Total For Fiscal 3rd Period   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1989  CAMBRIDGE, Mass. -- Intermetrics Inc., citing improved profit margins, reported fiscal-third quarter net income of $716,000, or 20 cents a share, up 19% from $602,000 or 17 cents a share in the year-ago quarter.  The latest-quarter net includes a tax-benefit of $180,000, down from a tax-benefit of $228,000 included in earnings in the previous-year period.
