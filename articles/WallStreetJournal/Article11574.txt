 Sony Pictures Picks Mel Harris to Run Revamped TV Unit with Wide Mandate   By Landro, Laura, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1992  Sony Pictures Entertainment Corp reorganized its TV operations, which include Columbia Pictures Television, TriStar Pictures Television, Columbia TriStar Television Distribution and Merv Griffin Enterprises, into a new Television Group. Mel Harris, Paramont Pictures's former TV chief, was named to head the unit.
