 Rooney Unit Failure Causes Plummet Of Certain Stocks --- Issues Sold by Market Maker Fell as Rooney Cut Back And as Rumors Spread   By By Priscilla Ann Smith, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1987  The collapse of Rooney Pace Group Inc.'s securities operations gives a rare look at what happens to a number of stocks when one of their biggest market-makers, who also retails the issues to customers, suddenly disappears.  Indeed, some issues in which Rooney Pace was practically the only market-maker and retail promoter collapsed yesterday. VR Business Brokers Inc., a Maine-based concern brought public by Rooney Pace a year ago, tumbled 56% in over-the-counter trading to 50 cents a share bid from $1.125 Wednesday.
