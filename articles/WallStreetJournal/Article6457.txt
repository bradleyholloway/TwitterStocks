 Investor's Calendar   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 2005  Health-Plan Merger: UnitedHealth agreed to acquire PacifiCare for $8.1 billion in cash and stock. The deal would add PacifiCare's three million health-plan members to UnitedHealth's 23 million and put pressure on smaller health insurers.  Inflation Patrol: June's consumer price index comes out Thursday. After a worrying rise in inflation earlier in the year, inflation has been fairly tame in recent months, suggesting that the Federal Reserve's campaign to raise interest rates will continue awhile longer.
