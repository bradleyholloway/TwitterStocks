 Business Brief -- Solutia Inc.: Chemicals Concern Expects Earnings to Miss Estimates   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 2001  Solutia Inc. said it expects its third-quarter earnings to miss analysts' estimates due to a global slowdown in demand in the wake of the Sept. 11 terrorist attacks. The St. Louis chemicals concern projects third-quarter earnings of between six cents and nine cents a share.
