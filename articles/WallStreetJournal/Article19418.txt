 TV Commercials Get a Hollywood Touch   By By Suzanne Vranica and Alessandra Galloni, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 2001  Now directors are accepting such work simply to stay busy, says Tarsem, director of "The Cell," last year's thriller starring Jennifer Lopez. Tarsem, who goes by one name in Hollywood circles and who has made commercials for brands such as Levi Strauss's Levi's, Toyota Motor's Lexus and Miller Lite, recalls advice given to him by [Ridley Scott]: "Everybody wants to work with people that aren't available." Tarsem is currently in Europe shooting ads for mobile-phone maker Telefon AB L.M. Ericsson. "I love doing ads -- as filler I did a movie in between," he jokes.
