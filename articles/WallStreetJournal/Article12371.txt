 Emanuel Goldman Quits PaineWebber To Join Merrill Lynch   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 1998  At PaineWebber, Mr. Goldman, who is respected for his earnings accuracy and well-known for his colorful commentary, had been covering tobacco firms, including Philip Morris Cos., RJR Nabisco Holdings Corp. and UST Inc. He also analyzed beverage and bottling companies such as Coca-Cola Co., Whitman Corp., PepsiCo Inc., Coca-Cola Enterprises Inc. and Anheuser-Busch Cos.
