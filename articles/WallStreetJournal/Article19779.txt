 Investor's Calendar   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 4, 2004  Consumer Credit Due: It's a quiet week for economic reports, but Thursday's consumer-credit data for May is worth watching. Consumers now have more than $2 trillion in debt outstanding, not including loans secured by real estate. Also, chain-store sales for June are due out Thursday.  Tyco Trial: Jurors will hear closing arguments Tuesday in the trial of Mark A. Belnick, Tyco's former top lawyer. He's charged with grand larceny, securities fraud and falsifying business records, and could spend as long as 25 years in prison. He has denied wrongdoing.
