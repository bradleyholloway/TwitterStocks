 Computer Associates says profit rose 34% in 4th period; Stock price jumps   By Ziegler, Bart, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 1995  Computer Associates International Inc said its quarterly profit jumped an unexpectedly strong 34% for the fiscal fourth quarter ended Mar 31, 1995. The software maker said preliminary earnings showed it earned $213 million, up from $159 million a year earlier. Its share price soared 8.3% on the news, to $68.625, up $5.25, in trading on the NYSE on May 2. For its full fiscal year, CA said it earned $432 million, up 8% from $401 million a year earlier.
