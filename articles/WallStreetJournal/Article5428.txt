 Noodle Kidoodle Inc.: Educational-products firm narrows 3rd-quarter loss   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1996  Noodle Kidoodle Inc, Farmingdale NY, said its fiscal third-quarter loss narrowed on a sharp increase in sales due to expansion. In the third quarter, the children's educational products retailer reported a loss of $2.5 million compared with a loss of $3 million in the year-earlier quarter.
