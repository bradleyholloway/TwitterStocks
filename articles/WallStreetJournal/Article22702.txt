 Business Brief -- ZapMe! Corp.: Plan Is Set to Stop Giving Free Computers to Schools   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 2000  ZapMe Corp., San Ramon, Calif., said it plans to stop giving away free computers and Internet access to schools as it restructures its business to focus on corporate clients. ZapMe said it has been under increasing pressure from media watchdogs and other advocacy groups, who have turned away potential sponsors of its Web service.
