 Letters to the Editor: Antiwar Stand Isn't Just to `Spite Clinton'   The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 1999  In a letter to the editor, Rabbi Aryeh Spero argues that Pat Buchanan's stand against full military deployment in Kosovo is not motivated simply by a desire to negate Pres Clinton's policy as Paul Gigot implies in his Apr 30, 1999 Wall Street Journal Potomac Watch column "To Spite Clinton, GOP Smites Itself."
