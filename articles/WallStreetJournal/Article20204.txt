 Intel Ends Its Collaboration With Nonprofit Laptop Project   By Stecklow, Steve, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 2008  The OLPC machine uses a microprocessor from Intel's chief competitor, Advanced Micro Devices Inc. After more than a year of public sniping between Intel and OLPC, an Intel representative joined OLPC's board in July, and the company had been planning to announce a new, low-cost, OLPC-designed laptop based on an Intel microprocessor at next week's Consumer Electronics Show in Las Vegas.
