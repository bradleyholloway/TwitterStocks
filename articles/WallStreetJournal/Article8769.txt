 Deutsche Telekom confirms plan for IPO, sets dividend   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1996  Deutsche Telekom AG confirmed that it will sell 500 million shares in what could be a DM15 billion ($979.9 million) IPO. The state-owned telecommunications giant said it will disclose the price range Oct 21, 1996, a day before banks begin accepting orders for the shares.
