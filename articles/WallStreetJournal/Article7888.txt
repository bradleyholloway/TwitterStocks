 Kodak Study Sees Big Role In U.S. for Manufacturing   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 7, 1989  The study, "Meeting World Challenges: U.S. Manufacturing in the 1990s," was conducted by Rudiger Dornbusch and Paul Krugman of Massachusetts Institute of Technology and Yung Chul Park of Korea University, Seoul, South Korea.  "America has been squandering world economic leadership in complacent self-indulgence, blind to ever more forceful world competition," said Kodak Chairman Colby H. Chandler, in releasing the study during a briefing with members of Congress yesterday.
