 Taiwan Approves TSMC Chip Plant Planned for China   By Dean, Jason, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 2004  TSMC's planned Chinese plant is a relatively small part of its overall strategy. The Shanghai plant will employ used equipment and produce chips that are technologically several generations behind the industry's leading edge. The majority of TSMC's investment is going into Taiwan, where it has built one cutting-edge factory and is constructing another.
