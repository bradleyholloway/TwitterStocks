 ProNet Inc. Expects To Post Lower Profit For Second Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1988  RICHARDSON, Texas -- ProNet Inc. said it expects to report net income of about $100,000, or two cents a share, for the second quarter. The company estimates quarterly revenue at about $2.5 million.  Those results would compare with net in last year's quarter -- before the company went public in July 1987 -- of $113,000, or four cents a share. Revenue in that quarter was $2.2 million. The average number of shares outstanding increased to 3,939,000 in the quarter from 2,909,000 a year ago.
