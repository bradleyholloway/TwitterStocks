 Business Brief -- Leo Burnett Inc.: Ad Agency Expects Billings To Increase by 16.5% for '88   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 1988  Leo Burnett Inc., a closely held ad agency best known for its McDonald's Corp. ads, said it expects its total 1988 billings to increase by $404 million, or 16.5%, to about $2.87 billion.
