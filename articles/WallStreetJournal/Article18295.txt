 Business Brief -- CommerceBancorp: Firm Expects to Post Loss Totaling $2.9 Million for '91   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1992  CommerceBancorp said it expects to report a loss of $2.9 million for 1991, reflecting a $5.5 million increase in loan-loss reserves at its CommerceBank unit in the fourth quarter.
