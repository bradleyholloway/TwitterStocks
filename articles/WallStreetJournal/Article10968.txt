 Elsinore Transfers $10 Million to Trustee Toward Debt of Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1987  LAS VEGAS, Nev. -- Elsinore Corp. said it deposited $10 million with a trustee to help pay off overdue interest on a unit's $115 million of 15 1/2% senior mortgage bonds.  In addition, the company said its 91.5%-owned subsidiary, Elsinore Shore Associates, filed a plan of reorganization under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code in New Jersey bankruptcy court. The unit, which owns the Atlantis Casino Hotel in Atlantic City, N.J., had previously filed a petition for Chapter 11 protection against creditors in November 1985.
