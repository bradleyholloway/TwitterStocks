 National City Corp.: Reported Earnings Are Cut Amid Drop in Assets' Value   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 2006  National City Corp. cut its third-quarter earnings to 86 cents a share from a previously reported 90 cents a share, to reflect a bigger-than-expected decline in the value of its mortgage-servicing assets and the need to set aside more money for future mortgage buybacks.
