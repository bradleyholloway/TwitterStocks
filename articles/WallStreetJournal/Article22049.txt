 Two former Honda officials convicted of accepting bribes from auto dealers   By Henderson, Angelo B, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 2, 1995  A federal-court jury in New Hampshire has convicted two former Honda Motor Co executives--Dennis Josleyn and John W. Billmyer--of taking bribes from car dealers in return for extra supplies of hot-selling Honda and Acura models and helping them obtain new dealerships.
