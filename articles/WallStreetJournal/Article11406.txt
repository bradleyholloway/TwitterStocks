 International Brief -- Rothmans International PLC: Rothmans Bids for 61.3% Of Irish Tobacco Concern   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 1990  Rothmans International PLC, a British tobacco products maker, launched a cash bid for the 61.3% of Irish tobacco concern P.J. Carroll & Co. it doesn't already own.  Rothmans is offering ordinary shareholders 160 Irish pence ($1.84) a share in a bid that values Carroll at 119 million Irish pounds ($207 million).
