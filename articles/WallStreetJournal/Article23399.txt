 Tip of the Week: Cell Plans Have an Out   By Little, Lyneka, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 2006  Note that if you got a phone with the new service, you'll be expected to return it in like-new condition. You could face a restocking fee. Some providers may not refund the activation fee you paid, if any. And you'll be charged for the period of time that you did have the service, however brief. Ask your provider or check the contract for details.
