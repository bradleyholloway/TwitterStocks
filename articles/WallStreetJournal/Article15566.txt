 The Jakarta juggernaut keeps 'em coming   By Solomon, Jay, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 1997  Defying the campaign riots and political uncertainty of recent months, Indonesia's economy has chugged full-stream ahead because the country offers one of the most promising emerging-market plays in the world, analysts say. Even after the recent turmoil, investors apparently don't believe 31 years of Suharto-led stability is unraveling.
