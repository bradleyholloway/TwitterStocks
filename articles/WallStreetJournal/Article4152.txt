 House Panel Clears Bill to Boost Exports Of Some U.S. Goods   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 1986  The bill, drafted by Rep. Robert Matsui (D., Calif.) and approved by a vote of 33-2, would require the Reagan administration to seek formal negotiations aimed at reducing barriers to U.S. telecommunications products in the 10 largest potential markets. The measure then would block imports of telecommunications equipment from the countries that refuse to allow imports of more U.S.-made goods.
