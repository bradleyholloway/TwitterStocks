 WEEKEND JOURNAL; The Home Front: House of the Week / Bali High Life   By Christina S.N. Lewis, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 2007  Due Diligence: Owned by Irma Lengkong, an Indonesian entrepreneur, this property consists of two adjacent pavilion-style buildings with folding-glass doors. The residence, in upscale Seminyak on the west coast of southern Bali, is around the corner from Ku De Ta, a popular beachfront restaurant. Homeowners can also arrange to use services from the Legian Beach Hotel, a nearby resort.
