 Developing nations exert power, blocking IMF aid plan for Russia   By Wessel, David, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1994  Developing countries, led by India and Brazil, flexed their muscles for perhaps the first time in the 50-year history of the postwar international financial institutions, and stunned others at the annual meeting of the IMF by blocking an IMF aid plan for Russia.
