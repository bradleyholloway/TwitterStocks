 Scandal that snared Chinese officials undermines Communist Party's image   By Brauchli, Marcus W, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1996  The announcement during the week that China will prosecute 18 former Beijing officials for allegedly embezzling a total of more than $2.2 billion shows how deep and corrosive corruption can be. If accurate, the enormity of the case would mark the worst scandal to hit China's Communist Party-led goverment.
