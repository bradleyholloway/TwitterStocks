 Zapata Corp.: Offer for Envirodyne shares as slate isn't elected   The Wall Street Journal,  May 23, 1997  Zapata Corp said its $8-a-share offer to acquire the shares of Envirodyne Industries Inc terminated because its slate of directors was not elected at Envirodyne's annual meeting in mid-May 1997. Zapata holds 40.3% of Envirodyne's 14.5 million shares outstanding.
