 International Finance: UBS Taps 2 to Expand Operations in Asia   By Gopalan, Nisha, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 2010  Last month, UBS's Asia head of global capital markets, Steven Barg, quit to join Goldman Sachs Group Inc. as its co-head of equity capital markets for Asia excluding Japan, days after Mr. Barg's subordinate, Mark Williams, moved to Japanese bank Nomura Holdings Inc. Mr. Williams was UBS's Asia head of equity capital markets.
