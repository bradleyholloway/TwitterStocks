 Armel Inc. Reaches Acquisition Agreement Valued at $7.75 a Share   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 1987  In American Stock Exchange composite trading Friday, Armel closed at $7, down $1. Arthur Kwiat, Armel president, said the company is being sold for $7.75 a share because "that's the price that's been agreed to." He said that while the stock had closed higher the day before, "I think that was speculation." Indeed, 104,700 shares were traded Thursday, unusually heavy volume for the stock. Armel has about 3.2 million common shares outstanding, giving the proposed transaction an indicated value of about $24.8 million.
