 Borders Tries About-Face on Shelves; Counting on Covers to Sell, Bookseller Changes Display While Cutting Titles Stocked   By Trachtenberg, Jeffrey A, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 2008  There won't be a new Harry Potter title to lure people into stores, and the Olympic Games and presidential elections may also damp interest in reading.
