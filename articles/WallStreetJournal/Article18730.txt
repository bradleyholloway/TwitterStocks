 Coram gets a waiver for 30 days on paying $10 million on loan   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1995  Cash-strapped Coram Healthcare Corp said that it had received a 30-day waiver from creditors on a $10 million loan payment that had been due on Sep 30, 1995. The provider of home health-care services had sought the postponement to gain time to restructure more than $200 million in long-term debt.
