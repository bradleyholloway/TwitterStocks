 Delta Air's May Traffic Rose   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 1992  Delta Air Lines said its May 1992 traffic rose 13.7% from a year earlier to 6.4 billion revenue passenger miles from 5.6 billion a year earlier. Load factor fell to 56.8% from 60.7%. For the fiscal year ended May 31, traffic rose 15%.
