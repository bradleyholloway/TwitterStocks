 Falconbridge Sells 49% Stake in Firm In South Africa   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 1987  Falconbridge Ltd. said it sold its 49% stake in Western Platinum Ltd. of South Africa, citing, in part, political instability in that country.  Falconbridge, a big nickel and copper producer, said it received $65 million for its interest in Western Platinum, a producer of platinum, gold, and base metals that is 51%-owned by Britain's Lonrho PLC. A further $10 million is due Oct. 1, for a total sale price of $75 million. The buyer wasn't identified.
