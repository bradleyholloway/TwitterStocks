 UPS to Report Gain From Sale of Stakes In 2 Start-Up Firms   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 2000  The package-delivery company said the gain was triggered by last month's acquisitions of Tradex Technologies, an Atlanta-based developer of electronic-commerce software, and iShip.com, an online-shipping concern in Bellevue, Wash. Tradex was purchased by Ariba Inc., Mountain View, Calif., for almost $2 billion, while Stamps.com Inc., of Santa Monica, Calif., bought iShip for about $238 million.
