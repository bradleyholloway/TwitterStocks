 Legal Beat: Drexel's Former Chief   By Lambert, Wade; Hagedorn, Ann, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 1991  According to documents filed in federal bankruptcy court in Manhattan NY, former CEO and current board member Frederick Joseph said in early Jan 1991 that after leaving Drexel he could end up working as a consultant to the FDIC. A spokesman for the FDIC said he was unaware of any proposed consulting arrangement.
