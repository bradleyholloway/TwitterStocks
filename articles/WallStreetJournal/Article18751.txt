 Compromise sought on curbs for Internet   By Pearl, Daniel, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1995  On-line computer services and some congressional Republicans are scrambling for a compromise in a final telecommunications bill to ban on-line smut on the Internet. But House members remain divided over whether the on-line services and bulletin-board operators should be held responsible for keeping kids away from raunchy material on parts of the Internet that the services do not control. Religious rights group say they should, but on-line services say it is impossible.
