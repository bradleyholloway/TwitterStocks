 Somalia and the spoils of power   By Tuttle, Alexandra, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 1993  Alexandra Tuttle reviews the complicated and brutal politics of Somalia that have led to the country's current crisis. She says that the myriad factions have no political agendas, except the causes of their leaders, which are always just the spoils of power. She notes that, in Somalia, politics is merely a means of survival.
