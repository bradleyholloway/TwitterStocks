 Provigo Inc. Names Nadeau and Bussieres to Fill Top Positions   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1990  Provigo Inc named Bertin Nadeau, who controls 51% of Provigo's stock, chairman. He will share the post of CEO with Yvan Bussieres, who was also named president. They replace Pierre Lortie who left when the grocery firm decided to sell nonfood operations.
