 Abreast of the market: As stock market surges ahead, 'predictable' profits are driving it   By McGee, Suzanne, The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 1997  Even as corporate operating profits soared in the 1997 first quarter, investors are not snapping up those stocks with the biggest positive earnings surprises. Instead, investors are flocking to companies they believe will continue to deliver predictable, if unspectacular, earnings growth. On May 2, 1997, blue-chip stocks surged. The Dow Jones Industrial Average just missed a record, closing up 94.72 at 7071.20.
