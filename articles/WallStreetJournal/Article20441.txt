 As Goes January, So Goes the Year? --- Good or Bad, Stock Performance Can Set Tone for Months That Follow; Refilling Retirement Account   By Browning, E S, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 2010  In years when the Dow has fallen, the median rise for the next 11 months is just 0.28%. Because of the inflows of new cash, January has seen stock advances 62% of the time since 1900, well above the average of 57% for all months.
