 Legal Beat: 'Slip-and-Fall'   By Woo, Junda, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 1992  In a case that will now make it harder for "slip-and-fall" plaintiffs to win in Texas, the Texas Supreme Court ruled that a plaintiff and jury could not assume that grocery-store operator Kroger Co knew of a dangerous condition simply because store employees had created it.
