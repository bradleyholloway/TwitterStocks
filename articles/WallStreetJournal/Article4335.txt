 First British Takeover Bid Since Collapse Is Launched   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 1987  Granada said its offer values Electronic Rentals at #224 million ($399.7 million), or 77 pence ($1.37) a share. Granada is offering nine new 7.5% convertible preference shares plus 585 pence in cash for every 20 ordinary shares in Electronic Rentals. Granada's ordinary shares dropped 29 pence, to 241 pence, yesterday on London's stock exchange, while Electronic Rentals' shares rose nine pence, to 64 pence.
