 Armco Debt Ratings Studied by Moody's For Possible Reduction   The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 1985  Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it is considering downgrading the debt ratings of Armco Inc., and lowered its ratings on General Instrument Corp.  Moody's said it is reviewing Armco's ratings because although the Middletown, Ohio-based concern intends to sell its aerospace and strategic materials business to raise cash to reduce debt, the timing and amount of the sales are uncertain.
