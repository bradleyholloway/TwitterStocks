 REVIEW --- Icons -- Artist Focus: Walid Raad: A Prize Born in Violence   By Marcus, J S, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 2011  The show, which opens next weekend, will include pieces that possess a mournful surrealism, like "We decided to let them say 'we are convinced,' twice," which shows a black-and-white smoke-filled skyline; the photo, shot by the artist during the 1982 Israeli invasion of Lebanon and rediscovered some 20 years later, is both damaged and decorated by turquoise-blue stains.
