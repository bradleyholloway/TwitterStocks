 Brokerage Firms, Like Individuals, Can Get Stuck in Partnership Deals   By Slater, Karen, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 1991  Florida brokerage firm Raymond James & Associates Inc, one of several companies that buy partnership units from investors and resell them, has received bids on some entities that were a mere fraction of their original value. The low prices at which some real estate LPs are changing hands reflect both changes in tax law and the depressed real estate market.
