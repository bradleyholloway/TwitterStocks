 China is leaning toward big order with Airbus   By Kahn, Joseph; Cole, Jeff, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 1996  In an apparent slap at US aircraft makers, China is leaning toward ordering about 25 of Airbus Industrie's A-320 planes during a visit to France the week of Apr 7, 1996 by Premier Li Peng, government and industry officials say. The Chinese order would be valued at $1.2 billion or more.
