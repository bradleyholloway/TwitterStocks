 Trading Stays Slow In Swiss Franc Sector Of Eurobond Market --- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 1987  Investors' perception that the dollar won't fall much further continued to damp trading in Swiss franc foreign bonds.  In addition, the stability of the Swiss franc and low Swiss interest rates, combined with the market's low liquidity, have kept foreign investors waiting before they enter the market or make any large moves, syndicate managers and traders said.
