 Associated Press Picks Jonathan P. Wolman As Executive Editor   The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 2000  The Associated Press named Jonathan P. Wolman as executive editor of the news-gathering cooperative, succeeding William E. Ahearn, who had held the post since 1988.  An AP spokesman declined to comment on the switch or whether Mr. Ahearn would stay with the company. Mr. Ahearn didn't return calls seeking comment.
