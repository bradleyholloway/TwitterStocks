 Chevron Corp.: Talks With Canada Are Halted Over Offshore Oil Development   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 2006  Chevron Corp. suspended negotiations with the government of Newfoundland and Labrador over the Hebron oil field, dimming prospects for further development of Eastern Canada's offshore oil resources. The San Ramon, Calif., company said "significant and fundamental gaps" on terms were preventing a deal.
