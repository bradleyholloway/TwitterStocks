 Outboard Marine Outlook   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 1989  WAUKEGAN, Ill. -- Outboard Marine Corp. said that, due to a slowdown in marine product and lawnmower sales, earnings for the third quarter ended June 30 will be at least 50% below the year-ago $28.3 million, or $1.48 a share.
