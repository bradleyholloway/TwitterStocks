 Moral Decay   By Van Alstyne, Debra M, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 1996  Debra M. Van Alstyne responds to the Aug 1, 1996 page-one article about Briggs & Stratton Corp and its libel lawsuit. Van Alstyne asks if there can be a more telling example of what is wrong in America than managers refusing to let moral considerations have any effect upon their business decisions.
