 Clorox Co. Recalls Cleaning Product Linked to a Death   By By Lee Gomes, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 1997  Clorox Co. said it recalled and halted production of QuickSilver, an automobile wheel-cleaning product that was implicated in the death of a child in Canada last month.  QuickSilver contains ammonium bifluoride and ammonium fluoride, both poisonous in small amounts. Clorox said production of the cleaner will be discontinued immediately in its present form and will eventually be replaced with a lesstoxic alternative.
