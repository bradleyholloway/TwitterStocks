 Japan Housing Starts Fall 2.3%   The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 1992  Japan's housing starts fell 2.3% in Mar 1992 from a year earlier to 109,554 units, marking the 17th consecutive month of year-on-year declines, but also showing significant improvement from preceding months. For fiscal 1991, which ended Mar 31, 1992, housing starts declined 19% to 1.343 million units.
