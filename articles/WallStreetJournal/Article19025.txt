 Vermont Teacher Has Tough Task: Act 60 101   By By Jeffrey Krasner, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1998  Still troubled by the complexities of Vermont's Act 60, the education-reform bill that seeks to address inequities in school funding by establishing a statewide income tax? You might want to sign up for Norman Messier's next course.  Mr. Messier, a retired school superintendent from Lyndonville, teaches a graduate course on school finance in the Education Department of Johnson State College. Act 60 dominates this semester's course, taking up two of 14 lectures and part of each week's discussion.
