 Dumping on the World Market   By Hawkins, William R, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 3, 1992  William R. Hawkins responds to the Jul 13, 1992 article "Summer Trade Follies," written by James Bovard, and claims that the author does not realize that "dumping" harms the working of the market. Hawkins argues that a firm that is truly efficient should be able to be make a profit on its own.
