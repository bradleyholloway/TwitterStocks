 Business Brief -- Howtek Inc.: First-Period Profit Estimate Cut on Weakness in Europe   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 9, 1993  Howtek Inc. estimated first-quarter 1993 earnings per share of 11 cents and revenue of about $6 million, down from earlier expectations, compared with a year-ago loss of four cents a share on revenue of $3.5 million.
