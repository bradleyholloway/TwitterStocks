 How to Land the Best Deals on Airline Tickets   By McCartney, Scott, The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 2009  Lots of people like Kayak.com because it searches not only online travel agencies but also airline Web sites themselves, and then offers a one-click link to whichever Web site had the price most appealing to you. With this tool, every time you search for airline tickets, either at an online travel agency or an airline itself, a SideStep box opens on your screen and asks if you want SideStep to conduct the same search.
