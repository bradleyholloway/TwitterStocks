 Business Technology: TI Trims Sales, Profit Outlook As Retailers Delay Stocking Up   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 2007  The company, which makes chips used in phones, telecommunications equipment, and calculators, is looking to snap two straight quarters of revenue declines in this current business period. It had been suffering since last fall from weaker demand for its higher-margin chips used in multifunctional phones, but has seen a resumption of growth in high-end phones. It has also been shipping more chips for basic phones sold in China, India and Brazil.
