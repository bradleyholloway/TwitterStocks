 Cargill Inc.: South Africa's Afgri to Sell Cotton-Ginning Operations   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 2006  Cargill Inc. agreed conditionally to buy South Africa's Afgri Ltd.'s cotton-ginning business for $42.7 million. The closely held Wayzata, Minn., agricultural company plans to acquire Afgri's Clark Cotton business and stakes in eight gins, which produce about 150,000 bales of cotton annually.
