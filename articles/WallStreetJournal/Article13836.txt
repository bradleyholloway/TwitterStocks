 City News -- Urban Gardner: All in the Four-Eye Family   By Gardner, Ralph, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2011  In more recent years, my business has gone to Loft Opticians on Lexington Avenue in the 70s. Besides superior service and affordable prices, they have a candy jar filled with Tootsie Pops, and they don't mind if you stop by on your way to the subway and swipe one, even if you're not planning to invest in a new pair of glasses any time soon.
