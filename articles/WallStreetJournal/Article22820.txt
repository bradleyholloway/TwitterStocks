 America West Talks With Lessors About Its Default on Plane Leases   By By Melanie Trottman, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 2, 2001  Also yesterday, ratings concern Standard & Poor's cut its credit rating for the Phoenix company and its America West Airlines unit to triple-C-minus from triple- C, citing the company's third-quarter loss and "minimal financial flexibility." America West's rating could be downgraded further, S&P said, noting that the carrier may have to file for bankruptcy if it doesn't get added financing and restructure its obligations.
