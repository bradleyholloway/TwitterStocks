Nominee Holbrooke To Pay Fine Over Conflict   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 1999  President Clinton's choice for United Nations ambassador, Richard Holbrooke, agreed to pay $5,000 to settle allegations that he violated conflict-of-interest laws. The move clears the way for Mr. Clinton to submit Mr. Holbrooke's name to the Senate for a confirmation hearing.
