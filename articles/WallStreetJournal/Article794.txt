 Argentina Plans to Swap Part of Its Debt for Equity   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 1986  Argentina's government announced in Buenos Aires a plan to exchange part of its $50 billion of debt for equity in development projects.  The government also said it plans to grant immunity from tax-evasion charges to those who bring back to Argentina capital now hidden in foreign banks. Finance Secretary Mario Brodersohn also disclosed a plan to crack down on the country's hundreds of thousands of tax evaders.
