 Ex-IDB Top Officials Indicted on Charges Of Fraud, Conspiracy   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1997  Former IDB Chief Executive Jeffrey Sudikoff, who also was part owner of the Los Angeles Kings hockey team, was indicted along with Edward Cheramy, former president of the telecommunications company. IDB, which was sold in 1995 to a company that eventually became WorldCom Inc., wasn't named as a defendant in the indictment.
