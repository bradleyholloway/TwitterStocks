 Prices Drop For Stocks And Bonds --- Investors Guessing About Fed's Stance On Interest Rates   By By Douglas R. Sease, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 1989  The Dow Jones Industrial Average edged down 4.91 points to 2736.77 in light trading. Long-term bond prices fell modestly, with a portion of the losses coming after the release of the Fed's "beige book," a summary of economic activity across the country. The dollar was mixed, as it posted tiny gains against the yen.
