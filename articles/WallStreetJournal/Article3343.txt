 Agilent Sounds Alert Of Weak Earnings In Fiscal 3rd Period   By By a Wall Street Journal, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 2000  Robert Walker, Agilent's chief financial officer, said the parts shortage was simply caused by heavy demand for certain semiconductors it needs for microwave and radio-frequency test equipment used in wireless communications.
