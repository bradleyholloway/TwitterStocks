 International Corporate Report: Overseas Business Notes... [1]  The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1990  VOLKSWAGEN AG of Germany and a Chinese factory signed a contract to establish one of China's largest automotive joint ventures. Construction will start next year on an $800 million auto plant in the northeastern city of Changchun, with the capacity to build 150,000 Golf sedans each year, the Xinhua News Agency reported. VW already operates a joint venture in Shanghai that produces Santana sedans.
