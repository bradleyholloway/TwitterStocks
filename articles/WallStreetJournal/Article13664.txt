 Corporate News: Five Chrysler Directors Are Named --- New Appointees Fill Out Nine-Member Board Ahead of Meeting in Late July   By Linebaugh, Kate, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 2009  George F.J. Gosbee, chairman and president of Tristone Capital Inc.; Douglas Steenland, former chief executive of Northwest Airlines; Scott Stuart, a founding partner of Sageview Capital LLC; Ronald L.
