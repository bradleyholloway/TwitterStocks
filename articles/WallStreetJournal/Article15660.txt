 Business Brief -- NEXTEL COMMUNICATIONS INC.: Period Net Loss Widens Amid Outlays for Network   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1998  Nextel Communications Inc said in Mar 1998 that its fourth-quarter loss widened sharply to $825.2 million, compared to a loss of $158.4 million a year earlier, due to large one-time charges and costs associated with building its digital wireless network in the US and overseas.
