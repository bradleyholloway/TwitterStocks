 Sales of New Homes Hit Record With Rise Of 7.6% in November   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 1999  Sales of newly built homes surged 7.6% in November, another sign of a booming housing market.  The Commerce Department said single-family home sales hit a record 965,000 at an annual rate, compared with a revised 897,000 units in October. November marked the 36th consecutive month that sales have exceeded an annual rate of 700,000.
