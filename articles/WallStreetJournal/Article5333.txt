 Sports & Recreation names Bebis chairman and chief executive   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 9, 1996  Sports & Recreation Inc, a sporting-goods retailer, named Stephen Bebis, a former executive with Home Depot Inc, as chairman and CEO. The company, which is struggling with weak sales, declined to say whether Jim W. Bradke, whom Bebis succeeds, stepped aside voluntarily or was asked to leave.
