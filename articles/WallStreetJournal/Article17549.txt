 Norlin Proposes Changes to Avoid Bankruptcy Filing   By By Johnnie L. Roberts, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1985  The financial-printing company is at the "brink of financial undoing," Mr. Rooney conceded. Norlin recently announced that it was in default on payments due on $20 million of debentures after reporting a string of losses and a generally deteriorating financial condition. But Mr. Rooney said the company hopes its plan will help it avoid filing for protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code.
