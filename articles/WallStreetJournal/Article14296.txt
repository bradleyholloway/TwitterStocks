 Tokyo Shares Fall; Stocks Rise Slightly In London Dealings --- A Wall Street Journal News Roundup   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 1986  Tokyo share prices declined for the third consecutive session yesterday, as institutions took to the sidelines to await the outcome of the U.S. elections.  In Tokyo, the Nikkei stock average declined 49.69 points from Saturday's half-day session, to 16786.90, and the stock exchange index fell 4.65 points, to 1396.94. Volume was a light 230 million shares, unchanged from Saturday's half day and down from 500 million shares Friday.
