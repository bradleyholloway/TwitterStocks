 Slow Back-to-School Sales Signal Weak Holiday Season Ahead   By Saranow, Jennifer, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 2008  Pinched consumers continued to focus on buying necessities and other items at low prices, giving a boost to discounters Wal-Mart Stores Inc., the world's largest retailer, and BJ's Wholesale Club Inc. But high-end Saks Inc. and Nordstrom Inc. posted worse-than-expected sales declines at stores open at least a year.
