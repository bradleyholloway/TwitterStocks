 Airlines' Traffic Increased in October on the Strength of International Gains   By O Brian, Bridget, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1991  The Oct 1991 traffic was up for many airlines in the US, as compared to the previous year, but domestic growth is still down. American Airlines posted a 7.5% increase and Delta Air Lines experienced a 16% increase in traffic. NWA Inc and Continental Airlines reported increases of 12% and 6.3%, respectively, while Pan Am Corp reported a 29% decline in traffic.
