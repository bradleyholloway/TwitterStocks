 Property: Proposal Nears Vote   By Kusisto, Laura, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 2012  Brooklyn Heights features a hodgepodge of architectural styles, ranging from federalist buildings from the early 19th century to an ultramodern building at 322 Hicks St. The Brooklyn Heights Historic District was the first in the city when it was created in 1965, and since then the community has frequently embraced modern architecture in context with the historic district.
