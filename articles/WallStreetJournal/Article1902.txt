 Mitsubishi Motors Applies to Be Listed On Japan Exchanges   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1988  Mitsubishi Motors Corp., Japan's No. 3 auto maker, has applied to list its shares on the Tokyo, Nagoya and Osaka exchanges.  Currently, Mitsubishi Motors shares aren't traded publicly. Mitsubishi Heavy Industries Ltd. and its subsidiaries hold a 37% share, Chrysler and its subsidiaries hold 24% and more than 100 other Japanese companies own the remainder of the 560 million shares.
