 Business Brief -- CKE RESTAURANTS INC.: Profit More Than Doubles, But Stock Still Drops 14%   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1998  CKE Restaurants Inc reported fiscal second-quarter net income of $22.6 million, or 46 cents a diluted share, more than double the year-earlier results of $10.5 million, or 25 cents a share, and ahead of analysts' estimates of 43 cents.
