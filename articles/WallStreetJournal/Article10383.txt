 MARKETWATCH: Newlyweds, Don't Forget Your Taxes   By Hoak, Amy, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 2010  Getting married is one of those life-changing events that affects many aspects of a person's life -- right down to his or her tax returns. Generally, for "the vast majority of couples out there, it doesn't make sense to file married filing separately," says Bob Meighan, a certified public accountant and vice president at Intuit's TurboTax.
