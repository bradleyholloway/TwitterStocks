 Ad Notes...   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 7, 2006  BRIEF: Safeway has put its $250 million advertising account up for review. The business includes broadcast creative, strategic planning, broadcast media planning and buying, and promotional marketing, the supermarket chain said. Interpublic Group's Dailey & Associates, which had been Safeway's agency of record, won't take part in the review, Safeway said.
