 ARENA --- Music: The Voice of 'My Generation' --- Pete Townshend's new book on his life, the Who and his crush on Jagger   By Jurgensen, John, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 2012  Mr. Townshend's quest to inject deeper meaning into rock 'n' roll would lead him to smash countless guitars in concert, and write rock operas such as "Tommy" and "Quadrophenia." The son of two swing musicians, Mr. Townshend, 67, documents the history of the Who and his solo career in a new autobiography, "Who I Am."
