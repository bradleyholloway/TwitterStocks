 Coach USA Inc.: Acquisitions are arranged of four tour, bus firms   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1997  Coach USA Inc said it agreed to acquire four closely held tour and bus concerns for $58.5 million in cash and stock. The Houston bus and tour concern said it agreed to acquire Antelope Valley Bus Inc, Lancaster CA, International Express Corp, Minneapolis, Kerrville Bus Co, Kerrville TX and Red & Tan Enterprises Inc, Bergenfield NJ.
