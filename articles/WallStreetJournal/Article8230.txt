 Labor Letter: Managed-Care   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1992  A survey of 2,409 employers by consultant Foster Higgins found that nearly 75% offered some type of managed-care plan in 1991, but 55% of all workers with health coverage still were enrolled in traditional indemnity plans and 45% were covered by HMOs and other plans that limit the choice of providers.
