 Big 3 auto makers press for changes in air-bag rules   By Nomani, Asra Q, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1996  The Big Three auto makers sought changes in federal air-bag rules but at the same time moved to put more of the responsibility for passenger safety on the consumer. The auto makers said they will send warning letters to the estimated 22 million people who own cars with passenger-side air bags.
