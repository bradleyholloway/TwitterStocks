 Ford to Put Computers Made by H-P in Homes Of All Its Employees   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 2000  Ford Chairman William Clay Ford Jr. and Chief Executive Jacques Nasser are expected to announce today that the No. 2 auto maker will put Hewlett-Packard Co. computers with Internet hookups in the homes of all its employees, Ward's Automotive Reports said yesterday in its online edition.
