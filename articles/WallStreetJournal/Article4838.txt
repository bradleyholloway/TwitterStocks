 Business Brief -- Dillard Department Stores Inc.: Christmas Sales Climbed 7% At Arkansas Retail Chain   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 1992  Apparently responding to its stock price falling amid rumors of a blue Christmas, Dillard Department Stores Inc. said sales for the nine weeks ended Jan. 4 rose 7% to $1.07 billion from $1 billion.
