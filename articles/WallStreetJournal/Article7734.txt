 International Corporate Report: ADT Posts 87% Increase In Pretax Profit for 1987   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1988  ADT LTD., the Bermuda-based, London-listed security and building-services company, reported an 87% rise in 1987 pretax profit, to $155.4 million from $83.2 million a year earlier.
