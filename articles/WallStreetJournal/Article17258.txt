 America's Troubling Investment Gap   By Malpass, David; Moore, Stephen, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 2011  To be sure, foreigners still park a huge amount of money in this country, but in the last several years they've shifted their investment toward U.S. Treasury securities and government-guaranteed bonds, and away from the private-sector staples -- corporate bonds, intellectual property, ownership of businesses -- that create sustainable jobs. Since 2009, foreigners have invested just over $1 trillion in U.S. Treasury bonds, according to the Bureau of Economic Analysis.
