 Business Brief -- Ampco-Pittsburgh Corp.: Firm Boosts Stake to 9.6% In Midway Airlines Common   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 7, 1989  In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, Ampco disclosed that from Aug. 10 to Sept. 5 it bought 297,400 Midway common shares at $19 to $21 each. The purchases bring the company's total holdings in Midway to 960,000 shares.
