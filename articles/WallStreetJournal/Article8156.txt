 Campbell Soup's Scales Resigns as President of Vlasic Foods Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 1991  Campbell Soup Co said John R. Scales, president of the firm's Vlasic Foods Inc unit, resigned. Charles V. McCarthy and Raymond A. Meillier, were named as interim co-presidents. The two will lead a strategic review of Vlasic and aid in the search for a permanent replacement in the post.
