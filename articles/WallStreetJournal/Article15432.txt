 Mobil must revise papers for Ampolex on takeover offer   By Witcher, S Karene, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 1996  A federal court in Australia ruled that Mobil Corp will have to redraft its takeover-offer documents for Ampolex Ltd, which means it will be at least two weeks before the US company can proceed with its 1.64 billion Australian dollar ($1.28 billion) cash offer for Ampolex.
