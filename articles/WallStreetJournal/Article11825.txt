 Campo sales increased in March   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 1994  Campo Electronics, Appliances & Computers Inc reported its sales at stores open more than a year rose 26% in Mar 1994 to $7.3 million from $5.8 million a year earlier. The company's total sales for the month nearly doubled to $15.1 million from $7.9 million.
