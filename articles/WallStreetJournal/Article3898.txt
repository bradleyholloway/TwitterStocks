 Siemens to Pay About $1 Billion To Buy CTI Molecular Imaging   By Matthew Karnitschnig and Rachel Graham, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 2005  Siemens, which is No. 2 behind GE in medical equipment, has had a joint venture for PET scanners with CTI since 1987. Under Friday's acquisition, Siemens will buy CTI's interest in the venture along with its other businesses.  Michael Reitermann, the head of Siemens's nuclear-medicine division, said CTI would complement Siemens's other medical-equipment divisions and offered the best prospects for future growth.
