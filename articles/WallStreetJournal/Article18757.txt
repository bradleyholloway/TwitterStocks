 Rural county balks at joining Global Village   By Bleakley, Fred R, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1996  Hancock County TN, population 6,700, is finding out that bootstrapping rural America into the Global Village involves far more than a simple job of hard-wiring. Technology is bound up with change in the minds of folks there, and change has never come easy to the hollows of rural Tennessee. Lagging computer literacy and the absence of a telecommunications infrastructure are making it hard to attract the businesses needed to revitalize the area.
