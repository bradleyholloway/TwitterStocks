 Small stock focus: Shares regain some lost ground, stirred by earnings   By Arvedlund, Erin, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1995  Small-capitalization stocks pieced together a rebound on Jul 20, 1995 as positive earnings reports in the technology arena renewed buying interest in the sector. The Russell 2000 Index gained 2.35 to 289.42, while the Nadsaq Composite Index advanced 7.70 to 960.57.
