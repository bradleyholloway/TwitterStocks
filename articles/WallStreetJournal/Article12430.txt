 Penney to Launch Free Teen Magazine   By By Emily Nelson, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 1999  J.C. Penney Co. plans to launch a free quarterly magazine for teenage boys and girls in the hope that articles and fashion spreads featuring items it sells will draw customers to its department stores.  The magazine, tentatively titled "Noise," will be published by Redwood CMP, a Toronto-based joint venture between Redwood Publishing Ltd., a London-based unit of Omnicom Group Inc., and WSP Marketing, a Toronto promotion agency.
