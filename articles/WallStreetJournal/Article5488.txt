 General Electric Co.: Capital Information unit to buy an IBM subsidiary   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1997  GE Capital Information Technology Solutions, Stamford CT, said it agreed to acquire an IBM Corp subsidiary in Sao Paulo Brazil for undisclosed terms, The unit, IVIX Sistemas Abertos de Informatica, is a reseller and integrator of IBM RS/6000 Unix operating systems.
