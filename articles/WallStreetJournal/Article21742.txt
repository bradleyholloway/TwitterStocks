 Royal Trustco to Offer Equity, Cuts Its Dividend   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1992  Royal Trustco Ltd said it plans to raise 200 million Canadian dollars ($161.2 million). Royal Trustco also reported a deep third-period loss of C$243 million because of about C$255 million in general and specific loan provisions. Royal Trustco said it will pay a quarterly dividend of C$.05 ($.04) a share, reduced from C$.10, to be paid Nov 23, 1992 to holders of record Nov. 12.
