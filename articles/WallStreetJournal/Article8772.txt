 Oppenheimer seeks review by Big Board of Chateau merger bid   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1996  Oppenheimer & Co has asked the NYSE to review the merger agreement between Chateau Properties Inc and ROC Communities Inc, both REITs, complaining that the renegotiated agreement may violate NYSE listing requirements. Oppenheimer owns 6.97% of Chateau's stock and says the new agreement nullifies the voting power of existing shareholders.
