 World Airways posts fourth-quarter profit   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 1997  World Airways Inc, citing strong demand for its leasing service as well as a "favorable pricing environment," reported solidly improved fourth-quarter results, with net income of $4.8 million, or 43 cents a share, up from a net loss of $1.6 million, or 13 cents a share, a year earlier.
