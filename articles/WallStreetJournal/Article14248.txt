 IRS Retains Tax Refunds To Recover Overdue Debt   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 1986  The government has recovered $135 million in overdue debt by withholding the tax refunds of delinquent borrowers, according to the Office of Management and Budget.  The funds were collected during the first four months of a two-year pilot program begun in January. James Miller, director of the budget office, predicted that an additional $13 million will be collected by the end of the year.
