 FRIDAY JOURNAL --- Sports: It's Time for the Ivies to Embrace Sports --- Jeremy Lin's Success Energizes Harvard, but the Ivy League's Athletic Prejudice Endures   By Bachman, Rachel, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 2012  Since Lin cracked the starting lineup Monday, Harvard has seen a surge in media requests for basketball coach Tommy Amaker and a flood of traffic to clips of Lin on the school's YouTube page. Martin Kessler, co-chair of the Harvard Crimson's sports section, said three of the most-read stories on the paper's web site this week have been about Lin. Since Saturday, Lin's Twitter followers have more than doubled.
