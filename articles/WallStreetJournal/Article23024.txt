 CNBC Shakes Up Production Staff, As Two Leave Station   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 2003  Leaving CNBC are executive producers Ted Shaker and Elyse Weiner. Mr. Shaker, a veteran television producer who had a long career at CBS Sports before joining CNBC in 2000, had primarily overseen "Business Center," one of the channel's premier shows. Ms. Weiner, who joined CNBC in 1999, was primarily involved in the network's daytime programming.
