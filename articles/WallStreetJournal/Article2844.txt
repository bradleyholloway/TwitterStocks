 Brokerage survey finds a high level of success for large arbitration cases   By Siconolfi, Michael, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 1996  Bigger is better for investors filing arbitration claims against Wall Street securities firms, according to a survey of brokerage-house disputes. Claims filed by investors alleging larger damages tend to win more frequently, and, in a surprise, investors also were found to win punitive-damage awards in nearly 7% of big claims alleging losses of more than $1 million.
