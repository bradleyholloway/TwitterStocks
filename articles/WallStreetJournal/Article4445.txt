 Samyang Chemical's Head Tops '87 Income Tax Roll   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 1988  SEOUL, South Korea -- Tear gas is no fun if you're on the receiving end. But it also may be a headache to Han YongJa, president of South Korean tear gas manufacturer Samyang Chemical Co.  According to a report released Friday by the Office of National Tax Administration, Ms. Han paid 2.9 billion won ($4 million) in 1987 income tax.
