 Scandal Widens at GE Medical Venture in Japan   By Kanabayashi, Masayoshi, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1991  General Electric Co's Japanese medical-equipment joint venture apologized for a bribery scandal which broke in Feb 1991, resulting in several arrests. Three more officials of the venture involving Yokogawa Medical Systems Ltd were arrested on charges of bribery.
