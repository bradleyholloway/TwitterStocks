 Sovereign Bancorp's Chairman Strikes Back By Filing Countersuit   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 1994  On Dec. 20, the bank holding company announced that it had brought suit against Mr. Jaindl and others for allegedly failing to disclose efforts to sell the company under terms that weren't in the best interests of shareholders. Terming the action a "joke," Mr. Jaindl said at the the time that the officers sued in order to block Sovereign from considering overtures made by two other large banking concerns.
