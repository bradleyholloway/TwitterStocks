 Mattel shares sink on news that results will trail forecasts   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 1996  Mattel Inc's stock price tumbled more than 10% to $26 after the toymaker announced that its sales and earnings would be below analysts' projections of 26 cents per share when it reports second-quarter results in Jul 1996. Mattel earned 24 cents a year earlier, adjusted for a stock split.
