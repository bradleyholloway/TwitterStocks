 IBM staffers will no longer send top, top, top, top-secret memos   By Hays, Laurie, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1995  The process through which IBM Corp is attempting to simplify its use of confidential memos is examined. At one point, IBM was using four classifications of confidential envelops, a system that CFO Jerome York found wasteful and annoying.
