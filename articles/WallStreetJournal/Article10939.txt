 Omnicom Posts Loss For the Third Period After Pre-Tax Charge   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 1986  Omnicom Group Inc. said its formation through a merger of three advertising agencies generated a pre-tax charge of $34.3 million, giving the company a third-quarter net loss of $29.8 million.  In its first earnings re port as a public company, Omnicom said third-quarter revenue from commissions and fees rose 17% to $178.5 million from $152.9 million.  Omnicom was formed last August from the merger of BBDO International Inc., Doyle Dane Bernbach Group Inc. and Needham Harper Worldwide.
