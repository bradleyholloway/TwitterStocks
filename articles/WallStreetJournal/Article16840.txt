 Blu-ray Lands a Big Fish: Warner; Exclusive Backing Might Tip Scales Against HD DVD   By McBride, Sarah, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 2008  The boom in regular DVDs, which lifted the financial performance of the movie studios in the early 2000s, has stalled, leaving the industry hungry for a new format that can entice consumers to replace their current DVD libraries with high-definition copies. Last year, DVD sales dropped 5% from 2006, according to Adams Media Research, contributing to fears among studio executives that consumers were holding off on buying DVDs of any kind until the format war was resolved.
