 Taiwan's ProMOS to Cut Memory-Chip Production   By Pevzner, Alex; Ho, Jessie, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 2008  Taiwan's ProMOS Technologies Inc. will cut fourth-quarter memory- chip output by 10% to 15% from the third quarter, as it shuts down its eight-inch chip plant and moves production to a more advanced 12-inch plant.
