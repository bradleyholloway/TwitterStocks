 Market Turmoil Stings Commercial Sector --- Demand for Mortgage-Backed Securities Takes a Hit as Some Key Lenders Are Backing Off From Making New Loans   By Brown, Eliot, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 2011  Late last month, for example, Hotel chain Strategic Hotels & Resorts Inc. obtained a $145 million loan for its InterContinental Hotel in Chicago from J.P. Morgan Chase & Co. But the closing of the mortgage, which J.P. Morgan plans to securitize, was delayed for days because of uncertainty in the markets, said Laurence Geller, Strategic Hotels' chief executive.
