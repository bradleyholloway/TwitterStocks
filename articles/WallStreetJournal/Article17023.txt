 Merck Says Emulsion Of Peanut Oil Extends Longevity of Flu Shot Firm's Researchers Also Begin Testing Eaton-Agent Vaccine Utilizing New Key Ingredient  By By a WALL STREET JOURNAL Staff Reporter, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 19, 1964  NEW YORK Merck & Co. has altered its influenza vaccine in an attempt to extend the life of flu shots by two to three years. The key new ingredient: Peanut oil.
