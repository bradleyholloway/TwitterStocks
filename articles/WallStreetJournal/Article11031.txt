 Gates Corp. to Sell Its Learjet Stake For $56.8 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 6, 1987  Gates Corp. said it signed a definitive agreement to sell its 64.8% stake in Gates Learjet Corp. for $56.8 million to a partnership formed by a unit of Integrated Resources Inc. and Gates Learjet management.  Under the latest agreement, Gates said it would sell its 7.8 million Gates Learjet shares for $7.25 each to the partnership led by Integrated Acquisition Inc.
