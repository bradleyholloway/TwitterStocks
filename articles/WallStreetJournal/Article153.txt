 Dollar Sends Stocks in Europe Lower, but Japan Climbs   By Whitney, Glenn, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 25, 1992  Stock and bond prices across Europe slid in Aug 24, 1992 trading as investors became increasingly worried by the weakness of the US dollar, but Tokyo stocks continued to surge in heavy trading. London's Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-stock index dropped 54.6 points, or 2.3%, to end at 2311.1, while Frankfurt's DAX 30-stock index fell 21.28 points, or 1.4%, to 1498.74. Tokyo's Nikkei 225-stock index closed up 411.08 points at 16627.96.
