 Marketing & Media Brief -- BCE Inc.: Part of Stake in Quebecor Will Be Sold to Levesque   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 1991  BCE said it will sell two million units, each consisting of one Quebecor Class B share and one-half a warrant, for C$17.50 a unit. Each full warrant will entitle the holder to purchase one Quebecor Class B share from BCE at C$17 until April 22, 1992. BCE said Levesque will place the units with institutional investors.
