 Business Brief -- BURLINGTON NORTHERN SANTA FE CORP.: Net Income Increased 12% With 8% Gain in Revenue   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 21, 1998  Burlington Northern Santa Fe Corp said 1998 third-quarter net income rose 12% to $317 million, or 66 cents a diluted share, from $283 million, or 60 cents a diluted share, a year earlier.
