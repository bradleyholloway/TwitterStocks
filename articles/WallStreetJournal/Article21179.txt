 International Corporate Report: Swiss Bank Corp.'s Earnings Declined by 3.2% During '87   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1988  SWISS BANK CORP., Switzerland's second-largest commercial bank, said net income fell 3.2% in 1987, to 652.3 million Swiss francs ($467.9 million) from 674 million francs a year earlier.
