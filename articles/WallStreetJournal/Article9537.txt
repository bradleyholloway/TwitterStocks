 Business Brief -- Benetton Group SpA: Rollerblade Business Is Sold; New Chief Executive Is Picked   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 2003  Italian clothing company Benetton Group SpA sold its Rollerblade inline skate business and said it has chosen a new chief executive, though it didn't disclose the official's name. Benetton acquired the Rollerblade, Nordica and Prince brands in 1998 to sell them in tandem with its new sportswear line Playlife in dedicated stores.
