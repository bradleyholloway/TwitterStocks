 International Brief -- LAGARDERE GROUPE: Net Profit Increases by 2.4% Amid Flat Growth in Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1996  Lagardere Groupe, a French defense, electronics and publishing concern, said consolidated net profit rose 2.4% in 1995 to 630 million francs ($124.6 million) from 615 million francs a year earlier.
