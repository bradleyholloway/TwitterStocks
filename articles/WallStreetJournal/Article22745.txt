 Business Brief -- Footstar Inc.: Firm Posts 31% Sales Rise For Latest 4-Week Period   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 2, 2001  Footstar Inc., Mahwah, N.J., said total sales for the four weeks ended Feb. 24 rose 31% to $172.3 million from $131.6 million for the year-earlier period. The company owns stores under the Footaction, Just For Feet and J.
