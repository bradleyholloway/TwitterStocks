 Another Bank in the Dow; In Rare Shift for DJIA, Bank of America Joins Along With Chevron   By Browning, E S, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 2008  The Wall Street Journal is published by Dow Jones & Co., which is owned by News Corp. Bank of America, now the largest U.S. bank by market value, and Chevron, the second-largest U.S. oil company after Exxon Mobil Corp., will replace Marlboro maker Altria, formerly known as Philip Morris, and Honeywell, a maker of aircraft components, specialty materials and other industrial products.
