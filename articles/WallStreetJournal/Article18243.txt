 Whittle Stake Will Be Bought by Forstmann   By Reilly, Patrick M; Smith, Randall, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 1991  Forstmann Little & Co agreed to invest $350 million to purchase a one-third stake in Whittle Communications LP, reducing Time Warner Inc's 50% stake and ending that firm's plan to buy up to 80% of Whittle. The value of the entrepreneurial publisher has nearly tripled since Time Warner acquired a half interest for $185 million in 1988.
