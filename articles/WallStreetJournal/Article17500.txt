 U.S. Health Care Targets HMO Market In New York Area   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 1985  BLUE BELL, Pa. -- U.S. Health Care Systems Inc. expects to start operating a health maintenance organization in the New York metropolitan area in 1986 and eventually to enroll about 850,000 members there, said Leonard Abramson, president and chief executive officer.  He said U.S. Health Care expects to start operating the HMO, called HMO of Greater New York, "no later than the first quarter of 1986." The date depends mostly on regulatory approval, he said in an interview.
