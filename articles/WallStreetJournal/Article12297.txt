 Jobless Rate in Germany Held at 12.6% in February   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 1998  The German unemployment rate remained at 12.6% in February, and economists said an expected pickup in economic growth won't reverse the high jobless count soon.  The jobless level ensures that unemployment will continue to be a leading issue in the election contest between Chancellor Helmut Kohl and Social Democratic opponent Gerhard Schroeder.
