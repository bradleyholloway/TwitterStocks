 Ciena Scales Back Its Forecast for Sales, Warns of Wider-Than-Expected Net Loss   By By Johnathan Burns, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 2002  The Linthicum, Md., company expects to report that sales for the quarter ended Jan. 31 fell to $160 million -- less than half the $351 million reported for the year-earlier quarter, and well below Ciena's forecast in December of between $220.7 million and $257.5 million. Ciena also expects to post a per-share net loss of between 21 cents and 24 cents instead of its December projection of eight cents to 12 cents. For the year-earlier quarter, Ciena had net income of $53.2 million, or 18 cents a share.
