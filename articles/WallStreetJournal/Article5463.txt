 United Auto Group's Cogan to become chairman, CEO, succeeding Spielvogel   By Davis, Ann, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 1997  United Auto Group said that Marshall S. Cogan will take over as chairman and CEO of the car-dealership chain, succeeding Carl Spielvogel. Cogan, United Auto's founder, is already the power behind the enterprise with a 19% stake through his private holding company, Trace International Holdings Inc.
