 U.K.'s Pearson, Chinese Firm Set TV Accord   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 2001  For the services venture -- Pearson CTV Media -- Pearson's broadband division will hold a 50% stake. (Broadband refers to the high-speed Internet connections being installed in residences by cable and phone companies.) CTV Media will own 40% and Cyber Solutions Ltd., a Beijing telecommunications company, will hold the remaining 10%.
