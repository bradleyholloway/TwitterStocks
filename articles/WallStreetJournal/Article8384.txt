 NBC Has Discussions Concerning Buying U.K.'s Superchannel   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 6, 1993  The purchase would mark a significant move for NBC, a unit of General Electric Co., into the European news marketplace. The six-year-old Superchannel broadcasts news and entertainment programming 24 hours a day in English and German to several countries. Among its offerings is NBC's CNBC business programming, seen on cable in the U.S.
