 Fund track: SEC examines variable-annuity business   By Gasparino, Charles, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1996  The recent explosion in the sales of variable annuities is catching the eye of Wall Street's top cop. The SEC has quietly put out the word that mutual-fund companies, insurance companies and anyone else cashing in on the booming variable annuity business can expect a heavy dose of scrutiny.
