 Business Brief: H&R Block Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 1987  In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the tax-preparation company said it holds 1,028,000 National Data shares, including 238,000 purchased Oct. 21 to Nov. 2 for $21.478 to $24.138 each. National Data provides electronic data processing services.
