 Holding company Saatchi & Saatchi plans to change its name to Cordiant   By Goldman, Kevin, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 1995  Saatchi & Saatchi PLC, the advertising agency that no longer employs co-founders Maurice and Charles Saatchi, is dropping the Saatchi name and proposing to replace it with Cordiant PLC.
