 To Outdo Rivals in Mobile Software, Samsung Turns to Outside Talent   By Yun-Hee, Kim; Lee, Jung-Ah, The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 2012  The emphasis on software took on new urgency over the past year after Google Inc., maker of the Android operating system prevalent in smartphones including many of Samsung's, announced it is acquiring Motorola Mobility Holdings Inc. Also last year, Nokia and Microsoft Corp. unveiled a partnership to jointly develop smartphones based on Microsoft's Windows Phone software.
