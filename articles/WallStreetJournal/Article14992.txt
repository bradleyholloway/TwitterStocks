 World Wire: Germany Leads in Farm Imports   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 1992  Germany's Agriculture Ministry said that Germany's imports of agricultural goods in 1991 rose 12% to a record 68 billion marks ($46.1 billion), making it the world's largest food importer. Farm exports rose 11% to DM35.8 billion, with Italy the biggest customer.
