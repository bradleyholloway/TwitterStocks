 In Brief   The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 2007  U.K. financier Guy Hands's private-equity group Terra Firma Capital Partners Ltd. said it acquired Pegasus Aviation Finance Co. for $900 million of equity, plus the assumption of $4.3 billion of debt. Terra Firma will combine the San Francisco aircraft lessor's operations with Dublin-based lessor AWAS, which was acquired by the private-equity firm in 2006. The combined fleet will comprise more than 250 owned and ordered airplanes, valued at more than $7.5 billion, and 94 managed planes leased to customers.
