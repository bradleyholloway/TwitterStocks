 Business Brief -- BARRICK GOLD CORP.: Net Income Declines 14% On Lower Gold Production   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 2000  Barrick Gold Corp., hurt by lower gold production, reported a 14% decline in second-quarter net income to $72 million, or 18 cents a diluted share, from $84 million, or 20 cents a share, a year earlier.
