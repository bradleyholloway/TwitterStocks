 Coffee prices rise on concerns about supply   By Ip, Greg, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1997  Coffee prices rose sharply Feb 3, 1997 on speculative buying and continued concern about South American supply. The price of coffee for March delivery rose 6.25 cents to set a contract high of $1.4565 a pound on the Coffee, Sugar & Cocoa Exchange. On other markets, gold ended higher.
