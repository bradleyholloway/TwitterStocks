 Genesco's Chairman, Two Directors Resign Over Possible Conflict   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 1986  NASHVILLE, Tenn. -- Genesco Inc. said its recently elected chairman, David K. Wilson, and two other directors resigned to resolve potential conflicts of interest as a result of their association with one of the company's banks.  Genesco said Mr. Wilson, chairman since January, and two other directors who resigned, Nelson C. Andrews and Kenneth L. Roberts, are directors of First American Corp., a bank holding company. First American's main unit, First American National Bank of Nashville, is a party to revolving credit agreements with Genesco and Genesco Financial. Mr. Roberts is chairman and chief executive officer of First American Corp.
