 Lockheed plans satellite system for $4 billion   By Cole, Jeff, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1995  Lockheed Martin Corp on Oct 3, 1995 proposed to build a $4 billion satellite system for two-way global computer networking, video-conferencing and other on-demand services in a challenge to plans by Hughes Electronics and a separate Teledesic Corp venture. Also, backers of the Globalstar LP venture withdrew their $400 million debt offering. AT&T Corp also joined the fray, saying it is considering launching 12 satellites as part of its own multibillion dollar plan.
