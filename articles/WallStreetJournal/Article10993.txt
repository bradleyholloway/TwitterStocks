 Burlington Northern Unit Agrees to Settle Suit Over 1986 Bonds   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 1987  Burlington Northern Inc. said its railroad unit agreed in principle to settle a two-year-old class-action suit by holders of two series of 1896 Northern Pacific Railway bonds.  The unit, Burlington Northern Railroad, said the agreement calls for it to provide a cash settlement fund of $35.5 million, as well as attorneys' fees, plaintiffs' other litigation expenses, and related charges. Burlington Northern also would be required to pay interest and principal on the bonds until maturity. One series of bonds becomes due in 1997, and the other in 2047.
