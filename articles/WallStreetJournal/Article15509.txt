 Viacom to exercise option to buy half of UPN from Chris-Craft   By Jensen, Elizabeth, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 1996  Viacom Inc took a big step to secure an outlet for its paramount studio's television shows, declaring that it will exercise its option to purchase half of the fledgling United Paramount Network from BHC Communications Inc, a subsidiary of Chris-Craft Industries Inc, for $160 million.
