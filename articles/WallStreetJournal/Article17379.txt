 The Business: An Engine Down South   By Bussey, John, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 2012  [...]South Carolina saw exports jump 21% in 2011 from 2010, driven not just by traditional industries such as cotton yarn and fabric, but by production of vehicles, electrical machinery, chemicals and medical equipment. Auto makers BMW, Volkswagen, General Motors, Daimler, Honda, Ford, Nissan, Kia, Hyundai, and Toyota have set up shop over the years across the region. Since 1990, foreign auto companies opened some 15 new assembly plants in the U.S., two thirds of which are located in the South.
