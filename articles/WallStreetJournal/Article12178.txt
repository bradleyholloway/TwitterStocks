 Thermo Instrument Systems Inc.: Tender for Life Sciences draws 85% of its shares   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 1997  Thermo Instrument Systems Inc said 85% of Life Sciences International PLC's shares outstanding have been tendered in connection with Thermo Instrument's cash offer for the company, and Thermo Instrument has now waived the conditions that at least 90% of the shares be tendered.
