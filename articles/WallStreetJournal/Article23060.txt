 Consumer Spending Loses Some Momentum   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 2003  Separately, the University of Michigan's consumer-sentiment index reached 89.6 in October, up from a midmonth reading of 89.4 and 87.7 in September. The consumer-sentiment numbers show consumers also haven't fully embraced the recovery. The labor market continues to be the chief source of worry.
