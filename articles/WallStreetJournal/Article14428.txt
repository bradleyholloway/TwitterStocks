 Martin Marietta's Augustine Named Chief, as Expected   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1987  Mr. Augustine, 52 years old, has been president and chief operating officer since April 1986. He joined the aerospace and information systems concern in 1977. As chief executive, he succeeds Thomas G. Pownall, who remains chairman. Mr. Pownall, 65, is expected to retire next spring when his one-year extention granted by the board expires.
