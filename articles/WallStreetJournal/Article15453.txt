 Chrysler is sued by U.S. to force recall of cars   By Nomani, Asra Q; Henderson, Angelo B, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 1996  The federal government is suing Chrysler Corp to force the auto maker to recall model year 1995 Cirrus and Dodge Stratus cars for alleged safety problems with the cars' rear seat belts. Government officials said they took the action after Chrysler opposed issuing a recall.
