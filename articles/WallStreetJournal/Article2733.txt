 World wire: Suez weighs Lyonnaise alliance   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 1995  Cie de Suez, France's biggest holding company, said it is considering closer links with Lyonnaise des Eaux SA to ward off a possible hostile raid. The proposed alliance with Lyonnaise marks Suez's third disclosure in two weeks in its search for allies amid takeover speculation.
