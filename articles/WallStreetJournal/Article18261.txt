 International Brief -- Komatsu Ltd.: Net Income Declined 3.4% In Half as Sales Fell 9.5%   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1991  Komatsu Ltd., a major Japanese maker of construction machinery, said net income in its fiscal first half ended Sept. 30 dropped 3.4% to 8.88 billion yen ($67.9 million) from 9.19 billion yen a year earlier. Sales fell 9.5% to 301.57 billion yen from 333.19 billion yen.
