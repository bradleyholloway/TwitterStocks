 The Lovable American   The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 2006  Regarding "Innocents Abroad" by Martha Bayles (Taste page, Weekend Journal, May 5), I offer the following simple tips: Leave the flip- flops, shorts, sweatshirts and baseball caps at home. Learn how to say thank you in the local language and say it often. Change your dollars into the local currency.
