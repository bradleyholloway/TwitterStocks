 WEEKEND INVESTOR --- The New Basics: Beware 'Leveraged' ETFs   By Light, Joe, The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 2012  Earlier this month, the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority fined brokers including Citigroup Global Markets, Morgan Stanley, UBS Financial Services and Wells Fargo Advisors for improperly selling leveraged and inverse ETFs. When the ETFs launched in the mid-2000s, many small investors looking to pump up their returns missed an important wrinkle. Because of daily compounding, long-term investments in leveraged ETFs don't mirror the indexes the funds tracked.
