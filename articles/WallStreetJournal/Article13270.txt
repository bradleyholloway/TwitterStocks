 Lehman Holders' Vote Points to Stock Split   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 2006  Lehman Chief Executive Richard Fuld said the company's board would come together after Lehman's annual meeting yesterday to consider splitting the stock, which was up $1.33 to $148.46 at 4 p.m. in New York Stock Exchange composite trading. Lehman's stock price has appreciated almost 20 times from $7.44 when the company went public in 1994.
