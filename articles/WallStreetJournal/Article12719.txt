 Money Funds' Assets Fall $28.07 Billion   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 2001  Average seven-day compound yields rose to 3.18% from 3.17%, the newsletter reported. Thirty-day simple yields slipped to 3.19% from 3.22%, while 30-day compound yields fell to 3.24% from 3.27%. Compound yields assume reinvestment of dividends.  Average seven-day simple yields on tax-exempt money-market funds fell to 1.76% from 1.81%, while seven-day compound yields fell to 1.78% from 1.83%.  30-day simple yields slid to 1.86% from 1.92%, and 30-day compound yields dropped to 1.87% from 1.94%. The average maturity of the tax-free funds' investments increased to 43 days from 41 days.
