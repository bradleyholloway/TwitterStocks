 Politics & Economics: Senate Panel Bids To Block New Rule On Airline Control   By Meckler, Laura, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 2006  The Transportation Department has proposed changing regulations that require U.S. citizens to control all aspects of U.S. airlines. Under the proposed change, foreigners could exercise influence over schedules, strategy, pricing and other aspects of operations. Decisions about safety, security and use of craft to aid the U.S. military would have to remain in U.S. hands.
