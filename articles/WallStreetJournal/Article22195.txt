 U.S. launches antitrust suit against GE over licensing of its medical software   By Simpson, Glenn R, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 2, 1996  The Justice Dept accused GE of violating antitrust law with restrictive licensing agreements for its imaging-equipment repair software. In a suit filed in Montana, the agency contended that the company shouldn't restrain hospitals that use its diagnostic software from going into the imaging-equipment repair business themselves.
