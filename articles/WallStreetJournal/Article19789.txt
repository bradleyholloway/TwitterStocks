 New Securities Issues   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 2004  Procter & Gamble Co. -- $1.5 billion of notes was priced in a multitranche offering, according to people close to the deal. It sold $900 million of 10-year notes through lead managers J.P. Morgan, Goldman Sachs and Morgan Stanley, and $600 million of 30-year notes through lead managers J.P. Morgan, Goldman Sachs and Citigroup. Terms: Amount: $900 million; maturity: Aug. 15, 2014; coupon: 4.95%; price: 99.671; yield: 4.992%; spread: 57 basis points over Treasurys; settlement: Aug. 10, 2004; call: make whole call at Treasurys plus 10 basis points; ratings: Aa3 (Moody's), double-A-minus (S&P).
