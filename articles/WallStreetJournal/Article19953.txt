 Investor's Calendar   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 2005  More Cost Cuts: Details of Ford's "Way Forward" cost-cutting plan may emerge when Ford's board meets Wednesday and Thursday. People familiar with the plan say it may involve closing plants in Atlanta, St. Louis and St. Paul, Minn. Rival General Motors recently said it will close nine plants.  Greenspan Warns: Federal Reserve Chairman Alan Greenspan said the U.S. economy is growing at a "reasonably good pace" heading into 2006, but warned the budget deficit will worsen significantly unless steps are taken to control entitlement spending, particularly on health care.
