 Tokyo Electric Posts 37% Jump In Net Profit   By Kachi, Hiroyuki, The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 2006  Japan's largest power utility in revenue terms, known as Tepco, posted a group net profit of 310.39 billion yen ($2.78 billion), compared with a year-earlier profit of 226.18 billion yen. Group revenue rose 4.1% to 5.255 trillion yen from 5.047 trillion yen.
