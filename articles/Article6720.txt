 Politics & Economics: APEC Pledges Trade-Talks Rescue   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 2007  The APEC program doesn't set targets on the greenhouse-gas emissions. Instead, APEC members will reduce "energy intensity" -- the amount of energy needed to produce a dollar of gross domestic product -- 25% by 2030. They pledged to increase forest cover in the region by at least 49.4 million acres.
