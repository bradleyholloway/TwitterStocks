 GAF Plans Sale of a 20% Stake in Its Main Unit   By Anders, George, The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 1991  GAF Corp announced a stock offering that would value the holdings of the company's chairman and major shareholder, Samuel Heyman, in excess of $900 million. The company announced plans for a public offering of as many as 20 million shares in International Specialty Products Inc, a subsidiary.
