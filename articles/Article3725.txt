 Business and Finance   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 2007  The Dow industrials hit another record, rising 45.52 points to 13907.52 despite a jump in oil prices. The blue chips had their best week since April, climbing 2.2% and bringing them up nearly 12% this year. Treasury prices rose. Germany's DAX index hit a record intraday high and London stocks touched a level not seen since September 2000. Asian indexes also set records.  A Dutch court cleared ABN to sell its LaSalle unit, a setback for an RBS-led group that is competing with Barclays for the bank.
