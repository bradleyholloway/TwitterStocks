 Japanese Committee Urges Consideration Of Offshore Bank   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 1985  An advisory committee to the Finance Ministry has resurrected a plan to set up an offshore banking market in Japan, a move that could substantially raise Tokyo's standing as an international financial center.  The committee recommended allowing financial institutions eventually to issue medium and long-term certificates of deposit in the Euroyen market, the market for yen held outside Japan.
