 Russian Communist woos capitalist elite   By Kempe, Frederick, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1996  Gennady Zyuganov, who brought Russia's Communist Party back from the dead with sweeping gains in the Dec 1995 parliamentary elections, is profiled.
