 Alliance Gaming Corp.: Bid for Bally Gaming stock is increased to $13 a share   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1995  Alliance Gaming Corp said it increased its offer for Bally Gaming International Inc to $13 a share in cash and stock for 5.4 million Bally shares. The bid is up from $12.50 a share for 4.4 million shares.
