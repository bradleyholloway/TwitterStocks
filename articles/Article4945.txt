 WestMarc Chief Jeffrey Marcus Will Step Down   By By Laura Landro, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 11, 1988  WestMarc Communications Inc. said Jeffrey A. Marcus will step down as chairman and chief executive officer of the cable television concern he started in partnership with cable giant Tele-Communications Inc.  Tele-Communications, which owns 54% of WestMarc, said its president, John Malone, will succeed Mr. Marcus as chairman. Larry Romrell, president of WestMarc, will assume the chief executive's post. Both companies are based in Denver.
