 Advertising: Geer, Dubois to shut doors   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1993  Two top officers at advertising agency Geer, Dubois Inc, CEO Steven Bowen and Chairman Rodney Underwood, have abandoned plans to buy out the firm, effectively shutting the agency's doors. The company recently lost the $60 million account of Baileys Irish Cream.
