 Dow Jones Names Pensiero to Global News Post   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 2001  As part of the Journal's recently announced plan to merge its domestic and international copy desks, Dow Jones also named Jesse Lewis, 46, as global copy chief, overseeing copy editors for the U.S., European and Asian editions of the Journal. Howard Hoffman, 42, was named global pagination chief for all editions. Messrs. Lewis and Hoffman both will report to Mr. [Jim Pensiero]. The new assignments are effective Jan. 1.
