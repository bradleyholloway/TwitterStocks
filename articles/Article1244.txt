 Tokyo's Nikkei Index Reaches a Record High While London Shares Finish Modestly Lower ---- A Wall Street Journal News Roundup   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1989  Tokyo stocks closed higher in moderate Monday trading with the key Nikkei index posting a record high, helped by concentrated buying.  The Nikkei index of 225 selected issues was up 86.92 points at 35750.12, exceeding the previous record of 35689.98, set Sept. 28. The index gained 5.78 points Friday.  The Second Section index, which added 8.18 points Friday, was up 16.64 points, or 0.45%, to close at 3689.43 Monday. Second Section volume was estimated at 19 million shares, down from 23 million Friday.
