 Europe Proposes Compromise in Dispute With Washington Over Banana Quotas   By By Geoff Winestock, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 2000  A U.S. trade representative official said, however, that the U.S. was still considering the detail of the EU proposal for distributing licenses and that the U.S. had some concerns. Proposals by the EU last year to revamp the complicated system of tariffs, quotas and licenses failed to convince the U.S. to lift sanctions. The U.S. has come under pressure from the EU recently in an unrelated dispute involving U.S. export subsidies, and both sides say they want to de-escalate trans-Atlantic trade tensions.
