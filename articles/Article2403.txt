Ahmanson's Net Rises to $99.5 Million, Above Analyst Views   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1998  Ahmanson, owner of the big thrift Home Savings of America, said earnings for the quarter were $99.5 million, or 89 cents a diluted share. In the year-earlier quarter, Ahmanson reported earnings of $91.2 million, or 74 cents a diluted share. Ahmanson's said its basic earnings, or net income divided by shares outstanding, were 97 cents a share in the latest quarter and 79 cents a share a year ago.
