 Renault and Volvo are on the verge of tying the knot   By Aeppel, Timothy; Moore, Stephen D, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 1993  Renault SA of France and Sweden's AB Volvo, after several years of close ties, are on the verge of a marriage that would pull the combined automaker into a tie with Peugeot Citroen SA for the No. 3 spot in Europe with a 12% market share. Many observers say the union is very logical for the two firms.
