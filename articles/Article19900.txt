 Investor's Calendar   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 2005  G8 Summit: Leaders of the Group of Eight nations meet in Scotland this week, under pressure from protesters to take action on poverty and global warming. The United Kingdom is calling for doubling aid to Africa, eliminating debt and improving trade terms for poor countries. While the debt plan has already won partial approval, the big aid boost has met some resistance in the U.S. and elsewhere.  Payback Plan: WorldCom founder Bernard Ebbers will transfer most of his assets to a liquidation trust for WorldCom holders to settle a lawsuit linked to his role in the company's accounting fraud.  CEO Changes: Boeing named 3M Chief Executive James McNerney as its third CEO in a year and a half. Morgan Stanley's board elected John Mack as chairman and CEO.
