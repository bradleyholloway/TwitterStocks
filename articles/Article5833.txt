 The front lines: Gibson does his part to make the economy safe from cycles   By Petzinger, Thomas Jr, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 1996  As chairman of Manugistics Inc, whose software is endowing intelligence to an ever-growing network of cash registers, delivery vehicles, distribution centers, factories and raw-material suppliers, William Gibson is doing his part to make the economy less vulnerable to cycles. Each player in a supply chain conducts his business with the latest and best information from everyone else in the chain, guiding supply and demand into ever-more-perfect balance.
