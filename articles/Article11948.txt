 Lehman hires brokers made available as Salomon, CS First Boston cut back   By Siconolfi, Michael, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 1995  Wall Street firms are swooping in to snatch brokers who have hit the pavement after Salomon Bros Inc and CS First Boston Inc dismantled their brokerage operations. In the latest move, Lehman Bros Holdings Inc plans to announce on Apr 6, 1995 that it has hired eight brokers from the brokerage units of Salomon Inc and CS Holding AG that cater to wealthy individuals and small institutions.
