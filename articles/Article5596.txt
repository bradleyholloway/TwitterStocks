 Letters to the Editor: We Don't Put Obstacles In the Path of Doctors   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 1994  There was a collective wince here at Blue Care Network of Southeast Michigan when we saw our chart in your March 24 edition ("A Taste of Managed Care"). Some readers might get the wrong idea that this chart is the route a physician must take to get access to care for a patient. Worse yet, someone might think these are the steps a patient must take.
