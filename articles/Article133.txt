 DAF Reports a Wider Deficit for First Half   By Hagerty, Bob, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 23, 1991  Dutch truck maker DAF NV reported a first-half loss of 179.1 million guilders ($91 million), compared with a loss of Fl 32.1 million a year earlier. The firm forecast a better second half and announced plans to sell preference shares.
