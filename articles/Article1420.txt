 Getting Going: What's Going On With the Dollar?   By Kansas, Dave, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 2009  [...] if you believe that the dollar will depreciate against other currencies, it is possible to simply open a bank account in the other currency and exchange your dollars.
