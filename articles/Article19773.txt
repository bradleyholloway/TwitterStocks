 Health & Technology: Omnicare Offer For NeighborCare To Turn Hostile   By Berman, Dennis K, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 2004  The Baltimore-based company has said in the past that Omnicare's offer was "blatantly opportunistic" and that NeighborCare would generate more value for shareholders via its "organic" plans to deploy new low-cost technology in its operations. In a previous interview, NeighborCare Chief Executive Officer John Arlotta said that shareholders "will be better off by allowing us to implement the programs and seeing them come to fruition."
