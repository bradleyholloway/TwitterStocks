 U.S. private pensions were underfunded by $71 billion in 1993   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 1994  The federal Pension Benefit Guaranty Corp said that private pensions were underfunded by an estimated $71 billion at the end of 1993, up from $53 billion at the end of 1992. The agency blamed low interest rates, which caused lower returns on pension funds' investments.
