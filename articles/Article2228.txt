 Heard on the street: Trump Hotels, haunted by Castle's weakness and the 'Donald discount,' spooks investors   By Orwall, Bruce, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1996  Donald Trump's deal to sell his Trump's Castle casino in Atlantic City to Trump Hotels & Casino Resorts Inc for $490 million has helped send Trump Hotels' stock plunging more than 30% since the late-Sep 1996 shareholders' vote. Trump concedes that the market is penalizing him for the Castle deal, which investors' said was $100 million too high, but contends the investment will be vindicated. Prospects for Trump Hotels shares are analyzed.
