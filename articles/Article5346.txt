 Siemens Unit Introduces Low-End PC; Company Plans to Try Direct Mail Sales   By Hooper, Laurence, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 1992  Siemens Nixdorf Informationssysteme AG unveiled the first model of its new "Economy Line" of PCs at the huge CeBIT computer show in Hanover Germany, joining the stampede to market such low-cost PCs. The Siemens AG unit's move signals similar moves by IBM Corp, Compaq Computer Corp and Olivetti & Co.
