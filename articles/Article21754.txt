 GPA Debt Plan Hinges on Talks with Jet Makers   By Lipin, Steven; Cole, Jeff; Coleman, Brian, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1992  Aircraft leasing giant GPA Group PLC's banks are demanding that any debt restructuring be linked to concessions from the company's manufacturers and to an infusion of equity from owners. The restructuring would postpone $900 million in principal payments and loosen financial convenants on roughly $2.7 billion in bank debt. Setting conditions could complicate efforts to reach an agreement, however.
