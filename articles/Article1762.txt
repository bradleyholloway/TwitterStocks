 Bush subpoenaed to testify at trial of BNL manager   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 1993  Former President Bush has been subpoenaed to testify in the trial of Christopher Drogoul, the former manager of the Atlanta branch of Banca Nazionale del Lavoro. The subpoena also asks Bush to produce a series of documents that will show a White House connection to efforts to encourage trade and financing for Iraq befor the Gulf War.
