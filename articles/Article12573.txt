 World Bank Is Likely to Back $3.5 Billion Exxon Pipeline   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 2000  The World Bank is expected today to give its support to a $3.5 billion pipeline linking oil fields in Chad to the Cameroon coast, despite fears that the Exxon Mobil Corp.-led project will damage the environment, spur social upheaval and feed corruption in both African nations.
