 International Brief -- Nissan Motor Co.: Pretax Profit for Fiscal Half Rose 5.4% on Record Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 1990  Nissan Motor Co. of Japan said unconsolidated pretax profit in its first half ended Sept. 30 rose 5.4% to 93.07 billion yen ($728.6 million) from 88.32 billion yen a year earlier, on record sales.
