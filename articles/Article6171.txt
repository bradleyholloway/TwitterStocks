 Barney's Inc. CEO Plans to Step Down By the End of May   By By Rebecca Quick, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1999  The management shift comes just as Barney's is emerging from a rocky, three-year struggle with Chapter 11 bankruptcy-law proceedings. Mr. (Tom) Shull, an executive at Meridian Ventures Inc., has built a reputation by restructuring and rebuilding financially troubled companies. Meridian was hired in August 1997 to help shore up Barney's finances; its contract will expire at the end of May.
