 Nintendo Raises Profit Forecast   By Takahashi, Yoshio, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 2006  Nintendo said it expects group net profit of 100 billion yen, or about $850 million, for the year ending March 31, a 20% increase from its prior forecast of 83 billion yen. For the fiscal first half that ended last month, Nintendo nearly doubled its estimate for group net, to 50 billion yen from 27 billion yen.
