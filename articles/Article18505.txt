 Daimler sees nine-month loss growing to $1.17 billion under U.S. accounting   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1993  Daimler-Benz AG projected that its net loss for the first nine months of 1993 will increase to two billion marks ($1.17 billion), from less than one billion marks in the first half. The results reflect accounting under US financial disclosure rules, which Daimler adopted to permit trading on the NYSE.
