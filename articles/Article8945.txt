 Roach to Retire as CEO Of Tandy at End of Year   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1998  FORT WORTH, Texas -- John V. Roach, Tandy Corp.'s longtime chief, will retire as chief executive officer of the company, the owner of the Radio Shack store chain, at the end of the year and be succeeded by Leonard H. Roberts, the company's president.
