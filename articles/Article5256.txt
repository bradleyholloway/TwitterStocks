 Energy agency lifts estimate for oil demand   By Tanner, James, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1995  The International Energy Agency now estimates 1995 petroleum demand will reach 69.5 million barrels a day, up from 1994's revised average of 68.4 million. On Jul 7, 1995 crude oil traded on the New York Mercantile Exchange settled $.23 lower at $17.14 a barrel for August delivery.
