 Synovus, like a Southern belle, goes courting quietly   By Brannigan, Martha, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1995  Due in part to its genteel style, Synovus Corp, a Columbus GA bank holding company, has emerged as the acquirer of choice for small Southern banks facing frenzied industry consolidation. Synovus's style is to acquire small community banks that are doing well, preserving their original names and leaving current boards of directors and managers to their own devices.
