 Overweight Women Often Earn Less   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 2004  "This suggests that, socioeconomically, obesity is not as stigmatizing for men as it is for women," concluded the University of Helsinki researchers.  The researchers combed national registries that track Finns' education, occupation and income to choose 2,314 men and 2,068 women for the study. The study was published yesterday in the American Journal of Public Health.
