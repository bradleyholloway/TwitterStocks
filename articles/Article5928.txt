 Corrections & Amplifications   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 9, 2001  KPMG CONSULTING INC.'s initial public offering of stock was increased before yesterday's first day of trading to nearly 112.5 million shares, up from the previously reduced 112 million, and its market capitalization at the $18-a-share IPO price was $2.76 billion. A Deals & Deal Makers article yesterday incorrectly said that the IPO was increased to 117 million shares and that the market cap was $2.1 billion; the size of the IPO, not the overall market cap, was $2.02 billion.
