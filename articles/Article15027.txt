 Motel Developer Bucks Slump with Rapid Expansion   By Tannenbaum, Jeffrey A, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1992  Although US hotel and motel development remains in a deep slump and the industry is posting losses of about $5 billion a year, Gary Tharaldson is doggedly building new motels. The Fargo ND developer runs franchises from several companies, including Choice Hotels International Inc and Days Inns.
