 AT&T will insure its card customers on its Web service   By Weber, Thomas E, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 1996  In a bid to calm consumer fears about security on the Internet, AT&T Corp said it will insure its Universal credit-card customers against unauthorized charges made in cyberspace--as long as the customers were using AT&T's own service to connect to the global computer network.
