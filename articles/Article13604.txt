 Japan's Nikkei Rallies 2% To Begin Its Trading Year   By Anonymous, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2009  J.P. Morgan stock strategists recommended that investors focus on companies and sectors that are capable of maintaining or increasing dividend payouts, such as telecom providers, and recommend an overweight position on telecom stocks in Europe and in the U.S. "With almost zero return on cash and record low yields on government debt, investors are seeking other assets that can provide stable income," they said. Deutsche Bank analysts predicted the stock price would remain volatile, "reflecting challenging fundamentals and financials balanced by a possible government guarantee and/or asset sales."
