 Legal beat: Roche brings leading institutions into lawsuit over patent rights   By Carlton, Jim, The Wall Street Journal,  May 25, 1995  Hoffmann-LaRoche, a unit of Swiss drug company Roche Holding Ltd, named about 40 US universities and government laboratories that "infringe" some of its patents on a method for amplifying DNA during the week of May 15, 1995. The filing was made in a long-running lawsuit between LaRoche and Promega Corp, a bioscience company in Madison WI.
