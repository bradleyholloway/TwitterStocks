 Shares Post Sizable Gains, With Help From Strong Bond Market, Rising Dollar   By By Larry Bauman, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1995  Small-capitalization stocks were sharply higher as a strong bond market and a rising dollar sparked a wave of across-the-board buying.  Technology stocks performed exceptionally well, leading the technology-laden Nasdaq Composite Index to post the most significant gains among closely watched market indicators.  Key to the gains in the bond market has been the rising dollar, a factor that favors small-cap stocks, with their limited international exposure. While falling bond yields favor all sectors of the economy, the dollar's contribution appears to be giving the small-cap arena an additional boost.
