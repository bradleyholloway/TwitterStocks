 Business Brief -- FMC Corp.: Concern Agrees With EPA To Reduce Sales of Furadan   The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 1991  FMC Corp. said it has agreed with federal regulators to gradually reduce sales of the granular form of Furadan, a popular insecticide that has been linked to bird deaths nationwide.  The Environmental Protection Agency has been mulling whether to ban the bug and worm killer since 1989. In recent months, some states such as Maryland and Virginia acted on their own to toughen restrictions on the use of Furadan.
