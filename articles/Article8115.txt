 USX Holders Clear Plan for New Stocks; Talks Under Way to Sell Part of Steel Unit   By Ansberry, Clare, The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 1991  USX Corp shareholders agreed to split the company's stock into separate steel and energy securities, backing a management plan unveiled in Apr 1991 to draw more investors.
