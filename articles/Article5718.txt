 BT to Announce Expansion Plans For Italy, Sweden   By By Richard L. Hudson, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 1995  British Telecommunications PLC, continuing its rapid international expansion, is completing plans for ventures in Italy and Sweden, industry sources said.  In simultaneous news conferences today in London and Rome, the British phone giant plans to announce a partnership to enter the fast-growing Italian market. A BT spokesman confirmed the news conferences but declined to say what the announcement would be. Such a project would make BT the latest competitor to Italy's national phone company, Telecom Italia SpA.
