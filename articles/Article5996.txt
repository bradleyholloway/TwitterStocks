 Gold gains for the third trading day in a row   By Lucchetti, Aaron, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1997  Inspired by a fidgety stock market and last week's bond market tumble, gold prices climbed for the third straight trading day on Aug 11, 1997. On the Comex division of the New York Mercantile Exchange, the August gold contract rose $2.10 a troy ounce, to $328.20.
