 Advertising: Ross Roy to drop clients   By Parker-Pope, Tara; Beatty, Sally Goll, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 1996  Ross Roy Communications, acquired in 1995 by Omnicom Group for an estimated $50 million, said it was dropping all clients other than Chrysler. In a statement, the company also said it plans to cut some jobs among its 700-employee workforce.
