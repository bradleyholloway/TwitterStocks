 Two rival suitors for Santa Fe Pacific report their results for third quarter   By Machalaba, Daniel, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 21, 1994  Two rival bidders for Santa Fe Pacific Corp reported 1994 third-quarter results: Burlington Northern Inc had a higher profit of $115 million, or $1.22 per share, compared to year-earlier results of $24 million, or 21 cents a share, while Union Pacific Corp reported a net loss after accounting for disposal of a unit of $213 million, or $1.04 per share.
