 Phoenix Rising: Developer Who Owes Millions on Failed S&Ls Is Still Buying Land   By King, Ralph T, Jr, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 1992  Although Robert Burns's land development business in Phoenix AZ collapsed in 1988, leading to defaults on more than $150 million in loans from banks and thrifts, he currently earns a living by dealing in cheap land he buys from the RTC. This is despite the fact that Burns is currently being sued by a number of investors in his earlier schemes, including Citibank and the RTC.
