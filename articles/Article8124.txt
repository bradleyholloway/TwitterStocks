 Gantos Inc.: Sales Were Little Changed in 4 Weeks Ended June 1   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 7, 1991  Gantos Inc reported sales of $20.6 million for the four-week period ended Jun 1, 1991, essentially unchanged from the same period a year earlier. Sales for the 17 weeks ended Jun 1, 1991 were $79.6 million, unchanged in comparison to the same period last year. Catalog sales for the period were $1.3 million compared with $2.4 million a year earlier.
