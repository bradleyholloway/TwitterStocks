 World-wide: Serb tanks advanced   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1995  Serb infantry and tanks pushed to the edge of the mainly Muslim enclave, Srebrenica, and fired shells into the UN-designated "safe area." The Serbs also overran UN observation posts and seized 15 Dutch peacekeepers, bringing to 30 the number taken in the two-day offensive in eastern Bosnia.
