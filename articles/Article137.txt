Herbert Allen, former president and chairman of the board of the Title Insurance and Trust Company of Los Angeles and a southern California civic leader, died yesterday at Huntington Memorial Hospital. He was 85 years old.  Mr. Allen, who suffered a stroke April 29, worked 40 years for Title Insurance and was a member of the company's board of directors for 10 years after his retirement as president in 1960.  He also was a former president of the Los Angeles Exchange Club, the Los Angeles Council of the Boy Scouts of America and the city's Merchants and Manufacturers Association.  Survivors include a son and two daughters. His wife, the former Eleanor Busch of New York City, died last year.
