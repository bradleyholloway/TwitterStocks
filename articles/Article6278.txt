 Retail Brief -- Barnes & Noble Inc.: Agreement Reached to Acquire Remaining Shares of Web Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 9, 2004  Barnes & Noble Inc., the nation's largest bookseller, said it agreed to buy the outstanding shares of Barnes&Noble.com Inc. for $3.05 each, or about $150 million. The transaction is expected to close in the first quarter of the next fiscal year.
