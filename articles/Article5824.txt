 This saint likes his holy spirits: Petitioners bring whiskey, vodka   By Beckett, Paul, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 1996  San Simon, a local "saint" in the Guatemalan village of Zunil to whom people offer cigarettes and liquor for answers to their prayers, is discussed. San Simon, who is not a saint recognized by the Catholic Church, has origins that stretch back to an ancient Mayan god.
