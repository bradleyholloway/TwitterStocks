 Business Brief -- TIFFANY & CO.: Outlook for 3rd-Quarter Net Strengthens as Sales Surge   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 2000  Tiffany & Co., the upscale jewelry retailer, told analysts it now expects to earn 21 cents a diluted share in the fiscal third quarter because of stronger-than-expected sales in the U.S. and Japan.
