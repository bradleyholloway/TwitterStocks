 General Motors Corp.: Saturn Plant in Tennessee To Pare Staff by 300 to 400   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 13, 2004  General Motors Corp. says it is cutting 300 to 400 jobs and slowing production at its Saturn assembly plant in Spring Hill, Tenn., because of slow sales of the Ion compact car. The auto maker told employees this week that it will reduce the number of work crews in the plant to two from three, effective Sept. 7.
