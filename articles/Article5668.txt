 Letters to the Editor: Edison Project: We're Still Committed   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 11, 1994  Recently, the media has had a field day passing along innuendoes that one such start-up venture, the Edison Project, is having trouble raising money or signing schools. The Wall Street Journal ("Education: Edison Project's Woes Scare More Deals Away," Oct. 24; "Philips Electronics Says It Wrote Off Whittle Investment," Oct. 25), the New York Times, and the New Yorker have suggested the Edison Project will not be able to meet its lofty goal of creating the kind of schools parents want: challenging, technologically sophisticated, safe -- and fun -- places where children and teachers work toward ambitious standards.
