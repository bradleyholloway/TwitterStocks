 Administration Is Again Asked to Begin Independent Inquiry into BNL Scandal   By Truell, Peter; Davidson, Joe, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1992  Sen Howard Metzenbaum (D-OH) and House Banking Committee Chairman Henry Gonzalez are among lawmakers requesting that independent counsel be appointed to review the Iraqi loan scandal at Banca Nazionale del Lavoro. The complaints come on the heels of embarassing new disclosures about the way the Bush administration has handled the case.
