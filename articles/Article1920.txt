 Science Institute Calls for Strong Moves To Curb Smoking Among Young People   By By Laurie McGinley, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 1994  The institute is the latest group to suggest focusing antismoking efforts on young people. "There is an emerging consensus that the emphasis needs to be on youth because it's so difficult to stop once you're addicted and because we're making very little progress, if any, with kids," said Michael Eriksen, director of the Office on Smoking and Health at the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention in Atlanta.
