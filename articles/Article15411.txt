 GM plans to recall million vehicles for collapsing seats, other safety issues   By Suris, Oscar, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1996  General Motors Corp said it is recalling more than a million vehicles to address several safety problems, including one involving collapsing seats that has been a problem before in GM vehicles. GM said 748,000 Chevrolet and GMC full-sized, extended-cab trucks from model years 1993 and 1994 may have seats that could recline suddenly. GM's other major recall involves seat belts.
