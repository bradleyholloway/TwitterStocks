 Bay Area -- Other Voices: Michael Chabon Takes In Region's Literary Scene   By White, Bobby, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 2010  The Bay Area's independent bookstores, writer's workshops and literary events have helped cultivate a vibrant community of authors, including Amy Tan and Tobias Wolff. Another active participant in the local literary scene is Michael Chabon, who has written 11 books including "The Yiddish Policemen's Union" and the Pulitzer Prize-winning "The Amazing Adventures of Kavalier & Clay."
