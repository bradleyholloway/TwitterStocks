 International Corporate Report: Elf Lifts Rhin-Rhone Bid; Unit Takes Nina Ricci Stake   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 1988  Elf, a French state-controlled oil company, raised its bid for Rhin-Rhone to 1,700 francs ($297.75) a share from 1,500 francs, topping an offer of 1,575 francs a share by Bollore. Elf's new offer also is above the 1,653 francs at which Rhin-Rhone shares have been suspended on Paris's stock exchange. Elf called its sweetened bid final.
