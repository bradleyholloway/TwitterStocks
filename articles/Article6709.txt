 Letters to the Editor: Hugo Chavez Is Following The Law -- Simple as That   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 11, 2003  I was dismayed to read your front-page article "As Nation Wobbles, Venezuelan Leader Tightens His Grip" (June 12) stating that President [Hugo Chavez] uses demagoguery and class warfare to control the political environment. The Venezuela Constitution (which was overwhelming ratified by the people in December 1999) sets the framework for a recall referendum, not President Hugo Chavez or the opposition.
