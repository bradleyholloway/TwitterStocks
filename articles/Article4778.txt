 Canadian Production Of Market Pulp Rose 3% in May From 1990   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1991  The Canadian Pulp and Paper Association, an industry group, said Canadian mills produced 566,000 metric tons of market pulp in May, compared with 550,000 metric tons a year earlier. The May 84% operating rate was up from 78% in April but down from 87% a year earlier.
