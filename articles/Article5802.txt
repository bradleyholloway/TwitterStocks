 Money-fund assets soar by $23.1 billion   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 1996  Money-market mutual-fund assets rose $23.1 billion to $787.1 billion in the week ended Jan 10, 1996. The increase was the largest in more than three years. ICI revised its asset total from the previous week to $764 billion. In the latest week, assets of 669 retail money-market funds increased by $14.06 billion to $547.78 billion.
