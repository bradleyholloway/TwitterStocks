 Banks Shift to Greener Policies   By Mincer, Jilian, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 2008  Today, some of the world's largest banks have embraced the issue by focusing on research on the impact of global warming on investments, cutting their own greenhouse emissions and funding clean-energy projects, according to a report released last week by Ceres, a coalition of investors, environmental groups and other public-interest organizations.
