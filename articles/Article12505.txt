 MIT Chooses Dublin To Host Counterpart Of Media Laboratory   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 1999  Massachusetts Institute of Technology and Ireland agreed to base MediaLab Europe, a counterpart to the MIT Media Laboratory, in Dublin.  Founded in 1985, the MIT Media Lab has attracted attention for its research into advanced technologies. To date, most funding has come from private industry. MIT said the collaboration marks the first time the Media Lab has agreed to help reproduce its research culture abroad.
