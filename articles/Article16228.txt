 Business Brief -- Bridgestone Corp.: Charges at European Unit Spur Downgrade of Profit Outlook   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 2002  Bridgestone Corp. cut its group net-profit outlook for the year to 40 billion yen ($320.9 million) from 70 billion yen due to restructuring charges at the tire maker's European operations. The Japanese company said it will post a loss of 35 billion yen to reorganize its European operations.
