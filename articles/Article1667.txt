 World News: Seoul Calls Out North Over Fire Exchange   By Gale, Alastair, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 2011  The North's state news agency cited an unidentified official in working-level military talks with the South as saying that South Korean authorities had spread "misinformation" that the North's army "perpetrated a shelling 'provocation.'"
