 New York Merc Gets $80 Million to Stay in the City   By Raghavan, Anita, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 13, 1992  Spurning a more attractive offer from New Jersey, the New York Mercantile Exchange's board unanimously voted on Nov 11, 1992 to relocate the exchange to 390 Greenwich St from Four World Trade Center by the second half of 1994. In return, the city and state will offer the Merc $80 million to develop the state-of-the-art trading floor.
