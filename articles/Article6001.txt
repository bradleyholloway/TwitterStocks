 Business Brief -- Red Herring Communications Inc.: Magazine Frequency Is Cut; Layoffs of 28 Workers Seen   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 2001  Red Herring Communications Inc. will publish its Red Herring magazine monthly instead of twice a month and will lay off 16% of its work force -- the latest piece of bad news in an industry hit hard by an advertising downturn. The New York company is laying off 28 of its 170 employees, mostly editorial staffers, a spokeswoman said.
