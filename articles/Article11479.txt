 Continental AG's Net Income Fell 59% to $54.3 Million in '90   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1991  Continental AG reported that 1990 net income plunged 59% to 93.4 million Deutsche marks ($54.3 million) from DM227.8 million in 1989. The German tire maker also slashed its dividend in half, to DM4 from DM8.
