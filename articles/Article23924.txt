 World News: Ivory Coast Leader Faces New Pressure to Quit   By Connors, Will; James, John, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 2011  The threat of military intervention in Ivory Coast by West African countries has faded, according to diplomats and analysts, so the delegation, this time of four leaders, will be offering Mr. Gbagbo expanded concessions, such as amnesty against prosecution for possible war crimes committed during the current standoff and exile in a foreign country.
