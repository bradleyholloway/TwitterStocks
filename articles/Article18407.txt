 Russian grain crisis flares up as bank presses U.S. for missed loan payments   By Kilman, Scott, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1993  The Russian grain crisis took a turn for the worse as the New York branch of French-based Banque Indosuez filed the first claim against the US government for grain loan payments missed by Russia. The USDA is obligated to repay the bank if Russia cannot.
