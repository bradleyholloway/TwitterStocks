 Hong Kong bank-deposit rates   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1994  A cartel of Hong Kong banks announced on Nov 19, 1994 an increase in local deposit interest rates to match the expecting tightening during the week of Nov 20, 1994 in the US, a move that includes a three-quarter-percentage-point rise in the Hong Kong dollar savings-deposit rates to 3.75%.
