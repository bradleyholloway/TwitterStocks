 Transport Boosts January Factory Orders 1.7%   By By Alejandro Bodipo-Memba, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1999  Factory orders rose 1.7% in January despite a drop in orders for nondurable goods.  The increase, the seventh in eight months, followed a 2.3% increase in December, according to the Commerce Department. But excluding transportation items, new orders fell 0.3% in January, the first drop since October. New orders were up 3.9% from the January 1998 level.
