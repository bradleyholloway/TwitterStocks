 J. Sainsbury PLC   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1993  J. Sainsbury PLC, one of Britain's biggest food retailers, reported an 11% increase in pretax profit for the 28 weeks to Sep 25, 1993. Pretax profit jumped to 434 million British pounds ($639.3 million), from BP391.1 million in the year-earlier period.
