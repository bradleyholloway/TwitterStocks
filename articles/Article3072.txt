 Gainsco CEO Macchia Resigns From Insurer; 1st-Quarter Loss Seen   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 1998  FORT WORTH, Texas -- Gainsco Inc. said founder Joseph D. Macchia "retired and resigned" as chairman, chief executive officer and president. The insurance holding concern also said it expects to report a first-quarter operating loss.  Gainsco said that Joel C. Puckett, 54 years old, is succeeding Mr. Macchia as chairman and that Glenn W. Anderson, 45, will become president and CEO. Mr. Anderson will also succeed Mr. Macchia as a director.
