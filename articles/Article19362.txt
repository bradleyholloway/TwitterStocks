 Ad Notes...   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 2001  In another discouraging sign for the country's newspapers, at least one big movie-theater chain is cutting back its subsidies of movie ads in newspapers. AMC Entertainment has told several independent film companies it intends to stop subsidizing their newspaper ads. Cutbacks with the bigger studios may be next. Typically, theaters pay studios a small percentage of box-office proceeds to help studios cover advertising costs.
