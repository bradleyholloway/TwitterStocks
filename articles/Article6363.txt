 Hong Kong Subway Tests IPO Market   By By Peter Wonacott, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 2000  The initial share offer of the Mass Transit Railway Corp., or MTRC, will represent the first time the Hong Kong government sells one of its major assets on the stock market. Hong Kong Financial Secretary Donald Tsang said it probably wouldn't be the last. "It sets the precedent for possible privatization of other public enterprises," he said after announcing the deal yesterday.
