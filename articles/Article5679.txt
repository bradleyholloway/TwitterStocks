 Dollar is seen staging slow, gradual climb   By Sesit, Michael R, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1994  A gradual rise for the US dollar is foreseen in 1995 due to a dynamic US economy, rising US interest rates and an inflation-fighting FRB. On Dec 9, 1994 in New York, the dollar stood at 1.5771 marks and 100.11 yen, compared to the previous trading day's DM1.5770 and Y100.45.
