 Tread Gingerly in Shanghai, U.S. Investors Are Warned   By Steiner, Robert, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1992  Caution is advised to US investors interested in China's Shanghai Securities Exchange. The market has plunged 49% since May 1992, which has some US investors questioning whether the time is right to buy. In world market activity on Oct 9, 1992, Tokyo's Nikkei 225-stock index lost 275.73 to 17059.78, London's Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-share index rose 2.4 points to 2541.2, and Frankfurt's DAX 30-stock index fell 11.46 points to 1439.66.
