 Small stock focus: Slump spreads as jitters grow about jobs data   By Mollison, Caitlin, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 2, 1994  Investor apprehension ahead of the Dec 2, 1994 release of Nov 1994 employment figures pushed small stocks down Dec 1 and the Russell 2000 Index of small capitalization stocks fell 2.22 points to 242.03. The Nasdaq Composite Index retreated 9.12 to 741.20.
