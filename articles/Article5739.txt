 Judge refuses to expedite Rockefeller Center case   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 1995  A federal bankruptcy-court judge in Manhattan refused a request by Rockefeller Center Properties Inc to expedite the bankruptcy process under which the landmark property's owner will attempt to reorganize. The REIT had asked the judge to shorten the 120-day period during which the debtor holds the exclusive right to present a plan for reorganization.
