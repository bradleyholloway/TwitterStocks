 Alliance to unveil on-line service for election news   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1995  ABC News, the Washington Post and Newsweek said they are teaming up to develop an on-line outpost for news about the 1996 election year on the Internet's World Wide Web. Dubbed ElectionLine, the service will allow users a wealth of news, photos, video and sound on all political races in 1996.
