 Royal Bank of Scotland Profit Gains 89% as Bad Loans Fall   By Carrick Mollenkamp, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 25, 2005  The bank's U.S. operations, including Citizens Financial Group Inc., continue to account for an increasing portion of profits. For 2004, the U.S. accounted for 25% of operating profit including the operations of Charter One Financial Inc., which was acquired by Citizens in August. In 1999, banking in the U.S. represented 10% of the bank's operating profit.
