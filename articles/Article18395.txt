 U.S. Shoe Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1992  U.S. Shoe Corp said sales at its retail operations fell 3% from a year earlier in the four weeks ended Nov 28, 1992, to $179.3 million. Same-store sales fell 1%. For the first 10 months, the company said sales fell 5.1% to $1.76 billion. On a same-store basis, sales fell 2.5%.
