 Germany Signals Its Commitment To Privatizations   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 1987  The West German government said it will sell its remaining 60% stake in Viag AG, an energy, chemicals and aluminum concern, sometime in 1988, an apparent signal that it intends to press ahead with denationalizations despite the expected delay in the sale of its 16% stake in Volkswagen AG.
