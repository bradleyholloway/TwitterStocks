 Corona Purchases 7% Of Bond International And May Boost Stake   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 1989  Corona Corp. said that it acquired a 7% equity stake in Bond International Gold Inc. and that it may increase its stake by a further 20% under an option granted by Bond International's controlling shareholder.  Corona, a major Toronto-based gold producer, said it acquired the $30 million stake, consisting of about four million Bond International ordinary shares and 800,000 ordinary share purchase warrants, for investment purposes. A Corona spokesman declined to elaborate.  Corona said its vice chairman, Timothy Hoare, will also join the board of Bond International, a gold mining concern controlled by Dallhold Investments Pty. Ltd. Dallhold is the family holding company of Australian financier Alan Bond, who is also Bond International's chairman.
