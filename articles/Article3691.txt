 Symbol Technologies Ex-Officer Pleads Guilty for Role in Fraud   By By Kara Scannell, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 20, 2003  Federal prosecutors alleged that Robert Korkuc, a former accounting executive until he left Symbol in March 2003, worked with other senior executives to manipulate earnings, revenues and expenses to meet Wall Street analyst estimates from 1997 until September 2002.  Mr. Korkuc is the second Symbol executive to agree to a deal with the government. In March, Robert Asti, a former vice president of sales, pleaded guilty to stuffing Symbol's distribution channel with phony orders at the end of each quarter to meet revenue and earnings targets.
