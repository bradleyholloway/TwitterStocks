 Warrant Is Issued For Seller on EBay Over Alleged Fraud   By By Nick Wingfield, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 2002  An affidavit by the Federal Bureau of Investigation in support of the warrant alleges that Mr. [Stewart C. Richardson], working out of a shop in White Lake, Mich., defrauded dozens of eBay bidders across the country who sent him money during the first weeks of January for collectible figurines that Mr. Richardson never delivered as promised. Mr. Richardson's activities were described in a page one story in The Wall Street Journal last month.
