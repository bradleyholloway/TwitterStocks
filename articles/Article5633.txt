 Australian Bank Joins China Agency In Mining Venture   By By S. Karene Witcher, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1994  SYDNEY, Australia -- The state agency that controls China's nonferrous mining industry is teaming up with an Australian bank to help finance mining projects in China.  While the Chinese agency provides an entree to China's nonferrous mines and related industries such as smelters, Macquarie Bank will bring to the venture its expertise in resource finance. The new venture's mandate includes such services as helping China determine the economic viability of its mines and arranging project financing.
