 Delinquencies Rose Slightly for Mortgages In '97 Fourth Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 1998  Mortgage delinquencies in the fourth quarter of 1997 rose slightly as holders of adjustable-rate mortgages found themselves in a costly interest-rate environment. The Mortgage Bankers Association of America said its delinquency survey of mortgages on one-to-four unit homes rose to 4.36% from 4.26% in the third quarter.
