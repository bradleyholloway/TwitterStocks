 Loewen Group Inc.: Acquisition Offer Is Planned for Toronto's Arbor Capital   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 13, 1992  Loewen Group Inc plans to offer 101 million Canadian dollars ($84.5 million), or C$13 a share, to acquire Arbor Capital Inc. Arbor Chairman Daniel Scanlan, who owns the controlling block of shares, reportedly has no intention of accepting the offer.
