 Share Prices Increase In London and Tokyo --- A Wall Street Journal News Roundup   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1986  London share prices rose in fairly active trading yesterday, buoyed by a better-than-expected figure for British retail sales. Tokyo stocks rose in a slow session.  In London, the Financial Times industrial share index increased 11.3 points, to 1228.7. The gain was the index's first since last Tuesday.  Prices were aided by the Department of Trade and Industry's announcement that British retail sales, in terms of volume, rose 3.6% in June from May, up from the 3.2% increase reported earlier.
