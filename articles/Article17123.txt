 Pharmacy Business Benefits CVS   By Dagher, Veronica, The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 2010  CVS Caremark Corp.'s first-quarter profit climbed 4.5% because of stronger sales at its pharmacy business, though a mild flu season and severe winter weather in some markets weakened results, especially sales of nonpharmaceutical products. Because of the company's performance, CVS raised the low end of its full-year earnings guidance by three cents a share.
