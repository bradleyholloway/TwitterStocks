 Goodyear Tire Sets '89 Capital Spending Totaling $760 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 1989  AKRON, Ohio -- Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co. plans capital expenditures of about $760 million for this year, up sharply from $492 million in 1988, the tire maker said in its recently released annual report.  Goodyear said the sum for 1989 will accommodate projects such as construction of the company's previously announced radial-tire plant in Napanee, Ontario, as well as other plant expansions and modernizations.
