 Canada Market Pulp August Output Rose 3% From Year Ago   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 1988  Canadian production of market pulp rose 3% in August from a year earlier, as the industry operated at 105% of capacity.  The Canadian Pulp and Paper Association, an industry group, said Canadian mills produced 655,000 metric tons of market pulp in August, compared with 634,000 tons in August 1987. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.62 pounds.
