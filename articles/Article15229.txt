 Defiance Chief Sees More Growth in Sales And Profit, New Pacts   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 1994  Defiance Inc. had a strong fourth quarter, ending the June 30 fiscal year poised for further sales and earnings growth, said Jerry A. Cooper, president and chief executive officer.  Mr. Cooper's fourth-quarter estimate indicates fiscal 1994 operating earnings in the area of 80 cents a share, up about 50% from fiscal 1993 net of $3.4 million, or 54 cents a share. In fiscal 1994, the company also will report a gain of nine cents a share from a change in accounting for income taxes made in the first quarter.
