 Pratt Hotel Merges With Drew National To Get Casino Stake   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1985  Drew National Corp. and Pratt Hotel Corp. said they completed the previously proposed merger of Pratt into Drew National. The surviving corporation will operate under the name Pratt Hotel Corp.  The transaction completed a complicated process enabling Drew National of White Plains, N.Y., to acquire a major interest in the Sands Hotel & Casino in Atlantic City, N.J. The hotel and casino had been 80%-owned by the Pratt family of Dallas.
