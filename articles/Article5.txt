 Budget Surplus In April Climbed To $12.37 Billion   The Wall Street Journal,  May 24, 1985  The federal government recorded a $12.37 billion budget surplus in April, slightly higher than the $11.49 billion surplus a year earlier, the Treasury said.  In its monthly statement, the Treasury said federal government receipts totaled $94.59 billion in April, compared with $80.18 billion a year earlier and $49.61 billion in March 1985. Federal outlays totaled $82.23 billion last month, compared with $68.69 billion a year earlier and $78.07 billion in March.
