 White House Seeks $300 Million in Aid To 4 Latin Nations   By By Robert S. Greenberger, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1987  The Reagan administration proposed an additional $300 million in economic aid for four Central American nations, in a move to help ensure smooth sailing on Capitol Hill for a final $40 million installment of $100 million of previously approved assistance for the Nicaraguan Contras.
