 Business Brief -- INMET MINING CORP.: Share Buyback is Planned To Avert Bid From Zemex   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 6, 1998  Inmet Mining Corp said it plans an offer to buy back as much as 63% of its common shares outstanding in a move aimed at thwarting a 517 million Canadian dollar (US$341 million) merger proposal from Zemex Corp.
