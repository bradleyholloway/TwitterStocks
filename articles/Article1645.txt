 SmartMoney Preview: Charity 'Cashathons': High-Cost Fund Raising   By Kadet, Anne, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 2011  To stand out in a crowded marketplace, many charities have made their events more elaborate, with skydiving jumps, hot-coal walks and icy "polar plunges" increasingly taking their place alongside old-fashioned walks and fun runs. Kim Irish, director of programming for Breast Cancer Action, an advocacy group, says the time and money invested could be better directed toward traditional volunteer efforts or direct donations.
