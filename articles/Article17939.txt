 Corporate Report: Royal Dutch/Shell Group   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1989  The Anglo-Dutch oil giant said earnings fell to $1.26 billion in the quarter from $1.43 billion a year earlier. In the latest quarter, the group's chemical unit had a #67 million ($115.4 million) charge for environmental protection measures in the U.S., while a year earlier it had a #125 million gain from the sale of its 49% share in Akzo Consumenten Produtken, a consumer-products business.
