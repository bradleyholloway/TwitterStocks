 FCC to charge 'pioneer' firms for their licenses   By Carnevale, Mary Lu, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 1994  The FCC took back its promise to give Mobile Telecommunications Technologies Corp a free license to offer an advanced paging and messaging service, and said the company must pay millions of dollars for it. The FCC is also likely to require hefty payments from Cox Enterprises Inc, Omnipoint Corp and American Personal Communications Inc.
