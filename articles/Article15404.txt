 Washington wire: Internal wrangling   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 1996  Internal wrangling is continuing in Washington amid budget-talk tensions. Some House Democrats see the hand of President Clinton's adviser Richard Morris in GOP Sen Trent Lott's attacks on Clinton aides Leon Panetta and George Stephanopoulos as obstructers of a deal. The Family Research Council's Marty Dannenfellser said that Senate Majority Leader Robert Dole, by urging the reopening of the federal government, "played right into the hands of Clinton."
