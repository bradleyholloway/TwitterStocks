On a hot Sunday afternoon in August 1978, five teen-age boys played basketball on a court in south Newark, went home for dinner and went out again. The five, all good friends 16 or 17 years old, have been missing ever since, as though swallowed by the sultry night.  Randy Johnson spent that Sunday, Aug. 20, visiting friends before playing basketball. He was seen walking down Fabyan Place in Newark around 7 P.M. Then he vanished.
