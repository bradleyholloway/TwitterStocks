 Volkswagen AG: Early retirement package will be extended to 2,500   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 1995  Volkswagen AG will be offering about 2,500 of its employees early retirement in an agreement reached in 1994 known as the "generational contract." An earlier offer, which was offered to employees born in 1933, will be extended to those born in 1940.
