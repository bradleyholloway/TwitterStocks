 Business Brief -- JOHNS MANVILLE CORP.: This One Is a Brief Headline Certain Polyester Lines Of Hoechst to Be Bought   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 1998  Johns Manville Corp said it agreed to acquire the Spunbond/Monofilament operations of the polyester business of Hoechst Group AG, a Frankfurt company with interests in the health-care, agriculture and chemicals industries.
