 WEEKEND JOURNAL; Food & Drink -- Eating Out: A New Shell Game; Endangered in the wild, farmed abalone proves to be delicious   By Sokolov, Raymond, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 2008  In a new year beset by bad news about a political murder, the falling dollar and the rising price of oil, here's a heartwarming story in which even the appetite of a newly rich China for a limited natural resource plays a positive role. At the ambitious neo-Catalan Manresa in Los Gatos, Calif., for a supplement of $10 on the four-course $95 tasting menu, Chef David Kinch offers abalone and slow egg, gem lettuce and bread crusts.
