 Danner retracts offer to resume Shoney's post   By de Lisser, Eleena, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 1995  Raymond L. Danner, the former chairman and CEO of Shoney's Inc, withdrew his offer to return to the company's top post. Danner's decision came only nine days after he offered his services to the restaurant chain. Danner is still the restaurant chain's largest shareholder.
