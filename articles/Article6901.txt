 U.S. News: Former Diplomat to Lead MacArthur Foundation   By Spector, Mike, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 2009  Mr. Gallucci, a former assistant secretary of state who led efforts to disarm Iraq after the Persian Gulf War and spearheaded negotiations during the 1994 North Korean nuclear crisis, takes the helm of a Chicago institution beset by investment losses similar to those suffered by foundations across the U.S. The MacArthur foundation, one of the U.S.'s largest philanthropies, ended 2008 with about $5.2 billion of investment assets, down about 25% from $6.9 billion a year earlier.
