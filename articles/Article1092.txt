 National Education's Third-Period Net Rose 95% to $12.5 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1988  IRVINE, Calif. -- National Education Corp. said third-quarter net income jumped 95% to $12.5 million, or 44 cents a share, on a 9% revenue rise to $103 million. The company cited its acquisition of Advanced Systems Inc.
