 New Stock Listings   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 1987  On-Line Software International Inc., based in Fort Lee, N.J., will begin trading today on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol OSI. The developer and marketer of software for International Business Machines Corp. previously traded over the counter.  Tech-Sym Corp., a Houston-based electronics and aerospace concern, will begin trading today on the Big Board under the symbol TSY. The company previously traded on the American Stock Exchange.  Tandem Computers Inc., Cupertino, Calif., will begin trading tomorrow on the Big Board under the symbol TDM. The computer company previously traded over the counter.
