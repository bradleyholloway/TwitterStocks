 Cable: A cable empire that was built on a tax break   By Robichaux, Mark, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 1995  Using a federal tax break known as the minority tax certificate, Frank Washington has built a cable TV empire that will become one of the US's 10 largest cable systems if a $2.2 billion acquisition of cable systems owned by Viacom Inc goes through. A profile of Washington is presented.
