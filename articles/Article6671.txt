 Quotation System For Corporate Bonds Adds More Issues   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 11, 2003  The NASD said the addition concludes "Phase Two" of NASD's Trade Reporting and Compliance Engine, or TRACE, program. On March 3, NASD raised the number of bonds for which trading prices are disclosed to some 4,200.  TRACE was introduced in July to improve price discovery and transparency in the corporate-bond market by requiring that trades be reported to the NASD.  NASD also said that pricing and trade information for the newly added bonds will continue regardless of any ratings changes, including a downgrade to junk.
