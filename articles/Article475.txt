Julie Alma Mendoza, daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Irwin C. Mendoza of New York and Locust, N.J., was married in the Cosmopolitan Club yesterday to David Joseph Pavlucik, son of Mr. and Mrs. Andrew J. Pavlucik of Stratford, Conn. The ceremony was performed by the Rev. Robert F. Pitcock, a Dutch Reformed minister.
