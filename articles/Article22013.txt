 Investor Steven Green plans to acquire a 40% stake in Canada's Bramalea Ltd.   By Greenberg, Larry M, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 1995  New Jersey investor Steven Green plans to add to his expanding international real-estate portfolio by acquiring about a 40% stake in Canada's struggling Bramalea Ltd for 300 million Canadian dollars ($213.1 million). The acquisition is conditioned in part on Bramalea's receiving agreements from holders of C$600 million of bank debt to convert their loans into equity.
