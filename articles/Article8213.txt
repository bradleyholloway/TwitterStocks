 Legal Beat: Test Drilling for Diamonds   By Hayes, Arthur S; Charlier, Marj, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 1992  A federal appeals court in St Louis ruled that test drilling for diamonds in a state park is permissible and that commercial use of a public recreation area isn't prohibited so long as it is temporary and "minimal." The case pitted the Sierra Club against mining companies.
