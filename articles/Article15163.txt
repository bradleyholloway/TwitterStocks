 MCA to build park in Japan featuring films   By Turner, Richard; Hamilton, David P, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 1994  MCA Inc plans to develop and help operate a "Universal Studios Japan" theme park in Osaka. The park, costing between $1 billion and $2 billion and slated to open in the spring of 1999, would be owned by several Japanese landowners and corporations, including Matsushita Electric Industrial Co.
