 World Bank's Issue In Euroyen Market Gets Modest Demand --- Special to The wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 11, 1987  Nomura International Ltd. appeared to fall short of its goal of reopening the volatile market for new Euroyen bonds, as an issue of 50 billion yen ($354 million) by the World Bank stirred only modest demand.  But participation was limited, as many traders and investors awaited today's release of the U.S. merchandise trade figure for July. The number, which analysts expect to show about a $15.6 billion deficit, compared with a $15.7 billion gap in June, may be critical to the near-term prospects for the dollar and the dollar-denominated bond markets.
