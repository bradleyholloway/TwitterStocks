 Metallgesellschaft's Buderus Distances Itself From Parent ---- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 1994  WETZLAR, Germany -- Buderus AG, a German heating and cooling technology company, on Friday tried to distance itself from the financial troubles plaguing parent company Metallgesellschaft AG, 80% owner of publicly traded Buderus.  Saying it wants to "minimize misunderstandings," Buderus released a statement saying it has "no contractual ties" to Metallgesellschaft that could interfere with the independence of Buderus or its management. "The current problems at Metallgesellschaft therefore also have no influence on the soundness, liquidity and earnings capacity of Buderus."
