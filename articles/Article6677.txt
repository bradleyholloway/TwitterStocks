 Restaurants Brief -- McDonald's Corp.: Same-Store Sales Fall 3.7%; Traffic Lower Than Expected   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 11, 2003  McDonald's Corp. said its March sales rose 5%, but traffic was lower than expected and same-store sales fell 3.7% in the month. The Oak Brook, Ill., restaurant company posted March systemwide sales of $3.7 billion. But McDonald's total sales fell 1% in March when using constant currencies.
