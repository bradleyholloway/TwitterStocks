 Legal Beat: Drexel Ex-Employees Say Milken Favored His Team in Settling Suit   By Moses, Jonathan M, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1992  Some former employees of Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc are complaining that Michael R. Milken is disproportionately favoring his former colleagues in the Beverly Hills CA junk bond office in determining how much each employee will have to pay to settle a $1.3 billion settlement of lawsuits brought by damaged parties against the company. The group has filed a complaint with US District Judge Milton Pollack stating their grievances.
