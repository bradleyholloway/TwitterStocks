 Business Brief -- TITAN CORP.: DBA Systems to Be Bought For $37 Million in Stock   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 1998  Titan Corp, a designer of systems for battlefield communications and control, said it agreed to acquire DBA Systems Inc, a maker of digital devices for battleground information, for about $37 million in stock. Under the deal, Titan will exchange 1.37 of its shares for each of the approximately 4.4 million DBA shares outstanding.
