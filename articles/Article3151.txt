 Letters to the Editor: Donald Regan Served His Country Superbly   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 13, 2003  The son of a railroad worker, [Donald Regan] served his country honorably for five years in World War II as a Marine, with action in several major battles in the South Pacific. Retiring as a lieutenant colonel, Regan then spent 34 years transforming Merrill Lynch into the pre-eminent brokerage house on Wall Street.
