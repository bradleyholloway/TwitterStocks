 Ad Notes...   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 2005  In an effort to promote the 50th anniversary of Disneyland in Anaheim, Calif., Walt Disney gave away a free ticket to one of its 11 theme parks to all 72,000 ticket holders who attended last night's Orange Bowl, the college-football championship game in Miami between the University of Southern California and the University of Oklahoma. The hitch: All fans had to scream "I'm going to Disneyland!" during the third quarter.
