 American Standard Sets Leveraged Buyout Of Mosler Safe Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1986  American Standard Inc. said it signed a letter of intent to sell its Mosler Safe Co. division to a new company that includes Mosler management.  Mosler, a Hamilton, Ohio-based maker of security equipment, has about 3,000 employees. American Standard, which had 1985 sales of $2.85 billion, said it doesn't break out unit sales.
