 Employers Take a Closer Look at GICs and Alternatives amid Insurers' Woes   By White, James A; Schultz, Ellen E, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 1991  As a way to safeguard employees' 401(k), profit sharing and other tax-deferred retirement plans that are heavily invested in insurance companies' guaranteed investment contracts, or GICs, employers are moving into higher quality GICs and diversifying GIC holdings. Other alternatives employers are turning to are discussed.
