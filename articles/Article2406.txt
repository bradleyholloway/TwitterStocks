 Labor Secretary Denies Claims Of Corruption   By By Glenn Burkins, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1998  Ms. Herman said she understood that the government was obliged to investigate the matter. "I accept that as part of this job," she said in a statement she delivered on the steps of the Labor Department yesterday. However, she added, "I want you to know that these allegations are not true," and vowed not to let the inquiry distract her from official duties. It was Ms. Herman's first public statement since news of the investigation came to light, and she refused to answer questions.
