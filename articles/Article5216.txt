 Japan's Calsonic Corp. expects to post rise in fiscal-year profit   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 1995  Calsonic Corp, a Japanese auto-parts company that is one of Nissan Motor Co's largest suppliers, expects to report pretax profit for the parent company of 4.24 billion yen ($47.2 million) in the fiscal year ending Mar 31, 1994, an increase of 44% over the Y2.95 billion reported a year earlier.
