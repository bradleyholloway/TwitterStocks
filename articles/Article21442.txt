 Henley Group Plans Spin Off of Biggest Units   By Bulkeley, William M, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 1990  In a continuing effort to increase shareholder value, Henley Group Inc plans to spin off subsidiaries Fischer Scientific Group Inc and Pneumo Abex Corp in tax-free distributions to shareholders. Chairman and CEO Michael D. Dingman said that Fischer and Pneumo could sell for at least $350 million each.
