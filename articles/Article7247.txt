 Fed's 'Operation Twist' Tangles Treasury Trade   By Zeng, Min, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 2012  The average total value of "sell" offers submitted in those auctions by primary-dealer banks, which trade directly with the Fed and underwrite Treasury debt sales, fell 27% from October to January, to $5.47 billion, according to data compiled by George Goncalves and Ankit Sahni, rate strategists at Nomura Securities.
