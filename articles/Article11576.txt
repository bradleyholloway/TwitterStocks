 Focus on Franchising: It Was a Nightmare   By Tannenbaum, Jeffrey A, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 1992  In Jan 1992, TV-set distributor Curtis Mathes Corp abruptly cut off shipments to some 550 dealers, most franchisees, citing problems financing warranties. But Stratos Technologies Corp, seeking to buy Curtis Mathes, says that franchisees are doing better now that shipments are going again.
