 HSAs Won't Benefit Those Who Need Insurance Most   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 2006  Your Feb. 21 editorial "Big Health-Care Ideas" noted that the impact of President Bush's proposed health-care reforms would be "profound." You're not whistling "Dixie." Health Savings Accounts would profoundly increase healthcare costs for many consumers, lead others to ration necessary care, and increase the number of uninsured.
