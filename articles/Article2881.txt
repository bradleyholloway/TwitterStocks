 American opinion: What money just can't buy   By Graham, Ellen, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 20, 1996  The spending and saving habits of Americans, as well as their anxiety about economic security, are examined using data from a Sep 1996 Wall Street Journal poll. Only 18% of respondents say they make enough to live comfortably, versus 25% in 1989. And 26% live paycheck to paycheck, up from 20%.
