 Business Brief -- KAISER ALUMINUM CORP.: Strikers' Offer Is Rejected; Lockout Looms at 5 Plants   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1999  Kaiser Aluminum Corp declined an offer from 3,000 striking factory workers to return to work and said it would impose a lockout "to support its bargaining position." The company said it would continue to operate the five plants affected by the strike, located in Louisiana, Ohio and Washington, with replacements and managers as it has since the strike began in Sep 1998.
