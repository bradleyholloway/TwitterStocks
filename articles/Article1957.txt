 Kiwi school of economics   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 1994  An editorial notes that the OECD has just released a glowing report on the economy of New Zealand, where the economy grew by 5% in 1993, and is expected to grow by 4% in 1994 and 1995. The editorial says there may be hope for countries to prosper without giving their people crash courses in Confucianism.
