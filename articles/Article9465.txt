 Business Brief -- Alcoa Inc.: Moody's Cuts Debt Rating Despite Cost-Cutting Efforts   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 2002  Alcoa Inc., which recently idled and closed plants due to sluggish aluminum demand, failed to ward off a ratings downgrade from Moody's Investors Service. The ratings firm cut Alcoa senior-unsecured debt rating to A2 from A1.
