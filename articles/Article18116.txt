 Abreast of the Market: Friday's Market Activity   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 1990  Stocks closed higher in the slowest session of the year on Aug 31, 1990. The Dow Jones Industrial Average gained 21.04 to 2614.36, bringing its rise for the week ended Aug 31 to 81.46 points, or 3.2%.
