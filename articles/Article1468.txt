 Saudis Sign Loan Accord with Banks for $3.6 Billion   The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 1991  Saudi Arabia has signed a $3.6 billion loan agreement with a group of foreign banks, its first international borrowing in over 20 years. The loan was arranged by Morgan Guaranty Trust Co, and is expected to be used to pay part of the $13.5 billion pledged to the US for help in the Persian Gulf War.
