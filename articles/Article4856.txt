 In-Store Advertising Plans to Restructure Terms of Leases, Loan   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 1992  In-Store Advertising Inc. said it signed a letter of intent for a proposed restructuring of equipment leases and a term loan, and reported a wider fourth-quarter loss.  The restructuring involves about $14.1 million of lease and loan obligations. The provider of electronic signs had suspended payments under the leases and loan in October 1991.
