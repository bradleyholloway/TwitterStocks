 Recovery Fires Up For Steel Industry   By Robert Guy Matthews, The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 2010  At Welspun's Little Rock operations, the large-diameter pipe maker said that it has a backlog of orders until 2012 thanks to a flurry of new pipelines being built across the U.S. Energy companies are building pipelines in anticipation of the country's growing energy needs several years from now, said Joel Johnson, vice president of North and South America sales.
