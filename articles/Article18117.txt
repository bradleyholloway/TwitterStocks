 Xerox Unit Is Selling Auto Insurer to GM, Puts Another on Block   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 1990  CRUM & FORSTER Inc. stopped writing in April all personal insurance lines, including auto policies. In yesterday's edition, it was incorrectly stated that the company stopped writing all insurance lines.  BASKING RIDGE, N.J. -- Crum & Forster Inc. said it intends to get out of the automobile insurance business, selling one unit to General Motors Corp. and putting another on the block.
