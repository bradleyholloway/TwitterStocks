 International Corporate Report [4]  The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 1986  FUJI HEAVY INDUSTRIES LTD. of Japan, the maker of Subaru cars, said it is negotiating with Vespa Co., a Taiwanese motorcycle maker, for joint production of a minicar. The plans under consideration would establish a joint-venture company in Taiwan to be capitalized at the equivalent of $29 million.
