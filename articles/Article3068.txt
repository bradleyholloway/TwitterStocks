 Letters to the Editor: Democrats Must Face Facts   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 2002  As for the claim that the number of seats picked up by the GOP is relatively meaningless: In an election with fewer than 30 competitive matches, in which it is assumed one side will lose based on history, any win is an upset. Had the Democrats picked up a single seat in the Senate we would be hearing how this was a massive refutation of the Bush agenda.
