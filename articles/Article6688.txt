 Tom Herman's Tax Tip: Beware Bogus IRS Emails   By Herman, Tom, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 2007  There are even more fraudulent schemes floating around. "Still another email claims the IRS's 'anti-fraud commission' is investigating their tax returns," the IRS warns.  IRS officials emphasize that they don't send out unsolicited emails or ask for someone's detailed personal and financial information. They also say the IRS "never" asks people for PIN numbers, passwords or similar secret access information for a credit card, bank or other financial account.
