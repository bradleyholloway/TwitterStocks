 Ford Motor's Visteon Buys Plastic Omnium In $475 Million Deal   The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1999  DEARBORN, Mich. -- Visteon Automotive Systems, Ford Motor Co.'s auto-parts unit, has acquired the interior division of French firm Cie. Plastic Omnium in a move that propels Visteon into a leading position in Europe.  As expected, Visteon is buying the division for $475 million. The acquisition is expected to be finalized by the end of June. Plastic Omnium's board approved the deal Monday night.
