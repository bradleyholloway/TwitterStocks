 U.S. News: Hints of Stability Emerge in Fragile Financial System   By Perry, Joellen; Hilsenrath, Jon, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 2009  In testimony to Congress Tuesday, Federal Reserve Chairman Ben Bernanke noted that a variety of credit indicators have improved in recent weeks, a sign that some of the Fed's rescue programs are working as planned. Since the start of the year, companies world-wide have sold $264.4 billion of investment-grade corporate bonds that aren't guaranteed through a government program, according to research firm Dealogic.
