 'Saturday Night Fever' dances up a big storm on Mexican television   By Millman, Joel, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 1996  "Saturday Night Fever" is catching on in a big way in Mexico. It is such a hit that rival Mexican networks are going to court over the right to broadcast the 1977 disco flick. In one corner is Grupo Televisa, while Television Azteca stands in the other--in a battle for the rights of the polyester pic.
