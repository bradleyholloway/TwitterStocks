 Smog Swapping: New Rules Harness Power of Free Markets to Curb Air Pollution   By Taylor, Jeffrey, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 1992  The environmental protection arena is treading on new ground as the laws adapt to meet the needs of companies in a market-based environmental compliance system. Mobil Oil Corp's operations in Torrance CA are one of the first to acquire pollution "credits" from a nearby city. Commodity exchanges such as the CBOT are competing to operate legally sanctioned markets that trade such pollution "rights." The reforms underway, and reaction to them, are described.
