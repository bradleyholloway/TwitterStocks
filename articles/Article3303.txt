 Morgan Stanley, SEC in Talks   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 2004  The proposed resolution wouldn't involve a monetary penalty, make fraud allegations, or require financial restatements, Morgan Stanley said in a quarterly report filed with the SEC.  Morgan Stanley also said the SEC is conducting an investigation of the fees charged by some of the company's mutual funds. The company said the investigation revolves around "the amount of fees charged by certain Morgan Stanley index funds, as well as the process by which those fees were determined."
