 Business Economists Revise Estimate for '89 Growth   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 1988  A national panel of economic forecasters said it believes the economy will grow at a slightly quicker pace in 1989 than it predicted three months ago.  But the panel of the National Association of Business Economists still expects the economy to slow significantly: from the 3.8% growth projected for 1988 to 2.5% for 1989.
