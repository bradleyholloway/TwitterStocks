 Clean, cozy rooms for rent--Please don't tell anyone   By Bigness, Jon, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1996  "Outlaw" bed and breakfasts, those that do not hang out signs and can't be found in Yellow Pages or city lodging directories, number in the thousands. At a time when hotel occupancy rates are making big-city hotel rooms difficult to find, the B&B industry as a whole is enjoying a resurgence. An article examines this segment of the service industry.
