 Walt Disney Net Increased 56% In Fourth Quarter   By By Kathleen A. Hughes, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 1989  BURBANK, Calif. -- Walt Disney Co. reported a 56% jump in fiscal fourth-quarter net income, reflecting record attendance at its theme parks and strong sales of home videos such as "Bambi."  Disney said net for the quarter ended Sept. 30 was $212.7 million, or $1.54 a share, compared with $136.4 million, or 99 cents a share, a year ago. Revenue rose 33% to $1.35 billion from $1 billion.
