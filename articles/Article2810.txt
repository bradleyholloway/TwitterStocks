 Business Brief -- Burlington Industries Inc.: Moody's Lowers Its Ratings On $900 Million in Debt   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 2001  Moody's Investors Service lowered the ratings on $900 million in debt of Burlington Industries Inc., Greensboro, N.C., citing weak operating performance in most of its businesses, and said the outlook is stable for the textile and apparel maker.
