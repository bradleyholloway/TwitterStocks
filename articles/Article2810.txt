 Economy is 'on track,' Greenspan says   By Wilke, John R, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 1996  FRB Chairman Alan Greenspan on Feb 20, 1996 told a House Banking subcommittee that the economy, despite a recent slowdown, is "on track." Greenspan also said that, in retrospect, he would not have changed the course of Fed policy over the past two years. Anticipating that the Fed would not cut interest rates further, the stock and bond markets fell as Greenspan spoke.
