 Morgan Stanley, Crescent team up to buy Woodlands   By Templin, Neal; Fritsch, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 13, 1997  Crescent Real Estate Equities Co and a Morgan Stanley real-estate fund said they are teaming up to buy the Woodlands Corp, which owns a sprawling residential and business development north of Houston, for $543 million in cash from Mitchell Energy & Development Corp.
