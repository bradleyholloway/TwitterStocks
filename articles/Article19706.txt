 More Workers Are Set To Lift 401(k) Levels   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 2003  This year, the stock market's rally -- the broad Standard & Poor's 500-stock index has gained more than 20% -- has helped beef up the average American's 401(k) plan to $51,500 at the end of October, from $44,000 at the end of 2002, Fidelity said.
