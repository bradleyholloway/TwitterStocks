 System Software sees likely revenue drop; Stock price falls 29%   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1996  Chicago-based System Software Associates Inc announced on Aug 8, 1996 that it anticipates declining third-quarter revenues, and on Aug 9, investors hacked another 29% from its share price, as the stock plunged $3.563 to $8.75 on Nasdaq trading volume of 2.1 million.
