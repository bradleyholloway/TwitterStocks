 Vietnam Carriers Lease Planes Built in the U.S.   By Carey, Susan, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1992  The increasing practice of US aircraft being leased for use in Vietnam, despite the fact that a US economic embargo restricts such activity, is examined. Recently, US export regulations were amended to make it easier for foreign airlines or lessors to provide US-built or US-equipped jetliners to Vietnam.
