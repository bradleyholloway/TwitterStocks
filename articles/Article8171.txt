 Washington Insight: Amid Peace Talks, Specter of Terrorism Will Return to Haunt President Bush   By Seib, Gerald F, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1991  Families of terrorism victims plan to assemble in Washington DC during the week of Oct 7, 1991 to criticize the Bush administration for failing to make terrorism more of an issue in its efforts for a Middle East peace conference. Meanwhile, signs of radical opposition to US intervention in the Mideast region have created worries that further terrorism could be forthcoming.
