 Small-Stock Focus: Indexes Keep Rising as Guess and Cytogen Jump   By By Cynthia Schreiber, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 2003  The Russell 2000 Index of small stocks finished up 5.46 points, or 1.2%, to 456.69. The Standard & Poor's SmallCap 600 Index gained 2.23 points, or 1%, to 222.15 The Nasdaq Composite Index of large- and small-caps advanced 11.36 points, or 0.7%, to 1646.01.  Chip companies edged higher ahead of Intel's mid-second-quarter update, with Asyst Technologies advancing 44 cents, or 6%, to 7.93, Cirrus Logic jumping 21 cents, or 5.3%, to 4.20, and Kulicke & Soffa climbing 76 cents, or 11%, to 7.46.  Cooper Cos. (NYSE) lost 2, or 5.5%, to 34.07, pressured by Baird's stock downgrade to neutral from outperform, after the Lake Forest, Calif., maker of health-care products turned in second-quarter sales that missed the firm's estimate.
