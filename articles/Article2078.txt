 Trimble Navigation Ltd.: Net income showed a rise of 22% in the third period   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 13, 1995  Trimble Navigation Ltd said 1995 third-quarter net rose 22% to $4.3 million, from $3.6 million in the year-earlier quarter. Revenue was up 35%, to $62.8 million from $46.7 million. The global-positioning satellite concern said a change in product mix lowered overall gross margins in the quarter.
