 Heard on the street: Auto stocks may be shifting into high gear, with lower rates possibly down the road   By Templin, Neal, The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1995  Declining interest rates and the likelihood of trade sanctions on Japanese imports sent shares of Ford Motor, General Motors and Chrysler soaring early in the week of May 8, 1995. Analysts are mixed in their forecasts, but generally see rising share prices if interest rates continue to drop.
