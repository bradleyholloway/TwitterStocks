 Tip of the Week: Grab a CD And Hang On   By Badal, Jaclyne, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 2006  Meanwhile, savers can still lock in 5% yields on five-year CDs, although such offers are becoming rarer. As of Friday, E-Loan's five- year CD had an annualized yield of 5.75% with a $10,000 minimum investment, according to Bankrate.com. Investors with only $1,000 to spare could get 5.48% at First Internet Bank of Indiana and 5.45% at Bank of Internet USA. Allied First Bank in Naperville, Ill., was offering 5.3% with a $500 minimum.
