 Dividend News: Illinois Power Says Proposed Rate Cut Threatens Payout   By By Bill Richards, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 1988  Illinois Power Co. said a proposed 9.1% cut in its electric rates would force it to cancel its common share dividend and jeopardize service to new customers.  The testimony, filed with the Illinois Commerce Commission, was the Decatur, Ill.-based utility's first formal admission that a proposed $79 million cut in electric rates would require it to suspend 66-cent-a-share dividend payments to common shareholders.
