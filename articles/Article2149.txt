 Business Brief -- CAMBIOR INC.: New Study Sharply Boosts Copper Reserves in Peru   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1996  Cambior Inc., Montreal, said a new study shows that proven and probable mining reserves at its La Granja copper mine in Peru are now estimated at 1.3 billion metric tons of ore, with 0.61% copper, representing nearly 16 billion pounds of copper.
