 Coca-Cola Signs Deal To Use go2 Systems Wireless Directory   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 2000  As part of the deal, Coke's customers get listed in go2's database. Coke executives say it was easier to provide the services for its customers, instead of each customer signing up for go2 services separately. "We can get to a lot of locations in a hurry," says Tom Moore, senior vice president and general manager of Coke's fountain business.
