 J.W. Gant Is Fined For Alleged Misdeeds In Penny-Stock Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1990  The National Association of Securities Dealers fined J.W. Gant & Associates Inc., an Englewood, Colo., brokerage firm, $30,000 and ordered it and two employees to return nearly $196,000 to customers because of alleged overcharges for securities.  Without admitting or denying wrongdoing, the firm; Salvatore A. Venezia, a principal and vice president at Gant; and James S. Gad, a representative and trader at the firm, consented to the sanctions.
