 McDonnell Douglas Unit Wins Navy Job Of $146.7 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1985  McDonnell Aircraft Co., a unit of McDonnell Douglas Corp., received a $146.7 million Navy contract to produce AV-8B aircraft.  United Technologies Corp. subsidiaries won contracts totaling $25.7 million for UH-60 helicopter parts for the Army and spare parts for J-57, TF-30 and TF-33 aircraft engines for the Air Force.
