 Informa Group PLC: Merger With Taylor & Francis Is Set in a $1.06 Billion Deal   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 2004  British publishing companies Informa Group PLC and Taylor & Francis Group PLC will merge in a stock deal valued at about GBP 565 million ($1.06 billion), creating a publishing and conference company with market capitalization of roughly GBP 1 billion.
