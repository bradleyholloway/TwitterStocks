 Dollar calms down, slips against yen, mark after Monday's volatile session   By Luxemburg, Carolyn, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 1995  Following an earlier wild ride, the dollar took a breather on April 11, 1995. In late afternoon New York trading, the dollar was at 1.4015 marks, down from the previous day's DM1.4090, and at 83.58 yen, off from Y84.
