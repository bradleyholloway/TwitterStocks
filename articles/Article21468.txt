 International Brief -- Istituto per la Ricostruzione: Group Profit Climbed 27% In '89; Revenue Rose 13%   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 3, 1990  Istituto per la Ricostruzione Industriale (IRI), Italy's giant state holding company, yesterday posted group earnings of 1.606 trillion lire ($1.38 billion) in 1989, up 27% from 1.263 trillion lire a year earlier.
