 Excite Founders Form JotSpot To Promote Wikis Technology   By Don Clark and Vauhini Vara, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 2004  Ross Mayfield, Socialtext's chief executive officer, said his company also helps its customers to make simple applications.
