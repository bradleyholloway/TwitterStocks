 Florida Beats Par in List Of Female-Friendly Links   By By Karen L. Tippett, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1997  Five Florida golf courses made Golf for Women magazine's list of the Top 15 friendliest tees for women in the March/April issue.  The Lake Mary publication features the ranking in conjunction with a list of 175 golf courses around the nation that go the extra mile to accommodate female golfers. Golf for Women surveyed more than 1,500 courses in the U.S. and Canada to select its 175-course list, which includes 36 Florida courses.
