 EC Consumer Prices Rise   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 1989  Consumer prices in the European Community rose a provisional 0.8% in April from March and were up 5.3% from a year earlier, the EC statistics agency, Eurostat, said.  The April rise in the consumer price index was higher than increases of 0.4% in March and 0.5% in February.
