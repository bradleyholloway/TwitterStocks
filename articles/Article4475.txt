 Corporate News: The Plight of the Lowly Gift Box   By Hagerty, James R, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2012  The sound of the holiday season here is the thumping bass beat of machinery inside a beige brick factory building where gift boxes for Macy's and other retailers are stamped out. Shipments of folding cartons by U.S. producers last year totaled 5.2 million tons, about 10% below 2007, before a deep recession sliced demand, according to the Paperboard Packaging Council, a trade group.
