 Stocks Firm in London after Lending-Rate Cut and in Frankfurt on Hopes of Strike Settlement   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1992  London share prices firmed a bit on May 5, 1992 as the Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-share index climbed 2.4 points to close at 2662.2. In Frankfurt, the DAX 30-stock index added 4.35 points to end at 1732.64. The Tokyo market was closed for a holiday.
