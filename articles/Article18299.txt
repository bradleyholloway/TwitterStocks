 Abreast of the Market: Industrials Gain 14.98 to a Record on Signs of Economic Rebound   By Smith, Craig S, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1992  On Mar 3, 1992, encouraging economic news sent the Dow Jones Industrial Average up 14.98 points to a new record of 3290.25. The stock market's gains were tempered though, by worries about interest rates, which have been edging higher in the bond market.
