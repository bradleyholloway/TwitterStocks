 Put Options on Ford Attract Attention After Profit Warning   By Schreiber, Cynthia, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 2005  Ford stock fell 59 cents, or 5.4%, to $10.44 a share at 4 p.m. in New York Stock Exchange composite trading, and the auto maker's bonds continued to tumble in value. Particularly active were Ford's January 10 2007 puts, which traded 62,815 contracts and rose 60 cents to $1.70 at the Pacific Exchange.
