 Electronic Arts Earnings Drop, Hurt by Restructuring Charges   By By Robert A. Guth, The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 2003  Electronic Arts' projections bode well for the overall videogame industry. Currently three videogame machines -- from Sony Corp., Microsoft Corp. and Nintendo Co. -- are battling for market share, and in coming months the companies are expected to lower prices to spur sales. That, in turn, could boost sales of games from Electronic Arts and other publishers.
