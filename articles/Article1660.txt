 Dr Pepper vs. Dr Pepper: Sweet Business Turns Bitter   By Esterl, Mike, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 2011  [...] the world's oldest Dr Pepper bottler is locked in an escalating dispute with the owner of the Dr Pepper brand over modern-day trademark rights, Internet sales and cane sugar as it tries to extend its reach across the country. The Food and Drug Administration says there is no significant health difference between cane and beet sugar or high-fructose corn syrup, and major soda makers say blindfolded consumers can't taste the difference.
