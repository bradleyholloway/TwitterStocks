 World News: India's First Exit Polls Point to Close Race   By Sheth, Niraj; Sircar, Subhadip, The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 2009  The notoriously unreliable exit polls -- the first available since voting began on April 16 because their release was banned while voting was under way -- gave no party an outright majority in parliament, setting the stage for weeks of political wrangling and horse-trading to reach a governing majority in the 545-member body.
