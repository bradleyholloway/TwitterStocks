 Seoul turns to Europe for arms as Pentagon guards technology   By Glain, Steve, The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1993  South Korea, hungry for weapons technology the Defense Department does not wish to share, is turning to European weapons makers to modernize its arsenal. The shift has taken market share away from such companies as McDonnell Douglas Corp, General Dynamics Corp and Boeing Co at a time when they need international sales to make up from losses in the domestic market.
