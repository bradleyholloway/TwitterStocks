 Business Brief -- PEP BOYS-MANNY, MOE & JACK: Net of $9.9 Million Posted Due to Charge Taken in '98   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 1999  Pep Boys-Manny, Moe & Jack swung to a profit in the third quarter, mainly because of a one-time charge a year earlier, but the company missed Wall Street expectations, citing "industry weakness" in the do-it-yourself category. The company said net income was $9.9 million, or 20 cents a diluted share, compared with a loss of $3.9 million, or six cents a share, a year earlier.
