 Palm to Add Colors in Bid To Attract Younger Buyers   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 2000  John Cook, Palm's senior director of product marketing, said the company's newest gadget, the M100, will soon hit store shelves. The M100, which will retail for $149, will feature four megabytes of memory and personal information manager functions. Unlike other Palm products, it will be the first of the company's devices to come in a choice of casing colors, such as silver and metallic blue.
