 Wells Fargo to cut overseas activities to boost its profit   By Dolan, Carrie, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 1985  Wells Fargo & Co. has announced it will close six representative offices, including those in London and Madrid, and will close branches in Asia and a New York unit. Some 300 employees will be affected by the closings which should be completed in three to six months. Wells will be the first major US bank to leave London.
