 Share Prices Climb in Asia and Europe   By Colin Ng and Aude LaGorge, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 2011  The news of Osama bin Laden's death helped lift most major Asian markets, while European share prices rose slightly, supported by deal news and some positive earnings report.  A number of markets were closed for holidays in both regions, including those in the UK, China and Hong Kong.
