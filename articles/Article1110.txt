 Tokyo Remains Atop List Of World's Costliest Cities   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 1988  The ranking, conducted by Business International Corp., a New York consulting firm, used New York City as a base of 100. The cost-of-living index for Tokyo was 203, followed closely by Osaka/Kobe with a reading of 201. Tehran was ranked third at 187.
