 Sento Technical Names Sorenson to Succeed Firm's Founder as CEO   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1997  OREM, Utah -- Sento Technical Innovations Corp., which has been struggling during a transition from providing Unixbased software products to becoming an information-services provider, said Kieth Sorenson will succeed Gary Godfrey, who founded the company, as chairman and chief executive officer.
