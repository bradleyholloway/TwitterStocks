 World News: Hope Is Fading for Earthquake Victims   By Wright, Tom; Trofimov, Yaroslav, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 2009  Relief workers struggled to clear debris and collapsed buildings on the earthquake-battered Indonesian island of Sumatra, as hopes faded that people still trapped under rubble would be rescued alive. The death toll from the region's other recent disaster, a tsunami that swept over the South Pacific islands of Samoa, American Samoa and Tonga in midweek, reached 169, as aid workers found more bodies buried in mud, sand and debris.
