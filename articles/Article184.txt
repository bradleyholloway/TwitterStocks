Few players realize that there are considerable opportunities for effective deceptive plays in the trump suit. There is, for example, a whole family of doubleton tenaces: jack-nine, queen-ten, king-jack and ace-queen. In each case it is sometimes productive to play the higher honor unnecessarily, and lead the declarer down a tempting path.
