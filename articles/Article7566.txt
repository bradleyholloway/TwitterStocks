 South Africa Plans to Pay $2 Billion of Foreign Debt   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1986  "We want to show international bankers that we have kept our word and intend repaying our borrowings," Mr. Stals said. He added that on the basis of August 1985 exchange rates, South Africa had lowered its foreign debt outstanding to $21.4 billion at the end of June from $23.7 billion at the end of August 1985. South Africa imposed a partial halt on debt repayments September 1985.
