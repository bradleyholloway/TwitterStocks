 Jitters Ahead of Bank Reports --- A Host of Pressures Have Investors Worried About Lenders' Quarterly Figures   By Rappaport, Liz; Lucchetti, Aaron, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 2011  With securities prices declining -- the Standard & Poor's 500-stock index is down 15% from its April high, while the spread between yields on corporate bonds and Treasury debt has widened -- banks' holdings of bonds, derivatives and loans will fall in value and become more expensive to borrow against, jittery investors fear.
