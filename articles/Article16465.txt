 BNP Paribas SA: Bank's Net Profit Rises 13%, Reflecting Lower Provisions   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 2004  BNP Paribas SA said lower provisions and tight cost controls helped lift the bank's third-quarter net profit 13%, despite pressure from slower economic growth in Europe and choppy equity markets. Net profit rose to 1.1 billion euros ($1.41 billion) from 970 million euros a year earlier.
