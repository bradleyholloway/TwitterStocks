 International Corporate Report: Japanese Steelmakers Seek Price Rise on Seamless Pipe   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1987  NIPPON STEEL CORP., NIPPON KOKAN K.K., KAWASAKI STEEL CORP. and SUMITOMO METAL INDUSTRIES LTD., which make up a price cartel, will ask Japan's Ministry of International Trade and Industry for an increase in the price of exported seamless pipe, a Nippon Steel spokesman said.
