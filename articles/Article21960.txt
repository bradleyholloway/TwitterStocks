 Amid analyst concerns, AIG confident in Indonesia's Bakrie Group investment   By Wong, Jesse; Borsuk, Richard, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 2, 1994  American International Group's commitment of $125 million for family-controlled Bakrie Group of Indonesia is said to reflect AIG's position that Bakrie is a promising player in Indonesia's telecommunications market. AIG's confidence in Bakrie comes despite concerns that the Bakrie family is seen as overly ambitious with the company and has lacked focus.
