 Washington wire: Regulatory redux?   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 20, 1995  Sen Bob Dole (R-KS) is planning a new vote, perhaps the week of Oct 22, 1995, on breaking the Democratic filibuster on a bill requiring cost-benefit analysis before new rules are issued. Dole hopes to pressure Sen Charles Robb (D-VA) to deliver sympathetic Democrats.
