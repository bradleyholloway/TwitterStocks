 BSkyB Posts Growth In New Subscribers   The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 2005  BSkyB, which is partly owned by News Corp., added 95,000 net new subscribers in the quarter ended March 31, a 45% increase from the year-earlier period, as a new marketing drive launched last summer continued to attract new customers. The growth topped analysts' forecasts of between 55,000 to 90,000 and took the subscriber tally at the U.K. pay-television service to 7.7 million.
