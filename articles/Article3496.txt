 Best Buy Co.: Net Fell 6.8% in the Quarter As Costs Offset Revenue Gain   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2005  Consumer-electronics chain Best Buy Co.'s fiscal third-quarter profit fell 6.8% as revenue couldn't keep up with higher costs, some of which were associated with a new customer-service and marketing program. The Richfield, Minn., company also trimmed its profit forecast for the year, and its share price fell 12%.
