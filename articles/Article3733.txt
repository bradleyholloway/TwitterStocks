 H-P Tops Palm In Regional Sales Of Hand-Helds   By Pringle, David, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 21, 2003  Figures for the global hand-held computer market in the third quarter aren't yet available, but analysts expect Palm still to be the leading supplier world-wide, thanks to the popularity of its gadgets in the U.S. Even so, H-P's elevation to the top spot in the Europe- Mideast-Africa region suggests that Palm is facing a tough fight in a global market it once dominated. H-P's renewed push is likely to be a boon for Microsoft Corp., which is in a battle with Palm to become the dominant supplier of the software that controls hand-held computers. H-P's hand-held products use Microsoft software.
