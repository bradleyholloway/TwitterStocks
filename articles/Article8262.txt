 U.S., Canada, Mexico Agree on Terms of Trade Pact to Block Certain Imports   By Davis, Bob; Gutfeld, Rose, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 1992  The US agreed with Canada and Mexico on provisions of a proposed free-trade pact that would allow any country to block imports that don't comply with stiff environmental regulations. The country blocking imports would have to prove that the rule is based on credible science and designed to protect against a genuine risk.
