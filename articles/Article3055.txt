 Ford Assembly Plant For Autos, Parts Wins Philippine Approval   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 20, 1998  The Philippine government said it has approved the application of Ford Motor Co. to set up a 4.18 billion peso ($106.8 million) automobile-assembly and parts plant in the country.  The Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the agency that oversees special investment zones, said Ford's output will be "totally domestic-market-oriented" during its initial years of operation. It declined to be more specific about Ford's timetable of operations.
