 Tenet Healthcare Says Its Net Income Surged In Fiscal 4th Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 2001  In the year-earlier quarter, Tenet posted net income of $20 million, or 51 cents a share, after one-time items. A month ago, Tenet said its per-share earnings before the one-time items in the quarter ended May 31 would exceed analysts' then-consensus per-share estimates of 61 cents. Earnings on that basis came in at 68 cents a share. Revenue rose 10% to $3.21 billion from $2.91 billion.
