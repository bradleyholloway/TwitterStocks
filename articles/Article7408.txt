 Continental Illinois Hires Homan to Help In Marketing Its Stock   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 7, 1985  Continental Illinois Corp. hired Paul M. Homan, a former top official of the Comptroller of the Currency, to help the government-controlled banking concern get its stock back into private hands.  A Continental spokesman confirmed the company hired Mr. Homan to head a new department responsible for corporate planning and research, and relations with federal regulators and other investors.
