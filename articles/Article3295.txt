 China's Iron Fist   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 2004  Dr. [Jiang Yanyong] and his wife Hua Zhongwei, also a doctor, were detained on June 1 and have not been seen since. The authorities have given no reason for their detention. On June 4 the Jiang children received a note from Dr. Hua saying they were fine and a week later a note from the government asking for personal belongings.
