 SuperMac Posts Loss For Latest Quarter; Radius Tops Forecasts ---- A Wall Street Journal News Roundup   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 1994  Two rivals in computer-display equipment surprised Wall Street with their earnings, SuperMac Technology Inc. reporting an unexpected net loss and Radius Inc. posting slightly better than expected net income.  SuperMac, based in Sunnyvale, reported a first-quarter net loss of $1.5 million, or 17 cents a share, compared with net income of $2.1 million, or 25 cents a share, in the year-earlier quarter. Financial analysts' mean estimate for SuperMac's earnings was for one cent a share, according to Zacks Investment Research Inc.
