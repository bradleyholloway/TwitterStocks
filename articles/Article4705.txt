 Share Prices Surge In Tokyo Trading --- A Wall Street Journal News Roundup   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 1986  Tokyo share prices surged in moderate volume yesterday, as morning buying of selected issues spread into a broader afternoon advance.  In Tokyo, the Nikkei stock average gained 247.36 points, to 17300.56, and the stock exchange index rose 16.71 points, to 1430.37. Volume increased to 540 million shares from 340 million shares Monday.
