 Fleet Financial Settles Civil Fraud Charges Brought by the SEC   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1999  The Securities and Exchange Commission brought civil fraud charges against a Fleet Financial Group Inc. unit, six former employees of Fleet's Shawmut Investment Advisers unit, and a Michigan brokerage firm in connection with an alleged scheme in which the former Shawmut officials improperly directed $1.8 million in commissions to certain brokers in exchange for client referrals to Shawmut.
