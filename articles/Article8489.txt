 Cox sees Times Mirror cable system as key to survival   By Sharpe, Anita; Robichaux, Mark, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 7, 1994  Cox Enterprises Inc agreed to buy the cable systems owned by Times Mirror Co for a whopping $2.3 billion, or about 12 times cash flow, at a time when cable-system values are depressed and publicly trading for about 9.5 times cash flow. The deal makes Cox the third-largest cable operator in the country.
