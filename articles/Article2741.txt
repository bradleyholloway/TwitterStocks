 Archipelago to Create With Pacific Exchange Open E-Market in U.S.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 2000  Archipelago, an electronic communications network, or ECN, already handles some Nasdaq trading. Archipelago's backers include Goldman Sachs Group Inc., Merrill Lynch & Co., J.P. Morgan & Co., E*Trade Group Inc., General Electric Co.'s CNBC-TV, and Instinet, the Reuters Group unit, which itself operates an ECN.
