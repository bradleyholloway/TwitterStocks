 Letters to the Editor: Ethics? Don't Worry, It's Only a Game   The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 1989  I thought I knew something about business ethics, but Citicorp's new "Work Ethic" board game has me stumped ("How You Play the Game," April 18).  Let's see now -- you complete a deal with a Japanese client, and he gives you an expensive vase. What to do?  1. Risk offending him by turning down the gift? Wrong. Deduct points. Lose next turn.
