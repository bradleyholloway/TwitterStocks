 RJR slashes planned payout on stock issues   By Shapiro, Eben, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 1993  Tobacco and food concern RJR Nabisco Holdings Corp cut the total dividend it plans to pay when it creates separate food and tobacco shares by 73%. The company proposes paying only a $.12 dividend on the food stock and delaying the payment of any dividend on the tobacco equity.
