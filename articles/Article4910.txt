 Your Money Matters: Tax Bill Could Have Big Impact on Investors   By Schultz, Ellen E, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1992  The impact of the major tax bill pending in the Senate on individual investors and prospective homeowners is discussed. Many of the provisions, such as full deductions for contributions to IRAs, should be welcome, but others, such as the permanent limits on deductions and personal exemptions for upper-income taxpayers, may not be.
