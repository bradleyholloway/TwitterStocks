 Russia lifts ban on all shipments of U.S. poultry   By Ingersoll, Bruce, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1996  US officials have persuaded Russia to lift its ban on all US poultry shipments, at least temporarily resolving the tense trade dispute. Vice President Al Gore announced that he had been assured by Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin that US poultry exports will continue "without interruption" while officials of both countries work out their differences.
