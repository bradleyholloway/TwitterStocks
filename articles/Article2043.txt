 Arizona Denies Goldsmith Injunction Over B.A.T Bid   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1989  Farmers Group Inc. said a federal judge in Arizona denied an attempt by Sir James Goldsmith to block the Arizona insurance department from reviewing his unfriendly #13.4 billion bid (US $20.8 billion) for B.A.T Industries PLC, parent of Farmers.
