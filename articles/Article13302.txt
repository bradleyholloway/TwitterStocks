 Mittal Steel Co.: Results of Offer for Arcelor Are to Be Released July 26   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 2006  Mittal Steel Co. plans to release the results of its 25.4 billion euros ($32.5 billion) offer for steel rival Arcelor SA on July 26, the company said. Mittal needs to win at least half of Arcelor's share capital for its bid to succeed.
