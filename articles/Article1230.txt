 Maguire, Facing Pressure, Again Explores a Sale   By Forsyth, Jennifer S, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 2007  The company announced it is forming a special committee to explore strategic alternatives. The committee will be made of independent directors and won't include Maguire Properties' chief executive and founder, Robert Maguire. JMB Capital Partners Master Fund LP, a hedge fund that raised its stake in Maguire Properties last week, has urged Maguire Properties to form such a committee and has demanded that Mr. Maguire not sit on it.
