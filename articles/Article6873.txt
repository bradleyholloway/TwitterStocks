 Business Bookshelf: Banking Without Tears   By Kansas, Dave, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 2008  The Orange Code By Arkadi Kuhlmann and Bruce Philp (Wiley, 252 pages, $27.95) It is fitting that a book about the success of ING Direct, the large U.S. online bank, would come out in the midst of the worst financial crisis in decades. In "The Orange Code," Arkadi Kuhlmann and Bruce Philp -- respectively, ING Direct's U.S. chief and the chairman of ING Direct's marketing partner -- offer a mix of corporate history and leadership lessons, together with a list of the rules, the so-called Orange Code, that have helped to guide their decisions over the years.
