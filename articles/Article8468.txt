 Dynamics Research Will Pay $1.8 Million To Settle Federal Suit   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1994  WILMINGTON, Mass. -- Dynamics Research Corp. said it will pay $1.8 million to settle a lawsuit brought against the company by the U.S. government in 1991.  The civil action stems from a dispute between Dynamics Research and the U.S. Air Force over the pricing in 1985 of a contract for a computer system to support aircraft maintenance.
