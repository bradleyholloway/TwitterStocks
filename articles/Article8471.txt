 Your money matters: Pros coast to an easy victory in latest stock-picking contest   By Jasen, Georgette, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1994  In the latest investment contest that ran from Oct 4, 1993 to Mar 31, 1994, four investment professionals averaged a 4.7% gain, while the Dow Jones Industrials edged up 1.6% and picks made by throwing darts at the stock tables lost 9.2%.
