 For everything under the sun, specialized sunglasses   By Mehta, Stephanie N, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1996  Premium sunglasses--those that retail for more than $30--now represent about 52% of the US sunglass market, compared to about 23% a decade ago. The industry racked up sales of $2.68 billion in 1995. In order to keep the growth going, sunglass makers are taking market segmentation to new limits.
