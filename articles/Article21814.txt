 Medicine: Vote sets back P&G, Syntex on analgesic   By Stern, Gabriella; Gutfeld, Rose, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1993  Procter & Gamble Co and Syntex Corp were dealt a blow by the FDA's refusal to approve an over-the-counter version of the pain reliever Naprosyn. The companies had planned to market the product in a joint venture.
