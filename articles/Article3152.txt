 Health & Technology Brief -- Cingular Wireless: Failure to Provide 911 Tracking Draws $675,000 Settlement   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 13, 2003  Cingular Wireless agreed to pay a $675,000 fine to settle a complaint by the Federal Communications Commission that it failed to meet deadlines for installing technology capable of identifying the location of an emergency 911 call made on a Cingular handset.
