 India, U.S. firm to market imagery from satellites   By Lachica, Eduardo, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 1995  On Feb 2, 1995, India's Antrix Corp and Earth Observation Satellite Co of Lanham MD entered into a partnership for the world-wide marketing of earth imagery. Eosat, a joint venture of Martin Marietta Corp and Hughes Aircraft Co, said the arrangement could result in as much as $1 billion in sales.
