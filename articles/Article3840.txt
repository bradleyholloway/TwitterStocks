 Redemption Notices   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 2004  Eaton Vance Limited Duration Income Fund will redeem in October a selection of its auction preferred shares, series A-E. The fund will redeem on Oct. 5 1200 of its auction preferred shares, series A, with Cusip number 27828H204, at a redemption price equal to $25,000 a share plus an amount equal to accumulated but unpaid dividends as of the redemption date.
