 Will Cellphones Become Devices For Downloading Music, Movies?   By By H. Asher Bolande, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 2003  The $12 billion South Korean mobile market is an ideal laboratory for testing new applications and offers a peek at what could become mobile-phone trends world-wide. South Korea has a high penetration of sophisticated handsets, world-beating infrastructure and -- most of all -- a population with a sweet tooth for new technology. SK Telecom also has 3G technology known as cdma2000 1x EV-DO, which has speedy data-transfer rates that let users quickly download media content.
