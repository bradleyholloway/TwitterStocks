 Evans Products Says '84 Loss Narrowed   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 1985  MIAMI BEACH, Fla. -- Evans Products Co., a Victor Posner-controlled concern in bankruptcy-law proceedings, said it estimates its net loss for 1984 was between $35 million and $40 million, compared with a net loss of $63.9 million in 1983.
