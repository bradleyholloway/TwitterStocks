 Business Brief -- MARSH & MCLENNAN COS.: Loss of $50 Million Posted On Charges From Purchase   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1998  Marsh & McLennan Cos reported a $50 million 1997 fourth-quarter loss because of one-time charges from its acquisition of insurance broker Johnson & Higgins. Without those charges, the company said profit would have jumped 45% from a year earlier.
