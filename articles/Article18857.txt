 Swiss Officials Say Probe of Nazi Era Won't Affect Banking Secrecy Today   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1996  According to government officials, today's banking secrecy will be unaffected by the planned appointment of a panel of experts. The experts will have the power to examine archives to pinpoint the movements of funds that arrived in Switzerland during the Nazi regime in Germany. They will also seek to discover the origination of these funds to determine whether they belonged to victims of the Nazi regime and were wrongfully taken from their owners.
