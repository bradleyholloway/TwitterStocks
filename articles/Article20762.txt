 U.S. News: Xerox Eyed in Texas Medicaid Probe   By Koppel, Nathan, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 2012  The Texas Health and Human Services Commission says it is concerned that Affiliated Computer Services Inc., which Xerox acquired in 2010, didn't dedicate enough trained staff to vet dentists' Medicaid requests, allowing dentists to receive payments for procedures not covered by the program.
