Donnelley Corrects Details On CEO's College Background   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 2006  David Swanson, 51 years old, attended St. Cloud State University in Minnesota from 1973 to 1976 but didn't earn a degree, the Cary, N.C., company said. Donnelley has twice issued news releases stating that he had. One was issued to announce Mr. Swanson's appointment as chief executive in 2002, the other announced a promotion in 1999.  Donnelley said it acted after receiving an anonymous letter questioning Mr. Swanson's educational background last week. The News & Observer of Raleigh and Forbes.com reported on Mr. Swanson's background last week.
