 Emcore Believers Bank On Bright Future in Solar   By Philbin, Brett, The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 2008  For 10 years, Emcore has been a player in the aerospace industry, producing solar panels for satellites for companies including Boeing Co., Lockheed Martin Corp. and Northrop Grumman Corp. But concerns over use of the solar technology for terrestrial systems has weighed on the company's shares.
