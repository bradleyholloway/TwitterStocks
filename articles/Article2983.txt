 Ad Notes...   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 2002  The San Francisco retailer has tapped Laird + Partners, a newly formed New York ad shop, to work with its in-house marketing team on ads for adult Gap, Baby Gap and Gap Kids. Laird + Partners was formed by Trey Laird, previously executive vice president and corporate creative director of Donna Karan International. The agency, which has about 20 employees, also creates ads for Donna Karan International. Mr. Laird is 37 years old. Gap has ended its relationship with Modernista, the Boston ad firm.
