 Capital journal: Bradley's advice to White House: Focus on deficit   By Seib, Gerald F, The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1993  Gerald F. Seib discusses the advice given by Sen Bill Bradley (D-NJ) on how to deal with the national deficit and the US's debt. One of Bradley's ideas that he plans to propose is that all revenue from the Clinton administration's tax increases go into a new fund dedicated strictly to cutting the deficit.
