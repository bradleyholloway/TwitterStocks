 Property and casualty insurers' 1992 results weak, A.M. Best says   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 1993  According to A. M. Best Co, record losses on Hurricane Andrew and other catastrophes led to weak 1992 results for property and casualty insurers. The industry's total capital increased only 2.7%, or $4.3 billion, to $163 billion.
