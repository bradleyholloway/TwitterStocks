 Deals & Deal Makers: Union Pacific Planning an IPO To Sell Overnite Trucking Unit   By Machalaba, Daniel, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 2003  A Union Pacific spokeswoman, declining to comment on analysts' estimates of Overnite's value, said Union Pacific would use proceeds to "enhance shareholder value." John Barnes, an analyst at Deutsche Bank Securities Inc., said he expects Union Pacific to continue aggressively reducing its debt and "to look at its dividend policy and share repurchases."
