 Private bankers court merely affluent   By Pae, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1992  Private banking is becoming available to individuals with incomes of about $75,000 and $300,000 to invest. Chemical Banking Corp. (New York City) now offers private banking services to a broader clientele. US Trust Corp. (New York City) offers asset management services to individuals with $250,000. Private banking allows an individual to do all transactions with one employee and receive personal attention. Citicorp (New York City) made more than $150 million in 1991 in its private banking division.
