 Willis Stein to Buy Baker & Taylor Inc. From Carlyle Group   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 2003  "It's a difficult economy, but they have been growing and taking share," said Daniel Blumenthal, managing director of Willis Stein & Partners. Mr. Blumenthal noted that Baker & Taylor is profitable but declined to be more specific. He noted that Baker & Taylor, as a distributor, is benefiting from the entertainment trend of increased DVD sales at the expense of video rentals.
