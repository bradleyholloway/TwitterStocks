 J. Walter Thompson Co. Lays Off 100 Employees   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1987  Advertising agency J. Walter Thompson Co., which last week lost the Burger King Corp. account, laid off about 100 employees, according to senior officials.  About 80 of the expected layoffs were in the 650-person New York office. After the layoffs, Thompson has about 2,000 employees in the U.S. and about 7,300 employees world-wide. It was acquired this year by WPP Group PLC, a London marketing conglomerate.
