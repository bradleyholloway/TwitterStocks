 McDonnell Douglas Unit Wins Navy Jobs Totaling $77.5 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1986  McDonnell Aircraft Co., a unit of McDonnell Douglas Corp., won Navy contracts totaling $77.5 million for FA-18 aircraft production and TAV-8B aircraft development.  Units of General Electric Co. received contracts totaling $51.9 million for Trident missile-engineering services and guidance parts for the Navy and training services for the Army.
