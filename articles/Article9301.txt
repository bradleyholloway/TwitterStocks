 Business Brief -- Vulcan Materials Co.: Extra Provision Will Reduce 4th-Quarter, Full-Year Net   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 2001  Vulcan Materials Co., a Birmingham, Ala., maker of construction materials and chemicals, said an extra provision for a previously announced $23 million arbitration award against a subsidiary will reduce both its preliminary fourth-quarter and full-year 2000 results by 13 cents each. The company announced preliminary results in January.
