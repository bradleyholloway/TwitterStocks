 Doubts voiced about India's economic goals   By Lehner, Urban C; Karp, Jonathan, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1997  Despite the euphoria over India's economic prospects, investors in the country's infrastructure are having doubts, and fear that a national commitment to succeed is lacking. Such investors are leery of Finance Minister P. Chidambaram's assertion that India will become as competitive as any other Asian country by 2000.
