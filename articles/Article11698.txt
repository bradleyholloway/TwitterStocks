 Sharper Image Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1993  San Francisco retailer Sharper Image Corp expects to report earnings of $.02 to $.04 a share for the 1992 fiscal year ended Jan 31, 1993, reversing the year-earlier's loss of $3.3 million. Sales for the year climbed 5.2% to $148.4 million.
