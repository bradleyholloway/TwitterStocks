 Apple Appeals Ruling For Tariff Covering Some Display Screens   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 1991  CUPERTINO, Calif. -- Apple Computer Inc. said it is appealing a decision to impose a stiff tariff on a type of display screen the company uses in one of its new notebook-sized computers.  Apple said it filed an appeal with the Court of International Trade in Washington in which it seeks to reverse an August decision by the International Trade Commission to impose a 62.63% tariff on displays relying on "active matrix" techniques.
