 DaimlerChrysler Recalls Some SUVs in `Salt Belt'   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 2000  DaimlerChrysler AG's U.S. operations are recalling about 638,000 Jeep Cherokee and Grand Cherokee sport-utility vehicles because of a brake problem. The company also said it will recall 474,000 Cherokees to replace a faulty air-bag module.
