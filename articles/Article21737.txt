 Treasury Note and Bond Prices Finish Lower as Investors Move to Sidelines ahead of Election   By Vogel, Thomas T, Jr; Lauricella, Thomas D, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1992  Cautious investors headed for the sidelines in Nov 2, 1992 bond market trading and treasuries generally fell. The 30-year Treasury bond dropped over 3/8 point to 95 3/32, for a yield of 7.65%, up from 7.62%.
