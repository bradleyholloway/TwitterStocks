He appeared one day, about two years ago, and took up an informal, unobtrusive residence inside and outside an Upper East Side neighborhood church. Yesterday he died, apparently of natural causes, and the neighborhood was saddened.  ''I didn't know he died,'' said a doorman, Frank Buchanan, gazing from the lobby of his building at 404 East 66th Street at the concrete steps of a school where he said the man, Richard Demattia, used to spend the night.
