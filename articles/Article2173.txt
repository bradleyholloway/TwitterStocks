 'Willfulness' of acts in mine blast leaves few shocked at officials' sentencing   By Burkins, Glenn; Nelson, Emily, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 1996  Three former executives of the Pyro Mining Co were sentenced to prison terms on Jun 12, 1996 for a mine explosion that killed 10 workers in 1989, and few in the industry were shocked by the sentences. The broad scope and "willfulness" of the safety violations in the case led to the harsh penalties.
