 ValueVision makes cash-and-stock bid for National Media   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 1994  Hoping to create a "leader in the interactive video shopping marketplace of the future," ValueVision International Inc, the nation's third-largest home-shopping network, has launched an unsolicited $10-a-share stock-and-cash bid for National Media Corp. The bid has an indicated value of about $117.5 million.
