 Assidomaen AB: Net profit tumbles by 59% on 12% drop in revenue   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 1997  Swedish forest group AssiDomaen AB's net profit tumbled 59% to 1.41 billion kronor ($188.5 million) from 3.42 billion kronor a year before. Revenue fell 12% to 19.32 billion kronor from 21.84 billion kronor a year earlier.
