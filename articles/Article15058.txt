 Commercial real estate is back in style   By Pacelle, Mitchell, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1993  With commercial real estate prices so low that rental incomes from properties can produce 8% annual returns, many investors are returning to the bloodied commercial real estate market. Analysts expect a long bottoming-out process, however, because of the huge oversupply of office buildings and other properties in the US.
