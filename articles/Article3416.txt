 Dollar Weakens Against Yen   By Laurence Norman and Steven Vames, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 2005  At least part of the dollar's climb came from "someone with a huge [dollar short] position to liquidate," said Tim O'Sullivan, chief dealer at Gain Capital in Warren, N.J. Once that order was out of the way, hedge funds and other investors came back into the market looking to buy euros on a price dip, he said.  Overall, Mr. O'Sullivan said, "it feels like we're at a crossroads" for the dollar. "It feels like it could go either way and the move would make sense," he said.
