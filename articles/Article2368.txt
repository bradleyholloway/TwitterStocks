 Business Brief -- STARBUCKS CORP.: Fiscal Fourth-Quarter Net Surged 47% to $18.8 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1997  Starbucks Corp said fiscal fourth-quarter net income rose 47% to $18.8 million, or 23 cents a share. The earnings were in line with Wall Street forecasts and buoyed Starbucks stock, which rose 68.75 cents to $32.125.
