 Deutsche Borse Loses Momentum in Effort Over Dutch Options   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 2002  Eurex, which is the world's largest derivatives exchange and is controlled by Deutsche Borse, traded 6.1% of all Dutch options traded on the two exchanges last month, down from 7.4% in July. For the options on 23 Dutch stocks it lists, Eurex's market share reached double-digits on only two days, down from 10 days in July.
