 Business Brief -- Dun & Bradstreet Corp.: Agreement Is Signed to Sell Collections-Services Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 2001  D&B, a credit-reports and business-information provider, said it has signed a definitive agreement to sell its RMS operations in the U.S., Canada and Hong Kong to its RMS senior management team. The business will be based in Bethlehem, Pa., moving from its current location in Murray Hill, N.J.
