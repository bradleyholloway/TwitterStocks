 Citibank Purchases Credit Card Portfolio Of Failed Bank Firm   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 1988  The FDIC acquired the Delaware credit card subsidiary of the former First RepublicBank Corp. when it took control of the failed bank holding company Aug. 2. Except for the credit card unit, First Republic was sold to NCNB Corp. of Charlotte, N.C., which renamed it NCNB Texas.
