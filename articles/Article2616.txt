 World-wide: Somali gunmen   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1994  UN sources said police have mutinied in northeastern Somalia and Somali gunmen have forced the closure of Mogadishu's port. In New York, UN Chief Boutros Boutros-Ghali suggested that the organization should reduce its nearly 19,000-member force in Somalia because of its failure to stop the fighting.
