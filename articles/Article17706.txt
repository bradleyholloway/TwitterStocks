 Britain Agrees to Sell Royal Ordnance PLC To British Aerospace   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 1987  The British government agreed to sell Royal Ordnance PLC to British Aerospace PLC, whose $305 million bid bettered a $257 million offer from GKN PLC.  The long-delayed sale will give Royal Ordnance an opportunity for growth "only really available in the private sector," Defense Secretary George Younger said in announcing the sale to the House of Commons.
