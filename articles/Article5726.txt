 Sony prices its advanced game player at $299, near low end of expectations   By Carlton, Jim, The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1995  Sony Corp said it will launch its advanced PlayStation game player on Sep 9, 1995 for a list price of $299, near the low end of expectations among industry analysts, who predicted the machine would list for $300 to $500. The price puts new pressure on competitors Nintendo Co, Sega Enterprises Ltd and 3DO Co.
