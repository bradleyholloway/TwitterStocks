 Business Brief -- Craig Corp.: Yucaipa Buys 5.1% Stake, Intends to Increase Holding   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1989  Yucaipa Capital Partners LP, a Claremont, Calif., limited partnership, acquired at 5.1% stake in Craig Corp., a diversified Los Angeles concern with interests in retail supermarkets and the import of photographic accessories.
