 Guest Worker Program Is Bad for Taxpayers   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 2005  Tamar Jacoby's June 20 Rule of Law column "Getting Beyond the 'A- Word'" tried to justify amnesty for illegal immigrants by combining it with enforcement of immigration laws. She rightly says that "No one wants to reward lawbreaking" and that "history shows that amnesty is no solution," but then endorses giving illegal immigrants "legal status," in other words, amnesty.
