 Business Brief -- Musicbank: Licensing Deal With EMI To Expand Online Offerings   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2000  Musicbank Inc., an online music service based in San Francisco, has signed a deal with EMI Group PLC's EMI Recorded Music to license its recordings to the company, helping musicbank's bid to legally offer services similar to those of MP3.com Inc. and Napster Inc.
