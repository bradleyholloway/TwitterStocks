 Dollar ends off against yen, rise in rates hinted   By Patterson, Rebecca H, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1996  After reaching a more than two-year high against the yen early on Apr 3, 1996, the dollar fell abruptly on hints that official Japanese interest rates soon may begin rising from historic lows. In late New York trading, the dollar was quoted at DM1.4812 and Y106.89, down from DM1.4825 and Y107.62 on Apr 2.
