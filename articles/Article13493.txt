 Financing: Starting Up: How Much Money Do I Need? Online edition  By Ransom, Diana, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 2008  The lack of staying power, according to the U.S. Small Business Administration, is due in large part to businesses running out of money before they reach sustained profitability. Start-up costs are generally broken down into two categories: "pre- opening expenses," such as marketing, advertising and administrative costs, and "organizational expenses," like legal and accounting fees.
