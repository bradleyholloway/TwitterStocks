 Moody's to Brokers: Proceed Cautiously in Derivatives Sales   By Siconolfi, Michael, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1991  Moody's Investors Service Inc is warning brokerage firms that the boom in derivatives during the 1990s could end up causing some trouble. A combination of market, credit, regulatory and other risks could work to hurt the hybrid securities.
