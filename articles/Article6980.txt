 Currents -- Religion: An Episcopal Flock Breaks -- and Heals -- Apart --- Two Factions in a Texas Diocese Parted Ways Over Different Beliefs, Leading to Lawsuits Over Property, and Now, for Some, Relief   By Simon, Stephanie, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 2009  Last November, the Episcopal diocese here in northeast Texas fractured over issues such as the ordination of women and gays, and the theology of salvation. Both factions celebrate Mass in the same high-church, Anglo-Catholic style, ringing bells as the priest blesses the bread and wine for the holy Eucharist.
