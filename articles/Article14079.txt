 First Fidelity S&L Expects to Report Fourth-Period Loss   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1985  ORLANDO, Fla. -- First Fidelity Savings & Loan Association said it will report a "small" loss for the fourth quarter, resulting from a write-down of assets by $1.2 million and a loss of about $200,000 incurred by its construction subsidiary.
