 Fall Sale by Christie's Of Contemporary Art Produces $9.3 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 13, 1998  Amid uncertainty about the market for a number of younger, untested artists and former 1980s stars, bidding was competitive and 42 of 49 works sold. It was the second auction of its kind in the U.S. All of the works were executed around 1970 or later and some were under two years old. Generally, auctions feature more-established material.
