 Letters to the Editor: Does America Need a Parliament?   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 1988  Peter Smithers offers a thoughtful argument that our Constitution fails to provide a cadre of qualified men, a mechanism of presidential selection and decision-making management ("Presidential Paralysis and the Constitution," editorial page, Dec. 24). He extolls the British system of cabinet government and rebukes our bicentennial celebration "of a manifestly obsolete document."
