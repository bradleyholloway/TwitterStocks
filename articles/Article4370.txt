 Health & Wellness -- In the Lab: In a Squeaky-Clean World, a Worm Might Help Fight Disease   By Wang, Shirley S, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 2012  Researchers at several universities have shown promising results in small numbers of patients with multiple sclerosis, Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis. In many autoimmune diseases, immune cells known as T1 cell cytokines proliferate and fight against the body's cells, the way they do when the body detects foreign invaders.
