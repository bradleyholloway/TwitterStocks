An independent examiner has cleared the senior management and the board of the Leslie Fay Companies of any knowledge of or participation in the accounting fraud that drove the apparel company into bankruptcy last year, a report released yesterday said.  But BDO Seidman, the accounting firm that was Leslie Fay's outside auditor until the fraud was uncovered in January 1993, was harshly criticized by Charles A. Stillman, the securities lawyer who wrote the report. Mr. Stillman had been appointed by the United States trustee in the bankruptcy case to determine whether Leslie Fay's audit committee had thoroughly investigated the matter.
