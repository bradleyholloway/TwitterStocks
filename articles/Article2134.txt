 American Continental Reorganization Plan Is Disclosed by U.S.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 20, 1990  The federal government and a committee representing unsecured creditors of American Continental Corp. provided details of a proposed reorganization plan under which American Continental would be liquidated, Charles A. Keating Jr. and other executives would be ousted and Lincoln Savings & Loan Association would be dropped from creditors' claims.
