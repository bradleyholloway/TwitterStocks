 India Must Face Reality Of Burgeoning Workforce   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 2007  Unfortunately, the amendments to the plan do more harm than good, allowing a proliferation of smaller SEZs that can't get the job done. Instead, the government should go back to the drawing board and find a way to follow through with a plan that more closely follows the China model.
