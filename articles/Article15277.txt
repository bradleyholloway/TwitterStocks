 Business Brief -- PennCorp Financial Group Inc.: Company Reaches Accord To Buy Integon Life Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 1995  Under the agreement, subject to regulatory approval and the satisfaction of certain other conditions, PennCorp will acquire Integon Life and its subsidiaries, Integon Financial Life Insurance Corp., Integon Life Insurance Corp. and Georgia International Life Insurance Co.
