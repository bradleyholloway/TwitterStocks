 ECU Bond Market Remains in Disarray   By Mitchell, Constance; Sesit, Michael R, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1992  One week after Denmark's voters rejected plans for the EC's economic and monetary integration, the once-booming market for bonds denominated in ECUs remains in disarray. The sell-off in some ECU bonds slowed somewhat in Jun 9, 1992 trading, but the market for new issues has dried up for now.
