 One of Fannie Mae's Jobs Is To Provide Liquidity   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 2008  "Fannie Mae Alchemy" (Review & Outlook, Feb. 29) correctly acknowledges risks faced by Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac, but completely ignores the public mission of these companies -- to provide liquidity to the mortgage markets in times like these when the private sector is unwilling or unable to do so.
