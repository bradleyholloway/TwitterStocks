 Theater: Nile queen; Illusion king   By Lyons, Donald, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 13, 1996  Donald Lyons reviews the Shakespeare Theatre's production of Shakespeare's "Antony and Cleopatra," starring Helen Carey and Tom Hewitt and directed by Ron Daniels, playing in Washington DC in Dec 1996. Lyons also reviews "David Copperfield: Dreams & Nightmares" at Martin Beck Theatre in New York City, starring illusionist David Copperfield.
