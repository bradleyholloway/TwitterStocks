 Wall Street Resists Agency Disclosures on S&L Asset Sales   By Thomas, Paulette, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 1990  With Congress looking over its shoulder, the RTC must routinely disclose prices and other transaction terms, but Wall Street firms are balking at such unaccustomed disclosures, threatening to boycott RTC asset sales if the agency unveils the prices too specifically or too quickly.
