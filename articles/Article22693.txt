 Business Brief -- MUSICMATCH INC.: Radio-Style Webcasting On 18 Channels Is Offered   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 2000  MusicMatch Inc., San Diego, a leading provider of online music storage and organizing services, will expand its offerings to include radio-style Webcasting. The new initiative, expected to be unveiled today, has 18 different genre channels, including alternative rock and smooth jazz.
