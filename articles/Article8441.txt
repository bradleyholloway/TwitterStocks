 Ultralife Batteries Inc.: Prepayment of Kodak note completes asset purchase   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 1994  In final settlement of a note payable, Ultralife Batteries Inc said it paid about $2 million to Eastman Kodak Co. Ultralife was formed in 1991, when it agreed to acquire lithium-battery technology and the "Ultralife" brand name from Kodak. Separately, Ultralife has won a $750,000 US Army contract for development of high-energy lithium batteries.
