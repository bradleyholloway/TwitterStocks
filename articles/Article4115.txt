 Stock Prices Plummet In Europe, Japan on Wall Street's Big Drop --- A Wall Street Journal News Roundup   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 1986  Shares prices plunged across Europe and in Japan Thursday, largely in reaction to Wall Street's record 39.10-point decline the day before on rising American interest rates.  In London, the Financial Times industrial share index dropped 17.7 points, to 1106.1. Wednesday's rise in British lending rates also depressed prices, brokers said.  In Tokyo, the Nikkei stock average fell 22.23 points, to 13034.19, also in reaction to Wall Street's drop. The exchange's index lost 5.08 points, to 1032.83.
