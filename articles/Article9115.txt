 International Brief -- KINOKUNIYA CO.: Sales in Japan Expected To Rise by 6.2% in 1999   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 7, 1999  Kinokuniya Co's sales in Japan will rise 6.2% to 120 billion yen in 1999, said Shuji Yoshino, general manager of the international business division at Japan's largest bookseller.
