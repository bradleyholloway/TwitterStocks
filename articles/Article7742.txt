 Lockheed Wins $251.1 Million Of Navy Orders   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1988  Units of Lockheed Corp. received Navy contracts totaling $251.1 million for development and production of the Trident II missile and for C-130 aircraft wings.  Texaco Refining & Marketing Co., a unit of Texaco Inc., received a $109.3 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel.  Sun Refining & Marketing Co., a unit of Sun Co., received a $60.1 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel.
