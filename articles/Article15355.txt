 Cyberpoliticking: Presidential races are being changed by latest technology   By Seib, Gerald F, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 1995  Political campaigns are trying to figure out how they can best use new technology in unprecedented ways to reach both mass audiences and ever-narrower niches of specific voters. So far, five presidential campaigns and the Clinton White House have launched Internet home pages, carrying carefully categorized information about their candidates' positions and backgrounds.
