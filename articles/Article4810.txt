 International Corporate Report: Germany Is Likely to Sell VW Stake by Year's End   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1987  West Germany's finance minister, Gerhard Stoltenberg, said the government "in all likelihood" will sell its 16% stake in VOLKSWAGEN AG by the end of this year. The prediction confirmed the government's plans to go ahead with the sale after acknowledging last month that it was considering the possibility.
