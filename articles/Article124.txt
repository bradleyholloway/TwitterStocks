Injuries at school account for up to 4 million or 20 percent of the 22 million injuries that children suffer in the United States each year and school boards and health departments should improve efforts to prevent them, according to a new study.  Most school injuries are suffered in playgrounds and sports areas but the trauma suffered should not be dismissed as ''part of the game,'' according to the authors of the study, reported in the January issue of Pediatrics, a journal published by the American Academy of Pediatrics.
