 Spendthrift Farm Inc. Says It Can't Make An Interest Payment   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 1986  LEXINGTON, Ky. -- Spendthrift Farm Inc., a breeding operation burdened with heavy debt in a weakening market for thoroughbreds, said it won't be able to make a $1.9 million interest payment due Sept. 15 on a debenture issue.  Spendthrift said it has retained Prudential-Bache Securities Inc. to advise it in restructuring its debt, including the $30 million in 12 1/2% senior subordinated debentures due 1994 on which the interest payment can't be made.
