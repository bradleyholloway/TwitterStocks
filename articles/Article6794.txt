 World News: Myanmar Regime Takes Control of U.N. Shipment; Junta Says It Isn't Ready to Welcome Some Foreign Help   By Barta, Patrick, The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 2008  Myanmar's military regime took control of distributing a United Nations aid shipment and said some foreign workers weren't welcome, as aid officials and diplomats warned the death toll from Cyclone Nargis may exceed 100,000. The impasse increases the likelihood that foreign governments or international aid agencies will resort to airdrops or other action to reach victims.
