 Tyson Foods Inc.: OSHA Levies $436,000 Fine After Probe of Worker's Death   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 2004  Tyson Foods Inc. was fined $436,000 in the asphyxiation death of a worker at a poultry plant. The Occupational Safety and Health Administration issued citations against the meat processor after investigating the death on Oct. 10 of Jason Edward Kelly, 31 years old, at the company's River Valley animal-feed plant in Texarkana, Ark.
