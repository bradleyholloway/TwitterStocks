 Kellwood Offers To Buy Kasper For $163.6 Million   By By Sally Beatty, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 13, 2003  Kellwood's offer for Kasper consists of $111 million in cash, $40 million in Kellwood common stock, and the assumption of prepaid royalties projected at $12.6 million at the time of closing. It also includes assumption of certain other liabilities. Any transaction is expected to close by September or October and will be subject to bankruptcy court approval. Kasper said in a statement that the transaction has the support of the company's official creditors' committee.
