 Pump Prices Look Ready for Run Uphill, but Image-Wary Oil Firms May Curb Rise   By Solomon, Caleb, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1991  Gasoline industry officials say that if the Persian Gulf crisis isn't resolved peacefully, gasoline pump prices will increase, possibly swiftly. Some of the nation's independent refiners have boosted wholesale gasoline prices 15 cents to 20 cents a gallon between Feb 15-16, 1991.
