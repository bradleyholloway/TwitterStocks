 Reinventing the Wheel / The latest car technology   By Mount, Ian, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2005  What Is It: Ever wish you could see around corners? Now you can -- sort of. Several luxury car makers now offer adaptive headlights that can read a driver's movements and direct the headlights to better illuminate kinks in the road. As a driver turns the steering wheel, a computer chip crunches data such as speed and turning angle, and directs the headlights to swivel (up to about 20 degrees) toward your destination.
