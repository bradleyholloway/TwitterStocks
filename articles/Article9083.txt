 Asides: It's About Hate, Not Guns   The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 1999  In the wake of the tragedy at Columbine High School in Littleton, an editorial applauds Colorado's GOP Secretary of State Vikki Buckley for her speech at the NRA convention in Denver on May 1, 1999 in which she declared: "Guns are not the issue. Hate, what pulled the trigger of violence, is the issue."
