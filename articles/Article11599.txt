 Digital Equipment to Give PC Buyers Lotus Software   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1992  Digital Equipment Corp will give away Lotus Development Corp's popular SmartSuite for Windows, a package that includes the latest versions of Lotus 1-2-3, Ami Pro, Freelance Graphics and the cc:Mail electronic mail package, to buyers of its PCs through Jun 30, 1992.
