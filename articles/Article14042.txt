 Roddick, in Loss, Takes His Final Bow   By Perrotta, Tom, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 2012  Del Potro -- who advanced to face second-seeded Novak Djokovic, the tournament's defending champion, in the quarterfinals -- spoke just a few words after the match and deferred to Roddick. "Since I was a kid I've been coming to this tournament and I felt lucky to sit where all of you are sitting today, to watch this game and see the champions that have come and gone, and I've loved every minute of it," Roddick told the crowd, his voice cracking.
