 Thorn-EMI PLC   The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 1995  Talks between Walt Disney Co and London-based Thorn-EMI PLC about a possible purchase of Thorn-EMI's music operations have broken off, according to people familiar with the talks. Thorn on May 3, 1995 issued a statement denying it is in discussions to sell all or part of its EMI Music business.
