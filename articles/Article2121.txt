 Southern Co.: Net fell 8.6% in 4th period, dividend is raised by 3.3%   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1996  Southern Co said fourth-quarter earnings fell 8.6% to $160 million from $175 million a year earlier. The company boosted its quarterly dividend to 31.5 cents a share, payable Mar 6, 1996 to holders of record Feb 5.
