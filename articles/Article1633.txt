 CBO Sees Gains in Deficits War for Short Term   By Wartzman, Rick, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 1992  In terms of the federal deficit, the CBO sees narrower deficits in the short run than the Bush administration, but is more pessimistic for the long term than the White House. The CBO expects the economic recovery to gain steam by 1993, and believes the deficit will swell to $314 billion for fiscal 1992, compared with the White House's projection of about $334 billion for the year ending Sep 30.
