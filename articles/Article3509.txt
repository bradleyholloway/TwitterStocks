 Sematech: Chip-Industry Research Group Lays Off About 15% of Workers   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 2006  Sematech, a semiconductor-industry research consortium, has laid off 80 employees, or slightly more than 15% of its work force. The Austin, Texas, group said the move was prompted by lower-than-expected revenue this year, as well as rising costs for research and equipment.
