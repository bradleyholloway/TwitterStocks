 New York Offers to Sell Defective Grumman Buses   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 1985  The New York City Transit Authority put up for sale 845 of its problem-plagued Grumman Corp. Flxible buses.  Last February, the buses were taken off the streets permanently following the discovery of mechanical defects. The transit authority subsequently sued Grumman for $324 million in a case that still is pending.
