 Takeoffs & Landings   By By Rafer Guzman, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 9, 2002  Major airlines say more fliers are stepping up to cash in: American and US Airways are reporting more redemptions in June than in the same month last year. During the first six months of this year, Continental saw a 9% increase in mile redemptions. More hotel stays are paid for with loyalty points, too. The Six Continents hotel company, whose brands include Holiday Inn and Crowne Plaza, says that so far this year, members have traded in 58% more points than the same time last year.
