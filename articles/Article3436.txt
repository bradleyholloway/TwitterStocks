 Business Brief -- Preview Systems Inc.: Plans Are Made to Shutter After Sale of Most Assets [1]  The Wall Street Journal,  May 21, 2001  Preview Systems Inc., a onetime contender in the race to distribute music in a protected form, said it plans to wind down its operations after selling most assets to Alladin Knowledge Systems Ltd. Alladin, based in Tel Aviv, Israel, has agreed to pay $5 million for noncash assets that include Preview's portfolio of patents and products.
