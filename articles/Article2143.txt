 Advertising: Taco Bell pulls out of sponsoring Dana Carvey Show   By Beatty, Sally Goll, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1996  Taco Bell said it is pulling all of its commercial commitments from the "Taco Bell Dana Carvey Show," which made its debut on Mar 12, 1996 on ABC. Taco Bell said the format of the show does not work for the company as title sponsor.
