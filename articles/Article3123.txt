 Program Trading   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 2003  Of the five most-active firms overall for the week, Morgan Stanley and UBS AG's UBS Warburg executed most of their program trading as principal for their own accounts. Royal Bank of Canada's RBC Dominion, Deutsche Bank AG's Deutsche Bank Securities, and Goldman Sachs Group Inc. executed all or most of their program trading activity for customers, as agents.
