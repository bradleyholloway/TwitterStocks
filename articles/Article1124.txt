 Australia's Bond Says That Lonrho Isn't a Target Now   By By S. Karene Witcher, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 13, 1989  SYDNEY, Australia -- Australian entrepreneur Alan Bond moved to damp speculation he intends to make a hostile takeover bid for British conglomerate Lonrho PLC, at least in the next few weeks.  "Certainly Lonrho isn't an acquisition target for us at the present time," Mr. Bond said in a television interview. "It's an investment we have in that company." Mr. Bond added that he isn't "necessarily seeking control" of Lonrho.
