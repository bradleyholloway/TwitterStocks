 REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial): Korean Fever   The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 1991  In South Korea's street protests the past two weeks, six protesters have gone beyond the ritual rock-throwing and fire-bombing, and instead set themselves on fire. These are tactics drastic enough to command attention. Once again, TV news world-wide is carrying South Korean scenes of protesters fighting with riot police and calling for the resignation of the country's elected President, Roh Tae Woo. Mr. Roh has said he will not step down. Nor should he.
