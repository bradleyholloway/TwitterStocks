 Grand Met reports pretax profit grew 4.6% for fiscal year   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 2, 1994  Grand Metropolitan PLC said pretax profit for the fiscal year ended Sep 30, 1994 rose 4.6% to 654 million British pounds ($1.02 billion) from BP625 million a year earlier. Sales fell 4.2% to BP7.78 billion.
