 FleetBoston Studies Offering of Shares In Robertson Stephens   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 7, 2000  In 1998, BankBoston Corp., which Fleet bought last year, acquired Robertson Stephens from BankAmerica Corp., Charlotte, N.C., for about $800 million.
