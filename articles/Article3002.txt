 Business Brief -- Xerox Corp.: Financing of $496 Million Is Obtained From GE Capital   The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 2002  Xerox Corp. strengthened its financial position with $496 million from GE Capital and said it expects more financing from the General Electric Co. unit this year. The Stamford, Conn., copying and printing company said the $496 million is secured by portions of lease receivables in the U.S. amortized into 2005.
