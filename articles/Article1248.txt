 Dallas Corp. Agrees To Buy-Out Offer Totaling $180 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1989  Dallas Corp. said it reached a definitive agreement to be acquired for $24 a share, or about $180 million, in a leveraged buy-out led by Bessemer Securities Corp.  The company said that some of its management will participate in the buy-out and will provide about $3 million in equity. Bessemer, a closely-held New York investment firm, has committed to provide about $52 million in equity. Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co. has committed up to $170 million in senior bank financing for the transaction.
