 Corporate News: Alcatel CEO Covets New Technology to Ward Off Rivals   By Gauthier-Villars, David, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 2008  Messrs. Camus and Verwaayen are taking over from Chairman Serge Tchuruk and CEO Patricia Russo, who are departing after two difficult years since the 2006 merger of Alcatel SA of France and Lucent Technologies Inc. of the U.S. Mr. Verwaayen said he would need a few months for an in-depth review of the company's businesses, which makes telephone and Internet equipment.
