 Fidelity Investments By Year's End to Open Japan Brokerage Arm   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 1997  Fidelity Investments plans to open its brokerage arm in Japan for institutional business at the end of this year, and expand its focus to Japan's retail investors toward the end of next year's first quarter, said Donna Morris, a Fidelity senior vice president and manager of the branch.
