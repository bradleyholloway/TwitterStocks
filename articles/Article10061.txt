 PURSUITS; Books: Children's Books   By Meghan Cox Gurdon, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 2007  This novel for young teens is getting a certain amount of buzz in bookstores because of its fashionable theme: the threat that global warming poses to a small, peaceful, vulnerable community. Happily, "First Light" is less a didactic political conversation-starter for the young than it is a neat bit of optimistic science fiction that highlights human ingenuity and survival under dire conditions.
