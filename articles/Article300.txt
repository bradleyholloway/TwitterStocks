The Tosco Corporation, a large independent petroleum refiner, took a big plunge into retailing gasoline yesterday, announcing that it had agreed to acquire the Circle K Corporation in a deal worth at least $710 million.  Circle K is one of the largest operators of convenience stores, and most of its outlets are in the South and Southwest and sell gasoline. A merger will allow Tosco to build up its retail outlets in the Northeast, where it has the rights to market gasoline under the BP, or British Petroleum, brand, said Ravi S.
