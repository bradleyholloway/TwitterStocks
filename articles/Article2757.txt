 Tultex Corp.: Third-quarter net to trail estimates, company says   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1995  Apparel maker Tultex Corp said 1995 third-quarter net income would be below analysts' estimates because of lower-than-expected sales. However, the company said profit would be higher than in the year-ago period, when it earned $7.4 million.
