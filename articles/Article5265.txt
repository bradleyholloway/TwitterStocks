 Sports: NBA union begins lobbying players for acceptance of its last-minute deal   By Thurow, Roger, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1995  Hours after reaching a midnight agreement on a new labor pact with the owners, the embattled National Basketball Players Association began waging a campaign for player ratification and its own survival. The union hopes to stave off a push by dissident agents and players who want to decertify the union and seek better contract terms through the court.
