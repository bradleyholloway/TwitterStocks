 Mortgage-Rate Surge Reverses in July But Economists See Climb Resuming   By By Eugene Carlson, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 1987  Home buyers with fixed-rate mortgages committed to an average effective interest rate of 10.72% in early July, down from 10.86% the previous month, the Federal Home Loan Bank Board reported.  As recently as March, fixed-rate home loans carried an average rate of 9.47% -- a figure that includes the rate offered by mortgage lenders plus points paid by the home buyer. The figures apply to single-family home mortgages maturing in 15 years or more.
