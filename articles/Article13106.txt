 Tyson Foods Inc.: Meat Plant to Close in Maine, Leading to Loss of 285 Jobs   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 2004  Tyson Foods Inc. is closing a meat plant in Portland, Maine, resulting in the loss of 285 jobs. Tyson, a producer of poultry, beef and pork based in Springdale, Ark., said the 65,000-square-foot Jordan's Meats plant it bought three years ago as part of its acquisition of IBP Inc. will close Feb. 1.
