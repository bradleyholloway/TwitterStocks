 Philippines' Curb on Foreign Investment Hurts the Deposit-Rich Mining Industry   By MacDonald, Lawrence, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 1992  The decline of the deposit-rich mining industry of the Philippines, due to provisions in the country's constitution that prohibit foreigners from owning more than 40% of any mine, is discussed. Most foreign investors find they have to supply the majority of capital for a minority interest.
