 Tip of the Week: Shoppers Get Web Rebates   By Spors, Kelly K, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 2004  Shoppers buying a watch from Fossil.com through the site get a 12% rebate. Butterflymall lets shoppers request payout checks once they rack up at least $12 in rebates. There's an automatic $3 credit just for signing up. Other rebate sites include FatWallet.com, Rebateshare.com and Ebates.com. Upromise.com puts rebates in a college-savings fund.
