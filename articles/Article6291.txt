 Technology Brief -- InterActiveCorp.: Profit Rises 5.5% on Strength Of Its Travel-Business Revenue   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 2004  InterActiveCorp's fourth-quarter net income rose 5.5%, thanks to strong revenue growth at its travel business and recent acquisitions. InterActiveCorp, whose properties include Expedia, Hotels.com, Ticketmaster and Home Shopping Network, reported net income of $152.8 million, compared with $144.9 million a year earlier....
