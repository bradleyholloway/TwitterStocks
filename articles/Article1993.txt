 FPL Group to raise payout 5% following last May's reduction   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1995  Utility holding company FPL Group Inc, nine months after startling the utility industry and investors by slashing its dividend, said it would raise its payout 5%. The move, which will increase the quarterly dividend to $.44 a share from $.42, resumes a 47-year tradition of annual dividend increases that was broken in May 1994.
