 Election 2012: Wins in South For Santorum   By O'Connor, Patrick; Murray, Sara, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 2012  Rick Santorum won the Republican presidential primaries in Alabama and Mississippi Tuesday, solidifying his claim to be the favored candidate of the party's conservative base and the main challenger to front-runner Mitt Romney. In Alabama, he carried self-described moderate and liberal voters, but he lost the far larger bloc of conservative voters to his two main rivals by double digits, according to exit polls released by media outlets.
