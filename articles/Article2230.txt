 Phone wars escalate as GTE files a suit and MCI a complaint   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 13, 1996  Local carrier GTE Corp filed a lawsuit in federal court in Johnstown PA alleging that state regulators improperly set a high discount rate that forces GTE to sell bulk local-phone services below cost to rival AT&T Corp. Separately, long distance carrier MCI Communications Corp filed a complaint with California regulators alleging that regional carrier pacific Telesis Group was thwarting MCI's efforts to woo away local-phone companies.
