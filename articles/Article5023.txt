 Imasco Stock Rises on News Of Bid for B.A.T Industries   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 1989  B.A.T owns 48.2 million shares, or 40.4% of Imasco. Imasco, based here, is the parent of Imperial Tobacco, Canada's dominant cigarette manufacturer, and controls Canada Trustco Mortgage Co., the country's largest trust concern, which is similar to a U.S. savings and loan. Imasco also owns Hardee's Food Systems, a large U.S. fast-food concern as well as drugstore chains in both Canada and the U.S.
