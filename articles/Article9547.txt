 Industrial Brief -- Alstom SA: Second Letter Is Received From Same Unknown Bidder   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 2003  Alstom SA received a second letter from the company seeking to make a bid for the Anglo-French engineering company but questioned the letters' seriousness. Alstom said the letters may be an attempt to manipulate its share price and has asked stock-market authorities to investigate.
