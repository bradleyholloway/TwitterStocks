 Miami officials, lobbyist charged in bribery probe   By Brannigan, Martha, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 1996  Federal authorities have charged two Miami city officials, City Commissioner Miller Dawkins, Miami city manager Cesar Odio, and lobbyist Jorge de Cardenas, with soliciting bribes and skimming payments related to contracts with Unisys Corp and Cigna Corp.
