 In Brief   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 2007  LG spokeswoman Judy Pae said the company's factories in South Korea and Poland will make only TVs that are 30 inches or larger. Ms. Pae said LG has an initial outsourcing agreement with Taiwan's Qisda Corp. A spokesman for Qisda declined to comment. LG said Monday it was in talks to outsource small LCD-TV production in Europe to Daewoo Electronics Corp.
