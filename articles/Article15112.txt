 VW is expected to hire an executive to assume Piech's public relations role   By Choi, Audrey, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 1993  Volkswagen AG's supervisory board is expected to name a new senior executive to take charge of VW's public affairs and external relations during an emergency meeting scheduled for Aug 6, 1993 in Germany. The new executive will replace Chairman Ferdinand Piech. The board is said to be unhappy with Piech's handling of the dispute with General Motors Corp. News of the upcoming meeting set off a rush to dump VW shares. The stock dropped 5.5 marks ($3.32).
