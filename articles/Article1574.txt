 Cigna Net Fell 9.2%, Continental Had Loss in Fourth Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1992  Cigna Corp recorded net income of $108 million for the fourth quarter, a 9.2% decline from $119 million a year earlier. Continental Corp posted a net loss for the fourth quarter of $19.3 million, compared with profit of $53.1 million a year earlier.
