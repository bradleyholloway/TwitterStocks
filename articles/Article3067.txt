 Drag of Dresdner Will Add to Loss At Allianz AG --- European Insurer to Feel Pinch From Stock Declines; Fischer to Run Winterthur   By By Marcus Walker and Charles Fleming, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 2002  Yesterday, it was disclosed that Mr. [Fischer] was chosen to lead Credit Suisse Group's Winterthur insurance unit. According to people familiar with the situation, the 39-year-old Mr. Fischer will assume overall responsibility for the life and nonlife insurance divisions of the Winterthur business, which operate under separate CEOs. The appointment is due to be announced in Credit Suisse's third-quarter profit statement today and could put to rest recent speculation about a possible breakup or sale of Winterthur, which has been hit hard in recent months by stock-market declines.
