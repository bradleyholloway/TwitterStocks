 P&G's Folgers Tests A Dark-Roast Coffee Brand, Extending Line   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1990  Procter & Gamble Co., in an extension of its Folgers brand, has begun selling a dark-roast coffee called French Roast in test markets.  Folgers rival Maxwell House, made by the Kraft General Foods unit of Philip Morris Cos., New York, launched a similar line, Rich French Roast, nearly two years ago.
