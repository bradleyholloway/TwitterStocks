 GNI Group Inc.: Write-down, charge lead to fiscal 3rd-period loss   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 1996  GNI Group Inc, a chemical maker and waste-management concern, said it will report a loss for the fiscal third quarter ended Mar 31, 1996 from an accounting write-down and a one-time charge from canceled operations. A year earlier, it reported net income of $914,000.
