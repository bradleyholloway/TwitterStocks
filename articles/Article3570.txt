 Credit Agricole To Launch Offer For Greek Lender   By Larner, Digby, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 2006  Agricole, which has had a partnership with Emporiki since 2000, already owns 8.93% of the Greek lender. Agricole previously said that it wanted to raise its stake in Emporiki but that any move depended on Emporiki addressing its pension liabilities. An Agricole spokesman said the pensions issue has been resolved.
