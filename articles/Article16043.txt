 Los Angeles Times Metro-Section Focus Shifts to `California'   The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 2001  At the same time, the paper has been criticized by some readers for overemphasizing local news and missing bigger-picture California issues. The Times produces four zoned editions, tailoring news to readers in different regions of Southern California, in a strategy that follows industry trends. With each zoned edition competing with different newspapers, the zoned metro sections varied widely in both content and space.
