 Nerco Inc. Says Oregon Court Ruled in Its Favor in Dispute   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 1985  PORTLAND, Ore. -- Nerco Inc. said the Oregon Court of Appeals issued an opinion favorable to Nerco in litigation with Poughkeepsie, N.Y.-based Deerfield Commodities Ltd. concerning a 1981 coal-contract dispute.
