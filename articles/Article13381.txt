 Traders Hedge Against Worries About Lenders   By Cheng, Roger, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 2007  Further dampening industry sentiment, Fremont General Corp., a major subprime home lender, said it was in talks to sell its subprime mortgage business. Shares fell $2.82, about 32%, to $5.89 as of 4 p.m. in NYSE composite trading.  Stifel Nicolaus & Co. analyst Chris Brendler said in a note yesterday that "this industry is now in a downward spiral whereby each negative development fuels additional deterioration in key fundamentals."
