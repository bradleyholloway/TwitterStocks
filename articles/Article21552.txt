 Business and Finance [4]  The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 1991  STOCK PRICES SURGED, sending several market gauges to record levels. The Dow Jones Industrial Average soared 63.86 points to 2945.05. The S&P 500 index and the Nasdaq Composite Index set records, as did the Big Board average and the Dow Jones Equity Market Index.  Former Vernon S&L owner Don R. Dixon was sentenced to five years in prison for misusing funds. The sentence was less than many expected. The judge said Dixon didn't deserve to be punished for Vernon's failure.
