Fifteen works of art by Robert Rauschenberg were removed on Friday from the walls of the Menil Collection in Houston by deputies carrying out a court order in a suit against the artist by a creditor.  The art -- a group of paintings, photographs, collages, prints and sculptures -- was part of a retrospective of Mr. Rauschenberg's work running through May 17.
