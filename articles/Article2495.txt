 THE WALL STREET JOURNAL / NORTHWEST --- Boom May Not Last, Officials Warn   By By Janet I. Tu and Jeff D. Opdyke, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1998  Branch networks are still expanding and new banks are still winning charters in the Northwest, but some observers think the bloom could be off.  In some areas in Oregon, regulators are trying to dissuade new banks from opening because, "the market is already saturated" with community banks, says Ed Simkins, head of the Division of Finance and Securities.
