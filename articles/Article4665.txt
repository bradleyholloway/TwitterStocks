 Dollar Rises against Most Currencies; Yen Is Firm Despite Tokyo Stocks' Fall   By Waite, Thomas L, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1990  In late New York trading, the dollar was quoted at 1.5965 marks, up from 1.5925 marks late Wednesday in New York. The U.S. currency was changing hands at 1.3433 Swiss francs, above 1.3385 francs late Wednesday.  In Tokyo Friday, the dollar was trading soon after the opening at 149.55 yen, down from Thursday's Tokyo close of 149.68 yen. Meanwhile, the U.S. currency was trading at about 1.5930 marks; in Tokyo Thursday, it closed at 1.5913 marks.  Meanwhile, the yen reclaimed ground against European currencies. The mark fell to 93.83 yen in late New York trading yesterday from 94.066 yen late Wednesday. The British pound eased to 279.83 yen from 280.07 yen.
