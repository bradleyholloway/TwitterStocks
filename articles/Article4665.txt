 Stock Volume Rises To Second-Highest Tokyo Total Ever --- A Wall Street Journal News Roundup   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 11, 1986  Tokyo share prices, continuing a volatile week, closed mixed yesterday as volume jumped to the second-highest daily total ever.  In Tokyo, the Nikkei Dow Jones average, which slid 228.04 points Wednesday, fell a further 36.29 points yesterday, to 17469.82. But the stock exchange index rose 3.38 points, to 1358.85.
