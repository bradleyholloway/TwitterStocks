 MarketBeat: The Summer of Our Discontent   By Kansas, Dave, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 2011  Along with concerns about the economy, investors will soon start to grapple more with political events as the presidential campaign kicks into higher gear and the congressional super committee debates how to rein in the federal deficit. Federal Reserve Chairman Ben Bernanke, speaking in Wyoming last month, said the fractious political debate, including the ferocious argument over the debt ceiling, is having -- and may continue to have -- a negative impact on the economy.
