 Airbus lines up 'superjumbo' partners, examines Boeing-McDonnell merger   By Goldsmith, Charles, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1997  Juergen Thomas, the man leading development of Airbus Industrie's 555-seat "superjumbo" passenger jet, said Alenia SpA of Italy intends to take as much as a 10% stake, Saab-Scania AB of Sweden plans a stake of as much as 5% and a South Korean consortium has expressed interest in becoming a substantial risk-sharing partner. Thomas also said Airbus is evaluating the antitrust implications of the recently announced Boeing Co and McDonnell Douglas Corp merger.
