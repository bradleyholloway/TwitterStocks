 Union Carbide, continuing cost cuts, agrees to sell OrganoSilicon division   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 1993  Union Carbide Corp, which has been selling assets in order to cut interest costs and reduce debts, said it agreed to sell its OrganoSilicon division to DLJ Merchant Banking Partners LP for $220 million in cash and $80 million in convertible preferred stock in the new company, which will be called OSi Specialties Inc.
