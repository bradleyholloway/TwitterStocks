 'Mattress Mac' is doing his darndest to capture 2008 Olympics for Texas   By Aalund, Dagmar, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1997  As the IOC prepares to announce which five cities are finalists in the running to host the 2004 Summer Games, the global scramble to capture the 2008 Olympics is already heating up. Competing for an Olympic bid is a marathon event. Just ask Houston's cut-rate furniture king, Jim "Mattress Mac" McIngvale, who is aggressively promoting a Texas bid.
