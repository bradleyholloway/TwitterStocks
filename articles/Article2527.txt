 AMR reports strong profit, USAir a loss   By Hirsch, James S; McCarthy, Michael J, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 21, 1993  AMR Corp, the parent of American Airlines, posted strong third-quarter profit of $118 million compared with a year-earlier loss of $100 million, but USAir Group Inc posted a third-quarter loss of $177.6 million compared to a year-earlier loss of $105.5 million.
