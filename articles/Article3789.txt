 DirecTV Group Inc.: Private-Equity Firm KKR Agrees to Buy Satellite Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 2004  DirecTV Group Inc. agreed to sell its 81%-owned satellite unit PanAmSat Corp. to private-equity firm Kohlberg Kravis Roberts & Co. for $3.55 billion and the assumption of $750 million in debt.
