 Jardine Matheson's Hotel Unit Reports 23% Decline in Profit   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 13, 1991  The Mandarin Oriental International Ltd hotel unit of Jardine Matheson Group reported that profit dropped 23% in the first half to $17.4 million from $22.6 million. Revenue fell 3% to $59.1 million from $60.8 million.
