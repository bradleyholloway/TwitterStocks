 Bank of America Shuffles Its Ranks   By Fitzpatrick, Dan; Sidel, Robin, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 7, 2011  Bank of America Corp. shook up its management team, as Chief Executive Brian Moynihan made his latest bid to steer the nation's biggest bank through the turmoil engulfing global markets.
