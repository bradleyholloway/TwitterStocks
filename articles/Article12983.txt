 Canadian Web Pharmacies Faulted; U.S. Research Firm Says Some Sites Apparently Fail On Safety, Lack Licenses   By Carlisle, Tamsin, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 2003  PharmacyChecker researchers checked out in detail the first 20 Web sites listed in a Google search in September for Canadian online pharmacies that ship medicine to the U.S. In addition to Internet pharmacies that didn't give drug-dispenser affiliations, the White Plains, N.Y., research firm found several that didn't provide secure financial transactions, didn't ensure privacy of information or didn't provide reliable contact information. Only six of the 20 sites met all the firm's criteria for protecting consumer safety and privacy. These were online extensions of licensed, brick-and-mortar pharmacies.
