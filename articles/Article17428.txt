 iTunes Gets An Upgrade Without Missing a Beat   By Mossberg, Walter S, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 2012  Apple's iTunes is the world's most popular computer program for playing, managing and buying music, movies and TV shows. A small arrow next to each song, album, movie, or TV show lets you perform actions like adding an item to a playlist, or jumping to the artist page in your library, or going to the item in the store.
