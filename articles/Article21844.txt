 Heard on the street: Future Communications' stock price surge sparks cries of foul, suspension of trading   By Cohen, Laurie P, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 1993  Short sellers at Aspen Capital and Fahnestock are battling over Future Communications, a small cable TV operator run by CEO Sergio Bosco, with broker Rafi Khan, whose recommendation of Future caused its share price to rise from 6 1/2 to 27 3/8. Shorts believe that Future has been cooking its books and the NASD has halted trading in Future's shares.
