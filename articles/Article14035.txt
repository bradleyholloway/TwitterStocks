 Allscripts Looks For Turnaround   By Kamp, Jon, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 2012  Allscripts is one of several companies in a competitive health information-technology market, which also includes bigger firms like General Electric Co. and Siemens AG. Allscripts used to focus on health records for outpatient facilities, but the company paid about $1.3 billion in 2010 to buy Eclipsys Corp. to add hospital systems.
