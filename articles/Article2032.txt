 Coffee prices fall ahead of Brazil crop forecast   By McGee, Suzanne, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 1995  Coffee-market speculators stampeded for the exits in anticipation of a bearish report on June 13, 1995. In trading on New York's Coffee, Sugar & Cocoa exchange, July coffee fell $.0715 to $1.4725 a pound. In other commodity markets, precious metals eased and cotton prices rallied.
