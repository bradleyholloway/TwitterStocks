 Notable & Quotable   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 1986  Rep. Morris K. Udall (D., Ariz.), asked in the July 1978 issue of TWA Ambassador what the most important change of the previous decade had been:  Unquestionably, it is the fact that America never again will have cheap energy. And although the ramifications of that single change may be more sweeping than any ever faced by this country, I think we can not only adapt to it, but face it head-on and conquer it.
