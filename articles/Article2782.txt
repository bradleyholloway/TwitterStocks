 Business Brief -- Hanger Orthopedic Group Inc.: Net Fell 54% in 3rd Quarter On Integrating Acquisitions   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 2000  Hanger Orthopedic Group Inc., Bethesda, Md., said third-quarter net income fell 54%, in part because of the cost of integrating NovaCare Orthotics and Prosthetics Inc. into its operations.
