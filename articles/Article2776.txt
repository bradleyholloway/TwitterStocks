 Gingrich says Clinton must move right on budget   By Rogers, David; Georges, Christopher, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1995  House Speaker Newt Gingrich said that President Clinton must move far to the right of his own party before any agreement can be reached with the GOP on the balanced budget issue. The common focus on a balanced budget highlights the continuing political divide over the $245 billion tax cut in the GOP's own seven-year plan.
