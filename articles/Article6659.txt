 Another Simplistic Blame-the-Parent Theory About Autism?   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 2007  Hiding behind meaningless correlations, Michael Waldman did "cross a boundary" by suggesting that television viewing causes autism ("Mind and Matter: Is an Economist Qualified to Solve Puzzle of Autism?" page one, Feb. 27). As the mother of three children on the autism spectrum and a regular visiting lecturer at Cornell's Johnson School of Management, I am horrified that this "junk science" has come out of such a fine institution and has been given validation by front-page coverage.
