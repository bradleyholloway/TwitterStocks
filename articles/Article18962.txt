 UPS is still struggling to get service on track, 2 weeks after strike's end   By Brannigan, Martha; Mathews, Anna Wilde, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 1997  Two weeks after the Teamsters' strike ended, United Parcel Service of America Inc is still struggling with delivery delays and customer-service problems. Small companies say they are still seeing problems while big customers say their shipments, for the most part, are going smoothly.
