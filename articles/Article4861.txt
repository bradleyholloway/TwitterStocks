 Toyota to Lift Prices Of U.S. Cars Up to 4%, Starting Early in April --- A Wall Street Journal News Roundup   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 11, 1988  Toyota Motor Corp. will raise its U.S. car prices as much as 4% early next month, company officials in the U.S. and Japan said.  Tsutomu Ohshima, Toyota vice president, said in an interview that the company will boost U.S. prices 2% to 4% in early April. Mr. Ohshima said it is hard for Toyota to maintain current U.S. prices because of the strong Japanese yen.
