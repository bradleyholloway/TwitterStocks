 Senate Blocks Proposed Rules For Auto Safety   By By David Rogers, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 2000  The industry says the agency's so-called static stability formula -- which measures the weight and tire tracks of vehicles -- is overly simplistic. Instead, the National Academy of Sciences would be given $500,000 to evaluate the ratings system and compare the static approach to a test with rollover metrics based on dynamic driving conditions. In a concession to safety advocates, the study could take no more than nine months and would evaluate the merits of requiring the availability of the ratings at the point of sale.
