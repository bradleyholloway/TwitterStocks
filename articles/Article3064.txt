 Asides: Too Young for Russia?   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 1998  An editorial says that Russia's veteran politicians should give 35-year-old Sergei Kiriyenko, Pres Boris Yeltsin's pick to replace ousted Prime Minister Victor Chernomyrdin, a chance to prove that he is not too "green" to help effect reforms, in light of relatively young figures in other countries who have hastened change.
