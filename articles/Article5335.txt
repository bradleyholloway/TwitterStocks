 Venezuela's Perez Vows to Continue Reform Program   By Kamm, Thomas, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1992  Venezuelan President Carlos Andres Perez said he would continue with free-market reforms in spite of pressure to relax the policies in the wake of the Feb 1992 failed coup attempt. Analysts have warned that the government must race against time to prove that the efforts will improve the general well-being of its citizens.
