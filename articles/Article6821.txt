 Microsoft Corp.: Japan Agency to Issue Warning For Antimonopoly Violations   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 2004  In a largely symbolic move, Japan's trade watchdog will issue a warning against Microsoft Corp. this week, but likely won't fine the software giant, a commission official said Friday. Microsoft has been under investigation on suspicion of violating the country's antimonopoly law. The Fair Trade Commission official declined to provide further details.
