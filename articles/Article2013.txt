 Pressure on Chrysler grows to counter Kerkorian bid   By Lipin, Steven; Stern, Gabriella; McGough, Robert, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 1995  On Apr 12, 1995, Kirk Kerkorian's Tracinda Corp made an unsolicited bid to buy the 90% of Chrysler Corp's shares he doesn't own for $22.8 billion. Chrysler is under pressure from major shareholders to respond to the bid with a big share repurchase or other steps, investors and arbitragers said.
