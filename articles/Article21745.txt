 Legal Beat: Aids Victim's   By Woo, Junda; Lambert, Wade, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1992  A trial judge upheld the $8.2 million verdict in favor of Susie Quintana, the plaintiff who sued United Blood Services for providing her with AIDS-tainted blood in 1983. The attorney representing the blood bank argued in part that the death of Quintana during the trial changed the nature of the case and eliminated certain categories of potential damages.
