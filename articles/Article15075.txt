 GM unit wins order halting VW recruiting   By White, Joseph B, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 1993  The dispute between General Motors Corp and Volkswagen AG has developed into a power struggle over VW's top cost-cutter, former GM purchasing boss J. Ignacio Lopez de Arriortua. GM's Adam Opel AG unit convinced a district court in Frankfurt am Main on Apr 2, 1993 to issue a temporary injunction to halt efforts by VW and Lopez to lure managers away from Opel and GM Europe.
