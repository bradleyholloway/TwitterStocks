 High court gives accountants a shield against civil racketeering lawsuits   By Barrett, Paul M, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1993  The Supreme Court, in a ruling that gave the embattled accounting profession a new shield against civil racketeering lawsuits, said outside professionals who don't help run corrupt businesses can't be sued under RICO. The ruling came in a case involving accounting firm Ernst & Young.
