 Bookmarks   By By Lionel Shriver, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 2002  Simple rather than simplistic, Mr. [Richard Russo]'s style is unpretentious and direct. He neither deploys arty ambiguity nor cheaply ties loose ends into a bow. Each tale is so straightforward and engaging from the first page that Mr. Russo makes writing short stories seem effortless. While this is doubtless an illusion, they are effortless to read.
