Penney Digs Deeper Sales Hole   By Mattioli, Dana; Talley, Karen, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 2012  Penney's sales over the first nine months of its fiscal year have fallen by $2.7 billion, nearly equivalent to the annual revenue of store chain Saks Inc. Penney reported a third-quarter loss of $123 million, or 56 cents a share -- narrower than the $143 million a year earlier as the company cut costs and spent less on restructuring.
