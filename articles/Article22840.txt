The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 2002  BRIEFS: Eisner Communications of Baltimore landed domestic ad duties for US Airways, work previously handled by Interpublic Group's McCann-Erickson. Billings are estimated at $20 million. Most recent efforts created for the airline have promoted new Caribbean destinations. Eisner has about $248 million in billings from clients such as the Maryland State Lottery and AMF Bowling Worldwide. . . .
