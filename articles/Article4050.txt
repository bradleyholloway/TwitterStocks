 'Princess and the Frog' Tops U.S. Box Office   By Smith, Ethan, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2009  "The Princess and the Frog" opened at No. 1 in the U.S. box-office rankings, taking in $25 million its first weekend in wide release, amid rave reviews and anticipation about Walt Disney Co.'s return to traditional animation techniques for the first time in six years.
