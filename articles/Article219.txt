Bobbie and Jonathan Leigh of New York and Saltaire, L.I., have announced the engagement of their daughter Alison Leigh to Dr. Michael Stephen Katz, a son of Dr. and Mrs. Gerson Katz of Johannesburg. An April wedding is planned.  Miss Leigh, 33 years old, graduated from Emory University and received a law degree from Syracuse University. She is an assistant district attorney in Philadelphia.
