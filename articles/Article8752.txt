 Oxford Health net nearly doubles on enrollment rise   By Winslow, Ron, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 1996  Oxford Health Plans Inc, avoiding the earnings rout that has bloodied much of the managed care industry in 1996, said second-quarter profit nearly doubled on the strength of soaring enrollment and lower administrative expenses. The company said second-quarter net income rose 98% to $22.5 million, from $11.3 million in the year-earlier period.
