 RJR Nabisco Holdings Corp.: Company Uses Bank Loans to Repurchase Junk Bonds   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 25, 1992  RJR Nabisco Holdings Corp lightened its debt load a bit by using low-cost bank loans to retire nearly $1 billion of junk bonds yielding an average of 14.9%. As a result, the company will incur a $160 million extraordinary charge in its first-quarter results.
