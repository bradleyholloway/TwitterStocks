 UBS Profit Rises As Costs Are Cut, Market Improves   By Gelnar, Martin, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 2003  UBS, which is Switzerland's largest bank and the world's biggest asset manager, said second-quarter net climbed to 1.64 billion Swiss francs ($1.2 billion) from 1.33 billion francs a year earlier and from 1.21 billion francs in the first quarter. The results beat a forecast of 1.32 billion francs from analysts polled by Dow Jones Newswires. Wednesday in Zurich, the UBS stock price declined 1.7% to 79.10 francs.
