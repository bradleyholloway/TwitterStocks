 Olivetti Stakes Its Future on Partnership   By Hudson, Richard L, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 13, 1992  The alliance between Ing. C. Olivetti & Co and Digital Equipment Corp, in which Olivetti will re-sell the new Digital Alpha AXP computer in Europe under its own name, is one that holds much promise for Olivetti. Thus far, the five-month-old relationship between Digital and Olivetti has been described as a success by analysts. The implications of the relationship for Olivetti and its suppliers are examined.
