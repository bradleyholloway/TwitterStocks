 Media Brief -- AOL Time Warner Inc.: Turner Will Remain on Board After His Managerial Stepdown   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 2003  Ted Turner, departing vice-chairman of AOL Time Warner Inc., decided to stay on the board after he steps down from his management position in May, an AOL spokesman said. Earlier this week Mr. Turner indicated he was leaning toward staying. Mr. Turner is one of AOL's biggest individual shareholders.
