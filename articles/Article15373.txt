 Kimberly-Clark Corp. defends Scott merger in EU investigation   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 1995  Kimberly-Clark Corp, citing market data, is contesting concerns among EU antitrust offficials that its proposed merger with Scott Paper Co will impede competition. In a joint statement, the two companies said their $7.36 billion merger plan, under investigation by the European Commission, will "not create a dominant position in the European tissue market."
