 Factory Orders Increased 2.3% during June   By Harper, Lucinda, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 3, 1992  The Commerce Department reported that US factory orders rose 2.3% to $244.2 billion in Jun 1992 after falling 0.9% in May, suggesting that the recovery in the manufacturing sector is still on track. Separately, the department said personal income, adjusted for inflation, fell 0.2% in June after rising 0.1% in May.
