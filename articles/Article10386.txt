 LSI Looks to Disk-Drive Market   By DiColo, Jerry A, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 2010  With Texas Instruments Inc., Renesas Technology Corp. and others shifting their focus from disk drives, some drive makers began buying all of their chips from a single supplier, Marvell, even though they often prefer to have more than one source. Because of long product-replacement cycles, LSI only now has an opportunity to demonstrate the success of its turnaround with its recent design wins, which represent plans with customers to use certain parts in upcoming products and often are used to gauge future revenue opportunities.
