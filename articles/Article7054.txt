 Me-First LeBron Was Not Our Guy   By Gay, Jason, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 2010  In a vein-popping public letter which appears to have been written after consuming 2,000 Red Bulls, Cleveland Cavaliers owner Dan Gilbert blasted his former superstar for a "shameful display of selfishness and betrayal" and promised the Cavs would win an NBA title before the Heat.
