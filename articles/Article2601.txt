 Packard Children To Split Efforts In Running Entity   By By George Anders, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 1999  The late Mr. Packard's flagship foundation, the David & Lucile Packard Foundation, said it is giving $1.5 billion in stock and cash to the Packard Humanities Institute. That entity had been operating with just a few million dollars in assets. It is managed by the late Mr. Packard's oldest son, David Woodley Packard. The two charitable entities are based in Los Altos, Calif.
