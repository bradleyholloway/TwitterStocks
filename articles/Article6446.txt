 BlackBerry Maker Seeks Enforcement Of Patent Accord   By Heinzl, Mark, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 2005  RIM Chairman Jim Balsillie accused NTP of refusing to live up to its agreement and insisted RIM isn't seeking to reduce the amount it plans to pay or make other changes to the pact. Mr. Balsillie declined to disclose the sticking points involved in the impasse. He maintained the March agreement is "binding" and "unambiguous" about allowing RIM to carry on with its business free from further litigation or payments to NTP.
