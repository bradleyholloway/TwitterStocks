 Trump Is Facing Suit over Casinos from Bondholders   By Lambert, Wade; Barsky, Neil, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 1990  A lawsuit filed on behalf of bondholders of two of Donald Trump's casinos accuses the developer of a breach of fiduciary duty in connection with the opening of his Taj Mahal operation. The suit charges that the Taj Mahal opening hurt the performance of Trump Plaza and Trump's Castle.
