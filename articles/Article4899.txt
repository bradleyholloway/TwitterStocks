 Beverage Firms, Led by Pepsi and Coke, Expected to Post Higher Quarterly Net   By Cooper, Helene, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1992  Analysts expect the major beverage companies to report mostly higher second-quarter earnings as the industry emerges from price competition and consumer skittishness. PepsiCo Inc is expected to post earnings gains of at least 20%. Coca-Cola Co and Anheuser-Busch Cos also expect gains.
