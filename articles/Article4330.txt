 The Americanization of Wakabayashi   By Stevens, Charles W, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 9, 1987  Eishi Wakabayashi, executive vice president of currency and bond trading for Nippon Kakumaru International Inc., is a well respected dollar-yen exchange rate forecaster. He left the Bank of Tokyo because of his desire for a share of the trading profits. Wakabayashi currently forsees a rebound of the dollar to 180 yen, and has sold his apartment in Tokyo and converted the yen to dollars.
