 Westinghouse Profit Rose 13% in Quarter, Exceeding Estimates   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1988  Westinghouse Electric Corp., bouyed by sharp improvements in several business segments, said third-quarter profit rose 13%.  Net income was $204.6 million, or $1.41 a share, compared with $180.6 million, or $1.25 a share, a year earlier. Sales rose nearly 11% to $2.87 billion from $2.59 billion.  The company didn't break out results for its various businesses, but said its Electronic Systems, Energy, Utility Systems and Industries segments had sharply higher operating profits in the quarter. Operating profit at the company's Broadcasting segment also rose, the company said.
