 Letters to the Editor: The Case Against the Income Tax   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 1996  Point one: As you know, all current state income taxes rely on the federal system for their basic infrastructure. Bill Archer [chairman of the U.S. House Ways and Means Committee] has sworn to "pull the IRS out by its roots," leaving nothing to which any ill-conceived state income tax could be appended.  Governor [Bush] is on exactly the right track when he says, "Don't come back recommending a state income tax" ("Why a State Income Tax May Make Sense for Texas," March 27).
