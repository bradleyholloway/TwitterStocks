 Stock Funds Declined 15.52% in Third Quarter   By Clements, Jonathan, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1990  Stock funds declined for a 15.52% loss in the third quarter of 1990, their biggest drop since a 20.38% thrashing in the 1987 fourth quarter, which included the stock market crash. Gold and world income funds were the only exception, and those gains were little more than 4%.
