 Harcourt Purchases Specialty Publisher From Times Mirror   The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 1998  CHESTNUT HILL, Mass. -- Harcourt General Inc., seeking to expand its health-sciences publishing business, said it agreed to acquire Times Mirror Co.'s Mosby Inc. unit for $415 million in cash.  The deal marks Harcourt's second acquisition of a health-science publisher in two years, making it a leader in that field. Last year it bought Pearson PLC's Churchill Livingstone unit for about $92.5 million. Mosby publishes health-sciences books and periodicals and has annual revenue of about $225 million.
