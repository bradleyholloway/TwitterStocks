 Legal Beat: Gaston & Snow   By Moses, Jonathan M; Harlan, Christi, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 1991  Harrison Goldin, former comptroller for New York City, was named the bankruptcy trustee for the former Boston-based law firm of Gaston & Snow, which filed for protection in Oct 1991. Goldin will oversee the reorganization plan of the former firm.
