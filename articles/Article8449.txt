 Healthy rise in factory employment shows labor market on solid footing   By Harper, Lucinda, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 1994  The Labor Department reported that only 62,000 jobs were created in Jan 1994, a sharp decline in the nearly 200,000 jobs the economy averaged in the four months before. The manufacturing sector gained 26,000 of the jobs, while the unemployment figure was 6.7%.
