 Delta Air Is Offering Raises and Incentives As Union Efforts Rise   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 2000  Delta Air Lines, facing intense union organizing efforts, said it will give a 3% pay raise to nonunion workers in the U.S., offer merit pay for nonmanagement supervisors and initiate an early-retirement plan.  Separately, the airline said that despite recent increases in the cost of aviation fuel, it expects to exceed current Wall Street expectations of $1.06 a diluted share for the fourth quarter. Earnings are scheduled for release Tuesday.
