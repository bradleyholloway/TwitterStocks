 Reich unveils administration plans for central 're-employment system'   By Salwen, Kevin G, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1993  Labor Secretary Robert Reich unveiled a "re-employment system" that would overhaul the way federal unemployment benefits are managed by creating a central system for financial aid, career advice, job search help and training. The plan would be targeted at workers whose jobs are cut by military downsizing, imports, environmental policy or changes in technology.
