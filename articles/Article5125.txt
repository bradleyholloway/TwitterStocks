 Stocks Follow Bonds Higher; Dollar Is Mixed   By Sease, Douglas R, The Wall Street Journal,  May 11, 1990  On May 10, 1990 stock prices followed the bond market higher despite the unspectacular reception given the Treasury's sale of $10 billion in 30-year bonds. The Dow Jones Industrial Average rose 5.63 points to end at 2738.51.
