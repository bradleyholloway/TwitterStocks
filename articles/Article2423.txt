 Reader Q&A: Have Pouch, Will Travel   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 13, 1998  One of the best sources for courier information is the International Association of Air Travel Couriers, in Lake Worth, Fla. This organization tracks all companies that use members of the public as couriers and has a web site (http://www.courier.org) that covers the basics. According to the IAATC, North American courier flights depart from New York, Miami, Chicago, San Francisco, Los Angeles, Toronto, Montreal and Vancouver, British Columbia, on a daily basis to a variety of international destinations. For a fee, the IAATC provides daily updates on availability.
