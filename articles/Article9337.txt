 Marketing Brief -- Wal-Mart Stores Inc.: Discounter Retains Its Place As Nation's Largest Retailer   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 2001  Wal-Mart Stores Inc., Bentonville, Ark., retained its position as the largest U.S. retailer in terms of annual sales, and food retailer Kroger Co., Cincinnati, stayed in the No. 2 spot, according to an annual survey by a retail-trade group.
