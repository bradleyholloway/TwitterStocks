 Toro to repurchase shares   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 9, 1995  Toro Co, a lawn equipment company, said its board has authorized the repurchase of up to 600,000 shares of its common stock outstanding. The purchases will be made from time to time at market price during an unspecified period. The stock will be used to fulfill obligations under a variety of benefit plans and for general corporate purposes.
