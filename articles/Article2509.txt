 Dun & Bradstreet plans initial public offering for its Gartner Group   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 20, 1993  Dun & Bradstreet Corp filed with the SEC for an initial public offering of its Gartner Group Inc unit, four months after it acquired control of the information services company. Following the offering of 2.3 million Class A common shares, D&B will continue to hold about 57% of the unit.
