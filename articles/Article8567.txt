 Heinz agrees to acquire Quaker Oats' North American pet-food operations   By Murray, Matt; Duff, Christina, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 1995  In a move that will nearly double its pet-food business and bring it such well-known brands as Gravy Train and Kibbles 'n Bits, H. J. Heinz Co agreed to acquire Quaker Oats Co's North American pet-food operations for $725 million.
