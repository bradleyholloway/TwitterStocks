 Industrial Brief -- Danaher Corp.: U.S. Dental-Imaging Company, Danish Concern to Be Bought   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 2003  Danaher Corp. will buy a Danish maker of blood-gas analyzers and a U.S. maker of dental-imaging products. Danaher, Washington, agreed to acquire Radiometer AS, Copenhagen, for $730 million.
