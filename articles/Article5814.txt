 TRW's sale of unit is likely to be only a first step   By Norton, Erle; Putka, Gary, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1996  An agreement by a buyout group led by Thomas H. Lee Co and Bain Capital Inc to acquire most of TRW Inc's Information Systems & Services unit for $1.01 billion presages further acquisitions by both the buyers and the seller. Insiders at Lee and Bain say they intend to use the TRW unit to acquire more information companies. TRW is also on the acquisition trail.
