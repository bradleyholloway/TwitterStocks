 Sell-off sends dollar to 20-month low against mark, record low of 97.03 yen   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 1994  The dollar fell prey to a frontal assault on Jul 11, 1994 on perceptions that the US and its Group of Seven partners won't take any serious action to salvage the teetering currency. In late New York trading, the dollar was quoted at 1.5290 marks and 97.73 yen, down from DM1.5620 and Y98.05 on Jul 8.
