 A stabilized population   By Oberlink, Ric, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1993  Ric Oberlink responds to an Oct 6, 1993 article about the Californians for Population Stabilization group, or CAPS. Oberlink says CAPS does support a number of measures aimed at reducing population growth so that the population may be stabilized in California and the US.
