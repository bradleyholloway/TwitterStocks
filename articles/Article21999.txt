 The auto show is driving Detroit to new heights of showmanship   By Lavin, Douglas, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 1995  The North American International Auto Show begins on Jan 3, 1995, and Ford Motor Co is promising that its redesigned Taurus will take center stage when it is unveiled. However, Chrysler Corp has other ideas, and hopes to steal the show with its redesigned minivan.
