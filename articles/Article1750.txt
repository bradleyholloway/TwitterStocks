 Nokia Crisis Deepens, Shares Plunge   By Troianovski, Anton; Moen, Arild, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 2012  In the U.S. and in other big markets for high-end smartphones, Nokia has struggled to gain traction despite a big bet on high-end devices powered by new software developed by Microsoft Corp. Nokia is also losing its dominance in developing countries like India and in the Middle East as consumers increasingly dump their basic Nokia phones for cheap smartphones running Google Inc.'s Android operating system.
