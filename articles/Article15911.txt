 B Harried Doctors Are to Get B A Bit of Paperwork Relief   By By Renae Merle, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 2000  Starting in June, the more than 6,000 physicians in the area will fill out a single credentials application for all the major health plans in North Texas. The applications are required by health plans to verify academic and professional histories as well as check for malpractice claims, disciplinary actions and loss of hospital privileges. Currently, doctors complain, they must fill out separate applications -- some running to 30 pages -- for each of a dozen plans they may belong to.
