 Schwab Unveils Rewards Card, Following Merrill and Citigroup   By Lieber, Ron, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 2004  The Schwab card is missing a few luxe perks that competitors offer. Smith Barney customers can trade their points for American Airlines miles, for instance, though they have to pay an annual fee for this version of the card. Meanwhile, Merrill Lynch cardholders who reach certain spending thresholds get free nights at Ritz-Carlton hotels. The Merrill card has no annual fee.
