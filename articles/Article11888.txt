 Bouygues wins a second contract, posts profit rise and will seek new capital   By Kamm, Thomas, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 1994  Bouygues SA said net income rose 19% in the first half and announced plans for a capital increase totaling one billion francs ($189 million) to fund its new expansion plans, which include a major acquisition in the construction business. On Oct 4, 1994, Bouygues won a hotly contested battle to operate France's new mobile-telephone network.
