 Mother of Mercy, Will Drexel Be the End of RICO?   By Crovitz, L Gordon, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 1990  L. Gordon Crovitz expresses the hope that the fall of Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc will also lead to the discontinuation of the RICO law, saying that the statute has been misapplied so many times that it is no longer effective.
