 Business Brief -- Lucent Technologies Inc.: Agere Systems Files a Plan For Initial Public Offering   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 2000  Agere Systems Inc., the microelectronics group being spun off by Lucent Technologies Inc., filed a plan to go public in an initial offering of stock. The company, based in Allentown, Pa., put the value of the planned offering at $100 million, but that is likely a nominal figure used for the filing.
