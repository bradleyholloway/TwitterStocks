 Business Brief -- RELIANCE ACCEPTANCE GROUP INC.: Chapter 11 Filing Is Made; Rival to Service Car Loans   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 1998  In Feb 1998, Reliance Acceptance Group Inc filed for Chapter 11 protection and agreed to have its used-car lender competitor, Ugly Duckling Corp, service its $226 million auto-loan portfolio.
