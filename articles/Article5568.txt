 Philanthropy: How a litte prep school learned that big money talks   By Stecklow, Steve, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 1994  After billionaire Walter Annenberg announced that he would give $100 million to the prep school he had attended, the small but respected school was inundated with offers from architects, investments advisers and others. The school is now very well known because of the gift. The school has decided to spend the income from the gift on financial aid, educational reform initiatives and increased faculty salaries.
