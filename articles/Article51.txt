 Eurobond Traders Spend Slow Session Watching the Dollar --- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 25, 1987  The dollar's weakness continued to occupy Eurobond market participants yesterday, even though the U.S. currency had declined only slightly from last week's levels.  In primary markets, General Motors Acceptance Corp. attempted to capitalize on the interest in non-dollar sectors, launching its first Eurobond denominated in European currency units.
