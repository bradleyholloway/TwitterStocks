 Corporate News: Pepsi to Cut Plastic Used in Bottles; Attempt Is Latest To Reduce Impact On Environment   By McKay, Betsy, The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 2008  The new half-liter (16.9-oz.) bottle contains at least 20% less plastic than the one it is replacing, and it will be used for some versions of Lipton iced tea, Tropicana juice drinks, a flavored water called Aquafina FlavorSplash and an enhanced water known as Aquafina Alive, Pepsi said.
