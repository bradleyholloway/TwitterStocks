 International Corporate Report: Peugeot Posts 87% Increase In 1987 Consolidated Profit   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 1988  PEUGEOT S.A. said consolidated net income after payments to minority interests surged 87% last year, to 6.71 billion French francs ($1.19 billion) from 3.59 billion francs in 1986.  Revenue rose 13% last year, to 118.17 billion francs ($20.89 billion) from 104.95 billion francs. Debt was reduced by about one third, to 19 billion francs from slightly over 30 billion francs, while capital and reserves nearly doubled, to 20.5 billion francs from 10.5 billion francs.
