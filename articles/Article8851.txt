 Sears to pay between $178 million and $265 million to settle dispute   By Kerber, Ross, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 1997  Sears, Roebuck & Co said it will pay between $178 million and $265 million and take an undetermined second-quarter 1997 charge to settle a dispute stemming from its admittedly overzealous collection of credit-card debts.
