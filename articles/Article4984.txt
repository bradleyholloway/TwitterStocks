 Ryan, Beck & Co.: Profit more than doubled for firm's fiscal 1st quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 9, 1993  Investment banking firm Ryan, Beck & Co said its net income more than doubled to $1.9 million for its fiscal first quarter ended Mar 26, 1993 from $756,000 a year earlier. The company declared a special cash dividend of $.34 a share in addition to the regular quarterly dividend of $.01.
