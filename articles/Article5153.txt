 Peat Marwick to pay U.S. $186.5 million to settle claims tied to bank failures   By Chang, Yi-Hsin, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1994  KPMG Peat Marwick agreed to pay the federal government $186.5 million to settle claims for alleged accounting and auditing failures at failed financial institutions. Under the agreement, Peat Marwick neither admitted nor denied wrongdoing.
