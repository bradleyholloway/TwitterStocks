 FFB Likely to Need Funds From Congress, Higher Lending Fees   The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 1997  The FFB, a financing entity within the Treasury Department, had an accumulated deficit of $2.5 billion on Sept. 30, the end of the last fiscal year. That gap resulted from Congress's authorization for certain borrowers whose loans are guaranteed by the Rural Utilities Services and by the Defense Security Assistance Agency to prepay their FFB loans on preferential terms.
