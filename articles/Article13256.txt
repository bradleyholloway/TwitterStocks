 Ahead of the Tape   By Lahart, Justin, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 2006  When Intel said its first-quarter sales would miss estimates, its shares fell by just 1% -- a meager drop compared with what's happened in the past when the Santa Clara, Calif., chip maker has disappointed investors. Maybe that isn't surprising considering that just two weeks earlier, Intel customer Dell issued a first-quarter sales outlook that fell short of expectations.
