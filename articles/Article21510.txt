 Hongkong & Shanghai Stake in Midland May Stand, but Merger Remains Possible   By Owens, Cynthia, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1990  Hongkong & Shanghai Banking Corp is unlikely to increase its 14.9% stake in Britain's Midland Bank PLC anytime soon after their standstill agreement lapses, but over time they are likely to inch toward a merger.
