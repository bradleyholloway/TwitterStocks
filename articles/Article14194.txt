 People Express Plans To Increase Its Fares On Route to Florida   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 1985  NEWARK, N.J. -- People Express Airlines said it plans to extend many of its low fares after the Christmas travel season, but will raise fares on heavily-traveled Florida routes from its current deeply discounted rates.  After the holiday season, fares between Newark and a number of Florida cities -- including Miami, West Palm Beach and Orlando -- will go up to $69 one-way on Monday through Thursday, compared with the current $49. The weekend fare will be $99. People's current weekend fare for these routes is $69. Those fares had been $99 off-peak and $129 peak.
