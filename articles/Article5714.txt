 Profit at Men's Wearhouse Inc. To Meet Target --- Sales of Suits, Casual Wear Were Strong at Retailer In Fiscal First Quarter   By By Calmetta Y. Coleman, The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 1999  FREMONT, Calif. -- Men's Wearhouse Inc. "will meet or slightly exceed earnings estimates" for its fiscal first quarter, according to David Edwab, president of the men's clothing retailer.  Mr. Edwab said sales of suits and casual wear were strong during the quarter, which ended May 1, offsetting flat sales of sports coats. Men's Wearhouse plans to report complete financial results for the quarter on May 20.
