 Corporate News: Satyam Investigators to Look at Maytas Companies --- Raju Family Property Holdings Have Gained Amid Boom in Hyderabad; Three Are Named to Satyam Board   By Bellman, Eric, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 2009  The investigation of fraud at India's Satyam Computer Systems Ltd. is spreading to the property and infrastructure companies connected to Satyam's disgraced former chairman B. Ramalinga Raju, a development that highlights the family's extensive property and other ventures in this southern Indian city. Maytas Properties' flagship project is Maytas Hill County, a sprawling gated community on the rocky outskirts of Hyderabad, 10 minutes' drive from High Tech City, the home of many of Hyderabad's technology businesses, including Satyam.
