 Baxter to buy back $500 million in shares to boost stock price   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1995  Hoping to further boost its share price, Baxter International Inc said it expects to buy back $500 million worth of its shares over the next two years. The hospital-supply and medical-care company has a market capitalization of $8.46 billion, with its 282 million shares outstanding closing at $30 on Feb 13, 1995.
