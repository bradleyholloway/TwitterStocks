 Regional Report: Econ Focus / Consumer Confidence   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 2001  Consumer confidence slumped in all regions except the Plains states in September compared with August, as the terrorist attacks further worried Americans already shaken by a souring economy. The 16.4-point drop-off in overall U.S. confidence since August contributed to a whopping 45-point year-over-year decline in the Conference Board's September index.
