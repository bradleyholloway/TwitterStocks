 International Corporate Report [2]  The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1985  DEAK-PERERA FAR EAST LTD. was ordered liquidated by Hong Kong's Supreme Court as the colony's attorney general and a group of investors considered filing charges against senior officials of the company. Deak-Perera Far East ceased operating in December after Deak & Co. of New York and two other U.S. affiliates filed for reorganization under the U.S. Bankruptcy Code.
