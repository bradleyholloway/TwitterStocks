 Sen. Kerry Is Set To Undergo Surgery For Prostate Cancer   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 2003  After routine medical tests late last year indicated the possibility of prostate cancer, Mr. [John Kerry], 59 years old, had a biopsy and learned Dec. 24 he had the disease, according to a chronology released by aides. Mr. Kerry's father died of prostate cancer at age 85.  The surgery will slow Mr. Kerry at a time when candidates in the wide-open Democratic nomination race are sprinting to raise money and court voters in key early states such as Iowa and New Hampshire. Mr. Kerry, a decorated veteran of the Vietnam War, has enjoyed early success on both fronts.
