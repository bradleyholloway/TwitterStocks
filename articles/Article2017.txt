 Chrysler profit tumbled 37% in 1st quarter   By Simison, Robert L, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 1995  A day after Chrysler Corp was stunned by Kirk Kerkorian's $20 billion takeover bid, Chrysler reported a 37% drop in first-quarter earnings, which totaled $592 million compared with $938 million in the same period a year earlier.
