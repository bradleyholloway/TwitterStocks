 Credit-Card Bonds Fight A Tougher Debt Market   By Saha-Bubna, Aparajita; Natarajan, Prabha, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 2008  Citigroup Inc. reported a second-quarter loss on securities made up of pools of credit-card loans, also indicating growing wariness among investors in a corner of the asset-backed market that had so far proved resilient. The credit-card issuer is also vulnerable because it has a large exposure to so-called private-label credit cards -- those affiliated with a particular retailer -- with a $55.2 billion portfolio.
