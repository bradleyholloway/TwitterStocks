 Foundation Plans to Give $70 Million for Telescope   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1985  PASADENA, Calif. -- A private foundation is proposing a $70 million grant to the California Institute of Technology to construct what would be the world's largest optical telescope.  The W.M. Keck Foundation of Los Angeles said the grant is contingent upon the completion of a feasibility study and the signing of contracts between the foundation, Caltech and the University of California, which would provide operating funds once the telescope is completed.
