 Sierra Pacific Says Four Executives Are Departing   The Wall Street Journal,  May 21, 2002  In a statement, Sierra Pacific said that Messrs. [Steve Oldham] and [Doug Ponn] are retiring, Mr. [Paul Heagen] "will return to his own consulting business" and Mr. [Mark Ruelle] "is leaving to pursue other opportunities." None of the men could be reached for comment and the company declined to give their ages.
