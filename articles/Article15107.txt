 Microsoft, in change of strategy, seeks minority stakes in multimedia ventures   By Zachary, G Pascal, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 1993  Discarding its past strategy, Microsoft Corp is seeking to become a minority investor in many multimedia ventures, according to Chairman William Gates III. Gates also said his well-publicized talks with the two biggest US cable operators, Tele-Communications Inc and Time Warner Inc, to form a venture known as CableSoft haven't resulted in any agreement.
