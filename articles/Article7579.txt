 Copperweld Holders To Vote on Spinoff Of Steel Bar Business   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1986  Copperweld Corp. said it will ask shareholders at a special meeting Dec. 3 to approve the spinoff of its depressed specialty steel bar business as part of a restructuring.  The proposal, which the company said it was considering last May, will authorize the distribution of all common shares of CSC Industries, a subsidiary that holds the stock of Copperweld Steel, to Copperweld shareholders.
