 Bernard Chaus Inc.: Loss widened to $8.9 million in the fiscal first quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1994  Charges for unusual items and restructuring caused women's apparel maker Bernard Chaus Inc to post a loss for the fiscal first quarter ended Sep 30, 1994 of $8.9 million, compared to a $1.7 million loss the year before.
