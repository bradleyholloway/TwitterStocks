 PURSUITS; Picks -- Music: Ring Frenzy; More opera houses cash in on a guaranteed ticket-seller, catering to diehard fans   By Levy, Paul, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 2006  IF OPERA HAD a World Cup, it would be the event going on now in Bayreuth, Germany: a new production of Richard Wagner's "Ring" cycle in the opera house that Wagner designed. It's an occurrence that takes place only about every six years, with a 10-year waiting list for tickets.
