 Thursday's markets: Stocks decline; Bonds retreat on Vinik news   By McGeehan, Patrick, The Wall Street Journal,  May 24, 1996  Stocks declined on May 23, 1996 despite a smoking-hot rally in tobacco stocks, while bonds retreated on news of the resignation of Fidelity Magellan chief Jeff Vinik.
