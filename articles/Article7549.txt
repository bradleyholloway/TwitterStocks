 Alaska Air Group Inc. To Buy Jet America In $14.3 Million Pact   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 1986  The acquisition is subject to a restructuring of lease and loan agreements covering certain Jet America aircraft, Alaska Air said. It also is subject to a definitive agreement, clearance by the U.S. Transportation Department and approval by Alaska Air directors and Jet America shareholders. A spokesman said Alaska Air expects to begin a tender offer next week, and that the transaction is expected to close in about 90 days.
