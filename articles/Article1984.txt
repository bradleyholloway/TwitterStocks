 Saatchi employees persuaded to reject Maurice Saatchi's offer to jump ship   By Goldman, Kevin, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1995  Ousted Saatchi & Saatchi PLC co-founder Maurice Saatchi invited as many as 40 of the advertising concern's employees to his house in week of Jan 9, 1995 to ask them to join his new firm, according to sources. Many planned to accept the offer, but Saatchi CEO Charles Scott talked them out of it.
