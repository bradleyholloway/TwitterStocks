 Huffy Discontinues Fitness-Gear Business, Sees 3rd-Period Charge   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 6, 1985  DAYTON, Ohio -- Huffy Corp. said it discontinued its physical-fitness product line and will take a $1.8 million to $2.2 million after-tax charge in the third quarter.  Huffy, a bicycle concern with interests in juvenile products and retail-service businesses, also said the move would eliminate 100 jobs at a Milwaukee plant.
