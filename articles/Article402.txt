Following its tradition of recruiting innovators, the Wadsworth Atheneum Museum of Art in Hartford has selected relatively unknown experimental architects from the Netherlands for an expansion and renovation of the institution, the oldest public art museum in the United States.  Ben van Berkel and Caroline Bos of UN (for United Network) Studio, based in Amsterdam, won not with a proposed design so much as with a collaborative approach that the architects compare to John Cage's performance method of strolling amid the orchestra rather than conducting from out front.
