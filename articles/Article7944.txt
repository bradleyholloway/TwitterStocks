 Dividend News: Atlantic Financial Federal to Suspend Its Stock Dividends   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 1989  Atlantic Financial Federal said it was suspending stock dividends because of losses totaling $161.1 million, or $39.33 a share, for the fiscal year ended Sept. 30.  Donald R. Caldwell, president and chief executive officer, said the Bala Cynwyd, Pa., thrift is unable to comply with "core, tangible and risk-based capital standards" required by recent legislation. He said this could result in regulatory agencies asserting greater control over the concern, "including the appointment of a conservator or receiver for the company."
