 Hynix Semiconductor Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 2004  Hynix Semiconductor Inc. of South Korea said it ended talks with Citigroup Inc.'s venture-capital arm to sell its nonmemory-chip operations. "After reviewing the market conditions, the company and creditors felt it was difficult to accept Citigroup's offer," Hynix said.
