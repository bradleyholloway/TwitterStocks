 Business Brief -- CHANCELLOR MEDIA CORP.: Acquisition Costs Cause Loss in Quarter to Widen   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 1997  Chancellor Media Corp said its 1997 third-quarter loss widened to $3.2 million, or 23 cents a share, in the year-earlier period. The company attributed the loss in the quarter to an increase in noncash depreciation and amortization expenses related to the acquisition of Viacom Inc radio stations.
