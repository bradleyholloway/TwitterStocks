 Real Estate Finance: Land Values Offer Insight to Future Of Home Builders   By Corkery, Michael, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 2006  "If you have a lot of land and you priced it late, that's the worst position" for a builder, says Ms. [Ivy Zelman], adding that it isn't easy to track details because many companies aren't always forthcoming about their land supplies. She downgraded D.R. Horton Inc. yesterday based partly on her report. The home builder's shares fell 92 cents, or 3.8%, to $23.54 as of 4 p.m. in New York Stock Exchange composite trading.
