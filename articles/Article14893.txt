 Factory Orders Slipped by 1.9% during August   By Santiago, Raquel, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1991  The Commerce Department reported that orders for factory goods dropped 1.9% in Aug 1991, following a record increase of 6.1% in July. Economists insisted that the recovery was still in effect and that most of the August decline was due to a drop in orders for transportation equipment.
