 Potlatch Profit Rose 26% in 2nd Quarter On 8% Revenue Gain   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 13, 1990  Potlatch Corp. said second-quarter net earnings rose 26% on an 8% growth in revenue.  Potlatch's wood-products group reported earnings for the quarter of $800,000, compared with a loss of $2 million in 1989, the company said. The Northwest Paper division reported earnings of $18.4 million, down from $20.4 million in 1989 because of higher costs, some related to new equipment, and to softer-than-expected markets, Potlatch said.
