 Cirrus Logic Inc.: Fiscal 3rd-quarter earnings reverse year-earlier loss   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1997  Fremont CA-based Cirrus Logic Inc bounced back from 1995's chip-industry slump, reporting fiscal third-quarter earnings of $10.3 million, or 16 cents a share, compared with a loss of $3.6 million, or six cents a share, in the year-ago quarter.
