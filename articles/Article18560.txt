 Clinton assails Haiti rulers for terror, threatens use of force if sanctions fail   By Robbins, Carla Anne; Ricks, Thomas E, The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 1994  President Clinton lashed out at Haiti's military rulers for their "reign of terror" and said the US could no longer rule out military force to restore democracy in the island country. Clinton said he hoped a new policy of stiffer economic sanctions would persuade the military to relinquish power.
