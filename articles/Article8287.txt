 Advanced Micro Says Profit Nearly Tripled in the Latest Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1992  Advanced Micro Devices Inc reported earnings for the third quarter ended Sep 27, 1992 nearly doubled to $49.1 million, compared with the year-earlier's earnings of $17 million. The chip maker attributed the gain to the continuing strong sales of its "clone" of Intel Corp's 386SX microprocessor.
