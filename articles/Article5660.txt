 Northwest Airlines' traffic falls   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 11, 1994  Northwest Airlines said its traffic fell 2.1% to 4.65 billion revenue passenger miles in Oct 1994, from 4.75 billion revenue passenger miles a year earlier. For the first 10 months of the year, the airline said it flew 48.9 billion revenue passenger miles, down from the 49.1 billion revenue passenger miles flow in the year-earlier period.
