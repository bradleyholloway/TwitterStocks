 Isuzu Motors Ltd.: Earnings More Than Double On Cost Cuts, Strong Truck Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 2006  Isuzu Motors Ltd., which signed a capital alliance with Toyota Motor Corp., said fiscal first-half earnings more than doubled, led by strong truck sales, cost cuts and pension-related gains. The Japanese truck maker raised its outlook for net income for the year ending March 31.
