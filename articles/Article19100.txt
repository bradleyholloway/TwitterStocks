 Workspaces: Have a Seat   By By Betty Hallock, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1998  -- WHO: Carl Gustav Magnusson, 58 years old, director of design, Knoll Inc., the publicly traded furniture company.  -- WHAT YOU SEE: The trappings of an enthusiast. Mr. Magnusson finds inspiration from the most unexpected sources: an old Spalding tennis racket, steel mesh from the grill of a racing Porsche, a foam-cushion flotation device.
