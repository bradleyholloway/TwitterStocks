 Two Publishers Of Cable Magazines Plan a Merger   By By Patrick M. Reilly, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 1990  TVSM Inc., a publisher of cable-television magazines, said it will merge with Crosby Vandenburgh Group, also a major publisher of cable magazines.  The Boston-based Crosby Vandenburgh will merge its Cable Publications Inc. division, which publishes the monthly publication TV Entertainment, with TVSM, which publishes the Cable Guide. The combined company will retain the TVSM name and will continue to be run by Mark Edmiston, chairman of the three-year-old TVSM.
