 Business Brief -- PHILIPS ELECTRONICS NV: Philips Magnavox Systems Are Recalled for Repairs   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 1998  About 25,500 Philips Magnavox home-audio-theater systems are being recalled for repairs, the CPSC said on Oct 2, 1998. The speaker voice coil can overheat, potentially leading to a fire. The recall covers the model MX920 made by Philips Consumer Electronics Co, a unit of Philips Electronics NV.
