 Nuevo Energy Set To Name Activist, Elson, to Board   By By Christopher Cooper, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 1998  According to some people familiar with the situation, Mr. Elson's appointment comes at the behest of Relational Shareholders LLC, a La Jolla, Calif., firm that invests in companies with undervalued stock. Relational Shareholders tries to turn the companies around by seeking to change the board, either through proxy battles or through the appointment of new board members. Relational Shareholders bought 5% of Nuevo Energy last year. Its largest investor is the California Public Employees' Retirement System, the nation's biggest pension fund.
