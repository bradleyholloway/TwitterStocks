 Dollar gains across the board, buoyed by German news that depresses mark   By Patterson, Rebecca H, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1996  The dollar posted across-the-board gains on Jan 15, 1996, buoyed by mark weakness triggered by a wave of negative news out of Germany. In late afternoon New York trading, the dollar was at 1.4490 marks, up from the previous trading day's DM1.4443, and at 105.30 yen, off a bit from Y105.31.
