 Angeles Signs Letter For Merger of Emcor With Unit's Affiliate   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1985  Angeles Corp. said it signed a letter of intent to merge Emcor Petroleum Inc. with an affiliate of Quinoco Oil & Gas Inc., an Angeles unit.  Terms of the proposed transaction call for Emcor to transfer its corporately owned oil and gas properties to a newly formed publicly traded master limited partnership managed by Angeles. In addition, Angeles would contribute corporately owned oil and gas properties valued at between $10 million and $15 million.
