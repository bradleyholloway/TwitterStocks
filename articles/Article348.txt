Mary Colleen Henry and Rajpal Sandhu were married yesterday at St. Agnes Roman Catholic Church in Greenwich, Conn. The Rev. Michael Lanning officiated. A Sikh ceremony was performed on Friday by Gianni Mahinder Singh, a Sikh priest, at the Asia Society in New York.  Ms. Henry, who is 39 and is keeping her name, is the managing director for communications technology stocks at Goldman, Sachs & Company in New York. She is a graduate of Northwestern University, from which she also received a master's degree in journalism. Her mother, Donna Henry of Bloomington, Ill., retired as a first-grade teacher at the Sheridan School there, and her father, the late Hugh Henry Jr., was a lawyer in private practice, also in Bloomington.
