 Book Excerpt: Buying Foreign Stocks in the U.S.   By Opdyke, Jeff D, The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 2008  The ADRs are tradable certificates, each backed by physical BA shares, which, in turn, are held in trust by a financial institution, such as J.P.
