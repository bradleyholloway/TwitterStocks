 MGM to Offer Benefits To Homosexual Partners   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 1996  SANTA MONICA, Calif. (AP) -- MetroGoldwyn-Mayer Inc. has joined the list of entertainment companies offering health-care benefits to live-in partners of homosexual employees.  Same-sex domestic partners will be eligible for medical and insurance coverage beginning on Feb. 1, MGM spokeswoman Anne Corley said yesterday.
