 Algeria stumbles toward democracy   By Kaplan, Roger, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1996  Roger Kaplan discusses a referendum approved in Algeria the week of Nov 25, 1996 that endorsed a new constitution, outlawing religious and political parties and giving the president new powers. Kaplan says as Islamic fundamentalism increases across the Middle East, Algeria has emerged as a key test of whether secular and democratic institutions can prevail in the Arab world.
