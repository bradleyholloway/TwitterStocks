 Cigarettes now on market are addictive, FDA drug abuse advisory panel finds   By McGinley, Laurie, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 3, 1994  The FDA's eight-member Drug Abuse Advisory Committee concluded that cigarettes currently on the market are addictive, but members said they didn't know what amount of nicotine causes addiction. The finding is significant because it gives the FDA support if it decides to regulate cigarettes.
