The engagement of Elizabeth Rhoads Buck to Charles H. King 3d has been announced by her parents, Mr. and Mrs. William C. Buck of Villanova, Pa. Her fiance is the son of Mrs. Howard E. C. Hall of Greenwich, Conn., and Mr. King Jr. of Wilton, Conn.  An October wedding is planned.  Miss Buck graduated from the Agnes Irwin School and the University of Virginia. She is a travel consultant and group coordinator with Round the Globe Travel in New York and is a member of the Junior League of New York. Her father is executive vice president of the TDH Capital Corporation, a venture capital company in Radnor, Pa., and a partner in the Philadelphia Phillies baseball team. The future bride is a granddaughter of the late J. Mahlon Buck, who was a vice president and director of Smith, Kline & French Laboratories, now the SmithKline Beckman Corporation, and president of its subsidiary, Smith, Kline & French Inc.  Mr. King, an assistant treasurer of the Morgan Guaranty Trust Company of New York, graduated from St. Paul's School in Concord N.H., and from Yale University. He is a grandson of the late Frederick C. Windisch of Greenwich, who was a senior vice president of Citibank. His father is a managing director of the Tucker Anthony Management Corporation in Stamford, Conn.  Photo of Elizabeth Buck (Bachrach)
