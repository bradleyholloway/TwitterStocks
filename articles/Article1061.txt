 Tricks of the Trade: Preparing the Perfect Sauce   By Lipton, Joshua, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 2006  To judge whether a sauce is thick enough for serving, Adam Timney, chef de cuisine at Bacar restaurant in San Francisco, uses what he calls the "drop test." He spoons just a little of the sauce onto a cold plate. This cools the sauce so he can tell if it's the right consistency. The sauce should run a little without being watery.
