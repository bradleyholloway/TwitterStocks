 New Securities Issues [2]  The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 1996  American Annuity Group Inc. -- $75 million of trust-originated preferred stock via lead manager Merrill Lynch & Co., according to MCM CorporateWatch. The terms: amount, three million shares totaling $75 million; maturity, Oct. 15, 2026; issue price, $25 a share; dividend, 9.25%; call date, at par after five years; ratings: Ba-1 by Moody's Investors Service Inc. and triple-B-minus by Standard & Poor's Ratings Group. SUSA Partnership L.P. -- $100 million of notes priced via Goldman, Sachs & Co., according to MCM CorporateWatch. The terms: amount, $100 million; maturity, Nov. 1, 2003; coupon, 7.125%; issue price, 99.721; spread, 95 basis points; call date, make-whole call at anytime at 20 basis points above Treasurys; rating, Baa-3 by Moody's and triple-B-minus by S&P.
