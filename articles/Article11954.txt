 Belgium is asked to lift immunity of NATO's Claes   By du Bois, Martin, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 1995  Prosecutors asked the Belgian Parliament to lift the immunity of NATO Secretary-General Willy Claes and two other former Belgian government ministers involved in a scandal centering on the alleged payment of bribes to Belgian politicians in a 1988 defense contract with Italian helicopter maker Agusta SpA.
