 Recent Gains in Stock Market Are Met With Skeptical Reception   By Schreiber, Cynthia, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 11, 2005  Options on Tyco International were more active than usual, especially puts, as traders positioned for possible stock movement. The reason for the activity was unclear, however market watchers cited speculation that Tyco's management ranks could be tapped to fill vacated spots at Hewlett-Packard after the ousting of H-P's chief executive. A Tyco spokeswoman said yesterday afternoon the company's chief executive wouldn't leave Tyco for the post at H-P.
