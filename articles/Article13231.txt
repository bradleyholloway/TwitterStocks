 Treasury Plans Sale Of Bills and Notes   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 2005  The Treasury plans to pay down $6 billion on the public debt with the sale today of $16 billion in four-week bills. Maturing bills outstanding total $22 billion. The sale amount is down from last week's auction of $20 billion. The four-week bills will mature on Jan. 5, 2006. The Cusip number is 912795WH0. Noncompetitive tenders for the bills, available in minimum $1,000 denominations, must be received by noon EST today, and competitive tenders by 1 p.m.
