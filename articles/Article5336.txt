 Drexel Seeks to Recoup Employee Bonuses   By Lambert, Wade; Moses, Jonathan M, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1992  The law firm of Cravath, Swaine & Moore, representing thrift regulators and Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc, filed documents in bankruptcy court attempting to recover more than $250 million in bonuses paid to 280 former employees of the firm before it declared bankruptcy in Feb 1990. The filing alleges that the money should have been preserved for creditors.
