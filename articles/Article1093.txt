 Business Brief -- Great Northern Nekoosa Corp.: Third-Period Net Rose 66%, 'Outstanding Year' Expected   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1988  Great Northern Nekoosa Corp. said third-quarter net income rose sharply and that, based on strong demand and expected paper price increases, it expects "an outstanding year" for 1988 and "the outlook for early 1989 is good, too."
