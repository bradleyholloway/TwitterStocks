 Kodak to spell out charges that Japan conspired to bar it from photo markets   By Bounds, Wendy; Cooper, Helene, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 1995  Eastman Kodak Co plans to submit to US trade officials on Nov 6, 1995 a 1,100-page report that, the company claims, contains compelling arguments that the Japanese government conspired with Fuji Photo Film Co to block Kodak from Japan's photography market. The two-volume opus is a compilation of Japanese government memos and speeches, newspaper and trade articles, and industry studies.
