 Vicorp Restaurants Expects to Post Drop In Net for 3rd Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1985  Vicorp Restaurants Inc. said it expects to report that its net income for its third quarter ended Aug. 4 fell to between $1.5 million and $2 million, or 15 cents to 20 cents a share, from the year-earlier $2.7 million, or 40 cents a share.
