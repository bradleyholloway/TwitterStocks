 China Invites Bid by Boeing for Jet Venture   By Cole, Jeff, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 1992  Boeing Co reported that government officials in China have invited it to submit a proposal to assemble its 200-seat model 757 jetliners there. The move would give Boeing an advantage over rival McDonnell Douglas Corp in both company's efforts to establish operations in China, but would likely raise the ire of US workers, who fear the loss of jobs to foreign operations.
