 BJ's Wholesale Club Inc.: Same-Store Sales Rise 4.5% On Strength in Food Sector   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 2004  BJ's Wholesale Club Inc.'s August same-store sales rose 4.5% as higher food sales offset a decline for general merchandise. However, the result still missed the jump of 5.6% forecast on average by a survey of 13 analysts by Thomson First Call.
