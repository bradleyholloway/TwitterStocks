 Letters to the Editor: Chapter 13   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 1985  Re. the Nov. 11 Your Money Matters column on bankrupts starting over. Consumers who file, or are considering filing, under Chapter 13 of the Bankruptcy Act should understand that, strictly speaking, a Chapter 13 filing is not one for bankruptcy; it is a plan for wage earners to repay a substantial portion or all of their debts over an extended period.
