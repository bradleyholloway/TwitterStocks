 Government watch: A ruling   By Mehta, Stephanie N, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 1996  A ruling in Ohio could invalidate minority-contracting plans in other cities. The decision, issued in Aug 1996 by a US district court judge in Columbus, prohibits the city from awarding construction contracts on the basis of gender or race. The ruling's effect on set aside contracts is analyzed.
