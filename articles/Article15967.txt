 Digex Shareholder Gets Judge to Expedite Suit To Stop Intermedia Sale   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 2000  TCW Technology LP, which owns about 4.3% of Digex, asked the Court of Chancery of the State of Delaware to stop the Intermedia-WorldCom deal and expedite its suit, which was filed Sept. 20.  TCW, a Los Angeles institutional investor, alleges Intermedia violated its fiduciary duties and caused irreparable harm when it elected to sell itself to WorldCom instead of selling Digex at a premium.
