 Business Brief -- Lone Star Steakhouse & Saloon Inc.: Unidentified Firm to Buy Chain in $547.3 Million Deal   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 2002  Lone Star Steakhouse & Saloon Inc. said it agreed to be acquired by an unidentified buyer for $547.3 million. The Wichita, Kan., concern said the buyout firm entered into a nonbinding letter of intent to buy its common stock for $20.50 a share. The offer is a 3.6% premium to its 30-day average stock trading price of $19.78.
