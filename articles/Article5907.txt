 Monday's markets: Dow industrials and the Nasdaq stroll to new highs   By Lohse, Deborah, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 1996  The Nasdaq Composite Index and Dow Jones Industrial Average hit new highs on Nov 11, 1996, while the bond market took Veterans Day off. The dollar fell. The Dow jumped 35.78 to a record 6255.60, and the Nasdaq index rose 5.16 to a record 1262.67.
