 Energy Shares Suffer 'Gas' Pangs   By Scaggs, Alexandra, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 2012  Not only does the cleaner-burning fuel account for an increasing share of companies' output, it also has been the focus of billions of dollars in investments as companies sought to unlock vast amounts of natural gas from previously untapped shale-rock formations in the U.S. Nevertheless, a supply glut of natural gas has caused prices of the fuel this year to hit their lowest levels in decades.
