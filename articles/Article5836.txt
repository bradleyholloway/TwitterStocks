 Forest-products firms show weak 1st-period profits   By Hardy, Quentin, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 1996  The boom in the forest-products industry on 1995 has turned to bust in 1996, with companies reporting sharply lower first-quarter earnings due to plunging pulp prices, excess capacity and bad weather. The earnings of Georgia-Pacific Corp, Boise Cascade Corp, Potlatch Corp and International Paper Co are provided.
