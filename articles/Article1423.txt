 CBO Forecast Sees Economy Growing 1.3%   By Birnbaum, Jeffrey H; Wessel, David, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1991  The CBO sees the economy growing by 1.3%, after adjusting for inflation, between the fourth quarter of 1990 and the fourth quarter of 1991, with the GNP shrinking by 2.6% in the fourth quarter of 1990 and by 1.7% in the current quarter.
