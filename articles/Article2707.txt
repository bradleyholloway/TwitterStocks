 Tokyo prices climb amid hopes of big infusion; European stocks get a lift from dollar rebound   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 1995  Tokyo stocks advanced 1.6% on Apr 20, 1995, pushed up by the prospect of a buying infusion from public pension funds. The Tokyo Nikkei 225-share index was up 266.98 to 16643.06. The London Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-share index was up 4.6 to 3174.7. The Frankfurt DAX 30-share index rose 4.93 to 1955.97.
