 Lukens Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 1992  Lukens Inc's stock price surged $2.625 a share to $45.125 in Jan 6, 1992 trading after its largest unit, Lukens Steel Co, reached a tentative agreement with the United Steelworkers of America Local 1165 that could end a four-month-long strike.
