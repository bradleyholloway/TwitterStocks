 An IFI question: Are multilaterals the solution ...   By Summers, Lawrence H, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1995  Lawrence H. Summers, deputy secretary of the Treasury, warns that the US must continue to fund the International Financial Institutions, including the World Bank, arguing that economic disengagement from the world could lead to a spiral of protectionism and isolationism that would be financially catastrophic for the US economy.
