 A.H. Robins Co. Asks Judge to Seal Data In Study of Payments   The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 1986  RICHMOND, Va. -- A.H. Robins Co. asked a federal judge here to seal all information uncovered by the government in an investigation into $7 million in unauthorized payments the company made after it filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy law protection last August.
