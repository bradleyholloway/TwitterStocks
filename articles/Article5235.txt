 Semi-Tech Net Rose 90% for Year; Firm Will Reincorporate   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 1991  Semi-Tech (Global) Ltd announced that after-tax profit rose 90% in the year ended Jan 31, 1991 to 453 million Hong Kong dollars ($58.1 million) from HK$239 million a year earlier. The firm also disclosed plans to reincorporate in Bermuda.
