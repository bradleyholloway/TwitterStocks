 Autos: GM is offered plan to resolve truck dispute   By Lavin, Douglas; Ingersoll, Bruce, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1993  Although consumer advocates say that General Motors Corp could resolve the nasty controversy surrounding the safety of over 4.7 million pickup trucks with side-mounted gas tanks for $65 a vehicle, the auto maker says that there is no need for such a solution. The dilemma GM faces over whether to act to assuage anxious regulators and consumers is discussed.
