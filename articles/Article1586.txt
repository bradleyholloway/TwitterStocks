 Bond Prices Fall on Strong Retail Sales Report; Long-Term Rates Hit Highest Level in 4 Months   By Sherman, Robert; Donnelly, Terence; Adler, Lynn K, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 13, 1992  Bond prices fell on Mar 12, 1992 on news of strong retail sales. The government's 30-year benchmark bond ended at 99 15/32 to yield 8.04% compared with 100 12/32 to yield 7.95% the day before. The government's two-year notes ended at 99 12/32, down 1/4 point, to yield 5.70%.
