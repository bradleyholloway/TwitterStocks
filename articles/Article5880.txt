 Politics & people: Albert Gore warms up for 2000   By Hunt, Albert R, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 1996  Albert R. Hunt discusses Vice President Albert Gore's chances of winning the presidency in the year 2000. Hunt says that Gore's future is affected by the outcome of the 1996 presidential election and the success or failure of a second Bill Clinton term, asserting that he will have an even more powerful role in those years.
