 Technology (A Special Report) --- The Wall Street Journal 2009 Technology Innovation Awards   By Totty, Michael, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 2009  Researchers at ITRI, a nonprofit organization, devised a way to create arrays of tiny speakers that can be combined to produce high-fidelity speaker systems of almost any size. Because the Flexspeaker is lightweight and consumes little power, it could be attractive for use in cellphones or in car sound systems.
