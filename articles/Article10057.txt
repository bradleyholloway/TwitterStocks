 Macy's Trading Is Driven by Deal Talk   By Hadi, Mohammed, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 2007  With [Macy]'s shares rising, some traders also turned to put options on the stock -- ostensibly to lock in profits or speculate on a pullback like the one that followed the last round of heavy speculation on Macy's. All told, a heavier-than-usual 48,500 call options and 20,000 put options on Macy's changed hands. A spokesman for Macy's said the company doesn't comment on speculation or trading.
