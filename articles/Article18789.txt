 Mutual funds quarterly review: Single-state muni funds: Tough game   By Jeffrey, Nancy Ann, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1996  Brokerage firms and mutual-fund companies are learning the hard way that single-state municipal-bond funds are not always attractive to investors. Exemptions from state and federal taxes, and sometimes local taxes as well, make the funds attractive to high-income investors, but in recent years, such funds have been beset by problems. In some states, single-state muni funds face severe shortages of bonds. And the market was depressed in 1995 by flat-tax fears.
