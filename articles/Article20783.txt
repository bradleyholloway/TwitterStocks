 The Two Manhattans   By Lewis, Al, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 2012  Below 40th Street folks were asking questions like, "When will the lights come back on?" "How are they going to pump all that water out of the subways?" and "Where am I going to live, now?" Several blocks above 40th Street, it was, "Do you think that Picasso will fetch $50 million?" Hurricane Sandy destroyed homes and took lives, but it also forced Sotheby's to postpone its sale of Impressionist and modern art until Nov. 8 and its contemporary art auction until Nov. 13.
