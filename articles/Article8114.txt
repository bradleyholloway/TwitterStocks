 Frankfurt Share Prices Fall Broadly, Tokyo and London Closed for Holiday   The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 1991  Share prices closed mostly lower in Europe on May 6, 1991 and Frankfurt stocks declined broadly, led down by pessimistic news for the chemical sector. The DAX index closed at 1623.99, off 7.85 points, as the BASF chemical sector fell 4.5 marks to DM250.20. Markets were closed for a holiday in Tokyo and London.
