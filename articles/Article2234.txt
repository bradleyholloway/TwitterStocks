 American opinion: What do we want? Smarter kids, safer streets   By Hunt, Albert R, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 13, 1996  According to the latest Wall Street Journal/NBC News survey of more than 2,000 Americans completed the week of Dec 8, 1996, Americans are more optimistic than at any other time in the past four years. A plurality believes the country is headed in the right direction. The public is focusing less on the economy and more on quality-of-life issues.
