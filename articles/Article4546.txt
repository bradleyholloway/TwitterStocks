 Stocks Drop After Brush With 2700 --- Takeover Speculation Can't Stop Decline; Bond Prices Retreat   By By Douglas R. Sease, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1989  Takeover speculation failed to sustain the Dow Jones Industrial Average's push above 2700 mark, and bond prices retreated in the face of a less than enthusiastic Treasury auction.  The Dow Jones industrials slid 13.09 points to 2686.08 in another day of heavy trading. Bond prices dropped following results of the Treasury's auction of 10-year notes. The dollar rose modestly.
