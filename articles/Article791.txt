 Credit Ratings: Revco D.S.'s Debt Put on CreditWatch By Standard & Poor's   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 1986  Standard & Poor's Corp. placed Revco D.S. Inc.'s triple-B-plus senior debt and preliminary triple-B-plus shelf registration on CreditWatch with negative implications.  About $200 million of the discount drug store operator's debt is affected. The rating concern said it affirmed the company's A-2 commercial paper rating on an interim basis, but will withdraw the rating at the company's request.
