 PBS's 'Sesame Street' is 'Plaza Sesamo' for Hispanic viewers   By Patton, Susannah, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 1995  Responding to the growing demand for Spanish-language children's programming, Children's Television Workshop is expanding the reach of "Sesame Street," the most widely viewed children's series in the world. The Spanish-language version of the program began in the US during the week of Apr 3, 1995, and producers have plans for "Sesame Street" productions in China, South Africa, Russia and Poland.
