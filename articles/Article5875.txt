 Buchanan vows to stay a Republican, indicates a run for president in 2000   By Perry, James M, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1996  Conservative commentator Patrick J. Buchanan vowed to remain in the Republican Party, in his last major speech on Aug 11 as a 1996 presidential candidate. At the same time, Buchanan also all but announced that he is prepared to run for president again in 2000.
