 Deals & Deal Makers: Credit Suisse Unit Loses Arbitration To Two Investors   By Cowan, Lynn, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 2004  A National Association of Securities Dealers arbitration-panel ruling issued last week determined that Credit Suisse did require Mr. [William Levine]'s and the Haberkorns' permission to roll up the fund, and directed the investment bank to unwind their participation in the new fund. The investors' attorneys calculated that the pair had lost about $1 million on their investment after the roll-up. The panel also ordered Credit Suisse to pay the pair $960,000 in attorney and other costs totaling $17,000.
