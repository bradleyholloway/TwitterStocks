 Automotive Brief -- Volkswagen AG: Former BMW Sales Executive To Head Sales of Audi Brand   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 2003  Volkswagen AG said Ralph Weyler, a former sales executive with rival Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, will head sales of its Audi brand, succeeding Georg Flandorfer. Mr. Flandorfer, 57 years old, will take over responsibilities for sales of the entire VW group in Germany.
