 Inside track: Insiders nibble at Applied Bioscience stock   By Peers, Alexandra, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1993  Five executives at Applied Bioscience International Inc, a clinical testing, toxicology and environmental services concern whose stock has fallen 62% from its 1992 high, bought a total of 16,000 shares in Dec 1992 and Jan 1993, according to SEC filings. The insider nibbling comes amid the company's one-million-share stock buyback, which was announced Jan 7.
