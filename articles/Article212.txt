A lawsuit filed against the Rev. Bruce Ritter by a former resident of Covenant House who claimed the priest seduced him has been thrown out on the grounds that the statute of limitations had expired.  Darryl Bassile, 31 years old, had charged in court papers that Father Ritter, the founder of Covenant House, had sex with him several times in 1973 when he was 14.
