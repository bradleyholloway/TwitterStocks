 Two Companies Change Name   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 1998  Among recent stock-listing changes, Freeport-McMoRan Resources Partners LP, trading on the NYSE, is changing its name to Phosphate Resource Partners LP, with new symbol PLP. CD-Max Inc, trading on the Nasdaq, is changing its name to Imark Technologies Inc, with the same symbol, MAXX.
