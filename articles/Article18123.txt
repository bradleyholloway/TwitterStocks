 Chrysler Joins GM to Offer Early Rebates on '91 Cars   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 1990  HIGHLAND PARK, Mich. -- Chrysler Corp., following the lead of General Motors Corp. is offering rebates on most of its 1991 model cars and trucks before the model year begins.  Chrysler said it will offer rebates of as much as $1,000 on most 1991 car and truck models, but not on its restyled minivans and certain other models.
