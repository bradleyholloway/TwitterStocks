Leslie Ann Hill, a daughter of Patricia A. Hill of Atlanta and Leslie S. Hill of Parma, Ohio, was married yesterday to Alec Dov Sokolow, a son of Diane Sokolow of New York and the late Mel Sokolow. Cantor Debra Stein-Davidson performed the ceremony at the Southampton, L.I., home of Mortimer B. Zuckerman, the real-estate developer and publisher.  Mrs. Sokolow, 28 years old, is a freelance photographer in Santa Monica, Calif. She graduated from the University of Georgia. Her father is a technician for the Chevrolet-Pontiac-Canada Division assembly plant in Parma. Her mother is an advertising sales representative for The Atlanta Journal-Constitution.
