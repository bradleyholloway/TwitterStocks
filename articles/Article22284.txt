 Art sellers rush to spring auctions while market's still the picture of health   By Peers, Alexandra, The Wall Street Journal,  May 2, 1997  As the $550 million spring art-auction season opens in New York City in May 1997, there's a race to get property to the market while the going's still good. Recent sales have included a record $10.4 million price on Apr 29 for a nine-piece collection of Ming and Qing dynasty Chinese porcelain.
