 Insurers General Re, Continental report declines in earnings   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1993  General Re Corp, the US's largest reinsurer, reported first-quarter net income declined to $160.6 million from $207.8 million a year earlier, while Continental Corp, a New York property and casualty insurer, posted first-quarter earnings of $22.6 million, down from the year-earlier's $32.3 million.
