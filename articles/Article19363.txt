 Business Brief -- Health Net Inc.: Firm to Receive Settlement From U.S. of $389 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 2001  Health Net Inc., Woodland Hills, Calif., said it expects to receive $389 million from the federal government to resolve various issues related to its Tricare program, a federally managed health-care plan for active and retired military personnel and their dependents.
