 Six Tips for Succeeding With Licensing Deals Online edition  By Needleman, Sarah, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 2008  If a new movie or other entertainment property is a good fit for your business, you'll want to pitch a product line in as far advance as possible of its release date, says Karen Diamond, an independent licensing agent in Franklin Lakes, N.J., who negotiates deals on behalf of property owners. Licensing consultants can help small businesses choose appropriate licenses, figure out how to pitch decision-makers and negotiate contract terms, says Charles M. Riotto, president of the International Licensing Industry Merchandisers' Association.
