 Computer Associates Didn't Give Bonuses To Chairman, CEO   By By David Armstrong, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 2001  Mr. [Charles Wang] received a bonus of $4.78 million last year but was not similarly rewarded this year because the company, based in Islandia, N.Y., reported a loss for the fiscal year ended in March, according to the proxy statement. The company blamed the loss on a change in the way it recognizes revenue. Mr. Wang was paid a salary of $1 million, which was unchanged from the prior two years, according to the proxy statement.
