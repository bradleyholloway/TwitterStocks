 Tyco International Ltd.: Recovery of Looted Funds Begins From Jailed Executives   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 2006  Tyco International Ltd. has begun to recover money from two former executives who were jailed last year for looting millions from the company. L. Dennis Kozlowski, Tyco's former chief executive, and Mark H. Swartz, the company's former finance chief, were convicted in June of pilfering more than $150 million from Tyco.
