 Copper futures prices dive on stockpile growth   By Lucchetti, Aaron, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1996  Copper futures prices fell on news that stockpiles at the London Metal Exchange may be turning around. On Dec 2, 1996, prices of copper for December delivery fell 4.7 cents to $1.0560 a pound at the Comex division of the NYME. In other commodity markets, grain and precious-metal futures prices dipped.
