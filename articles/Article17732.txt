 TRW, Focusing On 3 Core Lines, Disbands Group   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 1987  The group, based in El Segundo, Calif., has annual sales of about $390 million. The automotive, aerospace and electronics concern said it plans to transfer three of the group's units to other parts of TRW while five units -- with annual sales of about $290 million -- will be sold. TRW said the sale of the businesses, combined with other divestments it may make later this year, are expected to be accomplished without any "significant" charges to 1987 earnings.
