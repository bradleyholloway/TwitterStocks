 Dollar gains on yen as U.S. trade gap for June was narrower than expected   By Liu, Betty W, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 1996  The yen ended lower against the dollar and mark in global trading on Aug 20, 1996, as traders reacted to a narrower-than-expected US trade deficit and hedged their bets on the next German interest-rate move. Late in New York, the dollar was at DM1.4886, up from DM1.4875 on Aug 19, and at Y108.40, up from Y107.84.
