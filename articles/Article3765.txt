 Noted...   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 2007  KIA MOTORS Corp. (Seoul, South Korea) -- Len Hunt was named president and chief executive of Kia Motors America, the sales, marketing and distribution arm of this automobile company, effective immediately. Mr. Hunt, 52, succeeds B.G. Lee, "who has returned to Korea," according to a company press release. Mr. Hunt was executive vice president and chief operating officer of KMA.
