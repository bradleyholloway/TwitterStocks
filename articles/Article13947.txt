 AMR Filing Will Trigger Payouts   By Burne, Katy, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 2011  Greece plans to restructure its debt, with private investors voluntarily agreeing to write off half of the country's 206 billion euros ($277 billion) in debt and to exchange old bonds for new. Because the debt exchange is voluntary, the ISDA committee isn't expected to declare the Greece restructuring a credit event, which would have triggered payouts on the $3.4 billion of Greek CDS outstanding.
