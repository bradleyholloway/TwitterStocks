 Two Tobacco Firms In Canada Challenge Ban on Smoking Ads   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 2, 1988  Two Canadian tobacco manufacturers separately mounted court challenges to the Canadian government's ban on tobacco advertising that's scheduled to take effect Jan. 1.  Imperial Tobacco Ltd. and RJR Macdonald Inc. filed motions in Quebec's Superior Court, each claiming the law violates freedom of speech guarantees in the constitution and is outside the federal government's jurisdiction.
