 FTC plans to review how 'Made in U.S.A.' is used in advertising   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 1995  The FTC is taking a new look at what "made in U.S.A." means in advertising, saying it will review its longstanding requirement--that a "made in U.S.A." product must be wholly domestic--"is appropriate in today's global economy."
