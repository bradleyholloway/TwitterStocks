 Barron's Insight: Value Is Seen At Limited   By Santoli, Michael, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 2004  Despite the prowess of two of the company's chains, Victoria's Secret and Bath & Body Works, analysts are preoccupied with the apparel stores operating under the Limited and Express names, which were largely to blame for the parent's report last week of a 5% drop in November same-store sales.  Even though Victoria's Secret and Bath & Body Works together account for nearly 90% of Limited Brands' profit, Wall Street is hung up on the other chains' struggle to stand out in clothing-choked malls.
