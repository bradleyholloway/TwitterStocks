 BellSouth Rises on Acquisition Offer; Major Stock Indexes Decline On Interest-Rate Concerns; RIM Leaps After Settlement   By Talley, Karen, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 2006  Blue chip 3M dropped 93 cents, or 1.3%, to 71.95.
