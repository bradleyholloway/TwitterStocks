 Markets Brace for Washington's 'Fiscal Cliff'   By Zuckerman, Gregory, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 2012  "The quickly approaching fiscal cliff is big, scary, [and] yet avoidable," argues Bill Stone, chief investment strategist at PNC Wealth Management. A messy fight between Democrats, many of whom are reluctant to reduce spending, and Republicans, who are loath to raise taxes, also could serve to undermine confidence in U.S. markets--even if an eventual compromise is reached.
