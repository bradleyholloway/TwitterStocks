 Benchmark Treasury takes back some ground in session marked by wide price fluctuations   By Lauricella, Thomas D; Young, Lauren, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1994  Treasury securities recovered some ground after taking a wild ride Mar 2, 1994. The Treasury's 30-year bond ended the day up 1/8 at 93 10/32, bringing the yield down to 6.77% from 6.78% the day before.
