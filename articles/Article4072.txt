 License Approved For Philadelphia Electric's Limerick   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 9, 1985  The Nuclear Regulatory Commission granted Philadelphia Electric Co. a full-power operating license for Unit 1 of the Limerick nuclear power plant.  Philadelphia Electric said it immediately will begin six weeks of full-power testing at the plant in Montgomery County, north of Philadelphia. The utility said it expects Limerick Unit 1 to be operating commercially by the 1986 first quarter.
