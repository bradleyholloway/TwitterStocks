 Time Warner Mulls Revising Rights Offering   By Landro, Laura; Smith, Randall, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1991  Time Warner Inc is considering some revisions of its controversial proposed rights offering to raise as much as $3.5 billion, though executives and advisers deny that the plan will be scrapped entirely. The company's stock plummeted about 25% after the rights offering was announced, as shareholders reacted negatively to certain coercive aspects.
