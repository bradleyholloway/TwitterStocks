 Consumer Products Brief -- Adolph Coors Co.: Earnings Increase by 27% Amid a 76% Jump in Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 2003  Adolph Coors Co. reported a 27% increase in fourth-quarter earnings, but the results badly missed Wall Street estimates amid declining sales in the Americas. The Golden, Colo., brewer posted net income of $20.2 million, or 55 cents a share, for the quarter, up from $15.9 million, or 44 cents a share, a year earlier.
