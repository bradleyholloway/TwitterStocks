 Mazda will cut in half output for North America   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 1995  Mazda Motor Corp said it will reduce the domestic output of passenger cars bound for the North America market by half in April and May 1995 amid plunging US auto sales and the surging yen. Mazda said the combined output for the two months will be more than 20,000 units fewer than the 124,500 cars it built during the same period a year earlier.
