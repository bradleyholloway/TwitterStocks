 Deal Journal / Breaking Insight from WSJ.com   By Moore, Heidi N, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 2009  (J.P. Morgan will syndicate its $8 billion loan package later.) In the M&A boom that ended in mid-2007, one often was struck both by the number of deals that experienced preannouncement stock run-ups and the ever-widening circle of people working on deals: M&A advisers, legal advisers, financing banks, in-house executives and accountants and legal advisers, and so on.
