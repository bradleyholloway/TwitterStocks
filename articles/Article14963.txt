 Retired RJR Chief Takes the Waters, Will Bottle Them   By Gupta, Udayan, The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 1992  J. Paul Sticht, retired chairman of RJR Nabisco Inc, with the help of his two sons and some wealthy family friends plans to launch Castle Springs LP, a company that will sell natural spring water produced from a 5,200-acre New Hampshire estate he bought in 1991.
