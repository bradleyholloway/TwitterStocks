 WEEKEND JOURNAL; The Home Front: South of the Border, the Market's Still Hot; Americans Find Second-Home Boom Endures; Wildlife in the Neighborhood   By Fletcher, June, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2007  Since 2003, annual home-price appreciation has been running at 20% in the Dominican Republic, and could reach 50% in the near future, according to Boomerang Unlimited, a Napa, Calif., real-estate investment advisory firm. Shams Deitrick, a Walnut Creek, Calif., financial adviser, recently bought a furnished, two-bedroom "ocean view villa" for $375,000 in Canto del Mar, a new 35-unit development in the southern Costa Rica town of Dominica; the project has already sold out.
