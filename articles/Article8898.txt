 Business Brief -- BERNARD CHAUS INC.: Profit Soars to $2 Million On Sales Gain, Cost-Cutting   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1997  Bernard Chaus Inc said fiscal 1998 first-quarter net income rose more than five-fold, thanks to stronger sales and cost-cutting. The company said it expects results for the last two quarters of its 1998 fiscal year to benefit from a recently announced capital-restructuring plan as well as from strong orders.
