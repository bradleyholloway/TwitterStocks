 Great Western unveils restructuring in an effort to reduce operating costs   By Hill, G Christian, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1996  Great Western Financial Corp, trying to achieve a major, one-time reduction in operating expenses, disclosed a restructuring program that will trigger pretax charges totaling an estimated $115 million in the fourth quarter. The thrift-holding company's plan includes a work-force reduction of 800 employees, consolidation of its chain of mortgage offices, and divestiture of nonperforming assets.
