 Pay 'N Pak Holder Lifts Bid by Raising Value of Preferred   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 1987  TAMPA, Fla. -- Florida investor Paul Bilzerian said he sweetened his proposal to acquire the 90% of Pay 'N Pak Stores Inc. he doesn't already own by raising the value of the preferred stock included as part of the proposed purchase.
