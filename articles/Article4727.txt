 CFTC Says Expulsion Of First Commodity Is Delayed for Appeal   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 1987  The Commodity Futures Trading Commission said it is delaying the National Futures Association's bid to expel First Commodity Corp.  The CFTC said the delay, which allows the Boston-based company to remain in business, will continue until the commission reviews First Commodity's appeal of its expulsion from the MidAmerica Commodity Exchange.
