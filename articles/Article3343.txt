 Tech Watch: Cable Operators May Join Cellphone Fray   By Drucker, Jesse, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 2004  The most likely partner for the cable companies is Sprint, which operates the country's third-biggest cellphone operator, Sprint PCS. Sprint already has wireless wholesale arrangements with AT&T and Qwest Communications International, allowing them to sell cellular service under their brand names but using Sprint's cellular network. Sprint also has deals with Time Warner Cable, Charter and Mediacom Communications to help them offer traditional phone service.
