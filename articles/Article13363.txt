 PURSUITS; Books: The Glossies   By Carroll, Timothy J, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2007  It has been 10 years since [James W. Finegan] published almost simultaneous accounts of his "pilgrimages" to the golf courses of Scotland ("Blasted Heaths and Blessed Greens") and Ireland ("Emerald Fairways and Foam-Flecked Seas"). Mr. Finegan, who lives in Villanova, Pa., has been traveling to those countries for five decades to play golf; he is certainly an authority on the subject.
