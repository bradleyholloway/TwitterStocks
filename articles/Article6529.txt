 Marriott International Inc.: Net in Fourth Quarter Rose 25% As Rates, Occupancy Increased   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 2006  Increases in occupancy levels and room rates led Marriott International Inc. to sharply higher fourth-quarter revenue and profit. The hotel operator said net income rose 25% to $237 million, or $1.07 a share, from $189 million, or 79 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue rose 16% to $3.64 billion from $3.14 billion.
