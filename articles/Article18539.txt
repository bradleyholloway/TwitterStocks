 Judge finds 'substantial likelihood' GM committed perjury in pickup case   By White, Joseph B, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1994  A federal court judge in Anderson SC blasted General Motors Corp, finding "a substantial likelihood" of "perjury and the systematic destruction of documents" relating to a civil case involving the automaker's 1973-1987 full-size pickups with side-saddle tanks.
