 REVIEW --- Icons: Selling the Real Thing's Relics   By Gamerman, Ellen, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 2011  Ahead of the sale, auction promoters reached out to the roughly 2,700-member Coca-Cola Collectors Club and others such as antique car enthusiasts who associate Coke with roadside culture. Since its creation in 1886, Coke has marketed itself with hatchets, calendars, dolls, trays, roller skates -- even toasters that pressed the logo on bread.
