 Calls Trade Strongly Ahead of Meeting Today Where Fed Is Expected to Cut Rates Again   By By Kopin Tan, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 2001  As the Nasdaq Composite Index tested the 1800 level for the first time since Aug. 31, investors flocked to calls in the Nasdaq 100 Tracking Stock, or QQQ, as well as other large-cap technology companies. But while tech-sector volatility indexes typically fall when tech stocks rise, the Nasdaq 100 Index's 3.82% gain yesterday did little to soothe a key fear gauge. The American Stock Exchange's Nasdaq Volatility Index, or QQV, edged up 1.15 to 52.25, a sign investors remained cautious despite the Nasdaq gains.
