 More Layoffs Announced In London Financial Sector   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1988  McCaughan Dyson Capel-Cure, the London brokerage unit of Australia & New Zealand Banking Group Ltd., said it is laying off 40 people as part of a decision to eliminate most of its activities in European stocks and parts of other businesses. Director Andrew Beeson said yesterday the cutbacks resulted from difficult market conditions.
