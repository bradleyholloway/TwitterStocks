 Court Dismisses Owens Corning Tobacco Lawsuit   By By Queena Sook Kim, The Wall Street Journal,  May 25, 2001  Many asbestos companies have argued that smoking strongly contributed to injuries suffered by asbestos plaintiffs. Owens Corning claimed in the case that R.J. Reynolds Tobacco Holdings Inc.'s R.J. Reynolds Tobacco Co. and other tobacco companies knowingly helped cause plaintiff's injuries by not revealing the fatal synergies between smoking and asbestos.
