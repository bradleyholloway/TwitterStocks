Trying to avoid the appearance of politics, a tenants' group has asked Mayor Rudolph W. Giuliani not to speak to a large rent control rally in Albany next week.  The Mayor announced last month that he planned to travel to Albany on Tuesday to speak in favor of continued rent protections on Tenant Lobby Day at the invitation of the New York State Tenants and Neighbors Coalition, the sponsor of the rally.
