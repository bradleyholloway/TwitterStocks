 Honda's operating profit rose sharply for quarter, but difficulties aren't over   By Reitman, Valerie, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 25, 1994  Honda Motor Co said consolidated net profit for its fiscal first quarter ended Jun 30, 1994 nearly quadrupled to 23.14 billion yen ($236 million) from Y6.09 billion a year earlier, although much of the increase stemmed for a special gain. Operating profit rose 16% to Y25.8 billion, but the troubles of Honda and its fellow Japanese auto makers are far from over.
