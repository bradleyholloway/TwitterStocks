 Media: Papers Put Faith in Paywalls --- Digital Subscription Sales Are Slow, but Publishers Are Encouraged by Trends   By Adams, Russell, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 2012  Pearson PLC's Financial Times, which launched its current pay model in late 2007, had 267,000 digital subscribers as of the end of last year, the company said. Since New York Times Co. began charging for online access to its flagship paper last March, it has signed up 380,000 paid digital subscribers.
