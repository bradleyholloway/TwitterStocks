 Net for Dick Clark Productions Declined In Fiscal 1st Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 1987  BURBANK, Calif. -- Dick Clark Productions Inc. expects to report lower earnings and revenue for its first quarter ended Sept. 30, and for the full year ending next June 30, said Francis La Maina, the company's president.  For the quarter, net income was about $100,000, or one cent a share, down from about $1.6 million, or 23 cents a share, a year earlier, Mr. La Maina said. Revenue was between $7.5 million and $8 million, down from about $9 million a year ago, he added.
