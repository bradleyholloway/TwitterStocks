 Eradicating Raccoon Species Is Impossible   By Barbosa, Patricia, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 25, 1992  Patricia Barbosa responds to the Jul 14, 1992 article "Rabid Raccoons Give Whole Species a Blacker Eye," and says the solution is not to kill raccoons indiscriminately. She mentions the 80,000-plus vaccine-laced baits dropped as part of an experiment by Charles Rupprecht of the Wistar Institute.
