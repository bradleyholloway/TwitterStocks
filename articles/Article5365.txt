 Businesses Are Paying Bills Faster but Risk of Problems Is High   The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1992  A new Dun & Bradstreet Corp survey concluded with its D&B index of payment performance indicating that companies of all sizes improved to minus 7.8 at the end of Mar 1992 from minus 8.9 a year earlier. Zero indicates an equilibrium between prompt and late payers.
