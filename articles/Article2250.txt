 Developer in Texas Linked to Failed S&Ls Agrees to Plead Guilty   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 1991  Texas real estate developer Louis G. Reese III, who borrowed heavily from several now-failed thrifts, agreed to plead guilty to federal criminal fraud charges related to real-estate transactions with two defunct Texas thrifts, Lamar Savings Association of Austin and Western Savings Association of Dallas.
