 Business Brief -- Martin Color-Fi Inc.: Charge Taken for 3rd Period And Earlier Net Is Restated   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 1995  Martin Color-Fi Inc., Edgefield, S.C., said it took a $2.5 million pretax charge to third-quarter earnings and restated its net income downward for 1993 and for the first six months of 1994.
