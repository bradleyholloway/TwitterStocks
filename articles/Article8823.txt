 The home front: Brokers dangle freebies: TVs, knives   By Fletcher, June, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1997  As part of an effort to win new business and generate new sources of income, real-estate brokers are offering all sorts of incentives. The temptations, tumbling out of credit-card bills and highlighted in newsletters, are many. But consumer watchdogs and some state officials are wary. Eleven states ban or restrict certain inducements, saying not enough benefit reaches the consumer. The lures also dampen the resolve of many would-be comparison shoppers.
