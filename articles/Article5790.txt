 Fed ponders how soon to reduce rates   By Wessel, David, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1995  The FRB is poised to cut short-term interest rates sometime in the next few months. A move could come as soon as the Dec 19, 1995 meeting of the policy committee. At least three Fed governors think rates are too high, given prospects for the economy, but the economy is not so weak that the case for cutting rates now is overwhelming.
