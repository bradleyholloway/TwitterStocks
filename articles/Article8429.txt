 New York Times sees $4.5 million charge for fourth quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 1993  New York Times Co will take a $4.5 million charge against fourth-quarter earnings as a result of severance and related charges resulting from voluntary early retirements from the composing room of the New York Times newspaper. Excluding one-time items, the company expects fourth-quarter 1993 earnings will be in the range of $19 million to $27 million.
