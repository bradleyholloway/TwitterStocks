 Insider-Trading Probe Of Corel's Chairman Is Near Completion   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 1999  The Ontario Securities Commission is nearing completion of a 21-month probe into allegations of insider trading against Corel Corp. Chairman Michael Cowpland.  Mr. Cowpland acknowledged that the provincial regulator is investigating his sale of 2.4 million Corel shares between Aug. 11 and Aug. 14, 1997. But he denied acting on inside information, saying the shares were sold to pay off personal "taxes and bank debts."
