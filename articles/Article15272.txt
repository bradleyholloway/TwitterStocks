 Planar says that earnings estimates for fiscal 1st period are 'reasonable'   By Stepankowsky, Paula L, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 1994  James M. Hurd, Planar Systems Inc's president and CEO, said earnings estimates of $.20 or $.21 a share for the first quarter of Planar Systems Inc's 1995 fiscal year are "reasonable," given continued growth in the company's North American sales.
