Gaiter and John Brecher, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 2008  WINE FOR THE ROAD Can you remind me if it is legal to take an unfinished bottle of wine home from a restaurant in New York? -- Mike Scarangella, Garden City, N.Y. Most states now have wine "doggy bag" laws that allow diners to take home unfinished bottles -- and we obviously think this is a very good thing -- but there are generally some complexities to the laws.
