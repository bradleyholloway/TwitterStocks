 Business Brief -- EGGHEAD.COM INC.: Plans Are Made to Relocate 20% of Staff to Trim Costs   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 2000  Egghead.com Inc., Menlo Park, Calif., said it plans to move about 20% of its work force from its Menlo Park headquarters to offices in Washington state, as part of a plan to reduce operating expenses and to reach profitability by the end of 2001.
