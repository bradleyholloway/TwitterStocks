 Storage Technology's Aweida Quits Board To Start Own Firm   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1985  LOUISVILLE, Colo. -- Storage Technology Corp. said Jesse I. Aweida, its principal founder and longtime chief executive officer, resigned from the company's board to begin a new company.  Mr. Aweida had earlier resigned as chairman and chief executive of the struggling data-storage equipment manufacturer, which has been operating under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code since Oct. 31.
