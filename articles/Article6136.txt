 Microsoft's Goals Include Making Software Accessible   By By David Bank, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1998  Mr. (Bill) Gates, who recently relinquished some business responsibilities to focus on Microsoft's technology and product strategies, outlined the challenges in a 14-page memo sent to the Redmond, Wash., company's top managers last month. The memo, which makes no mention of the antitrust suit against the company that is scheduled to go to trial next week, was intended to keep the company focused on its core business.
