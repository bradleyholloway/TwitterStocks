 Real estate: Rain now worries building owners   By Pacelle, Mitchell, The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1994  Owners of non-industrial commercial properties are worried that regulations enforcing a 1987 law about water runoff that applied to industrial properties will be expanded by legislation now being considered in the Senate. Potential targets of broader runoff regulations include shopping mall parking lots, which generate huge amounts of runoff.
