 Chipcom's poison pill is adopted to thwart Cabletron takeover   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 3, 1995  Chipcom Corp adopted a shareholder-rights plan designed to prevent Cabletron Systems Inc from interfering with Chipcom's proposed $775 million sale to 3Com Corp. The poison pill plan gives shareholders the rights to new voting preferred stock that could be exercised if a party acquires 15% of Chipcom or announces a tender offer for it.
