 Advertising: Fine for egg producer   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1996  Eggland's Best will pay a $100,000 fine to settle charges that it continued to advertise its eggs as lower in cholesterol than others although it had agreed not to. It was the second FTC challenge to advertising for Eggland's eggs, which cost up to $1 more a dozen than regular eggs.
