 OEA Inc.: Strong sales boost profit for fiscal first-quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1995  OEA Inc said it earned $6.1 million on sales of $34.6 million in the quarter ended Oct 31, 1995. In the same period a year earlier, the maker of automotive safety and aerospace products earned $2.3 million on sales of $28 million.
