 Check Rates For Shipping   By Jonnelle Marte, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 2010  (Currently, UPS rates aren't included in the comparison, except for some corporate customers.) One recent comparison example: A two-day delivery of a five-pound package from New York to Miami would cost $4.95 using the Postal Service's flat-rate box, $14.95 through Priority Mail, $25.61 via AdShip/FedEx and $33.49 with regular FedEx.
