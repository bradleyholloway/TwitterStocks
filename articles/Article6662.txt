 LTX and PMC-Sierra Post Gains; WCI, Technical Olympic Decline   By Curran, Rob, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 2007  Several stocks in the semiconductor sector rose after large- capitalization company National Semiconductor projected strong bookings in 2007 and Stifel Nicolaus said the testing industry has reached the low point of its cycle. Among small stocks, LTX, which Stifel Nicolaus upgraded, added 29 cents, or 4.8%, to $6.40. Linear Technology, upgraded by ThinkEquity Partners, rose 66 cents, or 2%, to 33.38, and PMC-Sierra rose seven cents, or 1.1%, to 6.68.
