 EADS: Airbus to Cut Suppliers by 83% As Part of Its Restructuring   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 2006  European plane maker Airbus, hit by delays for its A380 superjumbo, will slash the number of suppliers it uses by 83%, to 500 from 3,000, as part of a restructuring program, a spokeswoman said. Airbus spokeswoman Marcella Muratore gave no information on which suppliers would be dropped or the timetable for the cuts.
