 Dollar gains further on yen in sell-off of that currency, eases against mark   By Liu, Betty W, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 9, 1996  The dollar ended higher against the yen on Aug 8, 1996, but fell slightly against the mark. In late New York trading, the dollar was quoted at Y108.26, up from Y107.93 on Aug 7, and at DM1.4840, down from DM1.4848.
