 Fitch to Unveil Ratings to Measure Risk of Collateralized Mortgage Obligations   By Donnelly, Barbara, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1992  Fitch Investors Service Inc will introduce a rating system that measures the potential market risk of collateralized mortgage obligations. The rating system, which is the first of its kind, will attempt to measure the volatility of CMOs under different interest-rate conditions.
