 Business Brief -- LANDRY'S SEAFOOD RESTAURANTS INC.: Chairman, CEO Increased His Stake After Stock Fell   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1999  Tilman J. Fertitta, chairman and CEO of Landry's Seafood Restaurants Inc, in Mar 1999 increased his stake in the company by about 35%, buying 1.2 million common shares for between $5.92 and $6.13 a share. He said he bought the stock to show shareholders his continued commitment to the company and because he believes his stock is undervalued.
