 Eurodollar Bonds End Session Lower As U.S. Treasurys Fall --- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1986  One catalyst for yesterday's decline was the report that U.S. factory orders rose 0.1% in April. Many analysts had expected a decline in orders. The factory orders report -- coming after last week's big rise in April leading economic indicators, another surge in money supply and better-than-expected trade figures -- strengthened beliefs that the U.S. economy is rebounding.
