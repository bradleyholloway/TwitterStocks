 S&P lowers ratings on debt of 3 entities Perelman controls   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 1995  S&P Ratings Group said it lowered its ratings on certain debt in three entities controlled by investor Ronald O. Perelman. The rating agency said it downgraded--to single-B-minus from single-B-the senior secured debt of Marvel III Holdings Inc, Marvel (Parent) Holdings Inc and Marvel Holdings Inc, three debt-issuing entities without actual operations. S&P said the downgrade reflects developments at Marvel Entertainment Group Inc.
