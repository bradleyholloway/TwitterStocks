 AIG, Sosin settle dispute as insurer agrees to buy stake   By Steinmetz, Greg, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 1993  In an apparent end to a stormy six-year relationship that made vast amounts of money for both parties, American International Group settled its dispute with its former derivatives wizard, Howard Sosin, agreeing to buy out Sosin's stake in its AIG Financial Products unit for an undisclosed amount.
