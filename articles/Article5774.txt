 Grain and soybean prices soar on crop report   By McGee, Suzanne, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1995  Grain and soybean prices moved higher following the release of unexpectedly bullish crop forecasts by the USDA on Oct 11, 1995. On the CBOT, corn set a new contract high for the second day running, as December corn surged $0.05 to $3.2275 a bushel, while November soybeans gained $0.105 to $6.5925 a bushel. On other commodity markets, cotton prices fell, and crude-oil futures slipped while product prices edged higher.
