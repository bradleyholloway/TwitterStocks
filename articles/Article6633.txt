 Business Brief -- Cadbury Schweppes PLC: Independent Bottlers' Pacts For 7 Up Are Being Finalized   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 2002  Dr Pepper/Seven Up Inc., Plano, Texas, is in the process of finalizing agreements to relicense its 7 Up lemon-lime soft-drink brand to independent bottlers and distributors in various parts of the nation.
