 Shanghai, in a Bid To Be Regional Hub, Will Expand Airports   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 2004  Shanghai expects to have 100 million air travelers annually by 2015. That is almost triple the 35 million who have traveled through Pudong International and the city's older Hongqiao Airport so far this year, Shanghai Daily said. Hongqiao is now used exclusively for domestic flights.
