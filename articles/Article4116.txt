 Woodward's Expects Operating Net for Year To More Than Double   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 1986  Woodward's Ltd. expects that operating profit will more than double for the year ending Jan. 25 and that it will rise an additional 50% in fiscal 1987, Frank Robertson, president, said.  Mr. Robertson cited the western Canadian department and food store chain's vigorous expansion program and changes in stores and fashion lines.
