 Paging industry prepares to counter buzz about souped-up cell phones   By Buckman, Rebecca, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1996  Several years after exploding onto the market, paging devices are facing a new form of competition, from souped-up cellular phones. High-tech, digitally based cellular phones run on networks called personal-communication services threaten to supplant pagers because they offer phone and paging capabilities.
