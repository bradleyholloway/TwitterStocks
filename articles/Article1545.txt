 Beverage Concerns Are Expected to Post Modest Increases in 4th-Quarter Profits   By McCarthy, Michael J, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 1992  Most of the major beverage companies will report moderate gains in fourth-quarter earnings. Coca-Cola Inc should lead the group with a profit gain of at least 14%. PepsiCo Inc's net income will increase by at least 5% and Anheuser-Busch Cos should report an earnings gain of about 10%.
