 Shearson Pays Restitution Of $238,108 to 30 Investors   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 1988  Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc. paid a total of $238,108 in restitution to 30 Maine investors who bought limited partnership interests in Damson Oil & Gas Income Fund from 1982 to 1984, according to the securities division of the Maine State Bureau of Banking.
