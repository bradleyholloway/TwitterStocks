 How BCCI Took from Depositors, Gave Billions to the Rich   By Truell, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 1991  Banking regulators around the world expect that it could take months, or perhaps years, to fully investigate the affairs of Bank of Credit and Commerce International. At the center of the scandal are 12 Arab sheiks and their Pakistani bank managers who allegedly plundered BCCI for their own purposes. They include company founder Agha Hasan Abedi.
