 First federal execution in 33 years scheduled in Indiana on March 30   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1995  The Clinton administration has scheduled the first federal execution in 33 years. Convicted drug kingpin David Ronald Chandler, who was sentenced to death in 1991 for hiring another person to kill a member of his gang who had turned into an informer, will be executed on Mar 30, 1995.
