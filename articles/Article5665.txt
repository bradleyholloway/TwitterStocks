 Customer protection and CFTC policies   By Feigin, Philip A, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 11, 1994  Philip A. Feigin responds to Oct 4, 1994 article critical of the Enforcement Division of the Commodities Futures Trading Commission. Feigin, the Colorado Securities Commissioner, says he feels the CFTC has done a most competent job, given deregulatory tendencies and limited resourses available.
