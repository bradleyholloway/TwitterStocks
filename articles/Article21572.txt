 World-Wide: Mobs of Kurds   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1991  Mobs of Kurds attacked a police station, hospital and telephone office in the northern Iraqi town of Zahku. Separately, Baghdad charged that a UN plan to deduct up to 30% of Iraq's oil revenue to pay for Gulf War reparations was a deliberate effort to harm the Iraqis.
