 Automobiles: Buyers still batty over the VW bug   By Suris, Oscar, The Wall Street Journal,  May 13, 1994  Although Volkswagen has set for itself the ambitious goal of selling more than 100,000 vehicles in 1994, the public seems more fascinated with a car Volkswagen doesn't sell--the Concept 1, a prototype modeled after the popular, but discontinued, Beetle.
