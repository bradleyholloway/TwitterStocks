 Oil Hits 3-Week Low of $122.30   By Baskin, Brian; Resnick-Ault, Jessica, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 2008  Now that Asian demand is in question, as governments there cut fuel subsidies, attention has reverted back to the U.S. and the extent Americans will cut back on driving as gasoline pump prices approach $4 a gallon.
