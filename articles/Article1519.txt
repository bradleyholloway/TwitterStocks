 Carter-Wallace Inc. Agrees to Relabel Lambskin Condoms   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1991  Carter-Wallace Inc agreed to relabel its lambskin condoms to include warnings that they don't protect against certain sexually transmitted diseases. The company's Carter Products unit had been charged with false and misleading labeling of its Trojan brand condoms by the US attorney's office.
