 Hovnanian Earnings More Than Doubled During 4th Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 2002  Hovnanian increased its earnings forecast for fiscal 2003 to about $5 to $5.20 a share on revenue of more than $2.7 billion. For the year ended Oct. 31, Hovnanian reported net of $137.7 million, or $4.28 a share, on revenue of $2.55 billion.
