 ArcelorMittal Keeps Earnings Forecast   By MacDonald, Alex; Amiel, Geraldine, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 2008  ArcelorMittal, the world's largest steel producer by volume, confirmed its forecast for the third quarter and the second half of the year despite a global economic slowdown that has curtailed steel demand from key automotive, appliance and construction sectors.
