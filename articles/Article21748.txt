 Marketing: Christmas Tree Growers Spruce Up Sales and Fell Rivals by Boughing to Fashion   By Stipp, David, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1992  The traditional Christmas tree is being increasingly molded by the demands of competition and fashion, and planters are introducing new types of trees loaded with novel features and improved basics such as better fragrance, softer needles, richer blue-green hues and better-shaped branches.
