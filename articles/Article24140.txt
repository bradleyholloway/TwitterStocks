 World News: In Corner of Myanmar, Muslims Seek Relief --- A Port-Town Ghetto Springs Up as Checkpoints, Violence Hem In Rohingyas; Clashes Threaten Economic, Democratic Gains   By Hookway, James, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 2012  The Rohingyas, numbering around 800,000, comprise less than 1% of Myanmar's population, but around a fifth of the people in Rakhine state, where tensions with local Buddhists run deep. Since 1982, the government hasn't classified the Rohingyas as citizens, considering them to be illegal migrants from Bangladesh despite many Rohingya families having lived in the area for generations.
