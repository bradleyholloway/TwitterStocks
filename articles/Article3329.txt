 Nextel Is Facing Racial-Bias Complaints Filed With the EEOC by 25 Employees   By By Nicole Harris, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 2000  Denise McNeil, a 42-year-old former Nextel employee, said in an interview that as one of the company's few black female managers, she was often treated unfairly. She said other Nextel managers routinely "kept her out of the loop" and overlooked her for meetings. Ms. McNeil also complained that she was never assigned special projects "that her white counterparts used to catapult their careers."
