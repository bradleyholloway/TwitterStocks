Morgan net climbed by 10% in third quarter   By O Brien, Timothy L; Blackmon, Douglas A, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 13, 1995  Several large banks posted robust 1995 third-quarter earnings, with J. P. Morgan & Co especially trouncing Wall Street estimates. Morgan said its third-quarter earnings climbed 10%, to $360 million, up from $327 million in the year-ago quarter. First Union Corp's profit rose 5.5%, First Fidelity Bancorp's 9.2%, and Wachovia Corp's rose 9.6%.
