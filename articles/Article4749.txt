 Mondadori Holders Agree on Composition of Board   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 1991  Warring shareholders of Italy's largest publisher, Arnoldo Mondadori Editore SpA, reached an agreement about the composition of Mondadori's board. Under the agreement, the alliance between media magnate Silvio Berlusconi and the Formenton family will receive 10 positions on the board.
