 FDA Group Votes to Allow Use of Implants   By McMurray, Scott, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 1992  An FDA advisory panel voted on Feb 20, 1992 to allow the use of silicone gel breast implants, but with tighter restrictions. The panel voted to limit the number of women who will be able to obtain the implants for cosmetic breast enlargement, but women needing reconstructive surgery should be able to obtain the implants under closely monitored conditions.
