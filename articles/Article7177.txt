 Under Armour Shareholders May Not Be Well Protected   By Williams, Christopher C, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 2011  Under Armour might be getting too much love from investors. Since going public in 2005, the Baltimore-based sports-apparel company has sprinted to $1 billion in annual sales, challenging giant rivals Nike and Adidas as it pioneered sweat-wicking T-shirts and shorts.
