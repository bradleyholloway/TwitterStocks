 Freei Networks Files For Initial Offering   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 2000  Freei offers free Internet access to consumers through its Freeinternet.com service, in exchange for personal information sent anonymously to advertisers. The company said since launching its service in December 1998, the number of registered users has grown to about 2.2 million as of March 26. In 1999, Freei had a loss of $18.7 million on revenue of $983,000, according to filings.
