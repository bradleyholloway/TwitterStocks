 ITV PLC: Reunited Web Site to Be Bought To Get New Sources of Revenue   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 2005  ITV PLC, Britain's largest commercial broadcaster, said it will buy United Kingdom Web site Friends Reunited Ltd. for as much as GBP 175 million ($305 million), in a move to tap new sources of growth. Friends Reunited is a forum that enables people to contact old school friends.
