 Family Finances: Sizing Up Your Flood Risk   By Spors, Kelly K, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 2005  Damage caused by flooding can quickly become a financial nightmare, since it's not covered by homeowners insurance. Flood insurance essentially covers any damage caused by streams of water flowing outside your home and affecting your area. (An outdoor water leak that affects only your home doesn't count.) The average claim on flood insurance has been around $20,000 in the past year, says Ed Pasternick, an NFIP senior adviser.
