 Legal Beat: Insurers Cleared in Utilization Review Case   By Lambert, Wade; Gibson, Richard, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 1992  In the case of a man who was found dead three weeks after being discharged from a hospital following a so-called medical utilization review, a Los Angeles jury found that the insurers, Blue Cross Blue Shield of Alabama, were not negligent. The controversial reviews have been used increasingly in recent years as employers and insurers try to limit health care costs.
