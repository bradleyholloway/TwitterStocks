 Investor Financing Valued at $30 Million Boosts Critical Path   By By Nick Wingfield, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 2001  The San Francisco provider of e-mail services said the financing, led by private-equity firm General Atlantic Partners and including Hong Kong's Cheung Kong Ltd. and Hutchison Whampoa Ltd., also involved the retirement of $65 million in Critical Path's convertible debt. Critical Path will issue 52.4 million shares of convertible preferred stock to the investors as well as warrants to purchase 2.5 million shares of common stock. The company said it would disclose terms related to the conversion price for the shares in a filing with securities regulators today.
