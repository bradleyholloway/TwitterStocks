A giant robot spacecraft called Cassini is to streak past Earth tonight on its way to Saturn, culminating a circuitous, two-year journey through the inner solar system designed to send it on its way.  This journey, which has already involved two trips around Venus before the Earth swing, is like the extremely long windup of a celestial baseball pitcher before throwing a curve ball 2 billion miles.
