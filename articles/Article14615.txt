 Business Brief -- Hooker Corp.: Unit to Sell Certain Assets To a Subsidiary of Kajima   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 1989  L.J. Hooker Corp., the wholly owned U.S. subsidiary of Hooker Corp. of Australia, agreed to sell $60 million of industrial properties in the U.S. to Industrial Developments International Inc., a subsidiary of Kajima Corp. of Japan.
