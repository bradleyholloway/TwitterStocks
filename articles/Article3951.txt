 Fidelity Hires Former Top Treasury Official   By Levitz, Jennifer, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 2009  At the end of 2008, assets in Fidelity's stock mutual funds -- whose fees are a prime source of revenue for the firm -- had dropped 46%, to $395.9 billion from $730 billion a year earlier, according to Chicago research firm Morningstar Inc. Assets in Fidelity's famous Magellan Fund, for instance, have fallen to less than $20 billion for the first time since 1991, Morningstar said in a research note Friday.
