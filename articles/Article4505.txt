 Salomon Inc. Unit Dismisses Some Staff In Effort to Cut Costs   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 1989  A Salomon spokesman said about 10 staffers had been dismissed so far, and that while there is no companywide target, the spokesman said he didn't think the cutbacks would total more than 100 of the securities firm's 6,100 staffers. Salomon Brothers is the principal subsidiary of publicly traded Salomon Inc.
