 Bill to Limit Contaminants In Water Cleared by House   The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 1986  The House, by a 382-21 vote, approved a House-Senate conference bill requiring the Environmental Protection Agency to set limits for 64 contaminants in tap water within three years.  Environmental groups praised the bill for tightening current law and forcing the EPA to step up its slow pace of setting standards.
