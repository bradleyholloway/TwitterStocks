 American Air and British Air may defy EU   By Goldsmith, Charles, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 1997  The British Airways-American Airlines alliance is prepared to openly defy European Union objections by selling slots at Heathrow Airport if told by British regulations to surrender a given number of slots, a senior American Air officials said. An airport slot refers to an opening assigned to an airline for a takeoff or landing. The EU claims slot-selling is illegal.
