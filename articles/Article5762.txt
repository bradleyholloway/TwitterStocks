 Time Off: Diversions and Excursions Sept. 12-25   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 1995  The Lyric opens its season Sept. 15 with one of Verdi's later and more somber operas, "Simon Boccanegra," with Kiri Te Kenawa as Amelia and Alexandru Agache in the title role. On Sept. 22 the company presents its premiere of an acclaimed English National Opera production of Handel's comic opera "Xerxes" with Ann Murray in the title role and Nicholas Hytner directing.
