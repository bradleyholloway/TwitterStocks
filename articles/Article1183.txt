 Noted...   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 2007  AMERIGROUP Corp. (Virginia Beach, Va.) -- President and Chief Operating Officer James G. Carlson was named president and chief executive, effective Sept. 1, with this health-services provider. Mr. Carlson, 55 years old, was also named to the board, effective immediately. He will succeed founder Jeffrey L. McWaters as CEO. Mr. McWaters, 50, announced his retirement, effective Aug. 31. He will continue to serve as chairman. Mr. Carlson's addition brings the board to eight members. The company hasn't named a successor for the operating-chief role.
