 Bridge Oil Ltd.: Oil company posts 37% rise in operating profit for 1992   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 1993  Australia's Bridge Oil Ltd said operating profit after taxes rose 37% in 1992 to 39.4 million Australian dollars ($27.8 million) from A$28.7 million in 1991. Sales fell 8.5% to A$179.2 million.
