 Repap to sell its U.S. unit, other assets   By Chipello, Christopher J, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1997  Cash-strapped Repap Enterprises Inc signed a letter of intent to sell its US coated-paper unit to Consolidated Papers Inc for $227 million, plus assumption of $447 million in debt and other obligations. Repap also said it reached an agreement in principle to sell its kraft-paper and lumber operations in Manitoba Canada to Tolko Industries Ltd, a closely held forest-products company based in British Columbia.
