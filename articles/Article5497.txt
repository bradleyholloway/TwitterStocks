 Merger planned by Columbia Hospital Corp.   By Tomsho, Robert; Stern, Gabriella, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 11, 1993  Columbia Hospital Corp and Galen Health Care Inc said they will merge in a $3.2 billion stock swap that will create one one of the US's largest health-care providers. The new entity, which will have combined annual revenues estimated to exceed $5 billion, will be named Columbia Healthcare Corp and will own and operate 99 hospitals with more than 22,000 licensed beds.
