 Difficult Week Fosters a Spirit of Cooperation, But Hectic Pace Stirs the Instinct to Compete   By By Kopin Tan, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 2001  To be sure, it has been a tough week for all. Yesterday, volatility continued its climb as selling persisted. The CBOE's market-volatility index, or VIX, jumped 13.4%, or 5.8, to 49.04. The ratio of equity puts traded to calls at the CBOE -- which rarely has finished above 1.0 -- reached 1.20. However, some traders say they discount the ratio's indications since that might be distorted in the heavy trading volume of this expiration week.
