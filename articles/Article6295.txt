 Equifax Agrees to Buy Consumer-Data Unit Of R.L. Polk & Co.   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 11, 2000  The acquisition, expected to close by April 30, would give Equifax a bigger presence in direct marketing. The Polk databases being acquired contain demographic and lifestyle information from 105 million households. Equifax said it also expects the deal to help it further build the company's established credit-information and payment-services businesses.
