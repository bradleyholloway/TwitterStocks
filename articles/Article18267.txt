 Sports: IRS to Tax Sponsors' Fees to Bowl-Game Organizers   By Fuchsberg, Gilbert; Robichaux, Mark, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1991  The IRS ruled that organizers of the Mobil Cotton Bowl and John Hancock Bowl must pay taxes on millions of dollars in sponsor fees. The sponsors, Mobil Corp and John Hancock Mutual Life Insurance Co, are not directly affected by the ruling, but they could be pressured to compensate for the tax effect experiences by the organizers of their events.
