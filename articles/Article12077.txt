 Small stock focus: Investors try to tally value of tech issues   By Lohse, Deborah, The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1996  Small-capitalization and Nasdaq stocks jumped higher on May 3, 1996 on news of the unexpectedly weak April employment report, only to have the gains erased by session's end. The Russell 2000 index rose 0.91 to 346.85, while the Nasdaq Composite Index gained 6.27 at 1184.60.
