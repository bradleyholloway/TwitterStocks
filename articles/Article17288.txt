 Ireland Will Hug You to Death --- With a Suffocating New Defense, the Shockingly Good Shamrocks Plot the Ultimate World Cup Coup   By Clegg, Jonathan, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 2011  The secret behind Ireland's surge is a revolutionary and suffocating defensive scheme, known as the "choke," that relies on ferocious tackling and a loophole in the rules of the game to sap energy and momentum of its opponents. The physical toll required to perform the choke is so exhausting that it would have been impossible to implement without the fitter, faster, physically stronger players in today's game or the time to practice it obsessively which is now available in the professional era.
