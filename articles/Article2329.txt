 Retailing: Armani Gamble Wins Support of Big Stores   By Trachtenberg, Jeffrey A, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 1992  The planned opening of as many as 150 free-standing A/X Armani Exchange stores in the US is discussed. The stores will feature casual weekend clothes, most of which are priced under $100. The stores are licensed to Simint USA, a division of Simint SpA, based in Modena Italy. Armani owns a 20% stake in Simint.
