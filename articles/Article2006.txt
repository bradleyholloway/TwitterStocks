 IBM's boss paid $12.4 million, stock options   By Hays, Laurie, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1995  Louis V. Gerstner Jr, chairman of IBM Corp, was given cash compensation of $12.4 million in 1994, plus new stock options that would provide a paper profit of an additional $4.7 million, for his role in the company's rebound and to cover contract terms.
