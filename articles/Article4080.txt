 W&J Sloane Files For Chapter 11 Status, Names New President   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1985  The furniture retailer also named Jerome W. Robbins as president and chief executive officer, succeeding Leon J. Levitz, who controls about 29% of the company's common stock. Mr. Levitz, 69 years old, continues as chairman.
