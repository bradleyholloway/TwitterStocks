 Suzuki Motor Co.'s Anderson Is Named Top U.S. Sales Official   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 13, 1989  BREA, Calif. -- Suzuki Motor Co.'s U.S. sales arm has named Gary Anderson its top U.S. official in the wake of a series of resignations last month.  Mr. Anderson, who joined American Suzuki Motor Corp. in June 1987, gets the title of marketing director for the company's automotive division. He inherits "bits of several positions," said a Suzuki spokeswoman, who added that a reorganization last month has eliminated some previous posts.
