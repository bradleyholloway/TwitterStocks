 UAL Corp.: United Pilot Shortage Forces Cancellation of 186 Flights   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 2006  UAL Corp.'s United Airlines canceled 186 flights out of about 3,000 during the weekend of June 24-25 due to a pilot shortage caused by poor weather on the East Coast, its "aggressive" flight schedule and insufficient numbers of reserve pilots standing by to fly, an airline spokeswoman said Friday.
