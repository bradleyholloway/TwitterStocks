 Camden Sets a Curfew on Businesses   By Haddon, Heather, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 2012  CAMDEN, N.J. -- Years of escalating violence and funding cuts for its police force have led this troubled city on the Delaware River to consider an unusual crime-fighting step: a curfew for most of its businesses. A proposal to scrap the city police force for a county agency has been discussed for years but is now moving forward with the support of Gov. Chris Christie, a Republican, and Ms. Redd and other South Jersey Democratic Party leaders.
