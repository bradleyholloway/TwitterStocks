 Business Brief -- SUNSTONE HOTEL INVESTORS: Private Group's Purchase Is Confirmed at $450 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 1999  Sunstone Hotel Investors confirmed it agreed to be acquired by a private group that includes some of its management for about $450 million plus the assumption of $450 million in debt. The private group, SHP Acquisitions, includes REIT's CEO Robert A. Alter.
