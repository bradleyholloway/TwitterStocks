 Algerian Police Detain Islamic Activists, Pushing Nation toward a Violent Clash   By Waldman, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1992  An announcement by the popular Islamic Salvation Front that police detained dozens of its activists is expected to lead to a violent clash between the party and Algeria's ruling junta. The military ousted President Chadli Benjedid and canceled the second round of elections scheduled for Jan 16, 1992.
