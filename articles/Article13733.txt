 SEC Discord Could Stymie Schapiro's Efforts   By Scannell, Kara, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 2010  [...] one big part of Securities and Exchange Commission Chairman Mary Schapiro's job -- winning the support she needs from the agency's commissioners -- is turning into a headache. Since taking over the embattled agency in January 2009, the 54-year-old Ms. Schapiro has brought in a new enforcement chief, created a division to scout out market risks and laid out an ambitious rule-making agenda to restore market confidence.
