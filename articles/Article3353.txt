 Bloggers Didn't Create Case of Career Suicide   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2004  During this past presidential campaign, bloggers helped many individuals see multiple sides of events in a very timely fashion. Many blogsites I visited were updated multiple times per day. I'm surprised by some of the mainstream media's lack of attention to the 'Net and how slowly many of them react to breaking news and wait to post. Internet users want information now -- not at some scheduled posting time.
