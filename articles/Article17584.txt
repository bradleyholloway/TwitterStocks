 AMC Entertainment Sees Rise in Earnings In Current Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1986  KANSAS CITY, Mo. -- AMC Entertainment Inc. expects an earnings turnaround in its fiscal fourth quarter ending March 31, Ron Leslie, president, said.  Though the movie theater operator's financial performance has been running behind that of fiscal 1985, "We expect a stronger fourth quarter," Mr. Leslie said, citing a 20% increase in admissions at theaters. "Our profit in January, for instance, was double last year's."
