 Fisher Scientific Stock Falls 13% as Growth At a Division Slows   The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 1997  HAMPTON, N.H. -- Shares in Fisher Scientific International Inc. fell 13% after the scientific-instruments maker announced slowing revenue growth in its clinical-products unit.  Separately, the company's largest investor group, which includes members of the Bass family, said it was "disappointed" with the performance but would seek to acquire up to a 25% stake.
