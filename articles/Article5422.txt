 Gardini Srl   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 1992  Gardini Srl of Italy, the holding company of entrepreneur Raul Gardini, is planning a capital increase of as much as two trillion lire ($1.47 billion) in preparation for a possible public tender offer for state food group Societa Meridionale Finanziaria SpA, known as SME. The Gardini family plans to keep a 60% stake in the company.
