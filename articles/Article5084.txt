 Nissan Unveils NX Coupe And a Redesigned Sunny ---- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 1990  Nissan Motor Co. unveiled the NX Coupe, a sporty, two-door liftback, with a 1.5-liter engine and digital instrument panel.  In Japan, the NX Coupe will cost 1.14 million yen to 1.96 million yen ($7,834 to $13,469). Exports, primarily to North America, are to begin in May, but Nissan said it hasn't decided on export prices.
