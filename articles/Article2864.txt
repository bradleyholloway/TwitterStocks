 For Bank Acquisitions, The Action Is in Alabama   By By Rick Brooks, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 1996  According to data compiled by SNL Securities L.P., three Birmingham banks -- Regions Financial Corp., Compass Bancshares Inc. and SouthTrust Corp. -- announced or completed a total of 28 deals in the year ended July 31. That ranks the banks first, third and fourth among all U.S. financial institutions, says SNL, a bank and thrift research firm in Charlottesville, Va.
