 Enterprise: A Hairdresser's Secret: Keeping His Clients Close; Stylist Arrojo Excels With Focus on Beauty And the Bottom Line   By Bounds, Gwendolyn, The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 2008  In the annals of luxury beauty and hair care, the latest entrepreneurial insurgent is a 42-year-old Brit who left school at age 16, started working in a salon for GBP 22 a week (about $40, then) because he "needed a job," and stayed because he "could meet girls and listen to music all day." Today, Mr. Arrojo is stylist on the TLC channel's "What Not to Wear," owner of a New York salon, and plans to open a cosmetology school this summer.
