 Delta Queen Orders Steamboat, Sees Net above Some Forecasts   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 1992  Delta Queen Steamboat Co has contracted to have its third steamboat built by McDermott Industries Inc. The boat, scheduled to begin operations in 1994, would seat 420 passengers. Also, the firm's chairman, S. Cody Engle, said that the firm's third-quarter earnings would be slightly above analysts' projections of $.23 a share.
