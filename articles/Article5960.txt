 New Securities Issues   The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 2001  Newmont Mining Corp. -- $275 million offering of notes was priced through lead managers Salomon Smith Barney and J.P. Morgan Chase, according to MCM CorporateWatch. Terms: maturity: May 15, 2011; coupon: 8.625%; issue price: 99.729; yield: 8.666%; spread: 350 basis points above Treasurys; call date: make-whole call at Treasurys plus 45 basis points; ratings: Baa-3 (Moody's); triple-B (S&P).  WorldCom Inc. -- $10.1 billion offering of debt was priced in three parts through lead managers Salomon Smith Barney and J.P. Morgan Chase, according to people familiar with the deal. Terms: Part one: amount: $1.5 billion; maturity: May 15, 2004; coupon: 6.50%; issue price: 99.823; yield: 6.566%; spread: 215 basis points above Treasurys; call date: noncallable; ratings: A-3 (Moody's); triple-B-plus (S&P); Part two: amount: $4 billion; maturity: May 15, 2011; coupon: 7.50%; issue price: 98.904; yield: 7.659%; spread: 245 basis points above Treasurys; call date: make-whole call at Treasurys plus 30 basis points; ratings: A-3 (Moody's); triple-B-plus (S&P); Part three: amount: $4.6 billion; maturity: May 15, 2031; coupon: 8.25%; issue price: 98.098; yield: 8.425%; spread: 265 basis points above Treasurys; call date: make-whole call at Treasurys plus 35 basis points; ratings: A-3 (Moody's); triple-B-plus (S&P).  Koninklijke Philips Electronics NV -- 2.25 billion euros were priced in two tranches, 1.5 billion euros of 5.75% Eurobonds and 750 million euros of 6.125% Eurobonds. Tranche one: 1.5 billion euros of 5.75% Eurobonds, due May. 16, 2008, at 99.72, via ABN Amro, Deutsche Bank and Schroder Salomon Smith Barney. Spread: 62 basis points above midswaps; 97 basis points above the January 2008 bund. Fees 0.35%. Tranche two: 750 million euros of 6.125% Eurobonds due May 16, 2011, at 99.744, via ABN Amro, Deutsche Bank and Schroder Salomon Smith Barney. Spread: 80 basis points above midswaps;123 basis points above the January 2011 bund. Fees 0.375%.
