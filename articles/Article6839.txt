 Chinese Auto Sales Hit Platinum Futures; Bright Spot Dims For Industrial Use; Funds in Exit Mode   By Whittaker, Matt, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 2008  Selling by hedge, pension and other speculative funds -- coupled with Chinese auto-sales data that disappointed the market -- pushed most-active October platinum down $90.80, or 6.7%, to $1,262.10 an ounce on the New York Mercantile Exchange, a life-of-contract low.
