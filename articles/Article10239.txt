 Large Stock Focus -- Risky Businesses Rise: H-P, AmEx and GM   By Curran, Rob, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 2009  Stocks rose Tuesday as traders tuned out dire economic data and Federal Reserve Board views to take a chance on stocks in risky sectors such as technology and consumer finance. The leading gainers on the Dow Jones Industrial Average were computer maker Hewlett-Packard, credit-card issuer American Express and car maker General Motors, all considered to be on the riskier end of the stock spectrum because of the consumer-sensitive nature of their business lines.
