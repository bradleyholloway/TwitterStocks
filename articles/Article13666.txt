 Off The Beaten Track / Timbavati, South Africa   By Davis, Bob, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 6, 2009  A tracker sits on a seat bolted to the hood and searches for paw prints and dung to direct the driver, who is a knowledgeable ranger.
