 Crude-Oil Prices Stage a Breakout   By Pleven, Liam, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 2010  Investors pushed crude prices above $82 a barrel in early trading Friday on the New York Mercantile Exchange -- near the highest point in 17 months -- as positive sentiment about rosier-than-feared U.S. jobs data overrode persistent concerns about the economic recovery.
