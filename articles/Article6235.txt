 Business Brief -- ONEIDA LTD.: Cost-Cutting Helps Boost 2nd-Quarter Profit by 51%   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1999  Oneida Ltd, a maker of stainless-steel and silver-plated flatware, said second-quarter earnings rose 51%, largely because of cost savings. Net income rose to $7.7 million, or 46 cents a diluted share, from $5.1 million, or 30 cents a share, a year earlier.
