 Earnings Digest: Infosys Outsourcing Orders Drive 70% Surge in Profit   By Romit Guha and Jackie Range, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 2007  The Bangalore-based company, which pioneered India's outsourcing industry by leveraging on the country's vast pool of English-speaking software engineers to service Western companies, now gets about 98.5% of its revenue from exports. Its clients include Goldman Sachs Group Inc. and J.C. Penney Co. It is India's second-largest software exporter by revenue after Tata Consultancy Services Ltd.
