 Dividend News: Prospect Group Plans To Distribute Stock In 2 Units to Holders   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 1990  Prospect Group Inc. said it plans to distribute common stock holdings in two units, Illinois Central Corp. and Sylvan Foods Inc., to Prospect's shareholders.  The Illinois Central distribution is expected to be made on Jan. 5 to shareholders of record on that date. Prospect's holding of common stock in Illinois Central will decline to 32% from 64% after the distribution. Remaining shares are held by management and third party investors.  The shares of Illinois Central and Sylvan will be "stapled" to the related Prospect shares and initially traded as a single unit.
