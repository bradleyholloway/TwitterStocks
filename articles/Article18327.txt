 MiniScribe Sets Settlements of All Litigation against It   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 1992  Formal settlements of all litigation involving the defunct Mini-Scribe Corp were reached, but are pending approval by a federal judge and bankruptcy judge overseeing the case. MiniScribe's former executives, accounting firm Coopers & Lybrand, and the investment banking firm of Hambrecht & Quist agreed to pay $128.1 million to settle the claims of shareholders and creditors.
