Four men wearing ski masks and a woman companion who had pretended to be delivering flowers forced their way into a house in the exclusive Forest Hills Gardens section of Queens yesterday afternoon and stole a safe containing about $1 million worth of jewels, as well as antique rugs valued at $150,000, the police said.  The home at 170 Slocum Crescent belongs to Agmajan Nassimi, 76 years old, who owns an importing and exporing business, Nassimi Corporation, at 185 Madison Avenue in Manhattan, said a police spokesman, Officer Joseph Gallagher.
