 Insurers split over plan to revamp Home Holdings   By Baker, Molly, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1995  Battle lines are being drawn in the property- and casualty-insurance industry over Home Holdings Inc, the holding company for Home Insurance Co and the object of a restructuring proposal by Zurich Insurance Group. The outcome, if approved by regulators, could have a major impact on the shape of the business.
