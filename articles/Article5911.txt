 GE's NBC Unit Says '96 Cash Flow to Hit A Record $1 Billion   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1996  NBC President Robert Wright, speaking at a PaineWebber Inc. media conference, said the cash flow -- earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization -- is the best ever for NBC, and up more than 16% from 1995. He said revenue this year, boosted in part by the network's Olympics coverage, is expected to exceed $5 billion. "This is a very good year for us," Mr. Wright said.
