 Tailoring Treatment for Black Addicts   By Boyce, Joseph N, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 1990  Kenneth Beverly and Nate Mack, two black former drug abusers, have formed a for-profit partnership with health care professionals that specializes in "culturally specific" therapies for black substance-abusers. The venture is located in Atlanta and is called Coalesce.
