 Fund track: Stock mutual funds surge $18.5 billion   By Gasparino, Charles, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 13, 1997  The Investment Co Institute estimated on Jun 12, 1997 that investors poured $18.5 billion into stock mutual funds in May, an 18% increase above the $15.7 billion stock mutual funds gleaned during April. But the big story may not be in all the new money heading for stock funds. Investors are now showing increased interest in bond funds, which have lagged behind turbo-charged returns investors have found with stock mutual funds.
