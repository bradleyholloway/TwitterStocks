 Outlining a vision: Dole's economic plan calls for a 15% cut in personal income tax   By Calmes, Jackie; Harwood, John, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 1996  On Aug 5, 1996 during a speech in Chicago, Robert Dole plans to propose a 15% across-the-board cut in all personal-income tax rates as part of a broad agenda for invigorating the economy heading into the 21st century--and for lifting his struggling Republican presidential campaign in the meantime.
