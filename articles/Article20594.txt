 World News: Japan Dispatches   By Anonymous, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 2011  Megumi Fujikawa --- DISASTER RELIEF Softbank Chief Executive Donates Aid for Victims Masayoshi Son, Japan's wealthiest person and the chief executive officer of Softbank Corp., said Sunday that he will support the region and people affected by the March 11 earthquake and tsunami by personally donating 10 billion yen, about $120 million, by far the largest amount given by any individual so far to that cause.
