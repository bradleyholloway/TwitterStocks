 Charge of $39 Million Taken by Jiffy Lube In Its Third Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 1989  Jiffy Lube International Inc. said it took a one-time charge of about $39 million against third-quarter earnings.  The move reflects efforts to break up some of the automotive lubrication chain's large franchisees into smaller groups and the creation of a reserve for losses as it restructures agreements with troubled franchise operators who owe the company money.
