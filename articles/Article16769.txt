 U.S. probes Jeep Wranglers   By Kosdrosky, Terry, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 2007  US safety regulators opened a preliminary investigation into the Chrysler Group's 2007 Jeep Wrangler after receiving complaints of engine stall at highway speed. The NHTSA said that it has received 53 complaints and that a majority of those drivers said they experienced stalling on more than one occasion.
