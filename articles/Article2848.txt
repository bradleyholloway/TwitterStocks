 Business Brief -- Health Management Associates Inc.: Pretax Charge of $17 Million Will Be Taken in Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 2001  Health Management Associates Inc. said it expects to take a pretax charge of $17 million in its fiscal second quarter ending March 31 related to retirement benefits for its chairman and the write-down of certain assets. The rural South hospital chain said the charge will reduce fiscal second-quarter net income by $10.3 million, or four cents a diluted share.
