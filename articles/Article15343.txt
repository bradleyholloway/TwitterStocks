 Dollar is mixed in European trading quieted by holiday closing in the U.S.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 1995  The dollar was mixed against other major currencies on Jul 4, 1995 in European trading becalmed by the US's Independence Day holiday and anticipation of an FRB panel's meeting on interest rates. The Jul 4 holiday closed markets in the US and kept markets abroad in quiet trading.
