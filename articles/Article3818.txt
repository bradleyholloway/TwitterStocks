 More Corporate Career Sites Satisfy Job Hunters' Demands Online edition  By Needleman, Sarah E, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 2008  Features that respondents say they most want to find easily include detailed company and job profiles, descriptions of ideal candidates, insights into long-term career opportunities and a clearly defined application process.
