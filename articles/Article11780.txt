 Partnership woes facing more brokers   By Siconolfi, Michael, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 1993  Merrill Lynch & Co and PaineWebber Group Inc have joined the ranks of big Wall Street firms that are saddled with limited partnership woes. Merrill Lynch, the nation's biggest brokerage firm, is offering to settle a class-action suit filed in Chicago federal court by 16,500 investors in Arvida/JMB Partners LP, a $235 million partnership that bought raw land to develop homes, and PaineWebber recently settled several arbitration cases without admitting or denying guilt.
