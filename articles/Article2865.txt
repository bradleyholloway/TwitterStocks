 Spotty profits at apparel retailers are posted on soft clothing sales   By Bird, Laura; Berner, Robert, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 1996  Several apparel retailers reported spotty profits for the fiscal second quarter. Talbots Inc reported that net income fell 20% to $6.6 million for the quarter ended Aug 3, 1996. AnnTaylor Stores Corp swung to a profit, reporting net income of $627,000, compared with a year-ago loss of $3.8 million. Earnings figures for Limited Inc, whose earnings were up 16%, and Dayton Hudson Corp, whose earnings more than tripled to $101 million, are also given.
