 Economy Seen Slow in Future, Index Shows   By Harper, Lucinda, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1992  The Commerce Department reported that its gauge of future economic activity, the index of leading indicators, fell 0.3% in Sep 1992 following a revised 0.3% decline in August. However, the index is up 2.4% for the year, reinforcing the view that the nation is still in economic limbo.
