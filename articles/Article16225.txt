 Business Brief -- Church & Dwight Co.: Net Income Advances by 16%, Fourth-Period Forecast Given   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 2002  Church & Dwight Co., of Princeton, N.J., posted a 16% increase in third-quarter profit on strong sales at its personal-care division, but issued a fourth-quarter earnings forecast below analysts' expectations. The maker of Arm & Hammer household products said net rose to $17.6 million, or 42 cents a share, from $15.2 million, or 37 cents a share, a year earlier.
