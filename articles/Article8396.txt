 Ex-chairman, CEO of Giddings & Lewis cuts all ties to firm   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 7, 1993  William J. Fife Jr, the former chairman and CEO of Giddings & Lewis Inc who resigned under pressure in Apr 1993, has severed his last ties with the company, forfeiting certain stock rights and agreeing to purchase some real estate that played a role in his surprise departure from the company.
