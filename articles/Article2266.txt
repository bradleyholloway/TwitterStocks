 Both Freddie Mac and Fannie Mae post robust earnings   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1997  The FHLMC and the FNMA, the US's two largest purchasers of home mortgages, reported improved 1996 fourth-quarter earnings. The FHLMC said net income was $321 million, up 10% from $291 million a year earlier. The FNMA said net was $712.1 million, up 12% from $635.8 million a year earlier.
