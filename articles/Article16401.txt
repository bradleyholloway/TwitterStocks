 Health Brief: Pfizer Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 2004  Pfizer Inc. filed additional lawsuits against Internet site operators based in India for allegedly selling an unapproved copy of Pfizer's top-selling cholesterol medicine, Lipitor. Lipitor contributed $9.2 billion of Pfizer's 2003 sales of $45.19 billion.
