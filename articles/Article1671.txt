 U.S. Has Jellyfish That Nobody Wants Except Maybe in Asia   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 1992  Jack Rudloe, who runs a nonprofit marine laboratory with his wife, is profiled for efforts to establish a jellyfish industry in the US. In 1991, Rudloe received a $13,000 grant from the Commerce Department's Economic Development Administration to study Malaysia's and Thailand's jellyfish processing operations.
