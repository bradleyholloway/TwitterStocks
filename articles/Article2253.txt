 Fund track: Soros's Quantum fund: No leap   By Jereski, Laura, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1997  George Soros's flagship $7 billion Quantum Fund suffered through its worst year in 1996 since 1981, losing 1.49%, thanks to too big a stake in the free-falling Japanese securities markets and too small a bet on US stocks.
