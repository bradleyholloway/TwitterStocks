 California Pension Plan Hires Firm to Quiz Time Warner on Rights Plan   By Smith, Randall; Landro, Laura, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 1991  In the latest sign of investor concern about a $3.5 billion Time Warner Inc stock rights plan, the $63 billion California Employees Retirement System, known as Calpers, has hired Providence Capital Inc to quiz Time Warner management on the plan. Calpers owns 570,000 shares of Time Warner.
