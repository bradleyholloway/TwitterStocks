 Ryland Group Inc. Indicates Net Rose in Third Quarter   By Blumenthal, Robin Goldwyn, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1992  Ryland Group Inc, a home-building mortgage concern, said it is comfortable with analysts' estimates that it earned $.55 to $.65 a share in the third quarter, or about $8.6 million to $10.2 million. In the year-earlier quarter, the company earned $6.9 million, or $.49 a share.
