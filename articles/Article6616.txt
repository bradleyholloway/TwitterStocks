 American Electric Says Earnings In 2002 Will Decline by 11%   By By Kathryn Kranhold, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 11, 2002  AEP estimated its 2002 earnings would be between $2.85 and $3.15 a share, compared with an earlier estimate of between $3.20 and $3.35 a share. AEP blamed the revised estimates on lower trading income and wholesale-energy-generation sales. The company said its power plant in Britain, which it purchased last year, also was performing poorly. AEP said its 2003 earnings would be flat.
