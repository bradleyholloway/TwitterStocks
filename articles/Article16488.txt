 Hollywood Entertainment CEO Quits After New Blockbuster Bid   By Flint, Joe, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 2005  Mr. Wattle's resignation came the day after Blockbuster Inc. raised its bid to acquire Hollywood to $14.50 a share in cash and stock, or $991 million. The new bid topped a $13.25 a share, or $850 million offer from Movie Gallery Inc., a bid that last month had won support of Hollywood's board. Yesterday Hollywood said a special committee of its board of directors would consider the new Blockbuster bid and make a recommendation to shareholders by Feb. 17.
