 Money Funds See $37.73 Billion Net Outflow   The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 2001  Average seven-day simple yields on taxable money funds fell to 4.25% from 4.33%, while average seven-day compound yields fell to 4.34% from 4.42%, the newsletter reported. Thirty-day simple yields fell to 4.45% from 4.55%, while 30-day compound yields declined to 4.55% from 4.66%. Compound yields assume reinvestment of dividends.
