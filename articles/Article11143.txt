 International Corporate Report: Isuzu, Volvo to Study Sales Of Swedish Trucks in Japan   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 1988  ISUZU MOTORS LTD., Japan's top commercial vehicle maker, and Sweden's VOLVO TRUCK CORP., a unit of AB VOLVO, said they signed a "letter of intent" to undertake a joint market survey exploring the possibility of exporting heavy-duty Volvo trucks to the Japanese market.  "We want to study if there is sufficient demand to justify such an action," an Isuzu spokesman said. If so, Isuzu said, it will begin importing and distributing this type of vehicle inside Japan.
