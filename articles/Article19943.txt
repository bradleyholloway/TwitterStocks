 China Petroleum & Chemical Corp.: Sinopec to Take Refining Unit Private to Improve Efficiency   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 2005  China Petroleum & Chemical Corp. said it plans to take 71%-owned Sinopec Zhenhai Refining & Chemical Co. private to improve the group's efficiency in a deal some analysts estimate could be valued at as much as 7.96 billion Hong Kong dollars (1.03 billion US dollars).
