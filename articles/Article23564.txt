 Politics & Economics: Japan Struggles to Correct Pension-System Mistakes   By Moffett, Sebastian, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 2008  Less than six months after Japan's pension-recording turmoil helped trigger the downfall of one prime minister, revelations about the wide scale of the problem are threatening the current leader. Last month, the government found that a large number of pension records contained mistakes due to inaccurate data entry several decades ago.
