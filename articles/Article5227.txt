 Small stock focus: Nasdaq unveils studies in bid to refute charges   By Baker, Molly, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 1995  Teams of Nasdaq-hired economics and finance professors unveiled three new studies to refute charges of collusion made against the market.
