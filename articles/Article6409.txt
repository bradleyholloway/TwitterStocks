 Money-Market Funds Decrease   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 2005  The average seven-day simple yield for taxable funds rose to 1.81% from 1.75%. The average seven-day compound yield, which assumes reinvestment of dividends, rose to 1.83% from 1.77%. Thirty-day simple yields rose to 1.73% from 1.69% last week, while 30-day compound yields climbed to 1.74% from 1.70%.  The highest seven-day compound yield among retail taxable general- purpose money funds belonged to the PayPal Money-Market Fund at 2.48%, followed by Scudder Money-Market Series Premium/AARP at 2.37% and Scudder Money-Market Series Premium/Cl at 2.35%.
