 Brascan Ltd. Offers $600 Million of Debt   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 1989  Brascan is a financial services, natural resources and consumer products concern controlled by Toronto businessmen Edward and Peter Bronfman through Brascan Holdings Ltd. It said the financing involves a $500 million syndicated debenture issue underwritten by a group led by Chemical Bank and $100 million of debentures placed privately with Commerzbank.
