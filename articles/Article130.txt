Anne Segal and Mark D. Silverschotz, lawyers in Manhattan, were married yesterday at Temple Beth Shalom in Livingston, N.J., by Rabbi Azriel C. Fellner.  The bride, who will retain her name, is a daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Alfred Segal of Riverdale, the Bronx. She is a graduate of Radcliffe College and received a master's degree in American civilization from Brown University and a law degree from the University of Pennsylvania.
