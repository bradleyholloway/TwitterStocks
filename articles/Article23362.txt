 Merrill Lynch, Mitsubishi UFJ Open Venture   By Shimamura, Kazuhiro, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 2006  The joint venture, Mitsubishi UFJ Merrill Lynch PB Securities Co., started operations Monday. Merrill Lynch contributed its Japanese unit's private-client business, with 8,000 accounts and more than one trillion yen in assets under administration, to the joint company.  The venture's chief executive, Junji Okabayashi, said the firm will initially target existing customers of Mitsubishi UFJ, the world's largest banking group by assets.
