 Apex to Unveil Console to Play PC Games on TV   By Clark, Don, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2004  The device, which plugs into a TV set, resembles a personal computer with modifications to work like a dedicated game console, such as Sony Corp.'s popular PlayStation 2 system. It relies on chip technology from Taiwan's Via Technologies Inc. and a special slimmed-down version of Microsoft Corp.'s Windows operating system, said Richard Brown, Via's associate vice president for marketing.
