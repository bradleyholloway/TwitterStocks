 Corporate News: Grupo Mexico Bids for Asarco --- Move Seeks to Free Unit From Chapter 11, Thwart Rival   By Millman, Joel; McLaughlin, David, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 2009  Mexican mining company Grupo Mexico SAB threw a fresh punch in its fight to retain U.S. copper subsidiary Asarco LLC, pledging $1.3 billion in cash to take the unit out of Chapter 11 proceedings and defeat a rival bid by Vedanta Resources PLC. Though Vedanta's deal to acquire Asarco has a higher total -- $1.7 billion -- it includes just $1.1 billion in cash, plus a note for $600 million.
