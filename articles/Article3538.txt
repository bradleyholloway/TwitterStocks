 Business Brief -- Hubbell Inc.: Agreement Reached to Buy U.S. Industries' LCA Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 2002  Hubbell Inc. agreed to acquire LCA Group, the domestic lighting unit of U.S. Industries Inc., for $250 million. Lighting is one of Hubbell's core businesses, and the company anticipates LCA's brands will strengthen its place in the lighting-fixture supply market. LCA Group had 2001 sales of more than $575 million, according to Hubbell.
