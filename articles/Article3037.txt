 Volatility Indexes Ease Slightly After Fed Holds on Interest Rates   By By Kopin Tan, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 2002  Dell's options were moderately active, with some investors covering their short positions and possibly bracing for an upside move. Dell stock fell 18 cents to $25.72. Its September 27.50 calls traded 3,489 contracts, compared with open interest of 9,284 contracts, and rose five cents to $1 at the CBOE. Its September 25 puts traded 1,899 contracts, compared with open interest of 3,306 contracts, and eased five cents to $1.30 at the International Securities Exchange.
