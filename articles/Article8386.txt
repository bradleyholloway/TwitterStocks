 Schering says profit rose 3.4% in 1st half, upgrades '93 outlook   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 6, 1993  Schering AG, a German pharmaceutical and agrochemical group, reported a first-half rise of 3.4% in net income and said it expected its profit for the full year to match that of 1992. For the first half, Schering said said net income totaled 185 million German marks ($108.2 million), up from DM179 million.
