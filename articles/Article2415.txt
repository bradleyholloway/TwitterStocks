 Credit Ratings: Debt of Inland Steel, Weirton Downgraded By Standard & Poor's ---- A Wall Street Journal News Roundup   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 20, 1992  The rating agency lowered its rating on Inland Steel Industries' preferred stock to single-B-minus from single-B-plus, and it dropped its ratings on the company's Inland Steel unit's senior secured debt to double-B-minus from double-B and on senior unsecured debt to single-B from double-B-minus. About $760 million of consolidated debt is outstanding.
