 Retail sales advanced 0.6% last month   By Duff, Christina, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1997  Consumers snapped up merchandise when it went on sale in Jan 1997 after holding back at Christmastime, the Commerce Department said. The government's measure of retail sales showed a respectable 0.6% gain in January following a meager 0.3% rise in Dec 1996.
