 OPEC Seeking Peace After Friction   By Faucon, Benoit; Said, Summer, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 2011  Members know the "organization . . . cannot cope with another disastrous meeting," said a person familiar with OPEC members' thinking. Since June, leaders of the 51-year-old group have been quietly trying to mend fences, promising the Dec. 14 gathering would go smoothly and result in an accord, most likely to maintain current output.
