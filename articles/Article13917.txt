 Corporate News: Corporate Watch   By Anonymous, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 2011  Norihiko Shirouzu --- ALCATEL Talks to Sell a Division To Permira Have Lapsed Alcatel-Lucent's exclusive talks to sell its corporate-phone business to private-equity firm Permira have lapsed, according to people familiar with the matter, in a setback for a deal that could fetch $1.5 billion or more for the Franco-American communications-gear maker. Since the spring, Alcatel-Lucent has been seeking a buyer for the so-called enterprise business.
