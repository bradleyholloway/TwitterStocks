Raises Dividend 40% In 2nd Increase for Auto Maker This Year   By By Melinda Grenier Guiles, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 1986  HIGHLAND PARK, Mich. -- Chrysler Corp. raised its common stock dividend 40%, citing confidence in its continued profitability and optimism about its businesses.  Chrysler's action marks the second time this year the auto maker has boosted its quarterly payout. In February, Chrysler raised its dividend 50% when it declared a 3-for-2 common-stock split.
