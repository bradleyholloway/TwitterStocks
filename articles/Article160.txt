Henry C. Bohack Jr., retired president of the H. C. Bohack Realty Corporation and vice president of the former Bohack food chain, died May 23 at a nursing home in Delray Beach, Fla., after an illness of several months. Mr. Bohack, who lived in Boynton Beach, Fla., and Woodhaven, Queens, was 85 years old and had retired two years ago.  He was born in Germany and came to the United States as a youth.  He was the nephew of Henry C. Bohack, who came to the United States when he was 17 years old and founded Henry C. Bohack Inc., which operated food stores and service stations.  He is survived by his wife, Alma; two daughters, Carol Gehrhardt of Glen Cove, L.I., and Norma Bohack of Woodhaven, and three granddaughters.
