 Unilever PLC reports earnings rose 6.1% for second quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 1995  Unilever PLC, the Anglo-Dutch consumer products concern, posted a 6.1% increase in pretax profit for the second quarter, on a 5% rise in sales. Pretax profit for the quarter rose to 646 million British pounds ($1.03 billion), compared with BP609 million a year earlier, at constant exchange rates. For the first half, pretax profit rose 5% to BP1.1 billion.
