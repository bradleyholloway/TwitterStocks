 Business Brief -- NEW PLAN REALTY TRUST: Net Income Increases 11%; Acquisitions, Leasing Cited   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 11, 1998  Retail and residential REIT New Plan Realty Trust reported strong results for its fiscal fourth quarter ended Jul 31, 1998, with net income of $22.2 million, or 37 cents per diluted share, vs $19.8 million, or 33 cents a diluted share, a year earlier.
