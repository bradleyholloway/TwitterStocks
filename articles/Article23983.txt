 Checks & Balances: Why Friends Help Strengthen a Marriage   By Rosman, Katherine, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 2011  Though I have always put time into staying connected to old friends, their temporary absence from my life hadn't jarred me. When a friend says to me, "I saw Joe and your daughter at the park and she has him wrapped around her finger," my focus is drawn past dirty socks left on the floor and onto the fact that I married a terrific guy who is loved by many.
