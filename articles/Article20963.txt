 Credit Ratings   The Wall Street Journal,  May 2, 1986  ASSOCIATES CORP. OF NORTH AMERICA -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. raised the rating on senior debt of this Dallas-based financial services company to double-A-3 from single-A-1 and on its subordinated notes and debentures to single-A-2 from single-A-3. About $4.8 billion of debt is affected. Moody's cited the "above-average earnings performance" of the company, a unit of Gulf & Western Industries Inc., New York.
