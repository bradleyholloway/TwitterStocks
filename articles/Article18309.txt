 Orthomet Inc. Says It Ousted Amdahl from 2 High Posts   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 1992  Orthomet Inc ousted its president and chief operating officer, B. J. Amdahl, following its announcement that the company will post a fiscal third-quarter loss. The company also requested resignations from vice presidents Ira Benson and Joel S. Norenberg, while senior vice president Douglas E. King resigned on his own.
