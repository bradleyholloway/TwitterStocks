 Simon's Venture Sells a Major Stake in HonFed Bank, Hawaii's Biggest Thrift   By Schmitt, Richard B, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 1990  Wealthy Hawaiian educational trust and landowner Kamehameha Schools/Bishop Estate agreed to invest in a major stake of William E. Simon's thrift venture HonFed Bank, Hawaii's largest thrift. Kamehameha will invest $50 million in HonFed which has assets of $3.2 billion.
