 Business Optimism Grows in Japan, Central Bank Finds   By By Peter Landers, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 2000  The so-called diffusion index for large manufacturers, the most closely watched number in the survey, rose to minus 9 from minus 17 in the previous quarter, the central bank said. The survey asks companies whether business conditions are good, bad or so-so; the diffusion index is the percentage saying "good" minus the percentage saying "bad," with the "so-so" responses discarded.
