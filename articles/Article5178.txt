 Big news? North Koreans may have different idea   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 1994  When North Korea's official media broadcast an alert on Nov 9, 1994 for listeners to stand by for an "important announcement," currency and stock markets in Tokyo and Seoul reacted in expectation of big news about Kim Jong Il. They were disappointed by the announcement of plans to finish a bridge and a tunnel in Pyongyang.
