 Goodyear Tire, Its Share Price Injured, Begins to Attract Some Value-Conscious Investors   By Dorfman, John R, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 1990  An analysis of Goodyear Tire & Rubber stock is given. Shares closed at slightly over 37 Mar 20, 1990, but were trading at more than 60 six months ago. The dividend yield is near 5%, compared to about 3% for the average stock.
