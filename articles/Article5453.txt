 Bank of Boston Corp.: Bank to close 65 branches in post-merger revamping   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 1997  Bank of Boston Corp said it will close 65 bank branches as part of its consolidation in the wake of its acquisition of BayBanks Inc in Jul 1996. It said the closings will generate about 20% of the $230 million in cost savings anticipated from the merger.
