 Banks in the U.K. are expected to see strong earnings, further stock gains   By Bray, Nicholas, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1993  Some analysts expect UK banks to be on a sharply upward trend with regards to profit for the next few years. After three years of recession, Barclays Bank PLC, TSB Group PLC, Abbey National PLC, National Westminster Bank PLC and Standard Chartered Bank are all expected to benefit from the economic upswing.
