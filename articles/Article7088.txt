 The Aggregator: Retailers Team Up Against Amazon.com   By Edited by Nikki Waller, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 2010  Under a cooperative program called ShopRunner, launched last week, retailers are dangling a $79-a-year loyalty program that offers unlimited two-day shipping across several online stores, including Babies "R" Us, Rockport, Wilson's Leather and RadioShack. With 8% of the U.S. e-commerce market, Amazon is the biggest single online retailer in the country, according to Hudson Square Research.
