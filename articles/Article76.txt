 Inco Recapitalization Is Facing Opposition By One Major Holder   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 25, 1988  The decision by Caisse de Depot et Placement du Quebec, one of Canada's largest institutional investors and a holder of about 4% of Inco's shares as of Dec. 31, comes as several institutions are considering a legal challenge to Inco's plan, which includes a $1.06 billion special dividend and a U.S.-style "poison pill."
