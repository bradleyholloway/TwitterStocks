 Health Care Brief -- BCE Emergis Inc.: MultiPlan to Pay $214.3 Million For Most Assets of U.S. Health   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 2004  BCE Emergis Inc. agreed to sell U.S. Health for 276 million Canadian dollars (US$214.3 million) to MultiPlan Inc., a health-care network based in New York. The sale, which will close next month, excludes the care-management operations and certain other assets.
