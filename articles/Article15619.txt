Posts Net of $186.4 Million, Surpassing Estimates   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 1997  OPERATING PROFIT at Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Inc. more than doubled to $120.3 million in the third quarter, and operating profit at Alliance Capital Management L.P. rose 35% to $66.2 million. An article yesterday misstated the figures as $83.7 million and $19.2 million, respectively. Those figures are the contributions that Donaldson Lufkin and Alliance Capital made to the operating profit of parent company Equitable Cos. (WSJ Nov. 6, 1997)  NEW YORK -- Equitable Cos. reported third-quarter net income surged 78%, easily surpassing expectations, as it continued to chalk up across-the-board gains in its insurance, investment-banking and asset-management businesses.
