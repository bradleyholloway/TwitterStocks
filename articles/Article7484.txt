 Holding in Aloha Inc. Is Increased to 15.7%   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 1986  An investment group said it raised its stake in Aloha Inc. to 15.7% from 12.9%.  In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, the group said that it holds 312,600 Aloha common shares, including 55,500 shares purchased Jan. 30 and Jan. 31 at $20 to $20.50 each.
