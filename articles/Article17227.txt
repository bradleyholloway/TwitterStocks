 Corporate News: Office-Space Occupancy on the Rise   By Anonymous, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 2011  Average effective rents, which include such benefits as free rent and interior work, rose by 0.5% to $22.20 a square foot, according to property-research firm Reis Inc. This marked the second consecutive quarter of rent increases and more evidence the industry has finally turned the corner after more than two years of dramatic rent and occupancy drops.
