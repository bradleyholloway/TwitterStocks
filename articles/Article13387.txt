 Treasury Sets New Offerings   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 2007  -- On Thursday, the Treasury will sell $6 billion of inflation- indexed 10-year notes, dated Jan. 15, 2007, and maturing Jan. 15, 2017. Noncompetitive tenders for the notes, available in minimum denominations of $1,000, must be received by noon Thursday, and competitive tenders by 1 p.m. The Cusip number is 912828GD6.
