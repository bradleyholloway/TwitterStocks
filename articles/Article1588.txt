 Perelman Is Said to Be Making Plans for Public Offer of 15%-18% of Revlon   By Smith, Randall; Deveny, Kathleen, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 13, 1992  Several Wall Street securities firms are competing to lead a planned public offering of 15% to 18% of Revlon Inc, an issue which could total about $400 million to $600 million. Sources close to Revlon said owner Ronald Perelman was likely to make the offer while the stock market was performing well.
