 Aspin Quickly Reverses Marines' Marriage Policy   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1993  In a statement circulated yesterday, the Marine Corps said that starting late in 1995, it would no longer accept married recruits. A few hours later, the order was overruled by Defense Secretary Aspin, who, according to a statement, "directed that any new policy proposals on this matter be subjected to a full policy review."
