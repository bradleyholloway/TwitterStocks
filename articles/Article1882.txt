 Automobiles: Chrysler bets on boom in minivan sales   By Suris, Oscar, The Wall Street Journal,  May 13, 1994  With Chrysler Corp headed for what looks like another record year in minivan sales, some industry observers are concerned that there will not be many minivan customers left when the next generation of vans roll out in 1995. Chrysler, however, is not worried, and is confident of sales that will continue to grow from new baby boomers entering the market.
