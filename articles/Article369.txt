 Voters for `American Idol' Make Winners of AT&T Wireless Calls   By By Kopin Tan, The Wall Street Journal,  May 23, 2003  The day's largest index-option trade occurred in the Standard & Poor's 500-stock index, where an investor sold nearly 30,000 contracts of July 750 puts while buying about 15,000 contracts of the September 750 puts.
