 Carrington Laboratories Inc.: Wider loss, lower sales are expected for 1st period   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1996  Dallas-based Carrington Laboratories Inc said it expects to report reduced first-quarter revenue and a wider loss, and said it is in negotiations with a lender over its violation of a loan covenant. Carrington reported a loss of $497,000 in the first quarter of 1995.
