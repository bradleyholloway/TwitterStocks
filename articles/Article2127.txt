 Forbes and rival Fortune duke it out after expose on Steve Forbes's ethics   By Reilly, Patrick M, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1996  Forbes and Fortune, the nation's two bimonthly business magazines, have slugged it out for years, but Fortune published a blistering attack during the week of Jan 15, 1996 on Steve Forbes, the editor in chief of the family-owned Forbes magazine and a current candidate for the GOP presidential nomination. In the Feb 5 issue, Fortune accused Forbes and his magazine of breaching "journalistic ethics" by softening stories about its prominent advertisers. Forbes retorted, calling Fortune an "also-ran" reduced to publishing in desperation "scurrilous, partisan cheap shots."
