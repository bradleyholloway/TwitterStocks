 Stocks Plunge in Tokyo on Coming Expiration of Futures Contract, and Their Decline Spreads   By Hardy, Quentin, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1991  Tokyo stocks declined on Dec 11, 1991 as the Nikkei 225-stock index fell 450.16 points to close at 21502.90 under the effect of arbitrage-related selling. In London, shares were pulled down partly because of Wall Street's slump. The Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-share index lost 11.8 points to end at 2380.2, ending two points above the intraday low. In Frankfurt, stock prices fell in a continuation of general listlessness. The DAX 30-stock index ended off 7.52 at 1543.59.
