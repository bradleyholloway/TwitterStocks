 WEEKEND JOURNAL; Taste: Tony & Tacky   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 2004  What's Up Doc? Let's just say that the California Highway Patrol is not amused. Citing a law written, as the Los Angeles Times puts it, in the days when doctors had to "race past streetcars and Packards" to make house calls, the California Medical Association advised its members that they would be allowed to speed and cut through car-pool lanes so long as they had big red "M.D." stickers on their cars.
