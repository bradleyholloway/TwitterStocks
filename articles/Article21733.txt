 Trustees Lose Effort for RICO Charges against Hunt Friends   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 2, 1992  Court-appointed trustees liquidating the estates of Nelson Bunker Hunt and William Herbert Hunt lost an effort to pursue RICO charges against various Hunt friends and relatives as part of a lawsuit seeking the return of assets they claim were fraudulently transferred out of the Hunt brothers' estates. Bankruptcy Court Judge Harold Abramson said the trustees lacked standing to pursue violations of RICO.
