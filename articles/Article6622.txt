Johnson & Son To Buy Bayer Unit For $734.6 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 2002  Under the agreement, the consumer-products company will purchase Bayer's household-insect-control-and-repellent business and other household specialty brands, which had combined sales in 2001 of 400 million euros. Bayer will retain the active ingredients used in the insecticides and supply them to S.C. Johnson on a nonexclusive basis.
