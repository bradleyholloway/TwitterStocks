 Supermarkets General Planning to Buy Back Bonds   By Freedman, Alix M; Anders, George, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1990  Supermarkets General Holdings Corp, which operates Pathmark and Purity Supreme supermarkets as well as Rickel Home Centers, is proposing to buy back some of its junk bonds at 27 cents on the dollar in an effort to shrink its heavy debt load.
