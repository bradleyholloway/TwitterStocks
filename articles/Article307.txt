 Gates Predicts Internet Is Ready For Its Next Stage   By By Paula L. Stepankowsky, The Wall Street Journal,  May 25, 2000  REDMOND, Wash. -- The Internet's next stage of development will shift online use from one-way portals and two-way business communications to an interactive world where digital meetings, paperless back-office operations and instant communications on multiple devices hold sway, Microsoft Corp. Chairman Bill Gates said.  Mr. Gates made the prediction in his keynote speech opening a two-day summit of more than 160 chief executives at Microsoft's fourth annual CEO summit.
