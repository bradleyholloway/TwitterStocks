Correction Appended Honoring an individual for some notable accomplishment usually entails the presentation of a plaque or paperweight and some laudatory speeches. So STEPHEN SCHWARTZ, an Academy Award-winning composer and lyricist, should consider himself particularly fortunate about the way he is being celebrated on Sunday at the Lambs Club on West 44th Street.  The Directors Company, an organization that encourages the development of theatrical directors, is putting on a show of highlights of Mr. Schwartz's career, including songs from "Godspell," "Pippin," "Rags" and the Walt Disney movie "Pocahontas."
