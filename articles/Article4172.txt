 Thrifty Sells Stakes In Crown Books Corp. And Trak Auto West   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1986  Crown, a national bookstore chain and a unit of Dart Group, said it purchased the 34.4% stake for $37 million, or $13.45 a share. Dart Group, a Landover, Md.-based retailer, now owns 52.4%, or 2.7 million shares, of Crown. Trak Auto Corp., 66%-owned by Dart Group, now wholly owns Trak Auto West. Neither Trak Auto nor Thrifty would disclose the price of the Trak Auto West stake.
