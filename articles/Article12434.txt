 Letters to the Editor: We Will Not Accept `Profiling' Injustice   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 1999  In letters to the editor, Gregory L. Kernisan and Shirley Buckhanon-Mouton deride a Mar 31, 1999 letter from Ralph Brown in which police harassment for driving sports cars or pickup trucks was equated with the harassment minorities must deal with on a daily basis.
