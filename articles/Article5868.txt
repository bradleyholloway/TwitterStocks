 Letters to the Editor: Patents Not Competitive   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 2000  In hindsight, it is not clear these changes helped anyone but the lawyers. Robert Hunt of the Federal Reserve Bank of Philadelphia has documented the questionable effects of the change in non-obviousness standards. Also, economist Eric Maskin (Institute for Advanced Study, Princeton) and I have shown that software patents have not increased innovativeness -- the firms that obtained the most software patents actually reduced R&D spending relative to sales.
