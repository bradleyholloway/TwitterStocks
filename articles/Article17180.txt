 Corporate News: News Corp.'s Bid for BSkyB Examined   By Cimilluca, Dana; Sonne, Paul, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 2010  The U.K. government waded into News Corp.'s bid to buy the remaining 60.9% of satellite-television company British Sky Broadcasting Group PLC, initiating a review of the proposed acquisition that highlights the obstacles the media giant faces in its quest to close the deal, currently valued at GBP 7.8 billion ($12.6 billion).
