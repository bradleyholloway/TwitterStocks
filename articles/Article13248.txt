 Disney Gets Investors for On-Demand Unit; Disney Gets Investors for On-Demand Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 2006  Disney executive Salil Mehta, who is executive vice president of ESPN Enterprises, will sit on the new board of Moviebeam. He will be joined by Norwest partner Matthew Howard, Mayfield managing partner Kevin Fong and Alexander Izzard, a former Disney employee who was involved in the original launch of Moviebeam, according to the SEC filing. Mr. Izzard will be chief executive.
