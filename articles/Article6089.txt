 Japan's Economic-Stimulus Package And Tax-System Review Move Forward   By By Norihiko Shirouzu, The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1998  Prime Minister Ryutaro Hashimoto's cabinet yesterday approved bills needed to carry out Japan's much-awaited economic-stimulus package, and the first funds from the package may reach the Japanese economy as early as June.  In addition, some leaders of Mr. Hashimoto's party, the Liberal Democratic Party, say they are willing to consider an overhaul of Japan's tax system that may include permanent reductions in income and corporate taxes.
