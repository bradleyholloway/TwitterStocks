 Dole's solid primary victory over Buchanan in South Carolina gives new order to GOP race   By Harwood, John; Stout, Hilary, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1996  Senate Majority Leader Robert Dole has reclaimed his role as Republican front-runner with his crushing victory over Pat Buchanan in South Carolina's primary. On Mar 3, 1996, the other candidates seemed to acknowledge Dole's elevated status in a relatively tranquil debate in Atlanta.
