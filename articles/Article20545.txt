 U.S. Watch   By Anonymous, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 2010  The Arizona program provides individuals a 100% tax credit of up to $500 for payments to state-authorized "school tuition organizations" that, in turn, award scholarships to private schools they specify. Because some tuition organizations exclusively fund scholarships to religious schools that admit only followers of their faith, several Arizona taxpayers contend the program violates the First Amendment, which prohibits a government "establishment of religion."
