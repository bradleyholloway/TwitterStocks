 Dravo to Post Charge Totaling $20 Million Pre-Tax for Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 1987  Dravo Corp. said it will take a $20 million pre-tax charge against its second-quarter results for losses on two waste-to-energy power plant contracts.  A spokesman for the engineering, construction and raw materials concern said the charge probably will result in a loss for the period. Dravo expects to report its second-quarter results late this month. The company said it hadn't calculated the after-tax amount of the charge yet.
