 Fujitsu Delays Building U.K. Computer-Chip Plant   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1996  Fujitsu Ltd., Japan's largest computer maker, said it will delay construction of its computer-chip facility in the United Kingdom.  According to a Fujitsu spokeswoman, the company will postpone the plant's construction to the end of 1996 from the previously projected April. As a result, it won't start operating until 1998.
