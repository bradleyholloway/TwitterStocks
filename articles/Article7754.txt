 International Corporate Report [1]  The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1988  LA FONDIARIA S.P.A., Italy's fourth-largest insurance company, said consolidated group profit was unchanged in 1987 from the previous year, at 95 billion lire ($76 million). La Fondiaria is 49.9% controlled by INIZIATIVA ME.T.A., the services holding company in the Ferruzzi-Montedison group.
