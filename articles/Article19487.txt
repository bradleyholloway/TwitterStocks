 Morgan Stanley Unit In Japan Is Accused Of Fraud and Penalized   By By Jason Singer, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 2002  This time, two Morgan Stanley traders were found to have intentionally manipulated the stock market on Dec. 4, said Atsushi Mizuno, an FSA official. "It's clear that the staff of Morgan Stanley wanted to get around the rules," he said.  The Morgan Stanley case is the latest in a recent series of embarrassing and costly flubs made by large U.S. and European brokerage firms in Japan. Regulators have been making an increasingly concerted effort to police the industry.
