 White House Counsel Jack Quinn to quit, fourth to leave the job under Clinton   By Simpson, Glenn R; Frisby, Michael K, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1996  White House Counsel Jack Quinn is planning to return to private practice in the next three months, marking the fourth person to depart from that highly charged post during the Clinton administration. Quinn's unexpected departure could cause problems for the White House as Congress makes plans to launch major investigations into possibly improper ties between President Clinton and major campaign contributors.
