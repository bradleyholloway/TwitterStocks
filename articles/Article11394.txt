 Business Brief -- Nevada Power Co.: Utility Reports Profit Drop Of 49.6% in Second Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 6, 1990  For the quarter, Las Vegas-based Nevada Power posted net income of $6.5 million, or 20 cents a share, compared with year-ago profit of $13 million, or 46 cents a share. There were 28.6 million shares outstanding in the recent quarter compared with 26.7 million in the year-ago period. Second-quarter revenues climbed 10% to $112.1 million from $101.8 million.
