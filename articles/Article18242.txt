 Intellicall Settles Suits, Agreeing in One Case to Sell Pay Phones   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 1991  Intellicall Inc settled three lawsuits and sold its pay telephones in transactions that will result in gains in the third quarter and in future periods. One suit was with Alpha Pay Phones Ltd and AT&T Co, one with Providence Lloyds and Western Alliance and the third with St Paul Insurance Co.
