 Mitsubishi Bank Ltd. Weighs Selling Shares in Bank of California   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 1990  Mitsubishi Bank Ltd., hungry for fresh capital, is thinking of selling stock in its Bank of California subsidiary.  The giant Japanese bank -- the world's fourth biggest, with $357 billion in assets -- is considering a private placement of new shares in its wholly owned subsidiary as a way of meeting stringent international capital-adequacy requirements, according to Kyota Ohmori, general manager of Mitsubishi's planning department.
