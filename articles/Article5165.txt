 Hollinger earnings hurt by price war during 3rd period   By De Santis, Solange, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 1994  Although a reduction in the price of the British newspaper Daily Telegraph boosted circulation by 10%, the British newspaper price war took its toll on the earnings of Hollinger Inc, the Daily Telegraph's majority owner. The company said that third-quarter operating profit was lower than the 25.2 million Canadian dollars ($18.7 million) earned in 1993. The company will release results on Nov 17, 1994.
