 Renewed Signs of Life Are Beginning to Surface in the Market for New Issues of Mexican Stock   By Moffett, Matt, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 1992  On Dec 11, 1992 stocks fell in Tokyo, London and Frankfurt. The Nikkei 225-stock index fell 60.28 points to 17441.02, and in London, the Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-share index fell 10.3 points to 2716.2. In Frankfurt, the DAX 30-stock index fell 18.47 points to 1476.01. After a six-month hiatus, interest appeared to be on the rise for new issues of Mexican stock. On Dec 11, Grupo Embotellador de Mexico launched a successful $173 million global offering.
