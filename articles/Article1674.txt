 A Decade-Old Grief, Suddenly Fresh --- The Sept. 11 Anniversary Is More Evident Around the City Than in Recent Years   By Hollander, Sophia, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 2011  The stricken faces of family members on television as they recalled a decade of life events missed by their loved ones -- births, marriages, new grandchildren -- summoned the vast scale of loss with a fresh grief. At Lincoln Center, models prepared for morning fashion shows, while outside in the plaza, men in tuxedos and women in black dresses gathered for a memorial concert that afternoon at Avery Fisher Hall.
