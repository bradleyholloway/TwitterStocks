 CBOE Plans to List Options Contracts Based on Nasdaq   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1993  The Chicago Board Options Exchange said it plans to list options contracts based on an index of 100 stocks traded on the fast-growing Nasdaq over-the-counter market.  "Retail investors view the Nasdaq 100 as a proxy for some of the most highly traded issues on the Nasdaq Stock Market," said Joseph R. Hardiman, Nasdaq's president. "Nasdaq 100 options will give investors a new tool for market timing and hedging in Nasdaq stocks."
