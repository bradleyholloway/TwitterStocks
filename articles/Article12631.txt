 British Telecom Sells Record $10 Billion In Bond Offering   By By Gautam Naik, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 2000  With its debt level soaring in the past year, BT had to offer relatively high interest rates to make the huge offering more palatable to investors. BT's debts are expected to soar to about $30 billion by March, reflecting heavy spending on new mobile-phone licenses and overseas investments. That is a major reversal for Britain's largest telecom provider: Two years ago, BT carried no debt on its books, and this past summer it had the best credit rating of all European telecom companies.
