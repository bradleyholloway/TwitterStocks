 Mao's Widow Killed Herself, China Reports   By McGregor, James, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 1991  The Chinese government reported that Jiang Qing, the widow of Mao Tse-tung and one-time persecutor of China's current rulers, committed suicide on May 14, 1991 in her Beijing residence. The three-week delay in making Qing's death public points out the fragility of China's political situation.
