 Singapore's new telecom licenses hold promise, but not for investors seeking quick gratification   By Mark, Jeremy, The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1995  Although the awarding of licenses for cellular-phone and paging services in Singapore creates new opportunities, investors will not likely see returns until as late as 1999. On May 11, 1995, the Tokyo Nikkei 225-share index was down 364.76 to 16461.73. The London Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-share index was up 27.8 to 3317.9. The Frankfurt DAX 30-share index was up 19.02 to 2078.08.
