 Financial Services Brief -- Travelers Property Casualty Corp.: Reserves for Asbestos Claims Are Increased to $3.4 Billion   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 2003  Travelers Property Casualty Corp. increased its asbestos reserves to $3.4 billion amid concern that the industry's reserves for asbestos claims are too low. The move will result in a fourth-quarter charge of $1.3 billion, Travelers said.
