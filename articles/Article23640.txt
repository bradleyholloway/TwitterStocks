 Think You're A Trader? IRS May Disagree   By Herman, Tom, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 2008  (There has been talk for many years of raising the annual net capital loss limits, but Congress hasn't taken action.) If you qualify as a trader and use the mark-to-market method, you aren't bound by those annual net-loss limits, or by certain limits that apply to itemized deductions. The top capital gains rate on long-term gains from securities sales is 15%, while the top federal income-tax rate on ordinary income is 35%.
