 Legal beat: Big Philip Morris cigarette recall may aid plaintiffs' liability cases   By Felsenthal, Edward; Geyelin, Milo, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 1995  Plaintiffs attorneys say the massive cigarette recall by Philip Morris Cos adds an unpredictable ingredient to the industry's courtroom battles over tobacco liability. The main setback for the industry, according to these lawyers, is that Philip Morris has for the first time articulated a limit beyond which even it concedes cigarettes may be unsafe for smokers.
