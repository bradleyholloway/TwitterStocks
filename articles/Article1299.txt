 Business Brief -- National Savings Bank of Albany: New York Company Agrees To Sale to Home & City   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1990  National Savings Bank of Albany agreed to be acquired by Home & City Savings Bank, also of Albany, in a transaction valued at about $48 million.  Russell Melton, National Savings treasurer, said Home & City Savings Bank approached National Savings late last week with a $40-a-share offer for its 1.16 million shares. National Savings directors approved the offer yesterday.
