 New Securities Issues [1]  The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 1993  Levitz Furniture Inc. -- $100 million of senior subordinated notes due July 15, 2003, priced at par to yield 9.525%, according to MCM CorporateWatch. The issue is noncallable for five years. Rated single-B-2 by Moody's Investors Service Inc. and single-B-minus by Standard & Poor's Corp., the issue will be sold through underwriters led by Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp.  Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp. -- $200 million of realestate mortgage-investment conduit securities offered by PaineWebber Inc. The offering, Series 1566, is backed by the agency's seven-year, 6% balloon mortgage securities. The collateral has a weighted average coupon rate of 6.6% and weighted average remaining term to maturity of 6.8 years.  Altana Finance BV Netherlands (German parent) -- 100 million marks of 7.125% Eurobonds due Aug. 3, 2000, at issue price 101.5 via Dresdner Bank AG. Bonds yield 7.24% (annual), a spread of about 55 basis points above comparable German Bund. Guarantor Altana AG. Fees 2.75 for lead managers, 2.125 for co-managers.
