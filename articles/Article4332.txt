 Next Orange Crop Is Seen Climbing 1% In Forecast by U.S.   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 9, 1987  The forecast for Florida's orange crop, at 130 million boxes, was 9% higher than the last harvest, while a 16% drop was forecast for California's crop, to 49 million boxes. The yield of Florida frozen concentrated orange juice in the coming season, beginning Dec. 1, was forecast at 1.46 gallons a box. A box weighs 90 pounds.
