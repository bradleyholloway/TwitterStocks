 SEC names Lindsey chief economist as it seeks help on mutual-funds issue   By Taylor, Jeffrey; Calian, Sara, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 1995  Yale University Professor Richard Lindsey will become the SEC's new chief economist, succeeding Susan Woodward in Jul 1995. Lindsey's first assignment will be to help resolve the hotly debated question of how mutual funds should disclose their risks to investors.
