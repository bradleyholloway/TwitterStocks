 United Airlines to Offer a Freebie After Three Trips   By By Ron Lieber and Audrey Warren, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 2003  The offer comes with an important catch for people flying in coach: Only flights that don't involve a Saturday-night stay will count toward the free ticket. (There's no such requirement for first- or business- class tickets.) This condition is designed to reward business travelers who might otherwise fly another airline for pricey trips. Since United's parent, UAL Corp., is operating under bankruptcy-court protection, some travelers are skittish about booking.
