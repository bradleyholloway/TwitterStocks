 Schroder's Troop Plan Faces Friendly Fire   By By William Boston, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 2001  Germany's main opposition parties, however, see the vote as a test of the coalition. The conservative Christian Democrats and their sister party, the Bavarian Christian Social Union, have warned for years that a center-left government of the SPD and Greens would be captive to the pacifist minority in their parties, making it difficult for Germany to live up to its international responsibilities. The CDU and the CSU called on Mr. [Gerhard Schroder] to resign if his coalition fails to back the troop deployment.
