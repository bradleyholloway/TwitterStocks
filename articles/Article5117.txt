 Plutonium facility in Japan is probed for a discrepancy   By Fialka, John J, The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 1994  International Atomic Energy Agency inspectors are investigating a discrepancy at a plutonium facility in Tokaimura Japan that may have left as much as 154 pounds of the explosive nuclear material unaccounted for. The missing material could be enough to make as many as nine small nuclear weapons.
