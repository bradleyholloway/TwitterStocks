 In Brief   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 2007  Kohlberg Kravis Roberts & Co. started a takeover bid for MMI Holdings Ltd., valuing the Singapore hard-disk-drive parts maker at 1 billion Singapore dollars ($661 million). At S$1.65 a share, the offer represents a 16% premium to MMI's closing price of S$1.42 on Feb. 8, a day before the company said it was in acquisition talks with a potential buyer. MMI, whose shares are halted from trading on the Singapore Exchange, last closed at S$1.66. MMI counts Seagate Technology among its clients.
