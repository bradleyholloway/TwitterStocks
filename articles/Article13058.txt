 Journal Register Cements Deal For 21st Century Newspapers   By Berman, Dennis K, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 2004  With 21st Century, Journal Register acquires a company that has a presence in 70% of all Michigan counties, and a strong hold on the suburbs of Detroit. Its properties consist of small papers such as the Macomb Daily and the Carson City Reminder.  J.P. Morgan, Goldman Sachs and Dirks, Van Essen & Murray advised 21st Century on the deal. Journal Register was advised by Bear Stearns & Co.
