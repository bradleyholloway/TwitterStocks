 Boy Scouts Faithful to Beliefs and Values   By Carmack, Nat, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1992  Nat Carmack responds to the Sep 16, 1992 letter to the editor from Paul A. Holmes, saying Holmes is mistaken in saying the Boy Scouts discriminate against homosexuals. Carmack says atheists and homosexuals are free to practice their life styles as they choose, but not as members of the Boy Scouts.
