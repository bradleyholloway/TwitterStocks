 Accounting: Legal-Liability Awards Are Frightening Smaller CPA Firms Away from Audits   By Berton, Lee, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1992  Many smaller certified public accounting firms are being scared away from doing audits and are cutting down on client services because of a flood of legal-liability awards, making it harder and more expensive for smaller businesses to get their audit work done. Several recent liability rulings against accounting firms are discussed.
