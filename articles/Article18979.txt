 Business Brief -- FIRSTMERIT CORP.: Purchase of CoBancorp Inc. For Stock, Cash Is Planned   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1997  FirstMerit Corp agreed to acquire CoBancorp Inc in a stock and cash transaction valued at $153.7 million. Under terms of the definitive agreement, CoBancorp holders will receive $44.50 a share in either stock or cash.
