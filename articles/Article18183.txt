 WIC Agrees to Buy Ontario TV Station From Maclean Hunter   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1991  VANCOUVER, British Columbia -- WIC Western International Communications said it agreed to acquire an Ontario television station from Maclean Hunter Ltd. for 48 million Canadian dollars (US$41.6 million).  Maclean Hunter, a Toronto-based publishing, printing and broadcasting concern, acquired the station, CHCH-TV, as part of its C$600 million acquisition of Selkirk Communications Ltd.'s broadcasting assets in 1988.
