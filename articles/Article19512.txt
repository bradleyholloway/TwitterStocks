 Service-Sector Growth Slowed During March Amid Price Increases   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 2002  Prices paid for services climbed in March, with a reading of 53 compared with 50 in February, according to the ISM, formerly known as the National Association of Purchasing Management. ISM called the gain "worrisome" because it represented the first indication of price increases in 10 months.
