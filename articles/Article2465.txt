 Options Activity in Intel Corp. Is Enormous As Chip Maker Prepares to Release Earnings   By By Steven M. Sears, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 1998  The anticipation surrounding Intel translated into enormous activity in the company's July 75 calls. A First Call consensus estimate had Intel earning 68 cents a share in the second quarter, compared with a stock-split adjusted 92 cents a share a year earlier. The whisper number for the quarter is 70 cents a share.
