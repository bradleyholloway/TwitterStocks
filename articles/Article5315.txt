 Inspectors Name Firms Whose Gear Was at Iraqi Sites   By Fialka, John J, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1991  Inspectors from the International Atomic Energy Agency's team in Iraq released a partial list of firms that contributed equipment found in facilities connected to Saddam Hussein's nuclear weapons program. The list included Du Pont Co, Hamamatsu Photonics KK and a unit of Leybold AG.
