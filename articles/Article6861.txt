 Input Is Sought on 'Fairness' Rule   By Davis, Ann, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 2004  "This is really about making sure investors understand the subjective nature of some fairness opinions," NASD Vice Chairman Mary L. Schapiro said. She added that "investors may not understand the extent to which the fairness opinion is reliant upon information that was provided by management. I worry that investors think that there is a great deal more verification of information than may be the case."
