 Delcath Takes On Tumors in Liver   By Cummings, Jennifer, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 2010  The company's Percutaneous Hepatic Perfusion system mechanically isolates the liver from the rest of the circulatory system, allowing doctors to deliver 10 times the normal dose of chemotherapy to the liver while blocking the toxic levels of the drug from damaging the rest of the body.
