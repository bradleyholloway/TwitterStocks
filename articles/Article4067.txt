 Donald Weeden Planning To Head Own Brokerage   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 9, 1985  Mr. Weeden said that his Weeden & Co., which has been a unit of Moseley, Hallgarten, Estabrook & Weeden since 1979, will become independent in January with total capital of about $10 million. The firm, which plans to become a Big Board member, will continue to specialize in trading blocks of stock, and intends to expand into trading other securities as well as venture capital, he said.
