 Healtheon/WebMD To Invest Cash, Stock In VitalWorks.com   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 2000  Healtheon/WebMD will pay $10 million in cash immediately for convertible preferred stock in VitalWorks.com, a stake representing 1% of VitalWorks.com shares outstanding following the completion of a planned initial public offering. Healtheon/WebMD agreed to invest an additional $90 million, $40 million in cash and $50 million in stock, upon completion of the IPO. Healtheon/WebMD provides information and services to doctors and consumers on the Internet.
