 Food Brief -- Kroger Co.: Fourth-Quarter Income Rises Despite Soft Sales Growth   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 2003  Kroger Co.'s fiscal fourth-quarter net income rose 8.7%, despite soft sales growth. For the quarter ended Feb. 1, the Cincinnati-based grocery-store operator reported net income of $381 million, or 50 cents a share, up from $350.4 million, or 43 cents a share, a year earlier.
