 Odd's-n-End's Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 11, 1994  Odd's-n-End's Inc said it will file a reorganization plan in Nov 1994 that will enable it to emerge from the Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection it filed for in May 1994. Under the plan, Universal International Inc will invest $1 million of new equity for 45% of the shares of the reorganized company.
