 CFTC Extends Period For Public Comment On OTC Derivatives   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 1998  WASHINGTON, D.C. -- The Commodity Futures Trading Commission extended to Oct. 13 the period allotted for public comment on its controversial "concept release" on over-the-counter derivatives.  The original deadline for comments was July 13, but was extended in June to last Friday. The release raised questions about whether the CFTC should regulate the burgeoning OTC derivatives industry and prompted the ire of prominent financial regulators, including Alan Greenspan.
