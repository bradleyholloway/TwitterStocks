 Canadian Economy Grew at Rate of 4.2% In 1999 First Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 11, 1999  Canada's economy recorded a second consecutive quarter of strong growth, signaling that the country is finally beginning to catch up with the robust growth rate of the U.S., its largest trading partner.  Statistics Canada said the country's gross domestic product grew at an annual rate of 4.2% in the first quarter this year, following a revised 4.8% growth pace in the final quarter of 1998. The fourth-quarter figure was previously reported as 4.6%.
