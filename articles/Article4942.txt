 Westinghouse Electric Corp.: Credit Unit Settles Lawsuit with Real Estate Developers   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 1992  Westinghouse Electric Corp's credit unit reached a non-cash settlement with real estate developers Robert and Edmond Tonti. The Tontis had alleged that Westinghouse used them as intermediaries to conceal losses resulting from speculative real estate deals and violated their partnership arrangement. Westinghouse has agreed to restructure the loans.
