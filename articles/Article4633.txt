 Bombardier Inc. Agrees to Buy Assets, Operations of Learjet for $75 Million   By Goad, G Pierre, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 1990  Bombardier Inc has agreed to acquire Learjet Corp's assets and operations for $75 million, a move that will increase the Canadian company's share of the world market for corporate jets. Learjet's parent, Integrated Resources Inc, is under Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection.
