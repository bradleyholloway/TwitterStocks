 Hunt Brothers Call Off Session With Bank Lenders   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 11, 1987  As previously reported, the Hunt brothers, through their First Boston Corp. investment advisers, had planned to meet here with representatives of their 23 U.S. and foreign bank lenders. The session was called to lay out a plan for the Hunts to sell major parts of their two leading energy companies to raise cash to pay off the banks, and end a legal battle that has been running for more than a year.
