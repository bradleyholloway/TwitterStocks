 Saab's U.S. sales rise   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 1994  Sweden's Saab Automobile AB reported sales of Saab cars in its key US market rose 11% to 2,335 units in Mar 1994 from 2,107 units a year earlier. Saab Automobile is jointly owned by Swedish automotive and aerospace group Saab-Scania AB and General Motors Corp.
