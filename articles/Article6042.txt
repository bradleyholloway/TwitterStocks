 Magna Will Acquire Austrian Auto Firm Steyr-Daimler Puch   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 1998  Magna, based in Aurora, Ontario, said it agreed to acquire a 66.8% interest in Steyr-Daimler-Puch from Austria's Creditanstalt AG, as well as Creditanstalt's 50% interest in Steyr-Daimler-Puch Fahrzeugtechnik AG, which is 50%-owned by Steyr-Daimler Puch AG. Terms of the acquisitions weren't disclosed.
