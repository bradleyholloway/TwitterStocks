 Polaroid Corp. Says it Expects to Post 'Somewhat Lower' Sales for 1st Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 1992  Polaroid Corp said it expects its first-quarter sales to be "somewhat lower" than the $441.2 million posted a year earlier, but said it still expects operating profit for 1992 to match 1991's $246.6 million. Polaroid's stock fell $2.50 on the news, to $25.625, in Apr 2, 1992 trading.
