 Business Brief -- PacifiCare Health Systems Inc.: Staff to Be Reduced by 15%, Spurring $60 Million Charge   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 2002  PacifiCare Health Systems Inc. plans to reduce its work force by 1,300, or 15%, and take a $60 million pretax charge that will produce a loss for the just-ended fourth quarter.
