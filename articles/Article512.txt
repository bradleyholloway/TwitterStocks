19820524
Patricia Ann Biederman, daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Robert Biederman of Scarsdale, N.Y., was married there yesterday to William Jeffrey Robbins, son of Mr. and Mrs. Norbert Robbins of Merrick, L.I. Rabbi Jeremiah Wohlberg performed the ceremony at the Fenway Golf Club.  The bride graduated cum laude from the Tufts University-Boston School of Occupational Therapy and received a master's in the joint program of business administration and public health at Columbia University.  She plans to join the Catholic Medical Center of Brooklyn and Queens as associate director of marketing in July. Her father is president of Brucol Industries Inc. in New York, a textile corporation.  Mr. Robbins, an associate with the New York law firm of Strook & Strook & Lavan, graduated summa cum laude from Yale College, where he was elected to Phi Beta Kappa, and from the Harvard Law School. His father is director of trade research of Jospeh E. Seagram & Sons Inc.
