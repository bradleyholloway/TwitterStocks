 Economic data to be released again by U.S.   By Wilke, John R, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 1996  The Commerce Department said it will resume releasing economic indicators beginning the week of Jan 15, 1996 after the federal government shutdown and severe winter weather. Separately, the Federal Reserve reported that consumer credit rose at a seasonally adjusted annual rate of 10.4% for Nov 1995, more slowly that the 13.7% annual rate in October.
