 Industrial Brief -- Akzo Nobel NV: To Focus on Pharmaceuticals, Three Businesses Will Be Sold   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 2003  Akzo Nobel NV said it plans to sell three chemicals businesses in a move which could raise 1 billion euros ($1.08 billion), as the Dutch conglomerate pushes to focus on pharmaceuticals. Akzo said it will sell its catalysts, coating-resins and phosphorus-chemicals operations.
