 Dillon, Read & Co.: U.S. firm to buy back 50% of stake acquired by ING   The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 1995  Dillon, Read & Co is preparing to buy half of the stake that Internationale Nederlanden Groep recently acquired in the US investment bank as part of ING's purchase of failed Barings PLC. Dillon Read is expected to buy half of the 40% stake for about $30 million.
