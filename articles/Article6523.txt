 Moving the Market: Nasdaq to Buy Shareholder.com   By Lucchetti, Aaron, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 2006  The Nasdaq moves may help counter the NYSE's advantage with executives curious about their why their company's stock price is moving. Executives of some companies that have moved to NYSE from Nasdaq have complained that their stock sometimes moved on Nasdaq without much explanation. At Nasdaq, trading isn't controlled at one central place like at the NYSE, but is spread across a loose network of Wall Street dealers.
