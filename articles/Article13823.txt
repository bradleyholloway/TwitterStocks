 World News: Militants Bomb Anti-Taliban Tribes   By Hussain, Zahid, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 2010  ISLAMABAD, Pakistan -- A suicide bomber struck a packed mosque during Friday prayers in northwest Pakistan, killing at least 67 worshippers, and a grenade attack at another mosque in the region killed at least four, the latest in a series of attacks by militants on groups allied with the government against the Taliban.
