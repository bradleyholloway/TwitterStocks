 Dole abandons his effort to link rise in minimum wage, gasoline-tax cut   By Georges, Christopher, The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 1996  Senate Majority Leader Robert Dole abandoned his effort to link a minimum-wage increase with a reduction in the gasoline tax, and instead will try to force a simple up-or-down vote on the gasoline-tax issue during the week of May 13, 1996.
