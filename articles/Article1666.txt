 Time Is Expected to Name Gaines Managing Editor   By Reilly, Patrick M, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 13, 1992  Henry Muller, managing editor of Time Warner Inc's flagship magazine since 1987, is expected to be succeeded by James R. Gaines, the editor credited with making People magazine a hit. Muller is preparing to take on the No 2 position overseeing Time Inc magazines.
