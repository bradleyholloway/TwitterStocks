 U.S. News -- Reporter's Journal: Highway M-22 Logo Revisited --- Michigan Attorney General Tells Brothers They Can't Trademark All Highway Signs; Owners of Niche Retail Company Blast Back   By Dolan, Matthew, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 2012  Federal records show at least 794 registered trademarks on stylized versions of road signs, but it is unclear how many unadulterated road signs like M-22 have trademark protection, says Brendan Way, a San Francisco lawyer who specializes in intellectual property.
