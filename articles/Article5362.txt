 Wal-Mart Stores Inc.: Retailer's Profit Rose 26% in Quarter to $387 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1992  Wal-Mart Stores Inc's net income for the first quarter ended Apr 30, 1992 rose 26% to $387 million from $307 million in the year-earlier period. Sales rose 26% to $11.65 billion from $9.28 billion.
