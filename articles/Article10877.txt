 Chicago Pneumatic Says Big Board Began Inquiry on Trading   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1986  Chicago Pneumatic Tool Co. said the New York Stock Exchange began an inquiry into the trading in its shares prior to a March 19 hostile tender offer by Danaher Corp. and DanWest Partners.  Chicago Pneumatic, an industrial machinery concern, said it will cooperate with the Big Board inquiry, noting that the company issued a news release March 13, cautioning that "speculators might be accumulating its shares."
