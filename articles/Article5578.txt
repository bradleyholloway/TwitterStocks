 Broderbund's stock surged before deal with Electronic Arts was announced   By Dolan, Carrie, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 11, 1994  Shares of Broderbund Software Inc rose 22%, to $41.25, in the three days preceding Electronic Arts Inc's Feb 9, 1994 announcement to buy Broderbund for 15.4 million shares of its stock. Some analysts believe that inside information about the deal had leaked out, but the SEC had no comment on whether it will look into reasons for the stock's rise.
