 Mannesmann expects a 'clearly negative' first-half report   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 1993  Industrial group Mannesmann AG Chairman Werner H. Dieter told shareholders at the company's annual meeting that although first-half sales were unchanged from a year earlier, the company will show "clearly negative" results. In the year-earlier period, Mannesmann AG posted an operating profit of 15 million marks ($8.8 million).
