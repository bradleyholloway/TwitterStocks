 Future of Finance (A Special Report) --- Don't Kill the Big Banks: Robert E. Diamond Jr. of Barclays on the benefits of size   By Deogun, Nikhil, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2009  [...] Robert E. Diamond Jr., president of Barclays PLC and chief executive for corporate and investment banking and wealth management, believes big banks play a vital role in international trade. When I think about large pension funds around the world, therefore, if you are a large pension fund with liabilities in different currencies and markets, in an environment of virtually zero, risk-free interest rates because the interest rates in most major economies are close to zero at the short end of the curve, you need banks to help you take on risk because you have liabilities that have to be paid out.
