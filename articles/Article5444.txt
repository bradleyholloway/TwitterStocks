 Ownership of airline in Philippines appears on verge of change   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 1993  Complex financial maneuvering among owners of newly privatized Philippine Airlines may shift control of the company from PAL Chairman Antonio Cojuangco Jr to Lucio Tan, chairman of Allied Banking Corp. Tan, who has accumulated a controlling stake, had close ties to late President Ferdinand Marcos.
