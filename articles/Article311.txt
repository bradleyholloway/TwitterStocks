Aristotle, Cicero and Quintilian -- the ancient masters of oratory -- would give Bob Dole no more than a beta for his acceptance speech in San Diego on Thursday night.  While his text included some elegant tropes and high-toned rhetorical devices that would have made Aristotle and company smile approvingly, Mr. Dole swerved back and forth from lofty to folksy.
