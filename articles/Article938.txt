 Two Oklahoma Banks, One in Texas Closed; All Reopen Today   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 1987  The three closed yesterday were First National Bank of Navasota, Navasota, Texas, which had assets of $30.8 million; First State Bank, Blanchard, Okla., with assets of $23.2 million, and First National Bank of Luther, Luther, Okla., with assets of $17.8 million.
