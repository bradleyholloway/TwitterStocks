 Woolworth's shares rise on speculation about store closings   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1997  Woolworth Corp's stock was heavily traded and rose 3.4% in a down market Jul 9, 1997 on speculation that the retailer soon may close most of its ailing five-and-dime stores. Woolworth shares closed at $26.50, up 87.5 cents, in twice-normal trading volume on the NYSE.
