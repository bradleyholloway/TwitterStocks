 VW Posts 4.6% Rise In Western European Unit Sales for 1995   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1996  Europe's largest automaker, Volkswagen AG, said it boosted its unit sales of cars in Western Europe 4.6% in 1995.  VW, based in Wolfsburg, Germany, said total car deliveries to customers rose to 2,143,000 units last year in Western Europe from 2,048,000 units in 1994.
