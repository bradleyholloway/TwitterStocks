 Specialty Retail Board Reduced to 3 Members As 4 Directors Resign --- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 1987  The specialty-food-store franchiser said two directors, R. Walter Jones IV and Clifford W. Perry Jr., resigned "as a result of their disagreement" with an action at a Nov. 19 board meeting, when Alan R. Kleinmaer resigned as chairman and president but was retained as vice president. Mr. Jones resigned effective Nov. 27 and Mr. Perry effective Dec. 4.
