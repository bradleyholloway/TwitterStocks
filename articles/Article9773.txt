 Consumer Services Brief -- EBay Inc.: Auctioneer to Cut Some Fees, Increase Customer Support   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 2005  EBay Inc., responding to seller anger about some planned fee increases, will lower some listing fees and expand customer support services. Effective today, eBay will reduce the minimum insertion, or listing, fees in some categories to 25 cents from 30 cents on its U.S. and its Canadian Web sites.
