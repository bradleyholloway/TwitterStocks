 Small stock focus: Nasdaq critic vows he isn't taking sides   By Getler, Warren; Power, William, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1994  William G. Christie, who co-wrote the controversial study "Stock Market for the Next 100 Years" with Paul H. Schultz, is seen by some small-stock traders and regulators as the Nasdaq Stock Market's public enemy No 1. However, Christie insists he isn't out to destroy the market. In Nov 18, 1994 trading, the Russell 2000 Index fell 1.00 to 248.58, while the Nasdaq Composite Index lost 1.17 to 764.67.
