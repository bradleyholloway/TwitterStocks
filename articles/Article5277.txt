 Drexel Bankruptcy Settlement Cleared by Judge, Advancing Reorganization   By Lambert, Wade, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1991  Manhattan NY Federal Judge Milton Pollack approved the massive class-action settlement of Drexel Burnham Lambert Group Inc's bankruptcy case, thus allowing the firm to proceed with its reorganization plan. The plan sets aside at least $1.53 billion for unsecured creditors.
