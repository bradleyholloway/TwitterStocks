 Sun Healthcare Group: Charges in fourth quarter led to loss of $4.6 million   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1997  Sun Healthcare Group said it had a net loss of $4.6 million for the fourth quarter, reflecting $35 million in charges. The results compared with a year-earlier loss of $56.3 million. Revenue rose 6.2% to $330.1 million from $310.5 million.
