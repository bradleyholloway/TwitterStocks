 Accounting Panel Turns Down Proposal On Derivative Rules   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 1997  The board of the International Accounting Standards Committee rejected a proposal to adopt the Financial Accounting Standards Board's proposed derivatives rules. The IASC is a London-based international accounting body which is currently working on an internationaol set of accounting rules that can be used by companies around the world to list their stocks in various international markets.
