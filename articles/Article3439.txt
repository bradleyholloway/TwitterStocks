 Telecommunications Brief -- PCCW Ltd.: Re-Entry Into Cellular Market Comes With Stake in Sunday   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 2005  Hong Kong fixed-line operator PCCW Ltd. said it will pay 1.16 billion Hong Kong dollars (US$149.1 million) to acquire a 60% stake in mobile operator Sunday Communications Ltd., re-entering the cellular market it left three years ago.
