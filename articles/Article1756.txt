 J.P. Morgan continues innovations in Europe, studies expansion in Asia   By Revzin, Phil, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 1993  J. P. Morgan & Co Chairman Dennis Weatherstone says that Europe is a place to experiment, and to make a lot of money. Weatherstone, during an interview, said the investment bank is continuing to innovate in Europe and is also considering increasing its activities in China and elsewhere in Asia.
