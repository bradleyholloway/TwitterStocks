 Seaman Furniture Completes Its Move For A Restructuring   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1989  The restructuring is a big step forward in the efforts by Kohlberg Kravis Roberts & Co., the nation's biggest leveraged buyout firm, to revive Seaman, an ailing furniture retailer. Bolstering Seaman's finances has been costly for KKR, whose buy-out fund previously held 80% of Seaman's stock as a result of its $360 million LBO of the retailer in 1987.
