 The Homestretch   By By Nicholas Kulish, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 2000  Island Voice: A group of Puerto Ricans who want to have a say in next month's election have taken their case to court. A federal appeals court in Boston will decide whether Puerto Rico has a right to representation in the Electoral College, and thus a role in deciding the next president. Puerto Ricans may cast ballots, but the result is essentially a beauty contest.
