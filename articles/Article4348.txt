 The Euro Crisis: Sarkozy's Electoral Bid Marred by Ratings Drop   By Parussini, Gabriele; Horobin, William, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 2012  Standard & Poor's Ratings Services' decision to strip France of its prized triple-A debt rating, arriving just over three months before France's presidential elections, exposes President Nicolas Sarkozy to hard criticism from his many challengers, creating a sizable weak spot in his re-election bid.
