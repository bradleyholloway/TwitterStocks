 Wall Street Asks: Why Are These Men Smiling?   By Tippett, Karen L, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1996  To some Wall Street observers, the image of the "coffee brothers" -- company founders Dennis and Samuel Boyer -- is an unwanted reminder of Brothers' heavy losses and roasted stock price. They blame the Boyers, who are no longer actively involved in the company's daily operations, for pushing the Boca Raton-based coffee wholesaler into the retail business. The company was forced to sell most of its retail "boutiques" a few months ago.
