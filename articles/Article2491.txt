 Litton to split its businesses into two firms   By Lubman, Sarah, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 1993  Litton Industries Inc plans to split into two companies in order to expand its commercial business and possibly its defense business. The move comes amid the shrinking US military budget. News of the planned split caused Litton's stock to soar 14% on Jun 18, 1993 to $65.125, up $8.125 a share.
