 NBC's `Today' Show to Replace Producer in Move to Ease Tensions   By By Joe Flint, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 11, 2002  The move at the country's most-watched morning show was widely anticipated. While "Today's" performance has been strong, there was growing tension behind Mr. [Jonathan Wald] and "Today" personalities Katie Couric and Matt Lauer, according to people close to the show. Mr. Wald, who won high praise as executive producer of NBC's "Nightly News," succeeded Jeff Zucker at the helm of "Today" after Mr. Zucker left that post almost two years ago to become president of NBC Entertainment.
