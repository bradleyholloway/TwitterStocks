 Angolan endgames   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 1993  An editorial notes the ethnic violence in Angola and Bosnia caused by power-hungry local leaders, saying that the solution for the long-term goal of diplomacy is to take the economy away from the state and distribute it to the people--that way the state doesn't become a football worth fighting and dying for. Finally, political power should be redistributed to a self-determining electorate.
