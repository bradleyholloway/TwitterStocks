 A taxing drag on the airlines   By Dasburg, John H, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 1995  John H. Dasburg examines why the domestic airline industry lost $9 billion between 1990 and 1993. While a number of factors contributed to the decline, he says a significant and long-lasting cause of airlines' profitability problems is the staggering increase in government-imposed taxes and charges.
