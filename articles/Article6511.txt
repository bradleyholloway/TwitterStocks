 IPO of Cynosure Advances 26%   By Cowan, Lynn, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 2005  WiderThan's stock closed flat at $12, its offer price on Nasdaq. The company, based in Seoul sold six million American depositary shares below the expected range of $14 to $16 set by underwriters J.P. Morgan Chase & Co. and Merrill Lynch & Co. The company develops cellphone entertainment like ringtones and games for SK Telecom Co., and its financial fortunes are tied to the South Korean cellphone provider. The company's prospectus contains a red flag: a warning about a material weakness in its accounting controls and competency with U.S. accounting principles.
