 Tales are oft told as TV talk shows fill up air time   By Jensen, Elisabeth, The Wall Street Journal,  May 25, 1993  Although all TV talks shows, of which there are an increasing number, say that they don't reuse subjects and guests from other shows, they all engage in the practice as they try to find interesting topics. Sherrol Miller has told the story of her short-lived marriage on several shows more than once.
