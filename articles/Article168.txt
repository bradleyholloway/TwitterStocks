Richard Jones 3d, a retired lawyer with the firm of Simpson, Thatcher & Bartlett, died of Alzheimer's disease Saturday at a nursing home in Manhattan. He was 84 years old and lived in Delray Beach, Fla.  Mr. Jones, who graduated from Harvard Law School, joined the Manhattan law firm in 1929 and retired in 1971. He served on the board of the Spence Chapin Adoptive Service from 1957 to 1971 and was appointed honorary director from 1971 to 1988.  To see the full article, subscribe here.
