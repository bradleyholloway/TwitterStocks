 Dollar Declines As Clouds Loom In Coming Data   By Vames, Steven, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 2005  Many analysts say risks are skewed toward further dollar selling this week, with data on the February U.S. trade deficit due today and on Treasury International Capital System flows due Friday. Those sets of data, which both describe key components of the U.S. current account, are among the factors the pushed the dollar sharply lower last year.
