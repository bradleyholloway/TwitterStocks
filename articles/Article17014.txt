 Perfection Isn't Modification's Aim   By Anonymous, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 2009  Omitted by the Journal were these facts from the Fitch report: that many 2007 loan modifications actually raised the monthly payment of borrowers, that 2008 loan modifications were much more likely to significantly reduce the monthly payment, and that delinquencies so far on subprime loans modified in 2008 are running at about 20%, or less than half the rate of the 2007 vintage at the same point in time -- and in a tougher economy.
