 Atlantic City Casinos Post Rise in Winnings Of 18% for September   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1987  According to the New Jersey Casino Control Commission, winnings last month increased to $222.3 million from $188.5 million in September 1986, when 11 casinos were operating. When results from the newest operation, Showboat Inc.'s Showboat Casino, Hotel and Bowling Center, are excluded, winnings are up 9.6% from last year. Showboat Casino opened in March.
