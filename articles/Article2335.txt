 Renault's 1991 Net More Than Doubled Due to Cost Cutting ---- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 20, 1992  Renault S.A., France's state-owned auto maker, posted 1991 net income of 3.08 billion francs ($548.6 million), more than double the year-earlier profit of 1.21 billion francs.  Renault's consolidated sales edged up 1.4% to 166 billion francs from 163.6 billion francs amid a depressed European car market, which grew by only 0.8% last year to 14.8 million units.
