 Profits Rise at Bell Atlantic, Nynex; First-Period Earnings Fall at SNET   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 20, 1990  Bell Atlantic Corp, U S West Inc and Nynex Corp, aided by strong non-telephone operations, such as cellular phone service, reported strong earnings gains. Pacific Telesis Group and Southern New England Telecommunications Corp had declines, primarliy due to higher expenses and lower phone revenues.
