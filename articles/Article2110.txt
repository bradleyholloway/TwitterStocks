 Tobacco companies are likely to post moderate to sharp rise in quarterly net   By Somasundaram, Meera, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1996  Tobacco companies are expected to show moderate to sharp increases in per-share earnings in the fourth quarter. Both Philip Morris Cos and RJR Nabisco Holdings Corp are expected to benefit from a growing industrywide shift to higher-profit margin premium cigarettes.
