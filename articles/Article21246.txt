 Seven Firms Settle With New York City On Toxic Waste Suit   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 1988  American National Can Corp., BASF Corp., Borg-Warner Corp., Dana Corp., Ford Motor Co., Koppers Co. and Public Service Electric & Gas Co. agreed to pay a total of $12.6 million in five installments over six years.  According to the complaint, the 15 companies generated hazardous industrial and chemical wastes that ultimately were dumped at five landfills by a waste-hauling contractor, Russell Mahler. The complaint said Mr. Mahler gained access to the landfills by bribing an employee of the city's Department of Sanitation.
