 New Securities Issues [6]  The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 1995  Federal Farm Credit Banks & Funding Corp. -- $75 million of debentures due June 23, 1998, priced at par to yield 6.79%, according to MCM CorporateWatch. The issue is noncallable for six months and was priced at a spread of 77 basis points above Treasurys. The issue will be sold through underwriters led by Morgan Stanley & Co.  Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp. -- $75 million of notes due June 16, 1997, priced at par to yield 6.44%, according to MCM CorporateWatch. The issue, noncallable for six months, was priced at a spread of 52 basis points above Treasurys. The issue will be sold through underwriters led by Fuji Securities Inc.  May Department Stores Co. -- $200 million of debt priced in two parts by underwriters led by Morgan Stanley & Co., according to MCM CorporateWatch. The first tranche consists of $100 million of notes due June 1, 2015, priced as 7.5s at 99.382 to yield 7.56%. The noncallable notes were priced at a spread of 85 basis points above Treasurys. The second tranche consists of $100 million of bonds due June 1, 2025, priced as 7.6s at 99.877 to yield 7.61%. The notes were priced at a spread of 90 basis points above Treasurys. Both parts are rated single-A-2 by Moody's Investors Service Inc. and single-A by Standard & Poor's Ratings Group.
