 Dexia SA: Net Profit Increases by 15% On Strength in Public Finance   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 2006  Belgian-French financial-services company Dexia SA posted a 15% rise in second-quarter net profit, helped by strong results from its public-finance division. Net profit rose to 584 million euros ($751.6 million) from 507 million euros a year earlier.
