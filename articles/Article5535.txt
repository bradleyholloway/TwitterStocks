 VF Corp.: Clothing maker to purchase H.H. Cutler for $132 million   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1993  VF Corp, a Wyomissing PA apparel maker, agreed to acquire clothing maker H. H. Cutler Co for $132 million. The acquisition of Cutler, whose 1993 revenue is expected to exceed $200 million, should increase VF's youth apparel business by half.
