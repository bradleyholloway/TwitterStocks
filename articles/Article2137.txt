 Business Brief -- CAMBRIDGE BIOTECH CORP.: Pact Is Made to Settle Suit As Part of Reorganization   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1996  Cambridge Biotech Corp. in Worcester, Mass., said it has agreed to settle a class-action lawsuit charging the drug company and certain former officers had made misleading statements in the months before Cambridge Biotech filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in 1994.
