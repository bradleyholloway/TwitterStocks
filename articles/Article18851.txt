 Digital's chief has no plans to fill No. 2 job   By Bulkeley, William M, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1996  In an internal memo, Digital Equipment Corp Chairman and CEO Robert B. Palmer indicated to employees he doesn't plan to name a successor to Enrico Pesatori, who quit in Jul 1996 as head of computer operations. Palmer continues to serve as acting head of the operations.
