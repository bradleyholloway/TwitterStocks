 Alcan Unit Is Struck At Kentucky Smelter After Talks Break Off   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1986  Alcan Aluminum Corp.'s Sebree, Ky., smelter was struck by about 450 hourly workers from the Aluminum, Brick & Glass Workers union Sunday after contract talks broke off.  A spokesman for the Cleveland-based unit of Alcan Aluminium Ltd., Montreal, said new talks weren't immediately scheduled. The previous contract at the plant expired Jan. 31.
