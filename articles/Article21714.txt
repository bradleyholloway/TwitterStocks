 Japan's Job Growth in June Was Slowest in Five-Year Period   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 3, 1992  Japan said its seasonally adjusted jobless rate stood at a low 2.1% in Jun 1992, but the growth of the number of jobholders marked its slowest pace in five years. Japan's unemployment rate has remained in the 2.0-2.1 range since Jun 1989.
