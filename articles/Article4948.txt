 Business Brief -- Mascotind Inc.: New Visions Entertainment Stake Cut to 3% of Stock   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 11, 1988  Mascotind Inc. said it lowered its stake in New Visions Entertainment Corp. to 3% of the common shares outstanding.  Mascotind, a Beverly Hills, Calif., investment firm, didn't give a reason for lowering its New Visions common stake.
