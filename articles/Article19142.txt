 Taxable Money Funds' Yields Are Mixed; Tax-Free Funds' Rise   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1999  Yields on taxable money funds were mixed, while those on tax-free money funds rose in the latest week.  The average seven-day compound yield on taxable money funds edged up to 4.47% from 4.44% in the week ended Tuesday, said IBC's Money Fund Report, an Ashland, Mass., newsletter. Compound yields assume reinvestment of dividends.
