 Business Brief -- American Tower Corp.: Pact Is Struck to Buy Rights To 2,193 Towers From Alltel   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 2000  American Tower Corp. agreed to acquire the rights to 2,193 communications towers from Alltel Corp. for as much as $658 million. Under the accord, American Tower has the option to buy the rights to about 200 additional towers for as much as $300,000 cash per tower.
