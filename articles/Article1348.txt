 Industrials Advance 37.13 to High in Wake of Greenspan Statement   By Wilson, David, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 13, 1990  The Dow Jones Industrial Average climbed 37.13 points to an all-time high of 2969.80 Jul 13, 1990, prompted by statements by FRB chairman Alan Greenspan that monetary policy might be eased.
