 General Motors Corp.: Saturn unit raises '95 prices an average of about 1.5%   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1994  General Motors Corp's Saturn unit announced in Troy MI, average price increases of about 1.5%, or $170 a vehicle, for its 1995 lineup of small cars. The biggest increase came on Saturn's SL1 sedan. Its price rose about 2% to $10,995 from its final, 1994 model-year price.
