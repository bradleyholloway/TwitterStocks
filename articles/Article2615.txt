 Ingersoll-Rand Says It Wants to Terminate Two Dresser Ventures   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 13, 1999  Ingersoll said it has given notice to Halliburton that it wants to sell its 49% interest in Dresser-Rand Co. for about $535 million and its 51% interest in Ingersoll-Dresser Pump Co. for about $385 million. Under terms of the longstanding joint-venture agreements, Halliburton may elect to acquire one or both of Ingersoll's stakes, or elect to sell its interests to Ingersoll.
