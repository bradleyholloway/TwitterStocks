 Questioning the Books: Williams Communications to Buy Network With Ex-Parent Responsible for Payment   By By Karen Blumenthal, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 2002  For making the payment, Williams Cos. would receive about $750 million in unsecured debt or equity of Williams Communications -- though the real value will almost certainly be less than that. Like many telecommunications companies, Williams Communications has been struggling amid a heavy debt load and a capacity glut. It is negotiating to rework its bank debt and recently said it is considering filing for bankruptcy-law protection as a way to restructure its debts.
