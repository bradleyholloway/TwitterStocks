 Tip of the Week: No Dive Seen In Gas Price   By Blackman, Andrew, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 2004  "As long as U.S. economic growth holds up and there's a high demand for petroleum products of all kinds, it doesn't seem likely that American motorists are going to get a break on gas prices," says AAA spokesman Geoff Sundstrom. In fact, AAA's long-term forecast is for prices to trend higher through the end of the decade: "We're just using more gas."
