 Aerospace Firms Are Expected to Report Higher Earnings for the Third Quarter   By Wartzman, Rick, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1991  Third-quarter earnings reports at most aerospace companies, including General Dynamics, Northrop Corp, Lockheed Corp, McDonnell Douglas Corp, Loral Corp and E-Systems, will be glowing, but storm clouds are gathering ahead as the industry's revenue base is eviscerated by shrinking defense expenditures.
