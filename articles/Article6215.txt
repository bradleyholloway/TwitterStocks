 Health Care Brief -- Centerpulse AG: Medical-Technology Company Weighs Bids From Two Suitors   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 2003  Centerpulse AG said it is too early to choose between competing takeover bids from medical-devices companies Zimmer Holdings Inc., Warsaw, Ind., and Smith & Nephew PLC of the United Kingdom. The stance suggests Centerpulse of Switzerland expects a counterbid from S&N.
