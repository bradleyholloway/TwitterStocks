 REVIEW --- Books: On Holy Ground   By Albinia, Alice, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 2011  Crossing the Torea Pass near the Tibetan border, the author struggles for breath in the thin air and a "memory rises, a pang of sadness": his dying mother in a hospital ward calling out for air and his hooking an oxygen mask over her face. [...] the skull too is smashed and becomes a morsel with its brains . . . the vultures crowd in.
