 Best Buy's Net Income Rose 16% in 4th Period, Aided by Acquisitions   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 2001  The results include both the Musicland and Magnolia Hi-Fi Inc. purchases. Best Buy said shoppers still are snapping up digital products, despite a weakening economy. Best Buy said it expects comparable-store sales to fall in the first quarter compared with a year earlier and to be flat in the second quarter. As a result, the company expects profit for the first quarter ending May 31 to fall between 23 cents and 25 cents a share.
