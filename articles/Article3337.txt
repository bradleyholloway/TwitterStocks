 TPI Polene PCL: Currency Loss, Higher Costs Push Net Profit Down Sharply   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 2004  TPI Polene PCL reported third-quarter net profit fell sharply, on a huge foreign-exchange loss and higher expenses. The Thai cement maker's net profit fell to 141.4 million baht ($3.4 million) from 2.36 billion baht a year earlier.
