 Dollar Advances Against Yen, Euro, As Japan Stimulus Plan Disappoints   By By Umberto Torresan, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 1999  The dollar held on to gains against the yen and euro amid light trading and high volatility.  Late yesterday in New York, the dollar was trading at 104.90 yen, up from 104.65 yen late Wednesday. The euro was trading at $1.0407, down from $1.0449.  In a lackluster trading day, the dollar kept its strength against the yen throughout the session after it surged by more than half a yen in Asia following the release of the latest Japanese stimulus package totaling 18 trillion yen ($171.59 billion).
