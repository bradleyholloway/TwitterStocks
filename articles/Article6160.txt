 Chattering Furby Is Forced To Sleep During Takeoffs   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 1999  Both the Federal Aviation Administration and Transport Canada have decided to categorize the toy as a portable electronic device, restricting its use on flights. "We treat the Furby as any other electronic device," such as a GameBoy or a CD player, said FAA spokeswoman Kathryn Creedy. Use of electronic devices is prohibited during takeoffs and landings.
