 AmBev: Net Soars on Higher Volumes As Market Share Increases   The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 2006  Brazilian brewing giant Companhia de Bebidas das Americas, or AmBev, said first-quarter net profit more than quadrupled as warm summer temperatures boosted consumption, and the company continued to gain market share from rivals. AmBev is part of InBev, the Belgian brewer formed in 2004 when Belgium's Interbrew took control of AmBev.
