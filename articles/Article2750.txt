 Heard on the street: Ideon, credit-card registry that bellyflopped this year, is drawing some bottom-fishers   By Browning, E S, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 1995  Beset by a failed too-fast business strategy and a slumping stock price, Jacksonville FL-based Ideon Group's stock closed at 10 on Aug 18, 1995, down 53% from 21 3/8 in February. Some analysts are recommending the stock, however, as a bargain. The credit-card registry company is profiled.
