 U.S. News: Subprime Suit Against FDIC Is Settled for $90 Million   By Maremont, Mark, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 2009  The Federal Deposit Insurance Corp. agreed to pay $90 million to settle allegations that it gave out high-interest subprime mortgages to unqualified borrowers, helping to close an embarrassing chapter in the agency's history.
