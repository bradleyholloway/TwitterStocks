 The Death of Osama bin Laden: Social Media Buzzes, Comes Into Its Own   By Fowler, Geoffrey A; Berzon, Alexandra, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 2011  On Monday, the number of postings about bin Laden on social-media sites including Twitter, Facebook and others eclipsed those on the Japan earthquake and the royal wedding of Price William in the U.K., according to NetBase Solutions Inc., a social-media analytics company.
