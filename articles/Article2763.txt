 House Passes Bill to Replace Tax Break That Triggered Complaints From WTO   By By John D. McKinnon And Geoff Winestock, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 2000  Adding to their concern, U.S. officials also believe that the dispute isn't grounded in any real unhappiness among European businesses over the FSC program, which they contend isn't that different from many European tax systems.
