 Heard on the street: AT&T breaks up, and 'tangerines' come in season: Stock gets boost but some investors won't be buyers   By Browning, E S, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1995  The breakup of AT&T Corp is viewed as positive by analysts because the three new specialized companies all can attack their markets without being held back by one another. AT&T closed on Sep 20, 1995 up 6 1/8 at 63 3/4, an 11% gain in a single day of frantic trading, with volume more than ten times normal levels.
