A 17-year-old youth was shot in the head by a friend early yesterday as they played with a handgun in a Manhattan subway station, the transit police said. The victim, Edwin Ulloa of Manhattan, was in critical condition at Harlem Hospital following the 5:30 A.M. shooting in the 191st Street subway station, said Albert W. O'Leary, a transit police spokesman. Mario Bran, 18, of Manhattan, was arrested, Mr. O'Leary said, and faces charges of attempted murder and assault. Misael Capellan, 17, of Manhattan, was charged with criminal possession of a deadly weapon for providing Mr. Bran with the gun, a .25-caliber semi-automatic, said Mr. O'Leary.
