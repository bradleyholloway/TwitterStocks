 Anheuser-Busch Cos.: Stake in Grupo Modelo SA of Mexico increased to 37%   The Wall Street Journal,  May 23, 1997  Anheuser-Busch Cos said it completed its plan to raise its stake in Mexican brewery Grupo Modelo SA to 37%. Anheuser said it paid $605 million for about 19% of Mexico's largest brewer.
