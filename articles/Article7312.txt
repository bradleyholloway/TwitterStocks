 Corporate News: Forest Agrees to Governance Changes   By Loftus, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 2012  In a proposed settlement of shareholder litigation, Forest's board would implement "measures related to conflicts of interest regarding board discussions, compensation consultants, and executive compensation policy, as well as the payment of certain agreed legal fees of the plaintiffs," the company said in its quarterly report filed Thursday with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission.
