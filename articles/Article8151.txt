 Fifteen Insiders at Beverly Enterprises Sell Shares with Value of $4.2 Million   By Peers, Alexandra, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 1991  Fifteen corporate executives, directors or major shareholders of Beverly Enterprises Inc have sold 390,567 shares valued at about $4.2 million since May 1, 1991. The firm's shares closed at $11.375 Aug 6, 1991 on the NYSE.
