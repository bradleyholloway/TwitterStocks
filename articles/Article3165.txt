 Conglomerates Brief -- Tyco International Ltd.: Limits Set on Golden Parachutes For Company's Top Executives   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 2003  Tyco International Ltd. said it adopted policies limiting executive golden parachutes and replacing stock options for directors with shares that vest on retirement. The golden-parachute changes are similar to a nonbinding recommendation approved by shareholders in March.
