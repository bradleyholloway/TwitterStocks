 Fate of Two Cendant Ex-Officers Soon to Be Decided in Fraud Trial   By Chittum, Ryan, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 2004  There were more than 700 exhibits and 40 witnesses in six months of testimony in the fraud trial. Jurors have been deliberating for about a month. Following the verdict, Cendant's lawsuit against CUC's former auditor, Ernst & Young LLP, will go forward. Cendant is seeking billions of dollars in damages due to what it alleges was "gross negligence" by the accounting firm related to auditing CUC's books. Ernst & Young settled a Cendant shareholder lawsuit for $335 million in 2000.
