 Bond Unit Misses Payment on Bonds In $510 Million Issue   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 1989  Bond Brewing Holdings Ltd., a unit of Australia-based Bond Corp., apparently missed an interest payment on its $510 million issue of junk bonds.  Bond Brewing, which is Australia's biggest brewer and also has interests in the U.S., is part of the heavily indebted empire controlled by Alan Bond. Although Mr. Bond has been struggling for most of this year, traders said they believed this was the first time that Bond Corp. or Bond Brewing had missed an interest payment.
