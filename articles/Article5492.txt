 U.K.'s Wimpey Plans Rights Issue to Raise About $157.6 Million ---- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 11, 1993  George Wimpey PLC, a British construction company, said it plans to raise about #104 million ($157.6 million) through a rights issue.  The company said it will issue around 72 million new shares at 148 pence ($2.24) a share. This represents a 36 pence discount to its closing price yesterday of 184 pence. The rights issue will be on a basis of one new share for every four shares held.
