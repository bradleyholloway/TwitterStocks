 Business Brief -- Ford Motor Co.: Prices to Rise Up to 21.6% On Some Restyled Models   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 1988  Ford Motor Co. said the restyled version of its Ford Thunderbird and Mercury Cougar models will have 1989 base prices as much as $3,493, or 21.6%, higher than the 1988 models.
