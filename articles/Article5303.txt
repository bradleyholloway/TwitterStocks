 Blame the Victim: This Time, Croatia   By Rosandic, Anton M, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 1991  Anton M. Rosandic, executive director of the Croatian-American Freedom Alliance, blasts the Oct 10, 1991 article profiling Yugoslav President Slobodan Milosevic as a populist. Rosandic says that Milosevic is no Boris Yeltsin and in fact may rival Hitler in his destruction of historic structures in Croatia.
