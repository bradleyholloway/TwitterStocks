 International Corporate Report: French Court Sets Study Of Sale of Buitoni Assets   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 1988  A French commercial court has appointed an expert to study whether minority shareholders in BUITONI S.A. would be unfairly treated by the Italian financier Carlo De Benedetti's sale, proposed last month, of the Buitoni group's assets to NESTLE S.A. of Switzerland.
