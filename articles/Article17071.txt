 Berkshire Sets Vote On Split   By Kingsbury, Kevin, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 2009  Berkshire Hathaway Inc. will hold a shareholder meeting on Jan. 20 on its proposal to undertake a 50-to-1 split of its Class B shares as part of its acquisition of Burlington Northern Sante Fe Corp. The railroad is already 23% owned by Berkshire, which last month agreed to pay $26 billion in cash and stock for the remaining stake.
