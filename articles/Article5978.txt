 BET Holdings Inc.: Quarterly net rises 20% on higher cable revenue   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 1997  BET Holdings Inc said that fiscal third-quarter earnings jumped 20% as higher revenue from its cable network was somewhat offset by costs associated with new businesses. BET reported net income of $6.3 million for the quarter ended Apr 30, 1997, up from $5.2 million a year earlier.
