 Tip of the Week: Ask for Time To Do Taxes   By Herman, Tom, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 2005  This time, however, the extension isn't automatic. You have to serve up a reason that the IRS considers acceptable. IRS officials, however, typically are lenient if you are missing key records or have suffered health problems.  You can file Form 2688 electronically with the IRS, or you can mail it to the IRS center where your return is supposed to be filed. Filing your tax return or extension request electronically typically is smarter because it's faster and safer than relying on standard mail delivery.
