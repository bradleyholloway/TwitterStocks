 Forest-product companies post solid gains for the third quarter   By Rigdon, Joan E, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 13, 1995  Forest-products companies reported solid third-quarter earnings gains. Georgia Pacific Corp reported record net income of $324 million from the year-earlier net of $87 million. Boise Cascade Corp's third quarter net totaled $118.5 million compared with a year-earlier loss of $31.7 million, while Potlatch Corp nearly tripled its net income to $31.7 million from $10.7 million and Union Camp Corp reported net income of $129.7 million from the year-earlier $21.7 million.
