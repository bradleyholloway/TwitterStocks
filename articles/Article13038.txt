 UnumProvident Corp.: Net Loss Widens Amid Costs For Restructuring of Policies   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 2004  UnumProvident Corp. reported a first-quarter net loss of $562.3 million, in large part stemming from the restructuring of a block of individual accident and disability policies it sold in the 1990s. The Chattanooga, Tenn., disability-income insurer said its net loss widened to $1.91 a share from $1.02 a share, or $246.4 million, a year earlier.
