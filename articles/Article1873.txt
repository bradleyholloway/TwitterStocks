 World wire: Hungary schedules sell-offs   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 1994  On Apr 18, 1994 Hungary will start a new program to put state enterprises in private hands. Ahead of the May 8 general election, critics said the timing is aimed more at getting votes for the governing coalition. State assets, 70 companies, valued at $1.16 billion will be sold over the next five years.
