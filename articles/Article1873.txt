 Lucky Stores Delays Poison Pill Plan, Talks With American Stores   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 21, 1988  DUBLIN, Calif. -- Lucky Stores Inc. said it is "continuing to review a variety of alternatives" to American Stores Co.'s pending $45-a-share, or $1.74 billion, tender offer. But it also delayed implementing a further step in its "poison pill" defense.
