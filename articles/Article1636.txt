 New German Defense Chief Is Redefining Agency's Role   By Roth, Terence, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 1992  German Defense Minister Volker Ruehe is profiled for his efforts in redefining the Germany agency. Since becoming defense minister in Apr 1992, Ruehe has been casting out old programs and bureaucrats in an agency that for most ministers has been a political dead-end.
