 Haitian Rescue Stymied Amid Chaos   By Forelle, Charles; de Cordoba, Jose, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 2010  PORT-AU-PRINCE, Haiti -- A massive but frenzied international relief effort struggled to reach Haiti's desperate residents Thursday, as survivors of the ferocious quake pleaded for medical care and raced against time to dig people from the rubble. Despair spread on the streets of the crowded and hard-hit capital, where tens of thousands of survivors huddled in the city's plazas and streets, hoping for the arrival of food, water and medical supplies.
