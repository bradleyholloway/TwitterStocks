 Union Machinists Approve 3-Year Contract at Boeing   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 1992  About 71% of union machinists of the International Association of Machinists and Aerospace Workers at Boeing Co voted to approve a three-year labor contract, which includes a $4,500 one-time lump-sum payment for the average worker in 1992. The pact also includes a 3.5% wage increase in 1993 and 1994.
