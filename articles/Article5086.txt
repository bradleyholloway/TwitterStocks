 GM's nonauto units had 2.5% decline in combined earnings for 4th quarter   By Simison, Robert L, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 1994  General Motors Corp's three nonautomotive units posted a combined 2.5% decline in fourth-quarter 1993 net income. General Motors Acceptance Corp, the finance unit, said net income for the period declined 29% to $207 million from a year ago's $290 million.
