 Numico NV: Profit Falls 93% on Charges, But Sales Are Forecast to Rise   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 2005  Baby- and clinical-food producer Numico NV reported a 93% drop in fourth-quarter net profit, hurt by impairment charges, but gave an optimistic forecast for 2005 sales growth. The company, of Zoetermeer, the Netherlands, owns brands such as Olvarit, Bambix and Cow & Gate.
