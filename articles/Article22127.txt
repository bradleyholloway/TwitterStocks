 People patterns: Working odd hours   By Crispell, Diane, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 2, 1996  According to a 1991 Census Bureau survey, a lean majority of Americans, 55%, work part-time or full-time hours only during "normal" daytime hours and weekdays. Despite the advent of flex-time and job-sharing, many Americans have little choice but to work the odd hours their employers require.
