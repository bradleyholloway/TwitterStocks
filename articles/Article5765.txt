 Business Brief -- AMERICAN HOME PRODUCTS CORP.: Sherwood Medical Unit, Davis & Geck to Combine   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 1995  American Home Products Corp., a Madison, N.J.-based drug company, said it is merging medical-device unit Sherwood Medical with Davis & Geck, a maker of wound-closure products that it acquired in its 1994 purchase of American Cyanamid Co.
