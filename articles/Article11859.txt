 Free markets for telecom: Don't stifle global merger mania   By Sidak, J Gregory, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 1994  J. Gregory Sidak comments on the rush of US telecommunications companies into various global alliances. Sidak says that under provisions of the Communications Act of 1934, foreign stakes in US telecom firms are limited to 25%, which stifles both innovation and competition in the industry.
