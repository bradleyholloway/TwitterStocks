 Tom Herman's Tax Tip: The Bailout Includes Lots of Treats   By Herman, Tom, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 2008  AMT Quick Fix If Congress had done nothing, about 26 million people would have been affected this year by the alternative minimum tax, or AMT, up from four million last year, according to Treasury Department estimates. Giving From IRAs Congress also revived a popular provision allowing people age 70 1/2 or older to transfer as much as $100,000 a year directly from an IRA to charity without owing income taxes on the money.
