 Alternative Plans for School Systems Get Corporate Help   By Stout, Hilary, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1992  The New American Schools Development Corp, a private concern set up at President Bush's request, pledged multimillion-dollar grants to 11 groups to initiate reform efforts intended to "reinvent" US education. The projects are sponsored by groups of educators, trade associations, multinational corporations and school systems that have devised new educational models that can be used in school systems across the US.
