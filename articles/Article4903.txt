 Apollo Computer Inc. Unveils Workstations In Midrange of Line   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 1988  CHELMSFORD, Mass. -- Apollo Computer Inc. introduced two new engineering workstations in the midrange of its product line.  Separately, a shareholder filed suit, charging that Apollo had made false and misleading statements about its outlook during the spring. Last Thursday, Apollo announced that it expects to report a loss for its second quarter ended June 30 due to weak sales in Germany. Its stock plunged in heavy trading after the announcement.
