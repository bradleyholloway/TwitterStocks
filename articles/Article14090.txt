 Group Is Considering Feasibility of Buying Crown Industries Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1985  An investment group with a 5.5% stake in Crown Industries Inc. said it is "considering the feasibililty" of acquiring the building-products and agricultural-equipment holding company.  In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, the group said its representatives met with Crown's chairman and president, Robert Dressler, last Tuesday to discuss the group's interest in a business combination. According to the filing, Mr. Dressler told the group's representatives he would communicate their interest "to an appropriate committee" of the company's board.
