 North West bid to buy Norweb climbs higher   By Bray, Nicholas, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1995  The UK's North West Water Group PLC raised its bid for regional electricity utility Norweb PLC to 1.83 billion pounds ($2.89 billion) in an apparent knockout blow to rival bidder Texas Energy Partners of the US. The joint venture of Houston Industries Inc and Central & South West Corp was not expected to raise its BP1.74 billion offer.
