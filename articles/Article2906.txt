 Other Bidders Likely After Clyde Rejects Gulf Canada's Offer   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 20, 1996  CALGARY, Alberta -- Gulf Canada Resources Ltd.'s C$972 million (US$712 million) bid for Britain's Clyde Petroleum PLC will likely attract higher offers, analysts said.  Gulf on Wednesday offered C$2.36, or 105 British pence, in cash per share for Clyde, whose North Sea oil and gas operations would add to Gulf's properties in Canada and Indonesia.
