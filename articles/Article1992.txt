 Motorola announces consumer-electronics version of PowerPC   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1995  Motorola Inc, continuing to broaden the target markets for its PowerPC microprocessor, announced a version of the chip called PowerPC 602 that is aimed at makers of consumer-electronics gear. Separately, Motorola said it won three cellular-phone contracts from Japan's DDI Corp valued at more than $70 million.
