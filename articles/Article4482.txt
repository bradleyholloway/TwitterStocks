 Holiday Inns Group To Buy Brock Hotel Properties and Assets   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 11, 1985  Under terms of the agreement, Holiday Inns, Memphis, Tenn., and investors led by Wichita, Kan., businessman Jack DeBoer will acquire three hotels and the rights to the Brock Residence Inns name and license rights. Brock Hotel will retain six open hotels, a substantial amount of franchise territory and the rights to nine properties currently in development.
