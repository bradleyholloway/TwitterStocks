 Redemption Notices   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 2006  Republic of the Philippines will redeem on Dec. 1 its 1992 series A floating-rate bonds, due June 1, 2010, with Cusip number 389612979; its 1992 series B floating-rate bonds, due Dec. 1, 2009, with Cusip number 389613704; its 1992 series A interest-reduction bonds, due Dec. 1, 2007, with Cusip number 389614538; and its 1992 series B interest- reduction bonds, due June 5, 2008, with Cusip number 389616103; at their principal amount plus interest accrued to the redemption date. Trustee is U.S. Bank National Association.
