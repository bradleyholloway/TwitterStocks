 Business world: Choose your partners in the new Japan   By Benes, Nicholas E, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 1995  Nicholas E. Benes, senior managing director of Kamakura Corp, discusses the changes that have begun within the Japanese economy, including the trend toward deregulation, widespread corporate restructuring and a focus on consumer interests. Benes suggests guildelines for potential foreign investors wishing to find the ideal local partner in the "new Japan."
