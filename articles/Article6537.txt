 Business Brief -- Intel Corp.: New Chip Products Aimed At Phones, Small Devices   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 2002  Intel Corp., Santa Clara, Calif., announced two new microprocessor chips designed to boost its market share in wireless telephones and hand-held computers. The products are an offshoot of a chip family called StrongARM that Intel purchased from Digital Equipment Corp. in 1997.
