 For a Progressing Gholston, It's a Process   By Cacciola, Scott, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 2010  Coach Rex Ryan said he expects defensive end Vernon Gholston to play more than usual when the Jets visit the Cleveland Browns on Sunday, and this qualifies as progress for Mr. Gholston, who has been skirting that fine line between prospect and bust for three seasons. Did the Jets make a mistake? "When that dude busts out of his shell, y'all are going to see some (expletive)," said defensive end Trevor Pryce, a 14-year veteran.
