 Legal Beat: Drexel and FDIC   By Pollock, Ellen Joan; Moses, Jonathan M, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1992  Lawyers for Drexel Burnham Lambert Corp filed a lawsuit in federal court against Martin A. Siegel and Dennis B. Levine seeking more than $6 million in salary and bonuses paid to the former Drexel investment bankers. The suit alleges that the two men opened up the firm to potentially large civil liabilities and damaged its business reputation by trading on inside information.
