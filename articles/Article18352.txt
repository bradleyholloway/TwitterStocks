 Business World: Muni Maven, Bullish on California, Digs In for Fiscal Fight   By Ferguson, Tim W, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 1992  Tim W. Ferguson presents the opinions of Andy Johnson, who heads the Franklin Advisers municipal-bond fund operation for Franklin Resources of San Mateo CA. Johnson is not fretting over California's longest-in-history budget standoff, and in fact is rooting for Governor Pete Wilson, who's demanding spending cuts and no more tax increases from the Democratic Legislature.
