 Revlon Cuts Outlook, Delays Equity Offering   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 2006  Revlon now expects its results excluding items to be "at or below" last year's levels with a "significant impact" on its second quarter. In 2005, Revlon's net loss was $84 million, or 23 cents a share, on sales of $1.33 billion.
