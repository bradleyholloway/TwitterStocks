 Letters to the Editor: Refocus the Debate On Fannie and Freddie   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 2002  2. The "huge advantages" are illusory. Yes, there are statutory provisions that facilitate the operations of these unique and highly successful companies. But these are offset by the inability of Fannie and Freddie to diversify into or concentrate on other businesses, as "banks and insurers" would exit the secondary mortgage market in a hurry if profits in that business fell. Fannie and Freddie can and will never do so.
