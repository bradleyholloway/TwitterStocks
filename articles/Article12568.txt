 Business Brief -- ARIBA INC.: Pact Signed With VeriSign And American Express Co.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 2000  Ariba Inc. signed an agreement with VeriSign Inc. and American Express Co. to allow companies on its business-to-business network to use American Express cards to pay for online transactions. Shares of Ariba rose 14% on the news.
