 Business Brief -- Key Tronic Corp.: Loss Narrows, Revenue Soars; Work Force Is Reduced 30%   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 2002  Key Tronic Corp. reported a fiscal fourth-quarter loss of $3.2 million, or 33 cents a share, narrower than a loss of $4.8 million, or 50 cents a share, for the year-earlier quarter. The electronic-equipment manufacturer reported revenue of $43.9 million for the quarter ended June 29, up 47% from $29.8 million a year earlier.
