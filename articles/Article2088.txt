 Bankers Trust shakes up top ranks of overseas asset-management group   By O Brien, Timothy L, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1995  Bankers Trust New York Corp has shaken up the top ranks of its international investment-management group in the wake of what analysts describe as a mediocre performance by the division in 1995. Ian Martin, head of the highly profitable Bankers Trust Australia Ltd unit, will now oversee Bankers Trust's entire asset-management group.
