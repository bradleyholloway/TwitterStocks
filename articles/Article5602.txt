 Mirage, Gold Strike sign pact to build a low-roller casino on Las Vegas Strip   By Yoshihashi, Pauline, The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1994  Mirage Resorts Inc and Gold Strike Resorts signed an agreement to build a 3,000-room, $250 million themed casino on the Las Vegas Strip that will be aimed squarely at low-budget gamblers.
