 International Brief -- Mitsubishi Heavy Industries Co.: Profit Forecast for the Year Is Cut Amid Sluggish Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 1993  Mitsubishi said it now expects pretax profit of 140 billion yen ($1.12 billion) for the year, down 10% from 155.45 billion yen in the previous year and 6.7% below the 150 billion yen it forecast in November.
