 Autodie Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1992  Autodie Corp posted a loss of $4.6 million for its first quarter ended Nov 30, 1991, widening the $283,169 loss of a year earlier. The company said it may resort to a "mass layoff" at its headquarters and a temporary closing of its only plant by mid-Mar 1992.
