 Letters to the Editor: Internet Pirates Profit On Health-Care Data   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 7, 2000  Public access to quality health-care information greatly benefits both patients and medical professionals. The AMA never would oppose law-abiding Web sites that properly provide the public with the wealth of reliable AMA-created health information. In fact, current AMA business practice encourages this accessibility to help patients make informed decisions about health-care choices.
