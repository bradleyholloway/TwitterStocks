 Money --- Worried About Finances? Breathe Easy   By By Anne Marie Chaker, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 2000  That's according to "The Best Cities to Earn and Save Money," a report that ranks the financial security of residents in the largest 125 metropolitan areas in the U.S., including five in New England. The study, to be released tomorrow, uses 15 measures, such as household income and wealth, job creation, cost of living and crime. It was developed by researcher Arundel Street Consulting, St. Paul, Minn., for ReliaStar Financial Corp., a Minneapolis life-insurance-holding company.
