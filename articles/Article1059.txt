 Essex Chemical Asks Holders to Reject Bid From a Swiss Concern   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 1988  CLIFTON, N.J. -- Essex Chemical Corp.'s board asked shareholders to reject a $24-a-share tender offer from a Swiss company, and said Essex might restructure itself or seek other bidders.  Essex said its board has told management to study alternatives including the sale of all or part of Essex. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, Essex said it "has held and is continuing to engage" in talks with third parties about such a sale. The board said other possibilities include a financial restructuring, or the sale of new stock or debt securities.
