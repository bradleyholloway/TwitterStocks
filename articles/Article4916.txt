 Lasting Debate on Clinton Draft Record Focuses on 2 Questions Raised by Critics: Were Strings Pulled and Was He Really Open to Induction?   By Birnbaum, Jeffrey H, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1992  The issue of whether or not Democratic presidential candidate Bill Clinton pulled strings to avoid going to Vietnam is still being pursued by Republicans in the hope that focusing on the military draft issue could strain Clinton's credibility with the public. Clinton's explanations about his role in the military draft are provided along with an analysis of suspect statements.
