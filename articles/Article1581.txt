 Operating Loss Fails to Reduce Interest in Buying Daily News   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 13, 1992  The New York Daily News had a loss in Feb 1992 of about $1.5 million, on top of a loss of more than $2.8 million in January. Nevertheless, the firm has heard from several potential bidders since its bankruptcy filing in Dec 1991, including real estate developer and publisher Mortimer Zuckerman.
