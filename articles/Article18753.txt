 Allan Bogner resigns from executive posts at He-Ro Group Ltd.   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1995  Allan Bogner, the president and CEO of apparel maker He-Ro Group Ltd, has resigned to "pursue other interests." A He-Ro spokesman characterized Bogner's resignation as a "mutual decision," which grew out of a corporate restructuring that began in Oct 1993. For its first quarter ended Aug 31, 1995, He-Ro reported a narrower loss of $89,000, compared with a net loss of $274,000 a year earlier.
