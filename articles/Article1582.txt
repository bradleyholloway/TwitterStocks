 Morgan Stanley Group Inc.: Insider Group Says It Sold Big Block of Firm's Shares   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 13, 1992  A group of 150 Morgan Stanley Group Inc employees sold 938,294 shares of the firm's stock, according to a filing with the SEC. The firm's share price was quoted at $57.25 on the NYSE Mar 12, 1992, down $.25.
