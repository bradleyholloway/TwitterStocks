 Premiums Drop For Acquisitions In Tech Sector   By By Janet Whitman, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 2003  Acquirers paid an average multiple of 1.69 times annual revenue for computer software, supplies and services companies, down from 3.77 times in the third quarter and 7.04 times in the fourth quarter of 2001, according to the latest quarterly M&A report by Ernst & Young National Office, a group specializing in transactional tax services.  Though valuations have slumped, the computer sector remains among the most-active areas for mergers and acquisitions. Of the 1,648 deals announced in the fourth quarter in the U.S., 522 involved companies specializing in computer software, supplies or services, the report said.
