 Tech Spending Bounces Back As Profits Rise --- Cisco Sees New 'Phase of the Recovery'   By Worthen, Ben; Clark, Don, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 2010  While most of the tech sector's initial gains came from selling products such as laptop computers and smart phones to consumers, recent results point to improvement in sales to business. Cisco's bullish outlook holds particular weight as it was among the first companies to say the downturn, once limited to the financial sector, was affecting the rest of the economy.
