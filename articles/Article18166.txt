 Lloyd's of London Lowers Premiums on Gulf Cargoes ---- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1991  War-risk insurance premiums for marine cargoes being transported to or from Iranian Gulf ports and offshore islands north of 27 30 N latitude had been lowered to 0.75% of the insured value, from 2%; while premiums for Persian Gulf ports south of 27 30 N latitude and west of 52 E longitude had been lowered to 1% of the insured value, from 2%.
