 Book Excerpt: Saving Early for College Pays Later   By Bradford, Stacey L, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 2009  The 529 plan is a tax-advantaged savings account that allows you to invest money today and withdraw the funds free of federal income taxes, provided the money is used for qualified college expenses. If you got a state tax deduction, you'll have to report money not used for college expenses as "recapture income" on your state tax return. --
