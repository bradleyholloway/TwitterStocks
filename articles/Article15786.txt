 Sepracor Provides $2 Million of Equity Funding to HemaSure   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 1999  Sepracor, also based in Marlborough, now owns 42% of HemaSure's shares outstanding. HemaSure said it could also receive about $1 million in additional equity through the exercise of 667,000 warrants acquired by Sepracor in the financing. The warrants are exercisable at $1.50 a share.
