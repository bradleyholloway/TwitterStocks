 Chemical Banking, Manufacturers Cut Payrolls by 950   By Lipin, Steven, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1991  Chemical Banking Corp and Manufacturers Hanover Corp have reduced their payroll by about 950 employees since their merger agreement was announced in the summer of 1991. The banks will begin the week of Nov 17, 1991 making the first cutbacks targeting an entire division. Further extensive reductions are planned.
