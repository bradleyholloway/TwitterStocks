 Bass, Westmont Reach Deal on Franchising Italian Hotels   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1999  Bass, a London lodging and spirits concern that owns the Holiday Inn brand, said Westmont, a Houston hotel-investment company, would relaunch a variety of its hotels in Italy as Holiday Inns and invest about $18 million in the properties during the next two years. No other financial details were disclosed.
