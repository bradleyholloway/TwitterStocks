 Singer NV: Kohlberg Affiliate Acquires Sewing Unit for $65.5 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 2004  KSIN Holdings Ltd., an affiliate of Kohlberg & Co., said it acquired Singer Sewing Co. for $65.5 million. The transaction, which effectively takes Singer NV out of the sewing business, also includes $22.5 million in promissory notes and the assumption of $46.3 million in debt.
