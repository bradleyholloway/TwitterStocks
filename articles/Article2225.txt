 PacTel ends accord to buy video firms, drawing challenge   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1996  Pacific Telesis Group is terminating the proposed $170 million purchase of two companies from Groupe Videotron and Transworld Telecommunications Inc that beam video services to homes through rooftop antennas. But Videotron may challenge PacTel's right to end the year-old pact.
