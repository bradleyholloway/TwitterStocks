 Dollar gains on mark late in session and slips back a bit against the yen   By Cullison, Alan S, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 1995  The dollar remained stuck on Jan 11, 1995 in recently established lower ranges, with traders unable to find news good enough to promote a rally. In New York, the dollar traded at 1.5366 marks and 99.95 yen, compared with Jan 10's DM1.5328 and Y100.05.
