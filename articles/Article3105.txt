 Michigan to Probe FirstPlus' Soliciting Of Second Mortgages   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 1998  Attorney General Frank J. Kelley of Michigan asked state regulators to investigate FirstPlus Financial Group Inc. for allegations of deceptive solicitations. Shares of the mortgage company dropped 12%.  Mr. Kelley asked for the probe after his office received an offer for a second mortgage of $42,800 on the state office building where he works.
