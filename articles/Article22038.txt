 Director of 'Waterworld' quits over fight with star   The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 1995  Motion picture director Kevin Reynolds, who has had a long and stormy friendship with "Waterworld" star Kevin Costner, has quit the picture after repeated clashes with the actor. The movie, which is being made by MCA Inc's Universal Pictures studio, completed shooting in Feb 1995 but is now in the critical editing stage. Daily Variety reported that Reynolds left on Apr 28.
