 Global Economic Turmoil: German Bankers Raise Cry Over Capital Plans   By Stevens, Laura; Eyk Henning; Blackstone, Brian, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 2011  The European Banking Authority, the pan-European Union banking regulator, suggested that the threshold for core Tier 1 capital requirements -- the ratio of a bank's core equity capital to its total risk-weighted assets -- could be raised to 9%, according to a confidential communication to national banking authorities, an EU official said Wednesday.
