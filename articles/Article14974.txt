 Coors Plans Search For New President At Brewery Unit   By By Marj Charlier, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 1992  GOLDEN, Colo. -- Adolph Coors Co. said it will begin a search for a new president of its Coors Brewery Co. unit as part of a management restructuring as the company prepares to spin off its non-beer, technology companies later this year.  William Coors, who is president and chairman of the parent company, will succeed his nephew Peter as chairman of the brewery unit. Peter Coors will remain chief executive officer of the unit, add the title of vice chairman and relinquish the presidency.
