 There's Only One Good Solution, and That's Bulldozers at 30 Paces   By Rose, Robert L, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1992  In the latest dispute between Caterpillar Inc and the UAW, the union claims that the construction-equipment company committed an unfair labor practice by prohibiting certain types of buttons, hats and black armbands in its Peoria IL factories. In response, the company said it believes that the NLRB will rule in its favor after hearing all of the facts.
