 Airbus strikes at McDonnell over subsidies   By Coleman, Brian, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 11, 1993  Airbus Industrie Managing Director Jean Pierson lashed out at McDonnell Douglas Corp in a thinly disguised accusation that the US company is violating a trade pact on aircraft subsidies. Separately, Ronald H. Brown, the US Commerce Secretary, said the US wants the GATT to adopt the US-EC aircraft-subsidy accord on a global basis.
