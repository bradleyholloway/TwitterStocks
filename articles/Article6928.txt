 U.S. News: Airbus Suspects Sensors in Crash   By Pasztor, Andy; Michaels, Daniel, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 2009  U.S. and European air-safety regulators are considering requiring that airlines replace certain airspeed sensors on Airbus jets, as an internal Airbus memo shows that investigators increasingly suspect faulty speed indications were major contributors to last week's crash of Air France flight 447.
