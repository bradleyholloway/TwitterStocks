 Sofamor Danek Group Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 1997  Sofamor Danek Group Inc, grappling with thousands of product-liability lawsuits, said it will take a pretax charge of about $50 million in its 1996 financial results. Sofamor Danek reported 1995 earnings of $5 million, or 20 cents a share, on sales of $188.8 million and including a one-time charge of $45.3 million.
