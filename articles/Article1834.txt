 Toronto Firm Offers Junk-Rated Notes In Canadian Dollars   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 1994  In what could be the initial step toward creating a Canadian junk-bond market, Rogers Cablesystems Ltd. sold what it said was the first issue of high-yield notes denominated in Canadian dollars.  The 300 million Canadian dollars (US$226.8 milion) of 20-year, 9.65% notes were snapped up quickly, but mostly by U.S. rather than Canadian buyers.
