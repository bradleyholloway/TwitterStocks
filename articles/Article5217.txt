 Courtaulds's 22% Rise In 1994 Pretax Profit Exceeded Forecasts   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 1995  United Kingdom textile manufacturer Courtaulds Textile PLC said pretax profit for 1994 rose 22% to #47.3 million ($76.1 million). The results were somewhat better than expectations for a 17% rise to #45.4 million.  Separately, the company announced that its chairman, Sir Christopher Hogg, was stepping down. John Eccles will take over for him at the company's general meeting on May 3.
