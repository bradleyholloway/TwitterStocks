 Thomson shakeup will affect sale of multimedia unit   By Lavin, Douglas, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 1997  The French government shook up the management of Thomson Multimedia and its holding company, Thomson SA, in a move that makes it unlikely the state will sell all of the consumer-electronics giant to Daewoo Group of South Korea, as it had planned in 1996, or to any other firm.
