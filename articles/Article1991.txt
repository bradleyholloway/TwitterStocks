 Lord Cairns forced to quit Warburg after failure of Morgan Stanley merger   By Whitney, Glenn, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1995  Lord Cairns was forced to resign as CEO of S. G. Warburg Group PLC only two months after he failed to engineer the merger of the company with Morgan Stanley Group. David Scholey will remain with the company and take the additional title of CEO.
