 M.I.M. Sells 4% Stake In Renison Goldfields In $29.8 Million Deal ---- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 1994  BRISBANE, Australia -- M.I.M. Holdings Ltd., an Australian metals company, sold its 4% stake in Renison Goldfields Consolidated Ltd. for 43.5 million Australian dollars (US$29.8 million).  M.I.M. said it sold 7.9 million common shares in Renison for A$5.50 (US$3.77) each. The company didn't announce who bought the shares.
