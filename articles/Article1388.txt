 MCA and Matsushita Electric Set Talks; Some Expect Buy-Out Pact Within Days   By Turner, Richard, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1990  Officials of MCA Inc and Matsushita Electric Industrial Co are scheduled to meet in New York Nov 19, 1990 and, insiders say, they hope to agree within days on terms of an acquisition of MCA by Matsushita. The acquisition may be valued somewhere between $7.4 billion and $8.3 billion.
