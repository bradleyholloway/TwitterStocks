 Mark Bonds Advance On Talk Bundesbank May Make Rate Cut --- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1986  West German mark bonds finished 1/8 to 3/8 point higher as the currency's continued appreciation against the dollar boosted optimism that the Bundesbank soon will cut the discount rate.  The visit to Frankfurt Sunday by Federal Reserve Board Chairman Paul Volcker added to speculation. The Bundesbank confirmed that Mr. Volcker met with Karl Otto Poehl, the Bundesbank's president, but no details were released.
