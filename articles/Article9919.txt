 Tip of the Week: Fight $3 Gas: Take the Bus   By Ransom, Diana, The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 2006  Smaller services that focus on localized hubs such as Boston, Chicago, and Philadelphia have sprouted in recent years. Their arrival can lead the bigger companies to trim fares. One of the latest entrants: Megabus.com, a subsidiary of Coach USA, currently offers some seats between Chicago and eight other Midwest cities for as little as one dollar plus a 50-cent booking fee.
