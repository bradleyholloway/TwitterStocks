 Business Brief -- Wine.com Inc.: Two-Thirds of Employees Laid Off in `Streamlining'   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 2001  Wine.com Inc., an Internet wine retailer, said it laid off two-thirds of its staff as part of a plan to "streamline" its business. The closely held company, one of the oldest online retailers, said 160 out of its 245 employees were laid off as of yesterday. The layoffs were distributed throughout the company, the Napa, Calif., company said.
