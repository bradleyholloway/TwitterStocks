 Juniper Networks to Buy Unit Of Siemens in $700 Million Deal   By By David Bank, The Wall Street Journal,  May 21, 2002  Juniper is Cisco's major challenger in the market for routers, which direct packets of Internet data to their destinations, but its progress has recently stalled. In the market for enterprise routers, both Juniper and Unisphere lost revenue in the first quarter, compared with the year-earlier period, while Cisco's revenue was flat, according to Dell'Oro Group, a market-analysis firm in Redwood City, Calif. In the past year, Juniper has been branching out from the enterprise market with moves into cable and wireless infrastructure equipment.
