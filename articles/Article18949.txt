 World wire: Lloyds TSB's profit series   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 1997  Lloyds TSB Group PLC, a British bank, has reported that first-half pretax profit surged 33% to BP1.5 billion ($2.47 billion) from year-earlier results, which were restated to BP1.13 billion--down BP11 million-- for an accounting change. The latest results reflected government tax changes that cut earnings by BP51 million.
