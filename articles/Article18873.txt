 ISG Technologies Inc.: Quorum growth is seeking proxy to remove board   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1996  The investment concern Quorum Growth Inc said it will file a dissident shareholder's proxy circular, seeking the removal of ISG Technologies Inc's board. Quorum, which holds 7.4% of ISG, said it wants a new board that will focus the company on developing a software and communications product that analyzes three-dimensional medical images.
