 Inland Steel, Nippon Steel Soon May Reach Pact to Build Facility   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1987  Inland Steel Co. said it may reach an agreement soon that will enable it to start building a long-delayed continuous cold rolling mill complex.  In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, the steelmaker said it may reach an agreement "in the near future" with Japan's Nippon Steel Corp. to form a joint venture to construct, own and operate the mill, which would produce one million tons of steel a year.
