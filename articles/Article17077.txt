 The Count: Wait a Minute, Didn't We Just Play These Guys?   By Smith, Michael David, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 2010  [...] while regular-season finales don't predict playoff rematches (perhaps because many NFL teams rest top players the last week), other regular-season results do predict playoff success. Since 1970, there have been 142 playoff games pairing teams that met once before, and the teams that won in the regular season went 80-62 (a .563 winning percentage) in the playoffs.
