 Media: Pay TV Reverses Decline --- Anemic Fourth Quarter Growth in Subscribers Follows Unprecedented Drops   By Schechner, Sam, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 2011  Michael Nathanson, an analyst at Nomura Securities, said investors' concerns about cord-cutting have led content companies to avoid including too much new TV programming in deals with companies like Netflix Inc. "The companies that own the most valuable content are becoming more disciplined about how they do these deals with online video distributors," Mr. Nathanson said.\n
