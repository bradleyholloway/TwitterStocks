 Luxury Goods Brief -- Waterford Wedgwood PLC: Restructuring Plan Seeks Annual Savings of $116 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 2005  Ireland's Waterford Wedgwood PLC issued a major restructuring plan to secure the long-term viability of its luxury-goods business. The crystal and china company said it will reduce excess capacity, cut overhead, upgrade existing facilities and close the Waterford Crystal plant at Dungarvan.
