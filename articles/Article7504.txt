 Campbell Soup Elects Baum to Added Post, Senior Vice President   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1986  CAMDEN, N.J. -- Campbell Soup Co. elected Herbert M. Baum to the additional post of senior vice president, a further indication that he is a leading candidate to succeed R. Gordon McGovern as president.  Mr. Baum, 49 years old, already has responsibility for all of Campbell's domestic operations as president of its Campbell USA division.
