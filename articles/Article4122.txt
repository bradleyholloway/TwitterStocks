 Books -- Review: The Long Flight From Tyranny --- Firsthand accounts of Burma's refugees, living difficult half-lives on a dangerous borderland   By Philip Delves Broughton, The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 2010  The refugees are victims of the Burmese government's war on political opponents and on ethnic groups within the country, notably the Karen, who have been fighting for autonomy ever since Burma was granted independence from Britain in 1948.
