 Markets in Turmoil: Poland Moves to Ease Debts in Swiss Francs   By Sobczyk, Marcin; Fairclough, Gordon, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 2011  Poland enacted a law aimed at easing the burdens of citizens with mortgages and other loans denominated in Swiss francs and other foreign currencies -- a sign of mounting political pressure on Central European governments from borrowers hit by sharp exchange-rate moves.
