 Oxoco Agrees to Cut Its Bank Borrowing By Selling Properties   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1985  As part of an agreement with its lenders, the oil and gas concern's lenders waived the company's prior defaults, modified certain financial covenants and allowed the balance of the loan to be amortized over five years. The banks also are requiring Oxoco to reach agreement on terms of its exchange offer with its debenture holders by Oct. 31.
