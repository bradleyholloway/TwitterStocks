 Banks and Allegheny International Reach New Credit Accord   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1987  Allegheny International Inc. said it has entered into a new secured revolving credit agreement for as much as $375 million with a consortium of banks headed by Mellon Bank.  The agreement, reached last week, replaces two secured agreements, the first of which expired last Wednesday and the second of which was to continue until April 29, 1988.
