 Citizens First to Sell 440,000 of Its Shares   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 7, 1985  GLEN ROCK, N.J. -- Citizens First Bancorp said it agreed to sell 440,000 common shares to Fox-Pitt, Kelton N.V., the London branch of a Hartford, Conn., investment banker, in a transaction valued at nearly $12 million.
