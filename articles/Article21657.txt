 Hyundai Owner Takes on Government   By Darlin, Damon, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1992  Chung Ju Yung, the peasant's son who founded the massive Hyundai Group business empire, has recently criticized South Korea's government and has formed a new political party, the Unification National Party. But there is evidence that the government is retaliating against Chung through regulatory means.
