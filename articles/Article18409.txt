 Aspin tells Defense Department to cut $8.3 billion from fiscal 1994 budget   By Ricks, Thomas E, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1993  Defense Secretary Les Aspin told the military services to expect to cut $8.3 billion from their budgets for the federal fiscal year beginning Oct 1, 1993. The services were told to plan on having 1.4 million uniformed personnel in 1996.
