 Financial Executives Urge Uniform Way Of Processing Trades   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 1989  An international group of financial industry executives called for uniform methods for processing securities trades, to promote globally oriented markets.  The so-called Group of Thirty, which includes banking and securities executives from several countries, called for changes in the way securities transactions are cleared and settled throughout the world. The recommendations were drafted by a committee headed by Citicorp chairman John S. Reed.
