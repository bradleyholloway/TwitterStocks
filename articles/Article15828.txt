 House Gives Final Approval To Aid for Steel, Oil Firms   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 1999  WASHINGTON (AP) -- The House gave final congressional approval to federal guarantees for $1.5 billion in loans for steel, oil and natural-gas companies, aiming to help industries that have been rocked by low prices.  The 246-176 vote came seven weeks after the Senate passed the bill 63-34. Supporters said they expect President Clinton to sign the legislation.
