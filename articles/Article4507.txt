 Seatrain Lines to File New Proposal to Exit Chapter 11 Status   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 1985  Seatrain Lines Inc. said it will file a new reorganization plan providing for cash payments of $15.5 million to settle $280 million of claims by unsecured creditors and debenture holders.  Under the new plan, current Seatrain shareholders would retain at least 50.1% of the reorganized company's stock. Two concerns that would help Seatrain raise the $15.5 million payment would receive stock in the company as well as conditional options to purchase the remaining shares.
