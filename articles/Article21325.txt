 Business Brief -- American Century Corp.: Company Defaults on Debt, Studies Options for Revamp   The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 1989  American Century Corp. said it defaulted on $1.7 million in subordinated convertible debentures after it missed an interest payment due April 1 and said its directors are weighing several options to restructure its debt, including a Chapter 11 bankruptcy filing.
