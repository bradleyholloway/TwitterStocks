 EPA Proposal to Reward Toxic Polluters for Speedy Cuts in Harmful Emissions   By Rosewicz, Barbara, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1991  The EPA proposed a new clean-air rule to reward toxic polluters for making speedy, voluntary cuts in harmful emissions by giving companies six extra years to install specified pollution controls if they voluntarily cut their toxic air pollutants by 90% before the deadline date.
