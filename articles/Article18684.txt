 Clinton, Congress set to collide on Cuba   By de Cordoba, Jose; Robbins, Carla Anne, The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 1995  On May 2, 1995, the US announced an immigration agreement with the Cuban government that would allow the bulk of the 21,000 refugees now in detention in Guantanamo Bay naval base in Cuba to come to the US, but would repatriate future refugees directly back to the island whether they were intercepted at sea or able to enter the Guantanamo base.
