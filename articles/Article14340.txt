 Computer Identics Names Wezniak Chief   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1987  CANTON, Mass. -- Computer Identics Corp. named Frank J. Wezniak, a Boston venture capitalist, to be president, chief executive officer and a director.  In the chief executive post, Mr. Wezniak succeeds Carl-Olof Henrikkson, who has been acting chief executive since December when he assumed that position from Mr. Collins and began a search for a new president. Mr. Henrikkson, a vice president of N.V. Bekaert S.A., a Brussels, Belgium-based company that owns 25% of Computer Identics's stock, remains a director.
