 Nods from Oscar may help small films win audiences   By Bannon, Lisa, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1996  A number of niche films, such as "Sense and Sensibility," "Dead Man Walking," and "Leaving Las Vegas," which were nominated for Academy Awards on Feb 13, 1996, could win larger audiences and ticket sales due to the publicity generated by their nominations. The box-office takes of several of the motion pictures nominated for the awards are detailed.
