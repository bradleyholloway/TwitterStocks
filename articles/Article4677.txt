 National Medical Says It Plans to Buy Back 10 Million Shares   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1986  National Medical Enterprises Inc. said it will buy back as many as 10 million common shares, or about 13% of the total outstanding. The buyback has a current market value of about $216 million.  National Medical, one of the nation's largest health care companies, said funds for the buyback will come from its planned sale of certain facilities, including nine of its 50 acute-care hospitals and a Florida health maintenance organization.
