 Investor's Calendar   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 2004  Tyco Mistrial: The long trial of former Tyco executives L. Dennis Kozlowski and Mark Swartz ended in a mistrial. The judge cited intense outside pressure on a juror for flashing the defense what looked like an "OK" sign, but said he wasn't suggesting that any jurors erred.  Dow Jones Tweaks Average: Three longstanding stocks in the Dow Jones Industrial Average will be replaced Thursday. Leaving the average of 30 blue-chip stocks will be AT&T (T), Eastman Kodak (EK) and International Paper (IP), replaced by insurer American International Group (AIG), drug maker Pfizer (PFE) and phone company Verizon Communications (VZ).
