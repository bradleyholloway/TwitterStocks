 Corporate News: Supreme Court Deadlocks on Costco's Watch Case   By Kendall, Brent, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2010  At issue was a lawsuit filed by Omega SA, a unit of the Swatch Group Ltd., alleging that Costco violated U.S. copyright law in 2004 by selling Omega Seamaster watches it obtained from third parties that had imported them into the U.S. Costco sold the watches for $1,299, below Omega's suggested retail price of $1,999.
