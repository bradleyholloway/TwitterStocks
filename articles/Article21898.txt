 Prudential adds legal reserves of $100 million   By Siconolfi, Michael; Steinmetz, Greg, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1994  Prudential Securities Inc announced plans to set aside an additional $100 million in reserves in 1994 to cover legal costs from limited-partnership claims. The reserves would bring Prudential's total legal tab from the soured partnerships to as much as $865 million.
