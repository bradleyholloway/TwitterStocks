 International Brief -- Whitbread & Co.: Pretax Profit Increased 16% In Half to $200.3 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 24, 1989  Whitbread & Co., a British brewer, reported a 16% rise in its first-half pretax profit to #128.5 million ($200.3 million) from #110.6 million a year earlier, with pubs and restaurants logging the fastest growth.
