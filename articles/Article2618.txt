 Business Brief -- PEP BOYS-MANNY MOE & JACK: Second-Quarter Net Grows By 13% on Flat Revenue   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 13, 1999  Pep Boys-Manny Moe & Jack, Philadelphia, said fiscal second-quarter net income rose 13% to $20.1 million, or 39 cents a diluted share, from $17.7 million, or 29 cents a share, a year earlier.
