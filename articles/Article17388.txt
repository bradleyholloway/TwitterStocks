 How Wiping Out Debt Can Affect Your Taxes   By Anonymous, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 2012  Insolvency.When the borrower is insolvent (with debts in excess of the fair market value of his or her assets) immediately before debt cancellation occurs, the resulting COD income is exempt from federal income taxation to the extent of that insolvency. To the extent COD income consists of unpaid interest that was added to your loan principal and then forgiven, any forgiven interest that you could have deducted--had you paid it--is exempt from federal income taxation.
