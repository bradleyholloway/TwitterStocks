 Serb leader's singing of pact tests Clinton   By Robbins, Carla Anne; Ricks, Thomas E, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 1993  President Clinton's plan to use military force if necessary against the Serbs has been put to the test by the decision of Bosnian Serb leader Radovan Karadzic to sign a peace agreement. Karadzic must still submit the peace plan to the Bosnian Serbs' Parliament, which will vote on May 5, 1993.
