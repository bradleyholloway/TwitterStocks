 Pacific Telesis Chief Sees Higher Profit, Says No Decision Yet on Japan-U.S. Link   By By Johnnie L. Roberts, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1987  Pacific Telesis Group expects to post higher earnings this year, plans several years of flat capital spending at its telephone company units, and will continue to diversify through acquisitions.  In a story in this newspaper yesterday, S. Ross Brown, chief executive of the Pacific Telesis International unit, said a decision had been made to join the venture. Mr. Guinn said Mr. Brown's statement was premature and that a final decision will be made soon.
