 Money Fund Assets Slipped $350.5 Million During Latest Week   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 13, 1985  The Washington-based trade association said the overall drop resulted from a $525.9 million decrease in general-purpose fund assets, which totaled $60.76 billion in the latest week. Assets of 74 institutional funds fell a slight $24 million, to $53.26 billion, while assets of 87 broker-dealer funds rose $199.4 million to $94.47 billion.
