 Business Brief -- COUSINS PROPERTIES INC.: Joint Venture Is Entered With Prudential Insurance   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 13, 1998  Cousins Properties Inc said it entered into a real-estate joint venture with Prudential Insurance Co of America. Under the terms of the partnership, Cousins will contribute nine properties valued at $284 million and subject to about $54 million in long-term mortgages and the insurer will contribute $230 million in cash. The cash will be used to fund some of Cousins' new real-estate developments.
