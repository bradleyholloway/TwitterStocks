 Industrial Brief -- Hercules Inc.: Tentative Agreement Reached To Settle Class-Action Lawsuit   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 2004  Hercules Inc. said it reached a tentative agreement to settle a class-action compensation lawsuit for $5.9 million, according to a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission. The Wilmington, Del., chemicals company said the settlement is subject to court approval and to a written agreement on the settlement terms.
