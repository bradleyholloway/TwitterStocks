 Business Brief -- Fleetwood Enterprises Inc.: Sales Fell an Indicated 11% In Fiscal Second Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1989  Fleetwood Enterprises Inc., which produces motor homes, travel trailers and manufactured housing, said preliminary results indicate sales for its second quarter ended last Sunday will be about $374 million, down 11% from $419.5 million a year earlier.
