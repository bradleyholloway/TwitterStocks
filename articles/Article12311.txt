 Generale des Eaux Stock Jumps 8.6% on Forecast   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 1998  The share price of French conglomerate Cie. Generale des Eaux shot up 8.6% on Apr 3, 1998 to Ffr1,129 ($181.66) after the company released a bullish earnings forecast and announced a new name, Vivendi.
