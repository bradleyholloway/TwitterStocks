 Charming Shoppes Inc.: Retailer to shut 290 stores, take a $65 million charge   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1995  Charming Shoppes Inc said it plans to close 290 "underperforming" stores, resulting in a one-time pretax charge for the fiscal fourth quarter ending in Jan 1996 of about $65 million. For the fiscal fourth quarter ended Jan 28, 1995, Charming Shoppes reported net income of $5.2 million on sales of $345.4 million.
