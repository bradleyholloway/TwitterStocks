 Ex-Foxboro Director, Two Associates Agree To SEC Settlement   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 1993  In its suit, the SEC charged that Purnendu Chatterjee gave "material nonpublic information" to Anjan Chatterjee, a management consultant in Westport, Conn., who is no relation, and Sukumar Shah, a resident of France, on or before June 11, 1990, concerning a coming tender offer for Foxboro shares.
