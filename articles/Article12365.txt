 National Semiconductor To Sell Part of Chip Plant   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 1998  The plant was the focus of a front-page article in The Wall Street Journal yesterday. The article detailed allegations by former workers of lax safety conditions at the plant and recounted concerns by some former employees that exposures to toxic chemicals at the plant were linked to workers' cancers, birth defects and miscarriages. The company denied the allegations.
