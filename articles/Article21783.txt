 Wheat, livestock prices jump on news of Russia lobbying U.S. for record $1 billion in food aid   By Kilman, Scott, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 1993  News that Russia was lobbying the US for food aid boosted wheat and livestock prices Mar 2, 1993. On the CBOT, the May contract for wheat rose $.055 to $3.40 a bushel. Hog futures also climbed. In other markets, petroleum fell while gold and silver rose.
