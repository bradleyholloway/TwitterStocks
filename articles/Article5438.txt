 Japan's Diet Passes Package to Boost Economy, but Leaders Call for More   By Chandler, Clay; Schlesinger, Jacob M, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 11, 1992  Despite the Diet's passage of a special 10.7 trillion yen ($86.24 billion) budget package to boost Japan's sagging economy, many business and government leaders are calling for even more government spending. Noboru Hatakeyama, vice minister for international affairs at the Ministry of International Trade and Industry, said he didn't expect the country's economy to recover until the second half of 1993.
