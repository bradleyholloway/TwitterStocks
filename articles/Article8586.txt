 Clinton to sign bill eliminating media tax break   By Frisby, Michael, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1995  President Clinton will sign legislation restoring health-insurance deductions for the self-employed, even though the measure ends a special tax break for the sale of broadcast outlets to minorities. That provision ended Viacom Inc's $640 million tax deferral on the $2.3 billion sale of its cable-TV stations to a minority-led partnership.
