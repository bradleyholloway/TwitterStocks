 Business world: Time to kill the thrifts for good   By Trafton, Stephen J, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 1995  Stephen J. Trafton, chairman and CEO of Glendale Federal Bank, says the thrift industry is obsolete, and cannot be justified either by standards of the market or public policy. Trafton says a common charter for banks and thrifts is a logical next step.
