 Ex-Colonial Reality Official Obtained Loans with Forged Papers, U.S. Charges   By Pacelle, Mitchell, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 1990  The former chief financial officer of Colonial Reality Co, Frank M. Shuch, used forged documents to obtain $10 million in loans for the company's limited partners and siphoned off $6 million from the partnerships for his own account, federal prosecutors charged.
