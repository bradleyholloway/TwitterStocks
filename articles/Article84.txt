Karen Rae Spar, daughter of Dr. and Mrs. Irving L. Spar of Rochester, N.Y., and Jay Bennet Kasner, son of Mr. and Mrs. Edward J. Kasner of Roslyn, L.I., were married last evening at the Shelter Rock Jewish Center in Roslyn by Rabbi Myron Fenster.  The bride, a third year visiting student at Fordham University, School of Law, expects to receive a J.D. degree from Suffolk University, School of Law, in Boston on June 14.  She was graduated cum laude from Union College, from which her husband was graduated magna cum laude, after he was graduated cum laude from Boston University School of Law, where he was an editor of the Law Review. The bridegroom is an associate of the New York law firm, Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom.  Mr. Kasner is associate dean of graduate studies at the University of Rochester Medical School. His father is president of Coordinated Graphic Packaging Inc., Long Island City manufacturers of folding paper boxes.
