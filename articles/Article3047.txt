 BAE, Thales Results Faulted by Investors; Stock Prices Decline   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 13, 2002  [Thales], Europe's largest defense-electronics supplier, said net income soared more than sixfold to 88 million euros ($86.4 million) in the first half from 14 million euros, helped by strong growth at its defense division and lower taxes. Revenue, which Thales had initially disclosed last month, jumped 15% to 4.98 billion euros from 4.33 billion euros.
