 Coca-Cola Co. Expects Growth in Global Volume   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 20, 1996  Coca-Cola Co., allaying concerns that its growth might be slowing, said it expects global volume to increase about 10% in the fourth quarter.  Investors breathed a collective sigh of relief. Coke's stock has struggled of late, but it rose sharply yesterday to close at $51.125, up $2.75, in composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange.
