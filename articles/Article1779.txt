 Caterpillar Inc.: Plant sets partial shutdown as inventory-cutting move   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 13, 1993  Caterpillar Inc plans to partly shut down its Track-Type Tractor Business facility in East Peoria IL for two weeks beginning Sep 13, 1993 to bring inventories in line with demand. The company said 1,600 employees at the plant will be affected, but 1,000 will continue to work during the period.
