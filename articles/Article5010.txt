 Paris Air Show, reflecting downturn in aerospace industry, will be leaner   By Coleman, Brian, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1993  Organizers of the biennial Paris Air Show, citing an industry downturn, say the 1993 event will have 10% fewer exhibitors than in 1991, and the companies that are attending say they will bring in fewer staff for their stands. The aerospace industry's outlook for the coming decade is discussed.
