 Investor's Calendar   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 2007  -- So Long, Cingular: AT&T rebrands its Cingular Wireless service with the AT&T name starting Monday.  -- Earnings News: Home builder Lennar expects to post a loss after land-related write-downs of between $400 million and $500 million. Other companies reporting earnings this week: Apple, Intel, Southwest Airlines, Harley-Davidson, Novartis, Continental Airlines, Citigroup and Motorola.  -- Whose iPhone Is it? After Apple announced the June release of its cellphone with iPod attributes, Cisco Systems filed a lawsuit alleging Apple's use of the "iPhone" name violates a Cisco trademark. Apple's planned prices: $499 and $599.
