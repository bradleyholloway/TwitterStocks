 Business Brief -- Spiegel Group Inc.: Dividend to Be Suspended At Beginning of Fiscal 2002   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 2001  Spiegel Group Inc. suspended its quarterly dividend of four cents a share, effective Dec. 30, the beginning of its 2002 fiscal year. Spiegel said it will use the funds "for other capital requirements" and continue to evaluate its dividend policy, under which it had been paying out about $5.3 million a quarter.
