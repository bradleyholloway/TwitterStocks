 Business Brief -- Quebecor Inc.: Agency Exercises Its Right To Buy Printing Unit Stake   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1992  Quebecor Inc. said Caisse de depot et placement, the Quebec government's pension-fund agency, exercised its right under an agreement with the company to acquire 7.2% of Quebecor's Quebecor Printing Inc. unit.
