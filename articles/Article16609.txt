 Tip of the Week: Keep Hands Off Rollover   By Spors, Kelly K, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 2006  Last week, the Internal Revenue Service sharply increased, to about $3,000 from $95, the fee for seeking an IRS ruling to correct a botched retirement-plan rollover. Translation: "It's going to cost a lot more to correct mistakes now," says Ed Slott, an accountant and individual-retirement-account specialist in Rockville Centre, N.Y. And that filing fee is on top of any fee you might pay an accountant or lawyer to help with your appeal.
