 CD Yields Were Mixed For the Latest Week   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1998  Yields on certificates of deposit were mixed in the latest week. The average yield on six-month "jumbo" CDs, which typically require deposits of $95,000 or more, inched up to 5.07% from 5.06%, according to a weekly index prepared by BanxQuote Inc.
