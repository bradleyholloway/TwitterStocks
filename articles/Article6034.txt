 Business Brief -- DAIMLER-BENZ AG: Mercedes-Benz to Invest $1 Billion in Brazil Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1997  Daimler-Benz AG's Mercedes-Benz subsidiary in Brazil will invest $1 billion to the year 2000, the company said. Mercedes-Benz do Brasil SA said $600 million would be invested in 1998.
