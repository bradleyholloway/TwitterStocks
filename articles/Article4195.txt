 Health & Wellness: The Research Report / New Medical Findings   By Lukits, Ann, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2010  Using a new and highly sensitive test, a cardiac protein associated with heart abnormalities, cardiovascular disease and death can be detected at levels 10 times lower than previously found, according to a study in the Journal of the American Medical Association. The new test may result in more false-positive diagnoses of heart attack in people who are not at risk for cardiovascular disease but have higher troponin levels due to age, sex, race, hypertension, diabetes or other conditions.
