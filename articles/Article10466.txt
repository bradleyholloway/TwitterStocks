 Corporate News: EDF Sells Stake in German Utility   By Hromadko, Jan; Amiel, Geraldine, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 2010  Selling its EnBW stake also allows EDF to eliminate 2.3 billion euros in off-balance-sheet liabilities related to an option held by OEW, a group of municipal shareholders that also owns 45% of the German utility, that would have allowed OEW to sell its shares to EDF.
