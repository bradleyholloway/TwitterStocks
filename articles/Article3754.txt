 Industrial Brief -- Stanley Works: Proposal Is Made to Purchase Quebec's Frisco Bay Industries   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 21, 2004  Stanley Works is offering about $45 million for security-systems company Frisco Bay Industries Ltd. as part of its bid to diversify its operations. Stanley Works, a New Britain, Conn., provider of tools, hardware and door systems, said it will pay $15.25 for each share of Frisco Bay, St. Laurent, Quebec.
