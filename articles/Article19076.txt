 Letters to the Editor: Brazil Should Avoid Rigid Exchange Rate   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 1998  Britta L. Hillstrom, Brazil desk officer with the US Treasury Dept, responds to the Aug 28, 1998 commentary by Sebastian Edwards arguing that Brazil should adopt Argentina's currency-board regime. Hillstrom argues instead for the "more sustainable solution" of aggressive fiscal reform.
