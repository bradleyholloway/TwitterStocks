 The $500 Million Scapegoat   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 1992  An editorial discusses former junk bond king Michael Milken's participation in the downfall of the S&L industry, saying that Milken is serving as someone else's $500 million scapegoat because it would take someone with a lot more power to wreck a business the size of the US thrift industry.
