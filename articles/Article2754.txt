 Zell criticizes Goldman's many roles in fight to control Rockefeller Center   By Pacelle, Mitchell, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1995  Chicago financier Sam Zell, who is competing against Goldman, Sachs & Co for control of New York City's Rockefeller Center, charged on Sep 20, 1995 that Goldman Sachs is putting its own interest above those of its clients, including himself and fellow investors General Electric Co and Walt Disney Co.
