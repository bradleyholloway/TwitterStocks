 Comex Investigating Big July Silver Trade   By Tanouye, Elyse, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 6, 1991  A large silver futures trade which occurred before the opening bell Jul 24, 1991 is under investigation by COMEX. The trade, which was for 200 silver futures contracts with a face value of $4,250,000, was made by seller Morgan Guaranty Trust Co of New York to Paul Tudor Jones, a major money manager in futures markets. The investigation centers on whether the trade violated trading rules by being prearranged and being executed before trading officially began.
