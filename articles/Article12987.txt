 U.S. Attempts to Bar Currency Tax Shelter   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 2003  The Treasury and the IRS declared the maneuver to be a "listed transaction" under its antitax-shelter rules. That means taxpayers must disclose use of the transaction to the IRS, and promoters must keep lists of taxpayers using them. Failure to do so will result in increased penalties.
