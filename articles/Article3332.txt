 II-VI Increases Bid To Buy Laser Power In Face of Competition   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 2000  II-VI, a laser-optics manufacturer, said yesterday that it was increasing the cash component of its cash-and-stock transaction to $2.89 a share from $2.32 a share. The stock swap would remain the same, with Laser shareholders receiving 0.052 share of II-VI stock for each share of Laser. II-VI also is guaranteeing a floor price of $5.15 a share.
