 Moody's Cuts Ratings on Two Power Firms Based in New England   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 13, 1992  Moody's Investors Service Inc cut its ratings on the debt of Maine Yankee Atomic Power Co and Vermont Yankee Nuclear Power Corp due to concerns over debt service in the event the plants were shut down. Moody's added that the plants are not likely to close for economic reasons, however.
