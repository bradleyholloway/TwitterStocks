 MasterCard, Visa Near An Antitrust Settlement   By Sidel, Robin; Catan, Thomas, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 2010  Visa Inc. and MasterCard Inc. are close to settling a Justice Department antitrust probe over credit-card acceptance rules, potentially giving merchants more flexibility to steer customers to cheaper forms of plastic, according to people familiar with the negotiations.
