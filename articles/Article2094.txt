 GATX Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 1995  GATX Corp said it offered to purchase the shares of CGTX Inc held by Hawker Siddeley Canada Inc for 93 million Canadian dollars ($67.5 million). Hawker Siddeley, a manufacturing concern, owns 55% of CGTX, a railcar-leasing company, and GATX, a Chicago transportation and distribution company, already owns 45%.
