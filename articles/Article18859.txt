 Derivatives industry needs upgrades in risk management, GAO concludes   By Connor, John, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1996  Despite progress in controlling derivatives risks, improvements are needed in the risk-management, internal control and corporate-governance systems of derivatives dealers and users, the GAO said. The agency reported that such controls are essential to ensure that the directors and senior management of major derivatives dealers and end-users exercise their fiduciary responsibilities.
