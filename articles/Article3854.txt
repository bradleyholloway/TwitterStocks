 Tip of the Week: It's the Time For Tipping   By Kaptik, Alexandra, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 2004  The Emily Post Institute, an etiquette center in Burlington, Vt., offers guidelines on what to tip around the holidays: Tips can range from $25 to $70 for your child's day-care provider to $20 to $80 for the superintendent of your apartment building, or the equivalent of one night's pay for your baby-sitter. (More examples are on the Web at www.emilypost.com/etiquette/holiday/holiday_tipping.htm.)
