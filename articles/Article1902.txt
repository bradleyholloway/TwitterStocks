 Mexican laws, pride keep the nation from making best use of energy source   By Torres, Craig, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 1994  Although Mexico owns more than 70 trillion cubic feet of natural gas, the country continues to be a net importer of natural gas from the US because it simply doesn't have the money to get at its abundant gas reserves. Many hope state-owned Petroleos Mexicanos, or Pemex, will find a way to allow foreigners and their money into the country without violating the constitution, which restricts foreign profits from production-sharing.
