 Business Brief -- Volkswagen AG: Slump in Car Sales in Brazil Brings With It 4,000 Job Cuts   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 2003  A sharp slump in car sales is prompting Volkswagen AG to cut nearly 4,000 jobs in Brazil, the company said. Volkswagen said it would eliminate nearly 16% of its 24,800-member work force due to weak sales and excess capacity at its five Brazilian factories. Brazilian car sales slipped 8% during the first half of 2003.
