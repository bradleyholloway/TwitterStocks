 Corporate Focus: Will duty-free Vuitton pack in profit?   By Bird, Laura, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 1997  France's LVMH Moet Hennessy Louis Vuitton SA shelled out $2.47 billion in cash for controlling interest in DFS Group Ltd and its chain of 180 Duty Free Shoppers stores in airports and other locales around the Pacific basin. The question now is whether the purchase, aimed at the most important consumers of luxury and designer merchandise, Japanese tourists, will prove worth the price.
