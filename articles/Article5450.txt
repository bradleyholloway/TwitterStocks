 New York court upholds libel verdict against former ABC station in Buffalo   By Geyelin, Milo, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1993  A New York appeals court upheld a $15 million libel verdict against a Buffalo NY TV station owned by Capital Cities/ABC Inc that mistakenly linked a restaurant owner to organized crime in a news broadcast, then issued a less than complete retraction. The TV station involved is WKBW-TV.
