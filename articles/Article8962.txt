 Nike's Chapa Resigns From Marketing Post In International Area   The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 1998  BEAVERTON, Ore. -- Nike Inc. said that Rudy Chapa, one of the architects of its high-rolling athletic endorsement strategy, plans to resign, adding to recent upheaval at the sportswear company.  Mr. Chapa, who was promoted to executive vice president of international marketing in January, cited his desire to spend more time with his family after six years at Nike.
