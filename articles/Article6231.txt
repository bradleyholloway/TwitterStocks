 Business Brief -- POTOMAC ELECTRIC POWER CO.: Gas-Distribution Networks In Netherlands Are Bought   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1999  A Potomac Electric Power Co unit said it bought 21 gas-transmission and distribution networks in the Netherlands from four Dutch municipal-owned entities for about $724 million. According to SEC filings and company officials, Potomac Capital Investment put up about $11 million and will finance the rest through third-party lenders.
