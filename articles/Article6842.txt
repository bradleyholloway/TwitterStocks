 Traveling for Care -- in the U.S.; Employers Set Deals For Workers Without Having to Go Abroad   By Knight, Victoria E, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 2008  Launched earlier this year, Healthplace America is targeting self- insured employers like Hannaford that are looking to achieve the cost savings of medical tourism without the need to leave the U.S. The company, based in Lisle, Ill., offers access to a specialty network of U.S. hospitals for procedures, such as hip and knee replacements, coronary artery bypasses and spine surgery.
