RATTNER--Henriette Judels, 100, passed away May 6 in her Greenwich Village home. Beloved widow of David, mother of Nina (Bill Gelbart), grandmother of Matthew (Petra) and Eva (Jason), great-grandmother of Patrik and Julia, legendary teacher and beautiful artist, her radiance will be sorely missed. A celebration of her life will be held Wednesday May 19 at 1pm at The National Arts Club, 15 Gramercy Park South, Manhattan. Donations in her memory may be sent to Doctors Without Borders, or to www.visitingneighbors.org.
