 Cisco earnings leap 54%, in line with expectations   By Gomes, Lee, The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 1997  Cisco Systems Inc shook off a slowdown in a key overseas market to post solid third-quarter results that were in line with analysts' expectations. The San Jose CA computer networking company said net income for the period ended Apr 26, 1997 rose 54% to $378.3 million, or 55 cents a share, from $245.6 million, or 37 cents a share, a year ago.
