 Appeals Court Reinstates Keating's State Convictions   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1998  The 9th U.S. Circuit Court of Appeals threw out Mr. Keating's appeal on procedural grounds that he raised an issue in his federal-court appeal that he hadn't mentioned in an earlier state-court appeal. The appeal could be revived after Mr. Keating's lawyers return to the state courts, the panel said.
