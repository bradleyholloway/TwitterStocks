 Stocks Close Mixed in Light Trading; Nasdaq Composite Index Gains 0.22%   By Pettit, Dave, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1991  OTC stocks finished mixed on Sep 9, 1991 as the Jewish New Year holiday left volume extremely light. The Nasdaq Composite Index fluctuated within a narrow range throughout the session before closing with a gain of 1.13, or 0.22%, to 518.07.
