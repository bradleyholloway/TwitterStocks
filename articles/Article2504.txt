 World wire: Pakistan will tax landlords   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 20, 1993  Pakistan's caretaker prime minister, Moeen Qureshi, announced sweeping reforms, including taxes on wealthy landlords, in order to resuscitate an economy crippled by corruption, mismanagement and maneuvering by the wealthy. Qureshi also pledged to press ahead with Pakistan's privatization program.
