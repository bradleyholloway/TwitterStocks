 New PT Astra International chairman takes center stage with lots of fanfare   By Borsuk, Richard, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 1997  Bankers and securities analysts piled into PT Astra International's news conference on Feb 19, 1997 for a glimpse of the Indonesian auto assembler's new chairman, Mohamad "Bob" Hasan. The news conference was a kind of coming-out party for the 65-year-old Hasan.
