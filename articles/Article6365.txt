 Federal Express Nears Alliance With French Postal System Unit   By By Alessandra Losciale, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 2000  [La Poste] already has a partnership with Dutch carrier TNT, but it expires in December, people close to the deal said. It also has an alliance with Chronopost Spain, a unit of the Spanish post office, and in April acquired Germany's DPD Militzer+Munch, which specializes in company-to-company parcels, and is Europe's second-ranked operator in the sector. And it's in talks over alliances or acquisitions in countries that include Greece, Portugal, and Italy, these people said.
