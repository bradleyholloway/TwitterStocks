 Coca-Cola Separates Its European Business Into Two Divisions   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 21, 2000  Coca-Cola Co. said it is dividing its European business into two divisions to devote greater local-management muscle to an area where the soft-drink company has stumbled recently.  A West Europe group will be based in Madrid and will be run by Jose Nunez Cervera, who was president of Coca-Cola's Iberian division. A Central Europe and Eurasia Group, based in Vienna, will be headed by Cem M. Kozlu, who had been responsible for Coca-Cola's business in Turkey, the Caucasus and Central Asia.
