 Upstart phone companies in Europe make connection with U.S. investors   By Lavin, Douglas, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 21, 1997  Colt Telecom Group PLC is sending survey crews into Paris's sewers to map out a 22-mile fiber-optic ring that will allow it to begin to challenge France Telecom SA. Colt was created by a venture-capital arm of Fidelity Investments, the US mutual-fund giant.
