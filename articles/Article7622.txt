 Noel Industries Plans A Private Placement Of Debt and Warrants   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 1987  Noel Industries Inc. proposed a private placement that could give an investor group a 16% stake in the company.  The group is led by James M. Stuart, a self-employed business consultant and investment banker. Mr. Stuart will become a director and vice chairman of Noel, a maker and importer of apparel.  The placement would raise $900,000. In addition, Leon Ruchlamer, Noel's president and chief executive officer, loaned the company $300,000.
