 U.S. News: California Thirst Quenched --- Snow, Rain Boost Farmers, Ski Resorts After Drought, as Some Areas Mop Up   By Carlton, Jim, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 2011  After three years of debilitating drought, California appears headed into a second straight wet year, giving a boost to ski resorts, farms and communities that languished during the dry spell. Since California's rainy season began Oct. 1, the state has received roughly double the mountain snowfall it normally gets, according to state estimates.
