 Internet 'Roadshows' For Private Offerings Get SEC Approval   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 1998  The Securities and Exchange Commission has approved Internet-based "roadshows" in the burgeoning market for large private securities offerings to institutional investors.  In a letter Jan. 30, the SEC said it would permit Net Roadshow Inc. to distribute on-line recordings of the oral promotions by companies and underwriters for securities issued under SEC Rule 144a.
