 Mitsubishi Bank Ltd. is acquiring control of Nippon Trust Bank   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1994  Japanese commercial bank Mitsubishi Bank Ltd, which owns a 5% stake in Nippon Trust Bank Ltd, will accept 100 billion yen ($996 million) in new shares to acquire a majority stake in the problem-plagued affiliate. Nippon Trust's market capitalization will more than double in the deal.
