 Chairman Sheds Fruit of the Loom Stock   By Peers, Alexandra, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 1990  William Farley, chairman of Fruit of the Loom Inc, has sold $7.3 million of stock in the Chicago-based underwear, socks and sweatshirt firm since Mar 1990, according to the SEC. Filings indicate that Farley plans to sell as much as $3.9 million more of the company's stock.
