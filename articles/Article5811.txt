 U.S. considers some sanction against China   By Greenberger, Robert S, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1996  The Clinton administration is considering sanctions against China for violating antinuclear proliferation treaty commitments by shipping nuclear weapons-making equipment to Pakistan. There are concerns that even one of the mildest actions under discussion--imposing penalties and then waiving them--still could disrupt Sino-US trade.
