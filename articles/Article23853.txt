 Corporate News: Electric Cars Fall Short in Mini Test   By Ramsey, Mike, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 2010  Nissan is building a battery plant and retrofitting an assembly plant to produce the Leaf in Tennessee in hopes of selling 150,000 vehicles a year starting in 2013 in the U.S. Mark Perry, who leads Nissan's product planning in North America, stands by the 100-mile estimate while acknowledging the actual range will vary depending on how people drive and other factors, such as the weather.
