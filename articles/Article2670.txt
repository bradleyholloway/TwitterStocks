 Top exporters of cars, light trucks to ship 25% more from U.S. this year   By Templin, Neal, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 1994  The five leading exporters of cars and light trucks made in the US and Canada are expected to ship at least 465,000 vehicles in 1994, up 25% from 1993, making the surging exports of light vehicles one of the bright spots in a generally dismal trade picture.
