 Reuters Arranges Direct Link of Data To Its Customers   By By Jared Sandberg, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1994  Reuters Holdings PLC unveiled plans for a suite of on-line services that will cater to niche corporate and consumer markets, aiming to expand its role as an information retailer by offering its first direct link to consumers.  London-based Reuters previously has sold wire services to newspapers and offered stories on-line to such commercial services as America Online Inc. and CompuServe Inc., a unit of H&R Block Co. Now the company's New Media division plans to expand beyond the on-line purveyors with a new electronic newspaper for students at home, a service for selling television advertising via on-line orders and a new on-line offering of entertainment news.
