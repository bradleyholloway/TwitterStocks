 GM Will Close Plant In Norwood, Ohio, For a Week in January   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1986  GM, the only domestic auto maker to report a decrease in production this week, said the plant is expected to remain closed for one week, with 3,500 workers laid off. The Norwood plant is one of nine plants targeted by GM for permanent closing during the next few years. The near-term production cuts are designed to help GM avoid another round of incentives that could have a negative effect on the company's future earnings.
