 Chinese Firms Using Ads More, Or Are They?   By By Michael Flagg, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 2001  So executives at companies that buy TV time and newspaper space for advertisers are skeptical that the numbers are anywhere near as large as ACNielsen estimates. Even ACNielsen says it is measuring differently this year. Last year's growth was probably closer to the 14% recently estimated by ad buyer Zenith Media. Still, the market is definitely continuing to grow, executives say. In 1998, China surpassed South Korea as Asia's second-largest advertising market. Japan, at more than $50 billion last year, is the world's second-largest market after the U.S.
