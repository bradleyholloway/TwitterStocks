 U.S. News: Bernanke Warns Lawmakers on Deficit   By Hilsenrath, Jon; Blackstone, Brian, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 2009  Federal Reserve Chairman Ben Bernanke warned Congress and the White House that the U.S. economy will suffer if they don't move soon to rein in the federal budget deficit, which the Fed chief blamed for helping to push long-term interest rates higher.
