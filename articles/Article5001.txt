 Air Canada slashes jobs, cuts executive ranks, widening its first-period loss   By Chipello, Christopher J, The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 1993  Struggling to restore its financial health, Air Canada cut three of its 18 executives and named Jean-Jacques Bourgeault, executive vice president, marketing and sales, to the new post of COO. In addition, the airline provided for the elimination of another 1,000 jobs in 1993.
