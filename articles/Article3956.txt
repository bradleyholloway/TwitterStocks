 Political, Media Animals Analyzed in Psych 101   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 2005  When the animal announces the possibility of a windfall tax on oil companies, or legal action against gasoline retailers, he is, metaphorically, pecking at a button that, when pushed, opens a chute and releases the political equivalent of a grain of corn. The animal's own learned behavior produces a reward -- hordes of reporters at his press conference.
