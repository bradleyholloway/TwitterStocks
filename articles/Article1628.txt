 Harsco Unit Receives Contract   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 1992  BMY-Combat Systems, a division of Harsco Corp, has been awarded a contract by an unidentified country for orders valued at more than $106 million for the co-production of artillery and combat support systems. The Harsco unit will supply sub-assemblies and certain parts in kit form to an overseas customer who will use local labor sources to assemble the finished product.
