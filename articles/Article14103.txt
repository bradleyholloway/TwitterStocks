 Bullet Pierces Window At Justice's Apartment   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1985  A spokesman for the Federal Bureau of Investigation said a 9-mm bullet was fired through a window in the high-rise apartment building in Rosslyn, Va., across the Potomac River from Washington. Both Justice Blackmun and his wife, Dorothy, were home at the time, after 10 p.m., but neither was hurt. The bullet, which the spokesman said was fired from a handgun, was found embedded in a chair.
