 Hong Kong Officials Move to Restore Confidence after Runs on Two Banks   By Owens, Cynthia, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1991  Two runs on major foreign banks in Hong Kong, the US's Citibank and Britain's Standard Chartered Bank, left financial officials and banking executives scrambling to restore depositors' confidence in the banking sector and announce an investigation into the rumors that caused the runs.
