 Last Chance To Undo Roth   By Ensign, Rachel Louise, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 2012  After 30 days, you can re-convert the traditional IRA into a Roth--this time with a tax bill based on the lower, current value of the portfolio, says Ed Slott, an IRA expert in Rockville Centre, N.Y. Some people may want to undo a conversion because it has resulted in a big tax bill that put too much strain on their finances, says Jeremy Kisner, a certified financial planner in Phoenix.
