 Business Brief -- EQUITY RESIDENTIAL PROPERTIES TRUST: Operations Funds Jump; Apartment Portfolio Grows   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 1997  Equity Residential Properties Trust said 1997 third-quarter funds from operations rose 90%. Earnings were $47.2 million, or 42 cents a share, up from $19.6 million, or 29 cents a share, a year ago.
