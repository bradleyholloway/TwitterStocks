 Dayton Hudson Says It Plans to Change Name to Target Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 2000  Dayton Hudson Corp. disclosed plans to change its name to Target Corp., acknowledging that the meteoric growth of its Target subsidiary means that the Target name now "best reflects our business and our company."  The retailer said the name change will become effective on Jan. 31, the first day of the company's new fiscal year. Dayton Hudson shares, which trade on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol DH, will begin trading then under symbol TGT.
