 Family Finances: Home-Equity Credit Lines Have Downsides   By Ruth Simon and Kaja Whitehouse, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2003  The rate isn't the only thing to consider. Some lenders charge application fees on a line of credit and an annual fee, typically $50 to $75, to keep the line open. Borrowers who don't draw down their lines may face a "nonusage" fee, typically $50. Homeowners who pay off the line within the first three years may also be hit with an early termination fee of $250 to $600.
