 Commercial Property: Stamford Steps It Up   By Li, Roland, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 2012  The vacancy rate of the city's 1.7 million square feet of retail space is below the national average of about 10%, according to Reis Inc., a real-estate data firm. Over the years, the city attracted numerous big-name companies including GTE Corp., a predecessor to Verizon Communications Inc., Royal Bank of Scotland Group and UBS.
