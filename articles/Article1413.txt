 International Brief -- Snecma: Despite a Decline, Profit Will Be Posted for 1990   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1991  Societe Nationale d'Etudes & de Construction de Moteurs d'Aviation of France, the aerospace-engine maker known as Snecma, said it will post 1990 profit of 50 million francs to 100 million francs ($9.6 million to $19.2 million), despite the trade embargo against Iraq and the depreciation of the dollar.
