 Exit Polls Show Estrada Is Leading In Philippine Vote   By By Jon Liden, The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1998  MANILA, Philippines -- Early exit polls showed Joseph Estrada ahead of all other candidates in Monday's presidential elections in the Philippines.  Business groups had opposed Mr. Estrada, fearing that he would dismantle the economic progress made by the current president, Fidel Ramos. The leadership of the Roman Catholic Church in the Philippines had also criticized Mr. Estrada, a former movie star, as a poor role model.
