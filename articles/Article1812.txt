 Air France Presents Unions With Goals For a Turnaround   By By Brian Coleman, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 1993  Air France Chairman Christian Blanc met with union leaders yesterday and put forward a set of goals he hopes will turn around the airline, which expects to lose 7.5 billion French francs ($1.29 billion) this year.  Among the most eye-catching ideas in Mr. Blanc's 13-page paper is a proposal that highly paid employees agree to salary reductions in exchange for an equity stake in the airline.
