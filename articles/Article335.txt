Viewers in New York were unable to see the last 20 minutes of Dallas's 17-14 victory over Washington yesterday because Channel 5, the Fox affiliate, showed ''World's Funniest!'' Channel 5 had televised the Cardinals-Giants game from 1-4 P.M., but had the option to return at 7 P.M. for the end of Dallas's victory.  ''Channel 5 was the only Fox station in the Eastern and Central time zones not to show the game,'' a Fox spokesman said. Channel 5 officials could not be reached for comment.
