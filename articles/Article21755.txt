 In Sex Survey, Britons Claim Parity with French   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1992  According to two comprehensive sex surveys of the French and the British by British journal Nature, the two peoples lead relatively similar sex lives. The studies indicated that the average French man has 11 lovers in his lifetime while a British man averages 9.9. A typical French women has 3.3 lovers during her lifetime, compared with 3.4 for British women.
