 Business Brief -- REPUBLIC INDUSTRIES INC.: Two Car-Dealership Groups To Be Acquired for Stock   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1997  Republic Industries Inc, continuing its spree of automotive dealership acquisitions, said it agreed to buy two Houston auto sellers for a total of $75 million in stock. Republic said it is buying Champion Planning Inc, a group of dealerships with annual revenue of $230 million, and Mike Hall Chevrolet-Geo, with revenue of $80 million.
