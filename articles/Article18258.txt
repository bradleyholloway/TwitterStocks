 Honda Expects Profit Increase to Top Forecast   By Ingrassia, Paul, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1991  Honda Motor Corp expects earnings for the fiscal year ending Mar 31, 1992 to exceed the 2% increase previously forecast. For the fiscal year ended Mar 31, 1991, Honda reported net income of 76.3 billion yen ($578 million) on sales of Y4.3 trillion. Higher earnings by Honda would be in contrast to the slump in auto sales noted by the US, Europe and Japan.
