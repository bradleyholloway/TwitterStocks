 American Medical International Plans To Sell 12 Hospitals   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1989  BEVERLY HILLS, Calif. -- American Medical International Inc. said it plans to sell 12 of its general-service and psychiatric hospitals as part of a plan to sell about $1 billion in assets following its acquisition by IMA Holdings Corp.  However, American Medical said it already has a definitive agreement to sell Glendora Community Hospital in Glendora, Calif. and has tentative agreements to sell Pembroke Hospital in Pembroke, Mass., and Westwood Lodge Hospital in Norwood, Mass. In all, the 12 hospitals had total revenue of about $229 million for the fiscal year ended Aug. 31.
