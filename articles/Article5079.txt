 Advanced Micro posts 40% drop in 4th-quarter net   By Rigdon, Joan E, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 1994  Advanced Micro Devices Inc said net income for the quarter ended Dec 26, 1993 was $41.6 million on sales of $413.4 million, up 3.2% from $400.2 million in the year-ago period. Net was $69.6 million in the year-ago period. Advanced has been hurt by eroding margins on its outmoded 386 chips and legal costs from battling Intel Corp.
