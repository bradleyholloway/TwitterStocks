Eastern  A weak cold front draped across the Great Lakes this afternoon is causing a bit of rain and drizzle from the Upper Peninsula of Michigan to western New York state. Another area of light rain and drizzle is found across parts of the Plains. Much of the rest of the nation is experiencing dry conditions this afternoon. Gusty winds are howling through the Rocky Mountain states; wind gusts of 50-60 mph have been reported across Montana, Wyoming and Colorado so far today. A strong southwesterly breeze blowing from Texas into the Ohio Valley is transporting milder air into the East this afternoon. Snow covered portions of Pennsylvania and New York state are warming well into the upper 30s to mid-40s. There's been a break from the soaking rains along the Pacific Northwest coast. Although, a shower or two are found on parts of the coast, it's nothing like the deluge of the past five days. High pressure over Nevada is responsible for warm conditions across the Desert Southwest where temperatures are warming into the 70s and 80s.
