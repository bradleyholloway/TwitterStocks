Eastern  A storm system in the Plains brought rain to many areas in this region this morning and this afternoon. As colder air worked in behind the system, some of the rain mixed with and changed to snow on the northern and western side of the storm in Nebraska, northwestern Kansas, northwestern Iowa, southeastern Minnesota and northwestern Wisconsin. The heaviest rain was found in central and eastern Kansas this morning. Another storm system was just off the Pacific Northwest coastline and it brought rain and mountain snow to western Washington, western Oregon, and northern California this morning and this afternoon. Farther eastward, some flurries fell in the mountains of northern Nevada. A large area of high pressure dominated the weather along the Eastern Seaboard on Saturday morning and Saturday afternoon along with mild temperatures. Temperatures in the mid-Atlantic along with southern and central New England were climbing into the 40s and 50s this afternoon. High pressure also dominated the weather in the northern Great Plains this afternoon along with chilly temperatures.
