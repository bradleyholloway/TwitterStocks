Eastern  A cold front moved across the Great Lakes and Ohio Valley bringing a few showers and thunderstorms. It remained dry and tranquil across the Northeast; however, it was another very warm and humid day with hazy sunshine. A tropical air mass remained in place across the Southeast allowing for a very warm and humid day with popup showers and thunderstorms. A complex of thunderstorms which developed last night over east-central Texas remained over the same area today producing more showers and thunderstorms. This area received a tremendous amount of rain last night and more rain falling today on top of what fell last night only exacerbated some of the flooding already occurring. A frontal boundary moving across the northern Plains produced a few showers and thunderstorms. Monsoonal moisture continued to feed into the Rockies allowing for more showers and thunderstorms during the afternoon. Sunny and nice weather dominated the West Coast.
