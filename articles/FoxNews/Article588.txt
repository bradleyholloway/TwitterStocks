Eastern  There is not a lot of unsettled weather occurring across the nation this afternoon. In fact, high pressure centered over the Great Basin is promoting bright sunshine from the central Rockies and the Desert Southwest eastward into the central and southern Plains. The only unsettled weather to be found across the nation is some rain across western Washington, light snow across northern New England, and a few sprinkles and flurries across the eastern Great Lakes. The Midwest remains shrouded in a blanket of low clouds this afternoon. Without any sunshine, temperatures are being held in the 20s across Minnesota and northern Wisconsin with 30s over the rest of the Midwest. Meanwhile, sunshine is abundant across the Southeast in the wake of a cold front. Despite the sunshine, temperatures are running much cooler than at the same time yesterday across Mississippi, Alabama and Georgia.
