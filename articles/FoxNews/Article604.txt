Eastern  The large storm that brought rain to much of the Northeast and wet snow to the Ohio Valley and Great Lakes is now across eastern Ontario. Colder air will continue to spread into the Northeast through tonight on strong west winds. Lake-effect snow will increase downwind of lakes Erie and Ontario through tonight and into Monday. Heavy rain will taper off later tonight across eastern Maine. The storm which brought the snow to the central Rockies last night has weakened and will cause some light snow and flurries across the central Plains into tonight. Rain will break out later tonight from southern Arkansas to northern Mississippi as another storm tracks quickly through the region. The West Coast will have a drying trend which will persist through much of the week as a ridge of high pressure builds in.
