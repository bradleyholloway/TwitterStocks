 McKesson Corp.: Distributor reports net fell 14% in fiscal second quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 21, 1994  McKesson Corp said net income for its fiscal second quarter ended Sep 30, 1994 fell 14% to $25.5 million from $29.7 million a year earlier. However, earnings from continuing operations, excluding a one-time loss, rose 5%.
