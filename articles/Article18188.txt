 In Global Buying Frenzy, Prices Soar in Tokyo and Frankfurt, Approach a Record in London   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1991  An explosive rally spread across world stock markets on Apr 3, 1991, propelling equities sharply higher. The Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-share index jumped 30.8 points to 2519.1, as the index flirted with record levels. The Nikkei index of 225 issues soared 528.06 to 26780.06 and the DAX index added 38.88 points to 1577.50.
