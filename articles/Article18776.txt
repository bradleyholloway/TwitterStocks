 Dia Met Minerals Ltd.: Lawsuit settlement is set; Stock price advances 16%   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1996  Dia Met Minerals Ltd of Canada said it agreed to settle a lawsuit brought by Sand River Resources Inc by issuing 350,000 Class A shares.
