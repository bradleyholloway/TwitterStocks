 Letters to the Editor: 'Shameful' U.S. Trade Barrier   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 1989  Despite protests by domestic sugar growers (letters to the editor, Jan. 19), the Journal is correct in its criticisms of the U.S. sugar subsidy and quota program (editorial, Dec. 19). Our nation's sugar policy does damage the economies of allies trying to trade with the U.S.
