 Actually, It's More Like a Bank and a Whimper   By Templin, Neal, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 1991  Ford Motor Co has just started a nationwide program asking customers who bring their ailing Fords, Lincolns and Mercurys in for service to fill out a questionnaire describing their car's symptoms. The questionnaire lists 22 different car noises, ranging from whirs to whistles and provides definitions of the sounds.
