 Netbooks Come Into Their Own   By Mossberg, Walter S, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 2008  Somewhere between the laptop and the smart phone, the computer industry has long believed there could be a small, low-cost device that would please consumers and sell well. Compared with even an amazingly powerful pocket device, like the iPhone, the Google G1, or the forthcoming BlackBerry Storm, a netbook, at about twice the price, offers a much larger keyboard and screen.
