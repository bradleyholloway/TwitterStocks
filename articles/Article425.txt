Carnegie Hall  The pianist Martha Argerich, the violinist Gidon Kremer and the cellist Mischa Maisky occasionally join forces for chamber music concerts. But their collaborations, either as a trio or in various combinations, have taken place mostly in Europe and on recordings, rarely in the United States. Carnegie Hall redressed that imbalance, at least for the moment, when it brought the three players together for a program of piano trios on Saturday evening.
