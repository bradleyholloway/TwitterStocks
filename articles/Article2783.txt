 Letters to the Editor: Trauma Ward Films Aren't `Candid Camera'   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 2000  On behalf of the Medical Center of Louisiana's Charity Hospital of New Orleans, I'm responding to William C. Schumacher's Oct. 13 editorial-page essay "Lights! Camera! Blood! Action!" Our hospital was featured in recent productions of The Learning Channel's series "Trauma: Life in the ER" -- one of which won an Emmy for TLC and the producers, NYT Television -- and its "Maternity Ward" series.
