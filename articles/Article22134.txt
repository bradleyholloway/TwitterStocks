 Forest Oil Acquires Atcor Resources Ltd., Closes Stock Offering   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 2, 1996  Atcor's exploration and production operations will be operated as a subsidiary of Forest Oil and will be renamed Canadian Forest Oil Ltd. Atcor's gas marketing operation, which sells into Canadian and U.S. markets and is one of the five largest independent marketing companies in Canada, will continue to operate independently. It will be renamed Producers Marketing Inc.
