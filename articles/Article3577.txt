 Williams Cos.: Firm to Pay $290 Million To Settle Shareholder Suit   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 2006  Williams Cos. will pay $290 million to settle a class-action shareholder lawsuit concerning accounting practices related to energy trading and potential liability associated with Williams Communications Group Inc., a former unit of Williams. The settlement covers investors who purchased Williams Cos. securities between July 24, 2000, and July 22, 2002.
