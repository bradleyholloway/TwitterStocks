 Balanced budget plan approved by Senate panel   By Georges, Christopher; Calmes, Jackie, The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1995  After brushing aside a succession of Democrat-supported efforts to wipe out billions of dollars of tax breaks for large corporations, Senate Republicans sent a balanced-budget plan to the floor. The proposal was approved May 11, 1995 by the Senate Budget Committee along party lines, 12-10.
