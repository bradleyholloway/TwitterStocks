The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 2001  Following several high-profile account losses, the New York office of Omnicom Group's DDB yesterday issued pink slips to as much as 10% of its 450 employees. An agency spokeswoman cited the softening economy for the decision. The office has been on a roller-coaster ride with its accounts. In early 2000, it was slow to win new business but ended the year on a positive note by landing Hasbro.
