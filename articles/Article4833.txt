 U.S. Closes 5 Banks In Louisiana, Texas   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 11, 1987  The closed banks were Heritage National Bank, Austin, Texas, with assets of $50 million; BancFirst-Austin, Austin, with assets of $23.7 million; First National Bank in Rhome, Rhome, Texas, with assets of $9.4 million; Louisiana Bank & Trust Co., Crowley, La., with assets of $17.9 million; and American Bank in Louisiana, Morgan City, La., with assets of $16.4 million.
