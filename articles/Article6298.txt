 Business Brief -- SIEBEL SYSTEMS INC.: Software Is Bought by IBM To Be Deployed Widely   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 11, 2000  IBM Corp is buying software from Siebel Systems Inc for a widespread deployment at the computer maker, one of the largest purchases of its kind to date. A suite of Siebel business applications is expected to be used by 55,000 IBM employees, 30,000 of its business partners, and millions of customers who tap into IBM Web sites, the companies said.
