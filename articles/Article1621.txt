 International Brief -- Banco Popular: Spanish Bank's Profit Rose By 19% In First Half of 1992   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 1992  Spain's Banco Popular said net consolidated profit climbed 19% in the first half of 1992 to 27.83 billion pesetas ($290 million) from 23.41 billion pesetas a year earlier.  Operating profit for the Madrid-based bank rose 4.2% to 53.99 billion pesetas, up from 51.82 billion pesetas last year.
