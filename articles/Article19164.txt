 Business Brief -- HURRICANE HYDROCARBONS LTD.: Missed Interest Payment Spurs Downgrade by S&P   The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 1999  Hurricane Hydrocarbons Ltd said it failed to make a semiannual interest payment due May 1, 1999 on its senior unsecured notes, prompting Standard & Poors to assign it a credit rating normally reserved for bankrupt companies.
