 Goodrich Posts Gains In Operating Profit And Net for Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1988  AKRON, Ohio -- B.F.Goodrich Co. reported that third-quarter profit from continuing operations more than doubled to $68.8 million, or $2.64 a share, from $28.3 million, or $1.11 a share, aided by gains in its chemical and aerospace businesses.  Net income increased 44% to $60.8 million, or $2.32 a share. The figure includes a loss of $8 million from discontinued operations, which comprise the company's industrial products businesses and share of Uniroyal Goodrich Tire Co.
