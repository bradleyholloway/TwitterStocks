 Business Brief -- TXU Corp.: Number of Officers Being Cut By 30% to Help Reduce Costs   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 2003  TXU Corp. is reducing the number of its corporate officers by about 30% as part of a cost-cutting program. The Dallas energy company will trim the number of officers to 70 from around 100, it said, adding that most of the reductions had already been made.
