 Business Brief -- SYMANTEC CORP.: Charge Pushed Net Down 26% in Fiscal 3rd Period   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1999  Symantec Corp posted a 9% rise in fiscal third-quarter operating income, though net profits fell 26% because of an acquisition charge. The software company reported net income in the period ended Dec 31, 1998 of $16.2 million, or 28 cents a diluted share, including a one-time charge for its acquisition in October of Quarterdeck Corp.
