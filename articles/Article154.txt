 Furniture Store Is Enticing Shoppers with Wild Rides   By Alexander, Suzanne, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 25, 1992  In today's sluggish economy, many retailers have turned to cross merchandising to lure in customers. Jordan Furniture Co, owned by Barry and Eliot Tatelman, is one such company that has invested $2.5 million in a marketing gimmick known as the Motion Odyssey Movie in order to boost sales.
