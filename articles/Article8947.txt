 Recent Stock-Listing Changes   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 7, 1998  Among recent stock-listing changes, Chilgener SA ADS, trading on the NYSE, is changing its name to Gener SA ADS, using the same symbol, CHR. Key Energy Group Inc, trading on the American Stock Exchange, is now trading on the NYSE under the same symbol, KEG. Teletouch Communications Inc, trading on the NYSE, is now trading on the American Stock Exchange under the new symbol, TLL.
