 Motorola to cut wireless data staff 20% in latest blow to hand-held computers   By Hardy, Quentin, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 1995  Motorola Corp is shrinking its wireless data group staff by about 20%, or about 180 employees, because of poor consumer demand for its much-touted hand-held computers known as personal digital assistants. The move is another blow to the PDA, which were once hyped as the next big thing in computerdom.
