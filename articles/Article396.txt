Jean Outland Chrysler, wife of Walter P. Chrysler Jr., the founder and chairman of the board of the Chrysler Museum in Norfolk, Va., died Tuesday at Norfolk Ge neral Hospital.  She was 60 years old and lived in Norfolk and New York City.  She was a graduate of the College of William and Mary, taught physical education at the college's Norfolk division and was a breeder and exhibitor of champion long-hair Chihuahuas from her kennels at Warrenton, Va.  Besides her husband, she is survived by two sisters, Louise O. Smith and Nancy O. Schandler, and a brother, Grover Cleveland Outland Jr., all of Norfolk.
