 Richard Lesher to leave U.S. Chamber of Commerce   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 25, 1997  Richard L. Lesher said he will step down as president of the US Chamber of Commerce in 1997 after 22 years in the post. During his tenure, the chamber built TV facilities from which it broadcasts weekly and daily shows and chartered the National Chamber Litigation Center, which represents business interests in court.
