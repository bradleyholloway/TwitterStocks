 Amfesco Industries Expects to End Soon Its Chapter 11 Status   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 1988  Amfesco Industries Inc. expects to emerge from more than two years of Chapter 11 bankruptcy proceedings next week as a reorganized business called New American Shoe Co.  Under a reorganization plan confirmed by the U.S. Bankruptcy Court in New York, New American Shoe will be headed by Robert Campbell, whose $20 million investment in the company is partly funding the plan.
