 AT&T Moves to Ease Tensions With IBM Over Ending Service   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 1996  AT&T officials said the agreement, which was expected, doesn't reverse AT&T's decision several weeks ago to end its proprietary Network Notes service. That major collaborative effort between AT&T and IBM's Lotus Development Corp. ended after AT&T decided to scrap the proprietary network service in favor of a broader Internet-service strategy for its business customers.
