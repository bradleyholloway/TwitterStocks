 Letters to the Editor: No Hard Evidence PCBs Cause Cancer   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 2001  As for Dr. [David Carpenter]'s concern about the presence of PCBs in breast milk, merely because a substance is detectable in human tissue does not mean it is a hazard to health. Indeed we can now document the presence of substances down to parts per trillion concentration, and even less. If companies were compelled to expunge from the environment every molecule of any chemical that was measurable at trace levels in humans and "might" be "linked" to disease, our nation would be in a state of paralysis.
