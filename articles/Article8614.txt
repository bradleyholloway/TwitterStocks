 MathSoft Inc.: Firm puts 4th-quarter loss at narrower than year ago   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 1995  MathSoft Inc said it expects to report a net loss of around $650,000 to $750,000 for its fourth quarter ended Jun 30, 1995. That would be narrower than the year-earlier loss of $1.4 million.
