 Letters to the Editor: Distribution Cost of 30% For Autos Is Exaggerated   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 2003  In his Oct. 28 Manager's Journal column "Next Exit, the Auto Megastore," J.D. Power indicts the U.S. auto retail industry, stating that consumers pay "a hefty price for artificial and anachronistic controls." He says the distribution cost is already 30% and could increase in the future.
