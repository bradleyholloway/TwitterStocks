 Southwest Air's Suit Says Travel Web Site Misleads Consumers   The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 2001  Southwest also alleges Orbitz publishes out-of-the-way itineraries that don't meet Southwest customer-service standards and could anger customers. A Southwest itinerary constructed by Orbitz from Albany, N.Y., to Baltimore had a stop in Orlando, Fla., Southwest says. "That makes no geographic sense," said Ron Ricks, the airline's vice president of governmental affairs.
