 Nascent Drive To Ban Quotas Gains Support   By By Pat Beall, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1997  A fledgling effort to duplicate California's successful 1996 ballot vote banning affirmative action is drawing early support from a group of Florida professors, graduate students and administrators.  "We're very receptive to the idea of trying to do something in Florida with regard to eliminating racial quotas and preferences, particularly in higher education," says Thomas R. Dye, a professor of government at Florida State University and president of the Florida Association of Scholars, a 100-member state chapter of the National Association of Scholars, based in Princeton, N.J.
