 Lehman Brothers, Bank of America set bridge-loan fund   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 1995  Lehman Bros Inc and Bank of America raised $600 million for a fund that will offer interim financing for leveraged acquisitions, recapitalizations and management-led buyouts. The plan, to be announced Oct 10, 1995, is one of the first times a commercial bank has teamed up with an investment bank to offer a "bridge-loan" fund.
