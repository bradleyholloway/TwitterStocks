 Mexico unveils program of harsh fiscal medicine   By Carroll, Paul B; Torres, Craig, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 1995  The Mexican government announced a fiscal austerity package that appeared likely to restore some investor confidence in the country and its troubled currency. The plan was unveiled after the Mexican peso plummeted 6.2% to 7.45 pesos per dollar on Mar 9, 1995, its fifth consecutive closing low.
