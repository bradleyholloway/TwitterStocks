 REVIEW --- Spring Books -- Children's Books: Sojourn Into Surrealism   By Meghan Cox Gurdon, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 2012  Frank W. Dormer's cheerful, screwball illustrations take any sting out of the pen's adventures as it makes its way from irate Uncle Flood ("You have a big nose," the pen tells him, when he tries to write, "The following story is all true") to Police Officer Wonkle ("You are a yam-headed organ grinder!") to the little boy named Horace we've been rooting for all along.
