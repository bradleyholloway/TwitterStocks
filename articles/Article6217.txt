 Details: Kitchen Couture   By By Rebecca Lowell, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 11, 1999  Professional chef uniforms are the latest restaurant gear being co-opted by amateurs who want to look more authentic in their own home kitchens. Even fashion designer Nicole Miller, famous for her bright and busy neckties, has just introduced a line of aprons, oven mitts and toques.  A month ago, jazz singer Lainie Cooke bought a white executive-chef jacket, a pair of Nicole Miller western-theme chef pants and mitt for $100. "If I wear it all together, I feel like an impostor," says Ms. Cooke, who wears the pants while cooking in her Manhattan apartment.
