 Letters to the Editor: Use Your Head About Helmets   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1997  I do not need some bean counter who has never sat on a motorcycle to tell me how to dress. I have ridden one million safe miles and want a little input on this helmet issue. I have about half of my miles with, and about half without, a helmet. Let me tell you, please, what a helmet will do for you on a bike.
