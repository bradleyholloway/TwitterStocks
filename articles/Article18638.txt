 Inside track: Union Carbide's shares are gobbled up by insiders   By Getler, Warren, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1995  Insiders at Union Carbide Corp, the top performer in 1994 among the 30 stocks that make up the Dow Jones Industrial Average, seem to be betting that the stock will continue to climb. Recent SEC filings show that three corporate insiders at the company bought a combined 6,500 shares in Nov 1994 at $27.25 to $34 a share.
