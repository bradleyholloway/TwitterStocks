 Business Brief -- Navistar International Inc.: Profit of $8 Million Posted For Fiscal Second Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  May 21, 1993  Navistar International Inc. said it earned $8 million, or one cent a share, in its second quarter ended April 30, compared with a year-earlier loss of $35 million, or 17 cents a share, reflecting stronger demand.
