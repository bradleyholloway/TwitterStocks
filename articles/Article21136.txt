 International Corporate Report: Sumitomo Life Plans to Buy Interest of 1.5% in Kleinwort   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1987  SUMITOMO LIFE INSURANCE CO. of Japan plans to buy 1.5%, or two million shares, of KLEINWORT BENSON LONSDALE PLC, the British merchant-bank holding company, for what a Sumitomo official said is expected to total about two billion yen ($14.6 million).
