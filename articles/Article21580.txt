 Dollar Rises on Jump in Factory Orders and More Talk of Troubles in Germany   By Scott, Alwyn, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 1991  The dollar increased sharply on Jul 2, 1991, reinforced by a sharp rise in US factory orders and more talk of economic troubles in Germany. In late New York trading, the dollar was quoted at 1.8356 marks and 138.84 yen, up from DM1.8285 and Y138.42 the day before.
