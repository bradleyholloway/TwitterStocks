 New Fake Fat to Be Marketed by NutraSweet   By Freedman, Alix M, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 1991  NutraSweet Co, hurrying to get its much-hyped artificial fat into the marketplace and in as many products as possible, said it will begin marketing a reformulated version of Simplesse with the FDA's blessing for use in all food categories. Announced in early 1988, Simplesse's huge lead was frittered away by a series of regulatory and marketing snafus that enabled rival fake fats to overtake the Monsanto Co unit's invention.
