 Business and Finance   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 2005  RETAILERS SAID same-store sales rose 3.6% in January, as shoppers cashed in holiday gift cards and took advantage of clearance sales. Results were mixed, though. Target exceeded expectations while Wal- Mart just missed analysts' forecasts and Gap posted disappointing sales.  France's Pernod Ricard, maker of Chivas Regal, is studying a possible takeover of Britain's Allied Domecq, known for its Beefeater gin and Stolichnaya vodka.  The CEOs of Qwest and MCI met to discuss Qwest's $6.3 billion offer. Verizon hasn't completed its analysis on whether to make a bid.
