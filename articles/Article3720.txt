 Genesee & Wyoming, Spectrum Decline, Bucking Up Trend   By Ceron, Gaston F, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 2007  Idenix Pharmaceuticals put development of a hepatitis C treatment on hold after talks with the Food and Drug Administration.
