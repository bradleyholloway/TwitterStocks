 ECC Wins $16.9 Million Contract   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 2000  ECC INTERNATIONAL Corp. received a contract from Raytheon/Lockheed Martin Javelin, a joint venture of Raytheon Co. and Lockheed Martin Corp. Based on incorrect information from ECC International, an article Tuesday said that the joint venture was with Texas Instruments Inc. and Lockheed.
