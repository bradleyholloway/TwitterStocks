 Swiss Stocks May Rise Again in 4th Quarter   By Lapreta, Laura, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 2, 1992  Swiss share prices are expected to rise further in the fourth quarter of 1992, after posting a 13% gain so far in the year. The stock prices are being buoyed by expectations of lower interest rates and a continuing decline in Swiss inflation. Investors have turned to the Swiss franc as a safe haven from the uncertainty of most currencies in the European ERM.
