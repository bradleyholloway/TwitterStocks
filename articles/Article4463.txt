 U.S. Denies Union Petition For Investigation of TWA   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 1988  The Independent Federation of Flight Attendants, which represents 9,000 TWA employees, asked the department in August to order that TWA's assets be "held intact" until after the agency investigated the airline. But the department didn't respond until yesterday, while TWA Chairman Carl Icahn had completed the process of taking the company private in late October.
