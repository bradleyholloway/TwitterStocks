 Worthington Sees 1993 Sales, Net at a Record   By Winter, Ralph E, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 20, 1992  Worthington Industries Inc, a steel and plastic products concern, predicts record sales and earnings for the fiscal year ending May 31, 1993, but pricing pressures will prohibit earnings from rising as rapidly as sales in the first half. The company said it was satisfied with analysts' 1993 earnings estimates of $1.05 to $1.15 a share.
