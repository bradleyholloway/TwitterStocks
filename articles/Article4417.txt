 Bay Area: Installers on Top In Solar Field --- Low Panel Prices Hurt Manufacturers, But Other Industry Segments Heat Up   By Sweet, Cassandra, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 2012  Individuals, businesses, or investors who buy solar-panel systems are eligible for federal tax credits equal to 30% of the cost of each system and they also qualify for state incentives and payments from their local utility for any electricity they generate that they don't use. MiaSole and Nanosolar have raised new venture capital to pursue their versions of next-generation thin-film technology, which the companies say have lower manufacturing and installation costs than conventional panels.
