 Burnham Pacific, Calpers to Acquire Shopping Centers   By By Rhonda L. Rundle, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 1999  The agreement, with a potential value of $948 million, is one of the first big deals since Calpers began restructuring its real-estate portfolio and seeking managers willing to share risk with the pension fund. Calpers will have an 80% stake in the shopping centers portfolio, while San Diego-based Burnham will have a 20% stake.
