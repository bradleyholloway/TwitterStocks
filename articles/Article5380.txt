 MGM's French owner asks for round of higher bids   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1996  Bids for legendary studio Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer Inc have come in so low that its French seller, Consortium de Realisation, is asking for new bids and may opt to take it off the market until later in 1996 if they aren't forthcoming, according to those familiar with the transaction.
