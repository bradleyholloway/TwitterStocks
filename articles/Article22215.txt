 Rank Organisation and Trump Hotels to develop Hard Rock Cafe at casino   By Orwall, Bruce, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 1996  The UK's Rank Organisation PLC is negotiating a deal to enter the US gambling business under which it would form a partnership with Trump Hotels and Casino Resorts Inc. The Trump's Castle casino in Atlantic City will be rethemed to exploit Rank's Hard Rock Cafe franchise.
