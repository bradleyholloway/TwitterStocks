 Microsoft Says Hotmail Sends Addresses to Firms   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 2000  It tends to occur when a Hotmail user reads an e-mail message or electronic newsletter that contains a banner advertisement. When users read such messages, Mr. [Richard Smith] said, their Hotmail log-in name appears in the address line of their Web browser program and can be sent to advertisers or third-party services that distribute such ads.
