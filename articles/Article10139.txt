 Delhaize SA: Supermarket Operator Posts Flat Net Profit   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 2008  Belgium-based supermarket company Delhaize SA -- operator of the Food Lion, Hannaford and Sweetbay chains in the U.S., as well as Delhaize supermarkets in Belgium -- posted a flat fourth-quarter net profit, curbed by the weak dollar.
