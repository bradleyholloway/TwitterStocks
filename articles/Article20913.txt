 Cost Overrun Figures For San Onofre Units Are to Be Reduced   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1985  The staff of the California Public Utilities Commission soon will reduce its estimate of cost overruns related to Units 2 and 3 of the San Onofre nuclear-power plant operated by Southern California Edison Co.  Commission staffers said that while calculations haven't been completed, the staff now estimates that the purported cost overruns on the facility total about $900 million. Early last month, the staff calculated that the overruns totaled about $1.1 billion. Total cost of the two units is $4.5 billion.
