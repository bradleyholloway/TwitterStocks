 Business Brief -- HOECHST AG: Hoechst Marion Roussel To Close Puerto Rico Plant   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1998  Hoechst Marion Roussel, Kansas City MO, plans to close its manufacturing plant in Manati Purerto Rico and transfer all the plant's operations to Kansas City by the end of 1998. Hoechst Marion Roussel is the pharmaceutical company of Germany's Hoechst AG.
