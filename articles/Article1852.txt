 Business Brief -- Matra-Hachette SA: Revenue for Year Fell 1.9% According to Legal Filing   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1994  French industrial and media group Matra-Hachette SA said consolidated revenue for 1993 was down 1.9%, to 54.05 billion francs ($9.08 billion) from a year-earlier 55.1 billion francs.
