 Business Brief -- Mattel Inc.: Last Manufacturing Plant In the U.S. Will Be Closed   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 2001  Mattel Inc., El Segundo, Calif., will close its last remaining U.S. manufacturing plant in Murray, Ky., in the next two years and transfer that production to Mexico. The move is part of a broader cost-cutting plan. The plant, which manufactures preschool toys for the company's Fisher-Price brand, employs 980 people. It will be gradually closed and officially shuttered by late 2002.
