 Kloeckner's First-Half Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1989  Kloeckner & Co., a trading company, said first-half sales edged up 1.2% to 5.7 billion West German marks ($3.08 billion) from 5.6 billion marks a year earlier, as the elimination of oil trading offset strong gains in other fields.
