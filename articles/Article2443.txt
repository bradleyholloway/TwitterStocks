 Business Brief -- COHO ENERGY INC.: Lower Prices Cause Loss Of $22.3 Million in Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 1998  Coho Energy Inc, citing lower oil and natural-gas prices, reported a first-quarter net loss of $22.3 million, or 87 cents a share, compared with net income of $2.1 million, or 10 cents a share, a year earlier.
