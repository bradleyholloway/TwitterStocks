 Babbage's Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 1993  Software retailer Babbage's Inc reported its net income for the first quarter ended May 1, 1993 doubled to $538,000 from $259,000 a year earlier as a result of higher demand for software. Sales climbed 24% to $46.3 million from $37.2 million.
