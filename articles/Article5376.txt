 Under Cover: Ross Perot Takes Surveillance Tactics to Unusual Lengths   By Rogers, David; Abramson, Jill, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 1992  Ross Perot, whose personal tough and can-do style has attracted a lot of attention, is also a super-cop and super-sleuth who has used his wealth and energy to build an intelligence network that goes beyond run-of-the-mill background checks and into investigations that have included videotaped surveillance of individuals, sometimes to probe for marital infidelities. Details of some of the investigations, and the impetus for initiating them, are examined.
