 Mazda Unveils Dual-Fuel Car   By Takahashi, Yoshio, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 2007  Mazda's existing hydrogen-gasoline car, released last year in Japan, is an RX-8 powered by just a rotary engine. Using an electric motor is more efficient, because it doesn't lose energy through a transmission as with a regular engine, said Akihiro Kashiwagi in Mazda's program- management division.
