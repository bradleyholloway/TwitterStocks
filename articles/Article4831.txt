 Toyota Motor Corp.: Truck Production Will Rise at Plant Shared with GM   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1991  Despite slumping truck sales, Toyota Motors Corp will step up the pace of its US pickup truck production when it begins building four-wheel drive models in Feb 1992 at the New United Motor Manufacturing Inc, or Nummi, factory it shares with General Motors Corp in Fremont CA.
