 Torrance, Calif., Voters to Determine if Mobil Can Continue Refining Process   By Solomon, Caleb, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1990  Voters in Torrance CA are to decide on a measure that would prohibit a Mobil Corp refinery from using hydrofluoric acid, a hazardous substance used commonly in making gasoline. The vote is receiving intense scrutiny from the oil industry.
