 SmithKline Beckman, American Hospital Form Lab Company   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 1985  SmithKline Beckman Corp. said it formed a joint clinical laboratory company with American Hospital Supply Corp.  SmithKline, which had the nation's largest independent clinical laboratory business before the combination, said it owns 72.5% of the new company, called SmithKline Bio-Science Laboratories. American Hospital Supply, based in Evanston, Ill., owns 27.5%. Independent clinical laboratories do medical tests for doctors and hospitals.
