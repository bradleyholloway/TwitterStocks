 Asia-Pacific Region Posts Strong Growth In Online Spending   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 2001  The three biggest Asia-Pacific markets -- Japan, South Korea and Australia -- accounted for 88% of online consumer e-commerce revenue.  Global online players haven't made many inroads in the Asia-Pacific region, according to the report, with only 16% of revenues going to companies such as Amazon.com Inc., eBay Inc. and Dell Computer Corp.
