 Continental Airlines Adds 5 Cities, Slates Some Discount Fares   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 1986  Continental Airlines is adding five cities to its system and, as expected, will offer discount fares out of Chicago's Midway Airport as part of the move.  Starting Nov. 20, Continental will fly to 30 cities out of Midway Airport. The carrier will be flying out of Midway for the first time.  United Airlines has matched Continental's fares out of Chicago's O'Hare Airport from Dec. 2 through Dec. 15. Northwest Airlines has matched the discount fares at Midway Airport through Dec. 15, and at O'Hare from Dec. 2 through Dec. 15.
