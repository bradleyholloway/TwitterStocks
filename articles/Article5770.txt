 99 days: Manzi quits at IBM and his many critics are not at all surprised   By Hays, Laurie, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1995  Jim Manzi resigned on Oct 11, 1995 as head of Lotus Development Corp, the company he had run for a decade as the enfant terrible of the software world, just 99 days after IBM Corp's acquisition of the company. The questions that his resignation poses for the future of IBM's investment in the firm are examined.
