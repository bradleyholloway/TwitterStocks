Ericsson, H-P Form Joint Venture for Network Systems   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 11, 1992  L M Ericsson, a Swedish telecommunications company, and Hewlett-Packard Co, a computer supplier, formed a joint venture company, named Ericsson Hewlett-Packard Telecommunications AB, to develop and market network management systems for telecommunications companies worldwide.
