 Smith Barney sets investment program tied to fees rather than commissions   By Henderson, Barry, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 1997  Smith Barney Inc is quietly rolling out a new no-commission investment program in its continuing battle with firms that sell financial advice for annual fees rather than commissions. Smith Barney's AssetOne allows investors with more than $100,000 to invest to pick from among stocks, bonds and mutual funds for a 1.5% annual fee. Traditionally, brokerage firms have charged a commission for each transaction.
