 90 Days: Working Well With Executive Recruiters Online edition  By Needleman, Sarah E, The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 2008  Yet recruiters work on a confidential basis, asserts Wes Richards, a senior client partner and managing director at Korn/Ferry International Inc. "We're like your doctor, your priest or your lawyer," he says. If a recruiter approaches you about a career opportunity, show you're job-search savvy by inquiring about the size of the employer, its culture, the competencies needed for the position and whether you're the first candidate to be considered, advises Mr. Richards.
