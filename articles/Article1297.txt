 Stock Close Mixed as Industrials Recover from Fall on Drexel News   By Wilson, David, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1990  Stock prices closed mixed Jan 13, 1990 as market players awaited word on whether Drexel Burnham Lambert would file for Chapter 11 bankruptcy. The Dow Jone Industrial Average finished with a gain of 4.96 at 2624.10.
