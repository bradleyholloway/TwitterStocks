 Microsoft Stock Sold in April by Chairman   By Pettit, Dave, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1991  Microsoft Corp Chairman William H. Gates III sold 500,000 shares of common stock at prices ranging from $100.50 to $103 each, giving the open market transactions an indicated value of about $51 million. The Apr 1991 sale followed sales between Oct 1990 and Feb 1991 totaling 1,050,000 shares.
