 U.K. to Ban Cigarette Displays   By Carolan, Michael, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 2008  A spokesman for Imperial Tobacco PLC -- the U.K.'s No. 1 tobacco seller, with 46% of the market -- called the ban on retail displays "unnecessary and disproportionate," adding, "We've not seen any credible evidence that showed young people start smoking because of displays in shops."
