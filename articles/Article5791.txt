 International Brief -- STORA AB: Unit to Build Operation To Make Magazine Paper   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1995  Stora AB, a Swedish forestry products group, will build a line to produce uncoated magazine paper at its unit in Nova Scotia, Canada, for three billion kronor ($449 million). The operation is to start up in early 1998 with annual capacity of 350,000 metric tons (equal to 2,204.62 lbs).
