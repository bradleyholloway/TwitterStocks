 O'Hara becomes Darden's president as Magruder leaves for Cracker Barrel   By Gibson, Richard; Brannigan, Martha, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1995  Newly independent Darden Restaurants Inc appointed Jeffrey J. O'Hara, one of its two vice chairmen, as president and COO. O'Hara's counterpart, Ronald N. Magruder, is leaving Darden to become president and COO at Cracker Barrel Old Country Store Inc.
