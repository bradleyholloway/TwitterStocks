 Bouchards Take Wheeling's Helm   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 2006  On Nov. 17, shareholders voted to oust Wheeling-Pitt's sitting board of directors, supporting a hostile takeover by the closely held Chicago Heights, Ill., steel distributor. The final vote was 6.1 million to 2.8 million, a majority that Esmark called an overwhelming victory.
