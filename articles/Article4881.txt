 Damages Are Sought By North American Holding From Broker   The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1988  In a filing with the National Association of Securities Dealers seeking the arbitration, North American charged that Donaldson's Pershing division had breached its fiduciary duties by failing to fulfill its obligations as the clearing broker for North American's North American Investment Corp. subsidiary and margin lender for its customers. New York-based Donaldson is a brokerage subsidiary of Equitable Life Assurance Society of the U.S.
