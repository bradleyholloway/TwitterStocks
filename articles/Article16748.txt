 Cage's Noise Compositions Are Windows Into the Void   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 2007  Peter Gutmann's celebration of John Cage's espousal of noise ("Masterpiece," Leisure & Arts, March 24) as opening "as wide a window to eternity as any venerable favorite by Bach, Mozart or Beethoven" is absurd. Cage's window was to the void, not to eternity. Mr. Gutmann refers to the "philosophical import" of Cage's noise without seeming to grasp it.
