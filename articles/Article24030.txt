 RIM Takes PlayBook Hit --- BlackBerry Maker's Crisis Deepens; Unsold Tablets Prompt $485 Million Charge   By Connors, Will; Cummins, Chip, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 2011  Research In Motion Ltd. wrote off nearly a half-billion dollars in unsold or discounted PlayBook tablets and warned it expects to sell fewer BlackBerrys this holiday, further clouding the company's future as it struggles against rivals Apple Inc. and Google Inc. in the smartphone market.
