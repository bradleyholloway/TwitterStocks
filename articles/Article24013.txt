 No Bull: Brazilian Cowpokes Dominate Rodeo's Main Event --- The Buck Stops in South America Now; Americans Study Portuguese, Try New Rope   By Karp, Hannah, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 2011  After decades of wowing the world with their beautiful version of the beautiful game, the Brazilians have found a new sport to dominate, albeit a slightly less pretty one: professional bull riding. The South American country has been producing strong contenders since the tour began nearly two decades ago, but never before this season have its cowboys been so dominant, with Brazilians winning 17 of the 27 events so far.
