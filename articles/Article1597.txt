 Paper, Forest-Products Industry Seems to Have Hit Bottom; Rebound Elusive   By Goad, G Pierre, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 1992  Most industry analysts fear that the North American paper and forest-products industry still faces an uphill battle before it climbs out of the deep, cyclical trough it has been in. For the first quarter, most companies are expected to report weaker results, and some will report losses. Activity in the areas of paper, lumber, newsprint and wood-products are discussed.
