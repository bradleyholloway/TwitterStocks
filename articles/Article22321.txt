 Dixie Group Inc.: Carpet line to be bought from Foamex International   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 1997  Dixie Group Inc said it agreed to acquire a carpet business from Foamex International Inc for about $33 million, plus the value of inventory. A Dixie spokesma said the value of the inventory to be acquired is currently estimated at $9 million; a final price hasn't been set.
