 Citigroup ATMs Come to China   By Areddy, James T, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 2005  In addition to giving Chinese travelers fee-based access to its machines in more than 50 countries, customers of Citigroup's Citibank unit visiting China increasingly will be able to access ATMs connected to the nationwide UnionPay system of 8,000 machines.  Mr. [William Rhodes] said Citigroup hopes talks to increase its stake in midsize Shanghai Pudong Development Bank Co., the partner in its card deal, to the legal maximum of just under 20% will conclude "in coming months." He called China the New York company's biggest investment destination outside the U.S.
