 Yoder & Son: Is It Too Soon to Think About a Career?   By Stephen Kreider Yoder; Yoder, Isaac S, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 2009  (What on earth could I do with that philosophy major I was thinking about?) The school newspaper reports that more and more students from my college, and from almost every liberal-arts college, are unable to get jobs because of the tough economy, and that they're delaying the job process by applying to graduate school. When I dropped him off on campus for freshman orientation last week, the job market was the last thing on my mind as I jealously read through the course lists.
