 Activision Inc.: Net Income Decreases 30%, Hurt by Weak Holiday Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 2006  Activision Inc.'s fiscal third-quarter profit fell 30% and the videogame publisher forecast fourth-quarter results below analysts' expectations. Activision encountered a tough holiday season as many consumers held off purchases as they waited for more next-generation consoles to hit the market.
