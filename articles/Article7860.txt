 Business Brief -- Belvedere Corp.: Sprout Group Seeks to Sell Its 49% Stake in Company   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1989  Sprout Group said it asked Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp. to find a buyer for its 49% stake in Belvedere Corp., a property and casualty re-insurer. Both Sprout and Belvedere are based in New York.
