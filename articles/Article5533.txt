 Intel 3rd-period net more than doubled, but profit was slightly below forecasts   By McCoy, Charles, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1993  Intel Corp's third-quarter net income more than doubled to $584.4 million, or $1.33 a share. The earnings were only slightly under analysts' expectations of $1.34 to $1.35 a share. The results signaled that the semiconductor maker is beginning to feel at least some pressure on its profit margins.
