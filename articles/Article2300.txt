 Counterpoint: Yes, We Should Cap Credit-Card Margins   By Mayer, Martin, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1991  Martin Mayer supports Sen Alfonse D'Amoto's credit-card interest-rate-limit amendment, saying that the gap between the interest people can earn on their savings and the interest they have to pay to borrow on credit cards has become one of the serious drags on the money supply, throttling hope of a consumer-led economic recovery.
