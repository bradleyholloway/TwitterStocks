 Bouncing back: Brick houses and soda on ice bear witness to Vietnam's revival   By Rogers, David, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 1994  A profile is offered of Thanh An Vietnam, a village in the southern part of the country, as an illustration of the progress that Vietnam has made in the two decades after the end of war there. New brick houses, electric power and an expanding rubber plantation show the economic strides that have been made.
