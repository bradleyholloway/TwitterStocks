 Swiss Reinsurance Co.: Catastrophe Bond Is Issued For $442 Million for AXA   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 2006  Swiss Reinsurance Co. placed a $442 million bond on behalf of AXA SA that would protect the French insurer in the event of an unexpectedly high death toll from a catastrophic event in the U.S., Japan or France.
