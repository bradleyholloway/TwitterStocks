 Foreign Born in U.S. Increase   By Schaefer, Sara, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 2003  "The foreign-born population continues to grow at the same pace we've seen in the last decade, even with the slow economy and the events of 9/11," said Kevin Deardorff, chief of the immigration statistics staff at the U.S. Census Bureau. The bureau's survey didn't break down how many of the foreign-born residents are in the U.S. illegally.
