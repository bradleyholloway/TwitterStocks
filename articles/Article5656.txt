 Inside track: McGraw-Hill chief profited amid rumors   By Getler, Warren, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1994  The stock of McGraw-Hill Inc is down slightly from a rumor-inspired peak, closing Oct 11, 1994 at $74.125 on the NYSE. Chairman and CEO Joseph L. Dionne disposed of 15,000 shares at a price of $75.38 on Sep 19 amid rumors that a European company was considering a takeover of McGraw-Hill, but since then there has been no bid from any company, and the company will not comment on whether it has conducted merger talks.
