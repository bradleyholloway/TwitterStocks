 Physicians like what they hear from Hillary Clinton   By Anders, George; Stout, Hilary, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 1993  In an address to the AMA's annual meeting in Chicago, First Lady Hillary Rodham Clinton promised doctors a system that would strip away bureaucracy, reform malpractice laws and ensure patients their choice of health plan. Clinton also railed against insurers' common requirement that doctors seek approval for costly treatments ahead of time.
