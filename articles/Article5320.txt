 Forte again rejects offer by Granada, despite sweetening   By Pope, Kyle, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 1996  Forte PLC rejected a sweetened 3.8 billion British pounds ($5.89 billion) takeover bid from Granada Group PLC. Shareholders will now decide the battle for one of the UK's best-known hotel groups. Both sides are promising reforms, perks for shareholders, and a reorganization of the company's assets.
