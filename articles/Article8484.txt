 Uncertainty about Bundesbank policies ignites fears of rising European rates   By Roth, Terrence, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 7, 1994  With a lot of confusion in the markets about the monetary policy of the German Bundesbank and worry that the central bank could soon secretly make a major policy change, rates in Europe may be driven up. This would be a boon to the relatively calm US bond markets.
