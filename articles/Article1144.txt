 Marketing Brief -- D'Arcy Masius Benton & Bowles: Marketing Firm Is Bought, Will Be Merged With Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1989  D'Arcy Masius Benton & Bowles Inc., New York, said it acquired Connecticut Consulting Group, a marketing-management and promotion-services company with annual billings of $50 million. Terms weren't disclosed.
