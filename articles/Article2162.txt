 World-wide: Russia's prime minister   The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 1996  Russia's prime minister has asked the Organization for Security and Cooperation in Europe to make contact with Chechen separatists on his behalf, according to a Russian news agency. An OSCE official is apparantly meeting with the rebels and will brief Viktor Chernomyrdin on May 15, 1996 about any developments.
