 Retailing: Mighty barrier stops Canadians at U.S. border: Exchange rate   By Chipello, Christopher J, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 1994  With the Canadian dollar's exchange rate having fallen from $.89 in 1991 to $.72 in 1994, it has become less of a bargain for Canadian shoppers to buy from US stores. Same-day car trips to the US, an indicator of cross-border shopping, fell by 28% in Apr 1994 from the year before.
