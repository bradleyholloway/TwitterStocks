 NASA to Lease Capacity In Spacehab's Shuttle Lab   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1990  The five-year contract with Spacehab Inc. represents a model of how NASA and some in the Bush administration would like to encourage the growth of the fledgling space industry. Spacehab plans to spend $140 million to build a laboratory module that will fit in the mid-deck of the space shuttle. The lab will consist of 50 lockers that can house space-based experiments.
