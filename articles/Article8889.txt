 Dominion Textile Inc. Forms Panel to Assess Strategic Alternatives   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1997  Dominion Textile Inc. said its board formed a special committee to "assess strategic alternatives available to the corporation to enhance shareholder value."  The move follows the disclosure last month by Polymer Group Inc., of North Charleston, S.C., that it had accumulated a 14.2% stake in Dominion and might buy more, or even all, of Dominion's shares outstanding.
