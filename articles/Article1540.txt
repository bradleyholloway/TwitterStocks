 Snapshots of the World's Major International Airlines   By Pulley, Brett; Nomani, Asra Q; O Brian, Bridget; Goad, G Pierre; Carey, Susan; Coleman, Brian, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 1992  A brief look at each of the world's major airlines is offered, along with comparisons of the biggest players in terms of passengers and traffic, based on 1990 statistics, and fleet size. The busiest international air routes are indicated on a map. Aeroflot, American Airlines, Delta Air Lines, United Airlines, USAir Inc, Northwest Airlines, British Airways and Continental Airlines are shown as leaders in the industry.
