 PPG, Owens Corning swap favors, catch trade spies   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1996  PPG Industries Inc and Owens Corning have cooperated again in an FBI sting operation to catch industrial spies. In Nov 1996, a man who supplied cleaning crews to PPG approached the chairman of Owens Corning and tried to sell proprietary information about its competitor. A few years ago, someone stole trade secrets from Owens Corning and tried to peddle it to PPG.
