 Two Total Compagnie Subsidiaries in Canada Are Planning to Merge   The Wall Street Journal,  May 13, 1988  VANCOUVER, British Columbia -- Total Erickson Resources Ltd., 52%-owned by Total Resources (Canada) Ltd., said it has tentatively agreed to merge with Getty Resources Ltd., a wholly owned unit of Total Resources.  Total Resources Canada, itself a unit of Paris-based Total Compagnie Francaise des Petroles, would own 71% of the stock of the merged company.
