 Abreast of the Market: Betting on Europe without Leaving Home   By Levingston, Steven E, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1992  Many money managers see investment opportunities in Europe for US investors through US multinationals, international mutual funds and ADRs of European companies. Analysts believe the the current upheaval in the region over currencies and economic unity will result in lower interest rates and speedier economic growth for Europe. On Sep 18, 1992, stocks prices rose, with the Dow Jones Industrial Average climbing 11.35 to 3327.05.
