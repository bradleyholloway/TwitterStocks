 First City Financial's Samuel Belzberg Turns Firm's Reins over to His Nephew   By Kilpatrick, Lynne, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1991  Samuel Belzberg will remain chairman of the family's flagship company, First City Financial Corp, but has appointed his nephew, Brent S. Belzberg, to the positions of president and CEO of the firm. First City lost 165 million Canadian ($139.1 million) in the first nine months of 1990.
