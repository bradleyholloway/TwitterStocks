 Manulife Financial Corp.: Quarterly Profit Rose 12% Despite Weak Equity Markets   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 2006  Manulife Financial Corp. posted a 12% rise in second-quarter profit despite weak equity markets and a hefty currency-exchange impact. The Canadian insurance giant posted net income of 944 million Canadian dollars (US$838 million), or 60 Canadian cents a share, up from $844 million, or 52 Canadian cents a share, a year earlier.
