 Business Brief -- Unocal Corp.: Headquarters Property's Sale To Hillman Affiliate Is Set   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1988  Unocal Corp. said it agreed to sell its 12.6-acre headquarters property in downtown Los Angeles for about $200 million to UC Land Co., a closely held, indirect affiliate of Hillman Co., a Pittsburgh investment firm headed by billionaire Henry L. Hillman.
