 Enron Corp.: Kroll Zolfo Cooper Executive Succeeds Chief Financial Officer   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 2004  Enron Corp.'s chief financial officer resigned and an executive with the restructuring firm that has guided Enron through its massive Chapter 11 case took his place. Raymond M. Bowen, 44 years old, was finance chief and treasurer since a month after the energy company went bankrupt in December 2001.
