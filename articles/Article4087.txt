 Westinghouse Profit Rose 14% In Third Period   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 1985  Westinghouse Electric Corp.'s third-quarter profit rose 14% as the defense contractor, electric products maker and broadcaster reported improved results from most of its businesses.  Net income grew to $148.6 million, or 85 cents a share, from $130.6 million, or 75 cents a share, in the year-earlier period. Sales advanced 2.4% to $2.61 billion from $2.55 billion.
