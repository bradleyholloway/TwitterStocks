 ESPN Hires ABC Producer To Create New Game Show   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 2000  ESPN, a unit of Walt Disney Co.'s ABC Inc., will own "2-Minute Drill." The show will air on ESPN on Mondays between ESPN's popular "SportsCenter" and "Monday Night Countdown" and on Thursdays before "College GameNight." Reruns will air on ESPN2. ABC owns 80% of ESPN. Hearst Corp. owns the remaining 20%.
