 Ups and Downs of 'Equity-Index' Annuities   By Lauricella, Tom, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 2008  A sampling of returns provided by annuity issuers and industry sources shows that indexed annuities are holding their value during the current bear market. The formulas can lead to wide variations in performance compared to the stock market -- and the annuities can look like stars or laggards depending on the market environment.
