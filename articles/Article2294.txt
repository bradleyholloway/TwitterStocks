 International Brief -- Brent Walker Group PLC: Leisure Firm to Get Interest In Some Allied-Lyons Pubs   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1991  Brent Walker Group PLC said it plans to acquire an interest in 750 pubs owned by Allied-Lyons PLC and, in turn, will sell 50 of its own pubs to Allied-Lyons.
