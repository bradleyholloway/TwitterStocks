 Bully pulpit: Egyptian Jihad leader preaches holy war to Brooklyn Muslims   By Waldman, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1993  Sheik Omar Abdel-Rahman of Egypt, who was accidentally let in the US in 1990 even though he was on the US terrorist list, preaches his message of holy war to Muslims in Brooklyn NY. The leader of the outlawed Jihad in Egypt, he may be forced to leave the US, but officials of Egypt are worried that he may stir up violence if forced to return there.
