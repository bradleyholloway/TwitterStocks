 How Do We Cope with Basement-Built Atom Bombs?   By Melloan, George, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1991  George Melloan uses the example of Iraq to highlight how easy it is to build atom bombs on the sly. He predicts that efforts to control nuclear proliferation will become less and less effective in the future and that space-based missile defenses will become necessary to the security needs of the US and Europe.
