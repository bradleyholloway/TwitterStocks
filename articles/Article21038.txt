 Reliance Group Unit Lifts Its Stake in Two Concerns   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 2, 1987  Reliance Financial Services Corp., a unit of New York-based Reliance Group Holdings Inc., said it raised its stake in Spectra-Physics Inc. to 11.8% of the common shares outstanding.  Separately, Reliance Financial, also based in New York, said it increased its holdings in Zenith National Insurance Co.'s common stock to 26.3% of the total outstanding.
