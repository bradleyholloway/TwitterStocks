 Aircraft-service concern pleads guilty in scheme   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 13, 1995  Tec-Air Services Inc, an East Northport NY company, and two of its executives--Jack Caloras and Steven Metal--pleaded guilty to charges they failed to properly service emergency gear, including oxygen systems on Air Force One and Air Force Two. Under the plea agreement, the executives will each pay $200,000 and the company $100,000.
