 Money-Fund Assets Fall $17.13 Billion   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 2008  Investors to money-market funds dropped $17.13 billion in the week ended Tuesday, bringing total net assets to $3.088 trillion, according to the Money Fund Report, published by iMoneyNet Inc. Institutional investors declined $18.40 billion, while individual, or "retail," investors added $1.26 billion.
