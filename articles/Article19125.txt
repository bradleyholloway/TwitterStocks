 Corrections & Amplifications   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1999  A Dec 2, 1998 Money & Investing article incorrectly stated that Ultraotc Profund tracks the Nasdaq Composite Index. It tracks the Nasdaq 100 Index. Other corrections to the article, about leveraged mutual funds, are also given. Separately, a correction is given to a Dec 30 article about HS Resources Inc's share repurchase program.
