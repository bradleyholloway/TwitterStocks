 Policy Management Gets a Buyout Bid; Stock Price Rises 37%   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 2000  COLUMBIA, S.C. -- Policy Management Systems Corp., a software and information-service-product maker with a beatendown stock and falling sales, said buyout firm Welsh, Carson, Anderson & Stowe agreed to acquire at least three-quarters of the company and assume debt, an offer valued at more than $600 million.
