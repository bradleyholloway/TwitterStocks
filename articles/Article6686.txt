 Best on the Street (A Special Report): 2003 Analysts Review --- Lodging   By By Motoko Rich, The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 2003  J. Cogan of Banc of America Securities, a unit of Bank of America Corp., rode Hilton to the No. 3 spot in this year's survey, even as his other picks delivered negative returns. After Hilton acquired Promus Hotel Corp. in 1999, Mr. Cogan and his team traveled around the country interviewing general managers of hotels throughout the expanded Hilton empire. "We were hearing that they were doing a wonderful job of integrating the brands," says Mr. Cogan.
