 CEOs on the Summit: Taxing Foreign Firms Won't Work   By Thieme, Heiko H, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 1992  Heiko H. Thieme discusses the problems with President-elect Bill Clinton's tax proposal concerning the taxation of foreign companies. Thieme says that by policing the internal activities of multinationals, Clinton may discourage foreign corporations from doing business in the US.
