 EchoStar Complains To FCC Over Rates Fox/Liberty Sports   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1997  Fox/Liberty Sports is a cable TV joint venture between Rupert Murdoch's News Corp. and Liberty Media Group, an affiliate of Tele-Communications Inc.
