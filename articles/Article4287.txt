 Bernanke Comments Deflate Dollar   By Kissi, Dawn, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 2011  During his semiannual congressional testimony, on Wednesday Mr. Bernanke acknowledged that the pace of the economic recovery has remained soft and that the Fed might yet be prepared to embark on what some market participants have expressed concern over: yet another round of quantitative easing, or QE3, to jump-start the economy.
