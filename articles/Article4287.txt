 International Corporate Report: Panel Seeks Changes In Japanese Steel Industry   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1987  Japan's six biggest steelmakers -- NIPPON STEEL CORP., NIPPON KOKAN K.K., KOBE STEEL LTD., KAWASAKI STEEL CORP., SUMITOMO METAL INDUSTRIES LTD. and NISSHIN STEEL CO. -- should make "further rationalization efforts" to prepare for a continued decline in demand, an informal panel set up by a Japanese ministry said.
