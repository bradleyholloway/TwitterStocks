 Southwestern Bell to Double Stake in Telmex to 10%   By Robichaux, Mark, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1991  Southwestern Bell Corp said it would pay $467.3 million to double its stake in Telefonos de Mexico to 10%. Southwestern acquired an option to purchase more shares in the firm when a consortium to which it belonged bought a controlling interest in Telmex.
