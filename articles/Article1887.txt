 Natwest sets Asian venture with Wheelock   By Smith, Craig S, The Wall Street Journal,  May 13, 1994  Wheelock & Co and UK's National Westminster Bank PLC announced that they would form a 50-50 joint venture investment bank with initial authorized capital of 1.5 billion Hong Kong dollars ($194.2 million). The venture will be called Wheelock NatWest Ltd.
