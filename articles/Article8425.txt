 Kaufman & Broad buyback   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 1993  Kaufman & Broad Corp, a homebuilder, said it will buy back $28.6 million of its special common stock warrants from SunAmerica Inc's Sun Life unit. Kaufman & Broad will purchase 2,377,000 warrants at $19 a share, less a $6.96-a-share exercise price.
