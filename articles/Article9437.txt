 SEC Chief Pitt Denies He Discussed Xerox With Auditor KPMG   The Wall Street Journal,  May 7, 2002  Mr. [Harvey Pitt] confirmed he met briefly on April 26 with KPMG LLP's new chairman and chief executive, Eugene O'Kelly, who had requested a "meet and greet" session with the SEC chairman.  Contrary to reports from Mr. O'Kelly, Mr. Pitt said there was no discussion in the meeting of a possible SEC enforcement action against Xerox, a KPMG audit client.
