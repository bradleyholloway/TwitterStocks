 Electronic Arts Inc.: Maxis agrees to acquisition in $125 million stock swap   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 1997  Electronic Arts Inc, a San Mateo CA-based maker of interactive entertainment software, said it agreed to acquire Walnut Creek CA-based Maxis Inc in a stock swap valued at $125 million. Electronic Arts said Maxis shareholders will receive 0.3644 shares of its stock for each Maxis share.
