 OFF DUTY --- Adventure & Travel: Will Hike for Cheese --- The 105-mile Mont Blanc trail through Italy, France and Switzerland is a test of endurance and appetite   By Squires, Kathleen, The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 2012  The Tour du Mont Blanc is one of the world's classic long-distance hiking routes, a circular path that winds through ice-capped mountains in France, Switzerland and Italy. At roughly seven to 12 miles a day, the route is supposed to take 10 days. Since our idea of a hike was walking across the Brooklyn Bridge, I figured we'd need a few more.
