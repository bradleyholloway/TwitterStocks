 Hanna Agrees to Sell Stake in Mining Firm For About $72 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 1986  M.A. Hanna Co. has agreed to sell its 96% interest in St. John d'el Rey Mining Co. PLC for $27 a share, or about $72 million, to a subsidiary of Companhia Auxiliar de Empresas de Mineracao, a Brazilian holding company.  St. John's principal asset is a 34% interest in Mineracoes Brasileiras Reunidas S.A., which Hanna says is Brazil's largest privately owned iron ore company.
