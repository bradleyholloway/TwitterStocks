 The Fraudulent Case against Japan   By Bhagwati, Jagdish, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1992  Jagdish Bhagwati discusses the perception that the Japanese are unfair traders, saying that the perception, while more than half a century old, is not true. Bhagwati notes two fallacies about America's trade with Japan, and dispels each in turn.
