 Canada's jobless rate falls to lowest level in 2 years   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 1997  Canadian manufacturers boosted their hiring in Jun 1997, pushing the country's unemployment rate down to its lowest level in almost two years. Statistics Canada said that employment in manufacturering rose 2.5% in June from May to 2,184,500, the highest level since Feb 1990.
