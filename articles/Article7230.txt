 Voyager Heads for the Stars   By Krauss, Lawrence, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 2011  Employing the same delightful undergraduate physics trick that allowed it to use the inner planets as slingshots to get to the outer planets, Voyager 1 used its encounter with Saturn in 1980 and with Saturn's tiny moon Titan to launch it on a course for the stars.
