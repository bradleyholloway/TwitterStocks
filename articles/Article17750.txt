 Kidde Is Studying Two Offers for All Or Large Part of It   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 1987  SADDLE RIVER, N.J. -- Kidde Inc., which put itself on the sale block last month, said it was studying two formal offers to acquire all or a large part of the company.  Kidde also said it continues to study a restructuring. Although the company didn't provide details, a person familiar with the company said Kidde officials were examining a recapitalization plan that would include the sale of some Kidde units, taking on new debt and making a one-time cash payout to shareholders.
