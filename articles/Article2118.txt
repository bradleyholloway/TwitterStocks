 Hong Kong re-export trade   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1996  By major market, Hong Kong's important re-export trade was mixed in Nov 1995, with a 2.4% pullback recorded with the US and a rise of 9.3% for China. In November, Hong Kong had a merchandise trade deficit of 12.30 billion Hong Kong dollars ($1.59 billion).
