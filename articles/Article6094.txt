 Forgotten Woman Buys Stores   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 1998  Forgotten Woman Inc said it acquired 24 Lillie Rubin Fashion Inc stores for $2 million. Specialty-clothing retailer Lillie Rubin has been under Chapter 11 bankruptcy-law protection since Feb 1998. The US Bankruptcy Court in Dalaware approved the sale on Jun 8.
