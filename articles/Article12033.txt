 Whitewater panel in growing fight with White House on lawyers' meeting   By Simpson, Glenn R, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 1995  The White House and the Senate Whitewater Committee are in a growing dispute over a Nov 1993 meeting between Clinton administration lawyers and personal lawyers for President Clinton and Hillary Clinton. Republicans contend that confidential data from the Treasury Dept and SBA were used improperly to help the Clintons' personal lawyers fend off Whitewater investigators. The White House says the discussions are protected by attorney-client privileges.
