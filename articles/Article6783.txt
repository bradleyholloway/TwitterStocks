 World News: Gambia Struggles to Get on Tourists' Map; In West Africa, Sun, Sand, Peace Remain Tough Sell   By Michaels, Daniel, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 2008  With the exception of one big new hotel operated by Sheraton Hotels & Resorts, a unit of Starwood Hotels & Resorts Worldwide Inc., Gambia lacks significant tourism infrastructure, such as resorts, fine restaurants or upmarket safari operators. General manager Jennifer Buhr says she is offering wedding and honeymoon packages, marketing the center for international conferences and hoping to develop a shopping mall, tennis courts and a golf course on the property.
