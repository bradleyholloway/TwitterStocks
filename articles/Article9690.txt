 Services Brief -- Adecco SA: New Directors Named to Board As Net Profit Plunges by 53%   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 7, 2004  Adecco SA named new directors to its board Friday in an effort to restore investor trust, after a prolonged and costly accounting probe hurt first-quarter earnings. Adecco said first-quarter net profit fell 53% to 30 million euros ($36.9 million) from 64 million euros a year earlier on audit charges of 36 million euros.
