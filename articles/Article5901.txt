 Business Brief -- Finova Group Inc.: Dividend Is Suspended To `Strengthen Company'   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 2000  Finova Group Inc. suspended its quarterly dividend of 18 cents a share. In a prepared statement, Chairman John Teets said eliminating the dividend was part of a "multipronged approach to improve liquidity and strengthen the company." Mr. Teets reiterated that Finova, Scottsdale, Ariz., was looking at divesting itself of certain business units and cutting operating expenses.
