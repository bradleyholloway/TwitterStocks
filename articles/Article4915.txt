 Gephardt Calls for Rewriting of Trade Pact   By Truell, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1992  House Majority Leader Richard Gephardt has suggested that the Bush administration should renegotiate the North American Free Trade Agreement to correct certain environmental and labor shortcomings in the proposed pact. Gephardt is the first senior Democrat to reject the trade agreement outright.
