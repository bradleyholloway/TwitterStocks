 Consumer Services Brief -- Blockbuster Inc.: Video Renter Swung to Profit On 17% Increase in Revenue   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 2003  Blockbuster Inc. swung to a fourth-quarter profit from a year-earlier loss, on a 17% increase in revenue. The Dallas-based video-rental company reported net income of $30.7 million, or 17 cents a share, compared with a net loss of $4.5 million, or three cents a share, a year earlier.
