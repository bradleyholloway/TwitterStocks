 Traders Expect Soybean Futures Prices to Rise in Response to Lower-Than-Expected Supplies   By Taylor, Jeffrey; Raghavan, Anita, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 9, 1992  In response to a government report showing lower-than-expected stocks of soybeans, soybean futures prices are expected to rise. Commodity markets in New York were hit hard on Oct 8, 1992 as traders dumped futures contracts before the release of the USDA's crop report. Orange juice for November delivery plunged $.0320 to $1.0340 a pound. In other commodity markets, cocoa futures prices crumbled while energy prices rose.
