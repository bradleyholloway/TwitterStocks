 Red Robin and Neurocrine Slide; Dollar Financial, Stellent Surge   By Cordeiro, Anjali, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 2006  The Russell 2000 index of small-cap stocks Friday gained 2.60 points, or 0.35%, to 752.73. For the week, the index fell 1.7%, while it is up 12% this year. The Standard & Poor's SmallCap 600 index Friday gained 1.11 points, or 0.29%, to 383.72. For the week, the index lost 1.8%, while it is up 9.4% for 2006.  "We had a period of euphoria, and we now got a normal pullback" this past week, said Barry James, president of James Advantage Funds. "You got a plethora of information during the week, and some of it pointed to a weaker economy."  The interest-rate-sensitive housing sector slid after a rise in bond yields, on which mortgage rates are based. Meritage Homes (NYSE) dropped 76 cents, or 1.7%, to 43.02, and WCI Communities (NYSE) fell 23 cents, or 1.5%, to 15.18.
