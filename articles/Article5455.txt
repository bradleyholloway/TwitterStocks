 Money-fund assets grew by $6.31 billion   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1993  Money-market mutual fund assets rose by $6.31 billion to $597.07 billion from $590.76 billion for the week ended Feb 10, 1993. Assets of 614 retail money-market mutual funds fell by $268.8 million to $383.37 billion.
