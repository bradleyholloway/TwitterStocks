 Business Brief -- First Constitution Financial Corp.: Loss Is Posted and Group Seeks to Renegotiate Sale   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 1990  First Constitution Financial Corp., New Haven, Conn., reporting a $22.6 million fourth-quarter loss and adding $33.5 million to its provision for loan losses, said an investment group wants to renegotiate its $273.5 million deal to acquire the thrift.
