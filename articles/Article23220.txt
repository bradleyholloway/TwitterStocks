 Telecommunications Brief -- Global Crossing Ltd.: Citigroup Settles Litigation On Research Reports, Conflicts   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 3, 2005  Citigroup Inc. said it has settled class-action litigation brought by investors in Global Crossing Ltd., but specifically denied any violation of law. The suit alleged that Citigroup, which was one of Global Crossing's bankers, inflated research reports and failed to disclose conflicts of interest.
