 Technology Brief -- Marconi PLC: U.K. Regulator Censures Firm For Breaching Listing Rules   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 2003  British telecommunications equipment maker Marconi PLC was censured by the United Kingdom Financial Services Authority for breaching listing rules by keeping investors in the dark about a sharp sales slowdown in July 2001. The FSA said, however, that there was "no finding of bad faith on the part of Marconi or its officers."
