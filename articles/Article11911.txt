 TCI is expected to get FTC approval for its bid to acquire TeleCable assets   By Robichaux, Mark; Novak, Viveca, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 1994  Tele-Communications Inc is expected to get FCC approval, which had been held up for an antitrust review, for the firm's proposal to buy the cable systems of TeleCable Corp for $1 billion. The move could break a logjam for other billion dollar cable deals by TCI.
