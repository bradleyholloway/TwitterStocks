 Business Brief -- America Online Latin America Inc.: Fourth-Period Loss Narrows As Subscriber Revenue Rises   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 2002  America Online Latin America Inc.'s fourth-quarter loss narrowed as revenue more than doubled on strong subscriber revenue growth. AOL Latin America posted a loss of $65.8 million, or $1.05 a Class A share, compared with a year-earlier loss of $101.8 million, or $1.68 a share.
