 Burdened by card debt? There are ways to ease the load   By Jeffrey, Nancy Ann, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 1996  Advice is offered for the growing ranks of consumers haunted by overdue credit-card bills. It is possible that banks may be willing to work out a compromise, and accept less than the amount owed, but they usually will report that fact. And even creditors or collection agents who offer to "clean up" a credit report often do not make good on their promises, consumer advocates say.
