 Perelman's Vaunted Marketing Skills Produce Only Mixed Results at Revlon   By Deveny, Kathleen; Hinge, John B, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 1991  A close look at the performance of Revlon Inc in the six-year period that the firm has been owned by Ronald O. Perelman indicates a mixed marketing record. Competition has increased and Perelman has been discussing the sale of the firm's beauty business.
