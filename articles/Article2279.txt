 Any Potential Pebble Beach Profit Is More Than Just a Chip Shot Away   By Carlton, Jim, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 20, 1991  Japanese investor and golf-course developer Minoru Isutani paid a reported $800 million a year ago to acquire California's famed Pebble Beach resort property, but a chain of economic and political events since then has seriously imperiled Isutani's original financing plan. The deal's main lender, Mitsubishi Trust & Banking Corp, agreed to extend the loan for one more year.
