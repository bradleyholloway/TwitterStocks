 Business Brief -- Newmont Mining Corp.: Bid for Normandy Mining Trumps AngloGold's Offer   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 2001  Newmont Mining Corp. raised the stakes again in the takeover battle for Australia's largest gold miner, Normandy Mining Ltd., by throwing in more cash to boost its offer to 1.90 Australian dollars (97.67 U.S. cents) a share.
