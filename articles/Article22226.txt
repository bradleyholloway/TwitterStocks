 In the lab: Liquid plastic may transform TV screens   By Strassel, Kimberley A, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1996  The labs at Cambridge Display Technology Ltd are developing glowing plastics, known as light-emitting polymers (LEPs), that could someday transform the screen-display market. If CDT can fine-tune its technology--no easy task--LEP displays could be not only lighter and cheaper than current TV and computer screens, but could even allow users to roll up displays and pop them into back pockets.
