 Cemex to Buy Spain's No. 2 Cement Firm   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1992  Cementos Mexicanos SA, or Cemex, said it agreed to purchase an option to buy Spain's Auxiliar de la Contruccion, or Sanson, from Unifund Group. Sanson, which is the second-largest cement maker in its country, would fetch an estimated $600 million.
