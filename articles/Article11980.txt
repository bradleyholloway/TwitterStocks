 Schroders renames Wertheim Schroder Schroder Wertheim   By Siconolfi, Michael, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 1995  Schroders PLC has changed the name of its US unit to Schroder Wertheim & Co effective Jul 5, 1995. In 1994, Schroders bought the remaining 50% of Wertheim Schroder for $92 million.
