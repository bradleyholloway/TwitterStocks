 Dictionary Use Is Another Habit That Might Help   By By Jonathan Weil, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 21, 1998  The seminar, which ended Monday, was part of what Superintendent Hal Guthrie calls a long-range "learning organization initiative." Everyone from teachers to bus drivers watched videotapes of Mr. Covey's presentations and met in small groups to learn how to "synergize," "sharpen the saw" and "be proactive."
