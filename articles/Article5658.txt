 Casino Officials, Atlanta Leaders Hold Early Talks   By By Nikhil Deogun, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1994  Among the executives who have discussed the issue with Atlanta Mayor Bill Campbell in a recent meeting: J. Terrence Lanni, president of Caesars World Inc.; Al Kokesch, executive vice president of Casino Magic Corp.; and Les Bond, a St. Louis investment banker.  Mr. Bond, a former attorney with the Atlanta law firm King & Spalding, calls the talks with the mayor "exploratory, to see if there's any interest." He describes Mr. Campbell's reaction as "very neutral."
