 Hartmarx Completes Refinancing and Sale Of $30 Million in Stock   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 1993  Hartmarx Corp. said it completed its financial restructuring by replacing most of its $350 million credit facility and selling $30 million of new common stock.  The credit agreements involve 18 separate lenders and increase the company's borrowing costs to a variable rate set at two percentage points above the prime rate. Under its old agreements Hartmarx paid prime rate plus one percentage point.
