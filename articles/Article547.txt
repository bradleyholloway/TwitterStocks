 Property: Developer Pieces Together Legoland Deal for Ridge Hill   By Kusisto, Laura, The Wall Street Journal,  May 25, 2012  Forest City Ratner Cos., the developer of Ridge Hill, has leased a roughly 33,000-square-foot space to Merlin Entertainments, of the U.K., which operates half a dozen other Legolands in the U.S. The stores sell Lego sets and offer a number of attractions for children ages 3 to 10 based on the popular building toy.
