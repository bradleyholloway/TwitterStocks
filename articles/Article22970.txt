 Bank of Japan Tries New Tack On Yen Policy   By By Sebastian Moffett, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 2003  The Finance Ministry official stressed, however, that the intervention wasn't aimed specifically to drive down the yen. He added that the ministry could intervene more aggressively to rein in excessive movements, but would not intentionally drive the yen down. The moves follow the appointment of a new yen czar, Zembei Mizoguchi, who assumed the position of vice finance minister for international affairs on January 14, taking over from Haruhiko Kuroda.
