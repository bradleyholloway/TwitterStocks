 Japan Urges Boost From Industries To Aid Gulf Force   By By Urban C. Lehner, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 1990  The Japanese government called in the leaders of six major manufacturing industries and urged their "utmost cooperation" in supplying U.S. forces in Saudi Arabia.  Cooperation would include adding production capacity to meet the needs of U.S. and other forces challenging Iraq, shipping the forces goods intended for other customers and billing the goods at cost, government officials said.
