 International Corporate Report: Ericsson Agrees to Purchase Thorn's Stake in Joint Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1988  Sweden's TELEFON AB L.M. ERICSSON and Britain's THORN EMI PLC said they agreed in principle that Ericsson will purchase the British company's 51% stake in Thorn Ericsson Telecommunications (Holdings) Ltd.
