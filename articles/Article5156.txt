 Glaxo chief executive hints that firm now may look toward acquisitions   By Moore, Stephen D, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 9, 1994  Richard Sykes, the CEO of Glaxo Holdings PLC, hinted that Glaxo may look toward acquisitions after Glaxo reported a disappointing 10% rise in pretax profit to 1.84 billion British pounds ($2.85 billion) for the fiscal year ended June 30, 1994, up from BP1.68 billion the year earlier.
