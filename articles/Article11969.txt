 Supreme Court will determine right of litigants to a jury in patent cases   By Barrett, Paul M, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 1995  On June 5, 1995, the Supreme Court agreed to decide whether combatants in a patent lawsuit have a constitutional right to have a jury hear their case. The justices said they will use a patent dispute involving American Airlines' computerized reservation system to resolve this procedural question.
