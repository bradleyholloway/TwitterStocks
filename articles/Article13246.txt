 Hot Metropolitan Pot   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 2006  If it chooses, Italy will now be able to take home the krater and five other treasures in the Met's collection. In exchange, it will provide the Met with "long-term loans" of works of art of "equivalent beauty and importance," the Met said in a statement. Italy pledged to make the returned works available for public display and research.
