 Hilton Will Sell All or a Part Of Its Scandic Brand in Europe   By Sanders, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 2006  Hilton's move to retain management contracts with some of the hotels is in line with the U.S. lodging-industry trend that has seen Hilton and rivals Marriott International Inc. and Starwood Hotels & Resorts Worldwide Inc. sell hotels to real-estate concerns and then focus on generating income from management fees. Hilton had pared its ownership stake to only a few hotels before it acquired Hilton Group. Company officials said in February, when the deal was finalized, that they planned to sell all of the 40 hotels that had been acquired.
