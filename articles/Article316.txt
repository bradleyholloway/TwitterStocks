Rebecca Joy Carda, a classical pianist, and Iain Dennis Leigh, an investment banker, were married yesterday at the Roman Catholic Church of St. Ignatius Loyola in New York. The Rev. Walter Modrys performed the ceremony.  The bride, 28, also teaches piano. She graduated from the Eastman School of Music and received a master's degree in piano performance from the Juilliard School.
