A Manhattan grand jury indicted a woman yesterday on charges of second-degree manslaughter in the shooting death of a New York City police sergeant, although the fatal shots were fired by another officer.  The grand jury held that the woman, Mercedes Perez, was criminally responsible for the bullet that killed Sgt. John F.
