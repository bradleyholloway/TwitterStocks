 Amazon Gets In Used-Game Business --- Online Giant Launches Trade-In Videogame Service, Threatening GameStop   By Yukari Iwatani Kane; Gallagher, Dan, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 2009  After enjoying a virtual monopoly in the used videogames market, videogame specialty retailer GameStop Corp. faces a threat from a potentially formidable rival, Amazon.com Inc. The Seattle Internet retailer launched a service Thursday in which it will allow customers to trade in used videogames for store credit, taking aim at a key portion of GameStop's business.
