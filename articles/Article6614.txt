 Letters to the Editor: Paid Family Leave Won't Hurt Business   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 11, 2002  Despite your disparagement of California's business climate, the state, under Gov. Gray Davis, continues to be the nation's economic power house ("Paris, California," Review & Outlook, Sept. 27). It is first in the nation in exports. It has created some 900,000 new jobs since January 1999. In size of its economy, it ranks number five in the world.
