 Jazz: Not Your Grandpa's Big Bands   By Johnson, Martin, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 2009  The music is dense and richly textured, with elements of the classic jazz big bands blended with contemporary classical music and numerous overtones of rock and electronic music. Dale Fitzgerald, owner of the Jazz Gallery, a venue that commissions work for large ensembles by up-and-coming musicians, said "the best of the youngest generation of big bands are able to take on such a surging, sprawling range of musical traditions and make music of them."
