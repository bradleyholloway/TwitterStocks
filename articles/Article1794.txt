 Travelers plans to take charge of $325 million   By Steinmetz, Greg, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1993  Travelers Corp announced it would report a loss for the third quarter due to a $325 million charge for pollution reserves. However, this quarter's loss is better than the comparable quarter. In the year earlier period, Travelers had a loss of $358.3 million, due to the effects from Hurricane Andrew and a restructuring charge.
