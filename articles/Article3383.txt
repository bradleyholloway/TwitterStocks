 Investor's Calendar   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 2005  More Corporate Trials: WorldCom's former chief, Bernard Ebbers, goes on trial in Manhattan federal court Tuesday on charges he led an $11 billion accounting fraud. Mr. Ebbers maintains his innocence. Two of Tyco's former top executives, L. Dennis Kozlowski and Mark Swartz, are also set for a retrial on charges that they stole about $600 million in unauthorized compensation and illicit stock sales. They deny wrongdoing.
