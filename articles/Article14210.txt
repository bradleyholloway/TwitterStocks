 Hutton Reports 4th-Period Loss Of $12.2 Million   By By Jerry E. Bishop, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1986  E.F. Hutton Group Inc. reported a fourth-quarter loss of $12.2 million because it more than doubled a recently established reserve for potential losses on a $44 million note owed to it.  Hutton's reserve for possible losses on the note, which is owed by a closely held corporation that wasn't identified, was increased to $26.3 million from $11.3 million announced in December.
