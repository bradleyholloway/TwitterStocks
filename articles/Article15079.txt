 Advertising: Lotus ads take blunt pokes at Microsoft   By Goldman, Kevin, The Wall Street Journal,  May 5, 1993  Lotus Development Corp's new advertising campaign that likens giant rival Microsoft Corp and Chairman William Gates to the tale of the emperor's new clothes, created by Hill Holliday Connors Cosmopulos, has Microsoft steaming. Microsoft's attorneys have sent a letter to Lotus demanding that the ads stop, but Lotus has planned a new round of ads in response.
