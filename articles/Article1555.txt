 Al's Emporium: Soup's Suffering Sales   By Lewis, Al, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 2010  Campbell Soup last week announced a $100 million advertising campaign to combat slumping soup sales. Campbell CEO Doug Conant suggested in a talk with investors that people may be buying fewer groceries in general: There is a palpable change in consumer buying behavior that is unlike anything we have experienced certainly for a few decades.
