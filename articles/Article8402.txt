 For U.S., hidden risks in Somalia's feudal chaos   By Coll, Alberto R, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 7, 1993  Alberto R. Coll discusses the dangers inherent in US efforts to promote democracy or Western-style government in Somalia, a widening of the original humanitarian mission. He says that Somalia is a feudal society whose political life is shaped by the relations among the leading clans.
