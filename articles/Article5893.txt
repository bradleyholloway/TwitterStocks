 Omnicare Inc.: All $73.6 million of notes are converted into shares   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 11, 1996  Omnicare Inc, a Cincinnati-based provider of pharmacy services, said that all $73.6 million of its 5.75% convertible subordinated notes due 2003 that had been called for redemption were converted into Omnicare common stock, in accordance with the terms of the notes. With the conversion of the notes, Omnicare issued 10.2 million new shares of Omnicare common stock.
