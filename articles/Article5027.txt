 The mobile guide: Crowd invades Buckingham Palace; Queen in Scotland   By Levy, Paul, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1993  Paul Levy discusses the tour of Buckingham Palace, home to the Queen of England. The tour, the first time the queen has opened her home to the public, was designed to help pay for repairs for the fire damage to one of her other houses, Windsor Castle.
