 WSJ Sports Page Pick: U.S. vs. Costa Rica   By By Stefan Fatsis, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 1997  U.S. coach Steve Sampson's side comes into Sunday's match in Civic Stadium in Portland, Ore., (3:30 p.m. EDT, ABC) in better shape and with more firepower than it did in its first World Cup qualifying game with Costa Rica, a 3-2 loss on the road in March. Costa Rica, on the other hand, has a new coach and will be without two key players: brilliant striker Paulo Wanchope and defensive anchor Ronald Gonzalez.
