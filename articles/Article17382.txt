 U.S. News: Mother's Blood Shows Birth Defects in Fetal DNA   By Amy Dockser Marcus, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 2012  A number of companies, including two co-founded by Dr. Quake, are already using molecular counting techniques to measure the presence of elevated amounts of chromosome 21 relative to other chromosomes in a mother's blood to determine if a fetus has Down syndrome, a genetic condition that causes cognitive disabilities.
