 Apple's New iMac to Be Delayed, Disrupting Supply at Key Time   By Pui-Wing Tam, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 2, 2004  In a statement, Apple said it had stopped taking orders for the current generation of iMacs and expects its inventory of these machines to run out in the next few weeks. "We planned to have our next generation iMac ready by [this] time. . . . But our planning was obviously less than perfect," Apple said. "We apologize for any inconvenience to our customers." An Apple spokesman declined to elaborate.
