 Machine-tool orders expected to show strong rise for '84, smaller gains in '95   By Narisetti, Raju, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1995  Machine-tool orders are expected to have hit a 14-year high in 1994. Statistics for the first 11 months of the year show that orders were up more than 37% from a year earlier and already have exceeded the $4 billion mark for the first time since 1980.
