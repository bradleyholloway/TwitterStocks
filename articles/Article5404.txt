 Corning to pay $119 million to settle a case of Medicare billing by Damon   By Thomas, Paulette, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 1996  Damon Clinical Laboratories Inc, a subsidiary of Corning Inc, agreed to plead guilty to one count of criminal conspiracy and to pay $119 million to settle charges that it bilked the Medicare system in a billing scheme prior to being acquired by Corning. The settlement is the largest for a health-care fraud prosecution.
