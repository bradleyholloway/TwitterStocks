 Businesses Plan Spending Boost of 4.3% in 1992   By Harper, Lucinda, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 11, 1992  According to a Commerce Department survey, US businesses plan to increase their plant and equipment spending 4.3% in 1992 while manufacturers plan to cut their outlays 3%. Even though the rise is down from a 4.7% increase forecast in the first quarter, it is still an improvement on 1991's 0.6% decline.
