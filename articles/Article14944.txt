 Miyazawa, Making Waves, Seeks to Curb the Clout of Tokyo University's Alumni   By Lehner, Urban C, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1992  Japanese Prime Minister Kiichi Miyazawa and his cabinet, as part of an attempt to change the country's political system, are aiming to bring Tokyo University's influence down a couple of pegs. Graduates dominate the bureaucracy, a cornerstone of the rigid hierarchical social structure.
