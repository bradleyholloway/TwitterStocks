 Nuveen's Fund Fees Undergo SEC Scrutiny For Possible Overcharge   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 2005  According to Nuveen's annual report, an SEC attorney believes the unit, Symphony Asset Management, should repay the unaffiliated family of funds $2.8 million in overcharges in subadvisory fees along with about $1 million in interest. Nuveen said Symphony is reviewing the fee calculations.
