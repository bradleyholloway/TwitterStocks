 Economists say jump in prices overstated   By Thomas, Paulette, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 1994  Although the unanticipated news of a 0.6% jump in wholesale prices in Aug 1994 gave financial markets a jolt Sep 9, 1994, several economists said that the rise may be overstated. They consider consumer prices which are reported Sep 13, to be a better guage of inflation.
