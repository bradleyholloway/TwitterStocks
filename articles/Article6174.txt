 Business Brief -- TASTY BAKING CO.: Net Rises 51% on Charge But Operating Profit Drops   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1999  Philadelphia-based Tasty Baking Co reported net income of $1.7 million, or 22 cents a diluted share, in the fourth quarter of 1998, as compared to income of $1.2 million, or 14 cents a share, in the year-earlier period.
