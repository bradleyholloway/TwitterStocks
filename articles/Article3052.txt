 EEOC says accord in a disability case is warning to firms   By Anonymous, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 20, 1998  The EEOC is heralding the settlement of a long-term disability claim by a man with severe depression as a warning to companies to treat physical and mental diseases equally. The EEOC had joined lawyers representing a man identified in court papers only as Leonard F. in a case against the Israel Discount Bank of New York, where the man worked before severe depression disabled him.
