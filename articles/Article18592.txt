 New Securities Issues   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 1994  Chase Manhattan Corp. -- $150 million of subordinated notes due Aug. 1, 2004, priced as 7.875s at 98.731 to yield 8.062%, according to MCM CorporateWatch. The issue, noncallable for five years, was priced at a spread of 94 basis points above Treasurys. Rated Baa-1 by Moody's Investors Service Inc. and single-A-minus by Standard & Poor's Ratings Group, the issue will be sold through underwriters led by Bear, Stearns & Co.  Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp. -- $100 million of debentures due Aug. 10, 2004, priced at par to yield 7.73%, according to MCM CorporateWatch. The issue, noncallable for five years, was priced at a spread of 61 basis points above Treasurys. The issue will be sold through underwriters led by CS First Boston Inc.  Showboat Inc. -- $120 million of senior subordinated notes due Aug. 1, 2009, priced at par to yield 13%, according to MCM CorporateWatch. The issue, noncallable for seven years, is rated single-B-2 by Moody's and single-B by S&P. The issue will be sold through underwriters led by Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp.
