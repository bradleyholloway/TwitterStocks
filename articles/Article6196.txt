 Automotive Brief -- Magna International Inc.: First-Quarter Net Rose 6% On 21% Increase in Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  May 9, 2003  Car-parts maker Magna International Inc. said net income rose 6% in the first quarter to $162 million from $153 million in the same period last year. On a per-share basis, net income was flat at $1.65 because there were more shares outstanding in the recent quarter.
