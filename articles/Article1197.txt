 MGM, Partners Reach Pact for Resort   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 2007  MGM Mirage announced a letter of intent in June with Kerzner to form a joint venture that would create a multibillion-dollar resort at the northern end of the Las Vegas Strip. The property in the Kerzner deal would be built on 40 of 78 acres owned by MGM Mirage, which would donate the land, valued at $20 million an acre. Kerzner, and now Istithmar, would provide cash equity. Kerzner will be responsible for the project's planning and conceptualization.
