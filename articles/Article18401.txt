 Concert business climbed above '91 but failed to top '90   By Cox, Meg, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1993  The ailing concert business was boosted by big-name bands, like Guns N' Roses and U2, in 1992. However, the recession kept ticket sales below 1990's record. Irish band U2 topped the lists of the year's top-grossing acts with $68 million.
