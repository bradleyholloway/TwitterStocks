 SEC chief to seek increased disclosure in secondary market for municipals   By Connor, John, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1993  SEC chairman Arthur Levitt has scheduled an Oct 18, 1993 session with top Wall Street executives in an apparent bid to sever the link between political payments and muni business. The plan, to be completed by early 1994, is for the SEC to prepare an interpretive release spelling out issuers' duties under the antifraud provisions of the federal securities laws.
