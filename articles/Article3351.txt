 Employers Remain Optimistic, Study On Hiring Finds   By Gray, Steven, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2004  The first-quarter hiring forecast across the four major U.S. regions improved from a year earlier, when about 20% of companies reported plans to hire more workers. Surveyed employers in the South expressed the most optimism about hiring. Their counterparts in the West and Northeast said they expect the same hiring level as in the final months of 2004. Midwestern employers said they expect modest declines in hiring.
