 At Yves Saint Laurent, Brand Lives On; Well Before His Death, Designer Handed Off Company He Founded   By Passariello, Christina, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 2008  The Yves Saint Laurent brand -- now part of French retail conglomerate PPR SA's Gucci Group luxury- goods division -- lives on. In 1999, pharmaceutical company Sanofi SA, which had taken a majority stake in the company in 1993, sold the label and its lucrative perfume business to Gucci Group.
