 New Securities Issues   The Wall Street Journal,  May 21, 1992  Commonwealth Edison Co. -- $243 million two-part issue of first mortgage bonds, priced through underwriters led by Salomon Brothers Inc. The first part, consisting of $103 million of first mortgage bonds, due May 15, 1995, was priced as 6 1/8% at 99.70 to yield 6.236%. The noncallable bonds were priced at a spread of 57 basis points above the Treasury's three-year note. The second part, consisting of $140 million of first mortgage bonds, due May 15, 2008, was priced as 8% at 99.11 to yield 8.10%. The noncallable bonds were priced at a spread of 85 basis points above the Treasury's 10-year note. Both parts are rated single-A-3 by Moody's and single-A-minus by S&P.  .
