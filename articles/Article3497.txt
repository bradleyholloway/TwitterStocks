 Harrah's, Keppel Narrow Focus   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2005  Besides the Harrah's-Keppel Land consortium, the other bidders for the Marina Bay casino resort are a pairing of U.S.-based MGM Mirage and Singapore's CapitaLand Ltd.; Las Vegas Sands Corp. of the U.S.; Singapore-listed Genting International PLC; and a consortium comprising Australia's Publishing & Broadcasting Ltd. and Hong Kong's Melco International Development Ltd.
