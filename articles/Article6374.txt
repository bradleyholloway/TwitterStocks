 Business Brief -- NACCO INDUSTRIES INC.: Operating Subsidiary Buys Interests in Mine Companies   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 2000  Nacco Industries, Inc., Mayfield Heights, Ohio, said its operating subsidiary, North American Coal Corp., Dallas, acquired interests giving it 100% ownership of two lignite-mining operations. For $128 million, North American Coal acquired interests in the mines and in undeveloped lignite reserves from Phillips Coal Co., Richardson, Texas.
