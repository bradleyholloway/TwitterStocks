 Concession on Haitian military leaders clouds Clinton's modest public-opinion gain, poll shows   By Birnbaum, Jeffrey H, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1994  Although President Clinton averted a violent invasion of Haiti and boosted his standing with the American people, a Wall Street Journal/NBC News poll indicates that the American people strongly dislike Clinton's concession that allows the military leaders to stay in Haiti even after they step down.
