 Numico   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 13, 2004  Baby food and clinical food producer Numico, owner of brands such as Olvarit, Bambix and Cow & Gate, said it posted a second-quarter net profit, and increased its growth targets for the year. Net profit was 49 million euros ($60 million), compared with a net loss of 104 million euros in the year-ago quarter.
