 Borland International Says 2nd-Period Net Exceeded Predictions   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 1989  SCOTTS VALLEY, Calif. -- Borland International Inc. said sales and earnings for its second quarter ended Sept. 30 will exceed most analysts' expectations. It also said it completed a 5-for-1 reverse stock split in preparation for its first U.S. public offering.
