 Kirschner Medical Corp.: Firm Agrees to Sell Stake to Columbia Hospital Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 1992  Kirschner Medical Corp and Columbia Hospital Corp signed a letter of intent under which Columbia would acquire a 58% stake, or about 3.7 million shares of common stock, in Kirschner. In exchange, the maker of orthopedic products and medical-video systems would receive control of Columbia's orthopedic unit.
