 Teradata Corp. Sells 3.4 Million Common In Its Initial Offering   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1987  The maker of computers for data-base processing said the offering was increased to three million shares from the original 2.4 million because the issue was oversubscribed. Underwriters exercised overallotment options to bring the total to 3.4 million shares. The offering brings Teradata's total shares outstanding to about 13 million, with about 76% of the shares held by the company's founders and private investors.
