 Britax Issues Child-Seat Recall   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 2004  Britax said no injuries have been reported because of the defect, which was discovered in company testing. Britax found that the adjuster strap could slip within the metal buckle that controls the tightness of the shoulder harness. If the strap slips far enough, the harness could loosen and the child could fall forward in an accident.
