 Judge Is Asked to Hold Robins Chief in Contempt   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1987  In a federal court filing in Richmond, Va., a committee representing Dalkon Shield users asked the judge to hold E. Claiborne Robins Jr. personally responsible for $2.2 million in payments the pharmaceutical maker allegedly made without required court permission. The committee said Mr. Robins should be fined, and possibly jailed, if the money isn't recovered promptly.
