Andrea Cuomo, the father of Lieut. Gov. Mario M. Cuomo, died Wednesday at Queens Hospital Center in Jamaica following a stroke suffered several weeks ago. Mr. Cuomo, who had owned a grocery store in Jamaica and lived in Holliswood, Queens, was 79 years old. Besides his wife, Immaculata, and his son Mario, he is survived by another son, Frank, of Copiague, L.I.; a daughter, Marie Vecchio of Mineola, L.I., and 11 grandchildren.
