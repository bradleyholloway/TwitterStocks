 Business Brief -- SYSCO CORP.: December Sales Contribute To 9.4% Gain in Earnings   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1998  Sysco Corp, Houston, said its net income for the fiscal second quarter ended Dec 27, 1997 rose 9.4% to $83.6 million from $76.4 million a year earlier. The food-service distribution concern said revenue for the quarter rose 4.9% to $3.79 billion.
