 OTC Focus: Nasdaq Plans Early Trading in Listed Stocks   By Calian, Sara, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1992  The Nasdaq OTC market's plans to trade listed stocks on an all-electronic system before the start of the normal market day are examined. The Nasdaq will begin the trading on Nov 23, 1992. In OTC trading on Sep 14, the Nasdaq Composite Index climbed 1.30 points, or 0.22%, to 589.12.
