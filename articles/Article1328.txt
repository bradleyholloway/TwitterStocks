 A Town Becomes Alive with Zombies, Who Act a Little   By Ansberry, Clare, The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 1990  The remake of George Romero's 1969 classic horror film "Night of the Living Dead" has many residents in the rural area of Washington PA, where the film is being made, acting as zombie extras in the movie. The making of the film and its effect on the residents there are discussed.
