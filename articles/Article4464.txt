 Corporate News: Harry Winston Takes Rocks Off BHP's Hands   By MacDonald, Alex, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 2012  Globally diversified miner BHP Billiton Ltd. has agreed to sell its Canadian Ekati diamond-mine interest and marketing operations to diamond-miner and jewelry-retailer Harry Winston Diamond Corp. for $500 million in cash, bringing an end to its involvement in the diamond industry.
