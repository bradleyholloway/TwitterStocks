 Accountants' Group Plans to Trim Staff, Raise Dues   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 1992  The 305,000-member American Institute of Certified Public Accountants plans to reduce its operating budget by $4 million through a 5% staff cut and other measures by Jul 31, 1992. A dues increase in the second half of 1992 is also scheduled.
