 Letters to the Editor: Renaming Airport Is Much Too Costly   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1998  Rep James P. Moran (D-VA) responds to the Jan 5, 1998 editorial supporting an attempt to rename the Washington National Airport after Ronald Reagan. Moran argues that the name change would impose significant costs on area businesses, which would have to change stationery, business cards and the like.
