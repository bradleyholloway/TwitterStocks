 VNU Nears Deal on Sale of Magazine Unit   By By Charles Goldsmith, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 20, 2001  VNU's consumer-magazine unit publishes about 150 titles, primarily in the Netherlands, Belgium, Hungary and the Czech Republic. The division had 2000 sales of 823 million euros. VNU is selling the subsidiary, which was once core to VNU's business, to help finance its 2.6 billion euro acquisition last year of U.S. media company ACNielsen Corp., the market-research firm. VNU also owns Nielsen Media Research, best known for its U.S. television-ratings service.
