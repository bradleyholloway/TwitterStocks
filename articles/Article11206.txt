 Letters to the Editor: Crisis Facing Thrift Industry   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1989  I read with shock and disappointment your Jan. 6 article about Federal Deposit Insurance Corp. Chairman William Seidman's testimony before the House Banking Committee. Mr. Seidman said he would welcome the removal of the federal government's guarantee of full faith and credit from notes issued by the Federal Savings and Loan Insurance Corp. in its efforts to sell insolvent savings and loan institutions.
