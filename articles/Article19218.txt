 Business Brief -- EXIDE CORP.: Cost-Cutting Efforts Yield 85% Leap in Net Income   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1999  Exide Corp said its fiscal second-quarter net income rose 85% for the quarter ended Oct 3, 199 to $4.2 million, or 20 cents a diluted share, vs net of $2.3 million, or 11 cents a share, a year earlier.
