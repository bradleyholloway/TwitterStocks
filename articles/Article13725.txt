 World News: U.S., Canada Set Deal On 'Buy American'   By Barkley, Tom; Pulizzi, Henry J, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 2010  Last year's U.S. stimulus package required that manufactured products used in projects paid for with federal stimulus funds be made in the U.S. While the restrictions were meant to exempt countries such as Canada that have existing trade treaties with the U.S. and have signed on to the World Trade Organization's government procurement pact, the Canadian government in the 1990s excluded its provinces and towns from those rules.
