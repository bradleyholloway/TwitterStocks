 Pink flamingos are out, but here come the concrete geese   By Stern, Gabriella, The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 1995  In the hierarchy of lawn ornaments, concrete geese have become golden. Ann Taylor of West Bloomfield MI is among folks all over the country who enjoy dressing up their concrete lawn geese in all kinds of outfits, from brides and grooms, to hula dancers to bearded Amish farmers.
