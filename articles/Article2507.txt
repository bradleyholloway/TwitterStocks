 Public Storage to Pay $408 Million in Stock To Buy Storage Trust   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 13, 1998  Public Storage is a real-estate investment trust that has interests in 1,089 self-storage facilities, making it the largest REIT in the storage business. Storage Trust Realty, of Columbia, Mo., is also a REIT that owns and manages 237 self-storage centers in 16 states.
