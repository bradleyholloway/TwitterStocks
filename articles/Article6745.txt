 America, the Source Of Russia's Problems   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 2007  In my many years in Russia, I found it impossible to convince many of my Russian friends that an average farmer in Wichita or an average worker at a factory in Denver does not wake up every morning with the thought in his mind of "what can I do today to put Russia down?" There is truly a belief out there that, the demise of Cold War notwithstanding, America does want to humiliate Russia and pursues that as a goal.
