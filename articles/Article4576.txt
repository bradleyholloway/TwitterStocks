 Elsinore Hires Chanin In Attempt to Rescue Its Troubled Casino   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 11, 1985  Mr. Chanin, 45, a former executive vice president of Wickes Cos., will be joined on the board by Joseph Harch, a Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc. vice president. Messrs. Chanin and Harch will fill vacancies created by the departures of Mr. Maxey and Jay A. Pritzker, 62, Hyatt Corp. chairman, who resigned earlier this month. Mr. Pritzker and his family own 21% of Elsinore's 9.8 million common shares outstanding.
