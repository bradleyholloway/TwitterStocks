 LTV Corp. Sets Another Price Rise On Flat-Rolled Steel   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1986  LTV Corp., exploiting a 125-day-old strike at its largest competitor, USX Corp., announced its third price increase on flat-rolled steel since October.  The nation's No. 2 steelmaker said it plans to slash current discounts by 20% on flat-rolled products, which account for nearly half of domestic steel shipments. The higher prices for hot-rolled, cold-rolled and galvanized sheet steel take effect on deliveries after March 28.
