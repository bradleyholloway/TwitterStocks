 Abreast of the market: Industrials climb 19.52, but finish well below the day's best levels   By Mollison, Caitlin, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 1994  Inflation fears eased after the release on Sep 13, 1994 of weaker-than-expected August consumer prices, helping stocks to moderate gains yet falling from their best levels of the day in choppy bond-market action. The Dow Jones Industrial Average climbed 19.52 to 3879.86.
