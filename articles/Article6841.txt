 Investor's Calendar   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 2004  Retailers Report Results: Best Buy reports Wednesday, followed by Circuit City Friday. On Tuesday we'll see the Commerce Department's tally of retail sales for August.  Airlines Struggle: US Airways could be forced to seek Chapter 11 bankruptcy-court protection from creditors as early as today, after a faction of its pilots' union leadership council blocked a plan to put a cost-cutting proposal out to a vote last week. Delta Air Lines said it would slash 7,000 jobs and abandon its Dallas-Fort Worth hub to cut $5 billion in annual costs.  Eisner Plans Departure: Under fire from shareholders, Michael Eisner said he plans to step down as Walt Disney's chief executive when his contract expires in two years.
