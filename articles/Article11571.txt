 Legal Morass: Hundreds of Businesses Wait and Run Up Bills as Tort Case Drags On   By Harlan, Christi, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1992  In what may be the largest toxic litigation currently in process in the US, Harold Nix and other lawyers have filed 36 lawsuits on behalf of 2,729 Lone Star Steel Co employees and former employees, naming 372 firms as defendants. Although few dispute there is some basis for litigation, the defendants say that many claims are spurious and show how a clever lawyer can tie up companies in litigation without specifying which firms did what to whom.
