 Dole advocates charity tax credit, blasts Clinton for veto of abortion bill   By Seib, Gerald F; Stout, Hilary, The Wall Street Journal,  May 24, 1996  Sen Robert Dole (R-KS), in an effort to show he is looking for private alternatives to government social programs, said that as president he would enact a tax credit for donations to private and religious charities. Dole also blasted President Clinton for his veto of a bill that would have banned certain late-term abortions.
