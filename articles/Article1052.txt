 Telecommunications Brief -- Symantec Corp.: Back-Tax Case to Be Settled; Veritas-Related Claim Remains   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 2006  Symantec Corp. agreed to pay $36 million to settle a claim by the Internal Revenue Service that it owed $100 million in back taxes, while the security- and storage-software company continues to dispute another claim that it owes $900 million in back taxes related to its recently acquired Veritas Software Inc.
