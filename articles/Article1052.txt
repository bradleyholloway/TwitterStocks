 Yankee Cos. Names Paul Montle Chairman, F.D. John President   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 1988  Paul J. Montle, 40-year-old chief executive officer of Yankee Cos., an energy-services concern, was named to the additional post of chairman, succeeding Graham E. Jones, 55, who was named to the new post of vice chairman.
