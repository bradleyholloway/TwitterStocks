 SCI Television Inc.'s Revamp Is Extended; Weak Response Cited   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 24, 1989  DENVER (AP) -- Investor George Gillett has been forced to extend the restructuring of SCI Television Inc., involving $507 million of junk bonds, because of a weak response from Wall Street, the company and investors said.  SCI said investors holding just $37,000 of the debt had agreed to Mr. Gillett's offer. SCI extended the deadline to Dec. 4 and said further extensions are possible.
