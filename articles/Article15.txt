 Ciba-Geigy to Reduce Holdings in Alza Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 25, 1985  PALO ALTO, Calif. -- Ciba-Geigy Ltd. agreed to reduce its stake in Alza Corp., the pharmaceutical companies said.  Ciba-Geigy currently owns 2.9 million shares of nonvoting Class B Alza common, which would have become convertible into Class A common beginning April 1. If converted, those shares would have represented about a 25% stake in Alza.
