 Letters to the Editor: Shocking! Gambling On College Sports!   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 13, 2001  I was shocked, shocked to find that betting on college athletics is an issue requiring federal legislation ("No Contest," page one, April 3). As gambling on college athletics is popular on most campuses, perhaps college administrators and the NCAA ought to clean their own houses first. Betting on college athletes was a popular sport when I was in college in the 1950s. Obviously, nothing has changed.
