 Letters to the Editor: Economic Determinism Doesn't `Call' Priests   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 7, 2000  It is true that 50 years ago, when Ireland's economy was not doing well, many young Irish men flocked to the priesthood. It is also true that there is a vocation boom in Nigeria at this particular time, when the impact of corruption is clearly visible on the economy. However, it amounts to nothing short of economic determinism solely to attribute the abundance of priests to economic decline and the shortage of priests to economic prosperity.
