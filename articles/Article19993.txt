 Politics & Economics: Business Optimism In Japan Is Hurt By Rising Prices   By Hayashi, Yuka, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 2006  Still, large manufacturers said they expect business conditions to improve. The diffusion index among large nonmanufacturers rose to 18 from 17, while the index among small manufacturers was unchanged at seven. The key index "is already at a very high level," said Kiichi Murashima, chief economist at Nikko Citigroup. "I believe the upward trend remains intact, but the pace of improvement is likely to slow."
