 Small business: All in the family: The parent trap   By Shellenbarger, Sue, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1994  Some 41% of the 25 million people running home-based businesses have children 18 and under, compared with only 19% of self-employed people who work outside the home. An article examines how several such individuals successfully juggle the role of parent and home-based entrepreneur.
