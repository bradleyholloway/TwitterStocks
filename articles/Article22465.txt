 TransCanada's Profit From Ongoing Lines Down 10% in Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1998  TransCanada, a Calgary energy services concern, said net from continuing operations totaled 154 million Canadian dollars (US$99.8 million), or 30 Canadian cents a share, down from C$172 million, or 34 Canadian cents a share, in the like period of 1997. The 1997 amounts were restated to reflect the recent merger.
