 Western Co. Reaches Accord With Lenders On Unsecured Debt   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1986  FORT WORTH, Texas -- Western Co. of North America said it reached an agreement with two of its unsecured bank lenders that will help it to restructure its debt to financial institutions.  The troubled oil field services concern said agreements were reached with lenders under the company's $500 million and $30 million credit facilities. The $500 million facility was arranged through a 16-member bank group led by Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co., while the smaller facility was arranged by Chemical Bank.
