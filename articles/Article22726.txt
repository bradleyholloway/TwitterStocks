 Business Brief -- Federal-Mogul Corp.: On 14% Decline in Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 2, 2001  Federal-Mogul Corp. reported a fourth-quarter net loss of $337.7 million, or $4.80 a share, compared with profit of $60.2 million, or 79 cents a diluted share, a year earlier, after twice lowering analysts' expectations in January.
