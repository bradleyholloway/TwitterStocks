 Bay Area -- Lunchbox / Mijita Cocina Mexicana: A Waterfront Taqueria   By Carlton, Jim, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 2012  The lunch menu at the Financial District restaurant features regional Mexican dishes, including $8 sopa de albondigas (beef and pork meatballs in a tomato-based soup), $5.50 quesadilla mijita (a cheese quesadilla with herbs and roasted chiles), and $5 taco de pescado, with fried or grilled cod.
