 Mining Brief -- WMC Resources Ltd.: Firm's Books May Be Opened To Prospective Bidders   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 24, 2004  WMC Resources Ltd. may soon open its books to prospective buyers as the Australian miner fights a 7.4 billion Australian dollar (US$5.8 billion) hostile bid from Xstrata PLC, a person familiar with the situation said. "It is pretty likely that WMC will set up a data room," the person said.
