 Broadway Strike Continues as Talks Remain Stalled   By By Brooks Barnes and Nancy Keates, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 2003  While estimates of the impact vary, producers say the three-day strike, which is over how many musicians should be mandated in Broadway-theater orchestra pits, has so far caused about $5 million in lost ticket sales. New York tourism officials say it has cost surrounding hotels, restaurants, tour groups, limousine drivers and other collateral businesses at least another $3 million to $5 million.
