 Business Brief -- Payless ShoeSource Inc.: Profit to Fall in 2nd Quarter Due to Disappointing Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 2001  Payless ShoeSource Inc. said it expects fiscal second-quarter profit to fall short of Wall Street estimates because of disappointing sales and more markdowns. The Topeka, Kan., discount-shoe retailer estimates net income for the period ending Aug. 4 to be $1.45 to $1.50 a diluted share, down sharply from a Thomson Financial/First Call forecast of $2.44 a diluted share.
