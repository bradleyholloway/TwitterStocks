 Tin Brokers Sue Council Over Losses of $160 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1987  A group of nine London tin brokers sued to recoup about $160 million in losses from member nations of the International Tin Council.  Tinco Realisations, an informal body representing brokers on the London Metal Exchange, filed suit in the London High Court against the 23 member countries, the tin council and the European Common Market.
