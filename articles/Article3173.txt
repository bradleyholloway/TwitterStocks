 Business Brief -- Skandia Forsakrings AB: Second-Quarter Net Loss Reverses Year-Earlier Profit   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 2003  Skandia Forsakrings AB swung to a second-quarter net loss of 168 million Swedish kronor ($20.6 million) from a net profit of 1.75 billion kronor in the year-earlier period. The earlier results were boosted by a capital gain of 2.02 billion kronor on the sale of its asset-management business to DnB Holding ASA of Norway.
