 GM May End Auto Venture with Daewoo   By White, Joseph B, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1991  General Motors Co indicated that its 13-year-old partnership with Daewoo Group could end unless disputes between it and the South Korean auto maker over management and funding of their 50-50 manufacturing venture are resolved. GM has met with South Korean government officials to discuss the issue.
