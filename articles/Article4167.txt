 Corporate News: Suzlon Plans China Export Hub   By Oster, Shai, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 2010  Mr. Tanti, speaking on the sidelines of the World Economic Forum's annual summer meeting of business leaders, politicians and others, said the planned export of 120 megawatts of wind turbines to Brazil marks the first time Suzlon is using its low-cost manufacturing and supply base in China for sales overseas and is part of broader plan to increase exports from China.
