 School Specialty Inc.: Sale to Bain Is Called Off After 'Disappointing Results'   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 2005  The $1.5 billion sale of education supply company School Specialty Inc. to investment firm Bain Capital Inc. won't happen this week as expected. School Specialty, of Greenville, Wis., said the sale has been called off because of its disappointing results for the last two months. The deal might still happen, and it could be at a lower price.
