 Leading the News: AMR Offers Stock-Options Deal to Employees   By By Scott McCartney, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 2003  AMR will issue options to purchase nearly 38 million additional shares of its common stock, according to the tentative agreements signed Monday between the airline's three unions and management. Details of the options plans were still being finalized and were released to union members yesterday. The tentative agreements still must be ratified by April 15, or AMR has said it will have to file for bankruptcy-court protection in order to preserve dwindling cash and assets.
