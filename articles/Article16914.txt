 BP Venture In Russia Hit as CFO Steps Down   By Osborn, Andrew; Chazan, Guy, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 2008  BP PLC sustained one of the most serious blows yet in its fight to hold on to its lucrative Russian oil venture Monday, as the operation's chief financial officer quit amid the increasingly rancorous dispute between its British and local owners. The health of TNK-BP, Russia's third-largest oil producer, also has repercussions for international oil markets; Russia is the second- largest exporter of crude after Saudi Arabia and sits on the biggest reserves of oil outside the Middle East and Venezuela.
