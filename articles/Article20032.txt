 Stock Gains Heat Up as Oil Pulls Back, Helping the Dow Hit Two-Month High   By McKay, Peter A, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 2006  Handicapping the Fed's next move has become Wall Street's obsession, with expectations shifting rapidly one way or the other, sometimes within the same day.  "I'd like to see the Fed raise rates by another quarter [percentage] point to 5.5%, but I'm not sure that's what's going to happen," said Peter Kretzmer, senior economist at Bank of America. "No one -- possibly including the Fed itself -- knows what it's going to do at this point. It should be an interesting meeting."
