 CBS Inc. Names Jay Kriegel to Post In Public Relations   By By Peter W. Barnes, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1987  Mr. Kriegel, 47 years old, is a longtime associate of Laurence A. Tisch, CBS's president and chief executive officer. Among other things, he worked from 1975 to 1978 as director of special projects for Loews Corp., the insurance, hotel and tobacco concern that controls 25% of CBS shares. Mr. Tisch is chairman of Loews.  Mr. Kriegel, who has headed his own communications, marketing and lobbying consulting firm since 1983, has been a behind-the-scenes adviser to Mr. Tisch, particulary in public relations and news operations, since Mr. Tisch was named CBS's chief executive in September 1986.
