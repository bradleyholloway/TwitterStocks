 Art: Beyond Fragile Fantasies   By Wilkin, Karen, The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 2011  Assembled mainly from the museum's own extensive collections, with a few key additions, the exhibition draws upon the latest scholarship to expand and correct our conceptions of Chagall and his work, reminding us of how daring his efforts could be and placing him in the wider context of his peers.
