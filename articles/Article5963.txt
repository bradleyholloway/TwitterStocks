 Gold futures receive a boost from dollar   By Lucchetti, Aaron, The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1997  The weakening of the US dollar aided battered gold prices on May 9, 1997. In trading at the Comex division of the New York Mercantile Exchange, the gold futures contract for June delivery gained $4.50 to close at $349.30 a troy ounce, the highest level since Apr 1 and the most dramatic rise since February.
