 Stocks slip in Tokyo as profit-taking continues, rise in London on bullishness about lower rates   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1995  Tokyo stocks slipped Dec 11, 1995 as profit-taking continued following the decline on Dec 8 from the highest level since January. London shares ended near session highs, buoyed by an optimism about interest-rate cuts and an early surge on Wall Street. In Tokyo, the Nikkei 225-stock index fell 60.19 to 19226.78. In London, the Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-share index climbed 22.1 points to 3652.1, and in Frankfurt, the DAX 30-stock index rose 5.32 points to 2272.82.
