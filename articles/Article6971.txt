 Transportation Brief -- Amtrak: Companies to Pay $24.8 Million To Settle Overcharging Case   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 2005  Foreign and U.S. companies will pay the government $24.8 million to settle allegations they inflated claims on Amtrak projects to electrify part of the Northeast Corridor, the Justice Department said.
