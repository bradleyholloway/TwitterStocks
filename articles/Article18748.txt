 Amre's Wagner quits as chairman; Charge planned in 4th period   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1995  Ronald I. Wagner resigned as chairman of Amre Inc to "pursue personal interests and travel." No successor was immediately named. Amre said that in connection with Wagner's departure, the company will incur a noncash charge of about $4 million in the fourth quarter in settlement of Wagner's employment contract and loan obligation to the company.
