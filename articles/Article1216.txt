 IPO Outlook: For IPOs Now, Timing Seems Off; Market Conditions Could Vex Debuts; Och-Ziff: Hot or Not?   By Cowan, Lynn, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 2007  "My guess is a lot of the deals scheduled are not going to get done," said Mr. [Richard Schultz]. "People are afraid. And when people are afraid, they pull back on new stocks."  "Basically, people right now are saying 'I need to protect my profits in Google and Apple,'" said Mr. [Scott Sweet]. "And I don't need IPOs now -- the market is too dangerous."  "There's also chatter running into this week about there being a Chinese IPO bubble," said Mr. [John Fitzgibbon]. "That probably has had an effect."
