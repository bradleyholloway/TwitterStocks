 Unilever's profit rose 15% last year; Firm to sell specialty-chemicals unit   By Rose, Matthew, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1997  Anglo-Dutch consumer-products giant Unilever Group announced a 15% rise in pretax profit for 1996 and also said it plans to sell its specialty-chemicals division. Unilever earnings for 1996 rose to BP2.66 billion from BP2.32 billion in 1995, on revenue of BP33.5 billion, up 6%.
