 WEEKEND JOURNAL; Books: Children's Books   By Meghan Cox Gurdon, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 2008  For not only did Ali float like a butterfly and sting like a bee, he'd also roar, "I am king of the world!" and "I am the greatest!" So at first, on opening this picture book for children ages 4-8, one is inclined to cheer the melodramatic rendering of the life of the tempestuous black athlete who was born Cassius Clay in 1942.
