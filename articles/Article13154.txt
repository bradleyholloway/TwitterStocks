 Shinsei Bank Wins Advantage In Bidding for Taiwan Lender; Japanese Firm Is Selected As Preferred Candidate For Control of Chang Hwa   By Kate Linebaugh in Hong Kong and Jason Dean in Taipei, Taiwan, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 6, 2005  Shinsei was told last week that it had been chosen as preferred bidder, the knowledgeable people said. That status doesn't mean a deal with Shinsei is certain, these people said, but rather that Chang Hwa and its adviser, Credit Suisse Group's Credit Suisse First Boston, are now negotiating with Shinsei to see whether they can finalize a deal based on its offer.
