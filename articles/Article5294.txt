 Australia's Unemployment Rises   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 11, 1991  The Bureau of Statistics said that Australia's unemployment rate rose to a seasonally adjusted 10.2% in Sep 1991 from 9.8% in August, and was up from 7.4% in Sep 1990. The number of persons employed, after seasonal adjustment, dropped to 7,705,200 from 7,728,600 in August and 7,873,300 a year earlier.
