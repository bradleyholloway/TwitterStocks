 Price Communications Buys Publishing Unit from SFN   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 1985  Price Communications Corp., a broadcasting concern, said it acquired New York Law Publishing Co. from SFN Cos. for $20.5 million.  New York Law Publishing Co. publishes the daily New York Law Journal, the weekly National Law Journal and various legal newsletters and law books. The purchase is Price's first acquisition in publishing.
