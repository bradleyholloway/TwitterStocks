 BHP Billiton: Olympic Dam Mine Won't Buy From Oil Search, Exxon Project   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 2005  Anglo-Australian miner BHP Billiton said it won't buy from Oil Search Ltd. and Exxon Mobil Corp.'s $2.3 billion Papua New Guinea Gas project. BHP decided not to extend a conditional agreement to buy from PNG Gas to power its Olympic Dam mine.
