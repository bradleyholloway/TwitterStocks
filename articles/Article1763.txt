 A Stingy Spirit Lifts Airline's Profit   By Nicas, Jack, The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 2012  In five years, Spirit has gone from the cusp of bankruptcy to leadership of ultra-low-cost carriers, the airline industry's fastest-growing niche. Frontier Airlines, a loss-making unit of Republic Airways Holdings Inc., is dabbling with the ultra-low-cost model in the back of its planes, where it is considering packing in more seats and charging for carry-ons.
