 Publicis Groupe SA: Ad Firm's Revenue Rises 7.4% Amid Increase in New Business   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 2005  Publicis Groupe SA, the world's fourth-largest advertising company, said third-quarter revenue rose 7.4% on an increase in new business won this year. Revenue in the July-to-September period rose to 1 billion euros ($1.2 billion) from 932 million euros a year ago, in line with an average of analysts' forecasts.
