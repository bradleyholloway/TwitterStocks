 Letters to the Editor: Investment Advice? Try the Racetrack   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1999  When I was about 12 years old my father made the ultimate comment on investment advisers ("The Best Investment Advice? Ignore Investment Advice," editorial page, July 21): He took me to the racetrack. We got there late and, by the side of the road, someone was selling "Sam's Green Sheet," which showed that Sam had picked the winners of the first three races already. (Of course, it was later discovered that Sam printed up a new green sheet after each race, but that was another issue.)
