 Electronics, Music Industries Set Pact on Digital Recording Use in the Home   By Reilly, Patrick M, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 1991  Leaders of the consumer electronics and music industries said they reached a compromise on digital home recording that should expand the market for a new generation of home and portable audio equipment. The agreement calls for the introduction of legislation in Congress that includes placing royalties on digital tapes and CDs.
