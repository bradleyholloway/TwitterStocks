 Arkansas Best Bid By 2 Partnerships Totals $20 a Share   By By Bill Paul, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 1988  Gemini Partners L.P. and Transport Partners L.P. said they plan a tender offer of $20 a share, or $181.6 million, for the 89.8% of Arkansas Best Corp. stock they don't already own.  A spokesman for Gemini Partners said Gemini and Transport yesterday raised their stake in Arkansas Best to 10.2%, or about one million shares.  The offer will be for each of Arkansas Best's 10.1 million common shares outstanding as of March 15. The partnerships also will offer to buy Arkansas Best's $50 million of convertible debentures outstanding for $771.01 for each $1,000 face amount.
