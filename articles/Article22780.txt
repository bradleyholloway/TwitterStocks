 Business Brief -- Barnes & Noble.com Inc.: Free Shipping to Be Offered For Orders of Several Items   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 3, 2001  [Barnes] & Noble.com Inc., following a similar move by Amazon.com, is offering free shipping on orders of two or more items. Barnes & Noble.com said it had considered such an offer even before Amazon, Seattle, launched its promotion June 20.
