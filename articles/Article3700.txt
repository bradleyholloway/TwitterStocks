 China's Gome Electrical Raises $837.6 Million, May Need More   By Poon, Aries, The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 2007  Gome Electrical, listed in Hong Kong and controlled by Chinese billionaire Wong Kwong-yu, said it sold 110 million new shares at HK$13.30 each and raised HK$1.46 billion. The shares were priced toward the lower end of the indicative price range of HK$13.25 to HK$13.68.  The company raised an additional HK$5.09 billion by issuing zero- coupon convertible bonds due 2014, with an initial conversion price of HK$19.95.
