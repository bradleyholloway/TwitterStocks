 Rexene reverses policy on bids, says it won't oppose $16 a share   By Ewing, Terzah, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 1997  Rexene Corp, facing a proxy fight over its recent rejection of a $16-a-share bid by closely held Huntsman Corp, did an about-face and said it won't oppose future offers of that size so long as they are "fully financed" and the transaction can be completed in 60 days.
