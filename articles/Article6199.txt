 Consumer Products Brief: Carlsberg AS   The Wall Street Journal,  May 9, 2003  Danish brewer Carlsberg AS said it swung into the red during the first quarter, due to lackluster sales and the strength of the euro. Carlsberg posted a net loss of 126 million kroner ($19.3 million) for the first quarter, compared with net profit of 36 million kroner a year earlier.
