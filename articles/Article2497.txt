 Business Brief -- PACKARD BELL NEC INC.: Patent-Infringement Suit Is Filed Against Chuntex   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1998  Packard Bell NEC Inc and its wholly owned subsidiary, Vantus Technologies Inc, said they have filed a patent-infringement suit against Chuntex Electronics Co, Taiwan, and two of its US subsidiaries. The suit filed in US District Court in Sacramento CA alleges violations of as many as five patents in desktop and portable computers.
