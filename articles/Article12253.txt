 New Stock Listings   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 1997  LEGEND PROPERTIES Inc., whose stock symbol is LPROE, was delisted from the Nasdaq Stock Market on Aug. 1. Because of information provided by the National Association of Securities Dealers, yesterday's New Stock Listings incorrectly said the company had joined the Nasdaq SmallCap Market as a seasoned offering from the American Stock Exchange and that its stock symbol was LPROV. (WSJ Oct. 7, 1997)
