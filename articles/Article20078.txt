 Ford, Chrysler Step Up Deal Incentives   By Stoll, John D, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 2007  Chrysler will offer as much as $5,000 in consumer incentives on some 2007 model-year vehicles and "low-rate" financing on certain vehicles this month under a new "America's Hottest Products" campaign. The campaign offers buyers incentives on 2007 model-year vehicles and will last at least through the end of this month.
