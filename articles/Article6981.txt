 Style -- Formalwear: Survey Says: Leopard Print   By Dodes, Rachel, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 2005  It may not be the new black, but leopard-print was the third most popular look for dresses overall. At New York's Henry Street Settlement benefit, 17% of women wore dresses with trains that dragged on the ground, even though it was raining outside. And while we couldn't find any fur at the Denver Symphony Opera's gala, 800 miles south at the Houston Grand Opera's opening, 17% of women were wearing fur.
