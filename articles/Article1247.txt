 International Brief -- Orkem S.A.: Coates Unit Buys ICI Line Of Inks in West Germany   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1989  The West German unit of Coates Brothers PLC, a specialty chemicals concern, acquired Imperial Chemical Industries PLC's screen printing inks business in West Germany, ICI Lacke Faren G.m.b.H., for #22 million ($34.7 million), the two British companies said.
