 Ohio strike that is crippling GM plants is tied to plan to outsource brake work   By Blumenstein, Rebecca; Christian, Nichole M, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 12, 1996  Individuals close to the situation say the local strike in Ohio that is rapidly shutting down General Motors Corp plants across North America grows out of GM's plan to buy antilock brake systems for a future model through an outsourcing plan with Robert Bosch GmbH.
