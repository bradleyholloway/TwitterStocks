 Information Display's Announced Agreement Is Denied by Creditors   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1985  The creditors' committee of Information Displays Inc. disputed the company's announcement of an agreement on a plan of reorganization.  Separately, the current management of the company filed suit in federal court, alleging that, among other things, the former management misstated results for 1981. The suit also named the company's auditor and investment banker.
