 At Last, Something to Put Next to the Lunar Rock   By By Rick Brooks, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 1995  First, Olympic marketers gave the world the Olympic brick. Now they're hoping to recycle portions of Olympic track into collectibles after the Summer Games in Atlanta.  "I'd love to have a piece of the finish line," says Bob Hollander, vice president of Atlanta Centennial Olympic Properties, which oversees sponsorship and licensing programs for the Atlanta Committee for the Olympic Games and the U.S. Olympic Committee. The groups will get a piece of the proceeds.
