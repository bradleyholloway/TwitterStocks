 Business Brief -- Boston Scientific Corp.: Maverick Balloon Catheter Is Set to Fight Competition   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 2001  Boston Scientific Corp., Natick, Mass., began selling a slim new balloon catheter that it hopes will help it regain the market share it lost to competitors over the past year. Balloon catheters, used in procedures called balloon angioplasties to open clogged coronary arteries, are long skinny tubes with balloons attached to the tips.
