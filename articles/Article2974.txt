 Business Brief -- Allied Waste Industries Inc.: Net Slides 85% on Flat Sales; 1st-Period Warning Is Issued   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 2002  Trash hauler Allied Waste Industries Inc. said its fourth-quarter net income slid 85% and it warned that results for the current quarter will be hurt by the weaker economy. The Scottsdale, Ariz., company's net fell to $5.5 million from a year-earlier net of $36.3 million.
