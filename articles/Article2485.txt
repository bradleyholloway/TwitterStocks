 Petco Adopts Plan Of Shareholder Rights Against Potential Bids   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 1998  Petco Animal Supplies Inc., after watching its stock get hammered in recent months, adopted a shareholder-rights plan to fend off potential acquisition bids.  "We think the stock is so tremendously undervalued, we wanted to protect shareholders," said Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Brian K. Devine in an interview, adding that Petco's stock is trading at 70% of the company's book value.
