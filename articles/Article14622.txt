 Recent SEC Filings   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 5, 1989  Boston Edison Co., shelf offering of up to $100 million face amount of debt on terms to be set at the time of sale. When combined with remaining unsold securities from a previous offering, the filing gives the company as much as $165 million face amount of securities available for sale, via Goldman, Sachs & Co. and Salomon Brothers Inc.  Aktiebolaget Svensk Exportkredit (Swedish Export Credit Corp.), shelf offering of up to $700 million face amount of debt securities, debt warrants and currancy warrants on terms to be set at the time of sale. When combined with remaining unsold securities from a previous offering, the filing gives the company as much as $752 million face amount of securities available for sale.
