 Blockbuster, Major Video Settle Suits, Revise Merger Terms   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1988  FORT LAUDERDALE, Fla. -- Blockbuster Entertainment Corp. and Major Video Corp. said all but one of the plaintiffs in lawsuits seeking to block their proposed merger agreed to drop their suits.  The two video rental concerns also said they agreed on new terms for the merger, which is to be executed through a stock swap, raising the number of Major Video shares needed for each newly issued Blockbuster share to 1.265 from 1.125.
