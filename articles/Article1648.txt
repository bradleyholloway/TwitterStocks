 Congress Is Seen Failing to Block Sale of F-15 Jets   By Greenberger, Robert S; Lubman, Sarah, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 1992  Pro-Israeli legislators criticized President Bush's decision to sell F-15 aircraft to Saudi Arabia, but Congress is not expected to gather enough votes to block the sale. The Saudis will receive a version of the McDonnell Douglas plane stripped of some of its state-of-the-art technology.
