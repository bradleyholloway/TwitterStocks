 Black's fund seeking more of O&Y debt   By Greenberg, Larry M; Pacelle, Mitchell, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 11, 1994  Apollo Real Estate Investments, a fund run by Leon Black, is attempting to become a bigger player in the restructuring of Olympia & York's US operations. Apollo has offered to buy about $140 million of debt secured by some of O&Y's best properties, including New York's World Financial Center.
