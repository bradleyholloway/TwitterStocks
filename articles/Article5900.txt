 As GM shifts gears, dread '14th floor' quits the 14th floor   By Blumenstein, Rebecca, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 1996  The 14th floor of General Motors Corp's headquarters in Detroit is no more. On Nov 11, 1996, GM ended an era when GM Chairman John F. Smith Jr and Vice Chairman Harry Pearce packed up the last of their belongings and moved to their new offices in Renaissance Center.
