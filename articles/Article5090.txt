 Reader's Digest Firm Completes Purchase Of Health Magazine   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1990  PLEASANTVILLE, N.Y. -- Reader's Digest Association Inc. said it completed the purchase of American Health magazine from American Health Partners for $29.1 million.  Reader's Digest, a closely held global publisher of magazines and books, said the magazine will be operated as part of its growing Reader's Digest Special Interest Magazine Group, which includes Travel Holiday, New Choices, and the Family Handyman.
