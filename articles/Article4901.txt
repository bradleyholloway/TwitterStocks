 Tracking Travel: Show of Hands May Speed Passport Checks   By O Brian, Bridget, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1992  Travel industry executives and customs and immigration officials from several countries are evaluating the use of infrared hand scans for use in airports to expedite passport checks. The scanner will measure the distance between finger joints and run a security check in two seconds. The system is designed by Recognition Systems.
