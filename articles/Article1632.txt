 Canadian 88 Energy Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 1992  Canadian 88 Energy Corp in Calgary Alberta Canada reported that, in a joint venture with its 1991 Diversified Energy Fund, it has acquired a portion of the Sturgeon Lake South D-3 oilfield from an undisclosed vendor, as of May 1, 1993. Terms of the transaction were not disclosed.
