 Blue Cross Operation, American Express Plan To Offer Billing Service   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1993  NEWARK, N.J.-- Blue Cross & Blue Shield of New Jersey Inc. and American Express Co. said they agreed to develop a health-care payment service simplifying billing and out-of-pocket payment for individual subscribers.  The new service, called Quattro, will be offered to selected Blue Cross & Blue Shield members and health-care providers starting in the fall. Employees of companies participating in Quattro will be automatically enrolled in the plan.
