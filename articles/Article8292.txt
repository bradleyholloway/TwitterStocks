 Hagadone Is Popular for Very Good Reasons   By Lotze, Keith A, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1992  Keith A. Lotze responds to the Sep 4, 1992 article "Hagadone Just About IS Coeur d'Alene," and says that there is no mention of the fact that everything Duane B. Hagadone does is first-rate, including design, development, construction, operation and maintenance. The opinion is stated that Hagadone has set a new standard for hotel, restaurant and resort excellence throughout the Northwest.
