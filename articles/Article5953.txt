 Chevron Seeks to Buy Online Marketplace It Helped to Launch   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 2001  Officials at closely held PetroCosm said Chevron aims to buy out the company's other shareholders, which include Texaco Inc., Ariba Inc., National Oil Well Inc., Requisite Technology Inc., KPMG Consulting Inc. and Crosspoint Venture Partners. The officials predict a deal in a week or two.
