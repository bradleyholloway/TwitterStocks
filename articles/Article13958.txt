 U.S. News: Pro-Union Boycott Hits Indiana House   By Nicas, Jack, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2012  Indiana Senate President Pro Tempore David Long, a Republican, said top lawmakers anticipated the House Democrats' boycott and scheduled a rare joint committee hearing on the identical House and Senate bills for Friday. Because the House session has yet to begin, the hearing will officially be on the Senate bill, but Mr. Long said the move would enable House Republicans to bypass a time-consuming hearing on their bill if the House session begins.
