 Potomac watch: Dole stays cooler than his comrades   By Gigot, Paul A, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 1996  Paul A. Gigot discusses presumed GOP presidential candidate Bob Dole's faltering campaign. Gigot criticizes Republicans for "beating the rats to the life boats," and Gov Pete Wilson for turning the abortion debate back on the GOP. Gigot's interview with Dole includes his campaign's strategy to cast President Clinton as a liberal and the chances of balancing the federal budget.
