 BCCI Used Letters of Credit to Guarantee Property Lending, New Documents Show   By Truell, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 23, 1991  Newly obtained documents showed that Bank of Credit & Commerce International used letters of credit in such unorthodox ways as guaranteeing property lending. The firm, which was seized by regulators Jul 5, 1991, has fought to prevent disclosure of records of its letters of credit.
