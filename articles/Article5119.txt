 AT&T wins job to rewire Saudi Arabia   By Keller, John J, The Wall Street Journal,  May 10, 1994  In one of the biggest ever international orders for telecommunications gear, AT&T Corp won a contract valued at more than $4 billion to rewire Saudi Arabia for advanced communications services. Competing offers had come from Siemens AG, L.M. Ericsson, Alcatel-Alsthom and NEC Corp.
