 Coca-Cola Amatil shares fall due to Philippine peso   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 1997  Shares of Australian soft-drinks company Coca-Cola Amatil Ltd fell 12% to A$15 (US$11.13) on Jul 11, 1997 as a sliding Philippine peso cast a shadow over the company's newest acquisition, Coca-Cola Bottlers Philippines Inc.
