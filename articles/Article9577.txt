 Energy Brief -- Royal Dutch/Shell Group: Jury Orders Punitive Damages For Concealment of Oil Leak   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 2003  A Midland, Texas, state-court jury ruled Texas-New Mexico Pipeline Co., a unit of Royal Dutch/Shell Group, was liable for $50 million in punitive damages because it hid a 1992 crude oil leak that damaged water wells and homes in a subdivision.
