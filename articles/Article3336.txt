 McTeer to Leave Fed for Texas A&M Job   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 2004  The Texas A&M Board of Regents named Mr. McTeer sole finalist to succeed Howard Graves, who resigned in August 2003 to battle cancer. He died a month later. The university's board must wait until Nov. 4 to consider Mr. McTeer's prospective position, as there is a 21-day public comment period mandated by state law.
