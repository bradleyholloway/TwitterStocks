 Fannie Arrives at a Deal to Sell $2.6 Billion in Unused Tax Credits   By Timiraos, Nick, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 2009  Fannie Mae said it reached an agreement to sell about $2.6 billion in unused low-income-housing tax credits to unnamed buyers and received approval for the deal from its federal regulator, the Federal Housing Finance Agency, in a filing Thursday with the Securities and Exchange Commission.
