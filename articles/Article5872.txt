 Advertising: Cordiant says Saatchi & Saatchi's president is leaving   By Beatty, Sally Goll; Parker-Pope, Tara, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1996  British advertising giant Cordiant said on Aug 9, 1996 that John Fitzgerald, president of its Saatchi & Saatchi unit, is leaving the agency. Fitzgerald was brought in just eight months earlier to help stabilize Saatchi after a management shake-up. Saatchi did not name a successor to Fitzgerald.
