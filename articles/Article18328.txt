 California Microwave Inc.: Firm to Post Quarterly Loss, Cites Disappointing Sales   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 1992  California Microwave Inc said it expects to report a net loss of about $.09 to $.14 a share for its fourth quarter, ending Jun 30, 1992, due to lower-than-expected sales. Analysts had estimated fourth-quarter net income of about $.24 a share. In the year-ago fourth quarter, net income was $2.5 million.
