 Media Brief -- CARMIKE CINEMAS INC.: Stock Slides After Warning Of Loss for Fourth Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 1999  Shares of Carmike Cinemas Inc fell 17% after the Columbus GA theater chain warned it would post a fourth-quarter loss. Analysts had expected earnings of 17 cents a share, according to First Call/Thomson Financial. In late NYSE trading Dec 3, 1999, Carmike closed down $1.875 at $9.4375 a share.
