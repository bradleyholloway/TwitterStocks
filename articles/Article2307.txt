 Sun-Diamond gets fine of $1.5 million in Espy affair   By Ingersoll, Bruce, The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 1997  Sun-Diamond Growers of California, the nation's largest fruit cooperative, was fined $1.5 million by a federal judge for bestowing illegal gifts on former Agricultural Secy Mike Espy and making illegal campaign contributions to his brother.
