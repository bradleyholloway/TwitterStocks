 AT&T is setting up data-service unit led by Unisys ex-official   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1995  AT&T Corp plans to announce Feb 14, 1995 that it has hired a former Unisys executive, Victor Millar, to head a new service that will use AT&T's massive networking and computer assets. The move is part of AT&T's efforts to set an aggressive entry into the corporate data-services market now ruled by IBM and EDS.
