 U.S. Service Sector Posts Solid Gain; Some Are Wary   By Dunham, Kemba J, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 2005  Service-sector hiring continued to grow in December, but at a slightly slower pace. The employment index dropped to 54.9 from 55 a month earlier. Some economists say this number put a damper on the report's optimism. "This suggests that we'll get the same pace of payroll service-sector growth that we saw in November and that's not terribly encouraging," says Mat Johnson, chief economist of ThinkEquity Partners, a San Francisco investment bank.
