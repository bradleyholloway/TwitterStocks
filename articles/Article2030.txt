 Raven Industries Inc. To Boost Dividend, Post Record Profit   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 1989  SIOUX FALLS, S.D. -- Raven Industries Inc. President and Chief Executive Officer David A. Christensen said the company will increase its quarterly cash dividend 25% and report that net income for the second quarter ended July 31 rose 24% to a record $1.2 million, or 38 cents a share.  He said that today Raven will announce a dividend rise to 7 1/2 cents a share from six cents a share, payable Oct. 13 to stock of record Sept. 20.  Mr. Christensen said Raven also expects to report today that sales rose 12% to $21.8 million from $19.4 million.
