 Shipping Brief -- Orient Overseas (International): Net Profit More Than Doubles On Increase in Shipping Rates   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 11, 2005  Higher shipping rates and increased capacity helped Orient Overseas (International) Ltd. more than double its net profit in 2004, the container-shipping and port operator said. Net profit rose to US$670.4 million from $329 million in 2003. Revenue increased 28% to $4.14 billion from $3.24 billion.
