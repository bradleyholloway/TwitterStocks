 Marilyn Quayle Plans To Seek Job In Law Firm   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 1989  Mr. Quayle's wife, Marilyn, plans to seek paid employment with a Washington law firm, if she can find a job that poses no conflict-of-interest problems for her husband, Mr. Quayle's spokesman David Beckwith said. If she accepted a job, she would be the first wife of a president or a vice president to hold a paying job during her husband's term.
