 ABC Aiming to Turn Off Radio Update of Its Soap   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1988  Since 1986, those unable to watch could catch up by listening to radio broadcasts of "The Cla'ence Update of ABC's All My Children." But ABC, after initially granting the radio show permission to air, now is suing to put a gag on Cla'ence and his 60-second radio broadcasts, which begin, "Hey babysitters, it's the Cla'ence update . . ."
