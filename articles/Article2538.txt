 Business Brief -- PLAYTEX PRODUCTS INC. Deal to Buy Diaper Genie From Mondial Confirmed   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 1999  Consumer and personal-products maker Playtex Products Inc, as expected, said it agreed to acquire the Diaper Genie diaper-disposal business from Mondial Industries LP. Playtex will pay $72 million and issue $50 million in convertible debt to Mondial.
