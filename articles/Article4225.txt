 Leggett Sees Increase In Per-Share Earnings For 4th Quarter, Year   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1986  CARTHAGE, Mo. -- Leggett & Platt Inc. said it expects fourth-quarter per-share earnings of about 54 cents, compared with a restated 47 cents a year earlier.  Earnings for the fourth quarter and year will include a gain of $2.5 million, or 14 cents a share, from the sale of a 50% interest in three plants. The year-earlier results were restated to reflect a 3-for-2 stock split paid in March.
