 OFF DUTY --- Cooking & Eating: WSJ Test Kitchen: Waffle Makers   By Miglore, Kristen, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 15, 2011  Wide grooves and an effective nonstick coating deflect even the stickiest burnt-on berries and chocolate chips -- important for a cooking surface that can't go anywhere near a sink. The hottest settings are timid -- and anything cooked at medium or below won't have any more texture than a steamed pudding -- but the relatively low heat and shallow pockets yield a waffle with one of the more evenly baked, golden shells.
