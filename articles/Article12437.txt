 Business Brief -- BORON, LEPORE & ASSOCIATES INC.: Loss of $1.1 Million Posted; Delays, Write-Down Cited   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1999  Boron, LePore & Associates Inc reported a 1999 first-quarter net loss of $1.1 million, or 9 cents a share, compared with year-earlier net income of $1.9 million, or 17 cents a diluted share.
