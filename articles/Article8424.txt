 International Brief -- Koninklijke Ahold NV: Food Retailer's 9-Month Net Rose 11% as Sales Grew 28%   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 1993  Ahold, based in Zaandam, said profit increased to 245.3 million guilders ($127.1 million) from 220.2 million guilders. Sales rose to 20.49 billion guilders from 15.99 billion guilders.
