 New Jersey Casinos July Winnings Rose 7.8% to $243.9 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1987  ATLANTIC CITY, N.J. -- The 12 casinos here reported that winnings in July rose 7.8% to the second-highest level in the industry's nine-year history, fueled by brisk gambling during the Fourth of July weekend.
