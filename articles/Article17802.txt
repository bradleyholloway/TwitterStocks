 MacGregor Says Plan To Sell Two Units Won't Be Completed   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 1988  EAST RUTHERFORD, N.J. -- MacGregor Sporting Goods Inc. said Oppenheimer-Palmieri Fund won't proceed with its previous plan to buy two MacGregor units.  The sale of Riddell Inc. and Equilink Licensing Corp. for about $44.5 million, plus payments based on future earnings, was to have been completed Jan. 31. But MacGregor said Oppenheimer-Palmieri was unable to obtain financing.
