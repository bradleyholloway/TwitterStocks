 Tokyo stocks decline by 2.6% early Monday in reaction to failed U.S.-Japan trade talks   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1994  Japanese stocks were sharply lower early on Feb 14, 1994 in the wake of failed trade talks between the US and Japan. The Nikkei Stock Average ended the morning session at 19475.65, down 515.05 points, or 2.6%. The Tokyo market was closed on Feb 11, 1994 due to a Japanese holiday. The British FT-SE 100-share index was down 28.1 points to 3378.9. In Frankfurt, the DAX 30-stock index lost 28.35 points to 2090.61.
