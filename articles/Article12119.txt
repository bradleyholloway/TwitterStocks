 Focus, drive and an eye for discounts, staples of Stemberg's business success   By Pereira, Joseph, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 1996  Thomas Stemberg of Staples Inc is profiled. Under Stemberg's direction, Staples has carved a niche in the $185 billion office-supply market. On Sep 4, 1996, Staples agreed to acquire its larger rival Office Depot Inc in a stock swap valued at $3.36 billion.
