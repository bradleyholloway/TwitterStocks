 Sound-Card Firm In Singapore Hits Sour Notes   By Tan, Jessica, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 2006  About S$161 million has been wiped from Creative's market capitalization, reducing it to S$866 million, while Chief Executive Sim Wong Hoo, who owns about 30.6% of Creative, has racked paper losses of nearly S$49 million.  Creative shares yesterday fell 2.9% to S$10.20 in Singapore. Creative's shares on the Nasdaq Stock Market fell 8.4% Monday to US$6.26. In trading yesterday, the stock was down a bit more.
