 Seagram Co. Reports Net Income Rose 29% For Third Quarter --- A Wall Street Journal News Roundup   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1987  Seagram attributed the rise to strong results from its international operations, strong sales of its wine coolers, and some stabilization of spirits consumption in the U.S., where consumption had been dropping. Net also was boosted by after-tax dividend income from Du Pont Co. shares held by the company, including $43 million for the latest quarter.
