 Can new CEO at Burger King help it abroad?   By Gibson, Richard, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1997  Grand Metropolitan PLC, disappointed at its Burger King Corp unit's failure to gain on McDonald's Corp overseas as it has in the US, abruptly replaced the burger chain's chairman and CEO, Robert C. Lowes, with Dennis Malamatinas. Paul E. Clayton was named president of Burger King's North American restaurant operations.
