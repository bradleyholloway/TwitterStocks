 ProSource Inc.: Second-quarter earnings to be 'significantly lower'   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1997  ProSource Inc, a food distributor, said it anticipates 1997 second-quarter earnings will be "significantly lower" than year-earlier profit. ProSource said it had net income of $1.2 million in the year-earlier quarter on net sales of $1.05 billion. ProSource became a publicly traded company in Nov 1996.
