OPPOSITE ATTRACTION The Lives of Erich Maria Remarque and Paulette Goddard By Julie Gilbert Illustrated. 540 pages. Pantheon. $32.50.  In the history of mismatched lovers, Erich Maria Remarque and Paulette Goddard belong to a well-known subset that runs from Caesar and Cleopatra to Arthur Miller and Marilyn Monroe: the brainy man and the glamour girl. And as with many such pairs, Remarque, the author of "All Quiet on the Western Front," and Goddard, a now hazily remembered movie star of the 1930's and 40's, were fond of slipping outside those neat labels.
