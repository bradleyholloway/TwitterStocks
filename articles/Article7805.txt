 Reliance Electric Co. Is Weighing the Sale Of Toledo Scale Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1988  Reliance Electric Co. said it is considering selling its Toledo Scale Corp. subsidiary to raise cash for debt reduction.  Toledo Scale, a producer of weighing equipment ranging from store scales to industrial-plant weighing systems, accounted for about $250 million of Reliance's 1987 sales of $1.41 billion.
