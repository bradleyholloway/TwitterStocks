 Moynihan asserts reform of welfare shouldn't wait on health legislation   By Karr, Albert R, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 1994  Senate Finance Committee Chairman Daniel Moynihan, while saying that health care should be a high priority, took issue with President Clinton's assertion that there is a health-care crisis and said that welfare reform should not have to wait in line behind health-care legislation.
