 Share Prices Increase In Tokyo and London --- A Wall Street Journal News Roundup   The Wall Street Journal,  May 23, 1986  In Tokyo, the Nikkei stock average, which rose 118.16 points Wednesday, gained a further 109.19 points yesterday, to 15917.33. The stock exchange index rose 10.25 points, to 1253.27. Volume increased to about 500 million shares from 460 million Wednesday.
