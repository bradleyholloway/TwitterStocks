 Airbus Industrie Turns to the Market for Financing to Build New Jetliner   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 21, 1991  Airbus Industrie called on capital markets to help fund development of a new airliner, marking the first time the European consortium has tried to go outside of its four member governments for financing. Also, Airbus announced a $960 million contract with International Lease Finance Corp.
