 Walco National Corp. Agrees to Be Bought For $63.5 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1985  Holders of about 57% of the industrial equipment maker's 3.9 million shares outstanding have given Mr. Jacobs options to buy their shares. The company also has given Mr. Jacobs an option to buy an additional one million authorized but unissued shares.  Public shareholders and some current or former officers of Walco would receive $16.50 for each of their Walco shares. But Walco's principal holder, Frederick W. Richmond, who holds about 1.6 million, or 42%, of Walco's shares, has given Mr. Jacobs an option to buy those shares for $16 each.
