 Fannie Mae Earnings Moved Higher by 12% In the First Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 10, 1998  Fannie Mae, benefiting from improved credit costs and higher miscellaneous income, said its first quarter net income rose 12% to $824.2 million, or 77 cents a diluted share, from $734.1 million, or 67 cents a diluted share, in the year-earlier period.
