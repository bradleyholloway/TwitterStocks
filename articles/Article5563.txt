 A more assertive Russia awaits Clinton as political winds change   By Ignatius, Adi, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 12, 1994  When President Clinton arrives in Moscow on Jan 12, 1994, he will find himself in a Russia that has undergone profound political changes since he last met with President Boris Yeltsin. Political realities now demand that Yeltsin distance himself from the US, at least publicly, especially on foreign-policy questions.
