 Lawyers and clients: Companies, in trying to cut legal expenses, hire another lawyer   By Stevens, Amy, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1994  Well-known Los Angeles litigator Charles B. Rosenberg is one of a handful of entrepreneurs who in the past two years have begun selling their services as intermediaries between companies and outside law firms. Others include Philadelphia's Legal Triage Services Inc and the Devil's Advocate in Alexandria VA.
