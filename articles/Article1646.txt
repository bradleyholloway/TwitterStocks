 Business Brief -- JIB Group PLC: Company's Pretax Profit Fell 3.6% in the First Half   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 1992  JIB Group PLC, the London-listed insurance brokerage arm of Hong Kong's Jardine Matheson Holdings Ltd., said pretax profit slumped 3.6% in the first half and warned that full-year results will fall short of initial expectations.
