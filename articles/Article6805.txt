 American Express Co.: U.S. Judge Vacates Settlement Of Suits Over Conversion Fees   The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 2004  American Express Co. said a judge vacated a pending $66 million class-action settlement related to the fees the company charges for currency conversions. A judge in the U.S. District Court for the Southern District of Florida preliminarily approved the settlement in February.
