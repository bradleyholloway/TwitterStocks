 Japanese Company Acquires 49% Stake Of Los Angeles Club   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 1988  Marukin Shoji Co., a Japanese real estate and construction company, identified itself as the previously undisclosed buyer of 49% of the Riviera Country Club, one of Los Angeles's most famous golf clubs.  Laaco Ltd., the closely held owner of the Riviera, announced late Friday that it had signed a formal agreement with Marukin to sell 49% of the club for $52.9 million. Marukin has a one-year option to purchase the rest of the Riviera.
