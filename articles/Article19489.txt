 Deals & Deal Makers: Venture-Capital Investing Is Flat; Fourth Period Halts Year's Slide   By By Lisa Bransten, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 2002  The stabilizing trend also showed up in figures released this past week by rival research firm VentureOne, although that firm showed a continued modest decline in venture spending. According to San Francisco-based VentureOne, venture-capital investors put $6.31 billion into about 610 companies in the fourth quarter, a 4% drop from the $6.57 billion that venture investors put into 629 deals in the third quarter. VentureOne figures are generally slightly lower than the association's figures because of different methodologies.
