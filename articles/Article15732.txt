 Yahoo Chief Expects Firm Will Be Able To Stay Independent   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 1998  Yahoo! Inc. can remain independent instead of linking up with traditional media companies for "a long, long time," Chief Executive Officer Tim Koogle said.  Mr. Koogle, speaking Friday at a Forrester Research Inc. conference about "Making Internet Marketing Pay Off," said he doesn't believe traditional media companies will dominate the Internet.
