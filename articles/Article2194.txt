 Hunter Douglas NV: Net climbs 24%, sales get a boost from dollar gains   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 1996  Hunter Douglas NV, the Dutch window-coverings and architectural-products group, said its net income rose 24% in the first half to 62.9 million guilders ($37.9 million) from a year earlier, largely because of higher volumes and favorable exchange rates.
