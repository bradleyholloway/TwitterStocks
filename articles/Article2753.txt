 Nissan raises prices between 5% and 6% on most '96 models   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 1995  Nissan Motor Co announced prices for its 1996 vehicles, with most models rising between 4% and 6% from a year earlier. Bob Thomas, head of Nissan's US sales and marketing arm, said the price increases would probably be moderated, because the yen has been sinking against the dollar in recent weeks. The Nissan Altima will have a starting price of $16,054, including destination charges, up $865 from a year earlier.
