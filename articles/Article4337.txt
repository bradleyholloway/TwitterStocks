 Iran Rumor Helps Send Oil Up 2.4%   By Berthelsen, Christian, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2011  The rumor, which said the Iranian government closed the Strait of Hormuz, was picked up on financial blogs and some news websites and sent Nymex crude futures rocketing as much as 3.6% over Monday's settlement, to $101.25 a barrel.
