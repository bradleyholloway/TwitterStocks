 Joint Ventures in Russia Face Unexpected Roadblocks   By Carlson, Eugene, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1992  The difficulties encountered by Mats Engstrom and his California Sunshine Fine Foods Inc in starting up a joint venture enterprise exporting caviar from the Russian Far East to California in 1991 is described. A low-level bureaucrat at the custom's level refused the export, causing frustration, confusion and a bureaucratic nightmare.
