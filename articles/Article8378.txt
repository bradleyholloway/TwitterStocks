 Electronic gear sales rose in first quarter, but at a slower pace   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 1993  Sales of electronic products and services produced in the US expanded in the first quarter of 1993, but the rate of growth slowed, the American Electronics Association reported. Global sales in the first quarter increased 4.8% to $81.4 billion, up from $77.7 billion a year earlier.
