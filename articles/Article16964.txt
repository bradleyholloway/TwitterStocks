 Housing Push for Hispanics Spawns Wave of Foreclosures   By Schmidt, Susan; Tamman, Maurice, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 2009  It was fueled by a campaign by low-income housing groups, Hispanic lawmakers, a congressional Hispanic housing initiative, mortgage lenders and brokers, who all were pushing to increase homeownership among Latinos. Hispanic lawmakers and community groups have blamed subprime lenders, who specialize in making loans to customers with spotty credit histories.
