The marriage of Kathleen L. O'Brien, daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Thomas I. O'Brien of New Canaan, Conn., to Joseph F. McGoldrick Jr., son of Mr. and Mrs. McGoldrick of Garden City, L.I., took place in the Lady Chapel at St. Patrick's Cathedral yesterday. Msgr. John J. Barry performed the ceremony.  The bride, who is an assistant secretary with the fiduciary department at the Bankers Trust Company, was graduated from Skidmore College. Her father is chief patent counsel for the Union Carbide Corporation.  Mr. McGoldrick, an alumnus of the Chaminade High School in Mineola, L.I,, and Manhattan College, is a municipal bond broker with R.W. Ellwood & Company. His father is chairman of the John W. McGrath Corporation, a stevedoring concern.
