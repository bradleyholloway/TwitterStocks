 Corporations Rebuilt Use of Tax Loopholes Since Fix, Study Says   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 20, 2000  The study, conducted by the liberal-leaning Institute on Taxation and Economic Policy, underscores the need for another big tax-reform bill, the group said. It said lack of interest in comprehensive tax overhaul allowed lobbyists and lawyers to push small tax breaks, effectively rebuilding the maze of exclusions that forced the last round of loophole closures in 1986.
