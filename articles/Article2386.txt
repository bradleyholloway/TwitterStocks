 Republican Convention `92: Washington Wire: Market Jitters   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 1992  Supply-side economists are irritated over recent financial market reaction to suggestions that President Bush may unveil a tax cut proposal. Economist David Hale claims the tax-cut talk worsens the gap between short and long-term rates by fueling deficit concerns, but some analysts are worried that the financial markets will react unfavorably.
