 Grand Metropolitan PLC: Food, spirits concern plans to drop 400 Pet Inc. jobs   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1995  Grand Metropolitan PLC said it will probably eliminate about 400 Pet Inc staff positions through its acquisition of the St Louis-based food concern. The British food and spirits conglomerate said it will only move about 150 Pet jobs to the Minneapolis headquarters of its Pillsbury Co unit.
