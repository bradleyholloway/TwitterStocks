 The Afternoon Report: Wachovia Wacked Online edition  By Moroney, Robin, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 2008  Wachovia's loss and capital-raising efforts go some way to confirming fears that, even if credit markets recover, regional banks will have to deal with a new round of loan losses as the economic slowdown leaves many borrowers unable to keep up with not just mortgages, but other kinds of loans as well. It was the most dramatic fall in a broad, Asian-Pacific sell-off due to Friday's bad earnings news from General Electric, the prospect of interest rate increases from Beijing and a sober official assessment of Japan's economic prospects.
