 Small stock focus: Company sues broker, Forbes for share fall   By Power, William, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1995  Positive Response Television, an infomercial producer whose stock is down 55% in 1995, has filed suit in US District Court against PaineWebber, PaineWebber superbroker Stan Trilling and Forbes magazine. The suit charges that Trilling is the "ring leader" of short-sellers who have improperly sold small-company stocks prior to negative news stories that they helped to generate. On Dec 1, 1995, the Russell 1000 Index rose 1.16 to 309.74, and the Nasdaq Composite Index was down 3.89 at 1055.31.
