Mr. and Mrs. Paul A. Schosberg of Katonah, N.Y., have announced the engagement of their daughter, Jill E. Schosberg and Timothy J. Stoller, son of Mr. and Mrs. David Stoller of Red Hook, N.Y.  An August 1983 wedding is planned. Miss Schosberg was graduated in May from the Cornell University College of Agriculture and Life Sciences and will begin studies at the Mount Sinai School of Medicine next September.  Her father is president of the Savings Association League of New York State in Scarsdale, N.Y., and is former acting chairman and a member of the New York State Housing Finance Agency. Her mother, Jane M. Schosberg, is a breeder of thoroughbred race horses at Pine Lane Farm in Katonah.  Mr. Stoller, who is engaged in renalphysiology research at Cornell, received a B. S. degree there in 1978. His father, a horticulturist, is superintendent of grounds at Vassar College.
