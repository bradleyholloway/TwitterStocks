 IMF Mission Makes No Cash Promises During Visit to Moscow to Discuss Debt   By By Mark Whitehouse, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 25, 1998  An International Monetary Fund mission ended a one-week visit to Moscow yesterday with no promises of money as Russian officials started talks in London to reschedule the country's Soviet-era debts.  Amid the financial wrangling, thousands of Russians turned out in St. Petersburg's bitter cold for the funeral of Galina Starovoitova, a respected liberal politician who was gunned down in an apparent contract killing last Friday. Meanwhile, President Boris Yeltsin spent his second day in the hospital yesterday with what his doctors say is a case of pneumonia.
