 Agouron Pharmaceuticals shares soar after discovery linked to common cold   By Turner, Richard, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1994  Shares of Agouron Pharmaceuticals Inc rose sharply on its announcement that it has discovered the three-dimentional atomic structure of a key enzyme involved in causing the common cold. On Mar 11, 1994, Agouron shares rose 15%, or $2.25, to $14.75.
