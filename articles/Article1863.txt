 AMCA Announces Offering of Rights; Dilution Substantial   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 1988  AMCA International Ltd. announced a 193.1 million Canadian dollar ($154.6 million) rights offering that will result in massive dilution of the company's common stock.  The offering, announced here, will provide a badly needed infusion of new equity for the industrial products and construction concern that has posted losses for four of the past five years. The offering could also result in Canadian Pacific Ltd. boosting its current 50.6% stake in AMCA, which is based in Hanover, N.H.
