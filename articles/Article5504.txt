 OTC focus: Starbucks bid to issue convertible notes comes as stock may be nearing peak   By Opdyke, Jeff D, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 1993  Despite the fact its stock may be nearing its peak, Starbucks, the highflying Seattle specialty coffee retailer, has filed with the SEC to issue $70 million of subordinated convertible notes, due 2003. In Jul 9, 1993 trading, the Nasdaq Composite Index rose 3.10, or 0.44%, to 705.81.
