 Harder drive: Decade after failing, Japan firms try anew to sell PCs in U.S.   By Hamilton, David P, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 1996  A decade after Japanese companies tried, and largely failed, to break into the US market for PCs, another Japanese invasion is looming. A half-dozen Japanese corporations, including Sony Corp and Fujitsu Ltd, are targeting the US market in order to ensure their survival as consumer-electronic and computer companies.
