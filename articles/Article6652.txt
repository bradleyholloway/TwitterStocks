 Earnings Digest -- Consumer Products: Hasbro Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 2007  Toy maker Hasbro Inc. said fourth-quarter profit rose 15% amid strong sales in core brands such as Star Wars, Playskool and Littlest Pet Shop, a line of miniature plastic pets and accessories. Summing up 2006, the Pawtucket, R.I., company said its board-game business had a strong year as Monopoly Here and Now, the updated version of the classic board game, sold more than one million copies.
