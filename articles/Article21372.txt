 New Securities Issues [5]  The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 3, 1989  Coastal Corp. -- $200 million of 10.25% senior debentures due Oct. 15, 2004, priced at 99.846 to yield 10.27%. The issue was priced at 199 basis points above the Treasury's 10-year note. Rated Baa-3 by Moody's Investors Service Inc. and triple-B-minus by Standard & Poor's Corp., the issue will be sold through underwriters led by Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc.  Student Loan Marketing Association -- $100 million of 16% notes due Oct. 11, 1990. The notes will be offered at various prices above par to bring the yield down to market rates. Further details on the pricing weren't available. The noncallable issue will be sold through underwriters led by Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.
