 Richard L. Evans is an eternal presence on KMOX radio   By de Lisser, Eleena, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1997  Even though Richard L. Evans has been dead for nearly 26 years, his "Thought for the Day" can be heard weekday mornings at 6:55 am on KMOX radio in St Louis, the No 1-rated news-talk station in the city. His tape-recorded thoughts on a variety of everyday topics can be heard five days a week. To his loyal fans, he is the embodiment of wisdom, and many listeners do not even realize he is dead.
