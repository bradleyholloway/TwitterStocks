 Texas Industries puts 4th period net higher on strength at unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 1995  Robert D. Rogers, CEO of Texas Industries Inc, said he expects the company to report earnings "just north of" $1.50 a share for the fourth quarter ended May 31, 1995. A year earlier, the company earned $15.9 million, or $1.33 a share.
