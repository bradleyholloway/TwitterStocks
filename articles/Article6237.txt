 Technology Brief -- MEDSCAPE INC.: Planned Offering Postponed Due to `Market Conditions'   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1999  Medscape Inc, a provider of medical information over the Internet, postponed its initial public offering of stock because of "market conditions." The New York startup had been scheduled to offer 5.4 million shares at an expected range of $11 to $13 each.
