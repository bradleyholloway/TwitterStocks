 Wholesale prices declined 0.5% in September   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1994  Producer prices fell 0.5% in Sep 1994, the biggest decline in more than a year, though economists said the deceptive level reflected in part a decline in prices of energy and food, which often vary widely from month-to-month. Excluding those two groups, producer prices actually increased 0.1%.
