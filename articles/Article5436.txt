 India's Religious Turmoil Won't Stop Economic Reforms, Government Vows   By Brauchli, Marcus W; Dubey, Suman, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 11, 1992  Manmohan Singh, India's finance minister and the key architect of the country's 16-month-old economic reform plan, pledged that the recent wave of religious violence in the country would not halt the reform program.
