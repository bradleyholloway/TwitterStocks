 REVIEW --- Ideas Market -- Mind & Matter: Are We Holding a New Ice Age at Bay?   By Ridley, Matt, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 2012  Cyclical changes in the earth's orbit probably weakened sunlight in the northern hemisphere summer and thus caused this slow cooling. Since the northern hemisphere is mostly land, this change in the sun's strength meant gradually increased snow and ice cover, which in turn reflected light back into space.
