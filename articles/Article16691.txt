 Mittal Steel NV: Striking Kazakh Coal Miners Agree to Threefold Pay Raise   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 2006  Mittal Steel NV struck a more than threefold pay-raise deal with striking coal miners in Kazakhstan and separately offered a 20% pay raise to metal workers at its giant Temirtau mill, the company's chief executive officer in the Central Asian country said.
