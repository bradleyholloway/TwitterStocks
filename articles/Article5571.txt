 New Securities Issues   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 1998  Duane Reade Inc. -- $80 million of senior subordinated notes were priced via lead manager Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities, according to MCM CorporateWatch. Terms: maturity: Feb. 15, 2008; issue price: par; yield: 9.25%; spread: 360 basis points; call date: noncallable for five years, then at 104.625, 103.084, 101.542, 100; debt rating(s): B3 (Moody's Investors Service Inc.), single-B-minus (Standard & Poor's Ratings Group).  Federal Home Loan Bank System -- $86 million of bonds were priced via lead manager Vining-Sparks IBG LP, according to BondData-Corporate Service. Terms: maturity: Feb. 20, 2003; issue price: par; yield: 6.295%; call: noncallable for three months.  Albert Lea School District No. 241 (Minnesota) -- $35 million of general-obligation bonds apparently was won by a Piper Jaffray Inc. group with a bid setting the true-interest cost at 4.81%. The bonds were priced to yield from 3.8% in 2000 to 5% in 2018. The bonds weren't insured and were rated double-A-1 by Moody's. They weren't rated by S&P. The bonds are initially callable in 2006 at par.
