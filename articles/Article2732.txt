 House Republicans Disclose Medicare-Drug Benefits   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 2000  Mr. [Bill Thomas] countered that all seniors would receive some subsidies for prescription-drug coverage under the GOP plan. Poorer seniors would have most of their costs paid for, while higher-income elderly would get subsidies of 30% to 35%. The payments would be in the form of government subsidies to insurers offering prescription-drug policies.
