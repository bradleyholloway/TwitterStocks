 Some donors to Democratic fund-raiser were illegally reimbursed, Specter says   By Kuntz, Phil; Simpson, Glenn R, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 13, 1997  Sen Arlen Specter (R-PA) alleged that congressional investigators have obtained records showing that eight donors to John Huang's first big Democratic National Committee fund-raiser in 1996 were illegally reimbursed a total of $23,000 for their contributions.
