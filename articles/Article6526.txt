 Business Brief -- Motorola Inc.: Cut in Manufacturing Jobs Planned Around the World   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 11, 2002  Motorola Inc. said it will cut 2,000 manufacturing jobs as it consolidates production in fewer sites around the world to cut costs. The layoffs come from four of the mobile-phone and silicon-chip maker's operations: two in Sendai, Japan; one in Austin, Texas; and a previously announced layoff of 800 in Hong Kong.
