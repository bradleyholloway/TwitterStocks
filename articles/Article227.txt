Hillary E. Barnett, the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Stanley Barnett of North Miami Beach, Fla., was married Friday in New York to Victor Bitar, a son of Mr. and Mrs. Georges Bitar of Beirut, Lebanon. Justice Stanley Ostrau of New York State Supreme Court officiated at the St. Regis.  Mrs. Bitar, 30 years old, graduated from the Fashion Institute of Technology. She was until recently a buyer for Bergdorf Goodman in New York. Her father, who is retired, was an executive sales manager for the Equitable Bag Company, a shopping-bag manufacturer in New York.
