 Kmart Announces Murasky Resignation As Its Finance Chief   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 12, 1995  The nation's No. 2 discount retailer said the 50-year-old Mr. [Thomas F. Murasky] resigned "to pursue other career interests." He wasn't available for comment yesterday, but a spokeswoman said the parting was "amicable." Mr. Murasky, a 22-year Kmart employee, has been chief financial officer since 1986.
