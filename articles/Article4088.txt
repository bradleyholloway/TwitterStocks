 Tobacco Firms' Liability In Asbestos Suits Is Sought   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 1985  GAF Corp., a Wayne, N.J., maker of chemicals and building materials, filed about 170 cross-complaints against tobacco companies, including American Tobacco Co., American Brands Inc., Philip Morris Inc., R.J. Reynolds Tobacco Co., Brown & Williamson Tobacco Co. and Liggett Group Inc. Also named are retailers, advertising agencies and tobacco company employees who weren't named.
