 SEC charges firms reaped $50 million in equipment-leasing pyramid scheme   By Lohse, Deborah, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 10, 1997  The SEC accused First Interregional Advisors and First Interregional Equity Corp, two Millburn NJ firms, with reaping $50 million from a fraudulent pyramid scheme of selling equipment-leasing assignments. The complaint was filed on Mar 6, 1997.
