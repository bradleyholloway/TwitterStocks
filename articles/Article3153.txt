 Letters to the Editor: The Greatest Madness?   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 13, 2003  Teaching chastity while handing out condoms is a bit like telling a child, "Don't cross the freeway but if you do wear this orange vest." Given the severity of the problems that abstinence education is attempting to address, public officials can hardly be blamed for rejecting the diluting effect of such a mixed message, or for treating the social crisis as well as the medical one. Granted, they are fighting an uphill battle against a popular culture fed by the likes of entertainers whose idea of "giving back" is hosting an AIDS fund-raiser on days they are not promoting a promiscuous lifestyle on-screen or off.
