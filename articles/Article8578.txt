 Chiron Corp.: Company to buy Iolab lines from J&J for $95 million   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 7, 1995  Chiron Corp, a health-product concern based in Emeryville CA, has agreed to acquire the ophthalmic surgical-product business of Johnson & Johnson's Iolab division for $95 million. Chiron said it plans to take an unspecified charge to earnings in the second quarter related to the merger.
