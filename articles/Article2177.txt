 Micron, Hurt by Fall In Chip Prices, Posts 73% Earnings Drop   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 1996  BOISE, Idaho -- Micron Technology Inc., hurt by plummeting memory-chip prices, reported that fiscal third-quarter net income slid 73%.  As a result, the chip maker said capital spending will be curtailed and output will remain flat this quarter.
