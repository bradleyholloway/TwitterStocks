 Technology Brief -- Solectron Corp.: More Layoffs, Closures Follow Posting of a Narrow Loss   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 2003  Solectron Corp. plans 12,000 additional layoffs and a new wave of factory closures after posting a slightly narrower fiscal second-quarter net loss on lower costs. The Milpitas, Calif., outsourced-electronics manufacturer also forecast a third-quarter loss.
