 Reinvent: Be More Productive   By Levit, Alexandra, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 2009  Kevin Purdy, an editor at Lifehacker.com, a productivity blog, says in 2010 he sees more individuals taking advantage of new Google products such as Google Voice, which transcribes text messages and voicemail directly to your email inbox, and Google Wave, a tool that facilitates virtual teamwork with a chat-like device that allows for video, instant messaging and document sharing.
