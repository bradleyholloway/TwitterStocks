 Tracking travel: One-way car rentals turn costly again   By Dahl, Jonathan, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 1993  Drop-off charges for rental cars have begun to resurface. In 1992, several big car-rental companies announced one-way rates for one-way travelers that were higher than the standard fees paid by those renters returning the cars to the spot where they picked them up, but the prices were cheaper than paying a drop-off fee.
