 The accused: How Richard Jewell and his lawyers seek revenge on the media   By Stevens, Amy, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 1997  The story of how Richard Jewell, the Atlanta security guard, managed to make the transition from well-known suspect in the 1996 Olympics bombing to a highly compensated victim is related. Jewell has teamed up with an old acquaintance, attorney G. Watson Bryant Jr, and the two are mounting a sustained assault on some of the US's largest media companies. They have already won a cash settlement from NBC to avert a possible defamation lawsuit.
