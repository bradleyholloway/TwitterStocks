 Judge upholds right of U.S. government to manage forests   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1995  Judge David W. Hagen of federal district court in Reno NV has upheld the government's ownership and management rights for national forests and other public land in Nevada, dealing a blow to the so-called county supremacy movement. The judge rejected claims in a suit brought by rancher Cliff Gardner
