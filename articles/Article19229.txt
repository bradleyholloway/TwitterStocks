 Recent Changes in Stock Listings   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 3, 1999  Among recent stock-listing changes, Tadeo Holdings Inc, trading on the Nasdaq SmallCap Market, is changing its name to TekInsight.Com Inc with the new symbol TEKS. Aspect Telecommunications Corp, trading on the Nasdaq Stock Market, is changing its name to Aspect Communications Corp under the same symbol ASPT.
