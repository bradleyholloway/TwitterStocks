 Leisure: Carnival Sets Plan For German Brand   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 2003  Carnival plans to transfer the 1,590-passenger cruise ship A'Rosa Blu to its Aida brand next summer. The Miami company agreed to sell three 200-passenger A'Rosa riverboats and the A'Rosa name to Arkona AG of Germany later this year. The price equals the net book value of the assets at Nov. 30, plus 800,000 euros ($877,000).
