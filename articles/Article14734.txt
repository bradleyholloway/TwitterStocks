 Top Louisville, Ky., Station Leaves CBS, Joins ABC Network   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 1990  WHAS, the No. 1 TV station in Louisville KY, terminated its contract with CBS Inc's network and signed on instead with rival ABC. ABC, in turn, is dropping its longtime affiliate, WLKY, a unit of St Louis-based Pulitzer Publishing Co's broadcasting subsidiary.
