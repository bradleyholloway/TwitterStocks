 Pimco Favors Bonds Of Germany to U.S. --- Gross Prefers Strong Fiscal Positions   By Zeng, Min; Natarjan, Prabha, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 2010  Investors should favor government bonds in countries with strong fiscal positions, such as Germany, this year, and prepare for a tougher year in the U.S. and the U.K. in particular, where interest rates will rise as governments and central banks start to withdraw their huge stimulus efforts, according to Bill Gross, co-chief investment officer at bond fund giant Pimco.
