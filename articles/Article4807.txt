 Allied-Signal to Cut Dividend and Staff, Take $880 Million Charge, Post Losses   By Naj, Amal Kumar, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 1991  Lawrence A. Bossidy, the CEO for Allied-Signal Corp, said the company plans to cut its dividend payable Dec 10, 1991 to 25 cents a share from 45 cents, trim 14% of its salaried workers, take an $880 million charge against third-quarter earnings and restructure broadly.
