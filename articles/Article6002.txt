 Business Brief -- Hartmarx Corp.: Talks End With Investors That Wanted to Buy Firm   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 2001  Hartmarx Corp., Chicago, said it ended talks with an investor group that wants to buy the men's suits maker for $135 million in stock, contending the group has made "misleading statements" about its financing and ability to muster funds for a takeover.
