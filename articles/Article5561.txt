 Business Brief -- NATIONAL DISCOUNT BROKERS GROUP INC.: 054 Net Income Plunges 37%; Increased Costs Are Cited   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 9, 1998  National Discount Brokers Group Inc said fiscal second-quarter net income fell 37% to $2.1 million, or 16 cents a share, from $3.3 million, or 25 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue rose 4.6% to $43 million.
