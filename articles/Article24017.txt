 The Days and Nights of Manny   By Gay, Jason, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 2011  A chess board surfaces and Pacquiao rises from his massage to play before a rapt audience of a dozen, packed tightly around a dining room table. Pacquiao has earned tens of millions of dollars boxing and is known to enjoy some toys -- there's now a Ferrari in the basement garage, and also a beautiful home in nearby Hancock Park -- but when he's training for a fight, he prefers to live here, with his friends, in a narrow two-floor apartment where nearly everyone but the host sleeps stacked in bunks, like a submarine crew.
