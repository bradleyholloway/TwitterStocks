 The Informed Patient: Behind One Hospital's Fight Against Deadly Infection   By Landro, Laura, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 2012  Recent studies have shown that some of the most common surfaces, from sink faucets to TV remote controls, can harbor the most deadly bacteria. Besides increased diligence, the Hunterdon team is mobilizing new technologies, including bathing patient rooms with germ-killing ultraviolet light. The hospital's successes led the Association for Professionals in Infection Control and Epidemiology, an industry group, to ask the Hunterdon team to help launch "Clean Spaces, Healthy Patients," a national program that includes Web-based seminars aimed at infection-control officials and cleaning staff.
