 Argentine 'Miracle': Talk of Buenos Aires Is That Latest Revival in Economy Is for Real   By Kamm, Thomas, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 11, 1992  The Argentine government announced that it is committed to a free market and to a fixed exchange rate for the peso against the dollar. The fiscal policies of Argentine President Carlos Menem and his economy minister, Domingo Cavallo, are being labelled the "Argentine Miracle." The economic conditions in Argentina and fiscal efforts underway are discussed.
