 Eyes on the Price: Is the CPI Accurate? Ask the Federal Sleuths Who Get the Numbers --- They Have to Extract Data From Surly Clerks, Study Costs of Dissimilar Goods --- At Stake: Billions in Benefits   By By Christina Duff, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1997  Mrs. Bloom wants to know the exact prices of some hospital services. "Nothing's changed," the woman says. "Well, do you have the ledger?" Mrs. Bloom asks. "We haven't changed any prices," the woman insists. Mrs. Bloom's fast talk finally pries the woman from behind her desk, and she gets the numbers. It turns out that a semiprivate surgery recovery room now costs $753.80 a day -- or four cents less than a month ago.
