 Clear Channel's Loss In Quarter Widened As Costs Escalated   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 2001  Clear Channel said the results reflect a 6% increase in radio revenue and a 10% rise in billboard sales on a pro forma basis. Earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization grew 94%, to $633.7 million. However, depreciation expenses more than doubled and interest expenses nearly tripled from the year before.
