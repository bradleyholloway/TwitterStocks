 No. 2 Search Firm Heidrick Considers Initial Public Offer   By By Joann S. Lublin, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 10, 1997  Heidrick & Struggles Inc., the nation's second-largest executive-search firm, is considering going public -- a move that could enrich well-known CEO-hunter Gerard Roche, its chairman and biggest shareholder.  The firm's executive committee, meeting last month in Switzerland, gave the go-ahead for top officials to explore an initial public offering of the privately held Chicago-based concern. A final decision is expected by March.
