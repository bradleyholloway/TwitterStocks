 Business Brief -- Ford Motor Co.: Offer for Ordinary Shares Of Jaguar Also Is $13.39   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1989  Ford Motor Co., Dearborn, Mich., said that its offer to acquire Jaguar PLC will include an offer to purchase all Jaguar ordinary shares held by U.S. residents for the $13.39 a share it is offering to pay for all Jaguar American depositary shares.
