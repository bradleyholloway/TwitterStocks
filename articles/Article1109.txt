 Calgene Inc. Cancels Its Proposal to Acquire Some Melridge Assets   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1988  DAVIS, Calif. -- Calgene Inc., citing "contingencies which could not be resolved," said it was dropping its $3.8 million proposal to buy certain assets of Melridge Inc., the nation's largest grower of lilies and daffodils.
