 Black Paper's Editor, Detained by Pretoria, Admitted to Hospital   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 1988  JOHANNESBURG, South Africa (AP) -- Zwelakhe Sisulu, the detained editor of the New Nation newspaper, has been hospitalized and is being treated for depression, his attorney said.  Mr. Sisulu, 37 years old, was taken Friday from Diepkloof Prison in the black township of Soweto and admitted to Johannesburg Hospital, said his attorney, Priscilla Jana.  Mr. Sisulu has been detained without charge since December 1986, and courts have rejected requests that the government charge or release him.
