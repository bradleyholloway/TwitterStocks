 Business Brief -- Promus Cos.: Harrah's Unit to Develop Indian Community's Casino   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1993  Promus Cos. of Memphis, Tenn., said its Harrah's casino division signed an agreement to develop and manage a $24.7 million casino for the Ak-Chin Indian Community about 25 miles south of Phoenix, Ariz.
