 Marketing gurus say: In India, think cheap, lose the cold cereal   By Jordan, Miriam, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 11, 1996  K. M. S. Ahluwalia, founder of ORG-MARG, India's largest market-research firm, is in the business of solving the marketing riddles that confound Western multinational companies in India. His firm has served as a guide to multinationals like Avon Products Inc, Coca-Cola Co, Gillette Co and Unilever PLC. Some of the marketing problems unique to India are examined.
