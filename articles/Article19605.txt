 Cadence Design Pares Its Estimate For Fourth Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 3, 2003  The maker of semiconductor-design software said it expects to report fourth-quarter pro forma earnings of two cents to four cents a share, well below the 14 cents to 16 cents a share it forecast in October. The company's pro forma estimate excludes a $70 million restructuring charge from severance pay, facility closures and asset write-offs. The estimate also excludes a $265 million gain from the settlement of litigation and $30 million in amortization of intangible assets.
