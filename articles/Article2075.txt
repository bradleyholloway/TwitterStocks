 Philip Morris and RJR likely to post sharp jump in 3rd-quarter earnings   By Somasundaram, Meera, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 13, 1995  The nation's two largest cigarette companies are likely to report a significant jump in third-quarter earnings. Philip Morris Cos is expected to report an 18% jump in earnings to $1.68 a share from $1.42 in the year earlier period, while RJR Nabisco Holdings Corp is expected to post earnings of $0.63 a share, a 15% jump from earnings of $0.55 a year earlier.
