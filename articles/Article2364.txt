 Two Men Indicted On Charges Relating To Insider Trading   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1997  The indictment charges Robert W. Hunter, a former director of Independence, with eight counts of insider trading, one count of mail fraud and one count of obstructing proceedings before the Securities and Exchange Commission. It charges Thomas Pileggi with three counts of insider trading and two counts of wire fraud.
