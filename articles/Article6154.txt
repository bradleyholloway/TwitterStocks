 Business Brief -- JACOBS ENGINEERING GROUP INC.: Pact Is Reached in Principle To Acquire Sverdrup Corp.   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 11, 1998  Jacobs Engineering Group Inc of Pasadena CA said it agreed in principle to acquire Sverdrup Corp, a closely held St Louis engineering and design concern, for about $200 million in cash. Terms of the transaction, expected to close in Jan 1999, also call for Jacobs to pay Sverdrup shareholders as much as $30 million more over the following three years, contingent on Jacobs Engineering's stock achieving certain price targets.
