 Bill Gates envisions computing's future at big trade show   By Clark, Don, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1994  Microsoft Corp Chairman William Gates has proposed a hypothetical device known as a Wallet PC that will store up digital money that can be zapped into next-generation cash registers, verify consumers' identity and even give directions to the store. Gates spoke at the Comdex trade show in Las Vegas.
