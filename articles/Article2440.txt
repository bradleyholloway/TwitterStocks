 InterVest Hopes to Create An Exchange for Bonds   The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 1998  VALLEY FORGE, Pa. -- An upstart bond firm, InterVest Financial Services, is in talks with the Securities and Exchange Commission to become the first trading exchange for all kinds of bonds. While the New York Stock Exchange trades some corporate bonds, InterVest would be the first to trade all types of securities, including corporate, municipal, government and other bonds.
