 New Securities Issues [7]  The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 13, 1995  Columbia/HCA Healthcare Corp. -- $150 million of bonds due Dec. 15, 2014, priced at par to yield 9%, according to MCM CorporateWatch. The noncallable issue was priced at a spread of 109 basis points above 6.25% coupon Treasurys maturing in August 2023. Rated single-A-3 by Moody's Investors Service Inc. and triple-B-plus by Standard & Poor's Ratings Group, the issue will be sold through underwriters led by Lehman Brothers Inc.  International Lease Finance Corp. -- $150 million of notes due Jan. 15, 1998, priced as 8.125s at 99.553 to yield 8.296%, according to MCM CorporateWatch. The noncallable issue was priced at a spread of 56 basis points above Treasurys. Rated single-A-2 by Moody's and single-A-plus by S&P, the issue will be sold through underwriters led by Morgan Stanley & Co. International Lease Finance is a unit of American International Group Inc.  WMX Technologies Inc. -- $200 million of senior notes due Feb. 1, 1998, priced as 8.125s at 99.671 to yield 8.248%, according to MCM CorporateWatch. The noncallable issue was priced at a spread of 44 basis points above Treasurys. Rated single-A-1 by Moody's and double-A-minus by S&P, the issue will be sold through underwriters led by Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp.
