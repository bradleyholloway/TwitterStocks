 Business Brief -- VORNADO REALTY TRUST: Funds From Operations Fall; Compensation Package Cited   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1997  REIT Vornado Realty Trust said its 1997 third-quarter funds from operations fell 2.8% from the previous year, due in part to the amortization of $6.8 million of the compensation package offered late in 1996 to lure Michael Fascitelli from his Goldman, Sachs & Co partnership to become president.
