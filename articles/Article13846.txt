 My Son's Favorite Sport is Soccer. Isn't It?   By Opdyke, Jeff D, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 2011  [...] I've talked about the sacrifices the family was making so he could pursue his soccer-loving dreams: the endless travel, the out-of-state soccer camps, the time we all spent away from home to attend his practices (two nights a week) and games (as many as four in a weekend). Looking back, I now recognize that my son never really expressed much emotion about soccer, other than frustration, which I wrote off as teenage moodiness because I didn't want to believe it was the sport.
