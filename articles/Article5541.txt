 Seeking control: Battle for properties keeps Nicaraguans on verge of anarchy   By de Cordoba, Jose, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 1993  The murder of Arges Sequeira, the president of an organization seeking the return of lands confiscated by the Sandinistas, by a former Sandinista army officer has become a symbol of the anguished politics of Nicaragua. Three years after the Sandinistas were defeated at the ballot box, the country remains on the verge of political anarchy and economic ruin because of the stalemate of the return of land.
