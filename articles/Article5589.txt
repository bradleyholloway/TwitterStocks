 Abu Dhabi to pay $1.8 billion to deal with BCCI claims   By Truell, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 11, 1994  Abu Dhabi's government agreed to pay $1.8 billion to the liquidators of BCCI Holdings (Luxembourg) SA as part of a revised accord to try to settle billions of dollars of creditor claims against the defunct bank. The Abu Dhabi government owned more than 70% of Bank of Credit & Commerce International.
