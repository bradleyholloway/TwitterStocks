 S&L Cleanup Unit Raises Cap on Financing Assets   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 10, 1990  The S&L cleanup agency, RTC, will provide more government loans for buyers of its properties. The RTC's Oversight Board gave final approval to a plan that would make more than $7 billion in hard-to-sell assets, such as underdeveloped land, eligible for government financing.
