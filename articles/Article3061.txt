 Siemens Predicts More Gloom   By By Matthew Karnitschnig, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 2002  In the fiscal fourth quarter ended Sept. 30, Siemens's net profit totaled 53 million euros ($53.4 million), or six European cents a share, as the company benefited from asset sales. In the year-earlier period, Siemens had a loss of 1.1 billion euros as a result of restructuring charges. Earnings before interest and taxes totaled 176 million euros after a 130 million euro loss a year earlier. Analysts forecast Ebit of 476 million euros. Fourth-quarter sales fell 13.2% to 21.3 billion euros.
