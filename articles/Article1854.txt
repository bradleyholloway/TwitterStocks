 American is set to lead British Aerospace PLC   By Coleman, Brian, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1994  Following rumors of a boardroom fight and criticism from some politicians, John Cahill will leave his post as chairman of British Aerospace PLC only two years into his five-year contract. Bob Bauman, the CEO of SmithKline Beecham PLC and an American, has been named as Cahill's replacement.
