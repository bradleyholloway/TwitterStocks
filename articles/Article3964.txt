 Corporate News: European Car Sales Fell 18% in February   By Rauwald, Christoph, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 2009  German premium car makers BMW AG and Daimler AG saw registrations fall 29% and 30% to 40,928 and 42,632 cars, respectively, as the scrapping plan in their domestic market mainly sparked demand for smaller cars rather than luxury vehicles. ---
