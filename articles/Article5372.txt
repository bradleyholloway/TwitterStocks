 White House calls FBI file searches a mistake, denies any ulterior motive   By Simpson, Glenn R, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1996  The White House committed an "inexcusable" blunder by obtaining FBI files on 341 people, Chief of Staff Leon Panetta said, and those people deserve an apology. But he insisted there was no sinister intent behind the information-gathering, as suggested by GOP presidential candidate Bob Dole.
