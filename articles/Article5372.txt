 Bank of Tokyo Nominates Gyohten As Next Chairman   By Chipello, Christopher J, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 1992  Bank of Tokyo nominated Toyoo Gyohten, a former vice minister of finance for international affairs, as its next chairman. Gyohten is to be officially installed in the position Jun 26, 1992. He succeeds Yusuke Kashiwagi.
