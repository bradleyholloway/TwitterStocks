 Canada's Bombardier, Family Agree to Issue 7.5 Million Shares   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 20, 1990  Bombardier Inc. said it and members of the Bombardier family agreed to issue 7.5 million common shares to the public for a total of about 146.3 million Canadian dollars (US$126.3 million).  The company said it will offer five million Class B subordinated voting shares, and the Bombardier family will sell 2.5 million Class B shares, all at C$19.50 a share.
