 WEEKEND INVESTOR --- The New Basics: Trading Down: Can It Still Bankroll Your Retirement?   By McQueen, M P, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 2010  According to an April study by the Society of Actuaries, 20% of not-yet retirees say they plan to downsize after the last child leaves the nest. According to his calculator, which is available at, a couple with a combined preretirement income of about $200,000 and living in a $900,000 paid-off home could boost their annual retirement spending from $40,314 to $47,289 just by trading down to a $450,000 home.
