 Microsoft Reassesses Tactics   By By Rebecca Buckman, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 2002  It appears that Microsoft is conducting the same type of in-depth research about Linux that it long has lavished on other competitors. The recent Linux memo, which was posted this week on the Web site of the Open Source Initiative advocacy group, summarized the results of a global telephone survey about Linux that Microsoft conducted among information-technology professionals last year, according to the document. A person familiar with the matter confirmed the memo was authentic. A company spokesman said Microsoft "is not going to comment on leaked or speculated information."
