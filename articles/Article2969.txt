 Business Brief -- Sprint Corp.: Earnings Target Reaffirmed, Revenue Estimate Lowered   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 2002  Sprint Corp. reaffirmed its fourth-quarter earnings estimate for its core FON Group, but slightly trimmed its revenue projection for the period. The Westwood, Kan., telecommunications company said it still expects fourth-quarter earnings of 31 cents to 33 cents a share for its FON Group phone unit.
