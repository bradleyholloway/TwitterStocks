 EBay Is in Discussions With a Korean Web Firm   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 2000  As of 4 p.m. in Nasdaq Stock Market trading, eBay was down $2.44, or 7%, to $32.31 after news reports that it is in talks to acquire uBid, an Internet-auction site owned by Andover, Mass., investment firm CMGI Inc.
