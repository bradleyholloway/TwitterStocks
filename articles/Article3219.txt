 Letters to the Editor: Latest Moves Doom French Oil Industry   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1999  The French government's choice (article, July 7) to exercise its "golden share" to block takeovers of French oil firms by "Anglo-Saxon or American" companies effectively initiates the demise of French oil firms' economic significance in the future of oil. TotalFina SA's hostile bid for Elf Acquitaine may be the only chance for a French oil industry survival in the first half of the 21st century, but the deal as structured -- or defended against -- is the wrong tool aimed at the wrong target.
