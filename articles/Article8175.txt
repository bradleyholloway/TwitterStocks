 Advertising: Chiat Expected to Get Account for Amex Cards   By Bird, Laura, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1991  American Express Co is expected to announce that it is pulling its $60 million charge-card advertising account from Ogilvy & Mather and placing it with Chiat/Day/Mojo. The action will be a major blow to Ogilvy, a WPP Group unit, which has handled the account for nearly 30 years.
