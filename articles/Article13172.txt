 Citigroup Hires 5 From ABN Amro   By Carrick Mollenkamp, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 6, 2005  Citigroup said Nigel Mills, 50 years old, chief executive of ABN- owned Hoare Govett, will become chairman of its U.K. corporate-broking business. Other Hoare Govett brokers jumping to Citigroup include Andrew Chapman, Tom Reid, Andrew Thompson and Chris Zeal. The brokers will join Citigroup in September, Citigroup said.
