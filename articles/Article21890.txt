 Motorola squares off against Japan in new fight for 'comparable' access   By Hamilton, David P, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 1994  Motorola Inc, which despite having trouble gaining access to the Japanese market has become one of the most prominent US success stories in Japan, is once again in a battle to gain "comparable" access where it feels it has been denied. Motorola's experience is said to demonstrate again the fragility of US-Japan trade pacts.
