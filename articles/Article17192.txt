 MarketWatch: Car Maintenance You Don't Need   By Waters, Jennifer, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 2010  The whole idea is to get you to also buy an air filter, rotate your tires or buy something else while you're there. Because car manufacturing has become so sophisticated and less reliant on human intervention -- more computers and technology are producing and installing parts, for example -- the car maintenance and repair business isn't as robust as it was 10 or even five years ago.
