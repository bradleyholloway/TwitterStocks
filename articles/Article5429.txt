 Clinton Strongly Links Normalization of Vietnam Ties to Data on U.S. Missing   By Seib, Gerald F; Birnbaum, Jeffrey H, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 1992  President-elect Clinton said he won't normalize ties with any nation "at all suspected" of withholding information on missing Americans. Clinton said he plans a vigorous effort to resolve the question of US soldiers missing since the Vietnam War.
