 Business Brief -- Cray Research Inc.: Stock Plummets on Forecast Of Pressure on '92 Margins   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 13, 1991  Cray Research Inc. told analysts that it expects to report an 8% to 10% increase in revenue for 1991, but said it expects pressure on profit margins to continue in 1992.  The Eagan, Minn., maker of supercomputers had revenue of $804.4 million and net income of $113 million, or $4.02 a share, in 1990. It wouldn't elaborate on its earnings targets for this year.
