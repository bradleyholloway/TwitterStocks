 Poor U.S. Whites Are Focus of Study by Advocacy Group   By Harper, Lucinda, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 9, 1992  A study called "White Poverty in America," prepared by the Center on Budget and Policy Priorities, found that even though a greater proportion of blacks are poorer than whites, whites fell into poverty at a faster pace than blacks between 1989 and 1991. Other findings in the report are discussed.
