 Kuwait to Seek $5 Billion Loan from Banks   By Forman, Craig, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 11, 1991  Kuwait plans to raise $5 billion in an unsecured syndicated loan in an arrangement led by J. P. Morgan & Co, along with National Bank of Kuwait and other international commercial banks. Proceeds from this and other fund raising efforts will be used to develop the nation's oil sector and infrastructure.
