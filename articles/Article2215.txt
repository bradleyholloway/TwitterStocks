 Bond firms and government mingle in Florida   By Gasparino, Charles, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1996  The FBI and the SEC are both undertaking broad inquiries into government and financial corruption in South Florida. So far, the investigations have embroiled county commissioners as well as prominent investment bankers. On Oct 11, 1996, the 30-year Treasury bond ended 1/2 point higher at 98 24/32, pushing the yield to 6.84% from 6.88% on Oct 10.
