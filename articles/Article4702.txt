 American Express Unit Plans $33.8 Million Bid For Systems Associates   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 1986  First Data Resources, a unit of American Express Co., agreed to buy Systems Associates Inc., Charlotte, N.C., in a tender offer totaling $33.8 million, the companies said.  First Data agreed to pay $13.25 for each of the 2,551,966 shares of Systems Associates, which designs, assembles and markets computer systems for hospitals.
