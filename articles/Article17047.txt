 Investing in Funds: A Quarterly Analysis --- Managing Your Cash: Looking Beyond Money Funds --- With yields on these portfolios barely above 0%, savers can consider bank accounts and short-term bond funds to earn a little more on their reserves   By Damato, Karen, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 5, 2009  With yields on money-market mutual funds at record lows, many individuals have been pulling cash out of these low-risk parking places and stashing it elsewhere. While the yields on money funds are a product of market conditions and the funds' expense levels, "yields on bank deposits are dictated by what the bank is willing to pay," says Greg McBride, senior financial analyst at Bankrate.com.
