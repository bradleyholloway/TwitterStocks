 Business Brief -- Grow Group Inc.: Grow Group Inc. Dissidents to Seek Election Of 3 Directors at Meeting   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1989  A group led by Edward S. Buchanan said it will seek to elect three directors at Grow Group Inc.'s annual meeting Oct. 25.  Grow Group, a New York maker of coatings, paint and household products, said it will "vigorously oppose" any such attempt by the group, which holds 9.4%.
