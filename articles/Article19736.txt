 Footstar Inc. Files for Bankruptcy Protection   By Kang, Stephanie, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 4, 2004  Footstar, West Nyack, N.Y., said it will receive $300 million in debtor-in-possession financing from lenders led by FleetBoston Financial Corp.'s Fleet National Bank and General Electric Co.'s GECC Capital Markets Group Inc. Judge Adlai S. Hardin of the U.S. Bankruptcy Court for the Southern District of New York granted interim approval yesterday afternoon, and the company said the funds would go toward paying footwear manufacturers for future products and covering Footstar's payroll.
