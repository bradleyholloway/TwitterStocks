THE bottles of Listerine and Pepto-Bismol are half empty, cotton balls spill out of a plastic bag and clothes are strewn over the bed. The stereo is splattered with paint and a half-eaten piece of toast sits on the kitchen counter.  ''Just what we had in mind,'' said Nina Ramsey, 30 years old, who with Kristi Zea, 40, created the sets for Martin Scorsese's segment of the movie ''Stories of New York.'' Ms. Zea, a film production designer, and Ms. Ramsey, a set decorator, recently completed a 10,000-square-foot loft near Astor Place for the film, which is to be released in February.
