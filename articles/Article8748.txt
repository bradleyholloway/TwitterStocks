 Zeneca PLC   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 1996  Zeneca PLC said pretax profit jumped 21% during the first half of 1996 and predicted that a bulging pipeline of new drugs would fuel 15% average annual earnings growth for the rest of the decade. During the first six months of the year, Zeneca's pretax profit climbed to BP610 million ($941.9 million) from BP506 million a year earlier.
