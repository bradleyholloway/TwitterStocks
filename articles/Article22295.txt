 Liberals head for a victory in Canada   By Urquhart, John, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1997  Canadian voters appeared on Jun 2, 1997 to have given a new mandate to their centrist Liberal Party government, headed by Prime Minister Jean Chretien. Preliminary indications were that the Liberals would win the most seats in the 301-set House of Commons, although they suffered heavy losses in eastern Canada.
