 Some Japanese Enjoy Latest Fringe Benefit: Bullet Train Commute   By Chipello, Christopher J, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1991  At least 50 blue-chip companies in Tokyo are offering the benefit to their employees of allowing them to live outside Tokyo and commute in on the Shinkansen bullet train. By 1990, an estimated 6,000 to 7,000 people were riding to work every day on the trains.
