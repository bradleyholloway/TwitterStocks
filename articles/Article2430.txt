 Defense Companies Prepare to Battle Military Services for Government Work   By Cole, Jeff, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 21, 1992  The defense industry has expressed concern about initiatives underway within the military that it sees as an attempt to "nationalize" the defense industry. Defense companies say new government proposals will take work away from the private sector. Central to the issue are USAF policy changes that make defense companies compete with military maintenance depots for much of $3.6 billion in annual contracts and a proposal to assemble an advanced F-22 fighter that contractors are developing.
