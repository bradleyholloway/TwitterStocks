Brenna Susan Freedman, daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Myron Freedman of Hollywood, Calif., was married at the Harmonie Club in New York yesterday to Julian Allan Stein, son of Mr. and Mrs. Ralph Stein of Queens Village. Rabbi Jerome Davidson performed the ceremony.  The bride, a certified registered nurse anesthetist at the Hospital for Special Surgery, graduated from the Cornell University-New York Hospital School of Nursing and received an M.A. in nursing administration from Teachers College of Columbia University. She is a former director of the School of Nurse Anesthesia at New York Medical College-Metropolitan Hospital Center, where she received her anesthesia training. Her father is manager of the rejuvenation division of the Hollywood Film Company.  Mr. Stein, who graduated from Queens College, is an analyst with the Department of Ports and Terminals. He was executive assistant to the Commissioner of Air Resources under Mayors John V. Lindsay and Abraham D. Beame. His father is a commercial artist.
