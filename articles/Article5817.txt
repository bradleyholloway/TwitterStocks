 Rogers Communications Inc.: Net loss of $48.9 million posted for fourth period   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1996  Rogers Communications Inc reported a fourth-quarter loss of $67.1 million Canadian dollars ($48.9 million), narrowed from C$73.9 million a year earlier. The latest loss included a C$34 million charge related to a write-off of an investment.
