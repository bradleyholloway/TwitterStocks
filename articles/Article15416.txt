 De Beers pact lets Russia sell more diamonds on side   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 5, 1996  The De Beers diamond cartel signed an agreement in Feb 1996 that allows Russia to sell three times as many diamonds to independent dealers. In return, De Beers received assurances that Russia will not continue violating the previous De Beers pact by leaking more than $1 billion a year in uncut gems onto the world market.
