 Tip of the Week: Comb Over Your 401(k)   By Chu, Kathy, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2003  This year it's especially important to review your 401(k) plan, because changes in the account's lineup of funds may have thrown your investment mix off balance.  You might also need to get back into stocks. Companies such as drug maker Merck are transferring participants' balances into money-market accounts when they eliminate a stock fund. If you don't put this money back into stocks, you may unwittingly be damping the returns of your nest egg.
