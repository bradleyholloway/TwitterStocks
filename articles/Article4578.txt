 GM Plans to Modify An Engine Facility; Auto Output to Fall   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 11, 1985  Separately, Ford Motor Co. will begin volume production of its Taurus and Sable midsize cars next Monday. Output for sale has been delayed for about two months mainly because of problems with the Atlanta plant's high-technology equipment. The cars go on sale Dec. 26. The original dealer introduction date was Nov. 14.
