 Japan's Economy Is Seen Growing Despite Slowdown ---- Special to The Wall Street Journal   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 1990  Japan's economy will enter into a period of gradual slowdown, but it will still expand at a respectable 4.1% pace in fiscal 1990 after inflation is taken into account, a think tank predicted.  Mitsubishi Research Institute said the Japanese economy, following sustained expansion, will move on a "plateau" in the fiscal year that began in April.
