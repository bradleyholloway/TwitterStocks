 Did a Texas Judge Show A Bias Against Texaco?   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1986  No one knows how the appeals justices in Texas will react to Texaco's deft end-run into the White Plains, N.Y., federal court in the Pennzoil case. But one of the trial judges, Anthony J.P. Farris, felt free to speak his mind in a remarkable exchange of letters with a partner in Texaco's Houston law firm.
