 EC Set to Approve Cross-Border Deal Of Telia, Telenor   By By Brandon Mitchener, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1999  The European Commission is set to approve the merger between Swedish telecommunications operator Telia AB and its Norwegian counterpart, Telenor ASA, following sweeping commitments by the two companies to open their national markets to greater competition, said people familiar with the situation.
