 P&G's Pepto shown effective to treat diarrhea in young   By Stern, Gabriella, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1993  A new study, published in the New England Journal of Medicine, found that Procter & Gamble Co's Pepto-Bismol stomach medicine, taken in combination with an oral rehydration solution of salts and glucose, reduces fluid loss in children as well as the duration and severity of diarrhea.
