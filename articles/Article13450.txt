 No Child Left Behind Is Bringing Them Along   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 2007  The Education Industry Association (EIA), which represents many of the tutoring providers currently delivering services under NCLB to more than 700,000 students whose schools have, indeed, left them behind, has learned a thing or two in our five years of collective experience. Where states and local school districts embrace the law's tutoring program as helpful rather than punitive, NCLB works -- and works well.
