 Health Care Brief -- Cigna Corp.: Quarter Returned to Profit;   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 2003  Cigna Corp. returned to profitability for the third quarter as it reduced losses in the reinsurance business from which it is exiting. But the real excitement was generated by projections for this year and next, and the company's forecast that health-care profit will begin to grow and medical costs slow down. As of 4 p.m. Friday in New York Stock Exchange composite trading, shares of Cigna surged $9.08, or nearly 19%, to $57.05, nearing their 52-week high.
