 Business Brief -- Chiquita Brands International Inc.: Banana Trade Disagreement Is Factor in $88.9 Million Loss   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 2001  Banana giant Chiquita Brands International Inc. posted a wider fourth-quarter loss of $88.9 million, or $1.40 a diluted share, compared to $77.8 million, or $1.25 a year ago. For the year, Chiquita posted a net loss of $94.9 million, compared to $58.4 million for 1999.
