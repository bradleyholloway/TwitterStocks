 U.S. Office Products Posts Loss in Quarter, After Taking Charges   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 3, 1998  The results include restructuring charges of $85.2 million, or $2.43 a share; a loss of four cents a share from discontinued operations; and a loss of one cent a share for early debt retirement. Excluding these one-time items, the company said it would have reported earnings of five cents a diluted share. Analysts' consensus estimate of earnings was two cents a share, excluding charges, according to First Call. Revenue rose 6% to $651.9 million from $614.8 million.
