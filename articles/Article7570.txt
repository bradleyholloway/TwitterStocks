 GM Names Jordan Vice President, Chief of Design   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 7, 1986  General Motors Corp. directors elected Charles M. Jordan vice president and chief of the company's design staff, responsible for the exterior design of the company's vehicles.  Mr. Jordan, 58 years old, becomes only the fourth design chief in GM's history. A 37-year veteran of the company's design staff, he succeeds Irvin W. Rybicki, 65, who retired after 42 years with the company.
