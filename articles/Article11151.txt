 General Cinema Unit's Former President Sues In Contract Dispute   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 1988  The former president of General Cinema Corp.'s Neiman-Marcus Group unit filed a suit against the concerns charging they broke his employment contract last year by firing him.  The suit was filed in June by David Dworkin in federal court in Los Angeles. It also names as a defendant Carter Hawley Hale Stores Inc., which last year sold a majority stake in Dallas-based Neiman-Marcus to General Cinema. General Cinema is a soft-drink bottler and movie-theater operator.
