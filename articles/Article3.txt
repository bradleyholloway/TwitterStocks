Felicia Dawn Stoler, a daughter of Francine and Jeffrey Stoler of Morganville, N.J., is to be married today to Alan Scott Wellman, a son of Pat and Bert Wellman of Oceanside, L.I. Dr. Ronald B. Sobel, the senior rabbi of Temple Emanu-El in New York, is to officiate at the Headquarters Plaza, a hotel in Morristown, N.J.  The bride, 29, is an associate in the rights, clearances, and permissions department at ABC News in New York. She graduated from Tulane University and is studying for an M.S. in applied physiology and nutrition at Columbia University.  The bridegroom, 32, is a global account manager in New York in the business market sales division of AT&T. A graduate of Babson College, he is studying for an M.B.A. at New York University.
