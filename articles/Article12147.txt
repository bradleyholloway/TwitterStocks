 Bond prices decline in U.S. and Canada on profit-taking amid interest-rate concerns   By Zuckerman, Gregory, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 1996  Bond prices fell in the US and Canada as investors rushed to cash in on recent gains amid fears that short-term interest-rate cuts may not be in the offing. The 30-year Treasury bond was down 1 16/32 in late trading at 99 25/32. The yield rose to 6.5%.
