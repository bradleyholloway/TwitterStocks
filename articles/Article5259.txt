 World wire: More Usinor shares offered   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1995  France will offer an additional 13 million shares for steelmaker Usinor Sacilor SA's offering to individual investors, after the tranche reserved for them was oversubscribed 2.1 times. The ministry said 800,000 individual subscribers subscribed to the issue for a total of $1.29 billion.
