 Comptroller lets banks into new areas but restricts the amount of investment   By Wilke, John R; Frank, Stephen E, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 21, 1996  Eugene Ludwig, the comptroller of the currency, announced new rules to allow national banks on a case-by-case basis to expand into insurance, securities underwriting and other financial services, but will require the banks to create separate units for new businesses and limit investments to 10% of their capital.
