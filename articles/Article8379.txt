 Management: IBM's Gerstner holds back from sales force shake-up   By Hays, Laurie, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 1993  Although IBM Corp Chairman Louis V. Gerstner Jr cited the company's sales force as a key to its ills, he has decided to let it ride for now, according to an internal memo communicated to his 13 top managers. Gerstner's decision to stick to the current management system of the 40,000-person sales force is bound to become controversial.
