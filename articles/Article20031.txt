 Gateway Posts Loss As Margins Shrink   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 2006  The Irvine, Calif., personal-computer maker posted a net loss of $7.7 million, or two cents a share, compared with net income of $17.2 million, or five cents a share, last year, when the company recorded a pretax gain of $15.1 million from a legal settlement with software maker Microsoft Corp. In the latest quarter, Gateway reported a comparable $8.6 million benefit from Microsoft.
