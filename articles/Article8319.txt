 Integra Inc. develops system to assess effectiveness of psychiatric treatment   By Winslow, Ron, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 7, 1993  Integra Inc, a mental-health services company, will introduce on Jan 7, 1993 a system to track the progress of outpatient psychiatric treatment and to measure the effectiveness of therapists. The system will help keep down health costs due to ineffective treatments.
