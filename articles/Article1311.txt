 Nissan Offical Backs Fuji Heavy Industries in Its Bid for Subaru   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 13, 1990  A top executive of Nissan Motor Co said that Fuji Heavy Industries should press forward with its efforts to acquire all the shares of Subaru of America Inc that it doesn't already own. Nissan is Fuji's largest shareholder with a 5.9% stake.
