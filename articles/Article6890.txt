 World News: Madrid Blames Bomb on ETA   By Catan, Thomas, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 10, 2009  Spain's government blamed Basque separatist group ETA for a car bomb that exploded on Monday outside the Madrid office of a unit of infrastructure company Grupo Ferrovial SA, the latest apparent flare-up in ETA's violent campaign against a planned bullet train linking the Basque region to Madrid.
