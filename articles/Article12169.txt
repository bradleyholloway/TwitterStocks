 Some big brokerage firms to examine future Van Kampen fund distribution   By Gasparino, Charles, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 6, 1997  When Morgan Stanley Group Inc acquired Van Kampen/American Capital in 1996, Wall Street brokerage firms eagerly distributed the mutual-fund family through their distribution systems. But all that is about to change in light of Morgan's surprise merger plan with Dean Witter, Discover & Co. Several major brokerage firms, including Merrill Lynch & Co and PaineWebber Group, are examining their relationship with Morgan's Van Kampen mutual-fund unit now that Van Kampen is slated to become part of the Dean Witter family.
