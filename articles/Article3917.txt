 Google Introduces Web Service For Users to Customize Pages   By Delaney, Kevin J, The Wall Street Journal,  May 20, 2005  "It's Google strengthening its relationships with its own users," said Danny Sullivan, editor of the "Search Engine Watch" Web site. He doesn't believe the new service will steal consumers away from rival offerings such as those from Yahoo and MSN. "It's more like shoring up a weak spot than breaking through with something competitors don't have," Mr. Sullivan added.
