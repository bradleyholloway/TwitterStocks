 Chiron talks about selling stake to Ciba   By Steinmetz, Greg; King, Ralph T, Jr, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1994  According to people close to the situation, Chiron Corp is negotiating to sell a 49% stake to Swiss drug and chemical giant Ciba-Geigy Ltd for nearly $2 billion. The purchase would give Chiron the financial resources to secure its position as a biotechnology leader.
