 Bond-market rally is fueled further by report suggesting manufacturing sector is contracting   By Vogelstein, Fred, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 2, 1995  May 1995's furious bond-market rally steamed into June on Jun 1, fueled by a report showing the US's manufacturing sector in May was much weaker than most anticipated. The benchmark 30-year Treasury bond rose more than 1/2 point to 113 3/32, with its yield falling to 6.60%.
