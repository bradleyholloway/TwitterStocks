 As Park Place Lowers Earnings Forecast, Hilton Deal Appears to Be Falling Apart   By By Christina Binkley, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 2001  Mr. [Ed Roski]'s only other experience in gambling is his ownership of the Silverton, a small casino on Las Vegas's outskirts.
