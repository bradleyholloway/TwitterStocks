 The Americas: Central bank autonomy may get Mexico back on the growth track   By Sarmiento, Sergio, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 1993  Sergio Sarmiento discusses the work of Bank of Mexico's chief, Miguel Mancera Aguayo, to gradually liberalize the country's financial system. Sarmiento comments on a bill that would give the Bank of Mexico more autonomy, expressing the hope that current financial policies that keep interest rates excessively high and growth low could be altered to allow for more growth.
