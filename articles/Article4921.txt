 Shawmut National Taps Kraus, Sanborn as Presidents at Units   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1992  Shawmut National Corp named presidents of its major subsidiaries, Connecticut National Bank, Hartford, and Shawmut Bank, Boston. Eileen S. Kraus becomes president of Connecticut National Bank, and Allen W. Sanborn becomes president of Shawmut Bank.
