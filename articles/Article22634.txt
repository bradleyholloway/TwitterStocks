 Prison Realty Trust Auditors Cite `Doubt' About Firm's Outlook   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 3, 2000  Prison Realty, which announced a major restructuring three months ago that included the ouster of its chief executive officer, also said it is "in default under its secured bank credit facility and certain of its convertible subordinated notes." In addition, the company said that Corrections Corp. of America, which is Prison Realty's primary tenant, is in violation of certain provisions of its bank credit facility and hasn't made certain lease and note payments to Prison Realty.
