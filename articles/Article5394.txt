 Stocks end mixed in Tokyo, supported by gains on Wall Street that spill into European bourses   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1996  The Tokyo bourse ended mixed on Sep 9, 1996, with the benchmark index edging up, supported by Wall Street's advance on Sep 6 that spilled enthusiasm over into European markets. World-wide, stock prices rose in dollar terms. In Tokyo, the Nikkei 225-stock index rose 49.74 to 20202.27. In London, the Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-share index gained 17.8 points to 3910.8, while in Frankfurt, the DAX 30-stock index rose 31.73 points to 2548.73.
