 Landmark Land Sale to Dai-Ichi Appears to Fail   By Barsky, Neil; Chipello, Christopher J, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 13, 1991  A proposed $739 million sale of Landmark Land Co's real estate holdings to Dai-Ichi Real Estate Co has apparently collapsed, the victim of regulatory delays and a decline in Japanese desire for US properties. Analysts said the failure of the deal could trigger a federal takeover of Landmark's ailing thrift, Oak Tree Savings.
