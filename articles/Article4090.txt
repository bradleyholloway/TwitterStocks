 Aaron's Isn't Just for Renters; Its Stock Looks Good to Own   By Ward, Sandra, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 2010  (Aaron's shares have been pressured by concerns that regulatory reforms of consumer-finance practices could extend beyond banks and credit-card companies to other groups, including the rent-to-own industry.)
