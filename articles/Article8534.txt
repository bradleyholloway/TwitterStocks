 Kirk Kerkorian weighs selling Chrysler stake   By Lavin, Douglas, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1994  Following an announcement by Kirk Kerkorian, the owner of 32 million shares of Chrysler Corp, that he is reviewing his stake in the company, analysts and investors are speculating that he will begin selling his 9.2% stake in the No 3 auto maker, putting downward pressure on its stock price. Kerkorian's SEC 13-D filing indicates that he is considering transactions that could affect 1% or more of the company's ownership.
