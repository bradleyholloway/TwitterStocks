 Suitor of Dunkin' Donuts Seeks to Meet With Adviser   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 1989  Unicorp Canada Corp. and Cara Operations Ltd. said they are trying to arrange a meeting with Dunkin' Donuts Inc.'s financial adviser, Goldman, Sachs & Co., to examine confidential financial and operating information on the doughnut shop franchiser.
