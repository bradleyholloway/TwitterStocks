 Moving the Market: Countrywide Issues Ratings Warning   By Hagerty, James R, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 2007  Countrywide said it believes it has "adequate funding liquidity," but that "the effect of future developments on the company may require us to . . . procure additional sources of financing." In August, Countrywide raised $2 billion by selling preferred stock to Bank of America Corp. That preferred stock is convertible into a stake of about 16% in Countrywide.
