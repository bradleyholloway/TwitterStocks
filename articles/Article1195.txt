 Sealright Predicts Net Will Exceed 54 Cents A Share in 2nd Period   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 1989  KANSAS CITY, Mo. -- Sealright Co. expects its second-quarter net income to exceed the 54 cents a share earned in the year-earlier period, Marvin W. Ozley, chairman and president, said.  Mr. Ozley said in an interview that it is still too early for the packaging company to make a precise forecast, but he added that it is likely that earnings will exceed analysts' estimates. They range from 56 cents to 61 cents a share, he said. During the year-earlier quarter, Sealright earned $4 million on sales of $46 million.
