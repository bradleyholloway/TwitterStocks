 Royal Bank of Scotland PLC   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 5, 1996  A unit of Citizens Financial Group Inc said it entered a definitive agreement to acquire Grove Bank for $87.1 million, or $51 a share. The unit, Citizens Bank of Massachusetts, said the price includes Greater Boston Bank, which Grove announced in Aug 1996 it will acquire for $18 million in cash and stock. Citizens Financial is 77% owned by Royal Bank of Scotland PLC.
