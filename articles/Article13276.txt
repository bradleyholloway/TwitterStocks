 NorthStar, Maker Of Medical Device, Gains 11% in IPO   By Cowan, Lynn, The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 2006  NorthStar's debut was a positive end to an otherwise sluggish week for the IPO market. Two other companies that came public, Delek US Holdings Inc. and DynCorp International Inc., had lackluster first trading days on Thursday; Delek ended the day up 1.6%, and DynCorp was down six cents from its $15 offer price.
