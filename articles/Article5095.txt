 Business Brief -- Pirelli S.p.A.: Sales for Year Climbed 11%; Profit Also Rose, Firm Says   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 12, 1990  Pirelli S.p.A. recorded consolidated sales of more than 10.1 trillion lire ($8.13 billion) in 1989, up 11% from 9.12 trillion lire ($7.34 billion) a year earlier, Leopoldo Pirelli, chairman of the Italian cables and tires group, said.
