 Mutual funds quarterly review: Whither the winners of yesteryear?   By Clements, Jonathan, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 5, 1995  An examination of how the 25 top-performing mutual funds of the 1980s have done in the first five years of the 1990s is provided. The 25 funds, which each returned 19% or more a year for the 10-year span through 1989, have returned an average 9.4% a year for the five years through Dec 1994.
