 Fees, Costs of $102 Million Approved in Manville Case   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1988  The professionals who worked on the building products concern's Chapter 11 proceedings had requested about $106 million in fees and expenses. A Manville attorney said the judge granted most of the fees. But he reduced some fees requested by professionals who worked for parties other than Manville, and denied or reduced all but one of the "completion bonuses" requested.
