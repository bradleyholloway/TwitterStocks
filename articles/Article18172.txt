 U.S. Newsprint Use Fell 0.1% in December   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1991  The trade group said that according to preliminary estimates newsprint consumption by all users was 1,027,997 metric tons in December compared with 1,029,186 metric tons a year earlier. For the year consumption by all users fell 0.8% to 12,147,755 metric tons from 12,241,360 metric tons a year earlier. A metric ton equals about 2,204.62 pounds.
