 Guys in Hard Hats Can Seem Like Animals Sometimes   By By Shirley Leung, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 1999  The latest addition to this city's surreal streetscape can be found on Wilshire Boulevard near downtown, where a regal crew of leonine construction workers seems hard at work on the rehabilitation of the historic Bryson Apartments. A second glance reveals that the hard-hat and orange-vest bedecked lions are actually a quartet of statues over the building's elegant entranceway.
