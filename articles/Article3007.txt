 Business Brief -- Lucent Technologies Inc.: Ratios for Shares in Spinoff Of Agere Systems Are Set   The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 2002  Lucent Technologies Inc. will issue one Agere Systems Inc. Class A common share for every 92.768991 common shares of Lucent in its spinoff of Agere. The telecommunications company also will issue one Class B common share of Agere for every 3.779818 shares of Lucent.
