 Marketing: Tortilla Chips Gain on Other Salty Snacks   By Gibson, Richard, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 1992  The Snack Food Association reported that the average American spent $51.45 in 1990 for pretzels, popcorn and other salty snacks. The figure was up 7% from 1989, with tortilla chips showing the biggest market share gains.
