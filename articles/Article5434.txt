 Business Brief -- Doskocil Cos.: Company Is Planning to Quit Hog-Slaughter Business   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 11, 1992  Doskocil Cos., Hutchinson, Kan., said it plans to quit the hog-slaughtering business, a move that will eventually throw more than 750 people out of work and result in a fourth-quarter pretax charge of between $30 million and $32 million.
