 The Evening Wrap: Mitt Splits Online edition  By Moroney, Robin, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 2008  Asian markets fell -- markets in mainland China and Taiwan were closed for a holiday -- and European shares fell heavily --- January Retail Sales Disappoint Retailers' January sales figures managed to fall below expectations that were already low, and in the process fanned fears that the U.S. economy is in or near recession. The International Council of Shopping Centers said its same-store sales reading rose 0.5%, the worst January since it began compiling the data in 1969.
