 Mozilo Seeks Pay Cut To Blunt Criticism   By Hagerty, James R, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 2007  Mr. [Angelo Mozilo], 68 years old, sells shares at regular intervals. In April, his sales totaled 580,000 shares for total proceeds of about $20.8 million. Countrywide last year promised him a "one-time award" of restricted stock units with a fair value on the grant date of $10 million. The company cited his agreement to remain CEO beyond last Dec. 31, when he had planned to retire. Mr. Mozilo also has a "supplemental" retirement plan that is to pay as much as $3 million a year.
