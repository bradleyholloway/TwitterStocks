 Honeywell Inc. Plans 4th-Quarter Charge of About $100 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 1992  Honeywell Inc said it will take a pretax charge of about $100 million in the fourth quarter for the downsizing of its operations. Honeywell declared a 2-for-1 stock split, payable Dec 18, 1992 to stock of record Nov 27, and raised its quarterly dividend to $.445 from $.4125 a common share, payable Dec 14 to stock of record Nov 27.
