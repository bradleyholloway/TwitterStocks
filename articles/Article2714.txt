 Big Win Eludes Korean Leader's Party, Casting Economic Reforms Into Doubt   By By Michael Schuman, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 2000  Mr. Kim's Millennium Democratic Party won 115 seats, an increase from its previous level, but will remain the second-largest party in the 273-seat assembly. The Grand National Party, a redesigned version of the party that held the presidency when Korea slipped into Asia's financial crisis, will remain the largest party in the assembly, with 133 seats, but it failed to win an absolute majority.
