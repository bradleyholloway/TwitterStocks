 Oliver's Stores Files Chapter 11 Petition   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 1987  RIDGEFIELD, N.J. -- Oliver's Stores Inc. said it filed a voluntary petition for protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code.  The filing in federal court in Newark, N.J., which the company had previously indicated was likely, followed the failure of talks between Oliver's and its major lenders on a possible debt restructuring. The two major lenders are Manufacturers Hanover Corp. of New York and Midlantic Banks Inc., Edison N.J.
