 Stuffing Ballots   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 1997  The Senate Rules Committee recently decided to end its investigation of vote fraud in Louisiana's contested Senate race. Democrats had quit the probe in June citing unfairness, and then Attorney General Janet Reno yanked FBI agents off the case because the probe wasn't "bipartisan." Last month, Democrats threatened to block several Senate committees from meeting by lodging objections to the probe. GOP Chairman John Warner threw in the towel, but not before he confirmed many of our suspicions about Bayou State voting.
