 Japanese chip makers to accelerate output of 64-megabit DRAM   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1997  Five Japanese semiconductor makers plan to increase by 10 times their cumulative monthly production of 64-megabit DRAM chips by the end of 1997. The companies will expand monthly production of the chips, to a total of 7.5 million units, up from 700,000 at the end of 1996.
