 Ex-U.S. Official Sentenced In Procurement Scandal   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 11, 1988  Frank Coccia, a former government official and a central figure in a military clothing procurement scandal, was sentenced to 10 years in prison and fined $50,000.  Appearing in federal court before Judge Edmund Ludwig, Mr. Coccia also agreed to forfeit $331,000, said Linda Dale Hoffa and Bucky Mansuy, assistant U.S. attorneys.
