 Texscan Corp. to Post Loss for Fiscal Year; President Quits Post   The Wall Street Journal,  May 14, 1985  PHOENIX, Ariz. -- Texscan Corp. said it expects to post a loss from operations for its fiscal year ended April 30 but it didn't specify a sum.  The maker of cable television equipment also said James A. Luksch, 54 years old, resigned as president and a director. Texscan, which didn't name a successor to Mr. Luksch, didn't give a reason for the resignation. Mr. Luksch, who couldn't be reached for comment, is the fourth top officer to leave the company since December.
