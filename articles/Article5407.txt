 Ex-Top Executive of Silverado Thrift Indicted on Fraud   By Harlan, Christi; Charlier, Marj, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 11, 1992  Michael R. Wise, the former chairman and CEO of failed Silverado Banking S&L, was indicted by a federal grand jury in Denver CO for three counts of bank fraud related to a $1.45 million loan from the thrift's parent, Silverado Financial Corp.
