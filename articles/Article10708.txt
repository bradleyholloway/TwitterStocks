 ARENA --- Art -- Museums: Art's New Haven   By Catton, Pia, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 7, 2012  Since the founding of the Yale University Art Gallery in 1832 -- nearly 40 years before the Metropolitan Museum of Art -- the institution has amassed a vast collection of more than 200,000 objects, ranging from African masks and Roman antiquities to works by Picasso and Richard Serra.
