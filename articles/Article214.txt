 Ceridian agrees to acquire Comdata for stock valued at $961.9 milllion   By McCartney, Scott, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 25, 1995  Ceridian Corp, a Minneapolis company spun off from Control Data Corp in 1992, agreed to acquire Comdata Holdings Corp for stock valued at $961.9 million. Ceridian said Comdata will broaden the company's reach into the rapidly growing transaction processing industry.
