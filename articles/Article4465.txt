 Mattel Targeting Boy Aisle With Launch of Max Steel   By Kell, John, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 2012  Mattel Inc. is more aggressively going after the boy side of the toy aisle, as the toy maker is planning a global launch of the action figure line Max Steel, a brand that is already popular in Latin America but little known in the U.S. market.
