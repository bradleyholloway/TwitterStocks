 Business Brief -- ELECTRONIC DATA SYSTEMS CORP.: Holders Reject Proposal Independent Chairman   The Wall Street Journal,  May 21, 1998  Electronic Data Systems Corp shareholders, at the company's annual meeting, rejected a shareholder's proposal to elect an independent chairman for the Plano TX computer-services company. The vote was 79% against and 21% for the proposal, which was brought by the California Public Employees Retirement System.
