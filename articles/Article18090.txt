 Business Brief -- Nashville Network: Radio Broadcaster to Halt Round-the-Clock Operations   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 4, 1990  The 18-month-old service, called TNNR, will continue to produce a weekly show that is sold through syndication. TNNR is a joint venture of Opryland USA, a privately held company owned by Gaylord Broadcasting Co., and Group W Satellite, a unit of Westinghouse Electric Co.
