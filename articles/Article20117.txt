 WEEKEND JOURNAL; The Home Front: House of the Week / Banker's Retreat   By Casselman, Ben, The Wall Street Journal,  May 4, 2007  Due Diligence: Retired banker John Boatwright built this house in 1998 on the northern, inland portion of Seabrook Island, a gated vacation and retirement community next to the better-known Kiawah Island. The interiors of the shingled house feature reclaimed heart- pine floors and exposed-beam ceilings.
