 World News: Japan's Squid Hunters, Beset by Red Ink, to Lead Strike Over Oil   By Tabuchi, Hiroko, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 12, 2008  Perhaps nowhere is the high price of oil felt so keenly as among the country's 4,500-strong squid fishing fleet, which operates at night using huge, oil-guzzling metal halide lamps, similar to ones used in baseball stadiums, to attract squid from their natural habitat about 200 feet underwater.
