 J.C. Penney Co.: Units apparel stores to close because of growing losses   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 1994  J. C. Penney Co said it will close its 113 Unit stores by Dec 30, 1994 as a result of falling sales and mounting losses. Penney said the value of the chain, which employs 850 workers, previously had been written off, and the shutdown won't materially affect Penney's earnings.
