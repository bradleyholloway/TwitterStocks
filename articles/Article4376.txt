 For Bonds, Bigger Is Better --- Data Show Deals of $500 Million or More Outperform Similar Corporate Debt   By Burne, Katy, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 2012  Better liquidity in larger bond offerings can be seen in their turnover, or the percentage of an outstanding bond issue that trades. Since the start of this year, the turnover rate on deals of more than $750 million was 12%, compared with 7.5% for deals under $750 million, according to MarketAxess Research.
