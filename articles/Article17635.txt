 Sales, Restructuring Weighed by Butler International Inc.   By By Allana Sullivan, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 4, 1986  MONTVALE, N.J. -- Butler International Inc. said it is studying whether to put itself up for sale or to restructure the company in the face of weak stock prices.  Butler said directors authorized Goldman, Sachs & Co. to act as financial adviser. Gary M. Cademartori, Butler senior vice president, finance, said Goldman will study options including selling all or part of Butler, acquiring other businesses or restructuring the company.
