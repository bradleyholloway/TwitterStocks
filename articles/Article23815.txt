 Corporate News: News Corp. Raises Outlook for Full Year   By Ovide, Shira, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 2010  News Corp. returned to a profit for its fiscal second quarter and raised its earnings outlook for the rest of its financial year, citing rebounding ad sales, the success of the movie "Avatar" and optimism about payments for its television stations. At the end of 2009, News Corp. had a highly publicized fight over rights fees with TV-service provider Time Warner Cable Inc. Neither company has disclosed financial terms, but in a conference call Tuesday, News Corp. Chairman and Chief Executive Rupert Murdoch called the deal "groundbreaking" and said it would put Fox on the path to a healthy future.
