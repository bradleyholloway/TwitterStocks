 Financial Managers Aren't Optimistic on '87 Economy   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 2, 1987  According to a survey by the National Association of Accountants, 43% of the controllers and managers responsible for corporate budgets and financial forecasts predicted the economy will be about the same a year from now. Another 28% said things would be worse. Only 20% felt that the economy would fare better.
