 OTC Focus: Biotechnology Stocks Surge, Boosting Index 5.23 Points, or 0.85%, to 622.05   By Calian, Sara, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 1992  Biotechnology stocks surged, helping to propel the Nasdaq Composite Index 0.85%, or 5.23 points, to 622.05 on Nov 9, 1992. The perception that the biotech stocks have hit bottom and changing views of President-elect Clinton's health-care policy helped feed the biotech upsurge.
