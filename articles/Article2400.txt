 Parker Drilling to Shed Assets   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1992  In an effort to streamline operations, Parker Drilling Co said it will dispose of its parts and rig components, resulting in a $19 million provision for the fiscal fourth quarter, ended Aug 31, 1992. In the year-earlier quarter, Parker Drilling earned $373,000 on revenue of $29.6 million.
