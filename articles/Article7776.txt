 International Corporate Report: BSN Acquires HP Foods, Lea & Perrins From Hanson   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 7, 1988  BSN S.A. said it acquired HP FOODS LTD. of Britain and LEA & PERRINS INC. of the U.S. from HANSON PLC, a London-based industrial conglomerate, for #53 million ($90.9 million). Antoine Riboud, president of the French food group, said that in addition to the cash payment, BSN will assume liability for net bank borrowings by the two newly acquired companies of #146 million.
