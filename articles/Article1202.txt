 Andrew Corp. to Post 26% to 41% Net Rise For Fiscal 3rd Period   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 14, 1989  The expected rise is because of increased sales of Andrew's commercial cable products, government communications systems and computer-related equipment. Floyd L. English, president and chief executive officer, also credited stronger foreign markets -- now 35% to 40% of overall sales compared with 30% to 33% four years ago. He said foreign sales should eventually account for half the company's total.  Net is estimated at $3.4 million, or 34 cents a share, to $3.8 million, or 38 cents a share, compared with $2.7 million, or 27 cents a share, a year ago. Sales rose 16% to 20%, or $74 million to $77 million from $64.3 million.
