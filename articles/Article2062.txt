 Dow Chemical Set Back On Breast-Implant Suits   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 1995  U.S. District Judge Denise Page Hood in Detroit ruled that the Dow Chemical cases may proceed -- a decision hailed as a victory by attorneys for women with silicone claims. A Dow Chemical spokesman said the chemical company will appeal the ruling. Judge Hood ruled that "the claims involving the shareholders [Dow Chemical and Corning] are not related to the bankruptcy action."
