 World News: Rocks, YouTube Undergird Kashmir Separatist's Bid   By Wright, Tom, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 2010  SRINAGAR, India -- A little-known Kashmiri separatist leader is spurring stone-throwing protests against India with tactics such as YouTube recruitment videos and protest calendars published in local media, fostering protracted violence that is bedeviling New Delhi. Mr. Bhat uses the Internet to spread his call to rise up for secession; in July, he made an impassioned video appeal, posted on YouTube, for Indian troops to leave the valley.
