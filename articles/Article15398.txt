 World wire: Derivatives in Hong Kong   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 1996  The Hong Kong Monetary Authority said it discovered control problems at 40 of the 100 institutions authorized to deal in derivatives in the territory. The quasicentral bank received auditors' reports of "weaknesses" in the wake of the collapse of Barings PLC because of futures-trading losses by a Singapore trader for the British merchant bank.
