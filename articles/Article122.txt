A lawyer for the wife and mother of a burglary suspect who died last week while in police custody said yesterday that the death was caused by police brutality and he called for a special prosecutor to investigate the case.  But the Queens District Attorney's Office said its investigation, which was continuing, had shown no evidence of brutality by the police.
