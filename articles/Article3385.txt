 Family Finances: Finding an Affordable Home Gets Harder   By Simon, Ruth, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 2005  In some high-cost markets, buyers trading up also are feeling the pinch. "We're seeing an awful lot of well-qualified move-up buyers who are stretching," says John Karevoll, a housing analyst for DataQuick Information Systems in La Jolla, Calif. "Instead of putting 20% down, they are putting 10% down. Instead of a 30-year fixed-rate mortgage, they're taking a seven-year adjustable."
