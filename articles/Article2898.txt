 Business Brief -- Comdisco Inc.: Third Period Swung to Loss As Revenue Declined 29%   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 2001  Comdisco Inc., which has filed for bankruptcy-court protection, said charges for bad debts and sour investments resulted in a fiscal third-quarter loss of $164 million, or $1.08 a share. In the year-earlier period, Comdisco earned $17 million, or 10 cents a share. Revenue fell 29% to $585 million from $810 million.
