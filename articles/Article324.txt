Apple Computer Inc. will ship several new products today, including a Powerbook model that will give the company the right to claim the world's fastest notebook computer. Apple is also shipping new and faster Power Macs, two new Newton devices and a digital camera.  The Powerbook 3400, which Apple executives said on Friday would be in stores this week, has microprocessor speeds of 180 or 200 megahertz, with a 240-megahertz version due in April. These speeds compare with 150 megahertz for the fastest notebook computers equipped with the Intel Pentium chip. The Powerbook 3400 will be priced at $4,500 to $6,500, depending on configuration.
