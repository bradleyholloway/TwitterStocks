 Mercury Air clears repurchase   The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1993  Mercury Air Group Inc's board approved the repurchase of up to $200,000 annually of the company's common stock and 8% redeemable preferred shares. The company, a supplier of aviation fuel and ground support services, has about 2.2 million shares of common stock and 574,000 of the 8% redeemable preferred shares outstanding.
