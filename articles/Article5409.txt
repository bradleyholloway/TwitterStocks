 Rhone-Poulenc S.A.: Operating-Profit Outlook Is Cut Due to Weak Dollar   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 11, 1992  Rhone-Poulenc SA, the French state-owned chemicals and pharmaceuticals group, said it would lower its forecast for 1992 full-operating profit due to a weak dollar and the weakness of its markets. At the end of August, Rhone-Poulenc was expecting a rise in 1992 operating profit of 20%.
