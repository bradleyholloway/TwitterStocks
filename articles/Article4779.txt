 They Could Discount This Stuff and Give It a New Name: Cheep   By Swasy, Alecia, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 10, 1991  The Procter & Gamble plant in Iowa City IA is wrestling with the problem of disposing of 192,000 lbs of Gleem toothpaste, once it was discovered that a bird got chopped up in the batch. Part of the batch has been sent to local landfills, which can present a problem if it is not quickly mixed with other trash.
