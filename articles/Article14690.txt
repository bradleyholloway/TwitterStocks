 Baby Bells Lose Appeal of Ruling In Equipment Case   By By Mary Lu Carnevale, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1990  The U.S. Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia concluded that U.S. District Judge Harold Greene, who oversees the consent decree that broke up the Bell System six years ago, was correct in interpreting the decree's ban on manufacturing to include design and development, as well as fabrication.
