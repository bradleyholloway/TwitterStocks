 Business Brief -- K mart Corp.: Discount Retailer's Earnings Rose 2.1% in 2nd Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 1990  K mart Corp., citing increased sales and "careful monitoring" of expenses, said its second-quarter earnings rose 2.1% to $148 million, or 74 cents a share, from $145 million, or 72 cents a share.
