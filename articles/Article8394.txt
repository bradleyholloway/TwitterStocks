 On fashion: Marketing: What's up, doc? Looney Tunes togs   By Agins, Teri, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 7, 1993  A popular look among schoolchildren that is as old as the hills is back: Clothing featuring the images of Bugs Bunny, Porky Pig and other vintage Warner Brothers cartoon characters. Warner Brothers, a unit of Time Warner, expects to sell $1 billion of Looney Tunes products in 1993.
