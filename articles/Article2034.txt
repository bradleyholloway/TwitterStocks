 Thai buyer of Bumble Bee is an apparent suicide   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 14, 1995  Dumri Konuntakiet, the president and CEO of Unicord PCL, the Thai tuna processor that has had crippling losses since its 1989 purchase of Bumble Bee Seafoods Inc, was found dead in his office on Jun 13, 1995 of an apparant suicide.
