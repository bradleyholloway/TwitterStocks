 China offers to finalize $4 billion order for Boeing, McDonnell Douglas planes   By Cole, Jeff; Greenberger, Robert S; Kahn, Joseph, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1996  China is offering to finalize $4 billion in orders for commercial jets from Boeing Co and McDonnell Douglas Corp. But Beijing is linking the orders to a delay by the US of sanctions in a software-trade dispute, government and industry officials said. Clinton administration officials say they won't swap the plane orders for a freeze on sanctions on any long-term basis.
