 Technology Brief: Peoplesoft Inc.   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 4, 2003  PeopleSoft Inc., Pleasanton, Calif., cut its earnings and license-revenue estimates, citing reduced capital spending and concerns about the war with Iraq amid the weak economy. The software company said it expects earnings of 11 cents to 12 cents a share, down from its January forecast of 13 cents to 14 cents a share.
