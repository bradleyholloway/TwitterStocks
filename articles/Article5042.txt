 Cadbury to buy A&W brands for $334 million   By Milbank, Dana, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 1993  Cadbury Schweppes PLC agreed to buy A&W Brands Inc for $334 million, or $24.50 a share, increasing Cadbury's share of the US soft-drink market to 5.6% from 3.4%. Cadbury increased it stake in Dr Pepper/Seven-Up Cos to 26% in Aug 1993.
