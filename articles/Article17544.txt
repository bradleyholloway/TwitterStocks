 MGM/UA Reports $49.5 Million Loss For 4th Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1985  Separately, a spokesman for Kirk Kerkorian, the investor who owns 50.1% of MGM/UA, said holders of about 10 million of the company's 53 million common and common equivalent shares outstanding had indicated their intention to buy from him stock in the United Artists Corp. studio that he plans to acquire from Turner Broadcasting after the MGM/UA merger is completed. The Kerkorian spokesman said that the offer to sell shares of United Artists at $9 each won't be extended, and that Mr. Kerkorian would own slightly more than 80% of United Artists after the transactions are completed.
