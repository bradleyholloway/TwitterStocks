 SmartMoney: Rankings Help You Pick the Right Broker   By William Mauldin and Eleanor Laise, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 7, 2005  Great news for investors: Competition among discount brokers has been brutal, forcing firms to slash prices and lavish their sites with new features. Premium discounters, such as Fidelity Investments and E*Trade, have cut commissions so much that they're hardly distinguishable from those of the bare-bones online trading platforms. The no-frills sites, such as Harrisdirect and Muriel Siebert, are adding tools at such a clip that they're beginning to look a little like the premium sites. The bottom line is that you can have more for less: more features and customer service with less worry about price.
