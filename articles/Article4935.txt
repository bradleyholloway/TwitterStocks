 Letters to the Editor: Gas Market Works; Why Fuel Fears?   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 10, 1992  After years of federal intrusion, the U.S. natural-gas market is working as a competitive market should -- and it's been working through periods of abnormally warm winters, a supply surplus, regulatory transition and even a hurricane. The marketplace allocated supply and protected residential customers in a way no regulation could.
