 Interactive media led 1992 growth in communications   By Simmons, Jacqueline, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 6, 1993  The 1992 annual survey of the communications industry by investment banking firm Veronis, Suhler & Associates Inc found that interactive software firms' revenue rose 21% to $3.4 billion. Revenue for the communications industry as a whole rose 6.4% to $137 billion.
