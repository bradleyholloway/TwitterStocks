 Letters to the Editor: Fund's Focus Is Jobs   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 12, 1998  We are writing in response to your article "Venture Fund Plans to Target Environment" on July 29. While we appreciate your coverage of the new Sustainable Jobs Fund, or SJF, we wanted to correct a misperception your article and headline may have created for readers. SJF is not a conventional venture-capital fund that is targeting environmental companies.
