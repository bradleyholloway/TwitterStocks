 Disney's Shanghai Park Plan Advances   By Areddy, James T; Sanders, Peter, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 2009  The media giant and the Shanghai municipal government this week agreed to submit a proposal to the Chinese central government that outlines a jointly owned park, hotel and shopping development on the outskirts of the coastal city. The joint application was signed Thursday in Shanghai by Nick Franklin, executive vice president for new business development at Walt Disney Parks and Resorts, and a Shanghai district governor, Li Yiping, the person said.
