 Tip of The Week: Track Your Cell Minutes   By Badal, Jaclyne, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 2007  Minute-Guard.com charges $2.99 a month or $24.95 annually to check usage daily. For a family plan that accommodates up to five phones, the charge is $3.99 monthly or $29.95 annually. The company keeps a database of users' information, so no download is required. Minute- Guard can send messages daily or after users pass certain thresholds, like 80% or 100% of the allotted minutes.
