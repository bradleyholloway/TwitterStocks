 Takeoffs & Landings   By By Paul Glader, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 2003  Overall, airlines arrived within 15 minutes of posted times in 82.1% of cases, up from 77.4% in 2001 and the best level in at least five years. "You have fewer people flying, and fewer flights operating," says Dean Headley, a business professor at Wichita State University in Wichita, Kan., who studies airline performance.
