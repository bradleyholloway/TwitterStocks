 Teamsters to Vote At-Large On Raising Dues for Fund   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1994  Top management of the Teamsters will seek a dues increase as early as next month in a bid to shore up the trucking union's strike fund.  Teamsters officials said this will be the first time the union's 1.4 million members in the U.S. and Canada will vote directly on whether to raise dues. Previous dues increases were voted on by delegates at a convention.
