 JLG Expects to Post Earnings of $270,000 For Fiscal 2nd Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 25, 1985  McCONNELLSBURG, Pa. -- JLG Industries Inc. expects to report net income this week of about $270,000 for its fiscal second quarter ended Jan. 31.  Those earnings would give the maker of aerial work platforms and hydraulic cranes net income for its fiscal first half of about $430,000, or 12 cents a share, John L. Grove, chairman, said. He added that results for the second half, which ends July 31, are likely to match or possibly exceed those of the first half.
