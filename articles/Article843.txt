 International Corporate Report: Murdoch's Bid for Herald Is Cleared by Government   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 1987  However, the offer still must be cleared by the supreme court in Victoria state. Last Friday, the same day that Mr. Murdoch sweetened his bid for Herald, Robert Holmes a Court, who is bidding against Mr. Murdoch, obtained a temporary injunction barring Mr. Murdoch from acquiring more than 15% of Herald. Mr. Murdoch already holds about 12% of Australia's largest media group.
