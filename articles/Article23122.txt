 Sprint Unveils New Cellphone Plan   By Young, Shawn, The Wall Street Journal,  May 3, 2004  Sprint, based in Overland Park, Kan., is seeking to increase its market share and shed an image for shoddy service. The new calling plan is the cornerstone of a set of initiatives the company is launching to improve service, attract new customers and hang on to existing subscribers. Sprint is the nation's fourth-largest wireless carrier by number of customers and the third-largest long-distance company by revenue.
