 Stockbrokers' compensation rose 10% in '93   By Siconolfi, Michael, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 11, 1994  US stockbrokers earned average 1993 paychecks of a record $128,000, up 10% from 1992, thanks to a surge in trading activity by small investors. Median compensation was a record $86,050 in 1993, 10% higher than 1992's figure of $78,227, but 33% lower than 1993's average compensation.
