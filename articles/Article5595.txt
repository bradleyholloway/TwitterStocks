 Dollar skids against yen as turmoil in Japan hurts trade talks' prospects   By Rosenberger, Gary, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 1994  Political turmoil in Japan depressed the dollar against the yen Apr 11, 1994, as weekend bickering by Japan's tattered ruling coalition dimmed prospects for bilateral trade talks with the US. In late New York trading, the dollar was quoted at 103.45 yen, down sharply from Y105.15 Apr 8, and 1.7120 German marks, down from DM1.7125 the prior trading day.
