 Letters to the Editor: Tax Equity   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 11, 1986  In his March 24 letter, the governor of my state, Richard Thornburgh, continues to assert that eliminating deductibility for state and local taxes "makes good sense." He incorrectly claims that present deductibility primarily benefits upper-income taxpayers in high-spending states. This myth must be debunked.
