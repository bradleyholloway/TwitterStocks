 Allstate stock sale raises $2.12 billion, largest initial offering for a U.S. firm   By Steinmetz, Greg, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 3, 1993  Allstate Corp went public Jun 2, 1993 and raised $2.12 billion in the largest initial public stock offering ever by a US company. Sears Roebuck & Co, which owned 100% of Allstate prior to the stock sale, will continue to own 82.1%. The insurer sold 78.5 million shares at $27 apiece.
