 Iran takes economic steps in response to U.S. curbs   By Bahree, Bhushan, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 1996  The Iranian government said on Aug 13, 1996 that it has embarked on diplomatic and legal initiatives to thwart the US's effort to isolate Iran with economic sanctions. Iranian officials intend to discredit the unilateral action by the US and make it costly for Washington to pursue its goals in the face of dissent from its allies in Europe and elsewhere.
