 One-Fourth of GNP   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1991  An editorial discusses the extraordinarily large sum the Bush administration has proposed taxpayers will turn over to the federal government in Fiscal Year 1992. The $1.45 trillion dollars can be viewed as 25% of the nation's annual wealth. In only two Bush years, federal outlays will have soared by three percentage points of the GNP.
