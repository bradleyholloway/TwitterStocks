 Letters to the Editor: Everything's Fine in San Antonio   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 6, 1995  In contrast to what you said in your Aug. 2 article ("Tourism Boom Is Seen Fading in San Antonio"), the visitor industry in San Antonio is in sound condition with a hotel occupancy rate of 70% for the first six months of 1995 and an average daily rate of $77.34 (up 4% from the first six months of 1994).
