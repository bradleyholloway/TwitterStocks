 The S&L Bailout: Looting the North for Texas' Benefit   By Wolpe, Howard; Horton, Frank, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 14, 1990  Reps Howard Wolpe and Frank Horton state that through state policy, Texas encouraged a massive construction boom and by abusing the federal deposit insurance system has levied a tax on citizens to pay for it. Wolpe and Horton say the S&L failures are mostly harming the Northeast and Midwest.
