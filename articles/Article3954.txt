 La-Z-Boy and Ethan Allen Fall As Rate Increase Pressures Stocks   By Cordeiro, Anjali, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 2005  Furnishings-related stocks were the day's worst decliners after large-cap Leggett & Platt cut its third-quarter and full-year projections. La-Z-Boy (NYSE) slid 56 cents, or 4%, to $13.50, Furniture Brands International (NYSE) fell 55 cents, or 2.9%, to 18.36, and Ethan Allen Interiors (NYSE) slid 1.36, or 4.1%, to 31.09.  Oil-related stocks fell, giving back some of Monday's gains, after crude oil slid $1.16 a barrel to $66.23. Oceaneering International (NYSE) shed 89 cents, or 1.8%, to 49.45, and Veritas DGC (NYSE) lost 69 cents, or 1.9%, to 35.08.
