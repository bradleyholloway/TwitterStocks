 Itel to Report Profit More Than Doubled For Fourth Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 7, 1989  Itel Corp. estimates fourth-quarter net income more than doubled to $4 million, or eight cents a share, from the year-earlier $1.7 million, or four cents a share, said its president, Rod Dammeyer.  Mr. Dammeyer said income from continuing operations about tripled from a year earlier to roughly $27 million, or 56 cents a share, compared with $9 million, or 20 cents a share.  Fourth-quarter net included financing fees of about $8 million and added interest expense of about $15 million from acquisitions. Last year's net included antitrust charges of $7.3 million against a subsidiary.
