 Clinton seeks funds for job training considered vital to Nafta's approval   By Frisby, Michael K; Salwen, Kevin G, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 21, 1993  Gaining approval for NAFTA will be difficult for President Clinton without a comprehensive plan to retrain workers who may lose their jobs under the pact if companies chose to relocate to Mexico to take advantage of lower wages. Clinton is trying to find money for a $5 billion worker retraining program.
