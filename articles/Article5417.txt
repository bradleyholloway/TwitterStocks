 Advertising: BBDO Aide Hops to Lowe   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 12, 1992  BBDO's Lee Garfinkel, a creative executive responsible for some of the agency's most popular Pepsi-Cola ads, is switching over to ad agency Lowe Group, which holds the Coca-Cola account. Garfinkel will be chief creative officer at Lowe's New York office.
