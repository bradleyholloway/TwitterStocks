 Family Finances: Manage College-Application Anxiety   By Gamerman, Ellen, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 2008  Katherine Cohen, co-founder of ApplyWise.com, an online college counseling program, says students might consider finishing college in three years instead of four by using credits from Advanced Placement exams or by taking community-college classes during the summer. Hank Herman, who wrote the book "Accept My Kid, Please! A Dad's Descent into College Application Hell," about his son's college-application experience, says he urged his child to write weekly emails to Emory University in Atlanta expressing his enthusiasm for the school.
