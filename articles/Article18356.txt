 Business Brief -- Sun Alliance Group PLC: Firm's Pretax Loss Narrows To $196 Million for 1st Half   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 1992  Sun Alliance Group PLC said its pretax loss narrowed to 97.9 million ($196 million) in the first half of 1992 from 114.1 million a year before, after the United Kingdom general insurer said it tightened its underwriting policy, raised rates and cut costs.
