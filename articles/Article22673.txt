 Business Brief -- ANACOMP INC.: Stock Price Plummets 57% On News of Wider Losses   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 3, 2000  Anacomp Inc., San Diego, slumped $1, or 57%, to 75 cents following the company's announcement of widening losses, personnel changes and disclosure that the document-management services company has hired advisers to assist in a financial restructuring.
