 Straight Arrow Plans To Buy Remaining 75% of US Magazine   By By Joe Sharkey, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 1989  Straight Arrow Publishers Inc., owner of Rolling Stone magazine, agreed yesterday to buy from Warner Communications Inc. the 75% share of US magazine that it doesn't already own.  Jann S. Wenner, chairman of Straight Arrow, said the purchase price was in excess of $10 million cash, though he wouldn't be specific. Mr. Wenner is also editor and publisher of Rolling Stone and editor-in-chief of US.
