 Redemption Notices   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 2003  Cellco Finance NV will redeem on Nov. 10, 2003, $300 million of its 15% senior subordinated notes due 2005 at a redemption price equal to $1,037.50 per $1,000 of the unpaid principal amount, together with unpaid accrued interest to the redemption date equal to $41.25 per $1,000.
