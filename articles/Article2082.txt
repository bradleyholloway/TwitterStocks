 Allegations of bribery probed in Air Canada's 1988 Airbus purchase   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 14, 1995  The Royal Canadian Mounted Police are investigating allegations that bribery may have been involved in the 1988 sale to Air Canada of 34 European A320 Airbuses by Airbus Industrie, a European consortium. The investigation was prompted by media reports that secret payments were made to politically influential Canadians to facilitate the sale.
