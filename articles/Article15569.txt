 Temple-Inland Inc.: Mortgage unit acquires Knutson for $71 million   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 1997  Temple-Inland Inc, said its mortgage unit acquired Knutson Mortage Corp for about $71 million. Temple-Inland Mortgage Corp, an Austin TX-based mortgage banking company, purchased all the issued and outstanding capital stock of Knutson, a Minneapolis-based mortgage banking concern.
