 AirTran to Get Room in Atlanta For More Flights   By By Motoko Rich, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 3, 1999  AirTran Airways is trying to spread its wings in overcrowded Hartsfield Atlanta International Airport. The solution: double-parking its planes.  The problem is that Hartsfield is running out of space. With 32 airlines competing for the airport's 170 gates, officials cannot accommodate all of the carriers' requests. AirTran, which currently uses 18 gates for its 131 daily Atlanta flights, would like five more.
