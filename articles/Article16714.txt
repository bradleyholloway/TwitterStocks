 Hong Kong & China Gas Co.: Panva Stake Deal Reached As Part of China Expansion   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 5, 2006  Hong Kong & China Gas Co. agreed to buy a 45% stake in Chinese gas distributor Panva Gas Holdings Ltd. for HK$3.23 billion (US$415.4 million), the companies said in a joint statement, as both firms speed up their expansion in China to meet strong demand.
