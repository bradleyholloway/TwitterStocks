 Unicom Corp. Unit Is Fined $330,000 For Violating Rules   By By Jeff Bailey, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 4, 1997  Unicom Corp.'s Commonwealth Edison Co. unit said the Nuclear Regulatory Commission proposed $330,000 in fines for regulatory violations at the electric utility's Zion nuclear power plant in Zion, Ill.  Unicom, struggling to improve the operations and regulatory performance at its vast nuclear-plant network, said it won't oppose the fines.  Separately, the utility realigned its management of nuclear plants and said Harry Keiser, vice president and chief nuclear operating officer, resigned.
