 Here's the One Guy You Don't Want To Invite Over on New Year's Eve   By By Mark Robichaux, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 25, 1987  Professor Emeritus Heinrich A. Medicus, a physicist at the Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Troy, N.Y., is looking for a breakthrough of a different kind. At 2:30 this afternoon, Prof. Medicus, the same man who once held the record for detecting the smallest nuclear-energy-level transition, will attempt to break the world's distance record for the flight of a champagne cork.
