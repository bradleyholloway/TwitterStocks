William A. Power, manager of Young & Rubicam Detroit, has been named an executive vice president of Young & Rubicam USA.  Mr. Power, 42 years old, first joined Y. & R. in 1966 as a field service account executive in San Francisco. In 1968 he joined Mazda USA as advertising and promotion director, returning to Y. & R. in 1975. Following a stint in Paris, coordinating Y. & R.'s European automobile business, he became senior vice president and manager of the Detroit office in 1979.
