Jennifer Tamar Meyers, a daughter of Sandy Meyers and Rabbi Joel Meyers of White Plains, was married on Thursday evening to Esteban Fabian Klor, a son of Dr. Adriana Klor and Isaac Klor of Cordoba, Argentina. Rabbi Paul Laderman officiated with the bride's father in the Beit Shmuel Educational and Culture Center at Hebrew Union College in Jerusalem.  Mrs. Klor, 25, is a sculptor in Jerusalem. She graduated from Carnegie Mellon University. Her father is the executive vice president of the Rabbinical Assembly, an international association of conservative rabbis, in New York.
