 Players doubt Japan's economic plan expected this week will stimulate yen   By Dimond, Diane, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 12, 1993  Analysts and traders do not think the yen will get another boost from an economic stimulus plan that the Japanese government is expected to unveil the week of Apr 12, 1993. In late Apr 9 New York trading, the dollar stood at 113.10 yen, down from Y113.75 on Apr 2.
