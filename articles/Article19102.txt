 Letters to the Editor: Movie Magic in the State   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 4, 1998  Since the demise of the rightly criticized Florida Entertainment Industry Council (I was the vice chairman of its original incarnation, the Florida Entertainment Commission, and have been involved in the state's entertainment industry for more than 30 years), the Florida Film Commissioner's Association has formed FilmFlorida, an organization designed to market the state to the entertainment industry and to serve as a catalyst to unite and strengthen the fragmented statewide industry.
