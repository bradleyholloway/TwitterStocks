 Rock & Pop Music: From Mouseketeer To Balladeer   By Fusilli, Jim, The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 2010  "Let It Go" is a romantic piano ballad that brings to mind Freddie Mercury, while "Bloodline" is a spiraling folk tune with vocal support by Patti Griffin; the Tosca String Quartet enriches both tracks. Taking a grassroots approach, Mr. Morris showcased his songs on the road, opening for the Indigo Girls and Joan Osborne, and playing a few festival dates.
