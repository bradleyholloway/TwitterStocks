 World wire: KPMG erects shield in Britain   The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 4, 1995  KPMG Peat Marwick, a big accounting partnership, said its British arm incorporated part of its auditing business to limit partners' unlimited liability potential. It cited increased litigation against accountants and said KPMG Audit PLC will audit only publicly quoted companies and financial-services companies that are subject to regulation.
