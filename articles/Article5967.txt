 Crossed wires: Cable pirates sought plunder but blundered into a major FBI sting   By Robichaux, Mark, The Wall Street Journal,  May 12, 1997  A cartel of cable TV thieves who plotted to rip off cable companies and corner the US black market in illegal descramblers has been thwarted. Fifteen people, including Frank Russo and Trey Prevost, have pleaded guilty to felonies, and the inquiry continues.
