 New Jersey Casinos Post a 17% Increase In Monthly Winnings   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 12, 1985  Eight of the casinos reported higher gross winnings for November. Golden Nugget Inc.'s casino-hotel posted the highest winnings, $20.5 million, for the month. Resorts International Inc.'s casino-hotel had winnings of $19.9 million, the second-highest among the group.
