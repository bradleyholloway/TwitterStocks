 Parker Hannifin Corp.: Remaining stake in venture is purchased from partner   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 5, 1997  Parker Hannifin Corp, Cleveland, said it acquired full ownership of SAES-Parker UHP Components Corp, a joint venture formed in 1993 by Parker with SAES Pure Gas Inc, a unit of SAES Getter Group of Milan. The purchase price was not disclosed.
