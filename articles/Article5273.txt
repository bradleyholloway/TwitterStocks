 The 1995 PGA championship: The club pros' clout   By Dennis, Larry, The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 10, 1995  The question of whether the strength of the PGA Championship's field is diminished by allowing club professionals to play is discussed. By PGA fiat, the 25 top finishers in the previous year's national club professional's tournament are exempted into the PGA.
