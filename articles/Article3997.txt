 Hamilton Oil Unit Gets Jet Fuel Contract Totaling $34.7 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 10, 1985  Wyoming Refining Co., a unit of Hamilton Oil Corp., received a $34.7 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel.  Boeing Co. got Air Force contracts totaling $19.2 million as economic price adjustments for E-3A airborne warning and control system aircraft work for the Air Force and the North Atlantic Treaty Organization in 1980, 1981 and 1982.
