 Business and Finance [2]  The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 1985  METROMEDIA is expected to announce the sale of six TV stations to Fox Film owners Rupert Murdoch and Marvin Davis for an estimated $1.5 billion. Its Boston station will be sold to Hearst for $450 million. The sale could give Fox Film the nucleus of a fourth TV network.  Mobil is expected to announce that Bernard Brennan, a former executive of its Montgomery Ward subsidiary, will return as the unit's president. The move is seen as a first step by Mobil in shedding the unit.  Storer Communications signed a pact approving a proposed $1.64 billion leveraged buyout. But speculation continued that Tele-Communications Inc. and at least one other party may make a bid for Storer.
