 EU drops effort to observe U.S.-Japan trade talks   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 1995  The EU has abandoned efforts to secure observer status in the US-Japan auto trade consultations starting Jun 12, 1995 in Geneva. The US and Japan will discuss US plans to slap some $6 billion of punitive tariffs on imports of Japanese luxury cars on Jun 28. Earlier, the US rebuffed the EU's request on the grounds that its justification for participating didn't identify specific interests.
