 Steel Imports by U.S. Fell 19.8% in January From Year-Ago Level   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 21, 1990  U.S. steel imports in January fell 19.8% to 1,430,000 tons from a year earlier, according to the American Iron and Steel Institute.  But the trade group's compilation of Commerce Department data showed that imports rose 39.1% from December's 1,028,000 tons.
