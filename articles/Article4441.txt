 Corporate News: Corporate Watch   By Anonymous, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 2012  Keach Hagey --- NORDSTROM Retailer Enters Canada Nordstrom Inc. will open four stores in Canada, including three in high-profile former Sears Canada Inc. locations, as the high-end U.S. department-store operator makes its first foray outside the U.S. Seattle-based Nordstrom is the latest U.S. retailer to enter Canada, teaming with Toronto-based property developer Cadillac Fairview Corp. The retailer will take over locations in Calgary, Alberta; Ottawa and Vancouver, British Columbia, from Sears Canada.
