 Business Brief -- Ford Motor Co.: Recall Set for Some Lynx, Escort Models for Emissions   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1989  The recall affects the following 1984 models with 1.6-liter engines: the Ford Escort EXP and Escort Wagon, and Mercury Lynx and Lynx Wagon. About half the 1985 models of the same vehicles, equipped with the same engines as 1984 models, also will be recalled, the EPA said.
