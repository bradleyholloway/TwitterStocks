 States' Efforts to Limit Number of Terms of U.S. Lawmakers Spark Controversy   By Wermiel, Stephen, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 14, 1991  A new constitutional controversy is building over efforts by some states to limit the number of terms that members of Congress may serve. Experts say states can't limit terms of federal legislators, but the harder issue is how and when the state restrictions may be challenged in federal court.
