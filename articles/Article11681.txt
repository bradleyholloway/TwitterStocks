 Advertising: Coffee saga continues   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1993  Nestle SA plans to introduce the next episode of the love saga of neighbors Tony and Sharon, who share an addiction to each other and Taster's Choice coffee. The new commercial reveals that the man in Sharon's apartment was actually her brother, and leaves viewers wondering if Tony will invite Sharon to Paris with him for his business trip. McCann-Erickson created the ads.
