 Yes, Let There Be Light, but What Kind of Light Bulb?   By Anonymous, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 10, 2009  Frank Felber San Diego --- I share Mr. Brandston's dissatisfaction, having tried fluorescent lighting for reading in my home. Since the Federal government plans to phase out incandescent lighting starting in 2012, my plan is to estimate my lifespan based on actuarial tables and my parents' longevity and then stockpile enough incandescents to last as long as I may live.
