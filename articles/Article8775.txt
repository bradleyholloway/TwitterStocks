 For first time, drug 'cocktail' seems to eliminate HIV in its hiding places   By Waldholz, Michael, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 7, 1996  Medical researchers say they have produced the first documented evidence that a new "cocktail" of drugs can eliminate all detectable levels of the AIDS virus from bodily tissues where it's known to hide, a finding certain to fuel hope for someday eradicating the virus.
