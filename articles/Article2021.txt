 New Securities Issues   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 21, 1989  Federal National Mortgage Association -- $400 million of Remic mortgage securities being offered in 10 classes by Morgan Stanley & Co. The offering, Series 50, is backed by Fannie Mae 9 1/2% securities and brings Fannie Mae's 1989 Remic issuance to $18 billion and its total issuance to $30.1 billion since the program began in April 1987. Separately, a $300 million issue of Fannie Mae stripped mortgage securities was being offered in principal-only and interest-only classes by Prudential-Bache Securities Inc. The offering, Fannie Mae's strips Trust 63, is backed by Fannie Mae 9 1/2% securities with an underlying weighted average coupon rate of 10.29% and weighted average remaining term to maturity of 26.4 years. The issue brings Fannie Mae's 1989 strips volume to $3.7 billion and its total issuance to $17.7 billion since the program began in January 1987. Pricing information wasn't immediately available from Prudential-Bache.
