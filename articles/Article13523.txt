 White Space Issues Are More Subtle Than You Think   The Wall Street Journal,  May 6, 2008  Lee Gomes's Portals column on the controversy over "white space" spectrum uses ("If Granny Were Real She'd Like the Idea of 'Wi-Fi on Steroids,'" Marketplace, April 2) takes issue with the lobbying efforts of the National Association of Broadcasters, which is in a debate with technology companies such as Microsoft and Google over how best to use spectrum that will be freed up after the soon-to-come nationwide transition from analog to digital terrestrial television signals.
