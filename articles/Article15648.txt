 Royal Typewriter: Fergie Fields Frivolous FAQs on the Internet   By By Thomas E. Weber, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 5, 1998  An estimated 4,000 Web viewers saw crisply typed rows of words, but the scene at the hotel suite was far less orderly. The duchess spoke her comments into a speakerphone while a staff of typists for the HomeArts Web site handled the keyboards in an office across town. In the span of a half-hour, the duchess fielded just six questions amid finger-tied typists, miscommunications and problems understanding British English.
