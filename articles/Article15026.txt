 Business Brief -- EnergyNorth Natural Gas Co.: Special Gain to Be Posted In Firm's Current Quarter   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 4, 1992  EnergyNorth Natural Gas Inc., Manchester, N.H., said it will record a gain of $935,000 in the fiscal first quarter ending Dec. 31 as a result of a New Hampshire Supreme Court decision allowing full recovery of certain liabilities.
