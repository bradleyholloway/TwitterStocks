 Viacom Loss Grew Sharply on Sales Rise in the Fourth Period   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 21, 1991  Viacom Inc posted a sharply wider fourth-quarter net loss of $87.4 million compared to a $29.1 million loss a year earlier, largely because of costs associated with a long-term deferred compensation plan. For the year, Viacom's net loss was $89.8 million compared with a $113 million profit in 1989.
