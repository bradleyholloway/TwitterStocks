 Legal Beat: Cravath Partner   By Geyelin, Milo; Woo, Junda, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 12, 1992  New York police officials said that David Schwartz, the 55-year-old head of the real estate section of the New York law firm of Cravath, Swaine & Moore, was found murdered on Nov 9, 1992 in a room at a motel in the Bronx. Schwartz was stabbed repeatedly and his wallet was missing.
