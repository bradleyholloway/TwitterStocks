 United Technologies Names 3 Executives to Jet Engine Unit   The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 1992  United Technologies Corp said it named three executives of its Carrier Corp air conditioner unit to its Pratt & Whitney jet engine unit, joining Karl J. Krapek, who was recently named Pratt & Whitney's president. Promoted were Richard M. Whiston, John P. Leary and Martin W. Kraegel Jr.
