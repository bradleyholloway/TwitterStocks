 Regulator, trade group clash over deregulation   By Lucchetti, Aaron, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 14, 1997  CFTC Chairman Brooksley Born told participants at the annual Futures Industry Association conference that the FIA's position was opposed to the CFTC's in the push for so-called professional markets. Born was responding to FIA Chairman Laurence E. Mollner's testimony before a Senate committee. In other commodity market news, coffee prices fell on Mar 13, 1997 on the Coffee, Sugar & Cocoa Exchange. The March contract fell 13.65 cents to $2.01 per pound. Gold prices rose.
