 Dana's Loss Widens Amid Consolidation, Job-Reduction Costs   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 14, 2002  Last October, Dana unveiled a major restructuring plan that called for a work-force reduction of more than 15%, on top of previous cuts, and the closure or consolidation of more than 30 facilities following declines in light-vehicle production. The company currently operates about 300 facilities and employs about 70,000 people in 34 countries.
