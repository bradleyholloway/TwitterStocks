 Nynex Unit Is Denounced on Bid for New York Rates   The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 13, 1990  State Attorney Gen. Robert Abrams denounced a proposed rate increase of about $1.4 billion being sought by Nynex Corp.'s New York Telephone Co. unit, urging that Nynex cut waste and eliminate "its unconscionable business practices" instead of seeking "a bailout from ratepayers."
