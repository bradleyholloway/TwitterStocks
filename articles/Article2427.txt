 Northwest Airlines Chief Dasburg's Pay Increased 11% in 1997   The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 13, 1998  The pay of Northwest Airlines' top executive increased 11% in 1997, closing in on the pay ceiling that he has maintained for the past eight years.  President and Chief Executive Officer John Dasburg took home $1,064,525 in cash during 1997, up from $956,790 in 1996, according to a preliminary proxy statement filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission. Cash compensation includes base salary; bonuses; and other items, such as life-insurance premiums, that the company pays. It doesn't include stock issued to Mr. Dasburg under the company's employee stock plan.
