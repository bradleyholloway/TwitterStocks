 Huffy to Post Record Profit for This Year   By Winter, Ralph E, The Wall Street Journal,  Dec 14, 1990  Huffy Corp will ride "fat tire" bicycles to record profits in 1990 for a second consecutive year, but the economic slowdown is likely to reduce fourth-quarter net. Analysts estimate the company will earn $1.85 to $1.93 a share in 1990, which will exceed 1989 net of $15 million.
