 World wire: Chile's economy may grow 6% ...   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1995  The Economy Ministry of Chile said Chile's economy should grow 6% in 1995, accelerating from the 4.3% pace of 1994. The report predicted that inflation will slow to an 8% annual rate from 8.9%, while the jobless rate should stabilize between 5% and 5.5%.
