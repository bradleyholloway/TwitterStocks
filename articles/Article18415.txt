 Lego Group narrows its search to two sites for U.S. theme park   The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 4, 1993  Lego Systems Inc, the US subsidiary of Denmark's closely held Lego Group, said it has narrowed its search for a US theme park location to two sites: Carlsbad CA and Prince William County VA. Lego Group has operated a Legoland theme park in Billund Denmark since 1968.
