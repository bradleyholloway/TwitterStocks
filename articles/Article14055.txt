 CFO Journal: Lightening the Pension Load --- Companies Weigh Following Verizon, GM by Transferring Obligations to Insurers   By Monga, Vipal, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 6, 2012  According to actuarial consulting firm Milliman, the 100 largest defined-benefit corporate pension plans -- those that guarantee a set payout to retirees -- were underfunded by $453 billion at the end of September, a 30% increase from a year earlier.
