 Stock prices fall in Tokyo and slump in London but rally in Frankfurt as fresh cash emerges   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 6, 1993  Tokyo and London shares sank on Jan 5, 1993, as Frankfurt issues rallied 1.6% in a postholiday market flush with institutional money. Tokyo's Nikkei 225-stock index fell 151.50 points to 16842.58, London's Financial Times-Stock Exchange 100-share index lost 27.9 points to 2833.6 and Frankfurt's DAX 30-stock index surged 25.09 points to finish at 1556.42.
