 A Pullout by Pensions Hammers a Hedge Fund   By Strasburg, Jenny; Eder, Steve, The Wall Street Journal,  Jul 6, 2011  Jim Elkins, an official at Michigan's state pension fund, told FrontPoint it had decided to yank its remaining $375 million investment in the Greenwich, Conn., firm's flagship hedge fund. The firm's rise and decline, extreme even for the boom-and-bust standards of many hedge-fund firms, shows just how sensitive pension funds can be to any allegations of insider trading.
