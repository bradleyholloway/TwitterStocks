 Corporate News: CAW Union, Detroit Face Off   By MacDonald, Alistair; Ramsey, Mike, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 2012  Union officials say the sides are still apart on terms and are ready to strike Monday if a deal isn't reached with General Motors Co., Ford Motor Co. and Chrysler Group LLC. Another argument union officials plan to use: GM and Chrysler owe Canadian governments that offered about $13 billion to help bail out the two on the condition that a certain percentage of North American production -- never specifically disclosed by the governments or car makers -- would be kept in Canada.\n
