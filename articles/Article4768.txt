 GM to Revamp an Unprofitable Car Operation   By White, Joseph B, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1991  General Motors Corp shook up the management of its biggest North American car-making group, Chevrolet-Pontiac-Canada, to make it function more like its Japanese competitors. The restructuring is another step in GM Chairman Robert C. Stempel's push to turn around unprofitable North American units.
