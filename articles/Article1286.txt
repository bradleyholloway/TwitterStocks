 Some Hutterites in South Dakota Fight Over a Corpse   By Niebuhr, R Gustav, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 16, 1990  Hutterites at the Bon Homme Colony have been visited by three dissidents who trespassed in order to visit the gravesite of a minister who the dissidents claim was the last honest leader of the colony. The issue has been taken to the court.
