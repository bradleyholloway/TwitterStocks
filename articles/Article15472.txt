 Hollinger International Inc.: Offering of preferred stock raised to 18 million shares   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 5, 1996  Hollinger International Inc said it is increasing the size of its offering of convertible preferred stock to 18 million from 15 million shares. Hollinger is also offering 10 million shares of Class A common stock. Both offerings were priced at $9.75 a share.
