 City News -- In the Boroughs: Illegal, but Very Popular   By Reddy, Sumathi, The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 14, 2012  While there are hundreds of legal commuter vans that ply their trade in the city every day, it has been estimated that there are an equal or greater number of illegal ones. According to Allan Fromberg, a spokesman for the city's Taxi and Limousine Commission, joint operations with police have resulted in 516 summonses being issued to unlicensed vans so far this year, and about 200 vans have been seized.
