 Barron's Insight: A Stock Picker Looks Ahead After a Year of Winning Bets   By Alpert, Bill, The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 4, 2009  From Cambridge, Mass., his Off Wall Street Consulting Group supplies ideas -- mostly those downside bets known as "short sales" -- to a small number of money managers. Since 1995, his shorts have outperformed a short of the Russell 2000 Index of small-cap stocks by an annual average of more than 22%.
