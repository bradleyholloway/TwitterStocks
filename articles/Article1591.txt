 Sabena Says It Still Owes Debt to KLM, British Air   The Wall Street Journal,  Apr 14, 1992  Belgium's Sabena airlines claims to still owe former partners KLM Royal Dutch Airlines and British Airways 700 million Belgian francs ($20.8 million) each from their failed joint venture. The two creditors plan to take further court actions if Sabena misses the May 4, 1992 deadline for payment.
