 General Motors plans to plug Cadillacs into automatic communication system   By Blumenstein, Rebecca, The Wall Street Journal,  Feb 9, 1996  General Motors Corp on Feb 9, 1996 is expected to introduce the first system for cars that will automatically call for help if a car's air bag is deployed in an accident and will include an array of other personal-communication services. The device, called OnStar, offers navigational assistance across the US and does not require reprogramming for each locale.
