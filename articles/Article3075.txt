 Someday, Perhaps, All Our Wars Will Be Fought via On-Line Poll   By By Jonathan Karp, The Wall Street Journal,  May 21, 1998  In the wake of India's first round of nuclear tests last week, Time magazine decided to gauge public opinion. But it may turn out that all it has gauged is the networking skills of the global Indian and Chinese communities.  On its Internet site, Time asked surfers whether, in the event of tensions between the world's two most populous countries, the U.S. should support India or China. China, which beat India into the atomic age by 12 years, raced off to a quick lead in public support: 69% to 28%.
