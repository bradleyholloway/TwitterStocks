 Chase Manhattan Debt Rating Is Reduced by Moody's; Real Estate Portfolio Cited   By Sease, Douglas R, The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 12, 1990  Moody's Investors Service Inc said it reduced Chase Manhattan Corp's senior debt rating to Baa-2 from Baa-1 as a result of deterioration in the bank holding company's large commercial real estate portfolio.
