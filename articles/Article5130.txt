 Sexually Explicit Materials To Be Banned by Comdex   The Wall Street Journal,  Jun 10, 1994  NEEDHAM, Mass. -- Nudity and sexually explicit materials will be banned from display at the Comdex computer trade shows, the shows' producer said.  Interface Group said it acted after receiving complaints. In recent years, adult-oriented computer disks and other such fare have been a growing presence at the shows, which attract thousands each year to Las Vegas and Atlanta.
