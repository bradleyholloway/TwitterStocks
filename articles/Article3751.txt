 Barron's Insight: Appetite for Art Propels Sotheby's   By Ward, Sandra, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 14, 2007  Indeed, the newly wealthy in fast-growing emerging economies around the globe continue to drive the art-collecting boom. Sotheby's recent Hong Kong auction of contemporary Chinese art set a new record and saw broad-based buying from Chinese and other Asian and Western buyers. The stage is set for more strong showings in the major auctions in New York next month, when Sotheby's will auction paintings by Van Gogh, Picasso, Koons and Bacon, among others, in its Impressionist and Modern Art and Contemporary sales.
