 Libbey Plans to Shut Ontario Site, Cutting Glassware Jobs by 560   The Wall Street Journal,  Jan 5, 1999  The move, which the company said is being made to improve profitability, will leave Libbey, based in Toledo, with 3,540 employees. Libbey, which has 12-month revenue of $441.2 million, will service its Canadian glass-tableware customers from other manufacturing and distribution sites, including Toledo, Shreveport, La., and City of Industry, Calif.
