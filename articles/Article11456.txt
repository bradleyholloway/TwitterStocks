 CFTC Prohibits ITG Founder From Trading   By By Jeffrey Taylor, The Wall Street Journal,  Mar 6, 1991  The settlement of the Commodity Futures Trading Commission's 1989 fraud suit -- the largest in its history -- also calls for ITG founder David C. Beare to have his CFTC registration revoked, said David Reed, deputy director of the CFTC's enforcement division.  Separately, Mr. Beare offered in U.S. District Court in Los Angeles to settle with former customers who have filed a class-action lawsuit. Terms of that offer call for ITG customers to receive the remaining assets of ITG and affiliated companies -- estimated at between $4 million and $10 million.
