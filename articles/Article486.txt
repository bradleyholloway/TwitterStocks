BBDO International yesterday was the first of the publicly held agencies to announce first-quarter earnings. Revenues gained 11.8 percent over the comparable period last year, to $47.2 million from $42.2 million. Net income was up 10.3 percent, to $3.2 million, or $1.15 a share, from $2.9 million, or $1.06 a share.
