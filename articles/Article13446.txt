 WEEKEND JOURNAL; Picks -- Interview: Author; Historian David McCullough on how to get kids interested in the past   By Trachtenberg, Jeffrey A, The Wall Street Journal,  Oct 6, 2007  This past week, Simon & Schuster published David McCullough's "1776: The Illustrated Edition." The book, an abridged version of the 2005 best seller, includes maps, paintings and facsimile source letters, and carries a $65 price tag. Mr. McCullough, whose eight books include Pulitzer Prize-winners "Truman" (1993) and "John Adams" (2002), spoke with us about his approach to writing about history.
