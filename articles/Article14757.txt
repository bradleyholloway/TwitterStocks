 Firm Says 5 Securities Units Merged into One Subsidiary   The Wall Street Journal,  Sep 5, 1990  Kemper Corp. said five securities units merged into one unit, Kemper Securities Group Inc. The five firms are Bateman Eichler, Hill Richards Inc.; Blunt Ellis & Loewi Inc.; Boettcher & Co.; Lovett Underwood, Neuhaus & Webb Inc.; and Prescott, Ball & Turben Inc.
