 Business Brief -- EARTHLINK INC.: InfiNet.com Subscriber List Is Bought for $19.9 Million   The Wall Street Journal,  Aug 21, 2000  EarthLink Inc. acquired a list of InfiNet.com Inc.'s subscribers -- about 92,000 names -- for $19.9 million. In June, InfiNet.com announced that it ended its consumer Internet-access service and transferred its existing list of subscribers to EarthLink....
