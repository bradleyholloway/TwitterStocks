 Freeport Indonesia project loses its OPIC insurance   The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 3, 1995  OPEC has terminated $100 million in political-risk insurance for Freeport-McMoRan Inc's gold-mining project in Indonesia because of "environmental issues" associated with the project. Freeport-McMoRan is contesting the cancellation and has appealed to an independent arbitrator.
