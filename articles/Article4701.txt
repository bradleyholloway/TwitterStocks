 Smith Kline Says Pretax Profit Rose but Sales Declined   By Koenig, Richard, The Wall Street Journal,  Nov 9, 1990  SmithKline Beecham PLC said that pretax profit in the third quarter rose 30% from a year ago, but lower sales caused the company's securities to decline. Sales from continuing operations fell 3% to 1.08 billion British pounds ($2.13 billion) from BP1.12 billion a year ago.
